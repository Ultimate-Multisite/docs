# Build a Niche Website Business with Ultimate Multisite

Most people who try to sell websites compete on price against Wix, Squarespace, and thousands of freelancers. They lose. The ones who win pick a specific audience, build a solution tailored to that audience, and charge for the value they deliver.

This course teaches you how to do exactly that using Ultimate Multisite.

## What You Will Build

Throughout this course you will build a real, working network called **FitSite** -- a website platform built specifically for fitness studios, personal trainers, and gym owners.

By the final lesson, FitSite will be a complete niche platform with:

- Purpose-built site templates designed for fitness businesses
- Plan tiers that match how fitness businesses actually operate
- A branded checkout and onboarding flow
- Pricing tuned to what the market will pay
- A strategy for finding and keeping customers in the fitness vertical

You will follow along step by step, and by the end you will have both a working platform and the knowledge to replicate the process for any niche you choose.

## Why Fitness Studios?

We needed a concrete example that anyone can follow. Fitness studios work because:

- **Everyone understands the business** -- no specialist knowledge required to follow along
- **They have clear website needs** -- class schedules, trainer profiles, booking, location info
- **They are underserved** -- generic website builders do not solve their specific problems
- **Natural plan tiers exist** -- a single personal trainer has different needs than a multi-location gym chain

Everything you learn building FitSite applies directly to whatever niche you choose: restaurants, real estate agents, law firms, churches, trade contractors, or any other vertical.

- A WordPress Multisite installation (or willingness to set one up)

No coding is required. If you have never used Ultimate Multisite before, that is fine -- Lesson 3 starts from a fresh install.
