# Touring the Admin Panel

Ultimate Multisite aims to be as simple and self-explanatory as possible, but with a tool this powerful it can help to have a guided tour. Let's walk through the admin pages to help you get situated.

## Dashboard

The Ultimate Multisite **Dashboard** shows basic reports and analytics including revenues, site activities, membership growth, visitor counts, and geographic data.

## Checkout Forms

**Checkout Forms** give you the flexibility to create custom registration pages. You can customize fields, insert custom classes and scripts, and restrict access based on visitor country or location.

![Checkout Forms list](/img/admin/checkout-forms-list.png)

## Products

The **Products** section is where you create different plans, packages, and services for your network. Define prices, billing frequencies, and limits & quotas at the product level.

![Products list](/img/admin/products-list.png)

## Memberships

The **Memberships** page shows all subscriptions in your network. Review and edit subscription details including plans, products, billing amounts and frequencies, payment history, and timestamps.

![Memberships list](/img/admin/memberships-list.png)

## Payments

The **Payments** page provides a quick view of payment history across your entire network, with detailed transaction information including specific products and amounts.

![Payments list](/img/admin/payments-list.png)

## Customers

The **Customers** page shows all members registered in your network with basic user information including name, email address, last login, and a "switch to" link for logging into their subsite.

![Customers list](/img/admin/customers-list.png)

## Sites

The **Sites** page lists all subsites in your network including site templates and customer-owned sites. Easily manage mapped domains, limits & quotas, plugins and themes, and site thumbnails.

![Sites list](/img/admin/sites-list.png)

## Domains

The **Domains** page is dedicated to custom domains mapped to subsites. As a super admin, you can manually add or map custom domain names and view DNS records and logs.

![Domains list](/img/admin/domains-list.png)

## Discount Codes

**Discount Codes** let you create coupon codes to offer discounts to your end-users. Specify the discount value and target specific users or groups.

![Discount Codes list](/img/admin/discount-codes-list.png)

## Broadcasts

**Broadcasts** is a tool for rolling out announcements or sending private messages to your subsite users. Target specific user groups based on the plan or product they're subscribed to.

![Broadcasts list](/img/admin/broadcasts-list.png)

## Settings

The **Settings** page is where you configure Ultimate Multisite — registration settings, payments, API and webhooks, domain mapping, and other integrations.

AI connector settings list the currently supported OAuth provider pools: Anthropic Max, OpenAI ChatGPT/Codex, and Google AI Pro. Each provider card lets super admins connect accounts, refresh saved accounts, remove accounts by email, and use the manual OAuth fallback when a sandboxed environment blocks the browser redirect. Cursor Pro setup options have been removed from the admin panel.

ChatGPT/Codex accounts support connector-backed tool use where the operation allows tools, so admin workflows that depend on connector-supported operations can use Codex tool behavior after the OpenAI account is connected.

## Events

The **Events** page keeps a record of all events and logs in your network. It tracks activities like plan changes, signups, and other movements — useful for monitoring your multisite.

![Events list](/img/admin/events-list.png)

## Webhooks

**Webhooks** allow you to deliver data to another application. Useful for sending data from Ultimate Multisite to platforms like Zapier.

![Webhooks list](/img/admin/webhooks-list.png)
