# Lesson 2: Apna Niche Chunna {#lesson-2-picking-your-niche}

Lesson 1 ਵਿੱਚ ਅਸੀਂ ਇਹ ਸਥਾਪਿਤ ਕੀਤਾ ਕਿ niche, general (aam) se behtar hai. ਹੁਣ ਤੁਹਾਨੂੰ ਆਪਣਾ niche ਚੁਣਨਾ ਹੈ। ਇਹ lesson ਤੁਹਾਨੂੰ ਇੱਕ framework ਦਿੰਦਾ ਹੈ ਤਾਂ ਜੋ ਤੁਸੀਂ ਇਸ 'ਤੇ time invest ਕਰਨ ਤੋਂ ਪਹਿਲਾਂ ਇੱਕ niche ਲੱਭਣਾ ਅਤੇ ਉਸਦੀ validation ਕਰਨਾ ਸਿੱਖ ਸਕੋ।

## Jithe Ruk Gaye ਸੀ {#where-we-left-off}

ਅਸੀਂ FitSite concept define ਕੀਤਾ: fitness studios ਲਈ ਇੱਕ website platform। ਪਰ ਅਸੀਂ ਖਾਸ ਤੌਰ 'ਤੇ fitness studios 'ਤੇ ਕਿਵੇਂ ਪਹੁੰਚੇ? ਇਹ lesson process ਵਿੱਚੋਂ ਲੰਘਾਉਂਦਾ ਹੈ ਤਾਂ ਜੋ ਤੁਸੀਂ ਇਸਨੂੰ ਵਰਤ ਕੇ ਆਪਣਾ niche ਲੱਭ ਸਕੋ।

## Teen Maayare (Criteria) {#the-three-criteria}

ਇੱਕ website platform ਲਈ ਇੱਕ accha niche ਤਿੰਨ conditions ਨੂੰ ਪੂਰਾ ਕਰਦਾ ਹੈ:

### 1. Ohna Nu Websites Chahiye Par Achhe Nahi Milde {#1-they-need-websites-but-struggle-to-get-good-ones}

ਉਸ industry ਦੀ ਤਲਾਸ਼ ਕਰੋ ਜਿੱਥੇ:

- Business nu web presence ਹੋਣੀ ਜ਼ਰੂਰੀ ਸਮਝੀ ਜਾਂਦੀ ਹੈ
- Industry ਵਿੱਚ ਜ਼ਿਆਦਾਤਰ current websites ਘੱਟ quality ਦੀਆਂ ਹੁੰਦੀਆਂ ਹਨ
- Generic builders unki khaas zarooraton ko hal nahi karte
- Custom development aam business size ke liye bahut mehenga hota hai

**Fitness studios fit karte hain**: members ko online class schedules, pricing, aur booking milne ki ummeed hoti hai. Zyada tar studio websites purani hoti hain ya generic templates par bani hoti hain jo maqsad poora nahi karti.

### 2. Business Monthly Subscription Da Kharcha Utha Sakde Hain {#2-the-businesses-can-afford-a-monthly-subscription}

Aapke target customers hone chahiye:

- Kaafi established hon ki unka marketing budget ho
- Software subscriptions (booking systems, payment processing, etc.) pay karne ke aadi hon
- Aise scale par kaam kar rahe hon jahan $49-$199/month ek reasonable business kharcha ho

**Fitness studios fit karte hain**: woh pehle se hi booking software, payment processing, aur marketing tools ke liye pay karte hain. Website subscription ek natural addition hai.

### 3. Oh Aapas Mein Baat Karte Hain {#3-they-talk-to-each-other}

Sabse acche niches mein hote hain:

- Industry associations, conferences, ya trade shows
- Active online communities (Facebook groups, forums, subreddits)
- Local networking jahan business owners recommendations share karte hain

**Fitness studios fit karte hain**: studio owners fitness industry events mein jaate hain, online communities mein hissa lete hain, aur aksar tools aur services apne saathi (peers) ke saath share karte hain.

## Niche Candidates Kaise Dhoondhein {#how-to-find-niche-candidates}

### Jo Aapko Pata Hai, Uss Se Shuru Karein {#start-with-what-you-know}

Aapka maujooda experience ek faayda hai. In baaton par विचार ਕਰੋ:

- Industries jismein aapne kaam kiya hai ya websites banayi hain
- Hobbies ya interests jahan aap community ko samajhte hain
- Professional networks jin ka aap pehle se hissa hain

### Pain Signals Ki Talaash Karein {#look-for-pain-signals}

Aisi saboot dhoondein ki niche ke business websites se pareshan hain:

- Google "[industry] website builder" search karein aur dekhein ki kya maujood hai
- Web presence ke baare mein complaints ke liye industry forums browse karein
- Niche mein asli websites dekhein -- kya zyada tar kharab hain?
- Check karein ki competitors maujood hain ya nahi aur woh market ko kitne acche se serve karte hain

### Economics Ka Mulyankan Karein {#evaluate-the-economics}

Har candidate niche ke liye, yeh estimate karein:

- **Market size**: Is niche mein kitne business hain? (Apne desh/region se shuru karein)
- **Willingness to pay**: Woh filhaal aisi tools par kitna kharcha karte hain?
- **Lifetime value potential**: Kya yeh business stable hain, ya woh jaldi chhod dete hain (churn)?

## Apne Niche Ko Validate Karna {#validating-your-niche}

Kuch bhi banane se pehle, demand ko validate karein:

### Potential Customers Se Baat Karein {#talk-to-potential-customers}

Apne target niche ke 10-15 business owners se sampark karein. Poochein:

- Aapki current website kahan se mili?
- Aap kya chahte hain ki woh kar sake jo woh nahi kar pa rahi?
- Aap filhaal is par kitna kharcha karte hain?
- Agar ek platform maujood ho jo khaas taur par [aapka niche] ke liye bana ho, toh kya aap interested honge?

Aap abhi bech nahi rahe. Aap sun rahe hain.

### Competitor Landscape Check Karein {#check-competitor-landscape}

- **Koi competitor nahi**: Iska matlab ho sakta hai ki koi demand nahi hai, ya yeh ek untapped opportunity hai. Customer conversations aapko bataengi ki kya hai.
- **Kuch competitors**: Accha sign hai. Demand ko validate karta hai. Study karein ki woh kya acche se karte hain aur kahan kamzor hain.
- **Zyada competitors**: Enter karna mushkil hai, lekin agar aap saaf taur par differentiate kar sakte hain toh ab bhi mumkin hai.

### Ek Landing Page Banayein {#build-a-landing-page}

Platform banane se pehle, ek simple landing page banayein jo aapke niche solution ko describe kare aur email signups collect kare. Interest test karne ke liye thoda targeted advertising chalaein. Agar log sign up karte hain, toh aapke paas validation hai.

## FitSite Par Ise Lagu Karna {#applying-this-to-fitsite}

Yeh raha ki FitSite ne criteria ke khilaaf kaise score kiya:

| Criterion | Assessment |
|-----------|------------|
| Need websites but struggle | Yes -- most studio sites are generic or outdated |
| Can afford subscription | Yes -- already pay $50-$200/month for booking software |
| Talk to each other | Yes -- active communities, industry events, local networks |
| Market size | 100,000+ facilities in the US alone |
| Competitor landscape | Few niche-specific website platforms exist |

Validation ne opportunity confirm kar di. Ab hum banate hain.

## Consider Karne Ke Liye Niche Ideas {#niche-ideas-to-consider}

Agar fitness aapka market nahi hai, toh yahan kuch aur niches hain jo criteria ko poora karte hain:

- **Restaurants and cafes** -- menus, reservations, hours, delivery integration
- **Real estate agents** -- listings, agent profiles, neighborhood guides
- **Law firms** -- practice areas, attorney bios, consultation booking
- **Churches and nonprofits** -- events, sermons, donation, community
- **Trade contractors** (plumbers, electricians, HVAC) -- service areas, booking, reviews
- **Salons and spas** -- services, booking, stylist profiles, galleries
- **Veterinary clinics** -- services, pet portals, appointment booking
- **Music schools** -- instructor profiles, lesson scheduling, recital info

In sab mein same characteristics hain: clear website needs, pay karne ki ability, aur community word-of-mouth.
