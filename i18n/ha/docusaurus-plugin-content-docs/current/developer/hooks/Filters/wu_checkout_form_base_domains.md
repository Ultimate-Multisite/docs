# Filter: wu_checkout_form_base_domains

Yana tace sunayen domain na tushe na fom ɗin checkout da aka raba waɗanda bai kamata su zama rikodin domain da aka map ba.

Yi amfani da wannan filter lokacin da haɗawa ke samar da ƙarin sunayen domain na tushe don filayen **URL na shafi** na fom ɗin checkout. Sunayen domain da wannan filter ya dawo da su ana ɗaukar su a matsayin hosts na rajista da aka raba maimakon custom domains na kowane shafi.

## Parameters {#parameters}

| Name | Type | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| $domains | `array` | Sunayen domain na tushe da aka raba waɗanda aka tattara daga saitunan fom ɗin checkout. |

### Tun daga {#since}

- 2.13.0

### Tushe {#source}

An ayyana shi a cikin `inc/functions/domain.php`.


## Abin da ake dawo da shi {#returns}

Array na sunayen domain na tushe na fom ɗin checkout da aka daidaita.
