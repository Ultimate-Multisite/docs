# Tace: wu_bypass_unset_current_user

Ba masu haɓakawa damar kauce wa lambar unset current user.

Mayar da wani abu dabam da null zai kauce wa unset na current user da ya shiga. Wannan na iya amfani a wasu yanayi, misali, lokacin da ake hulɗa da ƙananan shafuka da ake amfani da su a matsayin allunan gudanarwa.

## Sigogi {#parameters}

| Suna | Nau'i | Bayani |
|------|------|-------------|
| $null_or_bypass | `mixed` | Null don ci gaba, wani abu dabam don kauce masa. |
| $current_user | `false\|\WP_User` | Abun current user. |

- 2.0.11
