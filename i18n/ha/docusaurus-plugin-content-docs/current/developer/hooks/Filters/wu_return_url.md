# Tace: wu_return_url

Ba masu haɓakawa damar sauya URL na dawowa na hanyar biyan kuɗi da ake amfani da shi bayan matakan kammala saye.

## Sigogi {#parameters}

| Suna | Nau'i | Bayani |
|------|------|-------------|
| $return_url | `string` | URL da za a tura zuwa bayan tsari. |
| $gateway | `self` | instance na hanyar biyan kuɗi. |
| $payment | `\WP_Ultimo\Models\Payment` | instance na biyan kuɗi na Ultimate Multisite. |
| $cart | `\WP_Ultimo\Checkout\Cart` | odar kwandon Ultimate Multisite na yanzu. |
