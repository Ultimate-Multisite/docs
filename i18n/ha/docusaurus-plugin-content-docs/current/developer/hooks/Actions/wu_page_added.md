# Aiki: wu_page_added {#action-wupageadded}

Ba wa masu haɓaka plugin damar gudanar da ƙarin abubuwa lokacin da aka yi rajistar shafuka.

Ba kamar wu_page_load ba, wanda yake gudana ne kawai lokacin da ake kallon wani takamaiman shafi, wannan hook yana gudana a lokacin rajista ga kowane shafin admin da ake ƙarawa ta amfani da code na Ultimate Multisite.

## Sigogi {#parameters}

| Suna | Nau'i | Bayani |
|------|------|-------------|
| $page_id | `string` | ID na wannan shafi. |
| $page_hook | `string` | Sunan hook na wannan shafi. |

- 2.0.0
