# Ultimate Multisite Hooks and filters {#ultimate-multisite-hooks-and-filters}

Ultimate Multisite yana da yawa da ayyuka (functions) da tambayoyin database. Dole ne akwai hooks da filters waɗanda za su ba ka damar canza ko ƙara sabon code ga ayyukan da ke wajen.

Mun samar da wani lissafi na waɗannan hooks da filters don taimaka wa masu amfani masu girma su yi customization ga gida (network) suke da shi. Za ka iya samun wannan lissafin daga Github ta hanyar danna link a kan nan:

https://github.com/next-press/wp-ultimo-hooks-and-filters/blob/main/wp-ultimo.md
