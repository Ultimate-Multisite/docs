# Tarin da Ultimate Multisite {#translating-ultimate-multisite}

Ultimate Multisite an ƙirƙira ne cikin harshen Turanci, amma wannan plugin yana iya fassara duka kuma muna nan gaba don karɓar bayanan fassarar.

## Fassarar Plugin na Asali (Core) {#core-plugin-translations}

Plugin na asalin Ultimate Multisite ana fassara ta hanyar manhajar fassarar ofishal **WordPress.org**:

**[translate.wordpress.org/projects/wp-plugins/ultimate-multisite/](https://translate.wordpress.org/projects/wp-plugins/ultimate-multisite/)**

Wannan shi ne tsarin fassarar al'umma na WordPress. Idan fassahar ya kai matsayi na amincewa, ana sanar da shi dukkan masu amfani da Ultimate Multisite ta hanyar ƙarin plugin na WordPress.

**Muna buƙatar taimakon ku!** Idan kuna magana da wani harshe bayan Turanci, don karɓar bayanan fassara. Fassarar wasu kalmomi ma tana taimako. Don fara:

1. Zama a [translate.wordpress.org](https://translate.wordpress.org/projects/wp-plugins/ultimate-multisite/)
2. Zaɓi harshen da kake so ka fassara
3. Danna "Contribute Translation" (Ka bayar da Fassarar)
4. Zaka buƙatar ƙirƙirar asusun WordPress.org na kyauta

Idan kana son zama injin fassarar don harshenka (wanda za ka iya amincewa da fassahar), ka tuntuɓe ta hanyar [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite/discussions).

## Fassarar Addons (Addon Translations) {#addon-translations}

Fassarar addon plugins ana yin su a wuri na musamman:

**[translate.ultimatemultisite.com](https://translate.ultimatemultisite.com/)**

Fassahar addon suna samarwa ne ta AI a matsayin fara. Suna gona dukkan harsunan da ake samun su amma za su iya ƙirƙirar wani yadda ba ta dacewa ko kuma akwai rauni. Muna farin cikin karɓar masu fassarar mutane don bincika da ba da shawarwari don yin su zama mafi dacewa da gaske.

## Fassarar Gida (Local Translations) {#local-translations}

Idan kuna son ku yi fassara a kan shafukan ku na kai ba tare da ku sami damar yin contribution (tattalin bayani) ba, akwai zaɓuƙar biyu:

### Amfani da Poedit {#using-poedit}

Zaka iya amfani da manhajar desktop **[Poedit](https://poedit.net)** don fassara fayil ɗin `.pot`:

1. Download Poedit daga [poedit.net](https://poedit.net)
2. Buɗe fayil ɗin `.pot` daga `wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite/lang/`
3. Fassara rubutu (strings) a cikin interface na Poedit
4. Ajiye -- Poedit zai samar da fayil ɗin `.po` da `.mo`
5. Ajiye fayil ɗin da aka samar a cikin folder `lang`

### Amfani da Loco Translate {#using-loco-translate}

Wannan plugin na WordPress, **[Loco Translate](https://wordpress.org/plugins/loco-translate/)**, yana ba ku damar fassara kai tsaye daga admin panel ba tare da buƙatar wani kayan desktop ba. Yana kula da samar da fayil ɗin fassara da kuma kompilarsu don ku.
