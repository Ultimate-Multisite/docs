# ɗaukar Dashboard-nka da PRO Theme {#customizing-your-admin-panel-with-pro-theme}

Yawancin lokaci idan muna yin aiki ga abokan cinikin, muna buƙatar ba su wani hanya na musamman a dashboard ɗin WordPress. Idan kuna neman wani bayani mai inganci kuma mai kyau, PRO Theme zai iya taimaka muku.

PRO Theme wani theme ne da aka tsara don taimaka muku lokacin da kuke canza Dashboard ɗin abokan cinikin ku. Kuma shi ma ya shafi sashi na asali (core) na Ultimate Multisite.

Bayan kun yi sauri, tuntuɓi shafin _Plugins_ kuma danna don ƙarfafa shi a kan Network ɗinku.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: WP Plugins page showing Network Activate button for PRO Theme -->

_Ƙarfafa shi a duk Network-ninka a shafin Plugins_

_**MUHIMMI:**_ _Idan ka yi sauri kawai, zai nuna a abin da abokan cinikin ku za su iya duba cewa akwai wani zaɓi da za a iya alamar ƙara wannan theme ga wannan mutum ne kawai._

Yanzu, an sake tsara interface ɗin admin-ninka gaba ɗaya! Kuma kuna sabon menu a kan lamba na ƙasa mai suna PRO Theme.
