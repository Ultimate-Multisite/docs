# Gidanin Abokan Ma'aikaci (v2) {#your-clients-websites-v2}

Bayaninka a cikin shafukan ku bayan an rubuta (register) a kan yanarku, za su iya samun dashboard ɗinsu.

Bayan wajen **Account** (Kudi/Shiga), inda za su iya gani bayanan muhimmai game da biyan kuɗi, shiga (memberships), domain-su, iyakacin tsarin (plan limitations) da dama, suna da damar samun wajen **My Sites**, inda za su iya gani da sarrafa duk shafukan su. Duk abin da suke so shi ne danna "My Sites" a dashboard ɗinsu.

![Link na My Sites a dashboard ɗin abokan ciniki](/img/account-page/my-sites-link.png)

Za su gani duk shafukan su, tare da wajen **Manage** (Sarrafa) kusa da kowane wani. Idan suka danna shi, za su zama a dashboard ɗin wannan shafin musamman.

Za su iya gani kuma wanne daga cikin shafukan su ne wanda yake **primary** (na farko) don tsarin biyan kuɗi waɗanda ba su iya samun shafi ɗaya ba.

![Hanyar My Sites tana nuna shafukan abokan ciniki tare da wajen Manage](/img/account-page/my-sites-page.png)

Ga bayani na cikakken hanyar gyara shafi:

![Cikakken hanyar gyaran shafi](/img/admin/site-edit-full.png)

Idan suka so su canza wajen primary (na farko), dole ne su je wajen Account ta hanyar danna babban menu na **Account**.

![Link na menu na Account a dashboard ɗin abokan ciniki](/img/account-page/account-menu.png)

Za su iya gani da gyara bayanan abokan ciniki:

![Hanyar gyaran abokin ciniki](/img/admin/customer-edit.png)

Sannan, dole ne suka danna **Change Default Site** (Canza Wajen Primary).

![Babban babban Change Default Site a wajen account page](/img/account-page/change-default-site.png)

Wani shafi na pop-up zai bayyana. Danna kan siffar drop-down kuma zaɓi wanne shafi kake so ya zama wajen primary.

![Zaɓin dropdown na Change Default Site](/img/account-page/change-default-site-dropdown.png)

Sannan, kawai danna **Change Default Site** kuma an gama!
