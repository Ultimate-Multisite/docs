# Tarinke Wani (v2)

_**MUHIMMIYA MAIYAR: wannan makala tana nufin Ultimate Multisite vershon 2.x.**_

Abokan hulɗarka suna iya mayar da tsarin su a kowane lokaci. Za su iya mayar da shi zuwa wani tsari (plan) ko kuma siyan duk wani sabon ayyuka ko paketomin da kake bayarwa a kan shahararku.

A wannan koyarwar, za mu nuna yadda za su iya mayar da tsarin su kuma abin da zai farawa bayan an kammala tsarin mayar da shi.

Don mayar da tsarin su, abokan hulɗarka dole ne su shiga dashboard (babban shafinsa) su tafi wajen shafin **Account** (Kudi/Shaharar).

![Customer subsite dashboard with Account menu link visible](/img/account-page/account-menu.png)

A shafin Account, za a nuna mus tsarin ku na rikodin da ake samu a yanzu kuma wani tsari (plan) da ya shafi shi. Don mayar da shi zuwa wani tsari, dole ne su danna **Change** (Canza) a gefen ke suka shafi **Your Membership** (Rikodin Ku).

![Account page Your Membership card with Change button](/img/account-page/membership-change-button.png)

Za a tura su zuwa wani shafin biyan kuɗi inda duk dukkan tsarin da ake samu za su yi nuna.

Za su ma iya ganin **services and packages available for their current plan** (ayyuka da paketomin da ke cikin tsarin su na yanzu), idan sun so kawai su siyar wani ayyuka ko paketami musamman (kamar ziyara mara ƙarfi ko wurin disk) a maimakon mayar da shi zuwa wani tsari.

![Upgrade picker showing available plans and packages on the customer side](/img/account-page/upgrade-picker.png)

Bayan su zaɓi wani kayan da suka so su siyar, za su ga yadda za su buɗe kuɗi a yanzu - ba tare da ƙara kowane kuɗin da ya kasance a gare su ba - kuma yadda za su kashe shi a ran farawa na biyan kuɗi.

Yawanci, idan kayan da aka zaɓa wani tsari ne kuma biyan kuɗi zai yi tsakanin kuɗin rikodin (membership charge), za su samu ƙarin kuɗi (credit) ga yawan kuɗin da suka biya a kan tsarin farko.

![Upgrade payment summary showing credit applied and next billing amount](/img/account-page/upgrade-summary.png)

Idan suka za wani tsari ko paket da ba zai canza abin da yake a cikin shigarwa ku yanzu, za ku gani wata saƙo mai bayyana hakan.

![Notice when the selected plan does not change the subscription](/img/account-page/upgrade-no-change.png)

Bayan an kammala biyan kuɗi (checkout), sabbin kayayyaki za su ƙara a cikin asusun abokan hulɗar ku kuma duk iyakoki ko ƙarin siffofin sabbin kayayyakin za su ƙara nan nan: ziyara, wuri na disk, baki, da sauransu...

## Shika da Canza Matakin Biya (Upgrade and Downgrade Paths)

A kowane kayayyaki ku, za ku sami tab mai suna **Up & Downgrades**. Zaɓin farko a wannan tab yana wani makonshiya mai suna **Plan Group**.

**Plan groups** shi ne abin ke ba Ultimate Multisite su sani cewa wasu tsari (plans) suna cikin "ici" ɗaya, kuma saboda haka dole ne a yi amfani da su don samar da zaɓuwar shika/canza matakin biya.

![Product edit Up and Downgrades tab with Plan Group field](/img/config/product-upgrades-plan-group.png)

Misali, kuna da **Free plan**, **Basic Plan**, da **Premium Plan** da suke da sauƙi. Kuna so masu biyan kuɗin da ke cikin **Free Plan** su iya shiga hanya ga **Premium Plan** kawai kuma ba ku son su gani "Basic Plan" a matsayin wata hanyar canza matakin biya ba. Daga farko, duka da kake bukata shi ne ka ba su ko'ina mai suna Plan Group ɗaya don Free plan da Premium plan kamar yadda aka nuna a cikin hotunan da ke ƙasa.

![Free Plan product page with High End plan group assigned](/img/config/product-upgrades-free.png)

![Premium Plan product page with High End plan group assigned](/img/config/product-upgrades-premium.png)

Wannan yana nufin ka gaya wa Ultimate Multisite cewa akwai "banori" (family) na tsarin shafukan a cikin shaharar (network) wanda ake kira **High End**. Idan kana ba da zaɓuwar mai inganci ko kuma mai sauƙi, kawai zaɓuwar daga banorin guda ɗaya ne za su zama da dama ga mutum.
