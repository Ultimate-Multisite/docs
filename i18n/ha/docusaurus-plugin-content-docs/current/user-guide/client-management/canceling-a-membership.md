# Cancel Membership (v2) {#canceling-a-membership-v2}

_**MUHIMMANA:** Wannan makala yana nufin Ultimate Multisite versiyon 2.x._

Yin da kake so ka daina samun kuɗi a gefen abokan ciniki, yana sauƙi sosai.

Idan mai amfani ya so ya daina samun kuɗi, dole ne ya shiga hanyar **Account** (Kayan Aikin) ɗinsa ta farko.

A nan, dole ne ya danna zuwa **Danger Zone** (Wurin Haɗari), sannan ya danna don **Delete Account** (Kashe Kayan Aikin).

![Account page Danger Zone with Delete Account button](/img/account-page/danger-zone.png)

Wani module zai buɗe yana neman tabbacin shi. Dole ne ya shiga dawo aɓoye zaɓin **Confirm Account Deletion** (Tabbatar da Kashe Kayan Aikin) kuma ya danna don **Delete Account**.

![Confirm Account Deletion dialog](/img/account-page/delete-account-confirm.png)

Wannan zai sa shi ya tafi daga wurin shiga site kuma ya daina samun kuɗi a yanayin ku, tare da kashe site ɗinsa daga yanayin ku.

![Logged out after account deletion](/img/account-page/logged-out-after-delete.png)

![Membership canceled confirmation](/img/account-page/membership-canceled.png)

**MUHIMMANA:** Ka gani cewa wannan aiki ba zai sa abokin ciniki ya fita daga yanayin ku ba, saboda abokin ciniki zai iya samun sama da wata ko ba shiga kuɗi da aka haɗa da shi.

![Customer still exists in the network after cancellation](/img/admin/customer-after-cancel.png)

![Customer with no active memberships](/img/admin/memberships-customer-empty.png)
