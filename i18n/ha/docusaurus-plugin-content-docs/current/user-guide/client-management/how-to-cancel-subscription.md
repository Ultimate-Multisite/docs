# Zabi zanin da aboni na?

Idan kana da Ultimate Multisite license, za ka iya daina sake mayar da shi kowane lokaci da kake so. Kawai bi matakan da ke ƙasa:


<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Account page email with access link -->

Yi amfani da imel ɗin da ka ba a lokacin sayarwa kuma wudin sirrinka (password).

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Login page with email and password fields -->

Wannan shi ne shafin ka na asusunka na farko:

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Account page initial screen showing subscription details -->

Don daina sake mayar da aboni, a cikin menu na ƙasa, tafiya zuwa _Renewing & Billing._ (Sake mayar da sabon lokaci da biyan kuɗi).

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Renewing & Billing page with cancel option -->

Danna alamar wuri (arrow) a kan yankin hagu don buɗe shafi na gefe. Sannan, za ka zaɓi zaɓin da zai daina sake mayar da shi (_auto-renew_).

Wannan tsarin zai nuna maka saƙon tabbatarwa.

Idan kana da tabbacin kana so ka daina aboni, kawai danna maɓallin _**Cancel Renewals**_. Bayan wannan aiki, za a tambaye ka amsa bincike mai sauri.

An gama! Abonka ba zai sake mayar da shi ne ba.

**Za ka sami kunci mai inganci har abada aboni naka bai ƙare ba**. Idan kana so ka sake mayar da aboni naka, za ka buƙaci yin hakan kai tsaye (manually).
