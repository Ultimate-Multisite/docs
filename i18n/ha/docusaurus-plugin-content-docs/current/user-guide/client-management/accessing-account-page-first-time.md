# Baki zan in shiga wannan shafin amana a farkon lokacin?

Marhabisto Ultimate Multisite!

Don samun shafin amana (account page) naka, ka yi amfani da link ɗin da aka aiko maka ta imel bayan ka sayi plugin:

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Account page email with access link -->

Yi amfani da imel ɗin da ka ba a lokacin sayarwarka kuma kalmar sirri (password) da aka ba ka a cikin imel.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Login page with email and password fields -->

Tun wannan shine farkon lokacinka da kake shiga wannan asusun, za a tambaye ka canza kalmar sirrinka. Canza shi sannan ka danna maɓallin _Save_.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Change password form with Save button -->

A shafin amana (account page) naka, za ka iya sarrafa duk bayanan asusunka kamar kuɗi (billings), sake samun su (renewals), cire shi (cancellations), da abubuwa masu yawa.
