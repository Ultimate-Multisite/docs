# Idan Magani Linkin da Shareable Donawa

Ultimate Multisite ya ba ka zaɓi don ka raba linkin plan kai tsaye ga abokan ciniki. Don yin hakan, tafiya zuwa kowane shafin gyaran (edit pages) na plans ɗinka kuma danna maɓallin _Click to copy the Shareable Link_.

![Product edit page with shareable link button](/img/config/product-edit-full.png)

_A kan shafin gyaran Plan, za ka samu maɓallin da zai ba ka damar samar da linkin shareable._

Ka gane cewa wannan hanyar amfani da yadda ake samar da linkin ta amfani da yadda aka tsara karin kuɗi (billing frequency) na asali. Wannan yana nufin idan karin kuɗin ka na asali shine kowace wata shawarwar wata kanta, misali a ƙarshe za ka samu linki kamar haka: [_**https://network.com/register/premium/**_](https://network.com/register/premium/)

Idan kana so linkin shareable ya fara da karin kuɗi na uku (quarterly), dole ne ka ƙara 3 a ƙarshe:

[_**https://network.com/register/premium/3**_](https://network.com/register/premium/3)

Don zaɓar plan _premium_ don shawarwar wata shekara, za ka ƙara 1 sannan kuma kalmar:

[_**https://network.com/register/premium/1/year**_](https://network.com/register/premium/1/year)

Ka tuna cewa ba za ka iya samun wani link ɗaya kawai don nau'ikan karin kuɗi daban-daban ba. Dole ne ka raba wani link na musamman ga kowace nau'in shawarwar kuɗi da kake samarwa.

Bayan kana raba linkin da abokan ciniki, zai ɗauka su zuwa matakin farko na tsarin shiga (signup flow) naka.
