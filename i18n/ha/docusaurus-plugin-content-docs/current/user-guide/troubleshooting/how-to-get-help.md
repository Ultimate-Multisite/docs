# Zabi da taimako

Kake samu lambar License (License key) ta dace, kuma an haɗa tare da ita damar samun taimakon ƙungiyar mu don magance matsaloli da za ka iya fuskanta lokacin da kake shirya Ultimate Multisite.

Wannan bayanin yana nufin hanyoyin tallafi (support channels) da ake samu ga masu mallakar license kuma nau'ikan bayanai da za ka buƙaci don tuntuɓar ƙungiyar tallafinmu.

## Wurin Taimako (Help Site) {#help-site}

Za ka iya samun labarai da manhajoji (tutorials) daban-daban daga wurin taimakonmu, wanda za ka samu a link a nan:

[Link]

Muna ƙarfafa masu amfani su su san abubuwan asali (basic features) da ayyukan Ultimate Multisite. Akwai labarai na "Getting Started" (Fara Shirye) a wurin taimakonmu kuma za ka iya samun jawabin tambayoyin yau da kullum a wurin, kamar yadda ake sojoji add-ons, yadda ake haɗa payment gateways, da sauransu.

## Tuntuɓar Tallafi (Contact Support) {#contact-support}

Idan ka yi watsi kuma ba ka samu amsa daga wurin taimakonmu ba, za ka iya tuntuɓar ƙungiyar tallafinmu ta hanyar danna alamar ƙubawa (speech bubble icon) da take kusa gefen baya na gidan yanar gizo (website) ko kuma ta hanyar aiko imel kai tsaye zuwa support@ultimatemultisite.com.

Idan kana tuntuɓar tallafi kan matsaloli na fasaha, ka tabbata ka ba su bayanin matsalarka sosai tare da screenshot, saƙonnin matali (error messages), ko duk wani bayani da zai taimaka ga mutanen tallafinmu su fahimci da su bincika matsalar ka cikin sauki. Hakan kuma dole ne ka ba su lambar license ɗinka. Hanya masu mallakar license ne kawai suna da ikon samun tallafi.

## Wurin Alaka (Community Site) {#community-site}

Wurin alaka na mu [community site](https://community.ultimatemultisite.com/) shine wuri da Ultimate Multisite masu amfani za su yi juna da juna. Yana aiki a matsayin tsarin bayani inda mutane za su iya tura labarai, yin sharri kan tattaunawa, ba da shawarwari, haɗin kai, ko samun bayanai daga jama'a (crowdsource). Za ka buƙaci ba da lambar license ɗinka na Ultimate Multisite don buɗe wuraren/shafuka na sirri.
