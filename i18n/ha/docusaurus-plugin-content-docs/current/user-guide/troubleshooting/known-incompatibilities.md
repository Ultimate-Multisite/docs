# Takattunawa da wasu shawarwari tare da Addon Plugins {#known-incompatibilities-with-other-plugins}

Bayan da ka yi amfani da Ultimate Multisite kuma ka sake aiki (activate), yana iya samun matsala saboda akwai wata takamaiman abubuwa da ba a magance su da wasu add-on plugins.

Muna aiki sosai don rage wannan lissafi da kowane sabon wuri muke fitarwa, amma idan wani plugin ya shafi nan, zaka iya buƙatar ka sake aiki (deactivate) shi don Ultimate Multisite Network ɗinka ya yi aiki duka.

Ga lissafin su kuma abin da za ka yi a waɗannan lokuta:

**Wasu Add-on Plugins na WPMU DEV** Wasu plugins ba suke da gyara daga WPMU DEV suna sani cewa suna sa matsayin Ultimate Multisite (registration flow) ya tsaya a ƙarshe (ma'aikacin babu iya ƙirƙirar asusun). Ga lissafi na plugins da ke kawo waɗannan matsalar:

  * Signup Language: _Ultimate Multisite yana da add-on wanda ke ba da wannan aikin, wanda ake kira Ultimate Multisite: Language Switcher._

  * Set Blog Description on Blog Creation: _Wannan ya shafi Ultimate Multisite, don haka babu buƙatar wani plugin na ƙwarai._

  * New Blog Templates: _Wannan ya shafi Ultimate Multisite, don haka babu buƙatar wani plugin na ƙwarai._

  * Set Password on Multisite Blog Creation: _Wannan ya shafi Ultimate Multisite, don haka babu buƙatar wani plugin na ƙwarai._

  * Signup TOS: _Wannan ya shafi Ultimate Multisite, don haka babu buƙatar wani plugin na ƙwarai._

  * Anti-Splog: Duk da yake ba shi da ƙarfi sosai, wannan ma _ya shafi Ultimate Multisite._

**Gargaɗi**: Duk wasu WPMU Dev plugins na gaba za su iya amfani da su cikin sauƙi tare da Ultimate Multisite. Misalai sun haɗa da _Smush_ , _Forminator_ , _Defender,_ da sauransu.
