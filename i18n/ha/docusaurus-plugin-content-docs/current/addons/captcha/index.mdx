# Captcha {#captcha}

**Dakatar da Bots. Kare Kuɗaɗen Shiga. Babu Wahalar Saita Komai.**

Kana gudanar da hanyar sadarwar multisite da custom domains? Ka san wahalar: Google reCAPTCHA da hCaptcha suna bukatar ka yi rajistar *kowane domain guda ɗaya* a cikin admin console ɗinsu. Ka ƙara sabon shafin abokin ciniki? Sabunta captcha config. Ka haɗa custom domain? Sake sabuntawa. Ba ya ƙarewa.

**Ultimate Multisite: Captcha yana sauya komai.**

## Cap Captcha: Kariyar Self-Hosted Da Ke Aiki Kai Tsaye {#cap-captcha-self-hosted-protection-that-just-works}

Sabon **Cap Captcha** provider ɗinmu mai sauya wasa ne ga hanyoyin sadarwar multisite:

* **Babu Saita Komai** — Kunna addon ɗin kuma kana da kariya. Babu API keys, babu Google account, babu hCaptcha dashboard, babu domain whitelisting.
* **Yana Aiki a Kowane Domain Ta Atomatik** — Subdomains, mapped domains, domains na abokan ciniki—duk suna samun kariya nan take.
* **Self-Hosted & Na Sirri** — Bayananka ba sa barin server ɗinka. Cikakken dacewa da GDPR ba tare da bin diddigin ɓangare na uku ba.
* **Proof-of-Work Marar Ganuwa** — Masu amfani suna warware lissafin ƙalubale a bango. Babu danna fire hydrants, babu grids na hotuna masu takaici.
* **Mai Sauƙi** — Kawai 20KB. Ba zai rage saurin checkout ɗinka ba.

## Kariyar Gwajin Kati ta WooCommerce {#woocommerce-card-testing-protection}

Hare-haren gwajin kati suna jawo asarar biliyoyi ga ’yan kasuwa kowace shekara. Masu zamba suna amfani da bots don tabbatar da katunan kuɗi na sata a kan WooCommerce checkout ɗinka—kai ne kake biyan kuɗin sarrafawa, su kuma suna samun lambobin kati masu aiki.

**Muna kare inda ya fi muhimmanci:**

* **Kariyar Store API** — Yana toshe hare-haren bot a kan `/wp-json/wc/store/v1/checkout` da ke kewaye captchas na fom na gargajiya
* **Ya Dace da PayPal Payments** — Yana aiki ba tare da matsala ba da WooCommerce PayPal Payments
* **Kididdiga na Lokaci na Gaskiya** — Gani daidai adadin hare-haren da kake toshewa

## Har Yanzu Kana Bukatar Google reCAPTCHA ko hCaptcha? {#still-need-google-recaptcha-or-hcaptcha}

Sauya tsakanin providers a kowane lokaci daga saitunan hanyar sadarwarka.

* Saitunan dukan hanyar sadarwa tare da overrides na kowane shafi

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Loda `ultimate-multisite-captcha` zuwa directory ɗinka na `/wp-content/plugins/`
2. Network Activate plugin ɗin
3. **Shi ke nan. Kana da kariya.**

Cap Captcha yana kunnawa ta atomatik—babu saitunan da za a daidaita, babu API keys da za a shigar. Kowane endpoint da ake goyon baya a duk hanyar sadarwarka yana da kariya da zarar ka kunna.

**Na zaɓi:** Don amfani da Google reCAPTCHA ko hCaptcha maimakon haka, je zuwa **Ultimate Multisite → Settings → Captcha** kuma zaɓi provider ɗin da ka fi so.

## Iyakance Yawan Buƙatu {#rate-limiting}

Ultimate Multisite: Captcha v1.5.0 yana gabatar da rate limiter mai dakatarwa gaba ɗaya wanda ke kare hanyar sadarwarka daga hare-haren brute-force da card-testing.

### Yadda Yake Aiki {#how-it-works}

Rate limiter yana ƙirga kowane GET da POST request a kan wuraren da captcha ke karewa:

* **WordPress login endpoints** — wp-login, register, lost-password, comments
* **WooCommerce checkout** — my-account, checkout, pay-for-order
* **Ultimate Multisite** — checkout, inline-login

Lokacin da baƙo ya wuce rate limit, plugin ɗin yana amsawa da:

* **HTTP 429 (Too Many Requests)** status code
* **Retry-After** header da ke nuna lokacin sake gwadawa
* **Randomised tarpit sleep** (daƙiƙa 1–5 ta tsohuwa, an ƙuntata da ƙarfi zuwa daƙiƙa 15) don rage saurin maharan

### Configuration {#configuration}

#### Lokacin Tarpit {#tarpit-timing}

Sarrafa jinkirin randomised da ake amfani da shi ga requests da aka rate-limited:

* **`cap_rate_limit_tarpit_min`** — Mafi ƙarancin lokacin sleep a daƙiƙa (tsohuwa: 1)
* **`cap_rate_limit_tarpit_max`** — Mafi tsawon lokacin sleep a daƙiƙa (tsohuwa: 5, an ƙuntata da ƙarfi zuwa 15)

Ana samun waɗannan saitunan a **Ultimate Multisite → Settings → Captcha**.

### Faɗaɗawa {#extensibility}

#### IP Whitelist Filter {#ip-whitelist-filter}

Keɓe amintattun zangon IP daga rate limiting:

```php
add_filter( 'wu_cap_rate_limit_whitelist_ip', function( $is_whitelisted, $ip ) {
    // Whitelist your monitoring service
    if ( $ip === '203.0.113.42' ) {
        return true;
    }
    return $is_whitelisted;
}, 10, 2 );
```

#### Pre-Block Action {#pre-block-action}

Gudanar da custom logic nan take kafin a aika da rate-limit block:

```php
add_action( 'wu_cap_rate_limit_will_block', function( $ip, $surface ) {
    // Log the block, notify admins, or update custom metrics
    error_log( "Rate limit triggered for $ip on $surface" );
}, 10, 2 );
```

## Gano IP na Client & Amintattun Proxies {#client-ip-detection--trusted-proxies}

Ultimate Multisite: Captcha v1.5.0 yana gabatar da gano client-IP mai jure spoofing. Hanyar `Captcha_Core::get_client_ip()` yanzu ita ce tushen gaskiya guda ɗaya don danganta IP na baƙo a duk:

* Maɓallan bucket na rate-limit
* Mai samar da Captcha `remoteip` (reCAPTCHA, hCaptcha `siteverify`)
* Hashes na IP na ƙididdiga

### Samfurin Amincewa {#trust-model}

Plugin ɗin yana tilasta tsayayyen tsarin matakan amincewar IP:

1. **REMOTE_ADDR** — Ana amincewa da shi koyaushe (IP na abokin haɗin kai tsaye)
2. **CF-Connecting-IP** — Ana girmama shi ne kawai idan:
   - Abokin haɗin kai tsaye yana cikin kewayon IP na Cloudflare na yanzu
   - An kunna `cap_trust_cloudflare_headers` (tsoho: A KASHE)
3. **X-Forwarded-For** — Ana girmama shi ne kawai idan:
   - Abokin haɗin kai tsaye yana cikin jerin trusted-proxy da admin ɗinka ya saita
   - Ana karanta header ɗin daga dama zuwa hagu, ana tsallake hops na trusted/Cloudflare

### Saiti {#configuration-1}

#### Amincewar Header na Cloudflare {#cloudflare-header-trust}

Kunna amincewa da header na Cloudflare `CF-Connecting-IP`:

* **`cap_trust_cloudflare_headers`** — Saita zuwa ON don zaɓar shiga amincewar `CF-Connecting-IP`
  - Tsoho: OFF (a kashe)
  - Plugin ɗin yana zuwa da snapshot na Cloudflare CIDR a haɗe
  - Ana sabunta kewayon CIDR kowane mako ta wp-cron
  - Ana amfani da fallback ɗin da aka haɗa idan sabuntawar ta gaza

#### Saitin Trusted Proxy {#trusted-proxy-configuration}

Ƙayyade proxies da load-balancers na gaba-gaba:

* **`cap_trusted_proxies`** — Textarea na CIDRs ko IPs tsirara (ɗaya a kowane layi)
  - Ana yarda da sharhi (layukan da ke farawa da `#`)
  - Ba tare da wannan saitin ba, ana watsi da `X-Forwarded-For` ko da an kunna rate limiter
  - Misali:
    ```
    # Our load balancer
    192.0.2.0/24

    # Backup proxy
    198.51.100.50
    ```

#### Gano Kai-tsaye Lokacin Kunna Farko {#first-enable-auto-detection}

Lokacin da ka fara kunna rate limiter, plugin ɗin yana gano yiwuwar matsayin Cloudflare da proxy ɗinka sannan ya nuna sanarwar admin ta danna-ɗaya "Aiwatar da saitunan da aka gano".

* Plugin ɗin **ba ya taɓa sake rubutawa** ƙimomin da ka adana
* Idan zirga-zirgar baya ta nuna cewa config ɗinka ba ya ƙara dacewa da gaskiya (misali, Cloudflare ya canza kewayon CIDR), sanarwar rashin daidaito da ba za a iya rufewa ba tana fito da sabuntawar da aka ba da shawara

### Me Ya Sa Wannan Ke Da Muhimmanci {#why-this-matters}

Tsayayyen samfurin amincewar IP yana rufe muhimmin hanyar yaudara: buƙatun uwar-garken asali kai tsaye da ke ɗauke da ƙirƙirarren header `CF-Connecting-IP` a baya da an saka su cikin bucket ta IP ɗin da aka ƙirƙira maimakon ainihin abokin haɗin. Wannan yana da muhimmanci musamman ga:

* **Rate limiting** — Mahara ba za su iya kauce wa iyakoki ta hanyar ƙirƙirar IPs ba
* **Ƙididdiga** — Ma'aunin harinka yana nuna ainihin IPs na baƙi, ba na ƙirƙira ba
* **Tabbatar da Captcha** — Masu samarwa suna karɓar madaidaicin IP na baƙo don kimanta haɗari
