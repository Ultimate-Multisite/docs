# Material WP {#material-wp}

Mu’amalar gudanarwa ta WordPress ta kasance iri ɗaya tsawon shekaru. Amma ka taɓa tunanin yadda sigar Material Design ta mu’amalar gudanarwa za ta kasance?

Material WP jigon gudanarwa ne da aka haɓaka don ba ka wannan kamanni da ji, yana taimaka maka wajen ba abokan cinikinka Dashboard da aka fi keɓancewa.

## Fasali {#features}

- Tallafin Custom CSS
- Saitunan salo masu sauƙi
- Sake tsara menu
- Shigo da/fitarwa cikin sauƙi

## Takardu {#documentation}

**Keɓance Panel ɗin Gudanarwarka da Material WP**

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Loda 'material-wp' zuwa kundin '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Kunna karin ta cikin menu na 'Kari' a WordPress
