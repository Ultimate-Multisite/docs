# Tarihin canje-canje

## 1.18.0 — An fitar a 2026-06-29

### Sabbi

- **Kayan aikin Google Calendar** — karanta kalandodi da abubuwan da aka saita domin sarrafa-aiki masu la’akari da jadawali
- **Taswirar lamba da mataimakan mahalarta** — daidaita mahalartan kalanda da masu amfani da shafin da lambobi
- **Kofofin amincewar ɗan Adam da bayanan tunatarwa** — dakatar da sarrafa-aiki don dubawa kuma a guji sanarwa masu maimaituwa
- **Mai samar da SMS na TextBee** — aika sanarwar saƙonnin rubutu da aka saita ta hanyar TextBee
- **Kunshin abokin aiki na gaba** — ƙara kayan aikin tsarin fayil na amintaccen mai haɓakawa, bayanai, WP-CLI, mai rarraba REST, mai gina plugin, hoton git, sarrafa masu amfani, da gwaji waɗanda ake rarrabawa daban da ginin WordPress.org

### An inganta

- **Saitin sabis na Managed Superdav** — ƙara endpoints na sabis ɗin da aka karɓi bakunci da tanadin haɗi ta atomatik ga shafukan da ake tallafawa
- **Kunshin fitarwa** — gina ZIPs na core da Advanced daban, buga duka a GitHub, kuma aika kunshin core kaɗai zuwa WordPress.org

### An gyara

- **Dogaron buƙatar AI** — inganta zaɓin samfur, lokacin ƙarewar buƙata, samfurin saiti na asali, sarrafa rubutun tunani, da jagorar sake gwadawa don kiran kayan aiki marasa inganci
- **Ƙarfafa kalanda da tunatarwa** — ƙarfafa tokens na Google Calendar da cire maimaituwar tunatarwa
- **Farawa da ci gaba bayan amincewa** — gyara ƙaddamarwar farawa ta frontend da ci gaban ikon da aka tabbatar
- **Matsalolin bitar kunshin WordPress.org** — magance ra’ayoyin bitar kunshi don fitarwar core

## 1.16.0 — An fitar a 2026-05-20

### Sabbi

- **Ikon samar da Logo SVG** — Theme Builder yanzu na iya samarwa da saka SVGs na tambarin musamman tare da tsabtacewa mai aminci ga namespace
- **Loda hoto a hirar gano bayanai** — hirar gano bayanai ta Theme Builder yanzu ta haɗa da matakin loda hoto don ƙarin mahallin ƙira
- **Ikon Tabbatar da Bambancin Palette** — duba ma’auratan launi don bin ƙa’idar WCAG kafin a yi amfani da su a kan theme
- **Menu na baƙi** — Theme Builder yanzu na iya samar da shafukan menu na abinci da abin sha masu tsari don kasuwancin baƙi
- **Nunin samfoti na tebur da wayar hannu** — yi samfotin ƙirarka a na’urorin tebur da wayar hannu yayin zaɓin alkiblar ƙira
- **Parameter na alamar kewayawa** — ikon Create Menu yanzu yana tallafawa `navigation_label` daban daga taken shafi
- **Samuwar kayan aiki na Tier 1** — sd-ai-agent/site-scrape yanzu kayan aiki ne na Tier 1 da ke samuwa ta tsohuwa a cikin Theme Builder

### An gyara

- **Cache na AI Client** — yanzu transients ne ke tallafa masa don dorewa tsakanin buƙatu, yana hana asarar bayanai a kan ayyukan agent masu ɗaukar lokaci
- **Hanyoyin aikin layin plugin** — an gyara kuma an sake suna don ƙarin bayyana

## 1.10.0 — An fitar a 2026-05-05

### Sabbi

- **Binciken intanet na Tavily** — ƙara Tavily a matsayin mai samar da bincike don sakamakon binciken intanet masu wadata tare da Brave Search
- **Ƙwarewar ciki masu fahimtar theme** — jagororin ƙwarewar Block Themes, Classic Themes, Kadence Blocks, da Kadence Theme yanzu suna zuwa tare da plugin
- **Ikon fom ɗin tuntuɓa na mai gina shafi** — ƙara fom ɗin tuntuɓa zuwa kowane shafi kai tsaye daga mu’amalar hira

### An inganta

- **An sake fasalta haɗin WooCommerce** — yanzu yana amfani da APIs na asali na WooCommerce don ingantaccen dogaro da dacewa
- **Jerin masu samarwa yana sabuntawa ta atomatik** — lokacin da aka kunna ko aka kashe kowane plugin

### An gyara

- **ikon navigate-to** — an gyara madauwariyar sake lodi mara iyaka a wasu shafukan admin
- **ikon list-posts** — yanzu yana warware sunayen rukuni da tag daidai zuwa slugs
- **Umarnin WP-CLI** — an dawo da aliases na namespace da suka ɓace bayan sake fasalta na baya
- **Sarrafa-aikin abu** — yana kula da shafukan da ba a riga an ƙirƙiri teburan sarrafa-aiki ba cikin ladabi
- **ikon memory-save** — yanzu yana amfani da prefix na namespace daidai a cikin mai gina umarnin tsarin
- **Sakamakon kayan aiki na scalar** — yanzu ana nannade su daidai kafin a mayar da su ga AI
- **Ƙididdigar amfani** — yanzu suna kula da tsohon tsarin maɓallin iko daidai lokacin haɓakawa daga tsofaffin sigogi
