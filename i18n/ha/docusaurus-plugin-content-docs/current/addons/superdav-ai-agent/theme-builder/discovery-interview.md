# Hirar Gano {#discovery-interview}

**Hirar Gano** ita ce mataki na farko a tsarin zane na Theme Builder. Tana tattara bayanai game da shafinka, kasuwancinka, da abubuwan da ka fi so a zane domin ƙirƙirar theme na musamman.

## Bayani Gabaɗaya {#overview}

Hirar Gano tana jagorantarka ta jerin tambayoyi don fahimtar alamarka, masu sauraron da kake nufi, da manufofin zanenka. A sigar 1.16.0, hirar yanzu ta haɗa da **matakin loda hoto** wanda ke ba ka damar samar da wahayi na gani don zanenka.

## Matakan Hira {#interview-steps}

### 1. Bayanin Kasuwanci {#1-business-information}

- Sunan shafi da taken bayani
- Nau'in kasuwanci ko fanni
- Manyan manufofin kasuwanci
- Bayanin masu sauraro da ake nufi

### 2. Abubuwan da Aka Fi So a Zane {#2-design-preferences}

- Tsarin launuka da aka fi so
- Salon zane (na zamani, na gargajiya, mai sauƙi, na wasa, da sauransu)
- Shafukan yanar gizo ko alamomi na wahayi
- Abubuwan zane da dole a samu

### 3. Loda Hoto (Sabo a v1.16.0) {#3-photo-upload-new-in-v1160}

Loda hoto ɗaya ko fiye don tasiri kan alkiblar zane:

#### Tsarukan da Ake Karɓa {#accepted-formats}

| Tsari | Ƙarewa | Cikakkun bayanai |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| **JPEG** | `.jpg`, `.jpeg` | Ana ba da shawara don hotuna |
| **PNG** | `.png` | Yana goyon bayan bayyana bango |
| **WebP** | `.webp` | Tsari na zamani, ƙaramin girman fayil |
| **GIF** | `.gif` | Hotuna marasa motsi kawai (babu motsi) |

#### Bukatun Fayil {#file-requirements}

- **Matsakaicin girman fayil:** 10 MB
- **Ƙananan girma:** 400 × 300 pixels
- **Girman da aka ba da shawara:** 1200 × 800 pixels ko fiye
- **Rabon gefe:** Kowane rabo yana da karɓuwa

#### Yadda Hotuna ke Tasiri kan Alkiblar Zane {#how-photos-influence-design-direction}

Hotunan da ka loda suna taimaka wa Theme Builder fahimtar:

- **Palette na launuka** — ana fitar da manyan launuka kuma a yi amfani da su a cikin theme
- **Salon gani** — na zamani, na kauye, mai sauƙi, mai ƙayatarwa, da sauransu
- **Yanayi da muhallin ji** — na ƙwararru, na ƙirƙira, na abokantaka, na alfarma, da sauransu
- **Abubuwan zane** — tsaruka, laushi, da salon tsara abubuwa
- **Wahayin rubutu** — salon font da ke dacewa da theme na gani

### 4. Tsarin Abun Ciki {#4-content-structure}

- Manyan shafuka da ake buƙata (Gida, Game da Mu, Ayyuka, Tuntuɓa, da sauransu)
- Ana buƙatar sashen blog ko labarai
- Ana buƙatar aikin kasuwancin yanar gizo
- Siffofi na musamman ko haɗe-haɗe

### 5. Bita da Tabbatarwa {#5-review-and-confirmation}

- Taƙaitaccen duk bayanan da aka bayar
- Tabbatarwa kafin fara samar da zane

## Mafi Kyawun Hanyoyin Loda Hoto {#photo-upload-best-practices}

### Abin da Za a Loda {#what-to-upload}

- **Wahayin alama** — logos, samfuran launi, ko ƙa'idodin alama
- **Shafukan masu gasa** — hotunan allo na shafukan da kake so
- **Allunan yanayi** — tarin hotuna da ke wakiltar kyawun da kake so
- **Hotunan samfur** — idan kana da samfura da za a nuna
- **Hotunan ƙungiya ko ofis** — don gina alamar mutum ko ta ƙwararru
- **Hotunan salon rayuwa** — masu wakiltar masu sauraron da kake nufi

### Abin da Za a Guje wa {#what-to-avoid}

- **Hotuna marasa inganci** — hotuna masu duhu ko masu ɓacewar pixels
- **Hotunan da ba su da alaƙa** — hotunan da ba sa wakiltar alamarka
- **Hotuna masu yawan rubutu** — hotunan allo da ke da rubutu da yawa
- **Kayan da ke da haƙƙin mallaka** — tabbatar kana da haƙƙin amfani da hotunan
- **GIFs masu motsi** — hotuna marasa motsi kawai ake sarrafawa

### Shawarwari don Samun Sakamako Mafi Kyau {#tips-for-best-results}

1. **Loda hotuna 2-3** — hotuna da yawa suna samar da mahalli mafi kyau
2. **Yi amfani da hotuna masu inganci** — hotuna masu haske, masu kyakkyawan haske ne suka fi aiki
3. **Haɗa bambance-bambance** — cakuda launi, salo, da batun hoto
4. **Samar da mahalli** — bayyana a taƙaice dalilin da ya sa ka zaɓi kowane hoto
5. **Ka zama takamaimai** — idan kana son wani abu na musamman, nuna shi

## Sakamakon Alkiblar Zane {#design-direction-output}

Bayan Hirar Gano, Theme Builder yana samar da:

- **Palette na launuka** — manyan launuka, na biyu, da launukan ƙarfafawa
- **Typography** — dangin font don kanun magana da rubutun jiki
- **Salon shimfiɗa** — tazara, daidaitawa, da tsarin sassa
- **Abubuwan gani** — gumaka, tsaruka, da abubuwan ado
- **Allon yanayi** — wakilcin gani na alkiblar zane

## Matakai na Gaba {#next-steps}

Bayan kammala Hirar Gano:

1. Yi bitar alkiblar zane da aka samar
2. Ba da ra'ayi ko nemi gyare-gyare
3. Ci gaba zuwa matakin [Alkiblar Zane](./design-direction.md)
4. Theme Builder yana ƙirƙirar theme ɗinka na musamman
