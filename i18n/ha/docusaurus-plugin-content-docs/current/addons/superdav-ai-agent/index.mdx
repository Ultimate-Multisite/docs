# Superdav AI Agent {#superdav-ai-agent}

Superdav AI Agent mataimakin AI na WordPress ne mai ci gaba wanda ke faɗaɗa Gratis AI Agent da ƙwarewar da ke fahimtar jigo, ingantattun damar binciken intanet, fasalolin gina shafi, da haɗaɗɗun sarrafa-aiki na zaɓi. Sigar 1.18.0 tana ƙara kayan aikin karanta Google Calendar, daidaita lambobin tuntuɓa da mahalarta, ƙofofin amincewar mutum, kawar da maimaita tunatarwa, sanarwar SMS ta TextBee, samar da sabis na Superdav da ake sarrafawa, da kunshin abokin rakiyar Advanced da ake rarrabawa dabam don hanyoyin aikin masu haɓakawa amintattu.

## Muhimman Fasali {#key-features}

- **Ƙwarewar da ke fahimtar jigo** — yana daidaita jagora da damar aiki ta atomatik bisa jigon WordPress da kake amfani da shi da ƙarin-shirye da aka girka
- **Masu samar da binciken intanet da yawa** — zaɓi tsakanin Brave Search da Tavily don sakamakon bincike mai wadata da damar bincike mai zurfi
- **Fom ɗin tuntuɓa na mai gina shafi** — ƙirƙiri fom ɗin tuntuɓa masu cikakken aiki kai tsaye daga mahaɗin tattaunawa ta amfani da ƙarin-shirin fom da ka girka
- **Ingantaccen mai gina shafi** — ingantattun tsare-tsaren gina shafi masu matakai da yawa tare da mafi kyawun gano ƙarin-shirye da farfaɗowa daga kuskure
- **Ƙwarewar jigo da aka gina a ciki** — jagora na musamman don Block Themes, Classic Themes, Kadence Blocks, da Kadence Theme
- **Ingantaccen haɗin WooCommerce** — an sake fasalta shi don amfani da API na asali na WooCommerce don mafi kyawun dogaro da dacewa
- **Gano mai samarwa ta atomatik** — yana gano da sabunta ƙarin-shirye da jigogi da ake da su ta atomatik lokacin da aka kunna su ko aka kashe su
- **Ƙirƙirar tambari na musamman** — ƙirƙiri da saka tambarin SVG na musamman tare da tsabtace namespace-safe ta atomatik
- **Tabbatar da bambancin palette** — duba ma'auratan launi don bin ƙa'idar samun dama ta WCAG kafin amfani da su a jigonka
- **Ganowa da hotuna suka inganta** — loda hotuna yayin hirar ganowa ta Theme Builder don tasiri kan alkiblar ƙira
- **Menu na masauki** — ƙirƙiri shafukan menu na abinci da abin sha masu tsari don gidajen abinci, kafet, da kasuwancin masauki
- **Samfotin kwamfuta da wayar hannu** — duba samfotin ƙirarka a kan kwamfuta da na'urorin hannu yayin zaɓen alkiblar ƙira
- **Kwakwafon shafi na Mataki 1** — sd-ai-agent/site-scrape yanzu yana samuwa ta tsohuwa don nazarin shafukan yanar gizon masu gasa da na wahayi
- **Kayan aikin karanta Google Calendar** — karanta kalandoji da abubuwan da aka saita don sarrafa-aiki su iya fahimtar mahallin jadawalin da ke tafe
- **Daidaita lambobin tuntuɓa da mahalarta** — daidaita mahalarta kalanda da sanannun masu amfani da shafi da lambobin tuntuɓa kafin ƙirƙirar aikin bibiyar baya
- **Ƙofofin amincewar mutum** — dakatar da sarrafa-aiki masu muhimmanci don bita kafin aika saƙonni, ci gaba da aiki, ko yin canje-canje
- **Bayanan tunatarwa da kawar da maimaituwa** — bibiyi tunatarwar da aka riga aka aika don gudanawar sarrafa-aiki da aka maimaita kada ta sanar da mai karɓa ɗaya sau biyu
- **Sanarwar SMS ta TextBee** — aika sanarwar saƙon rubutu da aka saita ta TextBee lokacin da aka kunna mai samar da SMS
- **Samar da sabis na Superdav da ake sarrafawa** — haɗa shafuka masu tallafi zuwa endpoints na Superdav da aka karɓa tare da saitin atomatik maimakon daidaita endpoint da hannu
- **Kunshin abokin rakiyar Advanced** — kayan aikin amintattun masu haɓakawa da ake rarrabawa dabam don tsarin fayil, database, WP-CLI, REST dispatcher, mai gina ƙarin-shiri, git snapshot, sarrafa masu amfani, da hanyoyin aikin benchmark

## Bukatu {#requirements}

- WordPress 6.0 ko sama
- PHP 8.1 ko sama
- WordPress Multisite (an kunna a kan network) ko girkin shafi ɗaya
- Ƙarin-shirin haɗin mai samar da AI mai aiki (Anthropic, OpenAI, ko mai dacewa)
- Gratis AI Agent v1.9.0 ko sama (Superdav AI Agent yana faɗaɗa Gratis AI Agent)

## Girka {#installation}

1. Loda fayilolin ƙarin-shirin zuwa `/wp-content/plugins/superdav-ai-agent`
2. Kunna ƙarin-shirin ta fuskar **Ƙarin-shirye** a WordPress
3. Tabbatar Gratis AI Agent ma an kunna shi
4. Je zuwa **Gratis AI Agent → Saituna** kuma saita mai samar da binciken intanet ɗinka, haɗin sabis na Superdav da ake sarrafawa, Google Calendar, ko shaidun TextBee kamar yadda ake buƙata

Kunshin WordPress.org yana ƙunshe da manyan fasalolin Superdav AI Agent. Ana gina kunshin abokin rakiyar Advanced kuma a rarraba shi dabam don muhallai inda aka girka kuma aka ba da izini ga kayan aikin masu haɓakawa amintattu a sarari.

## Farawa Cikin Sauri {#quick-start}

Da zarar an kunna shi, Superdav AI Agent yana inganta girkin Gratis AI Agent ɗinka ta atomatik da ƙwarewar da ke fahimtar jigo da ƙarin damar aiki. Buɗe allon **Gratis AI Agent** a sandar gefe ta admin na WordPress kuma fara amfani da sabbin fasalolin.

**Misalan umarni:**

```

```

```

```

```
Ƙirƙiri sashen hero ta amfani da block patterns na jigon da ke aiki.
```

```
Duba abubuwan Google Calendar da aka saita na gobe kuma tsara tunatarwa ga mahalarta.
```

```
Aika tunatarwar SMS bayan na amince da saƙon.
```

## Ƙwarewar da Ke Fahimtar Jigo {#theme-aware-skills}

Superdav AI Agent yana gano jigon da kake amfani da shi da ƙarin-shirye da aka girka ta atomatik, yana loda ƙwarewar da ta dace da jigo zuwa mahallin mataimakin. Ƙwarewar da ake da ita ta haɗa da:

| Ƙwarewa | Yana Aiki Ga |
|---|---|
| **Block Themes** | Jigogi da ke amfani da tsarin WordPress na block-based (Full Site Editing) |
| **Classic Themes** | Jigogin WordPress na gargajiya da ke amfani da samfuran PHP da editan gargajiya |
| **Kadence Blocks** | Shafuka da ke da ƙarin-shirin Kadence Blocks a kunne |
| **Kadence Theme** | Shafuka da ke da jigon Kadence a matsayin jigon da ke aiki |

Duba [Ƙwarewar da Ke Fahimtar Jigo](../../user-guide/configuration/theme-aware-skills) don cikakken jagora kan kowace ƙwarewa.

## Masu Samar da Binciken Intanet {#internet-search-providers}

Superdav AI Agent v1.10.0 yana ƙara **Tavily** a matsayin madadin Brave Search don tambayoyin binciken intanet. Duk masu samarwa biyu suna bayar da sakamakon bincike na lokacin gaske kuma ana iya saita su a **Gratis AI Agent → Settings → Advanced**.

| Mai bayarwa | Fasali | Saitawa |
|---|---|---|
| **Brave Search** | Sakamakon bincike masu sauri, masu mayar da hankali kan sirri | [Brave Search API](https://brave.com/search/api/) |
| **Tavily** | Sakamakon bincike mafi wadatuwa, damar bincike na ci gaba | [Tavily API](https://tavily.com/) |

Duba [Binciken Intanet](../../user-guide/configuration/internet-search) don umarnin daidaitawa.

## Gudanarwar Kalanda, Amincewa, da Sanarwa {#calendar-approval-and-notification-workflows}

Superdav AI Agent v1.18.0 yana faɗaɗa ayyukan atomatik fiye da ayyukan abun ciki da gina shafi:

- **Kayan aikin Google Calendar** suna ba agent damar karanta kalandodin da aka daidaita da bayanan abubuwa idan bayanan shiga kalanda suna nan.
- **Masu taimako na tuntuɓa da mahalarta** suna daidaita mahalarta abu da masu amfani na WordPress ko sanannun lambobin tuntuɓa kafin shirya tunatarwa ko ayyukan bibiya.
- **Ƙofofin amincewar mutum** suna ba atomatik damar tsayawa har sai mai amfani mai izini ya duba aikin da aka ba da shawara.
- **Bayanan tunatarwa** suna hana maimaita tunatarwar kalanda ko sanarwa idan an sake gwada atomatik ko an tsara shi akai-akai.
- **Tallafin TextBee SMS** yana ba da damar sanarwar saƙon rubutu idan an daidaita mai bayarwa na TextBee.

Yi amfani da waɗannan kayan aikin don taimakon da ya san jadawali, bin diddigin mahalarta, da ayyukan tunatarwa waɗanda har yanzu ke buƙatar duba mutum kafin a aika saƙonni masu muhimmanci.

## Daidaitawa {#configuration}

Duk saituna suna ƙarƙashin **Gratis AI Agent → Settings** a cikin admin na WordPress. Superdav AI Agent yana ƙara zaɓuɓɓukan daidaitawa masu zuwa:

| Saiti | Bayani |
|---|---|
| **Mai Bayar da Binciken Intanet** | Zaɓi tsakanin Brave Search da Tavily (na zaɓi) |
| **Tavily API Key** | API key don binciken intanet na Tavily (idan an zaɓi Tavily) |
| **Sabis na Superdav da Aka Sarrafa** | Haɗa shafukan da ake tallafawa zuwa sabis ɗin Superdav da aka karɓi baƙunci tare da samar da endpoint ta atomatik |
| **Bayanan Shiga na Google Calendar** | Daidaita damar shiga kalanda domin agent ya iya karanta kalandodi da abubuwa |
| **Mai Bayar da TextBee SMS** | Daidaita bayanan shiga na TextBee don ayyukan sanarwar SMS da aka amince da su |

## Kunshin Aboki na Ci Gaba {#advanced-companion-package}

Superdav AI Agent v1.18.0 yana ƙara kunshin aboki na Advanced da ake rarrabawa dabam don ayyukan masu haɓakawa amintattu. Waɗannan damar ba a haɗa su cikin kunshin ainihi na WordPress.org ba. Girkawa kunshin aboki kawai a kan shafukan da masu gudanarwa suka amince a fili agent ya samu damar kayan aikin haɓakawa.

Kayan aikin ci gaba sun haɗa da ayyukan tsarin fayil, damar bayanan ajiya, WP-CLI, gudanarwar REST dispatcher, kayan amfani na mai gina plugin, hotunan git, masu taimako na sarrafa masu amfani, da kayan aikin gwajin aiki. Ka iyakance waɗannan kayan aikin ga amintattun masu gudanarwa kuma ka duba daidaitawar ƙofar amincewa kafin kunna su a kan shafukan samarwa.
