# Mailchimp {#mailchimp}

Multisite Ultimate: Haɗakar Mailchimp

Haɗa hanyar sadarwar Ultimate Multisite ɗinka da Mailchimp ɗinka. Za ka iya haɗa wasu tsare-tsare da Mailchimp Lists sannan a ƙara masu biyan rajista kai tsaye zuwa waɗannan jerin lokacin yin rajista.

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Loda 'ultimate-multisite-mailchimp' zuwa kundin '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Kunna plugin ta menu na 'Plugins' a cikin WordPress Network Admin ɗinka

## Jagorar Amfani {#usage-guide}

Daidaita imel na masu biyan rajista da jerin MailChimp ɗinka yana da sauƙi sosai da Ultimate Multisite: MailChimp Integration Add-on ɗinmu. Wannan koyarwar tana nufin taimaka maka ka sa komai ya shirya!

## Samun API key {#getting-an-api-key}

Kafin mu fara shiga saituna daban-daban da ake da su, muna buƙatar MailChimp API key. Wannan zai ba Ultimate Multisite damar magana da MailChimp API don dawo da jerin, ƙungiyoyi, da ƙara adiresoshin imel zuwa waɗannan jerin da ƙungiyoyi lokacin da masu amfani suka yi rajista.

Bi wannan koyarwar don samun MailChimp API key ɗinka: [Game da API Keys | MailChimp](https://kb.mailchimp.com/integrations/api-integrations/about-api-keys)

<!-- Hoton allo babu: saitunan MailChimp API key a cikin Ultimate Multisite Add-on Settings -->

_Ƙara MailChimp API key ɗinka a_ _**Ultimate Multisite Settings - > Add-on Settings -> MailChimp**_

## Yanayin Haɗakawa {#integration-mode}

MailChimp Integration add-on yana bayar da “yanayin haɗakawa” guda biyu, wanda ke nufin akwai hanyoyi biyu daban-daban da za ka iya amfani da add-on ɗin don daidaita adiresoshin imel.

<!-- Hoton allo babu: zaɓin yanayin haɗakar MailChimp tare da zaɓuɓɓukan Multiple Lists da Single List -->

_Yanayin haɗakawa guda biyu don ƙarin sassauci!_

### Yanayi 1: Multiple Lists {#mode-1-multiple-lists}

Yanayin tsoho yana amfani da **Multiple Lists**. Wannan yanayin zai ba ka damar zaɓar jerin MailChimp da yawa ga kowanne daga cikin tsare-tsaren. Wannan shi ne mafi kyawun zaɓi idan kana raba masu amfani ɗinka zuwa jerin daban-daban.

Misali, bari mu ɗauka kana da jerin daban-daban 3 a Account ɗinka na MailChimp: **Newsletter** , **Plan A** , da **Plan B**. Kana son duk masu amfani, ba tare da la’akari da tsarinsu ba, su ma su kasance a cikin jerin Newsletter, don kada su rasa sabuntawarka.

Don cimma hakan, za ka iya amfani da yanayin Multiple Lists don ƙara jerin **Newsletter** da **Plan A** zuwa Plan A MailChimp Settings, da **Newsletter** da **Plan B** zuwa Plan B MailChimp Settings.

<!-- Hoton allo babu: zaɓin jerin tsoho na MailChimp don sababbin account da account da aka soke -->

_Zaɓi jerin tsoho don sababbin account da kuma account da aka soke_

### Yanayi 2: Single List + Multiple Groups {#mode-2-single-list--multiple-groups}

MailChimp yana ba ka damar raba jerinka zuwa ƙananan ƙungiyoyin masu amfani da ake kira **Groups**. Wannan yana ba ka ƙarin iko saboda za ka iya aika kamfen zuwa wani takamaiman rukuni na masu biyan rajistarka ba tare da ka ƙirƙiri kwafi da yawa na kamfen ɗaya a MailChimp ba (wanda ke sa nazarin ma’aunan wannan kamfen daga baya ya zama da wahala).

Misali, za ka iya samun jerin guda ɗaya da ake kira **My Network Subscribers,** kuma a cikinsa akwai ƙungiyoyi don **Plan A** da **Plan B**.

<!-- Hoton allo babu: zaɓin jerin guda ɗaya da ƙungiyoyi na MailChimp don jerin tsoho -->

_Zaɓi jerin tsoho da ƙungiyoyi, idan kana amfani da yanayin haɗakawa na “single list + multiple groups”_

## Jerin Tsoho da Ƙungiyoyi {#default-lists-and-groups}

Bayan ka zaɓi wane yanayin haɗakawa ya fi dacewa da takamaiman yanayin amfaninka, lokaci ya yi da za ka zaɓi jerin tsoho (da ƙungiyoyi, gwargwadon yanayin). Za a yi amfani da jerin tsoho lokacin da wani tsari bai da takamaiman List da aka zaɓa.

Hakanan za ka iya zaɓar jerin tsoho don account da aka soke. Duk lokacin da mai amfani ya ƙare subscription ɗinsa kuma ya cire account ɗinsa daga hanyar sadarwarka, za a motsa adireshin imel ɗinsa zuwa jerin da aka saita don account da aka soke.

## Kunna Double Subscription {#enabling-double-subscription}

A kan shafin saituna ɗaya, za ka iya kunna **Double Subscription**. Wannan zaɓin zai ƙara mai amfani zuwa jerin tsoho da aka zaɓa DA kuma jerin da aka zaɓa ga tsarin wannan mai amfani. Idan ba a kunna wannan zaɓin ba, za a ƙara mai amfani ne kawai zuwa jerin da aka zaɓa don tsarin mai amfani. Idan babu wani da aka saita don wannan takamaiman tsari, to za a ƙara mai amfani zuwa jerin tsoho da aka zaɓa.

<!-- Hoton allo babu: saitin sauyawa na MailChimp double subscription -->

_Za ka iya sa masu amfani ɗinka su yi subscription zuwa jerin tsoho da jerin tsarin a lokacin yin rajista_

## Zaɓar Jerin da Ƙungiyoyi ga kowanne daga cikin Tsare-tsaren {#selecting-lists-and-groups-for-each-of-the-plans}

Je zuwa **Plans** ɗinka sannan ka zaɓi tsarin da kake son gyarawa. Ka lura cewa a kan meta-box na **Advanced Options**, sabon tab mai suna MailChimp zai kasance.

A kan wannan tab, za ka iya zaɓar jerin da yawa ko jerin guda ɗaya da ƙungiyoyi da yawa (gwargwadon yanayin haɗakawa) don wannan tsari.

<!-- Hoton allo babu: tab na MailChimp a shafin gyaran tsari yana nuna zaɓin jerin da ƙungiyoyi -->

_Zaɓi Lists da Groups da kake son amfani da su ga kowanne daga cikin tsare-tsarenka_

Lokacin da sabon mai amfani ya yi rajista don wannan tsari, za a ƙara adireshin imel ɗinsa zuwa jerin da ƙungiyoyin da aka zaɓa!
