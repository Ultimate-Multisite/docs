# Mailster

Mayar da cibiyar sadarwarka ta multisite ta zama na'urar gina jerin imel mai ƙarfi tare da ƙarin Haɗin Mailster na Ultimate Multisite. Ƙara sababbin abokan ciniki kai tsaye zuwa jerin imel ɗinka na Mailster lokacin da suka yi rajista ko suka yi sayayya, yana ƙirƙirar haɗi mara tangarɗa tsakanin dandalin membobinka da dabarun tallan imel.

## Manyan Fasali {#key-features}

**Daidaita Masu Rajista Kai Tsaye**
* Ƙara abokan ciniki kai tsaye zuwa jerin Mailster lokacin da suka yi rajista
* Zaɓi a daidaita nan take bayan rajista ko a jira tabbatar da biyan kuɗi
* Ci gaba da sabunta jerin imel ɗinka ba tare da tsoma bakin hannu ba

**Sarrafa Jerin da Sassauci**
* Saita tsoffin jerin gama-gari ga duk sababbin abokan ciniki
* Sauya tsoffin saituna da jerin da suka dace da samfur don kamfen da aka nufa
* Sarrafa rarraba jerin cikin sauƙi da sanannen mu'amalar Mailster

**Zaɓuɓɓukan Shiga da Yarda Masu Bin GDPR**
* Zaɓi tsakanin rajista kai tsaye ko yarda ta hanyar akwatin zaɓi
* Kunna shiga da yarda sau biyu don rajistoci da aka tabbatar
* Nuna/ɓoye akwatin zaɓin shiga da yarda bisa ga bukatun bin ƙa'idojinka

**Taswirar Bayanai Mai Kaifin Basi**
* Daidaita sunayen abokan ciniki da bayanan tuntuɓa kai tsaye
* Daidaita adiresoshin biyan kuɗi zuwa filayen masu rajista
* Bibiyi ranakun rajista don kamfen da aka nufa

**Ikon Matakin Samfur**
* Sanya jerin daban-daban ga samfurori daban-daban
* Ƙirƙiri kamfen da aka nufa bisa ga abin da abokan ciniki suka saya
* Kashe Mailster ga takamaiman samfurori lokacin da ake buƙata

**Ƙwarewa Ta Ƙwararru**
* Haɗin UI na Mailster na asali don zaɓin jerin
* Cikakken rubuta log don warware matsaloli da tabbatarwa
* Sabuntawa mara tangarɗa na masu rajista da suke akwai

## Ya Dace Da {#perfect-for}

* Masu rukunin membobi da ke gina jerin imel
* Dandamalin SaaS da ke son kula da sababbin abokan ciniki
* Masu ƙirƙirar kwasa-kwasai da ke raba ɗalibai bisa rajista
* Duk wanda ke gudanar da Ultimate Multisite tare da Mailster

## Yadda Yake Aiki {#how-it-works}

1. Shigar kuma kunna ƙarin a kan cibiyar sadarwarka ta Ultimate Multisite
2. Saita tsoffin jerin gama-gari a cikin saitunan Ultimate Multisite
3. Idan ana so, sauya su da jerin da suka dace da samfur
4. Zaɓi yanayin shiga da yarda da lokacin rajista
5. Kalli jerin Mailster ɗinka suna ƙaruwa kai tsaye!

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Loda fayilolin plugin zuwa kundin addons na Ultimate Multisite ɗinka
2. Tabbatar an shigar da Mailster kuma yana aiki a kan babban rukuninka
3. Kunna ƙarin ta shafin addons na Ultimate Multisite
4. Je zuwa Saituna > Haɗin Mailster don saiti
5. Zaɓi tsoffin jerinka kuma saita abubuwan fifikon shiga da yarda

## Tambayoyin da Ake Yawan Yi {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Shin wannan ƙarin yana aiki ba tare da Ultimate Multisite ba? {#does-this-addon-work-without-ultimate-multisite}

A'a, wannan ƙarin yana buƙatar Ultimate Multisite don ya yi aiki. An tsara shi musamman don haɗa Mailster da tsarin abokan ciniki da samfur na Ultimate Multisite.

## Shin ina buƙatar shigar da Mailster a duk rukunai? {#do-i-need-mailster-installed-on-all-sites}

A'a, Mailster yana buƙatar a shigar da shi kuma a kunna shi ne kawai a kan babban rukunin cibiyar sadarwarka. Ƙarin zai haɗa da shi kai tsaye daga duk ƙananan rukunai.

## Zan iya sanya jerin daban-daban ga samfurori daban-daban? {#can-i-assign-different-lists-to-different-products}

Eh! Za ka iya saita tsoffin jerin gama-gari, sannan ka sauya su bisa kowane samfur. Wannan yana ba ka damar ƙirƙirar kamfen na imel da aka nufa sosai bisa ga abin da abokan ciniki suka saya.

## Me zai faru idan abokin ciniki ya riga ya kasance a cikin Mailster? {#what-happens-if-a-customer-already-exists-in-mailster}

Ta tsohuwa, ƙarin zai sabunta masu rajista da suke akwai kuma ya ƙara su zuwa sababbin jerin. Za ka iya kashe wannan a cikin saituna idan ka fi son tsallake masu rajista da suke akwai.

## Shin wannan yana bin GDPR? {#is-this-gdpr-compliant}

Eh! Ƙarin ya haɗa da yanayin shiga da yarda ta akwatin zaɓi inda dole ne abokan ciniki su bayyana yardarsu sarai don karɓar imel. Wannan yana cika bukatun GDPR idan aka haɗa shi da manufofin sirri da suka dace.

## Zan iya amfani da shiga da yarda sau biyu? {#can-i-use-double-opt-in}

Tabbas! Kunna saitin shiga da yarda sau biyu kuma Mailster zai aika imel na tabbatarwa kafin kunna rajistoci.
