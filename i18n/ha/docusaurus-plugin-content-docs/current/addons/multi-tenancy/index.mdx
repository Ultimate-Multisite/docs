# Masu haya da yawa

Rarraba ƙananan shafuka a kan sabobin da databases don inganci da tsaro

## Muhimman abubuwan fitowar sigar 1.2.0 {#version-120-release-highlights}

Multi-Tenancy 1.2.0 yana ƙara tubalin tenant mai cin gashin kai don ayyukan warewa a samarwa:

- Autologin na tenant mara riƙe yanayi tare da ziyarar SSO masu gajeren rai da kariya daga sake kunnawa.
- Ware database da tsarin fayil ga kowane ƙaramin shafi don tenants masu cin gashin kai.
- Ƙwarewar Hostinger multi-tenancy don samar da tenant da ke la’akari da host.
- Ƙofofin tabbatar da ƙaura ta cin gashin kai don binciken tsohon bayanai da sarrafa turawa na async.
- Daidaitaccen ɗaure host na database a na’ura ɗaya don shigarwar Bedrock, FrankenPHP, da localhost.
- Ingantawa ga fara tenant, marubucin database, fara schema, da samar da masu amfani.
- Rushewar cin gashin kai mafi aminci da ke share bayanan shaidar tenant.

Duba jagororin mai amfani don [tsarin aikin ƙaura ta cin gashin kai](/user-guide/getting-started/sovereign-migration-workflow), [warewar multi-tenancy](/user-guide/configuration/multi-tenancy-isolation), da [ayyukan tenant mai cin gashin kai](/user-guide/administration/sovereign-tenant-operations).

## Bukatu {#requirements}

- WordPress 5.3 ko sama
- PHP 7.4 ko sama
- Multisite Ultimate plugin (a kunne)

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Loda fayilolin addon zuwa kundin adireshinka na `/wp-content/plugins/`
2. Kunna plugin ta menu na 'Plugins' a cikin WordPress
3. Daidaita saitunan addon a cikin admin na network

### Gwaji {#testing}

Addon ɗin ya haɗa da cikakken tsarin gwaji:

- **Gwaje-gwajen Unit**: Gwaje-gwaje masu tushen PHPUnit tare da tarin gwajin WordPress
- **Ajin Gwaji na Tushe**: Yana samar da hanyoyin taimako don yanayin gwaji na gama gari
- **Rufe Gwaji**: An daidaita shi don bin diddigin rufe code
- **Gwajin Multisite**: Gwaje-gwaje suna gudana a muhallin multisite

### Ƙa’idojin Code {#code-standards}

- **Ƙa’idojin Rubuta Code na WordPress**: Ana tilasta su ta PHPCS
- **Daidaituwa da PHP 7.4+**: Ana tallafa wa fasalolin PHP na zamani
- **Binciken Static**: Haɗin PHPStan don duba nau’i
- **Zamanantar da Code**: Dokokin Rector don haɓaka PHP

### Tsarin Gina {#build-system}

- **Sarrafa Kayayyaki**: Rage girman fayilolin CSS/JS
- **Fassara**: Samar da fayil ɗin POT
- **Kunshin Kaya**: Ƙirƙirar archive don rabawa
- **Yanayin Haɓakawa**: Kayayyaki marasa rage girma don gano matsala

## Daidaitawa {#configuration}

Addon ɗin yana tallafa wa zaɓuɓɓukan daidaitawa daban-daban da ake samu ta hanyar mu’amalar admin na network.

### Daidaitawar tenant mai cin gashin kai {#sovereign-tenant-configuration}

Don tenants masu cin gashin kai, daidaita database na tenant, tushen tsarin fayil, ɗaure host na database, da ƙwarewar host kafin tura zirga-zirga zuwa tenant. Gudanar da tabbatar da ƙaura bayan kowane canji da ke shafar bayanan shaidar database, ɗaure host, routing, ko samar da masu amfani.

Yi amfani da `localhost` kawai lokacin da runtime na tenant da izinin database ke buƙatar socket na gida ko samun dama a na’ura ɗaya. Yi amfani da `127.0.0.1` ko sunan host na sabis lokacin da shi ne grant host da mai samar da sabis ɗinka ya daidaita.

### Ƙwarewar Hostinger {#hostinger-capability}

Ƙwarewar Hostinger multi-tenancy tana aiki da token na haɗin Hostinger na bai ɗaya. Tabbatar cewa domain yana nan a hPanel, DNS yana nuni zuwa Account na Hostinger, kuma saitunan database na tenant sun dace da hPanel kafin tabbatarwa.

## Hooks da Filters {#hooks-and-filters}

### Actions {#actions}

- `ultimate-multisite-addon-template_init` - Ana ƙaddamarwa bayan fara addon
- `ultimate-multisite-addon-template_loaded` - Ana ƙaddamarwa bayan an loda duk fayilolin addon

### Filters {#filters}

- `ultimate-multisite-addon-template_settings` - Gyara saitunan addon
- `ultimate-multisite-addon-template_enabled` - Sauya matsayin kunna/kashe addon
