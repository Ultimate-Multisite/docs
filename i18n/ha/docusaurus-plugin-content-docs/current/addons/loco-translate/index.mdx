# Loco Translate

Ƙarin Ultimate Multisite: Loco Translate Integration yana kawo damar fassara ta musamman ga kowane shafi zuwa cibiyar sadarwar WordPress multisite ɗinka. Idan ka taɓa buƙatar shafuka daban-daban a cikin cibiyar sadarwarka su sami fassarori daban-daban na ƙarin manhaja ko jigo iri ɗaya, wannan ƙari shi ne cikakken mafita.

**Abin da Wannan Ƙari Yake Yi**

Wannan ƙari yana haɗa Ultimate Multisite cikin sauƙi da sanannen ƙarin manhaja na Loco Translate, yana ba kowane ƙaramin shafi a cikin cibiyar sadarwarka damar adana fassarorinsa na musamman. Ko kana gudanar da dandali na yankuna da yawa, kana hidimtawa al'ummomin harsuna daban-daban, ko kawai kana buƙatar sassaucin fassara a duk cibiyar sadarwarka, wannan ƙari yana sa hakan ya zama mai sauƙi.

**Ya Dace da:**

* Kasuwanci na yankuna da yawa masu buƙatun daidaitawa na gida daban-daban
* Cibiyoyin ilimi da ke hidimtawa al'ummomi masu bambanci
* Dandalin SaaS da ke bayar da tallafi na harsuna da yawa
* Hukumomin yanar gizo da ke sarrafa shafukan abokan ciniki da yawa
* Kowace cibiyar sadarwar multisite da ke buƙatar sassaucin fassara

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Tabbatar an shigar da Ultimate Multisite kuma an kunna shi a matakin cibiyar sadarwa
2. Shigar kuma kunna ƙarin manhaja na Loco Translate
3. Loda wannan ƙari zuwa kundin plugins ɗinka
4. Kunna ƙarin Loco Translate Integration a matakin cibiyar sadarwa
5. Fara ƙirƙirar fassarori na musamman ga shafi ta hanyar Loco Translate a kowane ƙaramin shafi

Shi ke nan! Haɗin yana aiki ta atomatik ba tare da wani ƙarin saiti ba.

## Tambayoyin da Ake Yawan Yi {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Shin ina buƙatar saita wani abu bayan shigarwa? {#do-i-need-to-configure-anything-after-installation}

A'a! Haɗin yana aiki ta atomatik. Da zarar ƙarin da Loco Translate duk sun kasance a kunne, ƙananan shafuka za su iya fara ƙirƙirar fassarorinsu na musamman nan take.

## Me zai faru idan ƙaramin shafi bai ƙirƙiri fassarori na musamman ba? {#what-happens-if-a-subsite-doesnt-create-custom-translations}

Shafin zai yi amfani da fassarorin cibiyar sadarwa gaba ɗaya ta atomatik a matsayin madadin. Babu abin da zai lalace, kuma komai zai yi aiki cikin sauƙi.

## Shin ƙananan shafuka za su iya samun damar fassarorin cibiyar sadarwa gaba ɗaya? {#can-subsites-access-network-wide-translations}

Masu gudanar da shafi na yau da kullum suna gani da gyara fassarorin shafinsu kawai. Manyan masu gudanarwa za su iya samun damar fassarorin cibiyar sadarwa gaba ɗaya da na musamman ga shafi dangane da wane shafi suke kallo.

## Shin wannan yana aiki da ƙarin manhajoji da jigogi duka? {#does-this-work-with-both-plugins-and-themes}

Eh! Fassarori na musamman ga shafi suna aiki ga ƙarin manhajoji da jigogi na WordPress duka.

## Shin wannan zai rage saurin cibiyar sadarwar multisite ɗina? {#will-this-slow-down-my-multisite-network}

A'a. Ƙarin yana amfani da loda fayil mai inganci wanda ke duba fassarori na musamman ga shafi ne kawai lokacin da ake buƙata. Babu wani tasirin aiki da za a lura da shi.

## Zan iya kwafe fassarorin cibiyar sadarwa zuwa wani takamaiman shafi a matsayin wurin farawa? {#can-i-copy-network-translations-to-a-specific-site-as-a-starting-point}

Eh! Yayin gyara fassarori a cikin Loco Translate, za ka iya fara da fassarorin cibiyar sadarwa cikin sauƙi sannan ka keɓance su ga kowane shafi.

## Shin wannan yana aiki da WooCommerce da sauran ƙarin manhajoji? {#does-this-work-with-woocommerce-and-other-plugins}

Tabbas! Duk wani ƙarin manhaja ko jigo da ke amfani da daidaitattun ayyukan fassara na WordPress zai yi aiki da fassarori na musamman ga shafi.
