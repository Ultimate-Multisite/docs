# Mai Ƙirƙirar Shafin Admin

## Abun ciki
Mai ƙirƙirar shafin admin zai ba ka damar ƙara sababbin shafukan admin da ƙananan shafuka cikin sauƙi a cikin WordPress admin dashboard. Ana iya amfani da shi don ƙirƙirar shafin maraba ko shafin koyarwa wanda zai iya zama shafin sauka ga sababbin masu amfani. Za ka iya amfani da mai gina shafi kamar Beaver Builder, Elementor, Brizy, da Oxygen don tsara shafukan ba tare da wahala ba.

Har ila yau yana bayar da tallafin HTML, CSS da JavaScript na musamman, da kuma sarrafa damar shiga bisa plan da rawar mai amfani!

## Fasali
* Tallafin HTML da CSS na Musamman
* Tallafin JavaScript
* Yanayin Abun ciki na PHP
* Izini bisa rawar Mai amfani
* Tallafi ga masu gina shafi

## Takardu
[Ƙirƙirar Shafukan Admin da Ƙananan Shafuka na Musamman ta amfani da Multisite Ultimate: Mai Ƙirƙirar Shafin Admin](https://help.multisiteultimate.com/article/305-creating-custom-admin-pages-and-sub-pages-using-ultimate-multisite-admin-page-creator)

## Shigarwa

1. Loda **ultimate-multisite-admin-page-creator** zuwa kundin **/wp-content/plugins/**
2. Kunna karin ta cikin menu na **Plugins** a cikin WordPress Network Admin ɗinka

## Jagorar Amfani

Yayin da WordPress ke sauƙaƙa ƙara abun ciki a ɓangaren gaba na shafukan yanar gizo, ƙirƙirar shafuka da ƙananan shafuka a ɓangaren admin na buƙatar wasu ƙwarewar coding na asali. Ultimate Multisite: Mai Ƙirƙirar Shafin Admin yana nufin kawo irin wannan sauƙin amfani na ɓangaren gaba zuwa ɓangaren baya na shafukan yanar gizon network ɗinka.

Wataƙila kana son bayar da shafi na musamman a panel ɗin admin na clients ɗinka tare da koyarwa, wataƙila kana son ƙara shafi na musamman mai FAQs da widget ɗin tuntuɓa. Damar ba su da iyaka tare da Mai Ƙirƙirar Shafin Admin, kuma wannan ne za mu bincika a koyarwar yau.

## Ƙirƙirar Shafin Admin na Musamman na farko

Bayan shigarwa da kunna Ultimate Multisite: Mai Ƙirƙirar Shafin Admin, je zuwa menu na network admin ɗinka sannan ka je Admin Pages.

![Jerin Shafukan Admin na Musamman a kan Network Admin](/img/admin/admin-pages-list.png)

Za ka ga jerin duk shafukan admin da ka ƙirƙira zuwa yanzu (tunda wannan shi ne ziyararka ta farko, jerin zai kasance babu komai). Ci gaba ka danna **Ƙara sabon Shafin Admin**. Za a tura ka zuwa editan Shafin Admin.

## Zaɓuɓɓukan Menu

A gefe, a cikin meta-box na General Options, za ka iya daidaita nau'in menu, alama, da ƙari mai yawa.

### Nau'ikan Menu

Sabon shafin admin na musamman ɗinka na iya samun nau'ikan menu biyu daban-daban: menu na matakin sama da ƙaramin menu.

Shafin menu na matakin sama, kamar yadda sunan ya nuna, zai ƙara sabon shafin da ka ƙirƙira zuwa menu na admin tare da tsoffin shafukan WordPress.

Idan ka zaɓi zaɓin matakin sama, za ka iya zaɓar tsarin menu da kuma alamar menu.

Idan kana son mai da wannan shafi ƙaramin shafin menu, za ka iya zaɓar shafin uwa don haɗa wannan shafi da shi. Za ka iya ƙara shafinka na musamman zuwa tsoffin shafukan WordPress ko zuwa shafukan admin na musamman na matakin sama naka.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Admin Page Creator menu type options with label, order, and icon settings -->

_Tsara abin menu gaba ɗaya don wannan Shafi na Musamman_

_Har ila yau za ka iya ƙara wannan shafi a matsayin ƙaramin menu na wasu shafukan menu, ciki har da naka na musamman na matakin sama._

## Zaɓuɓɓukan Ci gaba

Ya kamata ka iya ƙara kowane irin abun ciki zuwa shafukan admin na musamman ɗinka. Wannan ya haɗa da ƙa'idojin CSS da fayiloli, JavaScript, da kuma libraries na waje. Za ka iya yin hakan ta amfani da Advanced Options Tab.

### CSS na Musamman

A kan tab ɗin CSS, za ka iya ƙara lambar CSS ta musamman taka, da kuma shigo da fayilolin CSS daga shafukan yanar gizo na nesa (kamar CDNs, Google Fonts, da sauransu).

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Admin Page Creator custom CSS editor with external stylesheet import -->

_Ana kuma tallafa wa CSS na musamman. Hakanan za ka iya haɗa fayilolin salo na waje!_

### JavaScript na Musamman

Haka ma yana aiki ga JavaScript. Za ka iya ƙara libraries na waje don amfani da su a lambar JavaScript ta musamman taka.

_Ƙara lambar JavaScript taka!_

## Izini

Hakanan a cikin Zaɓuɓɓukan Ci gaba, za ka iya zaɓar waɗanne ƙananan shafukan yanar gizo ne za su sami damar shiga wannan shafi. Wannan yana da amfani idan kana shirin bayar da abun ciki na musamman daban-daban ga matakan plan daban-daban, misali.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Admin Page Creator permissions settings for plan and role-based access control -->

_Kana da cikakken iko kan inda wannan shafi zai bayyana_

**Muhimmi** : Dole ne a cika sharuɗɗan biyu domin a nuna shafin. Wannan yana nufin cewa idan mai amfani abokin ciniki ne na wani tsari, amma ba shi da ɗaya daga cikin rawar da aka zaɓa (ko akasin haka) shafin ba zai bayyana a kan panel ɗinsu ba.
