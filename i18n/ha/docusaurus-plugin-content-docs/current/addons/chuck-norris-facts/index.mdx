# Bayanan Chuck Norris {#chuck-norris-facts}

Ƙara ɗan barkwanci ga ayyukan gudanar da WordPress na yau da kullum da ƙarin **Ultimate Multisite - Chuck Norris Facts**. Idan an kunna shi, za ka ga wani bayanin Chuck Norris na bazuwar da aka nuna a kusurwar sama ta dama ta kowane shafin gudanarwa.

Ya dace sosai don sanya murmushi ga tawagarka, sauƙaƙa yanayi yayin dogayen zaman gudanarwa, ko kawai murnar fitacciyar ban mamakin ƙwarewa ta Chuck Norris.

## Me ya sa Bayanan Chuck Norris? {#why-chuck-norris-facts}

* **Ƙara Ƙwarin Gwiwar Tawaga**: Ƙara wani abu mai daɗi ga gudanar da hanyar sadarwarka
* **Fara Tattaunawa**: Kyakkyawan abin fara hira ga sabbin mambobin tawaga
* **Ƙara Samarwa**: Dariya ta ɗan lokaci na iya inganta mayar da hankali da samarwa
* **Barkwanci Mara Tsufa**: Bayanan Chuck Norris ba sa taɓa tsufa

## Manyan Fasali {#key-features}

**Bayanan Almara 110+**
Tarin barkwanci da bayanan Chuck Norris na gargajiya da ke murnar shi kansa gwarzon.

**Nuni Mara Katsalandan**
Bayanai suna bayyana a sandar gudanarwa ta hanyar da ba ta shiga hanya ba kuma ba ta hana aikinka.

**A Shirye Don Fassara**
An nade dukkan bayanai cikin ayyukan fassara, wanda ke sauƙaƙa mayar da su zuwa harshenka ko ƙirƙirar bayanai na musamman ga tawagarka.

**Mai Daidaitawa Cikakke**
Bayanai suna bayyana yadda ya kamata a allon kwamfutar tebur, kwamfutar hannu, da wayar hannu - Chuck Norris na da ban mamaki a kan duk na'urori.

**Tallafin RTL**
Cikakken tallafi ga harsunan dama-zuwa-hagu saboda Chuck Norris yana ƙetare shingen harshe.

**Ya Dace da Block Editor**
Ana ɓoye bayanai cikin hikima a cikin block editor don guje wa rikicin dubawa.

**Mai Mayar da Hankali kan Samun Dama**
Ya haɗa da rubutun mai karanta allo da ya dace don cikakken bin ƙa'idodin samun dama.

## Ya Dace Da {#perfect-for}

* Masu gudanar da hanyar sadarwar multisite da ke son ƙara hali
* Tawagoyin haɓakawa da ke jin daɗin barkwanci mai kyau
* Duk wanda ke buƙatar kashi na yau da kullum na ban mamakin Chuck Norris
* Shafuka da ke son ƙirƙirar yanayin aiki mai daɗi, mai kyau

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Tabbatar an shigar kuma an kunna Ultimate Multisite plugin a kan hanyar sadarwar WordPress ɗinka
2. Loda fayilolin ƙarin zuwa kundin `/wp-content/plugins/` naka
3. Kunna plugin a matakin hanyar sadarwa ta cikin menu na 'Plugins' a WordPress
4. Ji daɗin bayanan Chuck Norris na bazuwar a kowane shafin gudanarwa!

Ba a buƙatar saiti - kawai kunna ka yi murmushi!

## Tambayoyin da Ake Yawan Yi {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Shin wannan yana buƙatar Ultimate Multisite? {#does-this-require-ultimate-multisite}

Duk da cewa an tsara shi a matsayin ƙarin Ultimate Multisite, plugin ɗin yana aiki da kansa kuma yana iya gudana a kan kowace shigarwar WordPress.

## Zan iya ƙara bayanaina na kaina? {#can-i-add-my-own-facts}

Eh! Bayanai suna a shirye don fassara. Za ka iya ƙirƙirar fayil ɗinka na `.po` mai bayanai na musamman ka saka shi a cikin kundin `/lang`. Ko kuma za ka iya tace jerin bayanan ta hanyar shiri.

## Shin wannan yana rage saurin gudanarwata? {#does-this-slow-down-my-admin}

Ko kaɗan! Plugin ɗin yana da matuƙar sauƙi kuma ba shi da tasiri a kan aiki. Yana nuna rubutu ne kawai - babu kiran API na waje ko aiki mai nauyi.

## Zan iya kashe shi a wasu shafuka? {#can-i-disable-it-on-certain-pages}

Ana ɓoye bayanan kai tsaye a shafukan da za su iya kawo cikas (kamar block editor). Haka kuma za ka iya amfani da WordPress hooks don keɓance inda bayanai ke bayyana.

## Shin bayanan sun dace da kowane nau'in masu kallo? {#are-the-facts-appropriate-for-all-audiences}

Eh! Duk bayanan barkwanci ne masu tsafta, masu dacewa da iyali, suna murnar matsayin almara na Chuck Norris.

## Zan iya fassara bayanan zuwa wani harshe? {#can-i-translate-the-facts-to-another-language}

Tabbas! Duk bayanai suna amfani da ayyukan fassara na WordPress. Ƙirƙiri fayil ɗin fassara don harshenka ka saka shi a cikin kundin `/lang`. Text domain: `ultimate-multisite-chuck-norris-facts`

## Shin yana aiki da multisite? {#does-it-work-with-multisite}

Eh! An tsara shi musamman don hanyoyin sadarwar multisite kuma za a iya kunna shi a matakin hanyar sadarwa.
