# AffiliateWP {#affiliatewp}

Multisite Ultimate: Haɗin AffiliateWP

Yi amfani da AffiliateWP mai ƙarfi don haɓaka tushen abokan hulɗa na Ultimo Network ɗinka!

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Loda 'ultimate-multisite-git ' zuwa kundin '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Kunna ƙarin-shirin ta hanyar menu na 'Ƙarin-shirye' a WordPress
3. Bi Wizard mataki-mataki don saita ƙarin-shirin

## Jagorar Amfani {#usage-guide}

[AffiliateWP](https://affiliatewp.com/) ya zama mafitar da aka fi dogaro da ita yayin gina shirin wakilan tallace-tallace don kasuwancinka na kan intanet. Ultimate Multisite: AffiliateWP shi ne gada da zai ba ka damar gina irin wannan abu don premium network ɗinka.

## Farawa {#getting-started}

Da farko dai, **kana buƙatar samun** [**AffiliateWP**](https://affiliatewp.com/) **yana aiki**! Ba za ka iya amfani da add-on ɗinmu ba idan ba ka da **ƙarin-shirin da yake haɗuwa da shi**. Idan ba ka shirya barin abokan hulɗarka su yi amfani da shi a kan rukunonin yanar gizonsu ba, ya kamata AffiliateWP ya kasance yana aiki ne kawai a babban rukunin yanar gizonka.

Haka kuma, za ka buƙaci duka **Ultimate Multisite** da **Ultimate Multisite: AffiliateWP** **a kunna su** **a duk faɗin network**.

## Ƙara Ultimate Multisite a matsayin Haɗin AffiliateWP {#adding-ultimate-multisite-as-an-affiliatewp-integration}

A babban rukunin yanar gizonka, je zuwa Saitunan Affiliates, sannan a shafin Integrations, zaɓi Ultimate Multisite. Wannan zai gaya wa [AffiliateWP](https://affiliatewp.com/) ya fara bin diddigin ziyara da conversions daga tsarin rajistar Ultimate Multisite.

<!-- Hoton allo babu: shafin saitunan Haɗe-haɗen AffiliateWP tare da akwatin zaɓi na Ultimate Multisite -->

_Duba akwatin Ultimate Multisite a ƙasa sannan ka adana_

Wannan kusan shi ne duk abin da ake buƙata. Za a bi diddigin ziyara da suka zo daga hanyoyin referral, kuma idan daga baya wannan mai amfani ya yi biyansa na farko, za a ƙirƙiri conversion kuma za a ware kwamishan ga wakilin tallace-tallacen da ke da alhakin conversion ɗin.

Sai dai, biyan farko kawai da aka karɓa daga sabon mai amfani da aka ƙirƙira ne za a bi diddiginsa. Don kunna biyan referral masu maimaituwa, za ka buƙaci [AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals Add-on](https://affiliatewp.com/add-ons/pro/recurring-referrals/)

## Kunna Recurring Referrals {#enabling-recurring-referrals}

Bayan shigar da [AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals Add-on](https://affiliatewp.com/add-ons/pro/recurring-referrals/), je zuwa shafin Affiliates Settings Recurring Referrals, sannan ka kunna amfani da recurring referrals.

<!-- Hoton allo babu: saitunan AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals tare da maɓallin kunna Enable Recurring Referrals -->

_Mai sauƙi ne sosai: Kunna Recurring Referrals kuma komai ya kammala!_

Kuma, wannan shi ne duk abin da ake buƙata!

Yanzu, duk lokacin da aka karɓi sabon biyan kuɗi a kan account da aka kawo ta referral, wakilin tallace-tallacen da ke da alhaki zai sami nasa kaso ta atomatik!
