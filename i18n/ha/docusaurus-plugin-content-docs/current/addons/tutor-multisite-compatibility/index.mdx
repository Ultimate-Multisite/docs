# Tutor Multisite Compatibility {#tutor-multisite-compatibility}

Ƙarin WordPress da ke ƙara gyare-gyaren dacewa ga Tutor LMS a muhallan multisite.

## Bayani {#description}

Wannan ƙari yana samar da muhimman gyare-gyaren dacewa don gudanar da Tutor LMS a kan hanyoyin sadarwar WordPress multisite. Yana kula da rajistar masu amfani, sarrafa malamai, iyakokin darasi, da sauran ayyuka na musamman ga multisite.

## Fasaloli {#features}

- **Sarrafa Teburin Database**: Yana gano ta atomatik kuma yana ƙirƙirar teburan Tutor LMS da suka ɓace waɗanda suka kasa saboda ƙuntatawar maɓallin waje na multisite
- **Rajistar Mai Amfani na Multisite**: Masu sarrafa AJAX na musamman don sarrafa rajistar masu amfani a tsakanin shafukan hanyar sadarwa
- **Sarrafa Matsayin Malami**: Ba da matsayin malami da sarrafa shi ta atomatik a muhallan multisite
- **Iyakokin Darasi**: Haɗuwa da Ultimate Multisite don sarrafa iyakokin ƙirƙirar darasi da funnel bisa tsare-tsaren shafi
- **Ra'ayoyin Admin na Musamman**: Jerin malamai da ke fahimtar multisite da mu'amalar sarrafawa
- **Kayan Aikin Admin**: Mu'amalar sarrafa database ga masu gudanar da hanyar sadarwa da shafi

## Bukatu {#requirements}

- WordPress 5.0+
- PHP 7.4+
- Ƙarin Tutor LMS (ana tilastawa ta atomatik ta hanyar kanun `Requires Plugins`)
- Shigarwar WordPress Multisite
- Ƙarin Ultimate Multisite (don aikin iyakokin darasi)

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Loda fayilolin ƙarin zuwa `/wp-content/plugins/tutor-multisite-compatibility/`
2. Tabbatar an shigar da Tutor LMS kuma an kunna shi
3. Kunna ƙarin ta cikin menu na 'Plugins' a WordPress
4. Ƙarin zai haɗu da Tutor LMS ta atomatik

## Tsarin Fayil {#file-structure}

```
tutor-multisite-compatibility/
├── tutor-multisite-compatibility.php (Babban fayil ɗin ƙari)
├── includes/
│   ├── class-database-manager.php (Ƙirƙira da sarrafa teburin database)
│   ├── class-admin-interface.php (Mu'amalar admin don sarrafa database)
│   ├── class-course-limits.php (Masu sarrafa iyakokin darasi da funnel)
│   ├── class-instructor-manager.php (Sarrafa matsayin malami)
│   ├── class-ajax-handlers.php (Masu sarrafa AJAX na musamman)
│   ├── class-query-helper.php (Kayan taimakon tambayar database)

│       └── views/

```

## Aikin da Aka Matsar daga MU plugin {#functionality-moved-from-mu-plugin}

Wannan ƙari yana ɗauke da aikin da a baya yake cikin `wp-content/mu-plugins/daves.php`:

- Iyakokin sauya matsayin darasi
- Iyakokin ƙirƙirar darasi
- Haɗuwar CartFlows da iyakoki
- Sarrafa matsayin malamin Tutor
- Sarrafa rajistar mai amfani na multisite
- Nuna ID na samfurin darasi

## Sarrafa Database {#database-management}

### Matsalar {#the-problem}

A muhallan WordPress multisite, ƙirƙirar teburin Tutor LMS yana kasawa ga tebura masu ƙuntatawar maɓallin waje zuwa teburin masu amfani. Wannan yana faruwa ne saboda:

- Tebura na musamman ga shafi suna amfani da prefix kamar `wp_2_tutor_carts`
- Maɓallan waje suna nuni zuwa `wp_2_users` (wanda babu shi)
- Ainihin teburin masu amfani shi ne `wp_users` (ana raba shi a cikin hanyar sadarwar)

### Maganin {#the-solution}

Wannan ƙari yana ta atomatik:

1. **Gano Teburan da Suka Ɓace**: Yana duba teburan da suka kasa ƙirƙiruwa saboda ƙuntatawar maɓallin waje
2. **Ƙirƙirar Tebura da Madaidaitan Manazarta**: Yana amfani da sunan teburin mai amfani da ya dace (`wp_users` a multisite)
3. **Kula da Yanayi da Dama**:
   - Kunna/sake kunna ƙari
   - Ƙirƙirar sabon shafi a hanyar sadarwa
   - Duba tebur da hannu ta hanyar mu'amalar admin

### Teburan da Abin Ya Shafa {#affected-tables}

- `tutor_coupon_usages` - Yana ɗauke da maɓallin waje zuwa teburin masu amfani
- `tutor_carts` - Yana ɗauke da maɓallin waje zuwa teburin masu amfani
- `tutor_notification_preferences` - Teburin TutorPro mai maɓallin waje zuwa teburin masu amfani

### Mu'amalar Admin {#admin-interface}

Samu damar kayan aikin sarrafa database a:

- **Network Admin**: Settings → Tutor Multisite DB
- **Site Admin**: Tools → Tutor Multisite DB

Mu'amalar tana ba ka damar:
- Duba teburan da suka ɓace a duk shafuka
- Ƙaddamar da ƙirƙirar tebur da hannu
- Duba saitin database
- Duba cikakken bayanin matsayi

## Tallafi {#support}

An tsara wannan ƙari musamman don dandalin KursoPro. Don tallafin fasaha, tuntuɓi ƙungiyar haɓakawa.
