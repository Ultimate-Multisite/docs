# Tacewa: wp_ultimo_woocommerce_checkout_fields_to_clean

Yana ba masu haɓakawa damar tace tsoffin filayen adireshin biyan kuɗi na WooCommerce.

Tun da Ultimate Multisite yana da filayen adireshin biyan kuɗi, yana iya zama da ma'ana a cire tsoffin filayen da WooCommerce ke nunawa a kan fom ɗin checkout. Ta tsohuwa, ba a wuce wata ƙima zuwa wannan jeri ba, don haka duk filayen da aka saita a kan WooCommerce don a nuna su ana riƙe da su.

### Tun daga

- 2.0.0
### Tushen

An ayyana a cikin [`inc/gateways/class-woocommerce-gateway.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite-woocommerce/blob/main/inc/gateways/class-woocommerce-gateway.php#L277) a layi na 277


## Abubuwan da ake mayarwa
Jerin filayen da za a cire. Ƙimomin da ake da su sun haɗa da billing_first_name, billing_last_name, billing_company, billing_country, billing_address_1, billing_address_2, billing_city, billing_state, billing_postcode, billing_email, da billing_phone.
