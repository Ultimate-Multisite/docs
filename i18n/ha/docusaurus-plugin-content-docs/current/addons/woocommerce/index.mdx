# Haɗin WooCommerce {#woocommerce-integration}

Haɗin WooCommerce yana aiki da kama sosai da yadda Manual Gateway ɗinmu yake aiki. Yana ba masu amfani da ku sassauci mai yawa, yana ba su damar zaɓar kowane daga cikin fom ɗin biyan kuɗi da ke akwai a kan shigarwar WooCommerce ɗinku. Kuma yana da sauƙi: da zarar lokacin biyan kuɗin mai amfani ya ƙare, ana sanya subscription ɗin mai amfani a jira. A lokaci guda, za a ƙirƙiri oda a kan shigarwar WooCommerce na babban site na network ɗin. Daga nan mai amfani zai karɓi invoice a imel ɗinsa, tare da hanyar haɗi don aiwatar da biyan kuɗi. Da zarar an karɓi biyan kuɗin, ana sabunta subscription ɗin.

## Fasali {#features}

- Haɗawa mai sauƙi tsakanin Ultimate Multisite da WooCommerce
- Hanyoyin biyan kuɗi da dama suna akwai
- Biyan kuɗi mai maimaituwa ta atomatik ta amfani da ginshiƙin subscriptions da aka haɗa, tare da zaɓin ingantawa daga plugin na WooCommerce Subscriptions
- Jimillar checkout tana haɗa harajin Ultimate Multisite a matsayin layukan kuɗin WooCommerce daban, marasa haraji, domin abokan ciniki su iya duba adadin haraji kafin biyan kuɗi

## Takardun Bayani {#documentation}

**Saita Haɗin WooCommerce**

**Faɗaɗa Zaɓuɓɓukan Biyan Kuɗin ku da Ultimate Multisite: Haɗin WooCommerce**

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Loda 'ultimate-multisite-woocommerce' zuwa kundin '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Kunna plugin ta menu na 'Plugins' a cikin WordPress
3. Bi Wizard mataki-mataki don saita plugin ɗin

## Jagorar Amfani {#usage-guide}

_**MUHIMMIYAR LURA: Wannan makala tana nufin Ultimate Multisite sigar 2.x.**_

_**HANKALI:** Ultimate Multisite: Haɗin WooCommerce yana buƙatar a kunna WooCommerce aƙalla a kan babban site ɗinku._

Mun fahimci cewa _Stripe_ da _PayPal_ ba sa samuwa a wasu ƙasashe, abin da ke iyakance ko hana masu amfani da Ultimate Multisite amfani da plugin ɗinmu yadda ya kamata. Don haka muka ƙirƙiri **add-on don haɗa _WooCommerce_**.

_WooCommerce_ sanannen plugin ne na eCommerce sosai. Masu haɓakawa a duniya sun ƙirƙiri add-ons don haɗa gateways na biyan kuɗi daban-daban da shi. Mun yi amfani da wannan don faɗaɗa gateways na biyan kuɗi da za ku iya amfani da su a cikin tsarin biyan kuɗi na Ultimate Multisite.

## Saita Shi {#setting-it-up}

Da farko, kuna buƙatar shigar da add-on na WooCommerce Integration wanda yake **kyauta ga masu lasisi**. Don shigar da shi, danna Ultimate Multisite a kan dashboard na super admin ɗinku sannan ku zaɓi **Add-ons**.

![Shafin Add-ons](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

A nan za ku iya samun duk add-ons na Ultimate Multisite. Danna add-on na **Ultimate Multisite: WooCommerce Integration**.

![Shafin Add-ons da ke nuna WooCommerce Integration](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

Taga za ta bayyana tare da bayanan add-on. Kawai danna **Install Now** *.*

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: WooCommerce Integration addon install popup with Install Now button -->

Bayan an gama shigarwa, za a tura ku zuwa shafin plugins. A nan, kawai danna **Network Activate** kuma za a kunna add-on na _WooCommerce_ a kan network ɗinku.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: WP Plugins page showing Network Activate button for WooCommerce Integration -->

Bayan kun kunna shi, idan har yanzu ba ku shigar kuma kun kunna plugin na WooCommerce a kan website ɗinku ba, **za ku karɓi tunatarwa**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Admin notice reminding to install and activate WooCommerce plugin -->

Da zarar an shigar, za ku ga ƙarin zaɓi a ƙarƙashin saitunan biyan kuɗi na Ultimate Multisite. Daga nan za ku iya **kunna** zaɓin _WooCommerce_.

![Saitunan payment gateway na Ultimate Multisite tare da WooCommerce a kunne](/img/addons/woocommerce-payment-gateway.png)

Za ku iya saita wasu saituna na asali kamar sunan nunawa da zai bayyana a front end da umarni don jagorantar masu amfani da ku a cikin tsarin checkout ɗinsu. Sannan za ku iya zuwa **saitunan _WooCommerce_ na Main site ɗinku** don kunna da saita payment gateway da kuka fi so ku yi amfani da shi.

### Layukan kuɗin harajin checkout {#checkout-tax-fee-lines}

Lokacin da checkout na Ultimate Multisite ya haɗa da haraji, haɗin yana ƙara kowane adadin harajin Ultimate Multisite zuwa checkout na WooCommerce a matsayin layin kuɗi daban, mara haraji. Wannan yana sa jimillar haraji ta kasance a bayyane kafin biyan kuɗi yayin da yake hana WooCommerce sake sanya haraji a kan kuɗin harajin.

## Yadda ake sarrafa biyan kuɗi {#how-to-manage-the-payment}

Haɗin WooCommerce yana aiki da kama sosai da yadda **Manual Gateway** ɗinmu yake aiki. Rashin amfanin shi ne cewa dole a biya kowane biyan kuɗi da hannu a ƙarshen lokacin biyan kuɗi. Ga yadda tsarin yake aiki:

  * Da zarar billing cycle na mai amfani ya ƙare, ana sanya membership ɗin **a jira**. A lokaci guda, za a ƙirƙiri oda a kan shigarwar _WooCommerce_ a babban site na network ɗinku.

  * Daga nan masu amfani za su karɓi invoice a imel ɗinsu, tare da hanyar haɗi don aiwatar da biyan kuɗi (hanyar haɗin kuma tana bayyana a tarihin biyan kuɗinsu daga dashboard na subsite ɗinsu). Wannan hanyar haɗin tana tura mai amfani zuwa _**WooCommerce**_ **checkout** , inda masu amfani za su iya zaɓar ɗaya daga cikin **gateways na _WooCommerce_ da ke akwai** domin aiwatar da biyan kuɗinsu.

  * Da zarar an karɓi biyan kuɗin, ana **sabunta** membership ɗin , ana rubuta biyan kuɗin a Ultimate Multisite kuma ana saita odar ta kammala.

### Wasu Muhimman Lura: {#some-important-notes}

  * Ayyukan tsoho na Ultimate Multisite har yanzu suna aiki da wannan nau’in haɗin. Misali, **bayar da refunds** daga allon **Subscription Management** zai sadar da WooCommerce don bayar da refund ta amfani da fom ɗin biyan kuɗi da aka zaɓa.

  * Ultimate Multisite: WooCommerce Integration yana dogara da WP-Cron don aiki. Wannan yana aiki sosai a mafi yawan lokuta, amma idan site ɗin yana da ƙarancin zirga-zirga to ba a tunatar da cron don duba abubuwan da aka tsara ba kuma ana rasa abin da aka tsara, wanda zai iya haifar da jinkirin aiwatarwa.

## Plugin na WooCommerce Subscriptions {#woocommerce-subscriptions-plugin}

Fara biyan kuɗi da hannu kowane wata ba shi ne mafi dacewa ga masu amfani na ƙarshe ba saboda ana sa ran subscriptions su maimaita kai tsaye a kowane zagayen lissafin kuɗi. Add-on na WooCommerce Integration yanzu yana haɗa ainihin subscriptions code, don haka kayayyakin da ke maimaituwa koyaushe suna amfani da subscriptions don biyan kuɗi mai maimaituwa.

Ba kwa **buƙatar** saye ko shigar da [WooCommerce Subscriptions plugin](https://woocommerce.com/products/woocommerce-subscriptions/) na daban don sayar da kayayyakin da ke maimaituwa ta wannan integration. Idan an sayi wannan plugin, an shigar da shi, kuma yana akwai a babban site ɗinku, Ultimate Multisite zai yi amfani da shi kuma WooCommerce zai nuna ƙarin fasalolin sarrafa subscription. Idan ba ya akwai, ainihin subscriptions da aka haɗa har yanzu yana kula da kayayyakin da ke maimaituwa.

![Saitunan haɗin WooCommerce subscription](/img/addons/woocommerce-subscription-integration.png)

Kuma shi ke nan. Masu amfani na ƙarshe ba za su ƙara buƙatar yin biyan kuɗi da hannu a ƙarshen zagayen lissafin kuɗinsu don kayayyakin da ke maimaituwa ba.

Baya ga oda da ake ƙirƙira a babban site ɗinku ƙarƙashin _WooCommerce_, zai kuma ƙirƙiri subscription ga kowane Account.

### Halayen dakatarwa idan sabuntawa ta gaza {#failed-renewal-suspension-behavior}

Ta tsohuwa, memberships suna ci gaba da aiki yayin da WooCommerce Subscriptions ke aiwatar da jadawalin sake gwajin biyan kuɗin da ya gaza. Wannan yana kiyaye lokacin sassauci na al’ada na WooCommerce Subscriptions domin sabuntawar da ta dawo ta iya mayar da subscription da membership su daidaita ba tare da katse damar shiga da wuri ba.

Masu gudanarwa da suke son tsauraran sarrafa damar shiga za su iya kunna **Suspend Memberships Immediately on Failed Renewals** a saitunan WooCommerce Integration. Idan an kunna wannan saitin zaɓi, Ultimate Multisite yana dakatar da membership mai alaƙa da zarar biyan kuɗin sabuntawa ya gaza maimakon jira har WooCommerce Subscriptions ta gama sarrafa sake gwaji.

Masu haɓakawa za su iya sauya wannan shawarar da filter `wu_woo_suspend_on_payment_failure`. Ana samar da shafukan bayanin hook daga asalin add-on, don haka wannan taƙaitaccen bayani yana ambaton sunan filter kawai don manufofin dakatarwa na musamman.
