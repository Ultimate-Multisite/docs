# GoCardless {#gocardless}

Multisite Ultimate: Biyan Kuɗi ta GoCardless

Ba abokan cinikinka damar biyan membobin Ultimate Multisite da kayayyaki ta cire kuɗi kai tsaye ta amfani da GoCardless!

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Loda 'ultimate-multisite-gocardless' zuwa kundin '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Kunna ƙarin ta cikin menu na 'Ƙari' a WordPress
3. Bi Wizard mataki-mataki don saita ƙarin

## Jagorar Amfani {#usage-guide}

_GoCardless_ ƙofar biyan kuɗi ce da ake samu a **Turai da kuma a UK** wadda ke ba da damar biyan kuɗi ta **cire kuɗi kai tsaye**.

**Ultimate Multisite: GoCardless Gateway** yana ƙara tallafin _GoCardless_ zuwa Ultimate Multisite, yana ba ka damar caje abokan cinikinka a wannan yankin ta amfani da cire kuɗi kai tsaye.

Wannan koyarwar za ta rufe yadda ake saita _GoCardless_ don fara karɓar biyan kuɗi ta cikinsa a kan hanyar sadarwar Ultimate Multisite ɗinka.

_**Lura: GoCardless yana aiki ne kawai da waɗannan kuɗaɗe:**_ **EUR, GBP, AUD, SEK, da DKK.**

## Shigar da Add-on {#installing-the-add-on}

Da farko, za ka buƙaci shigar da add-on na GoCardless don Ultimate Multisite. Za ka iya yin hakan ta zuwa **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Add-ons (a kan sidebar)**. Sannan ka zaɓi add-on na GoCardless a wannan jerin.

Bayan an shigar da add-on kuma an kunna shi, je zuwa **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Payments**, kunna zaɓin GoCardless, sannan ka adana.

![Saitunan ƙofar biyan kuɗi](/img/config/settings-payment-gateways.png)

## Samun Access Token {#getting-an-access-token}

Ƙirƙiri Account a kan _GoCardless_ (ko a kan _GoCardless_ sandbox, idan kana son **gwada shi da farko** , wanda ake ba da shawara). Sannan, bayan ka shiga, kewaya zuwa abin menu na **Developers**.

<!-- Babu hoton allo: Dashboard na GoCardless yana nuna matsayin menu na Developers -->

A shafin **Developers**, danna maɓallin **Create** a kusurwar sama-dama ta allo, sannan ka danna zaɓin **Access Token**.

<!-- Babu hoton allo: Shafin Developers na GoCardless tare da maɓallin Create da zaɓin Access Token -->

Wannan zai buɗe sabuwar taga modal, yana ba ka damar ƙirƙirar sabon token. Ba shi suna da za ka iya gane shi kuma ka tabbatar ka zaɓi zaɓin **Read-write access** a menu na **Scope**.

<!-- Babu hoton allo: Modal na GoCardless don ƙirƙirar sabon access token tare da scope na Read-write -->

Access token zai bayyana a kan allo. Kwafi shi sannan ka liƙa shi a menu ɗinka na **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Payments > GoCardless**.

Ka tabbatar ka sanya shi a filin da ya dace. Idan **token na live** **ne** , kashe **yanayin sandbox** kafin ka liƙa token ɗin.

![Saitunan ƙofar biyan kuɗi tare da GoCardless](/img/config/settings-payment-gateways.png)

<!-- Babu hoton allo: Filayen token na GoCardless a cikin saitunan biyan kuɗi na Ultimate Multisite -->

Shi ke nan don mataki na farko. Na gaba, kana buƙatar saita mai sauraron webhook.

## Saita Mai Sauraron Webhook {#setting-up-the-webhook-listener}

Lokacin hulɗa da rajistocin biyan kuɗi, Ultimate Multisite yana buƙatar jin labarin canje-canje a matsayin rajista, sababbin biyan kuɗi da aka yi, da sauran irin waɗannan abubuwa.

Hanyar da ƙofofin biyan kuɗi ke sanar da Ultimate Multisite game da waɗannan canje-canje ita ce amfani da **kiran webhook**. Saboda haka, domin haɗin ya yi aiki 100%, muna buƙatar gaya wa _GoCardless_ wane URL zai kira da duk wannan bayani.

Koma zuwa panel ɗinka na **GoCardless > Developers** kuma ka sake danna maɓallin Create, amma wannan karon, zaɓi zaɓin **Webhook endpoint**.

<!-- Babu hoton allo: Shafin Developers na GoCardless tare da zaɓin Webhook endpoint -->Sabuwar taga za ta buɗe tare da zaɓuɓɓukan webhook endpoint. Yana tambayar URL na webhook, don haka koma zuwa **saitunan Ultimate Multisite > Payments > GoCardless** naka kuma ka kwafi URL na webhook da aka lissafa a can:

<!-- Babu hoton allo: Saitunan GoCardless na Ultimate Multisite suna nuna URL na webhook -->

Ƙari ga haka, za ka iya shigar da **sirrin webhook** da za a yi amfani da shi don sanya hannu ga kiran webhook ɗinka. Duk abin da ka shigar a wannan filin, za ka buƙaci shigar da shi a **filin Sirrin Webhook na Ultimate Multisite** ma.

<!-- Babu hoton allo: Fom ɗin saitin webhook endpoint na GoCardless tare da filayen URL da sirri -->

<!-- Babu hoton allo: Filin sirrin webhook na Ultimate Multisite GoCardless -->

Kammala ta hanyar adana endpoint ta danna maɓallin **Create webhook endpoint** sannan kuma **ka adana saitunan Ultimate Multisite ɗinka ma**.

Ya kamata ka kasance a shirye don karɓar _GoCardless_ yanzu!
