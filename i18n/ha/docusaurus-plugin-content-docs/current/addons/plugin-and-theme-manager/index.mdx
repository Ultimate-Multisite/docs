# Manajan Kari da Jigo

Multisite Ultimate: Manajan Kari da Jigo

Gyara ko ɓoye bayanan meta (take, marubuci, ƙaramin hoto da bayanin) na karuka da jigogi da aka shigar. Ƙirƙiri rukunai don karuka da jigogi domin ba masu amfani damar tace su, sannan ka ƙara kyakkyawan shafin Karuka na musamman a panel ɗin masu amfani da kai.

Don amfani da karin, kawai za ka iya zuwa shafukan Karuka ko Jigogi a cikin gudanarwar cibiyar sadarwarka sannan ka danna "Gyara bayanin Kari/Jigo".

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Loda 'ultimate-multisite-plugin-and-theme-manager' zuwa kundin '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Kunna karin ta hanyar menu na 'Karuka' a cikin WordPress Network Admin ɗinka

## Jagorar Amfani {#usage-guide}

Ultimate Multisite: Manajan Kari da Jigo ƙari ne na Ultimate Multisite mai ƙarfi kuma **na kuɗi** wanda yake ba ka cikakken iko kan yadda masu amfani da cibiyar sadarwarka suke gani da hulɗa da zaɓuɓɓukan kari da jigo a kan cibiyar sadarwarka.

Wannan ƙarin yana da manufofi uku:

  1. Yana ba ka damar canza metadata na karuka da jigogi gaba ɗaya. Wannan ya haɗa da suna, bayani, sunan marubuci, har ma da ƙananan hotuna!

  2. Yana ba ka damar rarraba karuka da jigogi cikin rukunai, domin ba masu amfani damar tsara su cikin sauƙi.

  3. Yana ba ka zaɓin maye gurbin tsohon shafin karuka mai ban gajiya da sabon shafi mai kama da na jigogi tare da tsara su bisa rukunai.

Bayan saukewa da shigar da shi, sabon tab - _Saitunan Ƙari_ \- zai bayyana a shafin Saitunan Ultimate Multisite.
