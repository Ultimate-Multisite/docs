# Mai Sayar da Domain {#domain-seller}

**Multisite Ultimate: Mai Sayar da Domain** yana sauya hanyar sadarwar WordPress Multisite ɗinka zuwa dandali mai ƙarfi na sayar da domain. Bari abokan ciniki su yi rajistar domain na musamman kai tsaye lokacin rajista ko daga Dashboard dinsu, tare da duba samuwa a ainihin lokaci da farashi mai bayyana.

### Me ya sa Mai Sayar da Domain? {#why-domain-seller}

Rajistar domain na ɗaya daga cikin abubuwan ƙarin sayarwa da ake yawan nema ga kowane dandali na WaaS (Website a matsayin Sabis). Maimakon aika abokan cinikinka zuwa wani mai rajistar ɓangare na uku, za ka iya bayar da domain kai tsaye — kana riƙe ƙarin kuɗin shiga a cikin hanyar sadarwarka yayin da kake ba abokan ciniki ƙwarewa mara tangarɗa.

### Muhimman Fasali {#key-features}

#### Tallafin Masu Rajistar Domain da Yawa {#multiple-domain-registrar-support}
* **OpenSRS** — Cikakkiyar rajista, sabuntawa, da gudanarwa
* **Namecheap** — Cikakken haɗawa da WhoisGuard da gwajin sandbox
* **HostAfrica** — Binciken domain, farashi, rajista, canja wuri, DNS, da kariyar ID ta Domains Reseller API
* **Openprovider** — Farashin reseller, rajista, sabuntawa, canja wuri, DNS, sirrin WHOIS, da daidaita TLD
* **Hostinger** — Binciken domain da rajista ta amfani da shared Hostinger integration token
* **Tsarin Gine-gine Mai Faɗaɗuwa** — An gina shi bisa tsarin Integration Registry, yana sauƙaƙa ƙara ƙarin masu bayarwa
* **Gwajin Haɗi a Ciki** — Tabbatar da bayanan shiga na mai bayarwa da haɗin kai kafin fara aiki kai tsaye

#### Mayen Saita Mai Jagoranci {#guided-setup-wizard}
* **Fara amfani da dannawa ɗaya** — Yana jagorantarka ta cikin saita mai bayarwa, shigo da TLD, da ƙirƙirar samfurin domain
* **Daidaita TLD ta atomatik** — Yana shigo da duk TLDs da suke samuwa da farashin wholesale daga masu bayarwa da ka haɗa
* **Ƙirƙirar samfurin tsoho** — Yana ƙirƙirar samfurin domain da aka shirya amfani da shi tare da kyawawan tsoffin ƙarin farashi

#### Farashin Domain Mai Sauƙin Daidaitawa {#flexible-domain-pricing}
* **Hanyoyin ƙarin farashi guda uku** — Ƙarin farashi bisa kashi, ƙarin farashi tsayayye, ko farashi tsayayye ga kowane TLD
* **Samfura na musamman ga TLD** — Ƙirƙiri samfuran domain daban-daban don rukunonin TLD daban (.com/.net, .io, lambobin ƙasa, da sauransu)
* **Farashin gabatarwa** — Bayar da farashin shekarar farko mai rahusa don jawo sabbin rajista
* **Farashi a ainihin lokaci** — Farashin wholesale kai tsaye daga masu bayarwa tare da cache mai basira
* **Tallafin coupon** — Cikakken dacewa da lambobin rangwamen Multisite Ultimate

#### Kariyar Sirrin WHOIS {#whois-privacy-protection}
* **Saita ga kowane samfur** — Saita sirrin WHOIS zuwa An Kashe, Kullum a Haɗe, ko Zaɓin Abokin Ciniki
* **Abokin ciniki ya zaɓi shiga** — Idan an saita zuwa Zaɓin Abokin Ciniki, akwatin zaɓi na biyan oda yana barin abokan ciniki su ƙara kariyar sirri
* **Farashi mai daidaituwa** — Saita farashin kowace shekara don ƙarin kariyar sirri, ko bayar da shi kyauta
* **Haɗawa da mai rajista** — Yana kunna WhoisGuard (Namecheap) ko sirrin WHOIS (OpenSRS) ta atomatik a lokacin rajista

#### Ƙwarewar Biyan Oda Haɗaɗɗe {#unified-checkout-experience}
* **Hanyoyin domain guda uku** — Abokan ciniki suna zaɓa tsakanin subdomain (kyauta), rajistar sabon domain, ko haɗa domain da suke da shi a yanzu
* **Binciken domain a ainihin lokaci** — Duba samuwa da AJAX ke sarrafawa ba tare da sake loda shafi ba
* **Shawarwari masu wayo** — Madadin shawarwarin TLD idan domain da aka fi so an riga an ɗauke shi
* **Farashi mai bayyana** — Bayyanannen nunin farashin rajista, farashin sabuntawa, da kuɗin sirrin WHOIS na zaɓi
* **Filayen bayanan mai rajista** — Yana tattara bayanan da ake buƙata na mai rajista (suna, adireshi, waya) a cikin layi yayin biyan oda
* **URL na shafi da ake ƙirƙira ta atomatik** — Ana samo adireshin shafi ta atomatik daga domain da aka zaɓa

#### Tsarin Sabuntawa ta Atomatik {#auto-renewal-system}
* **Sabuntawa masu alaƙa da membobanci** — Ana sabunta domain ta atomatik yayin da membobancin abokin ciniki yake aiki
* **Tsara lokaci mai wayo** — Ana jera ayyukan sabuntawa bisa ranakun ƙarewar domain
* **Dabarun sake gwadawa** — Sake gwadawa masu basira don yunƙurin sabuntawa da suka gaza
* **Sanarwar imel** — Faɗakarwa masu daidaituwa don sabuntawa masu zuwa, sabuntawa da suka yi nasara, gazawa, da ƙarewa

#### Gudanar da DNS na Abokin Ciniki {#customer-dns-management}
* **DNS na kai-tsaye** — Abokan ciniki za su iya ƙara, gyara, da share rikodin DNS (A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, TXT) daga Dashboard dinsu
* **Matsayin domain** — Matsayin rajista a ainihin lokaci, ranakun ƙarewa, da bayanan nameserver
* **Haɗa domain ta atomatik** — Domain da aka yi rajista ko na yanzu ana haɗa su ta atomatik zuwa shafin abokin ciniki

#### Kayan Aikin Admin {#admin-tools}
* **Rajistar domain da hannu** — Yi rajistar domain a madadin abokan ciniki daga allon admin
* **Sa ido kan domain** — Duba duk domain na abokan ciniki, matsayai, da ranakun ƙarewa daga shafi ɗaya
* **Rubutun log na musamman ga mai bayarwa** — Tashoshin log daban-daban ga kowane mai rajista (misali, domain-seller-namecheap.log)
* **Bayanan rajista** — Duba bayanan mai rajista da bayanan rajista a babban shafin gyaran domain

### Masu Bayarwa da Ake Tallafawa {#supported-providers}

* **OpenSRS** — Cikakken haɗawa da rajista, sabuntawa, gudanar da DNS, da sirrin WHOIS
* **Namecheap** — Cikakken haɗawa da rajista, sabuntawa, duba samuwa cikin rukuni, da WhoisGuard kyauta
* **HostAfrica** — Rajista, sabuntawa, canja wuri, gudanar da DNS, dawo da lambar EPP, kulle mai rajista, da kariyar ID
* **Openprovider** — Rajista, sabuntawa, canja wuri, gudanar da DNS, dawo da lambar EPP, kulle mai rajista, sirrin WHOIS, da daidaita reseller TLD
* **Hostinger** — Rajista, binciken domain, sabunta nameserver, kulle mai rajista, da sirrin WHOIS; Hostinger auto-renew ne ke kula da sabuntawa
* **GoDaddy** — Rajista da sabuntawa
* **ResellerClub** — Rajista, sabuntawa, da gudanar da DNS
* **NameSilo** — Rajista da sabuntawa
* **Enom** — Rajista, sabuntawa, da gudanar da DNS

### Bayanan Fasaha {#technical-details}

* **Yana buƙata:** PHP 7.4+, Multisite Ultimate 2.4.12+
* **Tsarin gine-gine:** Tsarin Integration Registry tare da Domain_Selling_Capability interface
* **Autoloading:** Jetpack autoloader tare da PSR-4 class mapping
* **Ka'idoji:** Ka'idojin rubuta code na WordPress, strict typing, prepared statements

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Tabbatar cewa **Multisite Ultimate v2.4.12+** an shigar da shi kuma an kunna shi a kan network
2. Loda plugin ɗin zuwa `/wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite-domain-seller/`
3. Kunna plugin ɗin a kan network daga **Plugins** a cikin WordPress Network Admin
4. **Mai Jagoran Saiti** yana farawa ta atomatik a kunnawa na farko — bi matakan don:
   * Haɗa domain registrar ɗinka
   * Gwada haɗin
   * Shigo da TLDs da ake da su da farashin wholesale
   * Ƙirƙiri domain product ɗinka na farko tare da pricing markup
5. Ƙara filin **Zaɓin Domain** zuwa fom ɗin checkout ɗinka a cikin **Ultimate Multisite > Fom ɗin Checkout**

## Tambayoyin da Ake Yawan Yi {#frequently-asked-questions}

### Wane sigar Multisite Ultimate ake buƙata? {#what-version-of-multisite-ultimate-is-required}

Siga 2.4.12 ko sama da haka. Addon ɗin bai dace da WP Ultimo v1.x ko sigogin Ultimate Multisite da ke ƙasa da 2.4.12 ba.

### Waɗanne domain providers ake tallafawa? {#which-domain-providers-are-supported}

Ana tallafa wa registrars tara: **OpenSRS**, **Namecheap**, **HostAfrica**, **Openprovider**, **Hostinger**, **GoDaddy**, **ResellerClub**, **NameSilo**, da **Enom**. Ƙwarewa tana bambanta bisa provider saboda registrar APIs suna bayyana ayyuka daban-daban; duba jagorar saiti don bayani provider-da-provider. Tsarin Integration Registry na addon ɗin yana sa ƙara custom providers ya zama mai sauƙi — duba [jagorar developer](/developer/integration-guide/custom-registrar).

### Zan iya bayar da kariyar sirrin WHOIS? {#can-i-offer-whois-privacy-protection}

Eh. Kowane domain product yana da saitin Sirrin WHOIS tare da hanyoyi uku: An Kashe, Koyaushe An Haɗa, ko Zaɓin Abokin Ciniki. Idan an saita zuwa Zaɓin Abokin Ciniki, abokan ciniki za su ga checkbox yayin checkout tare da farashin (ko "Kyauta" don WhoisGuard na Namecheap). Ana aika privacy flag ta atomatik zuwa registrar a lokacin rijista.

### Zan iya saita farashi daban-daban don TLDs daban-daban? {#can-i-set-different-pricing-for-different-tlds}

Eh. Ƙirƙiri domain products daban-daban don rukunin TLD daban-daban. Kowane product na iya samun nasa nau'in markup (kashi, fixed markup, ko fixed price), TLDs da ake tallafawa, da farashin gabatarwa.

### Waɗanne zaɓuɓɓukan checkout abokan ciniki suke gani? {#what-checkout-options-do-customers-see}

Filin Zaɓin Domain yana ba abokan ciniki tabs uku: **Subdomain** (yi amfani da subdomain kyauta a kan network ɗinka), **Rijistar Sabon Domain** (bincika kuma yi rijistar sabon domain), da **Domain da ke Akwai** (haɗa domain da suke da shi tuni). Hanyoyin da ka kunna kaɗai za su bayyana.

### Yana tallafa wa sabuntawa ta atomatik? {#does-it-support-automatic-renewals}

Eh. Ana iya saita domains su sabunta ta atomatik kuma ana haɗa su da matsayin membership na abokin ciniki. Ana sarrafa kuɗin sabuntawa ta hanyar payment gateway da abokin ciniki yake amfani da shi yanzu.

### Abokan ciniki za su iya sarrafa DNS records ɗinsu? {#can-customers-manage-their-dns-records}

Eh. Abokan ciniki suna samun hanyar sarrafa DNS a cikin dashboard ɗinsu don ƙara, gyara, da share records na A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, da TXT. Sarrafa DNS yana samuwa tare da OpenSRS, ResellerClub, Enom, HostAfrica, da Openprovider. Hostinger DNS don hosted domains ana sarrafa shi ta core Hostinger domain-mapping integration.

### Akwai mai jagoran saiti? {#is-there-a-setup-wizard}

Eh. Mai jagoran saiti yana gudana ta atomatik a kunnawa na farko kuma yana bi da kai ta hanyar haɗa registrar, shigo da TLDs, da ƙirƙirar domain product ɗinka na farko. Hakanan za ka iya sake gudanar da wizard daga saitunan addon.
