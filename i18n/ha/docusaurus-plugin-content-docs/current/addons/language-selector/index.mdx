# Mai Zaɓen Harshe

Multisite Ultimate: Mai Zaɓen Harshe a lokacin yin rajista

Ƙara fili na zaɓen harshe zuwa tsarin yin rajista.

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Loda 'ultimate-multisite-language-selector' zuwa kundin '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Kunna plugin ta cikin menu na 'Plugins' a cikin WordPress Network Admin ɗinka

## Jagorar Amfani {#usage-guide}

Da wannan add-on na kyauta za ka iya barin masu amfani da kai su zaɓi wane harshe suke son gani a tsarin yin rajista da kuma a shafin shiga.

Bayan [kunna lasisinka](../../user-guide/getting-started/installing-ultimate-multisite.md) za ka iya shigar da add-ons na Ultimate Multisite da lasisinka ya ba ka damar samu.

Je zuwa shafin saitunan Ultimate Multisite ɗinka sannan, a menu na hagu, danna alamar _Add-ons_.

![Shafin Add-ons](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

_Je zuwa shafin Add-ons don fara shigar da Mai Zaɓen Harshe_

A can, za ka sami duk add-ons da muke bayarwa.

Add-ons na kyauta suna samuwa ga duk abokan cinikinmu, ba tare da la’akari da tsarin biyan kuɗin da suka yi rajista da shi ba.

Danna maɓallin _Install it Now!_ da ke ƙasan ƙaramin hoton add-on ɗin. Sannan kunna shi a shafin Plugins.

![Shafin Add-ons tare da maɓallin shigarwa](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

_Danna maɓallin don shigar da add-on ɗin_

<!-- Hoton allo babu: Shafin WP Plugins da ke nuna maɓallin Network Activate don add-on na Language Selector -->

_Kunna shi a shafin Plugins ɗinka_

Yanzu, je zuwa menu na Saituna na Ultimate Multisite kuma shiga shafin _Network Settings_. A can, a ƙasan shafin, za ka sami sabon sashe da ake kira _Languages_.

![Shafin saituna](/img/config/settings-general.png)

_Sabon sashe gaba ɗaya ya bayyana!_

Za ka iya barinsa fanko don nuna duk harsuna a matsayin zaɓuɓɓuka ko zaɓar waɗanda kake son samarwa. Harsunan su ne waɗanda ka riga ka shigar a saitin WordPress ɗinka.

<!-- Hoton allo babu: Jerin zaɓen harshe da ke nuna harsunan da ke samuwa a shigarwar WP -->

_Jerin harsunan da ke samuwa a wannan shigarwar WP_

Bayan adana canje-canjen da ka yi, za a nuna menu na zaɓi tare da harsunan da ka zaɓa a shafin Shiga da kuma a Tsarin Yin Rajista.

![Saitunan shiga da rajista](/img/config/settings-login-registration.png)

_Mai Zaɓen Harshe a Shafin Shiga_

![Shafin rajista](/img/config/registration-page.png)

_Mai Zaɓen Harshe a Tsarin Yin Rajista_

## Saita Harshe ta hanyar URL {#setting-the-language-via-the-url}

Hakanan za ka iya canza harshen shafi ta hanyar wuce parameter na locale zuwa URL. Idan kana son tura zuwa shafin rajista a harshen Italiyanci, misali, ƙara ?locale=it_IT zuwa URL.

[https://yournetwork.com/wp-signup.php?locale=it_IT](https://yournetwork.com/wp-signup.php?locale=it%5FIT)

Dole ne a shigar da harshen a kan cibiyar sadarwar WordPress ɗinka domin wannan ya yi aiki.
