# PayFast {#payfast}

Multisite Ultimate: Kofar Biyan Kuɗi ta Payfast

Yana ƙara Payfast - kofar biyan kuɗi ta kan layi mai sauri da tsaro a Afirka ta Kudu - a matsayin zaɓin biyan kuɗi na Multisite Ultimate.

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Loda 'ultimate-multisite-payfast' zuwa kundin '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Kunna ƙarin ta cikin menu na 'Ƙari' a WordPress
3. Bi Wizard mataki-mataki don saita ƙarin

## Jagorar Amfani {#usage-guide}

PayFast babban mai sarrafa biyan kuɗi ta kan layi ne ga kasuwanci a Afirka ta Kudu. An sadaukar da shi wajen taimaka wa ’yan kasuwar kan layi su bunƙasa ta hanyar sa ma’amaloli su zama marasa cikas kuma amintattu. Bisa buƙatar mai amfani da Ultimate Multisite, mun yanke shawarar ƙirƙirar ƙari da zai ba da damar haɗawa da PayFast. Wannan labarin zai nuna yadda ake saita PayFast a matsayin kofar biyan kuɗinka don karɓar biyan kuɗi a cikin cibiyar sadarwar Ultimate Multisite ɗinka.

Mataki na farko shi ne shigar da ƙarin.

# Yadda ake shigar da ƙarin Payfast: {#how-to-install-the-payfast-add-on}

Je zuwa shafin ƙare-ƙare kuma zaɓi ƙarin Ultimate Multisite: Kofar Biyan Kuɗi ta Payfast.

![Shafin ƙare-ƙare](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

Bayan an gama shigarwa, za a mayar da kai zuwa shafin ƙare-ƙare. A nan, kawai danna Kunna a Cibiyar Sadarwa kuma za a kunna ƙarin _Payfast_ a kan cibiyar sadarwarka.

# Saita PayFast {#setting-up-payfast}

Bayan shigar da ƙarin, yanzu za ka iya zuwa saitunan Ultimate Multisite ka fara daidaita PayFast.

A ƙarƙashin shafin **saitunan Ultimate Multisite**, za ka iya samun shafin "Biyan Kuɗi". A ƙarƙashin wannan shafin, ya kamata ka ga "**Payfast** " tare da maɓallin kunnawa/kashewa don kunna ko kashe wannan kofar biyan kuɗi.

![Saitunan kofar biyan kuɗi](/img/config/settings-payment-gateways.png)Ci gaba ka danna maɓallin kunnawa/kashewa don kunna shi. Daga nan zai faɗaɗa akwatin don nuna filaye daban-daban da kake buƙatar cika kamar **Mechant ID, Merchant Key da PassPhrase.** Za ka iya samun wannan bayanin daga Account ɗinka na **Payfast**

<!-- Hoton allo babu: Saitunan PayFast da aka faɗaɗa suna nuna filayen Merchant ID, Merchant Key, da PassPhrase -->

Mataki na gaba shi ne shiga Account ɗinka na PayFast don samun bayanan da kake buƙata.

Da zarar ka shiga, nan take za ka iya ganin takardun shaidar da kake buƙata daga Dashboard na Account ɗinka na **Payfast**.

<!-- Hoton allo babu: Dashboard na Account na PayFast yana nuna takardun shaidar Merchant ID da Merchant Key -->Za ka iya kawai kwafa ka liƙa takardar shaidar sannan ka cika bayanan a saitunan Ultimate Multisite ɗinka. Sannan danna maɓallin **"Ajiye saituna** " don adana sauye-sauyen.

<!-- Hoton allo babu: Saitunan Ultimate Multisite PayFast tare da cike takardun shaidar da maɓallin Ajiye -->

Shi ke nan, haɗin biyan kuɗin PayFast ɗinka ya kammala!
