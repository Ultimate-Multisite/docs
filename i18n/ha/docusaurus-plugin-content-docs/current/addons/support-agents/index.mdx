# Wakilan Taimako

Gudanar da cikakkiyar hanyar sadarwa kai kaɗai ba aiki ne mai sauƙi ba. Yayin da hanyar sadarwarka ta fara girma, za ka fara ganin ƙaruwar buƙatar tallafi. Zai dace ka kawo mutane da za su taimaka maka rage nauyin aikinka. Duk da haka, ba da cikakken damar shiga/izini na da haɗari kuma zai iya jefa hanyar sadarwarka cikin matsala. A nan ne wannan ƙarin Support Agent yake shigowa.

Wannan ƙarin yana ba ka damar ƙirƙirar sabon nau'in mai amfani na WordPress da ake kira Support Agent, kuma yana ba ka cikakken iko kan irin izinin da wannan mai amfani yake da shi. Ba da izini yana da sauƙi kamar kunna maɓallin toggle, kuma kashe shi idan ka yanke shawarar cire shi.

## Fasali {#features}

- Jerin da aka rarrabe na izinin WordPress & Ultimate Multisite
- Kunna & kashe damar shiga cikin sauƙi
- Ƙirƙiri adadi mara iyaka na asusun Agent

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Loda 'ultimate-multisite-support-agents' zuwa kundin '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Kunna ƙarin ta hanyar menu na 'Plugins' a cikin WordPress
3. Bi Wizard na mataki-mataki don saita ƙarin
