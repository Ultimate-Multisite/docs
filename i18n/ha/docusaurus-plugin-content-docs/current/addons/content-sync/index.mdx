# Content Sync

Multisite Ultimate: Content Sync

Wannan addon don Multisite Ultimate yana ba masu gudanar da hanyar sadarwa damar daidaita abun ciki daga kowanne shafi zuwa wasu shafuka a cikin hanyar sadarwar. Yana goyon bayan duk nau'o'in rubutu ciki har da rubuce-rubuce, shafuka, nau'o'in rubutu na al'ada, da Elementor templates. Addon ɗin ya haɗa da keɓaɓɓen wizard mai matakai da yawa don zaɓar abun ciki da shafukan da ake nufi, tare da sarrafa rukuni-rukuni da martanin ci gaba a ainihin lokaci.

## Fasali {#features}

* Zaɓin Abun Ciki: Tsarin katuna na gani tare da thumbnails da tacewar nau'in rubutu
* Duk Nau'o'in Rubutu: Daidaita rubuce-rubuce, shafuka, kayayyaki, Elementor templates, da kowanne nau'in rubutu na al'ada
* Zaɓin Shafi: Zaɓi shafukan da za a daidaita abun ciki zuwa gare su, tare da cire shafin tushe da zaɓin shafukan 'ya'ya tun farko
* Sarrafa Rukuni-rukuni: Yana daidaitawa zuwa shafuka 10 a lokaci guda tare da sabunta ci gaba
* Daidaita Media: Yana kwafa da sake tsara fayilolin media (hotuna, bidiyoyi) ta atomatik daga abun cikin rubutu zuwa shafukan da ake nufi
* Daidaita Taxonomy: Yana kiyaye rukuni-rukuni, tags, da taxonomies na al'ada
* Goyon bayan Elementor: Cikakken goyon baya ga Elementor templates tare da sake samar da CSS da sake taswirar template ID
* Magance Rikici: Zaɓi don maye gurbin abun ciki da ke akwai ko tsallake shi
* Gudanar da Cache: Yana share Elementor cache ta atomatik a shafukan da ake nufi bayan daidaitawa

## Bukatu {#requirements}

* WordPress 5.3 ko sama da haka
* PHP 7.4 ko sama da haka
* Multisite Ultimate plugin (mai aiki)
* Elementor plugin (na zaɓi, ana buƙata don daidaita Elementor template)

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Loda 'ultimate-multisite-content-sync' zuwa kundin '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Kunna plugin ta cikin menu na 'Plugins' a WordPress Network Admin ɗinka
3. Saita saituna a Ultimate Multisite → Settings → Content Sync
4. Je zuwa Ultimate Multisite → Content Sync don fara daidaitawa
