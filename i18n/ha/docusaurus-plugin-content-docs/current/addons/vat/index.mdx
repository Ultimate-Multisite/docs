# VAT

Multisite Ultimate: VAT na Turai

Yana ƙara ƙarin fasali da kayan aiki ga tsohon sarrafa haraji na Multisite Ultimate don inganta goyon bayan VAT na Turai.
Yana ƙara ƙimar EU VAT ta atomatik zuwa shafin Ƙimar Haraji daga

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Loda 'ultimate-multisite-vat' zuwa directory na '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Kunna plugin ta cikin menu na 'Plugins' a WordPress
3. Je zuwa Ultimate Multisite > Saituna > Haraji kuma kunna EU VAT.
4. Je ka danna kan Ƙimar Haraji sannan ka Sabunta Ƙimar EU VAT a ƙasa kuma ka Ajiye Ƙimar Haraji.
5. An gama!
