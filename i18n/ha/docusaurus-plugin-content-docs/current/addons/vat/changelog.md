# Tarihin Canje-canjen VAT {#vat-changelog}

Sigar 1.0.7 - An fitar a ranar 2026-02-03

* Gyara: Lambobin VAT na Jamus (DE) da ke gaza tabbatarwa saboda kurakuran MS_MAX_CONCURRENT_REQ na sabis ɗin VIES SOAP. An sauya daga tsohon SOAP API zuwa EU VIES REST API na hukuma domin tabbatarwa mafi aminci.
* Gyara: Yanzu ana karɓar lambobin VAT lokacin da sabis ɗin VIES bai samu na ɗan lokaci ba, maimakon a ƙi su ba daidai ba. Ana ƙirƙirar shigarwar rajista don dubawa daga baya.
* Gyara: Filin VAT/Tax ID yanzu yana bayyane ga duk ƙasashe, ba kawai membobin EU ba. Ana adana Non-EU tax IDs (misali lambobin Swiss CHE) don nuna su a takardar lissafi ba tare da tabbatarwar VIES ba.
* Gyara: Neman prefix na VAT don Girka (GR), Monaco (MC), da Isle of Man (IM) ya lalace saboda kuskuren dabarar binciken array.
* Ingantawa: An cire dogaro na ci gaba na wp-cli-bundle marar buƙata wanda zai iya haifar da manyan kurakurai idan aka yi amfani da shi tare da addon na Site Exporter.
* Ingantawa: An ƙara cikakken tarin gwajin unit (gwaje-gwaje 53).

Sigar 1.0.6 - An fitar a ranar 2026-01-25

* Gyara: Ma'amalolin B2B na cikin gida yanzu suna cajin VAT daidai. Bisa ƙa'idodin VAT na EU, reverse charge yana aiki ne kawai ga ma'amalolin B2B na ketare, ba lokacin da ƙasar abokin ciniki ta yi daidai da ƙasar kamfanin ba.

Sigar 1.0.5 - An fitar a ranar 2026-01-22

* Gyara: Addon ba ya loda ko aiki yadda ya kamata.
* Canji: An sauya tushen bayanan ƙimar VAT daga euvatrates.com zuwa ma'ajiyar ibericode/vat-rates don bayanai mafi aminci kuma da ake kula da su sosai.
* Gyara: An gyara kuskuren rubutu na darajar zaɓin super_reduced_rates.
* Ingantawa: An ƙara binciken null don sarrafa lambar ƙasa domin hana kurakurai.
* Canji: An cire fayilolin fassara da aka haɗa don fifita sabuntawa ta atomatik ta hanyar Traduttore.
