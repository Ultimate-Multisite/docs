# Site Exporter {#site-exporter}

## Abun ciki {#content}

Mun fahimci buƙatar samun damar motsa ƙananan shafuka a cikin cibiyar sadarwarka zuwa shigarwar shafi guda, ko akasin haka idan abokin cinikinka ya yanke shawarar ƙaura da shafinsa. Ƙarin Site exporter da importer yana sauƙaƙa wannan aiki. Wannan kayan aiki yana fitarwa da shigo da shafuka ta amfani da fayilolin zip waɗanda kuma za su iya zama madadin ajiya.

## Fasali {#features}

* Fitarwa da Shigo da Shafuka

* Fuska mai sauƙin amfani

* Ƙirƙiri madadin ajiyar shafi

## Takardu {#documentation}
**Yadda ake Fitarwa da Shigo da Shafi**

## Shigarwa {#installation}

1. Loda **ultimate-multisite-site-exporter** zuwa kundin **/wp-content/plugins/**
2. Kunna ƙarin ta hanyar menu na **Ƙari** a cikin WordPress
3. Bi Wizard mataki-mataki don saita ƙarin

## Jagorar Amfani {#usage-guide}

Tare da Ultimate Multisite za ka iya fitar da shafi daga cibiyar sadarwarka ko shigo da shafi zuwa shigarwar multisite ko ta shafi guda.

Don fara aikin, je zuwa shafin ƙarinmu kuma ka shigar da ƙarin Ultimate Multisite: Site Exporter & Importer.![Shafin ƙari](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

![Shafin ƙari tare da zaɓin shigarwa](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

Je zuwa shafin Ƙarinka kuma danna don **Kunna a Cibiyar Sadarwa** ƙarin da ka shigar yanzu.<!-- Hoton allo ba ya samuwa: Shafin WP Ƙari yana nuna maɓallin Kunna a Cibiyar Sadarwa don ƙarin Site Exporter -->

Za a iya samun zaɓin **Fitarwa & Shigowa** a kan menu na Ultimate Multisite, a hagu.

<!-- Hoton allo ba ya samuwa: Menu na Ultimate Multisite yana nuna zaɓin Fitarwa da Shigowa -->

## Yadda ake fitar da shafi {#how-to-export-a-site}

Don fitar da shafi daga cibiyar sadarwarka, je zuwa **Fitarwa & Shigowa** a kan menu na Ultimate Multisite kuma, a kan shafin **Fitar da Shafuka**, danna don **Ƙirƙiri sabon Fitarwar Shafi**.

<!-- Hoton allo ba ya samuwa: Shafin Fitar da Shafuka tare da maɓallin Ƙirƙiri sabon Fitarwar Shafi -->

Nemi shafin da kake son fitarwa kuma zaɓi abubuwan da kake son haɗawa a cikin fayil ɗin da aka fitar. Za ka iya haɗa jigogi, ƙari, da lodawa. Idan shafinka ya yi girma sosai, za ka iya zaɓar gudanar da aikin fitarwa a bango.<!-- Hoton allo ba ya samuwa: Zaɓuɓɓukan fitar da shafi tare da neman shafi, akwatunan zaɓi na haɗa jigogi/ƙari/lodawa -->

Danna maɓallin **Fitar da Shafi**. Shafin da ka fitar zai kasance a kan jerin **Fitarwa da ke Akwai**. Danna maɓallin **Zazzage ZIP** don zazzage fayil ɗin. ZIP ɗin fitarwa suna haɗa da `index.php` mai kunna kansa da `readme.txt`, don haka za a iya shigar da su a kan sabon host ba tare da fara shigar da wani ƙarin shigowa daban ba.<!-- Hoton allo ba ya samuwa: Jerin Fitarwa da ke Akwai tare da maɓallin Zazzage ZIP -->

## Yadda ake fitar da dukan cibiyar sadarwa {#how-to-export-the-whole-network}

Don fitar da duk ƙananan shafuka tare, je zuwa **Fitarwa & Shigowa** a kan menu na Ultimate Multisite kuma yi amfani da zaɓin **Fitarwar Cibiyar Sadarwa** a kan allon fitarwa. Rumbun da aka ƙirƙira yana ƙunshe da duk ƙananan shafuka a cikin ZIP guda don ƙaurawar host, sake gina staging, ko madadin ajiya na matakin cibiyar sadarwa.

Fitarwar cibiyar sadarwa na iya zama babba. Gudanar da ita lokacin da zirga-zirga ta yi ƙasa kuma ka tabbatar uwar garken tana da isasshen ajiya don rumbun da duk wani lodawa, ƙari, da jigogi da aka haɗa.

## Yadda ake shigo da shafi {#how-to-import-a-site}

Don shigo da shafi, je zuwa shafin **Shigo da Shafi** kuma loda fayil ɗin .zip na shafin da kake son shigowa da shi. Za ka iya sauya URL zuwa sabo ko ka bar tsohon URL ta hanyar barin sararin a fili. Haka kuma, za ka iya zaɓar akwatin don share fayil ɗin .zip daga shigarwarka bayan shigowa. Sannan danna don **Fara Shigowa**.

<!-- Hoton allo ba ya samuwa: Shafin Shigo da Shafi tare da filin loda zip da maɓallin Fara Shigowa -->

Aikin na iya ɗaukar ɗan lokaci. Za ka ga shigowa da ke jiran aiki a cikin jerin da ke ƙasa da mai shigowa.

<!-- Hoton allo ba ya samuwa: Shigowa da ke jiran aiki a cikin jerin da ke ƙasa da mai shigowa -->

Za ka iya dakatar da aikin ta danna **Soke Shigowa**.

<!-- Hoton allo ba ya samuwa: Maɓallin Soke Shigowa don dakatar da aikin shigowa -->

Bayan aikin ya kammala, shafinka yana nan a shirye a kan cibiyar sadarwarka.

## Yadda ake shigo da shafi a kan shigarwa guda {#how-to-import-a-site-on-a-single-install}

Don shigo da shafi zuwa shigarwarka guda, da farko shigar kuma kunna mai shigowa a kan shafinka. Za ka iya samun hanyar haɗin zazzage shi a kan shafin mai fitarwa.

<!-- Hoton allo ba ya samuwa: Shafin mai fitarwa tare da hanyar haɗin zazzage mai shigowa na shafi guda -->

Za ka iya samun mai shigowa a kan menu na **Kayan aiki**, a hagu, a kan ƙaramin abu **Fitarwa & Shigowa**.

<!-- Hoton allo ba ya samuwa: Menu na Kayan aiki na shafi guda yana nuna ƙaramin menu na Fitarwa da Shigowa -->

Loda fayil ɗin .zip na shafin da kake son shigowa da shi. Za ka iya sauya URL zuwa sabo ko ka bar tsohon URL ta hanyar barin sararin a fili. Haka kuma, za ka iya zaɓar akwatin don share fayil ɗin .zip daga shigarwarka bayan shigowa. Sannan danna don **Fara Shigowa**.

<!-- Hoton allo ba ya samuwa: Fom ɗin shigowa na shafi guda tare da loda zip da maɓallin Fara Shigowa -->

Aikin na iya ɗaukar ɗan lokaci. Za ka ga shigowa da ke jiran aiki a cikin jerin da ke ƙasa da mai shigowa.

<!-- Hoton allo ba ya samuwa: Shigowa da ke jiran aiki na shafi guda a cikin jerin -->

Za ka iya dakatar da aikin ta danna **Soke Shigowa**.

<!-- Hoton allo ba ya samuwa: Maɓallin Soke Shigowa don dakatar da aikin shigowa -->

Bayan aikin ya ƙare, za a maye gurbin shafinka da shafin da ka shigo da shi yanzu.
