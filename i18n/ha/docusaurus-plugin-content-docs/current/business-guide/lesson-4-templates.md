# Darasi na 4: Gina Templates na Musamman

Templates su ne ginshiƙin tayin ƙimarka na musamman. Mai studio na motsa jiki da ya yi rajista kuma ya ga shafin da tuni yake kama da gidan yanar gizon motsa jiki -- tare da jadawalin darussa, bayanan trainers, da hotunan da suka dace -- ya fi yiwuwa ya ci gaba da amfani fiye da wanda ya ga farar takarda babu komai.

## Inda Muka Tsaya {#where-we-left-off}

Muna da network na FitSite mai aiki tare da Ultimate Multisite da aka girka kuma aka daidaita. Yanzu za mu gina templates da suke sa FitSite ya ji kamar an ƙera shi musamman don kasuwancin motsa jiki.

## Me Ya Sa Templates na Musamman Suke Da Muhimmanci {#why-niche-templates-matter}

Templates na gama-gari suna tilasta wa abokan cinikinka su yi aiki mai wahala: gano waɗanne shafuka suke bukata, wane tsarin abun ciki yake aiki, da yadda za a sa ya yi kyau ga masana'antarsu. Templates na musamman suna kawar da wannan cikas.

Mai studio na motsa jiki da ya yi rajista a FitSite ya kamata ya ga:

- Shafin farko da yake kama da gidan yanar gizon studio na motsa jiki
- Shafuka na darussa, jadawali, trainers, da farashi da tuni an ƙirƙira su
- Hotuna da abun ciki na wucin gadi da suka dace da masana'antarsu
- Zane da yake jin ƙwararre kuma a kan alama ga motsa jiki

Suna cike bayanansu. Ba sa farawa daga tushe.

## Shirya Templates ɗinka {#planning-your-templates}

Kafin gini, yanke shawarar waɗanne templates za ka bayar. Don FitSite, za mu ƙirƙiri uku:

### Template 1: Studio Essential {#template-1-studio-essential}

Don ƙananan studios da personal trainers.

- **Shafin farko** tare da hoton hero, fitattun darussa, da kiran daukar mataki
- Shafin **Game da Mu** tare da labarin studio da manufarsa
- Shafin **Darussa** tare da tsarin jadawali
- Shafin **Trainers** tare da katunan bayanan martaba
- Shafin **Tuntuɓa** tare da taswirar wuri da fom
- **Tsabtataccen zane na zamani** tare da tsarin launuka da ya dace da motsa jiki

### Template 2: Gym Pro {#template-2-gym-pro}

Don gyms da suka kafa da ƙarin ayyuka.

- Komai da ke cikin Studio Essential, ƙari da:
- Shafin **Membership** tare da teburin farashi
- Shafin **Gallery** don hotunan wurin
- Sashen **Blog** don shawarwarin motsa jiki da labarai
- Sashen **Testimonials** a shafin farko
- Zaɓuɓɓukan **alama mafi fitowa fili**

### Template 3: Fitness Chain {#template-3-fitness-chain}

Don ayyukan wurare da yawa.

- Komai da ke cikin Gym Pro, ƙari da:
- Shafin **Wurare** tare da jerin wurare da yawa
- Template na ƙaramin shafin **Franchise/wuri**
- **Alamar tsakiya** tare da bayanai na musamman ga kowane wuri
- **Kundin ma'aikata** a duk wurare

## Gina Shafin Template {#building-a-template-site}

A cikin Ultimate Multisite, template kawai shafin WordPress ne da aka daidaita yadda kake son sabbin shafukan abokan ciniki su kasance. Ga yadda ake ƙirƙirar ɗaya:

### Mataki na 1: Ƙirƙiri Shafin Template {#step-1-create-the-template-site}

1. Je zuwa **Sites > Add New** a cikin network admin ɗinka
2. Ƙirƙiri shafi mai suna `template-studio-essential`
3. Wannan shafin zai zama allon aikinka

### Mataki na 2: Girka kuma Daidaita Theme {#step-2-install-and-configure-the-theme}

Sauya zuwa dashboard na shafin template sannan:

1. Kunna theme da ya dace da kasuwancin motsa jiki
2. Daidaita saitunan theme, launuka, da typography
3. Saita header da footer tare da kewayawa da ta dace da motsa jiki

:::tip Theme Selection
Choose a theme that is flexible enough to look good for fitness but not so complex that your customers cannot manage it. Themes like Astra, GeneratePress, or Kadence work well because they are lightweight, customizable, and well-supported.
:::

### Mataki na 3: Ƙirƙiri Shafukan {#step-3-create-the-pages}

Gina kowane shafi da:

- **Abun ciki na wucin gadi** da yake karantuwarsa ta halitta ga motsa jiki ("Barka da zuwa [Studio Name]" ba "Lorem ipsum" ba)
- **Hotunan wucin gadi** daga shafukan hotunan stock na kyauta da ke nuna ayyukan motsa jiki
- **Tsare-tsare masu aiki** ta amfani da page builder ko block editor

Sanya abun ciki na wucin gadi ya zama na koyarwa inda zai yiwu. Maimakon rubutun cike gurbi na gama-gari, rubuta abun ciki kamar: "Maye gurbin wannan da taƙaitaccen bayanin studio ɗinka da abin da yake sa shi na musamman. Ambaci falsafar horonka, shekarun ƙwarewa, ko abin da clients za su iya tsammani."

### Mataki na 4: Daidaita Plugins {#step-4-configure-plugins}

Girka kuma kunna plugins da studios na motsa jiki suke bukata:

- Plugin na booking ko tsara jadawali (idan ya dace da matakin plan ɗinka)
- Plugin na fom ɗin tuntuɓa
- Plugin na SEO (an riga an daidaita shi da tsoffin saituna masu dacewa da motsa jiki)

### Mataki na 5: Yi Masa Alama a Matsayin Template {#step-5-mark-as-template}

1. Je zuwa **Ultimate Multisite > Sites**
2. Gyara shafin template
3. Kunna toggle na **Site Template**

Maimaita wannan tsari ga kowane template da kake son bayarwa.

## Jerin Binciken Ingancin Template {#template-quality-checklist}

Kafin sanya template ya kasance a samu, tabbatar:

- [ ] Duk shafuka suna lodawa daidai kuma suna kama da ƙwararru
- [ ] Abun ciki na wucin gadi yana da amfani kuma na musamman ga niche
- [ ] Hotuna sun dace kuma suna da lasisi yadda ya kamata
- [ ] Amsawar wayar hannu tana aiki a duk shafuka
- [ ] Kewayawa tana da ma'ana kuma cikakkiya ce
- [ ] Fomomin tuntuɓa suna aiki
- [ ] Babu links da suka lalace ko assets da suka ɓace
- [ ] Saurin lodin shafi abin karɓa ne
