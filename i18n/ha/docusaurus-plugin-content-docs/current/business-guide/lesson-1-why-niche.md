# Darasi na 1: Me Ya Sa Niche Ke Nasara {#lesson-1-why-niche-wins}

Kafin mu taba kowace software, kana bukatar ka fahimci dalilin da ya sa ginawa don takamaiman masu sauraro shi ne shawara mafi muhimmanci da za ka yanke.

## Matsalar Sayar da Shafukan Yanar Gizo Na Kowa {#the-problem-with-selling-generic-websites}

Idan ka bayar da "shafukan yanar gizo ga kowa," kana gasa da:

- Wix, Squarespace, da WordPress.com a kan sabis na kai-tsaye
- Dubban freelancers a kan gine-ginen musamman
- Fiverr da Upwork a kan farashi

Ba za ka iya yin nasara a farashi ba. Ba za ka iya yin nasara a fasali ba. Kasuwar shafin yanar gizo na kowa tsere ne zuwa mafi ƙasa.

## Abin da Ke Canjawa Idan Ka Zaɓi Niche {#what-changes-when-you-pick-a-niche}

Idan ka gina platform musamman don fitness studios (ko kowane niche), komai yana juyawa zuwa amfaninka:

### Kana Magance Matsaloli Na Gaskiya {#you-solve-real-problems}

Mai fitness studio ba ya son "shafin yanar gizo." Suna so:

- Jadawalin darussa da ke sabuntawa ta atomatik
- Hanyar da membobi za su iya yin booking na zaman horo online
- Bayanan trainers da ke kama da na ƙwararru
- Haɗawa da booking system ɗinsu
- Shafin da yake kama da na fitness brand, ba generic template ba

Idan platform ɗinka yana magance waɗannan takamaiman matsaloli kai tsaye, ba ka ƙara sayar da shafin yanar gizo ba. Kana sayar da mafita.

### Za Ka Iya Karɓar Kuɗi Fiye da Haka {#you-can-charge-more}

Generic WordPress site na iya sayuwa $500-$2,000 a matsayin aikin sau ɗaya. Niche platform da ke magance matsalolin da suka shafi masana'antu na musamman zai iya karɓar $49-$199/wata a matsayin subscription mai maimaituwa saboda darajar tana ci gaba kuma takamaimai ce.

### Kana Zama Kwararre {#you-become-the-expert}

Idan ka mayar da hankali kan masana'anta guda, kana koyon yarenta, wuraren radadinta, da tsarin sayenta. Masu fitness studio suna tattaunawa da juna. Idan ɗaya ya sami platform da ke aiki, yana gaya wa wasu. Maganar baki a cikin niche tana da ƙarfi saboda al'umma tana da kusanci.

### Tallanka Yana Sauƙaƙa {#your-marketing-gets-easier}

Maimakon "muna gina shafukan yanar gizo ga kasuwanci," ka ce "muna gina shafukan yanar gizo ga fitness studios." Landing page ɗinka yana magana kai tsaye ga masu gym. Templates ɗinka suna kama da fitness sites. Testimonials ɗinka suna zuwa daga trainers da masu studio. Kowane ɓangare na marketing yana fi tasiri saboda yana da takamaimai.

## Damar FitSite {#the-fitsite-opportunity}

Bari mu kalli masana'antar fitness ta wannan hangen:

### Girman Kasuwa {#market-size}

- Akwai fiye da fitness facilities 100,000 a Amurka kaɗai
- Mafi yawansu ƙananan kasuwanci ne masu zaman kansu
- Yawancinsu ko dai ba su da shafin yanar gizo, ko suna da mummunan shafin yanar gizo, ko kuma shafin yanar gizo mai tsada fiye da kima da ba za su iya sabuntawa da kansu ba

### Mafita Na Yanzu {#current-solutions}

- **Generic builders** (Wix, Squarespace): Babu fitness-specific features, masu studio suna wahala wajen gina abin da suke bukata
- **Custom development**: Mai tsada, na sau ɗaya, mai wahalar kulawa
- **Industry-specific platforms**: Kaɗan ne suke akwai, kuma mafi yawansu suna da alaƙa da booking systems maimakon bayar da cikakken kasancewa a yanar gizo

### Gibin {#the-gap}

Akwai fili bayyananne tsakanin "generic website builder" da "expensive custom development" ga fitness businesses. Platform da ke ba masu studio shafin yanar gizo na ƙwararru, na musamman don fitness, tare da fasalolin da suke bukata -- a farashin da ya dace da ƙaramin kasuwanci -- yana cike wannan gibi.

Wannan ita ce damar da FitSite zai nufa.

## Muhimmin Abu da Za a Riƙe {#key-takeaway}

Zaɓar niche ba wai yana nufin ka takaita kanka ba ne. Yana nufin ka mayar da ƙoƙarinka inda zai fi yin tasiri. Niche platform da ke hidimtawa customers 500 a $99/wata kasuwanci ne na $600,000/shekara. Ba ka bukatar miliyoyin customers. Kana bukatar waɗanda suka dace.

## Abin da Muka Gina a Wannan Darasi {#what-we-built-this-lesson}

Har yanzu babu aikin fasaha -- amma yanzu kana da tushen dabaru:

- **Me ya sa niche ke nasara**: takamaimai yana doke gama-gari a daraja, pricing, marketing, da maganar baki
- **Ra'ayin FitSite**: website platform da aka gina musamman don fitness studios
- **Damar kasuwa**: babbar kasuwa da za a iya kaiwa, ba a hidimta mata sosai ba, gibi bayyananne
