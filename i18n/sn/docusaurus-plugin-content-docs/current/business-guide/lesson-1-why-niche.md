# Chidzidzo 1: Nei Musika Wakasarudzika Uchikunda

Tisati tabata chero software, unofanira kunzwisisa kuti nei kuvakira vateereri vakasarudzika chiri chisarudzo chimwe chete chakanyanya kukosha chauchaita.

## Dambudziko Rekutengesa Mawebhusaiti Akajairika {#the-problem-with-selling-generic-websites}

Kana ukapa "mawebhusaiti emunhu wese," uri kukwikwidzana ne:

- Wix, Squarespace, neWordPress.com pakuzvishandira wega
- Zviuru zvevashandi vakazvimirira pakuvaka kwakagadzirirwa mutengi
- Fiverr neUpwork pamutengo

Haugoni kukunda pamutengo. Haugoni kukunda pazvimiro. Musika wemawebhusaiti akajairika imakwikwi ekudzika pasi.

## Chii Chinochinja Paunosarudza Musika Wakasarudzika {#what-changes-when-you-pick-a-niche}

Paunovaka platform yakanyatsogadzirirwa mastudio efitness (kana chero musika wakasarudzika), zvinhu zvese zvinotanga kukufambira:

### Unogadzirisa Matambudziko Chaiwo {#you-solve-real-problems}

Muridzi westudio yefitness haadi "webhusaiti." Anoda:

- Purogiramu yemakirasi inozvigadzirisa yega
- Nzira yekuti nhengo dzibhuke zvikamu online
- Maprofile evarairidzi anotaridzika seane hunyanzvi
- Kubatanidzwa nebooking system yavo
- Saiti inotaridzika seyemhando yefitness, kwete template yakajairika

Kana platform yako ikagadzirisa matambudziko aya chaiwo pakarepo, hausisiri kutengesa webhusaiti. Uri kutengesa mhinduro.

### Unogona Kubhadharisa Zvakawanda {#you-can-charge-more}

Saiti yakajairika yeWordPress inogona kutengeswa ne$500-$2,000 sechirongwa chenguva imwe chete. Platform yemusika wakasarudzika inogadzirisa matambudziko akanangana neindasitiri inogona kubhadharisa $49-$199/month sesubscription inodzokororwa nekuti kukosha kwayo kunoenderera uye kwakanangana nezvinodiwa.

### Unova Nyanzvi {#you-become-the-expert}

Paunotarisa paindasitiri imwe chete, unodzidza mutauro wayo, zvinovarwadza, uye maitiro avo ekutenga. Varidzi vemastudio efitness vanotaurirana. Kana mumwe akawana platform inoshanda, anoudza vamwe. Kuzivana nekukurudzirana mumusika wakasarudzika kune simba nekuti nharaunda yacho yakabatana.

### Kushambadzira Kwako Kunova Nyore {#your-marketing-gets-easier}

Panzvimbo pekuti "tinovakira mabhizinesi mawebhusaiti," unoti "tinovakira mastudio efitness mawebhusaiti." Peji rako rekusvikira rinotaura zvakananga nevaridzi vemagym. Matemplate ako anotaridzika semasaiti efitness. Zvipupuriro zvako zvinobva kuvarairidzi nevaridzi vemastudio. Chikamu chega chega chekushambadzira chinoshanda zvakanyanya nekuti chakanangana nezvinodiwa.

## Mukana weFitSite {#the-fitsite-opportunity}

Ngatitarisei indasitiri yefitness nenzira iyi:

### Kukura Kwemusika {#market-size}

- Kune nzvimbo dzefitness dzinodarika 100,000 muUnited States chete
- Ruzhinji rwacho mabhizinesi madiki ane varidzi vakazvimirira
- Mazhinji haana webhusaiti, ane webhusaiti yakaipa, kana webhusaiti inodhura zvakanyanya yavasingagoni kugadzirisa vega

### Mhinduro Dzazvino {#current-solutions}

- **Vavaki vakajairika** (Wix, Squarespace): Hapana zvimiro zvakanangana nefitness, varidzi vemastudio vanonetseka kuvaka zvavanoda
- **Kuvandudza kwakagadzirirwa mutengi**: Kunodhura, kwekamwe chete, kwakaoma kuchengetedza
- **Maplatform akanangana neindasitiri**: Mashoma aripo, uye mazhinji akasungirirwa kuma booking system pane kupa kuvapo kwakazara pawebhu

### Mukaha {#the-gap}

Pane mukaha wakajeka pakati pe"muvaki wewebhusaiti akajairika" ne"kuvandudza kwakagadzirirwa mutengi kunodhura" kumabhizinesi efitness. Platform inopa varidzi vemastudio webhusaiti ine hunyanzvi, yakanyatsogadzirirwa fitness, ine zvimiro zvavanoda -- pamutengo une musoro kubhizinesi diki -- inozadza mukaha iwoyo.

Uyu ndiwo mukana uchatangwa neFitSite.

## Pfungwa Huru Yekutora {#key-takeaway}

Kusarudza musika wakasarudzika hakusi kuzviganhurira. Kuri pamusoro pekuisa simba rako parinobatsira zvikuru. Platform yemusika wakasarudzika inoshandira vatengi 500 pa$99/month ibhizinesi re$600,000/year. Haudi mamiriyoni evatengi. Unoda vakakodzera.
