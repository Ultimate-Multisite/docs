# Chirevo cheZvirongwa

Peji rino rinotevera zvirongwa zvinokanganisa manejimendi ezuva nezuva uye shanduko dzichangobva kuitika mumaitiro muUltimate Multisite.

## Dzimwe Sarudzo {#other-options}

Nzvimbo ye**Dzimwe Sarudzo** inoonekwa pasi pe**Ultimate Multisite > Zvirongwa > Kupinda & Kunyoresa**.

| Zvirongwa | Tsananguro |
|---|---|
| **Batidza Jumper** | Inoratidza chishandiso cheJumper chekufamba nekukurumidza munzvimbo yeadmin. Chishandise kusvetukira zvakananga kumasikirini eUltimate Multisite, zvinhu zvenetwork, uye nzvimbo dzeadmin dzinotsigirwa. Chidzime kana usingadi kuti nzira iyi pfupi ionekwe. |

## Kutaura zvikanganiso uye telemetry {#error-reporting-and-telemetry}

Zvirongwa zvekare zvekubvuma kutaura zvikanganiso zvabviswa papeji rezvirongwa. Telemetry isingazivikanwi yakadzimwa uye hapana chinja cheUI chekuiibatidza.

Kana uchichengeta runbooks dzemukati kana screenshots dzepeji rezvirongwa, bvisa mareferensi kufield yekare yekubvuma kutaura zvikanganiso kuitira kuti maadministrator asatsvage zvirongwa zvisisipo.

## Zvirongwa zveImport/Export {#importexport-settings}

Tab yezvirongwa zve**Import/Export** inotsanangura zvirongwa zvainodzora uye inobatanidza zvakananga ku**Ultimate Multisite > Kutumira Saiti Kunze** kuitira maarchive esaiti nenetwork. Shandisa tab yezvirongwa pakugadzirisa import/export, shandisa **Zvishandiso > Kutumira Kunze & Kupinza** pakufambiswa kwebasa rekutumira/kuisa pasaiti imwe chete, uye shandisa chishandiso cheKutumira Saiti Kunze paunoda archive yakazara yeNetwork Export.

## Yambiro yebalansi yeDomain Seller HostAfrica {#domain-seller-hostafrica-balance-warning}

Kana addon yeDomain Seller yakabatidzwa kuHostAfrica, maadministrator enetwork zvino vanoona yambiro inobvisika yekuti balansi yakaderera kana balansi yeaccount yereseller yakanyanya kuderera kuti kunyoresa kana kuvandudza domain kuve kwakavimbika.

Bata chiziviso ichi seyambiro yekushanda: wedzera balansi yeHostAfrica reseller usati wagamuchira mamwe manyoreso edomain akabhadharwa, wobva wadzokera kuzvirongwa zveDomain Seller kana kusikirini yekutarisa domain kuti usimbise kuti manyoreso nekuvandudzwa zvinogona kuenderera mberi senguva dzose.

## Zvirongwa zvekubatanidza mupi weAI {#ai-provider-connector-settings}

Zvirongwa zvekubatanidza mupi weAI zvino zvinoratidza chete mapool emaaccount eOAuth anotsigirwa:

| Mupi | Mafambiro ekumisikidza |
|---|---|
| **Anthropic Max** | Batanidza account imwe kana dzakawanda dzeAnthropic Max nebhatani reOAuth. Shandisa nzira yekutsiva yeOAuth yemanyorero kana browser iri sandboxed isingakwanisi kupedza redirect otomatiki. |
| **OpenAI ChatGPT/Codex** | Batanidza maaccount eChatGPT kuburikidza nemafambiro ebasa akafanana epool yeOAuth. Mabasa anotsigirwa neconnector anogona kushandisa kudanwa kwezvishandiso zveChatGPT Codex mushure mekunge account yabatanidzwa. |
| **Google AI Pro** | Batanidza maaccount eGoogle AI Pro kuburikidza neOAuth, wozorodza connector kana rondedzero yemaaccount isingagadziridzwi pakarepo. |

Cursor Pro haisisiri mupi anotsigirwa. Bvisa screenshots dzemukati dzekare, runbooks, kana nhanho dzekutanga dzinotaura nezvefields dzekumisikidza Cursor Pro kana nzira dzeconnector.

Paunowedzera kana kubvisa maaccount emupi, isa kero yeemail inoshanda yeaccount iri kuzorodzwa kana kubviswa uye chengetedza zvirongwa zvemupi usati waedza mabasa anotsigirwa neconnector.
