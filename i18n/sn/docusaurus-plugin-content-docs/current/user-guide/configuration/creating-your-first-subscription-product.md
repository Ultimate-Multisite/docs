## Kuenzanisa Products (Adding Products) {#managing-products}

Kana uri kuenzanisa plan, package, kana service yakagadzirwa, unogona kuitira iye kupi **Ultimate Multisite > Products > Add Product**.

![Add Product button](/img/config/product-add-button.png)

Mhando inotevera inoratidza mabhara maviri makakosha. Pamakore, pane tabu dzakawanda dzinobatsira kuenzanisa product uye pamakore, pane mabara mamakore anotsanangura mari yakapfuma ya product, iye kana iye inenge yakati (active state), uye panyama ya product.

![Product edit page overview](/img/config/product-edit-full.png)

### Description (Chinyorwa) {#adding-products}

Mabhari ekurisa product anogona kuenzwa nekubuda zvinangwa reproduct uye chinyorwa chekugadzirwa. Izvi zvinhu zviri kunge kudzidziswa pamwe neproduct information inoshandiswa, seplan uye mari, invoices (invoices), upgrades (kuwedzera), uye zvinhu zvakawanda.

![Product description section](/img/config/product-description.png)

### Pricing Type (Mhando Yekurisa Mari) {#description}

Pamakore pane mari ekurisa anogona kuenzwa pamwe neinterface.

![Pricing and save section](/img/config/product-pricing-save.png)

Ultimate Multisite inoshandisa mari mamakore maviri. Mhando ya **paid** inokumbira administrator we network kuti akubatsire nemabhari ekurisa ya product uye nguva yakagadzirwa yekugadzirisa (billing frequency).

### Pricing (Kukurisa Mari) {#pricing-type}

#### Up & Downgrades (Kutanga neKupfuura/Kupfuura) {#delete}

Iri tab iyi rinore kana zvinoreva kuti upano uye kupfuura (upgrade) zvichiri kuita kune mutenda wese wekuti munhu akaita mupfiro wake wose.

Kuti uone iwe izvi, unogona kuona nzira yakasiyana dzinobva pfungwa yakaita seunoda kuti Ultimate Multisite inenge inopa zvinoreva kuratidza (learning management solutions) kune mutenda wakasiyana-siyane. Kuti zvinhu izvi zvinhu (Basic, Plus, uye Premium) zvakwanisa kuonekwa, zvinhu izvi zvakagadzirwa uye plugins dzakasiyana dzinobva kuti dzine mupfiro wese (see later in this section for instructions on how to activate plugins).

Kana Ultimate Multisite inenge inosanganisira websites dzehunhu kana eCommerce, izvi zvinoreva kuti zvinhu izvi (plans) zvakatora plugins dzakasiyana dzinobva kuti dzine mupfiro wose.

Kuti zvinhu izvi zvinhu zvakaita sei, zvakange kuva kune kunyanya uye kunge kuva mushonga kana kuita zvinoreva kuti mutenda wekuti anoda kuratidza (eLearning) akwanise kupfuura kune plans dzeecommerce, nekuti zvinhu izvi, mupfiro wese, uye kufamba kwavo haizvo.

Nekuti kuti uone nzira ya mutenda uye kuti usawane incidents, administrator wekuti network anogona kuita plan group uye munzira iyi (within that group) kuita zvinhu izvi plans dzinobva kuti mutenda akwanise kupfuura.

![Up and Downgrades tab](/img/config/product-upgrades.png)

Kuti uone plan group, unogona kuita **plan group** list yakaita plans dzakasiyana dzinobva (compatible plans). **Product order** inotarisira kuti plans dzine mupfiro wose dzine kuteerwa sei kubva pamweya kuno kupfuura.

Ultimate Multisite inosanganisirawowo **order bump**, unogona kuita zvinhu izvi (add-on products and services) dzinobva kuti dzine kugadzirwa kana zvinoreva kuti zvinhu izvi zvinogone kuwedzera plans. Izvi zinopetswa kune mutenda sezvinhu zvakawanda zvichiri kubva pamweya kuno kupfuura kana mumwe munhu akaita checkout.

#### Price Variations (Kutanga kwemupfiro) {#product-options}

Zuvaure dzinovhu dzinovhu (Price variations) kuno vadhiri administrator wekudzidzwa kuti aive zvinhu zvakasiyana dzinogona kuita kune nguva dzakasiyana. Izvi zvinokubvira kuti munenge uchipa pfungwa dzakare dzine zvinhu zvakasiyana dzinogone kuwera (billing periods) izvo, sekuita kuti chishandisiwe mwezi, kwemwezi, mwaka, kana nguva yose. Zvinogona kuita iwe product yakapfuma ne$29.99/mwezi uye kune option ya mwaka inotevera ne$249.99/mwaka.

![Price Variations tab](/img/config/product-price-variations-tab.png)

Kuti ushure vari price variations, shandisa toggle **Enable Price Variations** kuti iwe upfungwa (active) uye chibvumirano chekuti uingone kuita button **Add new Price Variation**.

![Product price variations](/img/config/product-price-variations.png)

Kuti ushure variation, shandisa nguva (duration), period, uye price ya variation iwe. Unogona kuita variation dzose dzinotevera nekuita buttonwo.

Zvinogona kuita iwe product yakapfuma ne$29.99/mwezi, unogona kuita izvi:

  * **3 Months** ne$79.99 (discount ndiri diki kune mwezi)
  * **1 Year** ne$249.99 (discount yakawanda kune kutenda mwaka)

:::tip Kuita Billing Period Toggle pa Frontend

Price variations chete hazvanyisa toggle kana switch kufrontend checkout. Kuti munenge uchipa kuti vagare zvinhu zvakasiyana dzinogone kuwera (billing periods) (semuenzaniso Monthly / Annual), unofanira kuita **Period Selection** field mucheckout form yako. Shandisa [Checkout Forms: Adding a Period Selection Toggle](checkout-forms#adding-a-period-selection-toggle) kuti uone mari dzakare dzinogona kuita zvinhu.
:::

#### Taxes (Kuteka) {#general}

Tab ya **Taxes** inosimbisa nezvinhu zvinotevera zvetaxes zvakasiyana zvakakosha mumapuro dzakasiyana mu **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Taxes** uye zvinhu zvakasiyana dzinogone kuita. Kuti ushure taxes uye ufanane rates dzakasiyana dzinogone kuita, unofanira kuona documentation pa **Ultimate Multisite: Settings**.

![Taxes tab](/img/config/product-taxes.png)

Mune chero, munhu wese anogona kuona kuti mutapfuirei. Mu nzira yakare, tikatanga rathi rate ya kudhanisa (tax rate) ya 7.25% inenge inogona kutangwa kune vanhu vachiti California (United States of America).

Ngeva rate ya kudhanisa iwe ine definira mu **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Manage Tax Rates**, inogona kuoneka neproduct.

![Taxes tab](/img/config/product-taxes.png)

Kuti utanise kuti product inenge inoshandiswa kune kudhanisa, ndichivaka (set) toggle ya **Is Taxable** iwe ine active uye utange rate ya kudhanisa yakakosha kubva mu dropdown ya Tax Category.

#### Site Templates {#up--downgrades}

Mune chokwadi, site templates ndiyo WordPress websites dzakakwana kuti dzinogone kuonekwa kune site ya munhu apo anowanikira (customer) panguva achatanga subscription yavo.

![Site Templates tab](/img/config/product-site-templates.png)

Administrator we network anokera uye anokera template site yakachengetedza seWordPress site yakare, nethemes, plugins, uye content dzakakosha dzinogone kuonekwa. Template site inogone kuonekwa zvakare (verbatim) kune munhu apo anowanikira.

Iye tab inogona kunge administrator we network anokera kuti aite chii kana iweya template sites zvinogona kuonekwa panguva kubva nesubscription yenyu. Kuti utange kuda kuita zvakanaka nemapurovedzi (site templates) ano, divaka toggle ya **Allow Site Templates** iwe ine active state.

Ngeva **Allow Site Templates** inenge inotarisirwa (disabled), vanhu vachiti panguva yenyu havanogone kuita chii kana vanoita zvinhu yakare kunge zvinogona kuita templates, pane kuti checkout form, shareable link, kana URL parameter dzinekuita templates. Ultimate Multisite inenge inokera iwe limit iye kuburikidza nechaina (fallback chain) pakati peentry points dzakanyorwa: plan settings dzine kuchekwa kune chinhu chiri chinokwanisa, ndipo pane settings ya checkout-form template, uye hapana templates dzinogone kuonekwa kana zvinogone kupewa neURL. Izvi zvinhu zvinoita kuti limits dzepiani dzine kuendesa (consistent) uye inopfuura kuti templates hazvane kuonekwa kune products dzinogona kuita iye.

**site template selection mode** inobva kunoreva chii kana behavior ya site templates panguva yenyu subscription.

Iri **D** **Default** setting inotenda mazuva anokuti unenge uonekwa mu checkout form. Kana kuti administrator we network akavandisa dingindiro dzeutapuro (template selection step) mu prosesi ye checkout uye kana dingindiro iye yakavandiswa netemplates, izvi zvinoregerera zvinhu zvakaita zvedingindiro dzine templates.

Kuti **A** **Assign Site Template** inenge unenge uchivandisa, izvi zvinoregerera kukosha kwetemplate iye yakavandiswa. Izvi zvinoregerera kuti dingindiro dzeutapuro dzine ku checkout process dzisave kuondwa.

Mune nguva yakare, **C** **Choose Available Site Templates** inenge inenge inobata zvinhu zvetemplates dzakavandiswa mu checkout step netemplates dzine kukosha mu setting iye. Unogona kudzidza template imwe yakavandiswa kuti uite chiro kune mutenda wekuti munhu akumbatsirwa kuita uchigadzira.

Muviri, kana administrator we network anoda kuti dingindiro dzeutapuro dzine ku checkout steps dzizove kuonekwa, setting ye ' _default_ ' inenge inokosha. Nekuti chii chinobva kune kukoresa ' _assign new template_ ' kana ' _choose available site templates_ ' zvinoregerera kukosha kwetemplates dzakavandiswa mu plan settings kuti mutenda wekuti dingindiro dzeutapuro dzine ku checkout steps dzizove kuonekwa.

#### Visits {#taxes}

Tabo ye **Visits** inenge yakaita chete kubva kune system ye limitations yaUltimate Multisite. Setting iye rinoregerera kuti mutenda wekuti munhu anogona kuenda (unique visitors) kwenye site yavo uye kusimudzira kweve (throttling) kwemutenda weve wese.

![Visits tab](/img/config/product-visits.png)

Kusina kupiwe kune marketing perspective, administrators ve network vanogona kuenda nemazuva anokuti vakwanise kuita iye nzira yekutsvaga makuberekwa kuti mamwe mutenda (customers) vave vachireva plan yavo. Iye setting inozvionawo kunogona kuita kune administrator we network kuti akupfume uye akapfume zvinhu zvinokosha kubatana nekupera kupfuura makaitiro ekutanga, uye kuti akubatsire kuramba zvinhu zvesystem zvakakosha.

Kuti unogone kuita iye feature, ndichida uingise **limit unique visits** toggle kune state yawo (active) uye utore kunzwisisa makore anokosha ekutanga mumapuro dziri munzira dzinobva mu **unique visits quota** field.

Ngekuramba limit iyi, Ultimate Multisite haizovada site yavo kubva nekuti inozvada message yakatidzidzera kuti limits zvakakosha zvakazara.

#### Users {#site-templates}

Kusarudza zvinokosha zve 'Users' mu Ultimate Multisite kunoita kuti administrator we network anogone kuita limit pamusoro pe kuita uye kuita users vachireva roles.

![Users tab](/img/config/product-users.png)

Kuti unogone kuita feature ya limits, ndichida uingise **limit user** toggle kune state yawo (active) nekuvaka kune dzamba dze-right.

Mune zvinhu zvakawanda zvinokosha kuti vave vachireva roles, ndichida uningise toggle inozvionawo pangu nekuita active uye utore limit yakawanda mumapuro anobva munzira dzinobva dziri mweya.

#### Post Types {#sites}

Tabo ya **Post Types** inogona kuita kuti administrator we network akupfume zvinhu zvinokosha pamusoro pe post types dzakawanda dzinowanikwa mu WordPress.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Post Types tab -->

Kuti WordPress inenge yakagadzirwa sei, posts uye post types ndizvinhu zvakakosha zvinobva mumweya wecore (core functionality), uye iye system ya limits ya Ultimate Multisite inogadzirwa kuti ibatsire administrator we network kuita uye kuramba zvinhu zvelimits.

Kuti unogone kuita subsystem yey limits iyi, ndichida uingise **limit post types** toggle kune state yawo (active) nekuvaka kune dzamba dze-right.

Kuti, kuenda kunei, kuti kwa kila aina ya post (post type) utapereka, chibvumira (toggle) kuti uwe mufanana nekuva kune upenyu (sliding it to the right) uye utipa limitino (maximum upper limit) mu chivimbo chinotevera.

#### Disk Space (Maviri Eruvo) {#visits}

Chivimbo che**Disk Space** chinopfungwa nekubatsira vatsara vanopereka kuti vave nemakore anogona kuva nekuvaka space.

![Disk Space tab](/img/config/product-disk-space.png)

Kune WordPress multisite, zvinhu zvinotevera (core files) zvichagadzirwa nekuti zviri panyanya kune mabhizinesi onosei, uye maunyangwe anogadzirwa kuti media files uye uploads zvakakosha, uye ndiyo pano zvinhu izvi zvinopfungwa nekuvaka limitino.

Kuti utange kuita limitino rekuti space inogona kutangwa, shandisa chivimbo che**limit disk size per site** kuti uwe mufanana nekuva kune upenyu (slide it to the right).

Zvedzo, shandisa chivimbo che**disk space allowance** kuti utipa limitino rekuti space inogona kuva pangu megabytes.

#### Themes (Maviri Eruvo) {#post-types}

Chivimbo che**Themes** chiri munzvimbo dzinopfungwa dzakawanda (product options), uye administrator we network anogona kuita kuti themes zvine kugona kune vatsara kuti vave vachigadzirwa nekuti vave vanogona kutamba, uye unogonawo utsvaka iwe state ya theme.

![Themes tab](/img/config/product-themes.png)

_**Chii: Kuti themes zvine kugona kune vatsara kuti vave vachigadzirwa, administrator we network anofanira kuva nevanhu.**_

![Network themes page](/img/config/product-themes-network-enabled.png)

Chivimbo che**visibility** chinobvunza kuti iwe theme inogone kune mwana wako kana uine kuona tab ya **Appearance > Themes** mu site yake. Kusvika chivimbo ichi kuita kuti iwe uwe **Hidden**, kunokera iwe utapera kutamba theme uye ndiyo unopara kuratidza nekubvumira.

![Themes tab](/img/config/product-themes.png)

Chivimbo che**behavior** chinopfungwa nekuti administrator we network anogona kuita kuti state ya theme iwe ine kana mwana wako akatanga site yake.

Muitawezi wese, mweya we (theme) unogona kuwanzika nekuva kwawe chete. Kwa upande we, iwe unengei kunge kuva kwawe chete. Pamwe, chine cha **Force Activate** chinogona kudzidza uye kuva kwawe chete, zvichizara kuti iwe iwe ine mweya wese (default) kana iweita site.

#### Plugins {#disk-space}

Sei chine chine nemweya we Themes, Ultimate Multisite inozova kunenge administrator we network anogona kuenda kutaura kuti plugins dziri kuone kana dzinenge dzakavara nekuva kwecustomers kana mune kuratidza kwenye yese.

![Plugins tab](/img/config/product-plugins.png)

Chine chine **visibility** chinogona kunge kupfungwa (visible) kana kuitwa kuone (hidden) nekuti iwe inoratidzorwa necustomers kana munenge unoratidza kwenye yese kubva mu Plugins menu.

Administrator we network anogona kuenda kutaura zvakanyanya nezvinhu zvinoreva plugins nekuda kweoptions mumweya we behavior.

![Plugins tab](/img/config/product-plugins.png)

Chine chine **Default** chinomira iwe mweya we plugin inenge yakaita kana iweita site yacho (site template). Izvi zvinoreva kuti plugins dzakavara mumweya wese dzinogona kuenda kutaura kana iweita clone ya template iwe.

Chine chine **Force Activate** chinogona kuva mweya we plugin yakaita kana iweita site, uye chine **Force Inactivate** chinogona kunengeiwe (deactivate) plugin kana iweita site. Mune zvinhu izvi zvinobva, iwe unogona kuenda kutaura mweya we plugin nekuva yakaita kana yakavara nekuti iweita WordPress Plugins menu yenyu.

Sikati ya **Force Activate & Lock** inogona zvakasiyana asi inopara kupera kuti mutsara we plugin ushurewe nekuva mu state yake. Saka, kungava kuna setting ya Force Activate and Lock inogona kusarudza plugin kune state yake ye active uye kunopara kupera kuti mutsara usave neactive. Vachishandisa zvakasiyana, setting ya **Force Inactivate & Lock** inogona kusarudza plugin kune state yake ye inactive uye kunopara kuti mutsara usave neactive.

Mudzidzisi we network anogona kuona kuti setting ya Force Activate & Lock na Force Inactivate & Lock kubva pfungwa nemapuro (site templates) ekuti zvinhu zviri kugadzirwa se plugins uye states dzine plugin dzinogone kutamburwa nesettings iyi kana zvinosarudzirwa.

#### Kusarudza Mhinduro (Reset Limitations) {#custom-domain}

Tabwo ya **Reset Limitations** inosarudza zvakasiyana kuti zvinhu zvakagadzirwa pamusoro pe product zvose. Kuti usarudze limitations, ndichida uchitambura button ya **reset limitations**.

![Reset Limitations tab](/img/config/product-reset-limitations.png)

Kuti utiwarise kuti chine zvakasiyana, ndichida ukumbira toggle ya **confirm reset** kuita state yayo ye active pamwe nekuita button ya **reset limitations**.
