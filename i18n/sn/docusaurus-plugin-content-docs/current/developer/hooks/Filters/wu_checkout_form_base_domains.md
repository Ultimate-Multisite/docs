# Sefa: wu_checkout_form_base_domains

Inosefa ma base domains akagovaniswa echeckout-form asingafaniri kuva marekodhi e mapped-domain.

Shandisa sefa iyi kana integration ichipa mamwe ma base domains eminda yecheckout form **Site URL**. Domains anodzoserwa nesefa iyi anobatwa sema hosts ekunyoresa akagovaniswa panzvimbo pema custom domains esaiti imwe neimwe.

## Maparameter

| Zita | Rudzi | Tsananguro |
|------|------|-------------|
| $domains | `array` | Ma base domains akagovaniswa akaunganidzwa kubva mukugadziriswa kwecheckout form. |

### Kubva

- 2.13.0

### Kwakabva

Yakatsanangurwa mu `inc/functions/domain.php`.


## Zvinodzoka

Array yema base domains echeckout-form akaitwa normalized.
