# GoCardless {#gocardless}

Multisite Ultimate: Kubhadhara neGoCardless

Bvumira vatengi vako kubhadhara unhuweUltimate Multisite nezvigadzirwa neDirect Debit vachishandisa GoCardless!

## Kuiswa {#installation}

1. Isa 'ultimate-multisite-gocardless' mudhairekitori re'/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Batidza wedzero kuburikidza nemenyu ye'Plugins' muWordPress
3. Tevera Wizard nhanho nenhanho kuti ugadzirise wedzero

## Gwaro Rekushandisa {#usage-guide}

_GoCardless_ igedhi rekubhadhara rinowanikwa mu**Europe nemuUK** rinobvumira kubhadhara kwe**Direct Debit**.

Iyo **Ultimate Multisite: GoCardless Gateway** inowedzera rutsigiro rwe_GoCardless_ kuUltimate Multisite, ichikubvumira kubhadharisa vatengi vako mudunhu iroro uchishandisa Direct Debit.

Dzidziso iyi ichafukidza magadzirirwo e_GoCardless_ kuti utange kugamuchira kubhadhara kuburikidza nayo panetwork yako yeUltimate Multisite.

_**Cherechedza: GoCardless inoshanda chete nemari dzinotevera:**_ **EUR, GBP, AUD, SEK, neDKK.**

## Kuisa Wedzero {#installing-the-add-on}

Chekutanga pane zvese, uchafanira kuisa wedzero yeGoCardless yeUltimate Multisite. Unogona kuzviita nekuenda ku**Ultimate Multisite > Zvirongwa > Wedzero (padivi)**. Wobva wasarudza wedzero yeGoCardless parondedzero iyoyo.

Mushure mekunge wedzero yaiswa uye yabatidzwa, enda ku**Ultimate Multisite > Zvirongwa > Kubhadhara**, batidza sarudzo yeGoCardless, wobva wachengetedza.

![Zvirongwa zvegedhi rekubhadhara](/img/config/settings-payment-gateways.png)

## Kuwana Token Yekupinda {#getting-an-access-token}

Gadzira Account pa_GoCardless_ (kana pa_GoCardless_ sandbox, kana uchida **kuiedza kutanga** , izvo zvinokurudzirwa). Wobva, mushure mekupinda, waenda kuchinhu chemenyu che**Vagadziri**.

<!-- Mufananidzo hausipo: Dashboard yeGoCardless ichiratidza nzvimbo yemenyu yeVagadziri -->

Papeji re**Vagadziri**, dzvanya bhatani re**Gadzira** pakona yepamusoro-kurudyi yesikirini, wobva wadzvanya pasarudzo ye**Token Yekupinda**.

<!-- Mufananidzo hausipo: Peji reVagadziri reGoCardless rine bhatani reGadzira nesarudzo yeToken Yekupinda -->

Izvi zvichavhura hwindo idzva remodal, richikubvumira kugadzira token itsva. Ripe zita rinonyatsozivikanwa uye iva nechokwadi chekuti wasarudza sarudzo ye**kupinda kwekuverenga-nekunyora** mumenyu ye**Scope**.

<!-- Mufananidzo hausipo: Modal yeGoCardless yekugadzira token itsva yekupinda ine scope yekuverenga-nekunyora -->

Token yekupinda ichaonekwa pasikirini. Ikopa wobva wainamira kumenyu yako ye**Ultimate Multisite > Zvirongwa > Kubhadhara > GoCardless**.

Iva nechokwadi chekuiisa mundima yakakodzera. Kana iri **token yechokwadi** **n** , dzima **sandbox mode** usati wanamira token.

![Zvirongwa zvegedhi rekubhadhara zvine GoCardless](/img/config/settings-payment-gateways.png)

<!-- Mufananidzo hausipo: Mindima yetoken yeGoCardless muzvirongwa zvekubhadhara zveUltimate Multisite -->

Ndizvozvo panhanho yekutanga. Zvadaro, unofanira kugadzira muteereri wewebhook.

## Kugadzira Muteereri weWebhook {#setting-up-the-webhook-listener}

Pakubata nema subscriptions, Ultimate Multisite inofanira kunzwa nezvekuchinja kwemamiriro e subscription, kubhadhara kutsva kuri kuitwa, nezvimwe zvinhu zvakadaro.

Nzira iyo magedhi ekubhadhara anozivisa nayo Ultimate Multisite nezvekuchinja ikoko kushandisa **kufona kwewebhook**. Nechikonzero ichocho, kuti kubatanidzwa kushande 100%, tinofanira kuudza _GoCardless_ kuti ndeipi URL yekufona nayo neruzivo irworwo rwese.

Dzokera kupaneru yako ye**GoCardless > Vagadziri** wobva wadzvanya bhatani reGadzira zvakare, asi panguva ino, sarudza sarudzo ye**nzvimbo yekugamuchira webhook**.
