# Hurukuro yeKutsvaga Ruzivo {#discovery-interview}

**Hurukuro yeKutsvaga Ruzivo** ndiyo nhanho yekutanga muhurongwa hwekugadzira hweTheme Builder. Inounganidza ruzivo nezvesaiti yako, bhizinesi, uye zvaunofarira pakugadzira kuti igadzire theme yakagadzirirwa iwe.

## Pfupiso {#overview}

Hurukuro yeKutsvaga Ruzivo inokutungamirira kuburikidza nemibvunzo yakatevedzana kuti inzwisise brand yako, vateereri vauri kunanga, uye zvinangwa zvekugadzira. Mushanduro 1.16.0, hurukuro iyi yava kusanganisira **nhanho yekuisa mufananidzo** inokubvumira kupa kurudziro inoonekwa yekugadzirwa kwako.

## Nhanho dzeHurukuro {#interview-steps}

### 1. Ruzivo rweBhizinesi {#1-business-information}

- Zita resaiti uye tagline
- Rudzi rwebhizinesi kana indasitiri
- Zvinangwa zvikuru zvebhizinesi
- Tsananguro yevateereri vauri kunanga

### 2. Zvaunofarira paKugadzira {#2-design-preferences}

- Chirongwa chemavara chaunofarira
- Maitiro ekugadzira (azvino, echinyakare, akapfava, ane mutambo, nezvimwe)
- Mawebsite kana ma brand anokukurudzira
- Zvinhu zvekugadzira zvinofanira kuvapo

### 3. Kuisa Mufananidzo (Chitsva mu v1.16.0) {#3-photo-upload-new-in-v1160}

Isa mufananidzo mumwe kana yakawanda kuti ikanganise nzira yekugadzira:

#### Mafomati Anogamuchirwa {#accepted-formats}

| Fomati | Extension | Tsananguro |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| **JPEG** | `.jpg`, `.jpeg` | Inokurudzirwa kumifananidzo |
| **PNG** | `.png` | Inotsigira kujeka |
| **WebP** | `.webp` | Fomati yazvino, saizi yefaira diki |
| **GIF** | `.gif` | Mifananidzo isingafambi chete (hapana animation) |

#### Zvinodiwa paFaira {#file-requirements}

- **Saizi huru yefaira:** 10 MB
- **Zviyero zvidiki:** 400 × 300 pixels
- **Zviyero zvinokurudzirwa:** 1200 × 800 pixels kana kupfuura
- **Chiyero chedivi:** Chero chiyero chinogamuchirwa

#### Kuti Mifananidzo Inokanganisa Sei Nzira yeKugadzira {#how-photos-influence-design-direction}

Mifananidzo yaunoisa inobatsira Theme Builder kunzwisisa:

- **Palette yemavara** — mavara anotonga anoburitswa uye anoshandiswa mu theme
- **Maitiro anoonekwa** — azvino, erustic, akapfava, ane simba, nezvimwe
- **Manzwiro nemamiriro** — ehunyanzvi, ekusika, ane hushamwari, eumbozha, nezvimwe
- **Zvinhu zvekugadzira** — mapatani, textures, uye maitiro ekuumbwa kwezvinhu
- **Kurudziro ye typography** — maitiro emafonti anowirirana ne theme inoonekwa

### 4. Chimiro cheZvirimo {#4-content-structure}

- Mapeji makuru anodiwa (Home, About, Services, Contact, nezvimwe)
- Chikamu cheblog kana nhau chinodiwa
- Kushanda kwe e-commerce kunodiwa
- Zvinhu zvakakosha kana kubatanidzwa

### 5. Ongororo uye Kusimbisa {#5-review-and-confirmation}

- Pfupiso yeruzivo rwese rwakapihwa
- Kusimbisa kugadzirwa kusati kwatanga

## Maitiro Akanakisisa ekuIsa Mifananidzo {#photo-upload-best-practices}

### Zvekuisa {#what-to-upload}

- **Kurudziro ye brand** — logos, masampuli emavara, kana mirayiridzo ye brand
- **Masaiti evakwikwidzi** — screenshots dzemasaiti aunoyemura
- **Mood boards** — muunganidzwa wemifananidzo inomiririra runako rwaunoda
- **Mifananidzo yezvigadzirwa** — kana uine zvigadzirwa zvekuratidza
- **Mifananidzo yechikwata kana hofisi** — ye personal kana professional branding
- **Mifananidzo yemararamiro** — inomiririra vateereri vauri kunanga

### Zvekudzivisa {#what-to-avoid}

- **Mifananidzo yemhando yakaderera** — mifananidzo isina kujeka kana ine pixelation
- **Mifananidzo isina hukama** — mifananidzo isingamiriri brand yako
- **Mifananidzo ine mashoko akawandisa** — screenshots dzine mavara akawanda
- **Zvinhu zvine copyright** — iva nechokwadi chekuti une kodzero dzekushandisa mifananidzo yacho
- **Animated GIFs** — mifananidzo isingafambi chete ndiyo inogadziriswa

### Mazano eMhedzisiro Yakanakisisa {#tips-for-best-results}

1. **Isa mifananidzo 2-3** — mifananidzo yakawanda inopa mamiriro ari nani
2. **Shandisa mifananidzo yemhando yepamusoro** — mifananidzo yakajeka, yakavhenekerwa zvakanaka ndiyo inoshanda zvakanyanya
3. **Sanganisira zvakasiyana-siyana** — musanganiswa wemavara, maitiro, uye nyaya yemufananidzo
4. **Ipa mamiriro** — tsanangura muchidimbu kuti nei wakasarudza mufananidzo wega wega
5. **Nyatsojekesa** — kana uchida chinhu chakati, chitaridze

## Kubuda kweNzira yeKugadzira {#design-direction-output}

Mushure meHurukuro yeKutsvaga Ruzivo, Theme Builder inogadzira:

- **Palette yemavara** — mavara makuru, echipiri, uye ekusimbisa

- **Zvinhu zvinoonekwa** — icons, mapatani, uye zvinhu zvekushongedza
- **Mood board** — kumiririrwa kunoonekwa kwenzira yekugadzira
