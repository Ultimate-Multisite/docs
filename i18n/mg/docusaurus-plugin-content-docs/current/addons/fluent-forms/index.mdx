# Fluent Forms

Ity add-on ity dia ahafahana manampy fetra amin’ny Fluent Forms ao amin’ny Multisite Ultimate, amin’ny vokatra, membership na tranonkala.
Fluent Forms dia mampiasa tabilao manokana fa tsy custom post types, ka tsy mandeha amin’ny Fluent Forms ny safidy famerana mahazatra.

## Endri-javatra {#features}

- Manampy fetra ho an’ny Fluent Forms na Conversational Fluent Forms
- Mahazo hafatra sariaka ny mpanjifa, manentana azy ireo hanavao ny plan-ny rehefa mahatratra ny fetra
