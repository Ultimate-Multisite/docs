# Sefa: wu_connector_enforcement_option_keys

Sefa mndandanda wa makiyi a option a opereka AI omwe amakakamizidwa kuchokera patsamba lalikulu.

Onjezani makiyi a option a custom kapena ma plugin a opereka AI a third-party kuti zokonda zawo zilowenso kuchokera patsamba lalikulu pa masamba aang’ono.

## Ma Parameter

| Dzina | Mtundu | Kufotokozera |
|------|------|-------------|
| $keys | `string[]` | Mayina a makiyi a option oti akakamizidwe (akuphatikiza kale makiyi a connector omwe apezeka mwamphamvu ndi EXTRA_PROVIDER_OPTIONS). |
