# Site Exporter {#site-exporter}

## Zomwe zili {#content}

Timamvetsa kufunikira kokhoza kusuntha masamba ang'onoang'ono mkati mwa netiweki yanu kupita ku kuyika kwa tsamba limodzi komanso mosinthana ngati kasitomala wanu angasankhe kusamutsa tsamba lawo. Chowonjezera cha Site exporter and importer chimapangitsa ntchitoyi kukhala yosavuta. Chida ichi chimatumiza kunja ndi kulowetsa masamba pogwiritsa ntchito mafayilo a zip omwe angathenso kugwiritsidwa ntchito ngati kopi yosunga.

## Mawonekedwe {#features}

* Tumizani Kunja ndi Lowetsani Masamba

* Mawonekedwe osavuta kugwiritsa ntchito

* Pangani kopi yosunga ya tsamba

## Zolemba {#documentation}
**Mmene Mungatumizire Kunja ndi Kulowetsa Tsamba**

## Kuyika {#installation}

1. Kwezani **ultimate-multisite-site-exporter** ku chikwatu cha **/wp-content/plugins/**
2. Yambitsani pulogalamu yowonjezera kudzera mu menyu ya **Mapulogalamu owonjezera** mu WordPress
3. Tsatirani Wizard sitepe ndi sitepe kuti mukonze pulogalamu yowonjezera

## Kalozera Wogwiritsa Ntchito {#usage-guide}

Ndi Ultimate Multisite mutha kutumiza kunja tsamba kuchokera pa netiweki yanu kapena kulowetsa tsamba ku kuyika kwanu kwa masamba ambiri kapena kwa tsamba limodzi.

Kuti muyambe ndondomekoyi, pitani ku tsamba lathu la zowonjezera ndipo yikani chowonjezera cha Ultimate Multisite: Site Exporter & Importer.![Tsamba la zowonjezera](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

![Tsamba la zowonjezera lokhala ndi njira yoyikira](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

Pitani ku tsamba lanu la Mapulogalamu owonjezera ndipo dinani **Yambitsani pa Netiweki** chowonjezera chomwe mwangoyika kumene.<!-- Chithunzi sichikupezeka: Tsamba la WP Plugins likuwonetsa batani la Network Activate la chowonjezera cha Site Exporter -->

Njira ya **Tumizani Kunja & Lowetsani** imapezeka pa menyu ya Ultimate Multisite, kumanzere.

<!-- Chithunzi sichikupezeka: Menyu ya Ultimate Multisite ikusonyeza njira ya Export and Import -->

## Mmene mungatumizire kunja tsamba {#how-to-export-a-site}

Kuti mutumize kunja tsamba kuchokera pa netiweki yanu, pitani ku **Tumizani Kunja & Lowetsani** pa menyu ya Ultimate Multisite ndipo, pa tabu ya **Tumizani Kunja Masamba**, dinani **Pangani Tumizo Latsopano la Tsamba**.

<!-- Chithunzi sichikupezeka: Tabu ya Export Sites yokhala ndi batani la Generate new Site Export -->

Sakani tsamba lomwe mukufuna kutumiza kunja ndipo sankhani zinthu zomwe mukufuna kuphatikiza mu fayilo yotumizidwa kunja. Mutha kuphatikiza ma theme, mapulogalamu owonjezera, ndi zokwezedwa. Ngati tsamba lanu ndi lalikulu kwambiri, mutha kusankha kuyendetsa ndondomeko yotumiza kunja kumbuyo.<!-- Chithunzi sichikupezeka: Zosankha zotumiza kunja tsamba zokhala ndi kusaka tsamba, mabokosi a include themes/plugins/uploads -->

Dinani batani la **Tumizani Kunja Tsamba**. Tsamba lanu lotumizidwa kunja lidzapezeka pa mndandanda wa **Zotumizidwa Kunja Zomwe Zilipo**. Dinani batani la **Tsitsani ZIP** kuti mutsitse fayiloyo. Ma ZIP otumizidwa kunja amaphatikiza `index.php` yodzidzimutsa yokha ndi `readme.txt`, kotero akhoza kuyikidwa pa host yatsopano popanda kuyamba kuyika pulogalamu yowonjezera yolowetsera yosiyana.<!-- Chithunzi sichikupezeka: Mndandanda wa Existing Exports wokhala ndi batani la Download ZIP -->

## Mmene mungatumizire kunja netiweki yonse {#how-to-export-the-whole-network}

Kuti mutumize kunja masamba ang'onoang'ono onse pamodzi, pitani ku **Tumizani Kunja & Lowetsani** pa menyu ya Ultimate Multisite ndipo gwiritsani ntchito njira ya **Tumizani Kunja Netiweki** pa chinsalu chotumizira kunja. Fayilo yosungidwa yomwe imapangidwa imakhala ndi masamba ang'onoang'ono onse mu ZIP imodzi ya kusamutsa ma host, kumanganso staging, kapena makopi osunga pa mulingo wa netiweki.

Zotumiza kunja za netiweki zingakhale zazikulu. Ziyendetseni pamene magalimoto ndi ochepa ndipo tsimikizirani kuti seva ili ndi malo okwanira osungira fayilo yosungidwa ndi zokwezedwa, mapulogalamu owonjezera, ndi ma theme aliwonse ophatikizidwa.

## Mmene mungalowetsere tsamba {#how-to-import-a-site}

Kuti mulowetse tsamba, pitani ku tabu ya **Lowetsani Tsamba** ndipo kwezani fayilo ya .zip ya tsamba lomwe mukufuna kulowetsa. Mutha kusintha URL kukhala yatsopano kapena kusunga URL yakale posiya malo opanda kanthu. Komanso, mutha kusankha bokosi kuti muchotse fayilo ya .zip kuchokera pa kuyika kwanu pambuyo pa kulowetsa. Kenako dinani **Yambani Kulowetsa**.

<!-- Chithunzi sichikupezeka: Tabu ya Import a Site yokhala ndi malo okweza zip ndi batani la Begin Import -->

Ndondomekoyi ingatenge kanthawi kochepa. Mudzaona kulowetsa komwe kukuyembekezera mu mndandanda pansipa pa cholowetsera.

<!-- Chithunzi sichikupezeka: Kulowetsa komwe kukuyembekezera mu mndandanda pansipa pa cholowetsera -->

Mutha kuyimitsa ndondomekoyi podina **Letsani Kulowetsa**.

<!-- Chithunzi sichikupezeka: Batani la Cancel Import loyimitsa ndondomeko yolowetsa -->

Pambuyo poti ndondomekoyi yatha, tsamba lanu likupezeka kale pa netiweki yanu.

## Mmene mungalowetsere tsamba pa kuyika kumodzi {#how-to-import-a-site-on-a-single-install}

Kuti mulowetse tsamba ku kuyika kwanu kumodzi, choyamba yikani ndi kuyambitsa cholowetsera pa tsamba lanu. Mutha kupeza ulalo wotsitsira pa tabu ya chotumizera kunja.

<!-- Chithunzi sichikupezeka: Tabu ya Exporter yokhala ndi ulalo wotsitsira cholowetsera cha tsamba limodzi -->

Mutha kupeza cholowetsera pa menyu ya **Zida**, kumanzere, pa chinthu chaching'ono cha **Tumizani Kunja & Lowetsani**.

<!-- Chithunzi sichikupezeka: Menyu ya Tools ya tsamba limodzi ikusonyeza submenu ya Export and Import -->

Kwezani fayilo ya .zip ya tsamba lomwe mukufuna kulowetsa. Mutha kusintha URL kukhala yatsopano kapena kusunga URL yakale posiya malo opanda kanthu. Komanso, mutha kusankha bokosi kuti muchotse fayilo ya .zip kuchokera pa kuyika kwanu pambuyo pa kulowetsa. Kenako dinani **Yambani Kulowetsa**.

<!-- Chithunzi sichikupezeka: Fomu yolowetsa ya tsamba limodzi yokhala ndi kukweza zip ndi batani la Begin Import -->

Ndondomekoyi ingatenge kanthawi kochepa. Mudzaona kulowetsa komwe kukuyembekezera mu mndandanda pansipa pa cholowetsera.

<!-- Chithunzi sichikupezeka: Kulowetsa komwe kukuyembekezera kwa tsamba limodzi mu mndandanda -->

Mutha kuyimitsa ndondomekoyi podina **Letsani Kulowetsa**.

<!-- Chithunzi sichikupezeka: Batani la Cancel Import loyimitsa ndondomeko yolowetsa -->

Pambuyo poti ndondomekoyi yatha, tsamba lanu lidzasinthidwa ndi tsamba lomwe mwangolowetsa kumene.
