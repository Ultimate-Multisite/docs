# Zowona za Chuck Norris {#chuck-norris-facts}

Onjezani nthabwala pang'ono kuntchito zanu za tsiku ndi tsiku za kasamalidwe ka WordPress ndi chowonjezera cha **Ultimate Multisite - Zowona za Chuck Norris**. Chikatsegulidwa, mudzaona chowona cha Chuck Norris chosankhidwa mwachisawawa chikuwonetsedwa pakona yakumanja yakumtunda ya tsamba lililonse la woyang'anira.

Ndi chabwino pobweretsa kumwetulira ku gulu lanu, kuchepetsa kulemera kwa mtima panthawi ya magawo aatali a woyang'anira, kapena kungokondwerera ukulu wodziwika bwino wa Chuck Norris.

## Chifukwa Chiyani Zowona za Chuck Norris? {#why-chuck-norris-facts}

* **Limbikitsani Mzimu wa Gulu**: Onjezani chinthu chosangalatsa ku kasamalidwe ka netiweki yanu
* **Yambitsani Kukambirana**: Ndi chiyambi chabwino cha zokambirana kwa mamembala atsopano a gulu
* **Kuwonjezera Kupanga Zinthu**: Kuseka mwachangu kungathandize kuika mtima pa ntchito ndi kupanga zambiri
* **Nthabwala Zosatha**: Zowona za Chuck Norris sizimakalamba

## Zinthu Zofunika {#key-features}

**Zowona Zodziwika Bwino 110+**
Mndandanda wokwanira wa nthabwala zakale za Chuck Norris ndi zowona zomwe zimakondwerera nthano iyeyo.

**Kuwonetsera Kosasokoneza**
Zowona zimawoneka mu bala ya woyang'anira m'njira yosavutitsa yomwe siyimasokoneza kayendedwe ka ntchito yanu.

**Zokonzeka Kutanthauziridwa**
Zowona zonse zayikidwa mu ntchito zotanthauzira, zomwe zimapangitsa kukhala kosavuta kuzisintha ku chilankhulo chanu kapena kupanga zowona zapadera za gulu lanu.

**Zimayankha Mokwanira**
Zowona zimawonetsedwa bwino pa zowonekera za kompyuta, tablet, ndi foni yam'manja - Chuck Norris ndi wodabwitsa pa zida zonse.

**Thandizo la RTL**
Thandizo lonse la zilankhulo zochokera kumanja kupita kumanzere chifukwa Chuck Norris amadutsa malire a chilankhulo.

**Zimagwirizana ndi Chosinthira cha Mabuloko**
Zowona zimabisidwa mwanzeru mu chosinthira cha mabuloko kuti tipewe mikangano ya mawonekedwe.

**Zoyang'ana pa Kupezeka kwa Onse**
Zimaphatikizapo mawu oyenera a owerenga zenera kuti zigwirizane mokwanira ndi kupezeka kwa onse.

## Zabwino Kwa {#perfect-for}

* Oyang'anira netiweki ya multisite omwe akufuna kuwonjezera khalidwe
* Magulu a chitukuko omwe amakonda nthabwala zabwino
* Aliyense amene amafunikira mlingo wa tsiku ndi tsiku wa ukulu wa Chuck Norris
* Masite omwe akufuna kupanga malo ogwirira ntchito osangalatsa komanso abwino

## Kuyika {#installation}

1. Onetsetsani kuti pulagini ya Ultimate Multisite yayikidwa ndipo yatsegulidwa pa netiweki yanu ya WordPress
2. Kwezani mafayilo a chowonjezeracho ku chikwatu chanu cha `/wp-content/plugins/`
3. Tsegulani pulagini pa netiweki kudzera mu menyu ya 'Plugins' mu WordPress
4. Sangalalani ndi zowona za Chuck Norris zosankhidwa mwachisawawa pa tsamba lililonse la woyang'anira!

Palibe kasinthidwe kofunika - ingotsegulani ndi kumwetulira!

## Mafunso Ofunsidwa Kawirikawiri {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Kodi izi zimafuna Ultimate Multisite? {#does-this-require-ultimate-multisite}

Ngakhale zinapangidwa ngati chowonjezera cha Ultimate Multisite, pulaginiyi imagwira ntchito payokha ndipo ingayende pa kuyika kulikonse kwa WordPress.

## Kodi ndingawonjezere zowona zanga? {#can-i-add-my-own-facts}

Inde! Zowonazo zakonzeka kutanthauziridwa. Mutha kupanga fayilo yanu ya `.po` yokhala ndi zowona zapadera ndikuyiyika mu chikwatu cha `/lang`. Kapena mutha kusefa mndandanda wa zowona mwa pulogalamu.

## Kodi izi zimachedwetsa woyang'anira wanga? {#does-this-slow-down-my-admin}

Ayi konse! Pulaginiyi ndi yopepuka kwambiri ndipo ilibe zotsatira pa kagwiridwe ka ntchito. Imangowonetsa mawu - palibe kuyitana kwa API yakunja kapena kukonza kolemera.

## Kodi ndingayimitse pa masamba ena? {#can-i-disable-it-on-certain-pages}

Zowonazo zimabisidwa zokha pa masamba pomwe zingasokoneze (monga chosinthira cha mabuloko). Mutha kugwiritsanso ntchito WordPress hooks kusintha komwe zowona zimawonekera.

## Kodi zowonazo ndi zoyenera kwa omvera onse? {#are-the-facts-appropriate-for-all-audiences}

Inde! Zowona zonse ndi zoyera, nthabwala zoyenera banja zomwe zimakondwerera udindo wodziwika bwino wa Chuck Norris.

## Kodi ndingatanthauzire zowonazo ku chilankhulo china? {#can-i-translate-the-facts-to-another-language}

Ndithudi! Zowona zonse zimagwiritsa ntchito ntchito zotanthauzira za WordPress. Pangani fayilo yotanthauzira ya chilankhulo chanu ndikuyiyika mu chikwatu cha `/lang`. Text domain: `ultimate-multisite-chuck-norris-facts`

## Kodi imagwira ntchito ndi multisite? {#does-it-work-with-multisite}

Inde! Idapangidwa makamaka kwa ma netiweki a multisite ndipo ingatsegulidwe pa netiweki.
