# Mailster

Sinthani netiweki yanu ya masamba ambiri kukhala makina amphamvu omangira mindandanda ya maimelo ndi chowonjezera cha Ultimate Multisite Mailster Integration. Onjezerani makasitomala atsopano okha ku mindandanda yanu ya maimelo ya Mailster akalembetsa kapena akagula, kupangitsa kulumikizana kosalala pakati pa nsanja yanu ya umembala ndi njira yanu yotsatsa kudzera pa imelo.

## Zinthu Zofunika Kwambiri {#key-features}

**Kulunzanitsa Olembetsa Kokha**
* Onjezerani makasitomala okha ku mindandanda ya Mailster akalembetsa
* Sankhani kulunzanitsa nthawi yomweyo polembetsa kapena kudikira kutsimikiziridwa kwa malipiro
* Sungani mindandanda yanu ya maimelo ili yatsopano komanso yosinthidwa popanda kulowererapo pamanja

**Kasamalidwe Kosinthika ka Mindandanda**
* Khazikitsani mindandanda yokhazikika yapadziko lonse kwa makasitomala onse atsopano
* Sinthani zokhazikika ndi mindandanda yokhudzana ndi mankhwala kuti mukhale ndi makampeni olunjika
* Yang'anirani mosavuta magawidwe a mindandanda pogwiritsa ntchito mawonekedwe odziwika a Mailster

**Zosankha za Kulowa Mwakuvomereza Zogwirizana ndi GDPR**
* Sankhani pakati pa kulembetsa kokha kapena kuvomereza kogwiritsa ntchito bokosi losankha
* Yambitsani double opt-in pa zolembetsa zotsimikiziridwa
* Sonyezani/bisani bokosi losankha lolowa mwakuvomereza malinga ndi zofunikira zanu zotsatira malamulo

**Kugwirizanitsa Deta Mwanzeru**
* Lunzanitsani okha mayina a makasitomala ndi zambiri zolumikizirana
* Gwirizanitsani maadiresi a billing ndi minda ya olembetsa
* Tsatirani masiku olembetsa kuti mupange makampeni olunjika

**Kuwongolera pa Mulingo wa Mankhwala**
* Gawirani mindandanda yosiyanasiyana ku mankhwala osiyanasiyana
* Pangani makampeni olunjika kutengera zomwe makasitomala amagula
* Letsani Mailster pa mankhwala enaake pakafunika

**Zochitika Zaukadaulo**
* Kulumikizana kwa UI ya Mailster yachibadwa posankha mindandanda
* Kulemba mwatsatanetsatane kuti muthetse mavuto ndi kutsimikizira
* Kusintha kosalala kwa olembetsa omwe alipo kale

## Ndi Yabwino Kwa {#perfect-for}

* Eni masamba a umembala omwe akumanga mindandanda ya maimelo
* Nsanja za SaaS zomwe zikufuna kulera makasitomala atsopano
* Opanga maphunziro omwe akugawa ophunzira malinga ndi kulembetsa
* Aliyense amene akuyendetsa Ultimate Multisite ndi Mailster

## Momwe Zimagwirira Ntchito {#how-it-works}

1. Ikani ndi kuyambitsa chowonjezerachi pa netiweki yanu ya Ultimate Multisite
2. Konzani mindandanda yokhazikika yapadziko lonse mu zokonda za Ultimate Multisite
3. Mwasankha, sinthani ndi mindandanda yokhudzana ndi mankhwala
4. Sankhani njira yanu yolowera mwakuvomereza ndi nthawi ya kulembetsa
5. Onani mindandanda yanu ya Mailster ikukula yokha!

## Kuyika {#installation}

1. Kwezani mafayilo a plugin ku chikwatu cha zowonjezera cha Ultimate Multisite
2. Onetsetsani kuti Mailster yaikidwa ndipo ikugwira ntchito pa tsamba lanu lalikulu
3. Yambitsani chowonjezerachi kudzera patsamba la zowonjezera la Ultimate Multisite
4. Pitani ku Settings > Mailster Integration kuti mukonze
5. Sankhani mindandanda yanu yokhazikika ndi kukonza zokonda zolowera mwakuvomereza

## Mafunso Ofunsidwa Kawirikawiri {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Kodi chowonjezerachi chimagwira ntchito popanda Ultimate Multisite? {#does-this-addon-work-without-ultimate-multisite}

Ayi, chowonjezerachi chimafuna Ultimate Multisite kuti chigwire ntchito. Chapangidwa makamaka kuti chilumikize Mailster ndi dongosolo la makasitomala ndi mankhwala la Ultimate Multisite.

## Kodi ndiyenera kuyika Mailster pa masamba onse? {#do-i-need-mailster-installed-on-all-sites}

Ayi, Mailster imangofunika kuikidwa ndi kuyambitsidwa pa tsamba lanu lalikulu la netiweki. Chowonjezerachi chidzalumikizana nacho chokha kuchokera ku masamba onse ang'onoang'ono.

## Kodi ndingagawire mindandanda yosiyanasiyana ku mankhwala osiyanasiyana? {#can-i-assign-different-lists-to-different-products}

Inde! Mutha kukhazikitsa mindandanda yokhazikika yapadziko lonse, kenako n’kuyisintha pa mankhwala payekha. Izi zimakulolani kupanga makampeni a maimelo olunjika kwambiri kutengera zomwe makasitomala amagula.

## Chimachitika ndi chiyani ngati kasitomala alipo kale mu Mailster? {#what-happens-if-a-customer-already-exists-in-mailster}

Mwachisawawa, chowonjezerachi chidzasintha olembetsa omwe alipo kale ndi kuwonjezera iwo ku mindandanda yatsopano. Mutha kulepheretsa izi mu zokonda ngati mukufuna kudumpha olembetsa omwe alipo kale.

## Kodi izi zikutsatira GDPR? {#is-this-gdpr-compliant}

Inde! Chowonjezerachi chimaphatikizapo njira yolowera mwakuvomereza yogwiritsa ntchito bokosi losankha, pomwe makasitomala ayenera kuvomereza momveka bwino kulandira maimelo. Izi zimakwaniritsa zofunikira za GDPR zikaphatikizidwa ndi mfundo zoyenera zachinsinsi.

## Kodi ndingagwiritse ntchito double opt-in? {#can-i-use-double-opt-in}

Ndithudi! Yambitsani zokonda za double opt-in ndipo Mailster idzatumiza maimelo otsimikizira musanayambitse zolembetsa.
