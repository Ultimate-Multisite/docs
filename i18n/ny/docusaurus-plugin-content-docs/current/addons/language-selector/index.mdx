# Wosankha Chiyankhulo {#language-selector}

Multisite Ultimate: Wosankha Chiyankhulo pa Kulembetsa

Onjezani gawo losankha chiyankhulo mu kayendedwe kolembetsa.

## Kuyika {#installation}

1. Kwezani 'ultimate-multisite-language-selector' ku chikwatu cha '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Yambitsani chowonjezeracho kudzera mu menyu ya 'Zowonjezera' mu WordPress Network Admin yanu

## Kalozera Wogwiritsa Ntchito {#usage-guide}

Ndi chowonjezera chaulerechi mungalole ogwiritsa ntchito anu kusankha chiyankhulo chomwe akufuna kuona pa kayendedwe kolembetsa komanso pa tsamba lolowera.

Mukamaliza [kuyambitsa laisensi yanu](../../user-guide/getting-started/installing-ultimate-multisite.md), mudzatha kuyika zowonjezera za Ultimate Multisite zomwe laisensi yanu imakupatsani mwayi wozipeza.

Pitani ku tsamba la makonzedwe a Ultimate Multisite yanu, kenako pa menyu kumanzere, dinani chizindikiro cha _Zowonjezera_.

![Tsamba la zowonjezera](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

_Pitani ku tsamba la Zowonjezera kuti muyambe kuyika Wosankha Chiyankhulo_

Kumeneko, mupeza zowonjezera zonse zomwe timapereka.

Zowonjezera zaulere zilipo kwa makasitomala athu onse, mosasamala kanthu za pulani ya kulembetsa yomwe analembetsa.

Dinani batani la _Ikani Tsopano!_ pansi pa chithunzi chaching'ono cha chowonjezeracho. Kenako chiyambitseni pa tsamba la Zowonjezera.

![Tsamba la zowonjezera lokhala ndi batani loyika](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

_Dinani batani kuti muyike chowonjezeracho_

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: WP Plugins page showing Network Activate button for Language Selector addon -->

_Chiyambitseni pa tsamba lanu la Zowonjezera_

Tsopano, pitani ku menyu ya Makonzedwe a Ultimate Multisite ndipo lowani pa tabu ya _Makonzedwe a Netiweki_. Kumeneko, kumapeto kwa tsambalo, mupeza gawo latsopano lotchedwa _Zilankhulo_.

![Tsamba la makonzedwe](/img/config/settings-general.png)

_Gawo latsopano kwambiri lawonekera!_

Mutha kulisiya lopanda kanthu kuti muwonetse zilankhulo zonse ngati zosankha kapena kusankha zomwe mukufuna kuti zizipezeka. Zilankhulozo ndi zomwe mwayika mu WordPress yanu.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Language selection dropdown showing available languages on WP install -->

_Mndandanda wa zilankhulo zomwe zilipo pa kuyika kwa WP kumeneku_

Mukamaliza kusunga zosintha zomwe munapanga, menyu yosankha yokhala ndi zilankhulo zomwe mwasankha idzawonetsedwa pa tsamba Lolowera komanso pa Kayendedwe Kolembetsa.

## Kukhazikitsa Chiyankhulo kudzera mu URL {#setting-the-language-via-the-url}

Mungathenso kusintha chiyankhulo cha tsamba popereka parameter ya locale ku URL. Ngati mukufuna kutumiza ku tsamba lolembetsa mu Chitaliyana, mwachitsanzo, onjezani ?locale=it_IT ku URL.

[https://yournetwork.com/wp-signup.php?locale=it_IT](https://yournetwork.com/wp-signup.php?locale=it%5FIT)

Chiyankhulocho chiyenera kukhala chayikidwa pa netiweki yanu ya WordPress kuti izi zigwire ntchito.
