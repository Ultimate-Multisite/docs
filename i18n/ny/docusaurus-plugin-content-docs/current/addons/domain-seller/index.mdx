# Domain Seller

**Multisite Ultimate: Domain Seller** imasandutsa netiweki yanu ya WordPress Multisite kukhala nsanja yamphamvu yogulitsira ma domain. Lolani makasitomala kulembetsa ma domain a makonda mwachindunji panthawi yolembetsa kapena kuchokera ku dashboard yawo, ndi kufufuza kupezeka nthawi yomweyo komanso mitengo yoonekera bwino.

### Chifukwa Chiyani Domain Seller?

Kulembetsa ma domain ndi imodzi mwa upsells zofunsidwa kwambiri pa nsanja iliyonse ya WaaS (Website as a Service). M’malo motumiza makasitomala anu kwa registrar wa munthu wina, mungapereke ma domain mwachindunji — kusunga ndalama zambiri mu netiweki yanu pamene mukupatsa makasitomala chidziwitso chosavuta popanda zopinga.

### Zinthu Zofunika

#### Thandizo la Ma Registrar Angapo a Domain
* **OpenSRS** — Kulembetsa, kukonzanso, ndi kasamalidwe kokwanira
* **Namecheap** — Kulumikizana kokwanira ndi WhoisGuard ndi kuyesa kwa sandbox
* **HostAfrica** — Kusaka domain, mitengo, kulembetsa, kusamutsa, DNS, ndi chitetezo cha ID kudzera pa Domains Reseller API
* **Openprovider** — Mitengo ya reseller, kulembetsa, kukonzanso, kusamutsa, DNS, chinsinsi cha WHOIS, ndi kulunzanitsa kwa TLD
* **Hostinger** — Kusaka ndi kulembetsa domain pogwiritsa ntchito token yolumikizirana ya Hostinger yogawana
* **Kapangidwe Kosavuta Kukulitsa** — Kamangidwa pa chitsanzo cha Integration Registry, kupangitsa kuti zikhale zosavuta kuwonjezera opereka ena
* **Kuyesa Kulumikizana Komangidwamo** — Tsimikizirani zidziwitso za opereka ndi kulumikizana musanayambe kugwiritsa ntchito pa live

#### Wizard Yotsogolera Kukhazikitsa
* **Kuyamba ndi kudina kamodzi** — Imakutsogolerani pa kukonza wopereka, kuitanitsa TLD, ndi kupanga chinthu cha domain
* **Kulunzanitsa TLD kokha** — Imalowetsa ma TLD onse omwe alipo ndi mitengo ya wholesale kuchokera kwa opereka omwe mwalumikiza
* **Kupanga chinthu chokhazikika** — Imapanga chinthu cha domain chokonzeka kugwiritsidwa ntchito chokhala ndi markup defaults zomveka

#### Mitengo ya Domain Yosinthika
* **Njira zitatu za markup** — Percentage markup, fixed markup, kapena fixed price pa TLD
* **Zinthu za TLD zapadera** — Pangani zinthu za domain zosiyana za magulu osiyanasiyana a TLD (.com/.net, .io, ma code a dziko, ndi zina)
* **Mitengo yoyambirira** — Perekani mtengo wotsitsidwa wa chaka choyamba kuti mukope kulembetsa kwatsopano
* **Mitengo ya nthawi yomweyo** — Mitengo ya wholesale yamoyo kuchokera kwa opereka ndi caching yanzeru
* **Thandizo la coupon** — Kugwirizana kokwanira ndi ma discount codes a Multisite Ultimate

#### Chitetezo cha Chinsinsi cha WHOIS
* **Kukonza pa chinthu chilichonse** — Khazikitsani chinsinsi cha WHOIS kukhala Disabled, Always Included, kapena Customer Choice
* **Kusankha kwa kasitomala** — Ikakhazikitsidwa kukhala Customer Choice, checkbox ya checkout imalola makasitomala kuwonjezera chitetezo cha chinsinsi
* **Mitengo yosinthika** — Khazikitsani mtengo pa chaka wa privacy add-on, kapena mupereke kwaulere
* **Kulumikizana ndi registrar** — Imayatsa yokha WhoisGuard (Namecheap) kapena chinsinsi cha WHOIS (OpenSRS) panthawi yolembetsa

#### Chidziwitso Chimodzi cha Checkout
* **Njira zitatu za domain** — Makasitomala amasankha pakati pa subdomain (yaulere), kulembetsa domain yatsopano, kapena kulumikiza domain yomwe ilipo kale yomwe ali nayo
* **Kusaka domain nthawi yomweyo** — Kufufuza kupezeka koyendetsedwa ndi AJAX popanda kutsitsanso tsamba
* **Malangizo anzeru** — Malangizo a ma TLD ena pamene domain yomwe yasankhidwa yatengedwa
* **Mitengo yoonekera bwino** — Kuwonetsa momveka bwino mtengo wolembetsa, mtengo wokonzanso, ndi chindapusa chosankha cha chinsinsi cha WHOIS
* **Minda ya kulumikizana ya registrant** — Imasonkhanitsa zambiri zofunikira za registrant (dzina, adiresi, foni) mwachindunji panthawi ya checkout
* **URL ya site yopangidwa yokha** — Adiresi ya site imachokera yokha ku domain yosankhidwa

#### Dongosolo la Auto-Renewal
* **Kukonzanso kolumikizidwa ndi membership** — Ma domain amakonzanso okha pamene membership ya kasitomala ili yogwira ntchito
* **Kukonza nthawi kwanzeru** — Ntchito zokonzanso zimayikidwa pamzere potengera masiku otha ntchito a domain
* **Njira yoyesanso** — Kuyesanso mwanzeru pa zoyesayesa zokonzanso zomwe zalephera
* **Zidziwitso za imelo** — Zidziwitso zosinthika za kukonzanso komwe kukubwera, kukonzanso kopambana, kulephera, ndi kutha ntchito

#### Kasamalidwe ka DNS ka Makasitomala
* **DNS yodzithandizira** — Makasitomala angawonjezere, kusintha, ndi kuchotsa ma DNS records (A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, TXT) kuchokera ku dashboard yawo
* **Mkhalidwe wa domain** — Mkhalidwe wolembetsa wa nthawi yomweyo, masiku otha ntchito, ndi zambiri za nameserver
* **Kulumikiza domain kokha** — Ma domain olembetsedwa kapena omwe alipo kale amalumikizidwa okha ku site ya kasitomala

#### Zida za Admin
* **Kulembetsa domain pamanja** — Lembetsani ma domain m'malo mwa makasitomala kuchokera ku admin panel
* **Kuwunika ma domain** — Onani ma domain onse a makasitomala, mikhalidwe, ndi masiku otha ntchito kuchokera patsamba limodzi
* **Logging yapadera ya wopereka** — Ma log channels osiyana pa registrar aliyense (mwachitsanzo, domain-seller-namecheap.log)
* **Tsatanetsatane wa kulembetsa** — Onani zambiri za registrant ndi data yolembetsa patsamba lalikulu losinthira domain

### Opereka Othandizidwa

* **OpenSRS** — Kulumikizana kokwanira ndi kulembetsa, kukonzanso, kasamalidwe ka DNS, ndi chinsinsi cha WHOIS
* **Namecheap** — Kulumikizana kokwanira ndi kulembetsa, kukonzanso, kufufuza kupezeka kwa batch, ndi WhoisGuard yaulere
* **HostAfrica** — Kulembetsa, kukonzanso, kusamutsa, kasamalidwe ka DNS, kupeza code ya EPP, registrar lock, ndi chitetezo cha ID
* **Openprovider** — Kulembetsa, kukonzanso, kusamutsa, kasamalidwe ka DNS, kupeza code ya EPP, registrar lock, chinsinsi cha WHOIS, ndi kulunzanitsa TLD kwa reseller
* **Hostinger** — Kulembetsa, kufufuza domain, zosintha za nameserver, registrar lock, ndi chinsinsi cha WHOIS; kukonzanso kumayendetsedwa ndi auto-renew ya Hostinger
* **GoDaddy** — Kulembetsa ndi kukonzanso
* **ResellerClub** — Kulembetsa, kukonzanso, ndi kasamalidwe ka DNS
* **NameSilo** — Kulembetsa ndi kukonzanso
* **Enom** — Kulembetsa, kukonzanso, ndi kasamalidwe ka DNS

### Tsatanetsatane wa Tekinoloje

* **Imafuna:** PHP 7.4+, Multisite Ultimate 2.4.12+
* **Kapangidwe:** Chitsanzo cha Integration Registry ndi interface ya Domain_Selling_Capability
* **Autoloading:** Jetpack autoloader yokhala ndi PSR-4 class mapping
* **Miyezo:** Miyezo ya WordPress coding, strict typing, prepared statements

## Kuyika

1. Onetsetsani kuti **Multisite Ultimate v2.4.12+** yaikidwa ndipo yatsegulidwa pa network
2. Kwezani plugin ku `/wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite-domain-seller/`
3. Tsegulani plugin pa network kuchokera ku **Plugins** mu WordPress Network Admin
4. **Wothandizira Kukhazikitsa** amayamba yekha pa kutsegula koyamba — tsatirani masitepe kuti:
   * Mulumikize domain registrar yanu
   * Muyese kulumikizana
   * Mulowetse ma TLD omwe alipo ndi mitengo ya wholesale
   * Mupange domain product yanu yoyamba yokhala ndi markup ya mtengo
5. Onjezani field ya **Kusankha Domain** ku fomu yanu ya checkout mu **Ultimate Multisite > Mafomu a Checkout**

## Mafunso Ofunsidwa Kawirikawiri

### Ndi mtundu uti wa Multisite Ultimate womwe ukufunika?

Mtundu 2.4.12 kapena wapamwamba. Addon siyigwirizana ndi WP Ultimo v1.x kapena mitundu ya Ultimate Multisite yotsika kuposa 2.4.12.

### Ndi opereka domain ati omwe amathandizidwa?

Ma registrar asanu ndi anayi amathandizidwa: **OpenSRS**, **Namecheap**, **HostAfrica**, **Openprovider**, **Hostinger**, **GoDaddy**, **ResellerClub**, **NameSilo**, ndi **Enom**. Kuthekera kumasiyana malinga ndi provider chifukwa ma API a registrar amawonetsa ntchito zosiyanasiyana; onani kalozera wokhazikitsira kuti muwone mwachidule provider aliyense. Kapangidwe ka Integration Registry ka addon kamapangitsa kuti zikhale zosavuta kuwonjezera ma provider apadera — onani [kalozera wa developer](/developer/integration-guide/custom-registrar).

### Kodi ndingapereke chitetezo cha chinsinsi cha WHOIS?

Inde. Domain product iliyonse ili ndi makonzedwe a WHOIS Privacy okhala ndi njira zitatu: Yaletsedwa, Yaphatikizidwa Nthawi Zonse, kapena Kusankha kwa Kasitomala. Ikakhazikitsidwa ku Kusankha kwa Kasitomala, makasitomala amawona checkbox pa checkout yokhala ndi mtengo (kapena "Yaulere" ya Namecheap's WhoisGuard). Chizindikiro cha chinsinsi chimatumizidwa chokha kwa registrar panthawi yolembetsa.

### Kodi ndingakhazikitse mitengo yosiyanasiyana ya ma TLD osiyanasiyana?

Inde. Pangani ma domain product osiyana a magulu osiyanasiyana a TLD. Product iliyonse ikhoza kukhala ndi mtundu wake wa markup (peresenti, markup yokhazikika, kapena mtengo wokhazikika), ma TLD othandizidwa, ndi mitengo yoyambira.

### Ndi zosankha ziti za checkout zomwe makasitomala amawona?

Field ya Kusankha Domain imapatsa makasitomala ma tab atatu: **Subdomain** (gwiritsani ntchito subdomain yaulere pa network yanu), **Lembetsani Domain Yatsopano** (fufuzani ndi kulembetsa domain yatsopano), ndi **Domain Ilipo Kale** (mapani domain yomwe ali nayo kale). Njira zokha zomwe mwatsegula ndi zomwe zidzaonekere.

### Kodi imathandiza kukonzanso kokha?

Inde. Ma domain akhoza kukhazikitsidwa kuti azikonzanso okha ndipo amalumikizidwa ndi udindo wa membership wa kasitomala. Malipiro a kukonzanso amasinthidwa kudzera pa payment gateway yomwe kasitomala ali nayo kale.

### Kodi makasitomala angayendetse ma record awo a DNS?

Inde. Makasitomala amapeza mawonekedwe oyendetsera DNS mu dashboard yawo kuti awonjezere, asinthe, ndi kuchotsa ma record a A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, ndi TXT. Kuyendetsa DNS kulipo ndi OpenSRS, ResellerClub, Enom, HostAfrica, ndi Openprovider. Hostinger DNS ya ma domain omwe ali hosted imayendetsedwa ndi integration yaikulu ya Hostinger domain-mapping.

### Kodi pali setup wizard?

Inde. Setup wizard imayenda yokha pa kutsegula koyamba ndipo imakutsogolerani kulumikiza registrar, kulowetsa ma TLD, ndi kupanga domain product yanu yoyamba. Mutha kuyendetsanso wizard kuchokera ku makonzedwe a addon.
