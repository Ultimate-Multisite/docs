# Mbiri ya Zosintha za WooCommerce Integration

Mtundu 2.2.0 - Watulutsidwa pa 2026-07-01
* Chatsopano: Ndalama za msonkho za Ultimate Multisite tsopano zimaoneka ngati mizere yosiyana ya chindapusa cha WooCommerce pa checkout, zomwe zimapangitsa chiwerengero chonse cha msonkho kukhala chomveka bwino musanalipire.
* Chatsopano: Kwonjezedwa zokonda za kusankha kulowa "Imitsani Umembala Nthawi Yomweyo pa Kukonzanso Kolephera" ndi fyuluta ya `wu_woo_suspend_on_payment_failure` kwa masaiti omwe akufuna kuyimitsidwa chifukwa cha kukonzanso kolephera pa nthawi ya kuyesanso ya WooCommerce Subscriptions.
* Kukonza: Zayanjanitsidwa ma status a WooCommerce Subscription omwe akanatha kusakhala ogwirizana ndi maumbembala a Ultimate Multisite pambuyo pa kukonzanso kolephera kapena kobwezeretsedwa.
* Kukonza: Kwonjezedwa ndalama za sitolo ya WooCommerce ku mndandanda wa ndalama za Ultimate Multisite pamene zinali kusowa.
* Kukonza: Zasungidwa zambiri za billing za kasitomala pamene olembetsa akulozedwa ku WooCommerce checkout.
* Zakonzedwa bwino: Kwonjezedwa kugwirizana ndi Jetpack Autoloader 5.
* Zakonzedwa bwino: Zayeretsedwa kupanga phukusi lotulutsa kuti ma zip a GitHub ndi marketplace apewe ma directory a staging obisika mkati ndi mafayilo a chitukuko.
