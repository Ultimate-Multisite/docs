# GoCardless {#gocardless}

Multisite Ultimate: Malipiro a GoCardless

Lolani makasitomala anu kulipira mamembala ndi zinthu za Multisite Ultimate ndi malipiro achindunji pogwiritsa ntchito GoCardless!

## Kuyika {#installation}

1. Kwezani 'ultimate-multisite-gocardless' ku chikwatu cha '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Yambitsani plugin kudzera mu menyu ya 'Plugins' mu WordPress
3. Tsatirani Wizard pang'onopang'ono kuti mukhazikitse plugin

## Kalozera Wogwiritsa Ntchito {#usage-guide}

_GoCardless_ ndi njira yolipirira yomwe ilipo ku **Europe ndi ku UK** yomwe imalola malipiro a **Direct Debit**.

**Ultimate Multisite: GoCardless Gateway** imawonjezera chithandizo cha _GoCardless_ ku Ultimate Multisite, kukulolani kulipiritsa makasitomala anu m'deralo pogwiritsa ntchito Direct Debit.

Phunziroli lifotokoza momwe mungakhazikitsire _GoCardless_ kuti muyambe kulandira malipiro kudzera mwa iyo pa netiweki yanu ya Ultimate Multisite.

_**Zindikirani: GoCardless imagwira ntchito kokha ndi ndalama zotsatirazi:**_ **EUR, GBP, AUD, SEK, ndi DKK.**

## Kuyika Add-on {#installing-the-add-on}

Choyamba, muyenera kuyika add-on ya GoCardless ya Ultimate Multisite. Mungachite zimenezo popita ku **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Add-ons (pa sidebar)**. Kenako sankhani add-on ya GoCardless pa mndandandawo.

Add-on ikayikidwa ndi kuyambitsidwa, pitani ku **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Payments**, yatsani njira ya GoCardless, ndipo sungani.

![Zokonda za njira yolipirira](/img/config/settings-payment-gateways.png)

## Kupeza Access Token {#getting-an-access-token}

Pangani account pa _GoCardless_ (kapena pa sandbox ya _GoCardless_, ngati mukufuna **kuyesa kaye** , zomwe zikulimbikitsidwa). Kenako, mutalowa, pitani ku chinthu cha menyu cha **Developers**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: GoCardless dashboard showing Developers menu position -->

Pa tsamba la **Developers**, dinani batani la **Create** pakona yakumanja yakumtunda ya sikirini, kenako dinani njira ya **Access Token**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: GoCardless Developers page with Create button and Access Token option -->

Izi zidzatsegula zenera latsopano la modal, kukulolani kupanga tokeni yatsopano. Ipatseni dzina losavuta kuzindikira ndipo onetsetsani kuti mwasankha njira ya **Read-write access** mu menyu ya **Scope**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: GoCardless modal for creating new access token with Read-write scope -->

Access token idzawoneka pa sikirini. Koperani imeneyo ndipo muyiike mu menyu yanu ya **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Payments > GoCardless**.

Onetsetsani kuti mwayiyika pa field yoyenera. Ngati ndi **live toke** **n** , zimitsani **sandbox mode** musanayike tokeni.

![Zokonda za njira yolipirira ndi GoCardless](/img/config/settings-payment-gateways.png)

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: GoCardless token fields in Ultimate Multisite payment settings -->

Zatha pa sitepe yoyamba. Kenako, muyenera kukhazikitsa womvera wa webhook.

## Kukhazikitsa Womvera wa Webhook {#setting-up-the-webhook-listener}

Mukamagwira ntchito ndi masubscription, Ultimate Multisite imafunika kumva za zosintha pa subscription status, malipiro atsopano omwe akuchitika, ndi zinthu zina zotere.

Njira yomwe njira zolipirira zimadziwitsira Ultimate Multisite za zosinthazo ndi kugwiritsa ntchito **ma webhook calls**. Pachifukwa chimenecho, kuti kulumikizanaku kugwire ntchito 100%, tiyenera kuuza _GoCardless_ URL yomwe iyenera kuitana ndi zonsezo.

Bwererani ku panel yanu ya **GoCardless > Developers** ndipo dinaninso batani la Create, koma nthawi ino, sankhani njira ya **Webhook endpoint**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: GoCardless Developers page with Webhook endpoint option -->Zenera latsopano lidzatseguka ndi zosankha za webhook endpoint. Limafunsa webhook URL, choncho bwererani ku **Ultimate Multisite settings > Payments > GoCardless** yanu ndipo koperani webhook URL yomwe yalembedwamo:

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Ultimate Multisite GoCardless settings showing webhook URL -->

Kuphatikizanso, mungalowe **webhook secret** yomwe idzagwiritsidwa ntchito kusaina ma webhook calls anu. Chilichonse chomwe mungalowe mu field imeneyo, muyenera kulowetsanso mu **Ultimate Multisite Webhook Secret field**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: GoCardless webhook endpoint configuration form with URL and secret fields -->

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Ultimate Multisite GoCardless webhook secret field -->

Malizani posunga endpoint podina batani la **Create webhook endpoint** kenako **sungani zokonda zanu za Ultimate Multisite nawonso**.

Muyenera kukhala okonzeka kulandira _GoCardless_ tsopano!
