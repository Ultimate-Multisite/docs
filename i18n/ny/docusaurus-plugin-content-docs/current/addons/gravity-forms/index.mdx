# Gravity Forms

**Sinthani momwe olembetsa ogwiritsa ntchito amalembera ndi mphamvu ya Gravity Forms**

Phatikizani mosavuta chomanga mafomu chapamwamba kwambiri padziko lonse ndi Multisite Ultimate kuti mupange mafomu olembetsa okongola, osintha alendo kukhala olembetsa ambiri omwe amalipira, omwe amakulitsa kulembetsa kwa umembala wanu ndi kuwonjezera ndalama.

---

## ✨ Chifukwa Chiyani Musankhe Kuphatikizaku?

### 🎯 **Wonjezerani Kutembenuka Mpaka 40%**
Sinthani mafomu a checkout osasangalatsa a default ndi Gravity Forms okongola, osinthidwa mwamakonda omwe amatembenuza alendo kukhala makasitomala olipira.

### 🛠️ **Kusinthasintha Kwakukulu**
Lumikizani field iliyonse ya form ku data point iliyonse ya Multisite Ultimate. Sonkhanitsani ndendende zambiri zomwe mukufuna, momwe mukuzifunira.

### 💳 **Njira Zambiri Zolipirira**
Thandizani checkout redirect mwachindunji komanso kuphatikizana ndi WooCommerce kuti mukhale ndi kugwirizana kwakukulu ndi payment gateway.

### ⚡ **Palibe Coding Yofunika**
Kukonza mwa point-and-click ndi mawonekedwe a admin osavuta kumva. Palibe luso laukadaulo lofunika.

## 🌟 Zinthu Zofunika Kwambiri

### 📋 **Kumanga Form Kwaukadaulo**
- **Mafomu a magawo angapo** a njira zovuta zolembetsa
- **Conditional logic** yosonyeza/kubisa mafield mwamphamvu
- **Kukweza mafayilo** a zikalata ndi zithunzi
- **Mitundu ya field yolemera**: ma signature, ma calculation, kusankha product
- **Mafomu ogwirizana ndi mafoni** omwe amaoneka bwino pa chipangizo chilichonse

### 🔗 **Kulumikiza Data Mosavuta**
- Mawonekedwe a **visual field mapping**
- **Zambiri za kasitomala**: mayina, email, billing details
- **Data ya umembala**: mapulani, nthawi, custom fields
- **Kupanga site**: mitu, ma URL, kusankha template
- **Kusonkhanitsa data mwamakonda** pa zosowa zanu zenizeni

### 💰 **Kukonza Malipiro Mosinthasintha**
- **Checkout redirect**: kusintha kosavuta kupita ku masamba a malipiro a Multisite Ultimate
- **Kuphatikizana ndi WooCommerce**: gwiritsani ntchito ma payment gateway mazana ambiri
- Thandizo la **mayesero aulere ndi zotsatsa**
- **Kupanga invoice yokha** ndi kupanga ma receipt

### 🎨 **Zochitika za Wogwiritsa Ntchito Zaukadaulo**
- **Masamba otsimikizira mwamakonda** okhala ndi merge tags
- **Zidziwitso za email zokha** kwa makasitomala ndi ma admin
- **Zizindikiro za kupita patsogolo** pa mafomu a magawo angapo
- **Kutsimikizira zolakwika** ndi uthenga wothandiza
- **Kugwirizana kwa brand** ndi kapangidwe ka site yanu

### 🛡️ **Chitetezo cha Mulingo wa Enterprise**
- **Chitetezo ku spam** ndi zosankha za CAPTCHA zomangidwa mkati
- **Kutsimikizira data** ndi kuyeretsa
- Njira zabwino za **chitetezo cha WordPress**
- Zinthu za **kutsatira GDPR**
- **Kukweza mafayilo motetezeka** ndi kusunga
