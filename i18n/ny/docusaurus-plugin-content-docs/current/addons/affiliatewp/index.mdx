# AffiliateWP {#affiliatewp}

Multisite Ultimate: Kuphatikiza kwa AffiliateWP

Gwiritsani ntchito AffiliateWP yamphamvu kuti mukulitse gulu la makasitomala la netiweki yanu ya Ultimo!

## Kuyika {#installation}

1. Kwezani 'ultimate-multisite-git ' ku chikwatu cha '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Yambitsani plugin kudzera mu menyu ya 'Mapulagini' mu WordPress
3. Tsatirani Wizard pang’onopang’ono kuti mukonze plugin

## Kalozera Wogwiritsa Ntchito {#usage-guide}

[AffiliateWP](https://affiliatewp.com/) yakhala yankho lodalirika kwambiri mukamapanga pulogalamu ya affiliate ya bizinesi yanu yapaintaneti. Ultimate Multisite: AffiliateWP ndi mlatho womwe ungakuthandizeni kumanga chinthu chomwecho pa netiweki yanu yapamwamba.

## Kuyamba {#getting-started}

Choyamba, **muyenera kukhala ndi** [**AffiliateWP**](https://affiliatewp.com/) **yoyatsidwa**! Simungathe kugwiritsa ntchito chowonjezera chathu ngati mulibe **plugin yomwe imalumikizana nayo**. Ngati simukufuna kulola makasitomala anu kuti nawonso ayigwiritse ntchito pa masamba awo, AffiliateWP iyenera kukhala yoyatsidwa kokha pa tsamba lanu lalikulu.

Komanso, mufunika kuti **Ultimate Multisite** ndi **Ultimate Multisite: AffiliateWP** zonse **ziyatsidwe** **pa netiweki yonse**.

## Kuwonjezera Ultimate Multisite ngati Kuphatikiza kwa AffiliateWP {#adding-ultimate-multisite-as-an-affiliatewp-integration}

Pa tsamba lanu lalikulu, pitani ku Zokonda za Affiliates, ndipo pa tabu ya Kuphatikiza, sankhani Ultimate Multisite. Izi zidzauza [AffiliateWP](https://affiliatewp.com/) kuyamba kutsata maulendo ndi zosintha kuchokera mu njira yolembetsa ya Ultimate Multisite.

<!-- Chithunzi sichikupezeka: tabu ya zokonda za Kuphatikiza kwa AffiliateWP yokhala ndi bokosi losankha la Ultimate Multisite -->

_Sankhani bokosi la Ultimate Multisite pansi ndipo sungani_

N’zimenezo basi zomwe zimafunika. Maulendo ochokera ku maulalo otumizira adzatsatidwa ndipo wogwiritsa ntchitoyo akadzapanga malipiro ake oyamba, kusintha kudzapangidwa ndipo komishoni idzasungidwa pambali kwa affiliate amene ali ndi udindo pa kusinthako.

Komabe, malipiro oyamba okha omwe alandiridwa kuchokera kwa wogwiritsa ntchito watsopano ndi omwe adzatsatidwe. Kuti muyambitse malipiro obwerezabwereza a zotumizira, mufunika [AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals Add-on](https://affiliatewp.com/add-ons/pro/recurring-referrals/)

## Kuyambitsa Zotumizira Zobwerezabwereza {#enabling-recurring-referrals}

Mukayika [AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals Add-on](https://affiliatewp.com/add-ons/pro/recurring-referrals/), pitani ku tabu ya Zokonda za Affiliates ya Zotumizira Zobwerezabwereza, ndipo yambitsani kugwiritsa ntchito zotumizira zobwerezabwereza.

<!-- Chithunzi sichikupezeka: zokonda za AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals zokhala ndi chosinthira cha Enable Recurring Referrals -->

_Zosavuta kwambiri: Yambitsani Zotumizira Zobwerezabwereza ndipo mwakonzeka!_

Apanso, n’zimenezo basi zomwe zimafunika!

Tsopano, nthawi iliyonse malipiro atsopano akalandiridwa pa Account yotumizidwa, affiliate amene ali ndi udindo adzalandira gawo lake lokha!
