# Support Tickets {#support-tickets}

**Support Tickets** ndi addon yamphamvu ya Ultimate Multisite yomwe imawonjezera dongosolo lonse la matikiti a chithandizo ku netiweki yanu ya WordPress. Ndi yabwino kwa mapulatifomu a SaaS, opereka WaaS (Website as a Service), ndi ma netiweki a multisite omwe amafunika kupereka chithandizo kwa makasitomala pa mulingo wa netiweki komanso wa tsamba lililonse.

## Chifukwa Chiyani Musankhe Support Tickets? {#why-choose-support-tickets}

* **Chithandizo cha Milingo Yambiri**: Ma admin a netiweki amasamalira matikiti a netiweki yonse pamene eni masamba amasamalira chithandizo cha makasitomala awo
* **Kayendedwe Kantchito Kaukalamba**: Kasamalidwe kokwanira ka moyo wa tikiti ndi zofunika mwachangu, ma status, ndi magawidwe
* **Zochitika za Makasitomala**: Mawonekedwe okongola, oyankha bwino omwe amapangitsa kuti makasitomala apeze thandizo mosavuta
* **Yotetezeka & Yodalirika**: Yomangidwa ndi njira zabwino kwambiri za chitetezo cha WordPress ndipo yakonzedwa kuti database izigwira ntchito bwino
* **Kuphatikizana Kosalala**: Imagwira bwino kwambiri ndi kasamalidwe ka makasitomala ndi masamba ka Ultimate Multisite

## Zinthu Zazikulu {#key-features}

**Kasamalidwe Kokwanira ka Matikiti**

* Dongosolo lapadera la manambala a matikiti kuti zikhale zosavuta kuzitchula
* Milingo ya zofunika mwachangu yomwe ingasinthidwe (Yotsika, Yabwinobwino, Yapamwamba, Yofunika Kwambiri)
* Kayendedwe kokwanira ka status (Lotseguka, Likuchitika, Likudikirira, Lathetsedwa, Latsekedwa)
* Kugawa matikiti m'magulu (Zonse, Zaukadaulo, Zolipira, Pempho la Mbali Yatsopano, Lipoti la Vuto)
* Gawirani matikiti kwa othandizira ndi kusankha kugawa kokha basi

**Zokambirana Zotsatizana**

* Kutsatizana kwathunthu kwa zokambirana pa mayankho a tikiti
* Maonekedwe osiyana a mayankho a ogwira ntchito ndi a makasitomala
* Zolemba zachinsinsi za ogwira ntchito zomwe makasitomala sangathe kuona
* Mawonekedwe a nthawi yeniyeni oyendetsedwa ndi AJAX
* Editor wamakono wa mawu wokhala ndi zosankha zokonza maonekedwe

**Mafayilo Ophatikizidwa**

* Kukweza mafayilo kotetezeka ndi chithandizo cha kukoka-ndi-kusiya
* Zoletsa za mtundu ndi kukula kwa fayilo zomwe zingasinthidwe
* Chitetezo chapamwamba chokhala ndi kuzindikira mafayilo owopsa
* Kutsitsa kotetezedwa ndi maulamuliro a mwayi wolowa

**Zidziwitso Zanzeru**

* Zidziwitso za email zokha basi pa matikiti atsopano, mayankho, ndi kusintha kwa status
* Ma template a email omwe angasinthidwe
* Zidziwitso zogwirizana ndi maudindo kwa ogwira ntchito ndi makasitomala
* Zosintha za template zosinthika za mauthenga opangidwa payekha

**Mawonekedwe Amphamvu a Admin**

* Mndandanda wapamwamba wa matikiti wokhala ndi kusanja ndi kusefa
* Kusintha mwachangu kwa status, zofunika mwachangu, ndi magawidwe
* Zochita zambiri nthawi imodzi kuti kasamalidwe kakhale kogwira mtima
* Dashboard ya ziwerengero yokhala ndi miyeso ya magwiridwe antchito
* Paneli ya zokonda yokwanira

**Portal Yokongola ya Makasitomala**

* Mawonekedwe oyera, oyankha bwino pa zida zonse
* Kapangidwe koyambira pa mafoni
* Kufufuza ndi kusefa kwapamwamba
* Editor wamakono wokhala ndi toolbar yokonza maonekedwe
* Mafomu osavuta kugwiritsa ntchito potumiza tikiti

**Kuphatikizana Kosinthika**

* Gwiritsani ntchito ma shortcode kapena zinthu za UI za Ultimate Multisite
* Onetsani mafomu ndi mindandanda ya matikiti kulikonse pa tsamba lanu
* Imagwira mosalala ndi kapangidwe ka netiweki ya multisite
* Ingakulitsidwe ndi ma hook ndi ma filter

## Yabwino Kwa {#perfect-for}

* Mapulatifomu a SaaS omwe amafunikira chithandizo cha makasitomala
* Opereka WaaS omwe amasamalira masamba angapo a makasitomala
* Masamba a umembala okhala ndi chithandizo cha milingo
* Oyang'anira netiweki omwe amasamalira ma brand angapo
* Eni masamba omwe amapereka ntchito yothandiza makasitomala

## Kuyika {#installation}

1. Onetsetsani kuti plugin ya Ultimate Multisite yayikidwa ndipo yatsegulidwa pa netiweki yanu ya WordPress
2. Kwezani mafayilo a addon ku directory yanu ya `/wp-content/plugins/`
3. Tsegulani plugin pa netiweki kudzera mu menyu ya 'Plugins' mu WordPress
4. Pitani ku Network Admin > Support Tickets kuti mukonze zokonda zanu

## Mafunso Ofunsidwa Kawirikawiri {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Kodi izi zimafuna Ultimate Multisite? {#does-this-require-ultimate-multisite}

Inde, addon iyi imafuna kuti Ultimate Multisite ikhale yoyikidwa ndi yogwira ntchito. Imaphatikizana mozama ndi zinthu za kasamalidwe ka makasitomala ndi masamba za Ultimate Multisite.

## Kodi eni masamba angasamalire chithandizo cha makasitomala awo? {#can-site-owners-handle-their-own-customer-support}

Ndithudi! Eni masamba angagwiritse ntchito dongosolo la matikiti kuti apereke chithandizo kwa makasitomala awo, pamene ma admin a netiweki angasamalire matikiti a chithandizo a netiweki yonse.

## Kodi makasitomala amatumiza bwanji matikiti? {#how-do-customers-submit-tickets}

Mungawonjezere mafomu otumizira matikiti kulikonse pa tsamba lanu pogwiritsa ntchito ma shortcode kapena zinthu za UI za Ultimate Multisite. Makasitomala amangodzaza fomu ndikutumiza pempho lawo.

## Kodi ndingasinthe ma status ndi zofunika mwachangu za tikiti? {#can-i-customize-the-ticket-statuses-and-priorities}

Inde! Mungasinthe kwathunthu milingo ya zofunika mwachangu, ma status, ndi mitundu ya matikiti mu zokonda za admin wa netiweki kuti zigwirizane ndi kayendedwe kanu ka chithandizo.

## Kodi imathandiza mafayilo ophatikizidwa? {#does-it-support-file-attachments}

Inde, makasitomala ndi ogwira ntchito onse angaphatikize mafayilo ku matikiti ndi mayankho. Muli ndi ulamuliro wonse pa mitundu ya mafayilo ololedwa ndi malire a kukula kuti mukhale ndi chitetezo.

## Kodi zidziwitso zimasamalidwa bwanji? {#how-are-notifications-handled}

Dongosololi limatumiza zokha basi zidziwitso za email pa matikiti atsopano, mayankho, ndi kusintha kwa status. Mungasinthe ma template onse a email mu zokonda.

## Kodi ndingagawire matikiti kwa othandizira enieni? {#can-i-assign-tickets-to-specific-support-agents}

Inde, mungagawire matikiti pamanja kwa othandizira kapena kuyatsa kugawa kokha basi kuti matikiti agawidwe zokha.

## Kodi pali zolemba zachinsinsi za ogwira ntchito? {#are-there-private-notes-for-staff}

Inde, ogwira ntchito angawonjezere mayankho achinsinsi omwe amaoneka kwa ogwira ntchito ena okha, osati kwa makasitomala.

## Kodi ndi yosavuta kugwiritsa ntchito pa foni? {#is-it-mobile-friendly}

Ndithudi! Mawonekedwe onse amamangidwa ndi njira yoyambira pa mafoni ndipo amagwira ntchito mokongola pa zida zonse.

## Kodi ndingasonyeze mindandanda ndi mafomu a matikiti pogwiritsa ntchito ma shortcode? {#can-i-display-ticket-lists-and-forms-using-shortcodes}

Inde! Ma shortcode omwe alipo:
* `[wu_support_tickets]` - Sonyezani mndandanda wa matikiti a kasitomala
* `[wu_support_ticket_form]` - Sonyezani fomu yotumizira tikiti yatsopano
* `[wu_support_ticket_view ticket_id="123"]` - Sonyezani tikiti inayake

## Ndi mitundu yanji ya mafayilo yomwe ingakwezedwe? {#what-file-types-can-be-uploaded}

Mungakonze mitundu ya mafayilo ololedwa mu zokonda. Dongosololi limaphatikizapo kusanthula kwapamwamba kwa chitetezo kuti liteteze ku mafayilo owopsa.

## Kodi ndingatumize deta ya matikiti kunja? {#can-i-export-ticket-data}

Inde, addon iyi imaphatikizapo kuthekera kotumiza kunja deta ya matikiti ndi ziwerengero.
