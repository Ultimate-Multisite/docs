# Mafunso Oyamba Ozindikira

**Mafunso Oyamba Ozindikira** ndi sitepe yoyamba mu njira yopangira mapangidwe ya Theme Builder. Amasonkhanitsa zambiri zokhudza site yanu, bizinesi yanu, ndi zokonda zanu za kapangidwe kuti apange theme yokonzedwa mwapadera kwa inu.

## Chidule

Mafunso Oyamba Ozindikira amakutsogolerani kudzera mu mafunso angapo kuti amvetsetse mtundu wanu, omvera omwe mukufuna kuwafikira, ndi zolinga za kapangidwe. Mu mtundu 1.16.0, mafunsowa tsopano akuphatikiza **sitepe yokweza zithunzi** yomwe imakulolani kupereka chilimbikitso chowoneka cha kapangidwe kanu.

## Masitepe a Mafunso

### 1. Zambiri za Bizinesi

- Dzina la site ndi tagline
- Mtundu wa bizinesi kapena gawo la ntchito
- Zolinga zazikulu za bizinesi
- Kufotokozera omvera omwe mukufuna kuwafikira

### 2. Zokonda za Kapangidwe

- Dongosolo la mitundu lomwe mumakonda
- Mtundu wa kapangidwe (wamakono, wakale, wochepa, wosangalatsa, ndi zina zotero)
- Mawebusaiti kapena mitundu yokulimbikitsani
- Zinthu za kapangidwe zomwe ziyenera kukhalapo

### 3. Kukweza Zithunzi (Chatsopano mu v1.16.0)

Kwezani chithunzi chimodzi kapena zingapo kuti zikhudze njira ya kapangidwe:

#### Mafomati Ovomerezeka

| Fomati | Extension | Tsatanetsatane |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| **JPEG** | `.jpg`, `.jpeg` | Yolimbikitsidwa pa zithunzi |
| **PNG** | `.png` | Imathandiza kuonekera momveka |
| **WebP** | `.webp` | Fomati yamakono, kukula kwa fayilo kochepa |
| **GIF** | `.gif` | Zithunzi zosayenda zokha (palibe animation) |

#### Zofunikira za Fayilo

- **Kukula kwakukulu kwa fayilo:** 10 MB
- **Miyeso yochepa:** 400 × 300 pixels
- **Miyeso yolimbikitsidwa:** 1200 × 800 pixels kapena kupitirira
- **Chiŵerengero cha mbali:** Chiŵerengero chilichonse ndi chovomerezeka

#### Momwe Zithunzi Zimakhudzira Njira ya Kapangidwe

Zithunzi zomwe mumakweza zimathandiza Theme Builder kumvetsetsa:

- **Gulu la mitundu** — mitundu yomwe imawoneka kwambiri imatulutsidwa ndi kugwiritsidwa ntchito mu theme
- **Mtundu wowoneka** — wamakono, wachilengedwe, wochepa, wowala, ndi zina zotero.
- **Maganizo ndi mlengalenga** — waluso, wopanga zinthu, waubwenzi, wapamwamba, ndi zina zotero.
- **Zinthu za kapangidwe** — ma pattern, ma texture, ndi masitayelo a kapangidwe ka zinthu pamodzi
- **Chilimbikitso cha typography** — masitayelo a font omwe amagwirizana ndi theme yowoneka

### 4. Kapangidwe ka Zomwe Zili

- Masamba akuluakulu ofunikira (Home, About, Services, Contact, ndi zina zotero)
- Gawo la blog kapena nkhani likufunika
- Ntchito za e-commerce zikufunika
- Zinthu zapadera kapena kuphatikiza

### 5. Kubwereza ndi Kutsimikizira

- Chidule cha zambiri zonse zoperekedwa
- Kutsimikizira kapangidwe kasanayambe kupangidwa

## Njira Zabwino za Kukweza Zithunzi

### Zomwe Muyenera Kukweza

- **Chilimbikitso cha mtundu** — ma logo, zitsanzo za mitundu, kapena malangizo a mtundu
- **Ma site a opikisana nawo** — zithunzi za ma site omwe mumawakonda
- **Mood boards** — magulu a zithunzi zomwe zikuyimira kukongola komwe mukufuna
- **Zithunzi za zinthu zogulitsa** — ngati muli ndi zinthu zoti muwonetse
- **Zithunzi za gulu kapena ofesi** — pa branding yaumwini kapena yaukadaulo
- **Zithunzi za moyo** — zikuyimira omvera omwe mukufuna kuwafikira

### Zomwe Muyenera Kupewa

- **Zithunzi za khalidwe lotsika** — zithunzi zosawoneka bwino kapena za ma pixel
- **Zithunzi zosagwirizana** — zithunzi zomwe siziyimira mtundu wanu
- **Zithunzi zokhala ndi mawu ambiri** — zithunzi za skrini zokhala ndi mawu ambiri
- **Zinthu zokhala ndi copyright** — onetsetsani kuti muli ndi ufulu wogwiritsa ntchito zithunzizo
- **Ma GIF oyenda** — zithunzi zosayenda zokha ndi zomwe zimakonzedwa

### Malangizo a Zotsatira Zabwino Kwambiri

1. **Kwezani zithunzi 2-3** — zithunzi zingapo zimapereka nkhani yabwino
2. **Gwiritsani ntchito zithunzi za khalidwe lapamwamba** — zithunzi zomveka, zowala bwino zimagwira ntchito bwino kwambiri
3. **Phatikizani kusiyanasiyana** — kusakaniza kwa mitundu, sitayelo, ndi mutu
4. **Perekani nkhani** — fotokozani mwachidule chifukwa chimene mwasankhira chithunzi chilichonse
5. **Khalani mwatsatanetsatane** — ngati mumakonda chinthu china, chisonyezeni

## Zotsatira za Njira ya Kapangidwe

Pambuyo pa Mafunso Oyamba Ozindikira, Theme Builder imapanga:

- **Gulu la mitundu** — mitundu yayikulu, yachiwiri, ndi yowonjezera
- **Typography** — mabanja a font a mitu ndi mawu a thupi
- **Sitayelo ya layout** — mipata, kulinganiza, ndi kukonza ma component
- **Zinthu zowoneka** — ma icon, ma pattern, ndi zinthu zokongoletsa
- **Mood board** — chiwonetsero chowoneka cha njira ya kapangidwe

## Masitepe Otsatira

Mukamaliza Mafunso Oyamba Ozindikira:

1. Onaninso njira ya kapangidwe yomwe yapangidwa
2. Perekani ndemanga kapena pemphani kusintha
3. Pitirizani ku sitepe ya [Njira ya Kapangidwe](./design-direction.md)
4. Theme Builder imapanga theme yanu yapadera
