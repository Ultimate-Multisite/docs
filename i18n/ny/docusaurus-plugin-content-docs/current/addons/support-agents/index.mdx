# Othandizira Pa Thandizo {#support-agents}

Kuyang'anira netiweki yonse wekha si ntchito yosavuta. Netiweki yanu ikayamba kukula, mudzayamba kuona kufunikira kwa thandizo kukuwonjezeka. Lingakhale lingaliro labwino kubweretsa anthu omwe angakuthandizeni kuchepetsa ntchito yanu. Komabe, kupereka mwayi/chilolezo chonse ndi koopsa ndipo kungasokoneze netiweki yanu. Apa ndiye pamene chowonjezera cha Othandizira Pa Thandizochi chimabwera.

Chowonjezerachi chimakulolani kupanga mtundu watsopano wa wogwiritsa ntchito WordPress wotchedwa Wothandizira Pa Thandizo, ndipo chimakupatsani ulamuliro wonse pa zilolezo zomwe wogwiritsa ntchitoyo ali nazo. Kupereka chilolezo n'kosavuta ngati kuyatsa batani losinthira, ndipo kulizimitsa mukaganiza chochotsa chilolezocho.

## Zinthu Zake {#features}

- Mndandanda watsatanetsatane wa zilolezo za WordPress & Ultimate Multisite
- Yatsani & zimitsani mwayi mosavuta
- Pangani ma Account a Agent opanda malire

## Kuyika {#installation}

1. Kwezani 'ultimate-multisite-support-agents' ku chikwatu cha '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Yambitsani chowonjezeracho kudzera pa menyu ya 'Zowonjezera' mu WordPress
3. Tsatirani Wizard sitepe ndi sitepe kuti mukonze chowonjezeracho
