# Woyang'anira Mapulagini & Mitu

Multisite Ultimate: Woyang'anira Pulagini ndi Mutu

Sinthani kapena bisani zambiri za meta (mutu, wolemba, thumbnail ndi kufotokozera) za mapulagini ndi mitu yoikidwa. Pangani magulu a mapulagini ndi mitu kuti mulole ogwiritsa ntchito anu kuwasankha mwa kusefa, ndipo onjezani tsamba lokongola la Mapulagini lopangidwa mwamakonda ku panel ya ogwiritsa ntchito anu.

Kuti mugwiritse ntchito pulaginiyi, mungopita ku masamba anu a Mapulagini kapena Mitu pa admin wa netiweki yanu ndipo dinani pa "Sinthani zambiri za Pulagini/Mutu".

## Kuyika {#installation}

1. Kwezani 'ultimate-multisite-plugin-and-theme-manager' ku chikwatu cha '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Yambitsani pulagini kudzera mu menyu ya 'Mapulagini' mu WordPress Network Admin yanu

## Kalozera Wogwiritsa Ntchito {#usage-guide}

Ultimate Multisite: Woyang'anira Pulagini ndi Mutu ndi chowonjezera champhamvu cha **premium** cha Ultimate Multisite chomwe chimakupatsani ulamuliro wonse pa momwe ogwiritsa ntchito netiweki yanu amaonera ndi kugwirizana ndi zosankha za pulagini ndi mutu pa netiweki yanu.

Chowonjezerachi chili ndi zolinga zitatu:

  1. Chimakulolani kusintha kwathunthu metadata ya mapulagini ndi mitu. Izi zikuphatikizapo dzina, kufotokozera, dzina la wolemba, komanso ma thumbnail!

  2. Chimakulolani kugawa mapulagini ndi mitu m'magulu, kuti ogwiritsa ntchito anu athe kuwasankha mosavuta.

  3. Chimakupatsani mwayi wosintha tsamba lakale, losasangalatsa la mapulagini ndi tsamba latsopano lofanana ndi la mitu lokhala ndi kusankha malinga ndi magulu.

Mukatsitsa ndi kuyiyika, tabu yatsopano - _Zokonda za chowonjezera_ \- idzaoneka pa tsamba la Zokonda za Ultimate Multisite.
