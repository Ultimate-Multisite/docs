### Kusintha Checkout Form

Muli ndi ulemu wanu womwe mungagwiritse ntchito za zinthu zosiyanasiyana. M'malo omwe muli ndi imeneyi, mudzakhudza kuphunzira form ya registration.

Poyamba, poyamba m'Editor ya checkout form, muli ndi kusankha dzina (kuti zimene zidzakhudza kwa chifukwa cha ndani) ndi slug (kuti zimene zidzakhudza kuti muphathe shortcodes, mwachitsanzo).

![Checkout Form editor](/img/config/checkout-form-editor.png)

Forms zimene zikuyenera ndi steps ndi fields. Mungagwiritse ntchito step wosiyanasiyana monga kuphunzira "Add New Checkout Step".

![Add New Checkout Step](/img/config/checkout-form-add-step.png)

Pa tab yoyamba ya modal window, muli ndi kusankha malo kwa step ya form yanu. Kodi muli ndi ID, dzina (name), ndi mawu ofunika (description). Zinthu zina zimene zikuyenera kutali kwa ndani.

![Checkout form step content tab](/img/config/checkout-form-step-content.png)

Mmodzi, muli ndi kusankha kukhala kwa step. Mungosankha monga "Always show" (Kukhala kwa nthawi zonse), "Only show for logged in users" (Kukhala kwa anthu omwe ali ndi account), kapena "Only show for guests" (Kukhala kwa wosavomerezeka).

![Checkout form step visibility tab](/img/config/checkout-form-step-visibility.png)

Mmodzi, muli ndi kusankha stili ya step. Zizi zina zimene zikuyenera kutali.

![Checkout form step style tab](/img/config/checkout-form-step-style.png)

Kuti muli ndi fields pa step yomwe yoyamba, mudzakhudza kuphunzira "Add New Field" ndipo musankhe aina ya section yomwe mukuwoneza.

![Checkout form step with fields](/img/config/checkout-form-step-with-fields.png)

Kukati kofunsi (field) kofunsi zina ziri ziri zomwe zimayenera kuwera. Kwa kuingilira m'malo woyamba, tutachokera kofunsi ya "Username".

![Field type selection dropdown](/img/config/checkout-form-field-type-dropdown.png)

### Fomu ya Mwayi wa Mapulogera (The Pricing Table Field)

Fomu ya **Pricing Table** (Mwayi wa Mapulogera) imwoneza mapulogera anu pa fomu ya checkout kuti makalata (customers) angathe chifukisa plan. Momwe muli kukhazikitsa fomu ino, muli ndi zochokera zambiri zomwe mungayesulure:

![Pricing Table field settings](/img/config/pricing-table-field-settings.png)

Izi ndiyo momwe Pricing Table imwoneka pa fomu ya registration ya m'moyo (frontend):

![Frontend checkout pricing table](/img/config/frontend-checkout-pricing-table.png)

  * **Products**: Mupereka kuti muli ndi mapulogera onse omwe mungamoneke ndipo muli ndi uthenga woperekera bwanji.
  * **Force Different Durations**: Momwe muli kukhazikitsa, onse mapulogera amwoneka osati muli ndi chifukisa cha mapulogera (price variation) lomwe limakwaniridwa kwa mpaka woperekedwa wina. Momwe muli kukhazikitsa (default), mapulogera osati ndi chifukisa lomwe limakwaniridwa kwa mpaka woperekedwa wina amwoneka.
    ![Pricing table force durations option](/img/config/pricing-table-force-durations.png)
  * **Hide when Pre-Selected**: Imalipira Pricing Table pamene plan imene imene poyenera m'URL (mwachitsanzo, `/register/premium`).
  * **Pricing Table Template**: Mupereka template ya zithunzi yoperekera kwa Pricing Table (Simple List, Legacy, osati).

Ngati muli kukhazikitsa mapulogera pa Pricing Table poyenera fomu imene imayenera kuti muchezedwe (complete) mfundo ya checkout ya mapulogera, editor imalipira chisoni. Gumule chisoni lili ndi chisoni lokonza (warning) kuti muli ndi chifukisa chomwe chimaperekedwa poyenera mopereka chifukisa chofunikira poyenera kupereka mapulogera kapena kukhazikitsa machitidwe osati.

### Kufamba Kufamba Ntchito Yoperekera Mfundo ya Mulungu

Ngati muliye yelekeze [Price Variations](creating-your-first-subscription-product#price-variations) pa zerevu (mifano, kukhala kwa mwezi na mwaka), muliye mwayi **Period Selection** field ku form yanu ya checkout. Field iyi imaliza toggle iliyoperekera wamene kuti mwayiye m'maka ya kulipira, ndipo table ya bei imalimbikira kwa wakati wote.

#### Kufamba Chombo 1: Kufamba Ntchito Yoperekera Mfundo pa Zerevu Zanu

Mmodzi mwa zerevu, mwayiye kuti zili ndi price variations zikufotokozedwa. Mwayiye ku **Ultimate Multisite > Products**, mwayiye product imodzi, ndipo mwayiye ku tab ya **Price Variations** kuti mwayiye billing periods zina (mifano: Annual kwa mphamvu yofunika).

![Price Variations tab on a product](/img/config/product-price-variations-tab.png)

#### Kufamba Chombo 2: Kufamba Ntchito Yoperekera Mfundo pa Form Yanu ya Checkout

1. Mwayiye ku **Ultimate Multisite > Checkout Forms** ndipo mwayiye checkout form yanu.

2. Scroll lero kupita ku step lomwe limayenera **Pricing Table** field ndipo mwayiye **Add new Field**.

3. M'maka ya kusankha aina ya field, mwayiye **Period Select**.

![Add New Field dialog showing Period Select](/img/config/checkout-form-add-field-dialog.png)

4. Kufotokozera zinthu za period. Kofunika kuti ndalama zina:
   * **Duration**: Iyi ndi anthu (mifano: `1`)
   * **Duration unit**: Iyi ndi aina ya period (Days, Weeks, Months, kapena Years)
   * **Label**: Iyi ndi mawu omwe wamene adzakhulupirira (mifano: "Monthly", "Annual")

5. Mwayiye **+ Add Option** kuti mwayiye zinthu zina za period. Zinthu zina zimene kukhala zofanana ndi price variations zomwe mwayiye pa zerevu zanu.

![Period Selection field settings](/img/config/period-selection-field-settings.png)

6. Mwayiye **Period Selector Template** (Clean ndi yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yomwe yom

#### Step 3: Panga Koma Field Upambira ya Pricing Table

Kuti mwanafunzi ampe uzozi woyelekeza, kuli mpe kuti Period Selection field ipereke **mbele** ya Pricing Table field m'checkout step yanu. #### Kuti Zikufunika M'frontend

Puma muli ndi kukhazikitsa, abwino amene akulowa pa registration page yanu adzakhulupatsa period selector m'mbele ya pricing table. Pamene amayamba ku-click billing period yosiyanasiyana:

  * Pricing table idzakhazikitsidwa m'malo woyelekeza kuti zikhulupatseni bei za period yomwe yali yoperekedwa (samayenda page).
  * Ngati **Force Different Durations** imapangidwa kuti iye, zinthu zomwe sizi ndi variation ya bei kwa period yomwe yali yoperekedwa zimapangidwa kuti zikuyesa.
  * Ngati **Force Different Durations** imapangidwa kuti iye, zonse za products zimayeneza komwe sizi ndi variation kwa period yomwe yali yoperekedwa (zizakhulupatsa bei yomwe yali yoperekedwa).

#### Kukhazikitsa Period Selection M'URL

Mungathe kudzagwira pre-select product ndi billing period m'URL. Ultimate Multisite imapambana ndi URL patternsizi zimene:

  * `/register/premium` — Imapangidwa kuti iye "Premium" product pafupi
  * `/register/premium/12` — Imapangidwa kuti iye product ndi mwezi woyelekeza wa 12 months
  * `/register/premium/1/year` — Imapangidwa kuti iye product ndi mwezi woyelekeza wa mwaka 1

### Template Selection Field

Template Selection field limapangidwa kuti abwino amene akulowa ampole site template m'checkout. Iyi imapambana kwa default m'single step nd inu multi-step checkout form templates zomwe zimapambanira m'Ultimate Multisite v2.6.1.

#### Kukhazikitsa field ndi chifukwa cha m'manual



Muli mukufuna ndi form yomwe imaperekedwa m'mene omwe uliyi m'mene wa v2.6.1, kapena yomwe yachotsegula kuchokera kwa template yosadzi:

1. Tiyeni ku **Ultimate Multisite > Checkout Forms** ndikupereka form yomwe imaperekedwa.
2. M'mene muli m'mene wopereka zinthu za site, chondeledwa **Add new Field**.
3. Kuti muli m'mene wa field type, chondeledwa **Template Selection**.
4. Onesha (Configure) field yomwe:
   - **Label** — Izi ndi m'mene omwe anthu amakuthandizira m'mene wopereka template (mwachitsanzo "Chonza template ya site").
   - **Required** — Kuti anthu kukhala ndi kuyesa template poyamba pa chiyembekeza.

#### Kodi imapereka bwanji?

Pamene munthu amapereka template m'mene wopereka, Ultimate Multisite imapereka kuti itimitsedwe pamene imaperekera site yanu yosiyanasiyana. Templates zomwe zimapereka zikuwoneka kuchokera kwa listi ya **Site Templates** yanu (**Ultimate Multisite > Site Templates**). Zomwe zimapereka zikuyenera kuti zikukhale zikuperekedwa kwa anthu.
