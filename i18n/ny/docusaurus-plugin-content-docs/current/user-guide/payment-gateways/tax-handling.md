# Kusamalira Misonkho

Ultimate Multisite ili ndi module yosonkhanitsira misonkho yomangidwa mu plugin yathu yayikulu, choncho ngati mukufuna kusonkhanitsa misonkho yogulitsa pa mapulani, mapaketi ndi mautumiki anu, mungathe kuchita zimenezi mosavuta popanda kukhazikitsa add-ons iliyonse.

Kwa makampani omwe ali ku Europe, timapereka **add-on** yomwe imawonjezera zida ndi mawonekedwe kuti zithandize bwino kutsatira **VAT**.

Ultimate Multisite siyipereka kapena kutumiza misonkho m'malo mwanu ku boma; timangokuthandizani kusonkhanitsa misonkho yoyenera pa nthawi ya transaction. **Mudzakhalabe mukuyenera kutumiza misonkho nokha.**

## Kuyatsa Kusonkhanitsa Misonkho

Kusonkhanitsa misonkho sikuyatsidwa mwachisawawa. Kuti muyatse, muyenera kupita ku **Ultimate Multisite > Zokonda > Misonkho** kenako kusintha kuti muyatse zokonda za Yatsa Misonkho.

![Kusintha kwa Yatsa Misonkho pamwamba pa tsamba la zokonda za Misonkho](/img/config/settings-taxes-enable.png)

Nayi mawonekedwe athunthu a tsamba la zokonda za misonkho:

![Tsamba lonse la zokonda za Misonkho](/img/config/settings-taxes-full.png)

Mungathenso kuona zokonda za misonkho za zinthu payekha:

![Zokonda za Misonkho za zinthu](/img/config/settings-taxes.png)

### Msonkho wosaphatikizidwa motsutsana ndi Msonkho wophatikizidwa

Mwachisawawa, mitengo yonse ya zinthu zanu siyiphatikiza msonkho, kutanthauza kuti misonkho **siyiphatikizidwa** mu mtengo wa chinthucho. Ngati tazindikira kuti kasitomala ayenera kulipira misonkho pa kugula kwina, tidzawonjezera misonkhoyo **pamwamba** pa subtotal.

Ngati mumakonda kuti misonkho iphatikizidwe mu mtengo wa chinthu chanu, mungachite zimenezi poyatsa zokonda za **Msonkho Wophatikizidwa**.

![Mzere wa kusintha kwa Msonkho Wophatikizidwa pansi pa zokonda za Yatsa Misonkho](/img/config/settings-taxes-inclusive.png)

Musaiwale **kusunga** zosintha zomwe mwapanga.

###

## Kupanga Mitengo ya Misonkho

Mukayatsa Kusonkhanitsa Misonkho, muyenera kupanga mitengo ya misonkho ya malo enieni pogwiritsa ntchito editor yathu ya mitengo ya misonkho.

Mungapeze editor podina batani la **Samalira Mitengo ya Misonkho** pa sidebar ya tsamba la zokonda za Misonkho.

![Ulalo wa Samalira Mitengo ya Misonkho mu panel ya Mitengo ya Misonkho pa tsamba la zokonda](/img/config/settings-taxes-manage-rates.png)

Pa tsamba la editor ya mitengo ya misonkho, mungawonjezere Mitengo ya Misonkho yatsopano podina batani la **Onjezani Mzere watsopano**.

![Tebulo la editor ya mitengo ya misonkho yokhala ndi batani la Onjezani Mzere watsopano pamwamba](/img/config/tax-rates-editor.png)

Muyenera kupatsa mlingo uliwonse wa msonkho **mutu** (wogwiritsidwa ntchito pa invoices). Kenako mungasankhe **dziko** (chofunikira), **state,** ndi **mzinda** (zonse ziwiri zosankha) komwe msonkho uwu udzalipiritsidwa. Pomaliza, onjezani **mlingo wa msonkho mu maperesenti**.

### Magulu a Misonkho

Mungathenso kupanga Magulu a Misonkho angapo, kuti muwonjezere mitengo yosiyanasiyana ya misonkho ya mitundu yosiyanasiyana ya zinthu.

Dinani kuti **Onjezani Gulu latsopano la Misonkho** , kenako lembani dzina la gulu lanu ndikusindikiza **Panga**.

![Batani la Onjezani Gulu latsopano la Misonkho pamwamba pa editor ya mitengo ya misonkho](/img/config/tax-categories-add.png)

![Field yolowetsera Dzina la Gulu la Misonkho mu modal yopangira gulu](/img/config/tax-categories-create-modal.png)

Kuti musakatule magulu, dinani **Sinthani** ndikusankha gulu lomwe mukufuna kuwonjezeramo misonkho yatsopano.

![Batani la dropdown la Sinthani kuti musinthe pakati pa magulu a misonkho](/img/config/tax-categories-switch.png)

![Dropdown yosankhira gulu la misonkho yomwe ikuwonetsa magulu omwe alipo](/img/config/tax-categories-select.png)

Mungakhazikitse gulu la msonkho la chinthu china popita ku **tsamba losinthira Chinthu** kenako ku tab ya Misonkho.

![Tab ya misonkho ya Chinthu yokhala ndi gulu la msonkho ndi kusintha kwa taxable](/img/config/product-taxes.png)

Pa screen yomweyo, mungazimitse kusintha kwa **Kodi ndi Yokhometsedwa Msonkho?** kuti mudziwitse Ultimate Multisite kuti isasonkhanitse misonkho pa chinthu chimenecho.

## Thandizo la European VAT

Monga tanena kale, tili ndi add-on yomwe ilipo kwa kasitomala ku EU omwe ali ndi zofunikira zina chifukwa cha malamulo a European VAT.

Zida zathu za VAT zimathandiza ndi zinthu zingapo zofunika:

  * Kutsegula mosavuta mitengo ya EU VAT;

  * Kusonkhanitsa ndi kutsimikizira VAT Number - komanso reverse charging kwa mabungwe osamasulidwa ku VAT (monga makampani okhala ndi VAT numbers zovomerezeka);

Kuti muyike add-on imeneyo, pitani ku **Ultimate Multisite > Zokonda** kenako dinani ulalo wa sidebar wa **Onani Add-ons athu**.

![Sidebar ya tsamba la Zokonda yokhala ndi ulalo wa Onani Add-ons athu](/img/config/settings-taxes-addons-link.png)

Mudzatumizidwa ku tsamba lathu la add-ons. Kumeneko, mungafufuze **Ultimate Multisite VAT add-on** ndikuyiyika.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: VAT add-on tile on the Add-ons page -->

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: VAT add-on Install Now dialog -->

Kenako, pitani ku **Network Admin > Plugins** ndikuyatsa add-on imeneyo pa network yonse.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Network Activate action for the VAT add-on on the Plugins page -->

Ngati mubwerera ku **tab ya Zokonda za Misonkho** , mudzaona zosankha zatsopano zilipo. Yatsani njira ya **Yatsa Thandizo la VAT** kuti muyatse zida zatsopano za VAT. Musaiwale **kusunga** zokonda zanu!

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Enable VAT Support toggle in the Tax settings after add-on activation -->

### Kukoka Mitengo ya Misonkho ya VAT

Chimodzi mwa zida zomwe integration yathu imawonjezera ndi kuthekera kotsegula mitengo ya misonkho ya mayiko a EU member states. Izi zingachitike poyendera tsamba la editor ya mitengo ya misonkho mutayatsa thandizo la EU VAT.

Pansi pa tsambalo, mudzaona zosankha zokokera VAT. Kusankha mtundu wa mlingo ndikudina batani la **Sinthani Mitengo ya EU VAT** kudzabweretsa ndi kudzaza tebulo lokha ndi mitengo ya misonkho ya dziko lililonse la EU member state. Kenako, mumangofunika kusunga.

![Batani la Sinthani Mitengo ya EU VAT pansi pa editor ya mitengo ya misonkho](/img/config/tax-rates-vat-pull.png)

Mungathenso kusintha ma value mutawakoka. Kuti muchite zimenezi, ingosinthani mzere wa tebulo womwe mukufuna ndikudina kuti musunge ma value atsopano.

### Kutsimikizira VAT

Thandizo la VAT likayatsidwa, Ultimate Multisite idzawonjezera field yowonjezera ku form ya checkout, pansi pa field ya billing address. Fieldyo idzangowoneka kwa makasitomala omwe ali ku EU.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: VAT Number field on the frontend checkout form below the billing address -->

Ultimate Multisite idzatsimikizira VAT Number ndipo ngati ibwerera ngati yovomerezeka, njira ya reverse charge imagwiritsidwa ntchito ndipo tax rate imayikidwa pa 0% pa order imeneyo.
