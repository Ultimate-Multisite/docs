## Widgets za Dashboard ya M'moyo Wanu (Network Dashboard)

Ultimate Multisite imapanga widgets zingali zofunika m'moyo wanu wa network. M'moyo wanu uliwonse wamukwera kwa kulemba **Ultimate Multisite** ndi chitsogo cha menu, ndi sub-menu zingali pamwamba.

## Widgets za Dashboard ya Network

Ultimate Multisite imapanga widgets zingali zomwe zimeneza m'moyo wanu wa network. M'moyo wanu umapera m'moyo wanu monga **My Sites → Network Admin → Dashboard**. Widgets hizo ziliwonse zikuyenera kulipatsa chithandizo cha mapulogalamu ndi zomwe zimeneza kwa admin wanu wanu.

![Network Dashboard with Ultimate Multisite widgets](/img/admin/network-dashboard-full.png)

### Zofunso Zoyamba (First Steps)

![First Steps widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-first-steps.png)

Widget ili lipera pamene m'moyo wanu umapera kwa Ultimate Multisite ndipo limapatsa chithandizo kwa admin wanu wanu kuti amakomera zinthu zina monga kukonza njira ya kulipa ndi kupanga account yomwe imaphatikizidwa.

### Malingaliro (Summary)

![Summary widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-summary.png)

Widget ya Summary imapereka ntchito za anthu omwe amaphatikizidwa ndi mapulogalamu awo kwa tsiku lili. MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) imapera ntchito za zinthu zomwe zimeneza m'moyo wanu wa anthu omwe ali ndi mapulogalamu omwe amapereka mapulogalamu.

### Mwayi Woyamba (Activity Stream)

![Activity Stream widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-activity-stream.png)

Widget ya Activity Stream imapera ntchito za zinthu zonse zomwe zimeneza Ultimate Multisite. Zinthu zomwe zimeneza ndi kuphatikizidwa, kukhalitsa, kupanga sites, kuchititsa chinthu, ndipo zinthu zina.

Choncho, muli m'moyo wanu wa documentation kuti muone kukhala ndi mawu omwe amapera ntchito kwambiri.

### Kuti Muli Bwino Ngati (Right Now)

![Right Now widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-right-now.png)

Izi widget ya ulipanga imeshiulitsa mu mfundo yofupi ya abwiri ndi sites m'moyo wanu. Mwayi wosonyeza m'moyo umene mwapanga sitete kapena abwiri. Koma m'moyo wosona m'moyo umene mwapanga sitete kapena abwiri.

### Ndauzo za Khabiri ndi Kufotokozera (News and Discussions)

![Ndauzo ya Khabiri ndi Kufotokozera](/img/admin/network-dashboard-news.png)

Izi widget imapereka ndimene imapereka ndimene imapereka khalidwe la kukhala kwa Ultimate Multisite. Kodi mwayi wosona umene mwapanga sitete kapena abwiri kuti muli ndi chondeza za kusintha, mabwiri omwe ali ndi mavuto omakhulupirira, ndipo zomwe zikuyendera.

## Ndauzo ya Ultimate Multisite Dashboard

M'moyo wosona umene mwapanga sitete kapena abwiri imapereka mwayi wosonyeza ntchito yomwe ndi mfundo yomwezi za m'moyo wanu, ndipo Ultimate Multisite dashboard (liyonse liyenera ku m'moyo wosona umene mwapanga sitete kapena abwiri) imapereka mwayi wosonyeza ntchito ya mwayi.

![Ultimate Multisite Dashboard](/img/admin/um-dashboard-top.png)

### Kukhala kwa Mwayi Woperekedwa M'maka (Monthly Recurring Revenue Growth)

Chart ya MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) imapereka kukhazikitsa kwa mwayi m'maka pa nthawi yomwe ndi miyezi 12.

![MRR Growth chart](/img/admin/um-dashboard-mrr.png)
