# Kufunga Plan (v2)

_**KUFUNDA MUWENYE UMA: Iyi article iyi ifi Ultimate Multisite version 2.x.**_

Mawonekedwe anu wosavuta planzi kwa nthawi yopambana. Anga kuti awasavute kuplanileni lina lina, kapena kuyachitira zinthu zozinzinga kapena zikati zomwe muli ndiwo pa mtandawu wanu.

M'mulumutsa wosavuta, muthandizira kuti awasavute planzi bwanji ndipo zimene zikuyera pano pamene kupambana kuli.

Kuti yachitire kufunga planzi lina, m'mulumutsa anu kudziwa ku access dashboard yawo ndipo kuyenda kwa ndau **Account** (Mabwera).

![Customer subsite dashboard with Account menu link visible](/img/account-page/account-menu.png)

Pa page ya Account, kudziwa kuti muli ndi membership yanu lili lili ndi planzi lomwe limakwanira. Kuti yachitire kufunga planzi lina, muli ndi kuyenda kwa **Change** (Kugawira) pa m'mulo wa kupita kwa **Your Membership** (Membership Yanu).

![Account page Your Membership card with Change button](/img/account-page/membership-change-button.png)

Muli kudziwa kuti mudzakhala kuwera pa checkout form pamene planzi onse zomwe zili kusonyezeka zimene.

Muli ndi kuyenda kwa **services and packages available for their current plan** (Zinzinga ndi zikati zomwe zili kupathulidwa kwa planzi yanu lili), monga momwe muli ndi kulankhula kupeza service kapena package imodzi (monga unlimited visits kapena disk space m'mulo wathu), komwe osavuta kufunga planzi.

![Upgrade picker showing available plans and packages on the customer side](/img/account-page/upgrade-picker.png)

Pambuyo pa kuyesa chinthu chomwe mukufuna kupambana, mudzakhala ndi kuona kuti muli ndi kukhala koti muli ndi kuwera kutali kodi - osati kulimbikira yomwe muli ndi kuwera kwa mbiri.

Monga momwe muli ndi planzi lina ndipo malipiro aliyense idzakhala m'mulo wa membership, mudzakhala ndi credit (kufunsa) kwa mlankhali woperekedwa pa planzi yomwe muli kupambana.

![Upgrade payment summary showing credit applied and next billing amount](/img/account-page/upgrade-summary.png)

Muli ang bilihana (plan or package) omalizani, omalizani kukhala komwe ndi chinthu chomwe chimakhala m'moyo wanu monga momwe muli m'moyo wanu wamba.

![Notice when the selected plan does not change the subscription](/img/account-page/upgrade-no-change.png)

Pambuyo pa kulimbikitsa (checkout) kukhala kukhala, zinthu zatsopano (new product(s)) zikukwera m'moyo wanu waomwe ndi zomwe zonse zosonyeza za zinthu zina zotsopano zikuyenera kuwonjezera m'moyo wanu: monga anthu omwe akulimbikitsa (visits), malo a disk (disk space), posts, ndi zina zambiri...

## Mwayi ndi Kukhala kwa Upgrades ndi Downgrades

Pa chinthu chomwe muli ndi, muli ndi tabu ya **Up & Downgrades**. Chinthu choyamba pa tabu imeneyi ndi chinthu chomwe chimakhala m'moyo wanu muli ndi chiyembekezo cha **Plan Group**.

**Plan groups** ndi zomwe zimuku kulimbikitsa Ultimate Multisite kuti muli ndi zinthu zina zomwe zikuyenera kukhala m'moyo wamba wosonyeza "family" yomwe, ndipo chifukwa imeneyi zimene zikuyenera kugwiritsidwa ntchito kupeza mashumakhale a Up/Downgrade.

![Product edit Up and Downgrades tab with Plan Group field](/img/config/product-upgrades-plan-group.png)

Mwachitsanzo, muli ndi **Free plan**, **Basic Plan** ndi **Premium Plan** zomwe zimakhala kukhala. Muli wofuna kuti anthu omwe akulimbikitsa m'moyo wa **Free Plan** angathe kukwera (upgrade) pansi pansi kwa **Premium Plan** ndipo muli wofuna kuti osonyeze "Basic Plan" komwe ndi chitsanzo cha upgrade. Zomwe mukufuna kulipira ndi kukhala ndi chinthu chomwe m'moyo wamba ndi kupeza zinthu zina, muli wofuna kuti muli ndi dzina la Plan Group lalikulu lomwe limakhala kwa Free plan ndi Premium plan monga momwe muli m'moyo wanu.

![Free Plan product page with High End plan group assigned](/img/config/product-upgrades-free.png)

![Premium Plan product page with High End plan group assigned](/img/config/product-upgrades-premium.png)

Kuti wamwamba ndi kuti muliwo ndi chinthu osankana wopambana na Ultimate Multisite kuti muli ndi "family" ya mapulogula m'mkwose wanu, yomwe ndi **High End**. Pamwoleka zopwera kapena kuwamba kwa mapulogula, pali mapulogula omwe ali m'mkwose woyenera (family) yomwe yomweyi muliwo monga chagera.
