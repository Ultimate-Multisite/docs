# Fyuluta: wu_sso_enabled {#filter-wussoenabled}

Yatsani/zimitsani kuthekera kwa kulowa kamodzi pa ma domain osiyanasiyana.

Fyulutani mtengo uwu kuti muzimitse single-sign-on kwathunthu, kapena kuti muyatse malinga ndi chikhalidwe m'malo mwake.

## Magawo {#parameters}

| Dzina | Mtundu | Kufotokozera |
|------|------|-------------|
| $enabled | `bool` | Kodi SSO iyenera kuyatsidwa? True kuti iyatse, false-ish kuti izime. |

### Kuyambira {#since}

- 2.0.11
### Gwero {#source}

Yafotokozedwa mu [`inc/sso/class-sso.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite/blob/main/inc/sso/class-sso.php#L110) pa mzere 110


## Zobwerera {#returns}
Ngati SSO yayatsidwa kapena ayi.
