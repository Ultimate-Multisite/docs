# Fyuluta: wu_independent_billing_cycle_product_types {#filter-wuindependentbillingcycleproducttypes}

Fyulutani mitundu ya zinthu yomwe ili ndi ma billing cycle odziyimira pawokha.

Zinthu zokhala ndi ma billing cycle odziyimira pawokha sizikakamizidwa kufanana ndi billing period ya pulani mu ngolo. Izi ndi zothandiza pa zinthu monga kulembetsa ma domain omwe nthawi zonse amalipiritsidwa pachaka mosatengera ngati pulaniyo ndi ya mwezi uliwonse kapena ya pachaka.

## Ma Parameter {#parameters}

| Dzina | Mtundu | Kufotokozera |
|------|------|-------------|
| $types | `array` | Array ya mitundu ya zinthu yokhala ndi ma billing cycle odziyimira pawokha. |

### Kuyambira {#since}

- 2.5.0
