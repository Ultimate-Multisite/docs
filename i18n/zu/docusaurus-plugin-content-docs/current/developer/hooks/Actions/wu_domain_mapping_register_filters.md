# Isenzo: wu_domain_mapping_register_filters

Amanye ama-plugin azogcina i-URL ngaphambi kokuthi ukumatanisa kusebenze noma azokwakha ama-URL ngendlela ehlukile engafakiwe kuzihlungi ezingenhla.

Ezimweni ezinjalo, sifuna ukwengeza izihlungi ezengeziwe. Ipharamitha yesibili edlulisiwe umsebenzi wokubuyisela we-mangle_url. Sincoma ukuthi ungasebenzisi lesi sihlungi ngqo. Kunalokho, sebenzisa indlela ethi Domain_Mapping::apply_mapping_to_url.

## Amapharamitha

| Igama | Uhlobo | Incazelo |
|------|------|-------------|
| $mangle_url | `callable` | Umsebenzi obizwayo we-mangle. |
| $domain_mapper | `self` | Le nto. |
