Sihlunga ukuthi i-auth cookie kufanele ithunyelwe kuphela nge-HTTPS yini.

## Amapharamitha

| Igama | Uhlobo | Incazelo |
|------|------|-------------|
| $secure | `bool` | Ukuthi i-cookie kufanele ithunyelwe kuphela nge-HTTPS yini. |
| $user_id | `int` | User ID. |
