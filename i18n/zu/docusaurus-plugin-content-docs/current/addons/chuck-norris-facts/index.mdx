# Amaqiniso ka-Chuck Norris

Engeza kancane ihlaya emisebenzini yakho yansuku zonke yokuphatha i-WordPress ngesengezo se-**Ultimate Multisite - Chuck Norris Facts**. Uma sesivuliwe, uzobona iqiniso lika-Chuck Norris elikhethwe ngokungahleliwe liboniswa ekhoneni eliphezulu kwesokudla lawo wonke amakhasi okuphatha.

Kuhle kakhulu ukuletha ukumamatheka ethimbeni lakho, ukwenza isimo sibe lula ngesikhathi sezikhathi ezinde zokuphatha, noma nje ukugubha ubukhulu obudumile obungu-Chuck Norris.

## Kungani Amaqiniso ka-Chuck Norris? {#why-chuck-norris-facts}

* **Khulisa Umoya Wethimba**: Engeza into ejabulisayo ekuphatheni inethiwekhi yakho
* **Qeda Ukungakhululeki Kokuqala**: Isiqalisi esihle sengxoxo samalungu amasha ethimba
* **Ukukhulisa Ukukhiqiza**: Ukuhleka okusheshayo kungathuthukisa ukugxila nokukhiqiza
* **Ihlaya Elingagugi**: Amaqiniso ka-Chuck Norris awagugi neze

## Izici Eziyinhloko {#key-features}

**Amaqiniso Adumile Angaphezu kuka-110**
Iqoqo elibanzi lamahlaya namacala akudala ka-Chuck Norris agubha inganekwane uqobo lwayo.

**Ukuboniswa Okungaphazamisi**
Amaqiniso avela kubha yokuphatha ngendlela engaphazamisi ukugeleza komsebenzi wakho.

**Kulungele Ukuhunyushwa**
Wonke amaqiniso afakwe emisebenzini yokuhumusha, okwenza kube lula ukuwavumelanisa nolimi lwakho noma ukudala amaqiniso akhethekile ethimba lakho.

**Iphendula Ngokugcwele**
Amaqiniso aboniswa kahle kuzikrini zekhompyutha, ithebhulethi, neselula - u-Chuck Norris muhle kuwo wonke amadivayisi.

**Ukusekelwa kwe-RTL**
Ukusekelwa okuphelele kwezilimi ezibhalwa ukusuka kwesokudla ukuya kwesobunxele ngoba u-Chuck Norris weqa imingcele yolimi.

**Iyahambisana ne-Block Editor**
Amaqiniso afihlwa ngobuhlakani ku-block editor ukuze kugwenywe ukungqubuzana kwesixhumi esibonakalayo.

**Igxile Ekufinyelelekeni**
Ifaka umbhalo ofanele wabafundi besikrini ukuze ihambisane ngokugcwele nezidingo zokufinyeleleka.

## Ilungele {#perfect-for}

* Abaphathi benethiwekhi ye-multisite abafuna ukwengeza ubuntu
* Amathimba okuthuthukisa awazisayo amahlaya amahle
* Noma ubani odinga umthamo wansuku zonke wobukhulu buka-Chuck Norris
* Amasayithi afuna ukudala indawo yomsebenzi ejabulisayo nenhle

## Ukufaka {#installation}

1. Qinisekisa ukuthi isandiso se-Ultimate Multisite sifakiwe futhi sivuliwe kunethiwekhi yakho ye-WordPress
2. Layisha amafayela esengezo kudayirekhthri yakho ye-`/wp-content/plugins/`
3. Vula isandiso kunethiwekhi ngemenyu ethi 'Plugins' ku-WordPress
4. Jabulela amaqiniso ka-Chuck Norris akhethwa ngokungahleliwe kuwo wonke amakhasi okuphatha!

Akukho kulungiselela okudingekayo - vele uvule bese uyamamatheka!

## Imibuzo Evame Ukubuzwa {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Ingabe lokhu kudinga i-Ultimate Multisite? {#does-this-require-ultimate-multisite}

Nakuba yakhelwe njengesengezo se-Ultimate Multisite, isandiso sisebenza ngokuzimela futhi singasebenza kunoma yikuphi ukufakwa kwe-WordPress.

## Ngingakwazi ukwengeza amaqiniso ami? {#can-i-add-my-own-facts}

Yebo! Amaqiniso akulungele ukuhunyushwa. Ungadala ifayela lakho le-`.po` elinamaqiniso akhethekile bese ulibeka kudayirekhthri ye-`/lang`. Noma ungahlunga uhlu lwamaqiniso ngokohlelo.

## Ingabe lokhu kwehlisa ijubane lokuphatha kwami? {#does-this-slow-down-my-admin}

Lutho neze! Isandiso silula kakhulu futhi asinawo umthelela ekusebenzeni. Sibonisa umbhalo kuphela - akukho kubizwa kwe-API yangaphandle noma ukucubungula okusindayo.

## Ngingakwazi ukusivala emakhasini athile? {#can-i-disable-it-on-certain-pages}

Amaqiniso afihlwa ngokuzenzakalelayo emakhasini lapho engase aphazamise khona (njenge-block editor). Ungasebenzisa futhi ama-hook e-WordPress ukwenza ngokwezifiso lapho amaqiniso avela khona.

## Ingabe amaqiniso afanele zonke izethameli? {#are-the-facts-appropriate-for-all-audiences}

Yebo! Wonke amaqiniso ahlanzekile, ayihlaya elilungele umndeni eligubha isimo sika-Chuck Norris esiyinganekwane.

## Ngingakwazi ukuhumushela amaqiniso kolunye ulimi? {#can-i-translate-the-facts-to-another-language}

Nakanjani! Wonke amaqiniso asebenzisa imisebenzi yokuhumusha ye-WordPress. Dala ifayela lokuhumusha lolimi lwakho bese ulibeka kudayirekhthri ye-`/lang`. Isizinda sombhalo: `ultimate-multisite-chuck-norris-facts`

## Ingabe isebenza ne-multisite? {#does-it-work-with-multisite}

Yebo! Yakhelwe ngokukhethekile amanethiwekhi e-multisite futhi ingavulwa ezingeni lenethiwekhi.
