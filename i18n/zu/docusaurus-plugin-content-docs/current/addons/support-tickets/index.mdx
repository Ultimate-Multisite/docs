# Amathikithi Osekelo

**Amathikithi Osekelo** yisengezo esinamandla se-Ultimate Multisite esengeza uhlelo olubanzi lwamathikithi osekelo kunethiwekhi yakho ye-WordPress. Ilungele amapulatifomu e-SaaS, abahlinzeki be-WaaS (Website as a Service), namanethiwekhi e-multisite adinga ukunikeza ukwesekwa kwamakhasimende ezingeni lenethiwekhi nasezingeni lesayithi ngalinye.

## Kungani Ukhethe Amathikithi Osekelo?

* **Ukwesekwa Kwamazinga Amaningi**: Abaphathi benethiwekhi baphatha amathikithi athinta yonke inethiwekhi kuyilapho abanikazi bamasayithi bephethe ukwesekwa kwamakhasimende abo
* **Ukuhamba Komsebenzi Kobungcweti**: Ukuphathwa okuphelele komjikelezo wokuphila wethikithi ngezinto eziphuthumayo, izimo, nokwabiwa
* **Okuhlangenwe Nakho Kwekhasimende**: Isixhumi esihle, esizivumelanisa namadivayisi esenza kube lula kumakhasimende ukuthola usizo
* **Kuphephile futhi Kuthembekile**: Kwakhiwe ngezindlela ezihamba phambili zokuphepha ze-WordPress futhi kwathuthukiselwa ukusebenza kahle kwedathabheyisi
* **Ukuhlanganiswa Okungenazihibe**: Kusebenza kahle kakhulu nokuphathwa kwamakhasimende namasayithi kwe-Ultimate Multisite

## Izici Eziyinhloko

**Ukuphathwa Okuphelele Kwamathikithi**

* Uhlelo oluyingqayizivele lokunombola amathikithi ukuze kube lula ukuwabhekisa
* Amazinga okuphuthuma alungisekayo (Phansi, Okuvamile, Phezulu, Okubucayi)
* Ukuhamba komsebenzi okuphelele kwezimo (Kuvuliwe, Kuyaqhubeka, Kulindile, Kuxazululiwe, Kuvaliwe)
* Ukuhlukaniswa kwamathikithi ngezigaba (Okuvamile, Ezobuchwepheshe, Ukukhokhisa, Isicelo Sesici, Umbiko Wephutha)
* Yabela amathikithi kuma-ejenti osekelo ngokwabiwa okuzenzakalelayo okungakhethwa

**Izingxoxo Ezinemicu**

* Ukuhlanganiswa okuphelele kwemicu yezingxoxo zezimpendulo zamathikithi
* Isitayela esihlukile sezimpendulo zabasebenzi nezamakhasimende
* Amanothi abasebenzi ayimfihlo amakhasimende angakwazi ukuwabona
* Isixhumi sesikhathi sangempela esiqhutshwa yi-AJAX
* Umhleli wombhalo wesimanje onezinketho zokufometha

**Okunamathiselwe Kwamafayela**

* Ukulayishwa kwefayela okuphephile okunokwesekwa kokuhudula nokudedela
* Imikhawulo elungisekayo yohlobo nosayizi wefayela
* Ukuphepha okuthuthukile ngokutholwa kwamafayela ayingozi
* Ukulandwa okuvikelwe ngezindlela zokulawula ukufinyelela

**Izaziso Ezihlakaniphile**

* Izexwayiso ze-imeyili ezizenzakalelayo zamathikithi amasha, izimpendulo, nezinguquko zesimo
* Izifanekiso ze-imeyili ezenziwa ngezifiso
* Izaziso ezisekelwe endimeni zabasebenzi namakhasimende
* Okuguquguqukayo kwesifanekiso okushintshashintshayo kwemiyalezo eyenziwe ibe ngeyakho

**Isixhumi Somphathi Esinamandla**

* Uhlu lwamathikithi oluthuthukile olunokuhlela nokuhlunga
* Ukuhlela okusheshayo kwesimo, okuphuthumayo, nokwabiwa
* Izenzo ngobuningi zokuphatha ngempumelelo
* Dashboard yezibalo enezilinganiso zokusebenza
* Iphaneli yezilungiselelo ephelele

**Iphothali Enhle Yamakhasimende**

* Isixhumi esihlanzekile, esizivumelanisa nawo wonke amadivayisi
* Idizayini eqala ngomakhalekhukhwini
* Ukusesha nokuhlunga okuthuthukile
* Umhleli wesimanje onebha yamathuluzi okufometha
* Amafomu okulula ukuwasebenzisa okuthumela amathikithi

**Ukuhlanganiswa Okuguquguqukayo**

* Sebenzisa ama-shortcode noma izakhi ze-UI ze-Ultimate Multisite
* Bonisa amafomu nohlu lwamathikithi noma kuphi kusayithi lakho
* Kusebenza ngaphandle kwezihibe nesakhiwo senethiwekhi ye-multisite
* Kunwebeka ngama-hook nama-filter

## Kulungele

* Amapulatifomu e-SaaS adinga ukwesekwa kwamakhasimende
* Abahlinzeki be-WaaS abaphatha amasayithi amaningi amaklayenti
* Amasayithi obulungu anokwesekwa ngamazinga
* Abaphathi benethiwekhi abaphatha amabhrendi amaningi
* Abanikazi bamasayithi abahlinzeka ngesevisi yamakhasimende

## Ukufakwa

1. Qinisekisa ukuthi i-plugin ye-Ultimate Multisite ifakiwe futhi yenziwe yasebenza kunethiwekhi yakho ye-WordPress
2. Layisha amafayela esengezo kufolda yakho ethi `/wp-content/plugins/`
3. Yenza i-plugin isebenze enethiwekhi ngemenyu ethi 'Plugins' ku-WordPress
4. Iya ku-Network Admin > Amathikithi Osekelo ukuze ulungise izilungiselelo zakho

## Imibuzo Evame Ukubuzwa

## Ingabe lokhu kudinga i-Ultimate Multisite?

Yebo, lesi sengezo sidinga ukuthi i-Ultimate Multisite ifakiwe futhi isebenza. Sihlanganiswa ngokujulile nezici zokuphathwa kwamakhasimende namasayithi ze-Ultimate Multisite.

## Ingabe abanikazi bamasayithi bangakwazi ukuphatha ukwesekwa kwamakhasimende abo?

Nakanjani! Abanikazi bamasayithi bangasebenzisa uhlelo lwamathikithi ukuze banikeze ukwesekwa kumakhasimende abo, kuyilapho abaphathi benethiwekhi bengaphatha amathikithi osekelo athinta yonke inethiwekhi.

## Amakhasimende awathumela kanjani amathikithi?

Ungangeza amafomu okuthumela amathikithi noma kuphi kusayithi lakho usebenzisa ama-shortcode noma izakhi ze-UI ze-Ultimate Multisite. Amakhasimende agcwalisa ifomu bese ethumela isicelo sawo.

## Ngingakwazi ukwenza ngokwezifiso izimo nokuphuthuma kwamathikithi?

Yebo! Ungenza ngokwezifiso ngokuphelele amazinga okuphuthuma, izimo, nezinhlobo zamathikithi kuzilungiselelo zomphathi wenethiwekhi ukuze kuhambisane nokuhamba komsebenzi wakho wosekelo.

## Ingabe isekela okunamathiselwe kwamafayela?

Yebo, amakhasimende nabasebenzi bangakwazi ukunamathisela amafayela kumathikithi nasezimpendulweni. Unokulawula okuphelele izinhlobo zamafayela avunyelwe nemikhawulo yosayizi ukuze kube nokuphepha.

## Izaziso ziphathwa kanjani?

Uhlelo luthumela ngokuzenzakalelayo izaziso ze-imeyili zamathikithi amasha, izimpendulo, nezinguquko zesimo. Ungenza ngokwezifiso zonke izifanekiso ze-imeyili kuzilungiselelo.

## Ngingakwazi ukwabela amathikithi kuma-ejenti osekelo athile?

Yebo, ungabela amathikithi kuma-ejenti mathupha noma uvumele ukwabiwa okuzenzakalelayo ukuze amathikithi asatshalaliswe ngokuzenzakalelayo.

## Ingabe kukhona amanothi ayimfihlo abasebenzi?

Yebo, amalungu abasebenzi angangeza izimpendulo eziyimfihlo ezibonakala kuphela kwamanye amalungu abasebenzi, hhayi kumakhasimende.

## Ingabe iyasebenziseka kumakhalekhukhwini?

Nakanjani! Sonke isixhumi sakhiwe ngendlela eqala ngomakhalekhukhwini futhi sisebenza kahle kuwo wonke amadivayisi.

## Ngingakwazi ukubonisa uhlu namafomu amathikithi ngisebenzisa ama-shortcode?

Yebo! Ama-shortcode atholakalayo:
* `[wu_support_tickets]` - Bonisa uhlu lwamathikithi ekhasimende
* `[wu_support_ticket_form]` - Bonisa ifomu elisha lokuthumela ithikithi
* `[wu_support_ticket_view ticket_id="123"]` - Bonisa ithikithi elithile

## Yiziphi izinhlobo zamafayela ezingalayishwa?

Ungalungisa izinhlobo zamafayela avunyelwe kuzilungiselelo. Uhlelo luhlanganisa ukuskena kokuphepha okuthuthukile ukuze kuvikelwe kumafayela ayingozi.

## Ngingakwazi ukukhipha idatha yamathikithi?

Yebo, lesi sengezo sihlanganisa amakhono okukhipha idatha yamathikithi nezibalo.
