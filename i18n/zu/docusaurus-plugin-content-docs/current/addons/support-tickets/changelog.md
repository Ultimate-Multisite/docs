# Ilogu yezinguquko yamathikithi osekelo

### 1.0.4 - 2026-05-05 {#104---2026-05-05}
* Kuthuthukisiwe: Kususwe uhla lwemibhalo vendor/ ekulandelelweni kwe-Git (seluvele lumbozwe yi-.gitignore), kunciphisa usayizi we-repository
* Kuthuthukisiwe: Kuhlolwe kuze kufike ku-WordPress 7.0

### 1.0.3 - 2026-05-01 {#103---2026-05-01}
* Kulungisiwe: Yamukela amanani angu-null kuma-setter emodeli yethikithi avumela i-null
* Kulungisiwe: Gcina wonke amathikithi kumathebula omhlaba wonke enethiwekhi yonke ukuze kube nokusekelwa okufanele kwe-multisite
* Kulungisiwe: Fihla izinkambu zabasebenzi futhi ulungise ukuqondiswa kabusha kwekhasi elingenalutho kwifomu lekhasimende lethikithi elisha
* Kulungisiwe: Shintsha izingcingo ze-add_meta() ezingachazwanga ufake i-update_meta() efanele yokugcinwa kwemethadatha
* Kulungisiwe: Shintsha ukuhlolwa kwekhono elingabhalisiwe ufake i-wu_view_all_support_tickets efanele
* Kulungisiwe: Engeza izibambi ze-AJAX ezishodayo zesimo sethikithi, ukwabiwa, nezenzo zokuhlela ngokushesha
* Kulungisiwe: Lungisa igama lendlela lokuthola impendulo yabasebenzi ekubukweni kwamathikithi
* Kulungisiwe: Hlanganisa izibambi zezimpendulo eziphindaphindiwe futhi uqondanise amagama ezenzo ze-nonce
* Kulungisiwe: Engeza ukubuka kwe-frontend okushodayo kwe-shortcode ethi [wu_submit_ticket]
* Kulungisiwe: Engeza ikholomu ye-user_id eshodayo futhi ulungise indlela ye-Support_Ticket::get_user_id()
* Kulungisiwe: Susa uphawu lokucaphuna olukabili olwengeziwe kuthegi yenketho yesihlungi sokubaluleka
* Kulungisiwe: Engeza iphaneli yokuphathwa kwamathikithi yomlawuli wenethiwekhi yama-super admin
* Kuthuthukisiwe: Hlanganisa i-CSS yomlawuli ibe yishidi lesitayela langaphandle elilodwa
* Kuthuthukisiwe: Susa imenyu engaphansi ye-Settings kumenyu yomlawuli ye-subsite
* Kuthuthukisiwe: Layisha izinsiza ze-frontend ngokwemibandela kuphela emakhasini amathikithi osekelo
* Kuthuthukisiwe: Yeqa i-autoloader ye-plugin lapho i-autoloader yempande ye-Bedrock isivele ilayishe okuncikile

### 1.0.2 - 2025-12-11 {#102---2025-12-11}
* Kungeziwe: Ukusebenza okuphelele kokubuka amathikithi kumlawuli naku-frontend
* Kungeziwe: Isibambi se-AJAX sokuthunyelwa kwezimpendulo zamathikithi
* Kungeziwe: Ukusekelwa kokusebenza kwezimpendulo zamathikithi ngokuphathwa kwefomu okufanele
* Kungeziwe: Ukuboniswa kwezaziso okufanele kokuthunyelwa kwamathikithi nezimpendulo kumlawuli
* Kungeziwe: Ukuhlotshaniswa okuzenzakalelayo kwethikithi nomsebenzisi wamanje ekuzithumeleni kwamakhasimende
* Kungeziwe: Ukuthuthukiswa kwezokuphepha ukuvimbela amakhasimende ekubhaleni phezu kobunikazi bethikithi
* Kungeziwe: Imisebenzi yosizo eshodayo (wu_format_date, wu_user_can_view_ticket, njll.)
* Kungeziwe: Ukulanda nokuphathwa okufanele kokunamathiselwe kwamafayela
* Kungeziwe: Uhlelo lwezaziso ze-imeyili lwezimpendulo zamathikithi nezinguquko zesimo
* Kulungisiwe: Ifomu lempendulo yethikithi manje lifaka i-ID yethikithi edingekayo
* Kulungisiwe: Izinkinga zokubonakala kwezaziso ekubukweni kwamathikithi komlawuli
* Kulungisiwe: Amaphutha esintaksi emisebenzini yamathikithi osekelo
* Kuthuthukisiwe: Isakhiwo sedathabheyisi esinamakilasi afanele ombuzo ne-schema ezimpendulweni nasekunamathiselweni
* Kuthuthukisiwe: Ukuthuthela ezakhiweni zangempela esikhundleni sama-attributes kumamodeli amathikithi
