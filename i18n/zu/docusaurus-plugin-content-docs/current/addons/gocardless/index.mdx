# GoCardless {#gocardless}

Multisite Ultimate: Inkokhelo ye-GoCardless

Vumela amakhasimende akho akhokhele ubulungu nemikhiqizo ye-Multisite Ultimate ngokudonswa okuqondile kwemali usebenzisa i-GoCardless!

## Ukufaka {#installation}

1. Layisha 'ultimate-multisite-gocardless' enkombeni ethi '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Yenza i-plugin isebenze ngemenyu ethi 'Plugins' ku-WordPress
3. Landela i-Wizard yesinyathelo ngesinyathelo ukuze usethe i-plugin

## Umhlahlandlela Wokusebenzisa {#usage-guide}

_I-GoCardless_ iyisango lokukhokha elitholakala **eYurophu nase-UK** elivumela izinkokhelo **ngokudonswa okuqondile kwemali**.

I-**Ultimate Multisite: Isango le-GoCardless** yengeza ukusekelwa kwe-_GoCardless_ ku-Ultimate Multisite, ikuvumela ukuthi ukhokhise amakhasimende akho akuleso sifunda usebenzisa ukudonswa okuqondile kwemali.

Lesi sifundo sizohlanganisa indlela yokusetha i-_GoCardless_ ukuze uqale ukwamukela izinkokhelo ngayo kunethiwekhi yakho ye-Ultimate Multisite.

_**Qaphela: I-GoCardless isebenza kuphela ngezimali ezilandelayo:**_ **EUR, GBP, AUD, SEK, no-DKK.**

## Ukufaka Isengezo {#installing-the-add-on}

Okokuqala, uzodinga ukufaka isengezo se-GoCardless se-Ultimate Multisite. Ungakwenza lokho ngokuya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Izilungiselelo > Izengezo (kubha eseceleni)**. Bese ukhetha isengezo se-GoCardless kulolo hlu.

Ngemva kokuba isengezo sesifakiwe futhi senziwe sasebenza, iya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Izilungiselelo > Izinkokhelo**, vula inketho ye-GoCardless, bese ulondoloza.

![Izilungiselelo zesango lokukhokha](/img/config/settings-payment-gateways.png)

## Ukuthola Ithokheni Yokufinyelela {#getting-an-access-token}

Dala i-akhawunti ku-_GoCardless_ (noma ku-_GoCardless_ sandbox, uma ufuna **ukuyihlola kuqala** , okunconywayo). Bese, ngemva kokungena ngemvume, uye entweni yemenyu ethi **Onjiniyela**.

<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: i-Dashboard ye-GoCardless ibonisa indawo yemenyu ethi Onjiniyela -->

Ekhasini elithi **Onjiniyela**, chofoza inkinobho ethi **Dala** ekhoneni eliphezulu kwesokudla lesikrini, bese uchofoza inketho ethi **Ithokheni Yokufinyelela**.

<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: ikhasi le-GoCardless Onjiniyela elinenkinobho ethi Dala nenketho ethi Ithokheni Yokufinyelela -->

Lokhu kuzovula iwindi elisha le-modal, likuvumele ukuthi udale ithokheni entsha. Yinike igama elibonakalayo futhi uqinisekise ukuthi ukhetha inketho ethi **Ukufinyelela kokufunda nokubhala** kumenyu ethi **Ububanzi**.

<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: i-modal ye-GoCardless yokudala ithokheni entsha yokufinyelela enobubanzi bokufunda nokubhala -->

Ithokheni yokufinyelela izovela esikrinini. Ikopishe bese uyinamathisela kumenyu yakho ethi **Ultimate Multisite > Izilungiselelo > Izinkokhelo > GoCardless**.

Qiniseka ukuthi uyibeka enkambini efanele. Uma kuyi-**thokheni ebukhoma** **n** , cima **imodi ye-sandbox** ngaphambi kokunamathisela ithokheni.

![Izilungiselelo zesango lokukhokha nge-GoCardless](/img/config/settings-payment-gateways.png)

<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: izinkambu zethokheni ye-GoCardless kuzilungiselelo zokukhokha ze-Ultimate Multisite -->

Yilokho kwesinyathelo sokuqala. Okulandelayo, udinga ukusetha umlaleli we-webhook.

## Ukusetha Umlaleli we-Webhook {#setting-up-the-webhook-listener}

Lapho uphatha okubhaliselwe, i-Ultimate Multisite idinga ukuzwa ngezinguquko esimweni sokubhaliselwe, izinkokhelo ezintsha ezenziwayo, nezinye izinto ezinjalo.

Indlela amasango okukhokha azisa ngayo i-Ultimate Multisite ngalezo zinguquko ukusebenzisa **izingcingo ze-webhook**. Ngaleso sizathu, ukuze ukuhlanganiswa kusebenze ngo-100%, sidinga ukutshela i-_GoCardless_ ukuthi iyiphi i-URL okufanele iyibize nayo yonke leyo mininingwane.

Buyela kuphaneli yakho ethi **GoCardless > Onjiniyela** bese uchofoza inkinobho ethi Dala futhi, kodwa kulokhu, khetha inketho ethi **iphoyinti lokugcina le-webhook**.
