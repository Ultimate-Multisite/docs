# Domain Seller

**Multisite Ultimate: Domain Seller** iguqula inethiwekhi yakho ye-WordPress Multisite ibe inkundla enamandla yokuthengisa izizinda. Vumela amakhasimende abhalise izizinda ezenziwe ngokwezifiso ngqo ngesikhathi sokubhalisa noma ku-dashboard yawo, ngokuhlolwa kokutholakala ngesikhathi sangempela namanani asobala.

### Kungani i-Domain Seller?

Ukubhaliswa kwezizinda kungenye yezinto ezengeziwe ezicelwa kakhulu kunoma iyiphi inkundla ye-WaaS (Website as a Service). Esikhundleni sokuthumela amakhasimende akho kumbhalisi wenkampani yangaphandle, ungakwazi ukunikeza izizinda ngqo — ugcine imali engenayo eningi kunethiwekhi yakho kuyilapho unikeza amakhasimende isipiliyoni esingenazihibe.

### Izici Eziyinhloko

#### Ukusekelwa Kwababhalisi Bezizinda Abaningi
* **OpenSRS** — Ukubhalisa okuphelele, ukuvuselela, nokuphatha
* **Namecheap** — Ukuhlanganiswa okuphelele ne-WhoisGuard nokuhlola kwe-sandbox
* **HostAfrica** — Ukusesha isizinda, amanani, ukubhalisa, ukudlulisa, DNS, nokuvikelwa kwe-ID nge-Domains Reseller API
* **Openprovider** — Amanani omthengisi ophinde athengise, ukubhalisa, ukuvuselela, ukudlulisa, DNS, ubumfihlo be-WHOIS, nokuvumelanisa kwe-TLD
* **Hostinger** — Ukusesha nokubhalisa isizinda kusetshenziswa i-token yokuhlanganisa eyabiwe ye-Hostinger
* **Isakhiwo Esinwebekayo** — Sakhiwe phezu kwephethini ye-Integration Registry, okwenza kube lula ukwengeza abahlinzeki abaningi
* **Ukuhlola Ukuxhumana Okwakhelwe Ngaphakathi** — Qinisekisa imininingwane yabahlinzeki nokuxhumeka ngaphambi kokwenza kube bukhoma

#### Iwizadi Yokusetha Eqondisiwe
* **Ukuqalisa ngokuchofoza okukodwa** — Ikudlulisa ekucushweni komhlinzeki, ukungeniswa kwe-TLD, nokudalwa komkhiqizo wesizinda
* **Ukuvumelanisa okuzenzakalelayo kwe-TLD** — Kungenisa wonke ama-TLD atholakalayo namanani esamba kusuka kubahlinzeki bakho abaxhunyiwe
* **Ukudalwa komkhiqizo okuzenzakalelayo** — Kudala umkhiqizo wesizinda osulungele ukusetshenziswa onezilungiselelo ezenzakalelayo zenzuzo ezifanele

#### Amanani Ezizinda Aguquguqukayo
* **Izindlela ezintathu zenzuzo** — Iphesenti lenzuzo, inzuzo engaguquki, noma intengo engaguquki nge-TLD ngayinye
* **Imikhiqizo eqondene ne-TLD** — Dala imikhiqizo yezizinda ehlukene yamaqembu ahlukene e-TLD (.com/.net, .io, amakhodi amazwe, njll.)
* **Amanani okwethula** — Nikeza intengo yonyaka wokuqala encishisiwe ukuze uhehe ukubhaliswa okusha
* **Amanani esikhathi sangempela** — Amanani esamba abukhoma avela kubahlinzeki ngokulondoloza kwesikhashana okuhlakaniphile
* **Ukusekelwa kwamakhuphoni** — Ukuhambisana okuphelele namakhodi esaphulelo e-Multisite Ultimate

#### Ukuvikelwa Kobumfihlo be-WHOIS
* **Ukucushwa ngomkhiqizo ngamunye** — Setha ubumfihlo be-WHOIS ku-Disabled, Always Included, noma Customer Choice
* **Ukuzikhethela kwekhasimende** — Uma kusethwe ku-Customer Choice, ibhokisi lokuhlola le-checkout livumela amakhasimende ukwengeza ukuvikelwa kobumfihlo
* **Amanani acushwayo** — Setha intengo yonyaka yesengezo sobumfihlo, noma ukunikeze mahhala
* **Ukuhlanganiswa nombhalisi** — Kunika amandla ngokuzenzakalelayo i-WhoisGuard (Namecheap) noma ubumfihlo be-WHOIS (OpenSRS) ngesikhathi sokubhalisa

#### Isipiliyoni Se-Checkout Esihlanganisiwe
* **Izindlela ezintathu zesizinda** — Amakhasimende akhetha phakathi kwesizinda esingaphansi (samahhala), ukubhalisa isizinda esisha, noma ukuxhumanisa isizinda esikhona asebevele benaso
* **Ukusesha isizinda ngesikhathi sangempela** — Ukuhlola ukutholakala okusekelwa yi-AJAX ngaphandle kokulayisha kabusha ikhasi
* **Iziphakamiso ezihlakaniphile** — Iziphakamiso ze-TLD ezihlukile lapho isizinda esithandwayo sesithathiwe
* **Amanani asobala** — Ukuboniswa okucacile kwentengo yokubhalisa, intengo yokuvuselela, nemali yokuzikhethela yobumfihlo be-WHOIS
* **Izinkambu zokuxhumana zombhalisi** — Kuqoqa imininingwane edingekayo yombhalisi (igama, ikheli, ucingo) ngaphakathi ngesikhathi se-checkout
* **I-URL yesayithi ekhiqizwa ngokuzenzakalelayo** — Ikheli lesayithi lithathwa ngokuzenzakalelayo esizindeni esikhethiwe

#### Uhlelo Lokuvuselela Ngokuzenzakalelayo
* **Ukuvuselela okuxhunywe ebulungwini** — Izizinda zivuselelwa ngokuzenzakalelayo kuyilapho ubulungu bekhasimende busebenza
* **Ukuhlela okuhlakaniphile** — Imisebenzi yokuvuselela ifakwa kulayini ngokusekelwe ezinsukwini zokuphelelwa yisikhathi kwezizinda
* **Ilogikhi yokuzama futhi** — Ukuzama futhi okuhlakaniphile kwemizamo yokuvuselela ehlulekile
* **Izaziso ze-imeyili** — Izexwayiso ezilungisekayo zokuvuselela okuzayo, ukuvuselela okuphumelele, ukwehluleka, nokuphelelwa yisikhathi

#### Ukuphathwa kwe-DNS Kwamakhasimende
* **I-DNS yokuzisiza** — Amakhasimende angangeza, ahlele, futhi asuse amarekhodi e-DNS (A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, TXT) ku-dashboard yawo
* **Isimo sesizinda** — Isimo sokubhalisa ngesikhathi sangempela, izinsuku zokuphelelwa yisikhathi, nolwazi lwama-nameserver
* **Ukuxhumanisa izizinda okuzenzakalelayo** — Izizinda ezibhalisiwe noma ezikhona zixhunywa ngokuzenzakalelayo kusayithi lekhasimende

#### Amathuluzi Omphathi
* **Ukubhalisa isizinda ngesandla** — Bhalisa izizinda egameni lamakhasimende kusuka kuphaneli yomphathi
* **Ukuqapha izizinda** — Buka zonke izizinda zamakhasimende, izimo, nezinsuku zokuphelelwa yisikhathi ekhasini elilodwa
* **Ukuloga okuqondene nomhlinzeki** — Iziteshi zelogi ezihlukene zombhalisi ngamunye (isb., domain-seller-namecheap.log)
* **Imininingwane yokubhalisa** — Buka imininingwane yombhalisi nedatha yokubhalisa ekhasini eliyinhloko lokuhlela isizinda

### Abahlinzeki Abasekelwayo

* **OpenSRS** — Ukuhlanganiswa okuphelele nokubhalisa, ukuvuselela, ukuphathwa kwe-DNS, nobumfihlo be-WHOIS
* **Namecheap** — Ukuhlanganiswa okuphelele nokubhalisa, ukuvuselela, ukuhlolwa kokutholakala ngobuningi, ne-WhoisGuard yamahhala
* **HostAfrica** — Ukubhalisa, ukuvuselela, ukudlulisa, ukuphathwa kwe-DNS, ukutholwa kwekhodi ye-EPP, ukukhiya kombhalisi, nokuvikelwa kwe-ID
* **Openprovider** — Ukubhalisa, ukuvuselela, ukudlulisa, ukuphathwa kwe-DNS, ukutholwa kwekhodi ye-EPP, ukukhiya kombhalisi, ubumfihlo be-WHOIS, nokuvumelanisa kwe-TLD yomthengisi ophinde athengise
* **Hostinger** — Ukubhalisa, ukubheka isizinda, izibuyekezo zama-nameserver, ukukhiya kombhalisi, nobumfihlo be-WHOIS; ukuvuselela kuphathwa ukuvuselela okuzenzakalelayo kwe-Hostinger
* **GoDaddy** — Ukubhalisa nokuvuselela
* **ResellerClub** — Ukubhalisa, ukuvuselela, nokuphathwa kwe-DNS
* **NameSilo** — Ukubhalisa nokuvuselela
* **Enom** — Ukubhalisa, ukuvuselela, nokuphathwa kwe-DNS

### Imininingwane Yobuchwepheshe

* **Kudinga:** PHP 7.4+, Multisite Ultimate 2.4.12+
* **Isakhiwo:** Iphethini ye-Integration Registry ene-interface ye-Domain_Selling_Capability
* **Ukulayisha ngokuzenzakalelayo:** I-Jetpack autoloader ene-PSR-4 class mapping
* **Amazinga:** Amazinga okubhala ikhodi e-WordPress, ukuthayipha okuqinile, izitatimende ezilungisiwe

## Ukufaka

1. Qinisekisa ukuthi **Multisite Ultimate v2.4.12+** ifakiwe futhi yenziwe yasebenza kunethiwekhi
2. Layisha i-plugin ku-`/wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite-domain-seller/`
3. Yenza i-plugin isebenze kunethiwekhi kusuka ku-**Plugins** ku-WordPress Network Admin
4. I-**Setup Wizard** iqala ngokuzenzakalelayo ekusebenzeni kokuqala — landela izinyathelo ukuze:
   * Uxhume umbhalisi wakho wesizinda
   * Uhlole ukuxhumana
   * Ungenise ama-TLD atholakalayo namanani esamba
   * Udale umkhiqizo wakho wokuqala wesizinda ngenzuzo yamanani
5. Engeza inkambu ye-**Ukukhetha Isizinda** efomini lakho le-checkout ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Checkout Forms**

## Imibuzo Evame Ukubuzwa

### Iyiphi inguqulo ye-Multisite Ultimate edingekayo?

Inguqulo 2.4.12 noma ephakeme. Isengezo asihambisani ne-WP Ultimo v1.x noma izinguqulo ze-Ultimate Multisite ezingaphansi kuka-2.4.12.

### Yibaphi abahlinzeki bezizinda abasekelwayo?

Kusekelwa ababhalisi abayisishiyagalolunye: **OpenSRS**, **Namecheap**, **HostAfrica**, **Openprovider**, **Hostinger**, **GoDaddy**, **ResellerClub**, **NameSilo**, kanye **Enom**. Amandla ayahlukahluka ngomhlinzeki ngoba ama-API ababhalisi aveza imisebenzi ehlukene; bheka umhlahlandlela wokusetha ukuze uthole ukubuka konke komhlinzeki ngamunye. Isakhiwo se-Integration Registry sesengezo senza kube lula ukwengeza abahlinzeki abenziwe ngokwezifiso — bheka [umhlahlandlela wonjiniyela](/developer/integration-guide/custom-registrar).

### Ngingakwazi ukunikeza ukuvikelwa kobumfihlo be-WHOIS?

Yebo. Umkhiqizo ngamunye wesizinda unesilungiselelo se-WHOIS Privacy esinezindlela ezintathu: Disabled, Always Included, noma Customer Choice. Uma kusethwe ku-Customer Choice, amakhasimende abona ibhokisi lokuhlola ngesikhathi se-checkout elinentengo (noma "Mahhala" ye-WhoisGuard ye-Namecheap). Ifulegi lobumfihlo lidluliselwa ngokuzenzakalelayo kumbhalisi ngesikhathi sokubhalisa.

### Ngingakwazi ukusetha amanani ahlukene kuma-TLD ahlukene?

Yebo. Dala imikhiqizo yezizinda ehlukene yamaqembu ahlukene e-TLD. Umkhiqizo ngamunye ungaba nohlobo lwawo lwenzuzo (iphesenti, inzuzo engaguquki, noma intengo engaguquki), ama-TLD asekelwayo, namanani okwethula.

### Yiziphi izinketho ze-checkout amakhasimende azibonayo?

Inkambu ye-Domain Selection inika amakhasimende amathebhu amathathu: **Isizinda esingaphansi** (sebenzisa isizinda esingaphansi samahhala kunethiwekhi yakho), **Bhalisa Isizinda Esisha** (sesha futhi ubhalise isizinda esisha), kanye **Isizinda Esikhona** (xhumanisa isizinda asebevele benaso). Kuzovela kuphela izindlela ozivulile.

### Ingabe isekela ukuvuselela okuzenzakalelayo?

Yebo. Izizinda zingasethwa ukuthi zivuseleleke ngokuzenzakalelayo futhi zixhunywe esimweni sobulungu bekhasimende. Izinkokhelo zokuvuselela zicutshungulwa ngesango lokukhokha elikhona lekhasimende.

### Amakhasimende angakwazi ukuphatha amarekhodi awo e-DNS?

Yebo. Amakhasimende athola isixhumi esibonakalayo sokuphatha i-DNS ku-dashboard yawo ukuze angeze, ahlele, futhi asuse amarekhodi e-A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, kanye ne-TXT. Ukuphathwa kwe-DNS kuyatholakala nge-OpenSRS, ResellerClub, Enom, HostAfrica, kanye ne-Openprovider. I-DNS ye-Hostinger yezizinda ezisingathiwe iphathwa ukuhlanganiswa okuyinhloko kwe-Hostinger kokuxhumanisa izizinda.

### Ikhona yini iwizadi yokusetha?

Yebo. Iwizadi yokusetha isebenza ngokuzenzakalelayo ekusebenzeni kokuqala futhi ikudlulisa ekuxhumaniseni umbhalisi, ukungenisa ama-TLD, nokudala umkhiqizo wakho wokuqala wesizinda. Ungakwazi futhi ukuqalisa kabusha iwizadi kusuka kuzilungiselelo zesengezo.
