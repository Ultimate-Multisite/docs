# Ultimate AI Connector yeziphetho ezihambisanayo

I-Ultimate AI Connector yeziphetho ezihambisanayo ihlanganisa ama-API abahlinzeki be-AI ahambisana ne-OpenAI nenethiwekhi yakho ye-WordPress multisite. Lungiselela abahlinzeki be-AI abaningi — ngamunye ene-URL yesiphetho sakhe, ukhiye we-API, nemodeli ezenzakalelayo — bese uqondisa izicelo ngokubuyela ngokuzenzakalelayo komunye umhlinzeki uma umhlinzeki engatholakali.

## Izici Eziyinhloko

- **Ukusekelwa kwabahlinzeki abaningi**: Engeza amakhadi abahlinzeki be-AI amaningi, ngalinye line-URL yesiphetho salo, ukhiye we-API, nemodeli ezenzakalelayo
- **Hudula ukuze uhlele kabusha**: Hlela abahlinzeki ngokubaluleka usebenzisa isibambo sokubamba ekhadini ngalinye; izicelo zisebenzisa abahlinzeki ngokulandelana
- **Ukuqondisa ngokubuyela ngokuzenzakalelayo**: Uma umhlinzeki oyinhloko engatholakali, izicelo zibuyela ngokuzenzakalelayo kumhlinzeki olandelayo olungiselelwe
- **Ukulungiselela komhlinzeki ngamunye**: Umhlinzeki ngamunye ulungiselelwa ngokuzimela ngemininingwane yakhe yokufakazela nobukhethwa bemodeli
- **I-UI yezilungiselelo esekelwe emakhadini**: Yakhiwe phezu kwezingxenye ze-Card ezizinzile ze-WordPress 6.9+ (CardBody, CardHeader, CardDivider)
- **Ukunwetshwa ngokuzenzakalelayo lapho kwengezwa**: Amakhadi amasha abahlinzeki anwebeka ngokuzenzakalelayo ukuze ukwazi ukugcwalisa imininingwane ngokushesha
- **Ukuqeda inqolobane yeskripthi**: Izikripthi zabahlinzeki ziqedelwa inqolobane lapho i-plugin ibuyekezwa ukuze kuqinisekiswe ukuthi inguqulo yakamuva iyalayishwa

## Izimfuneko

- WordPress 5.3 noma ngaphezulu
- PHP 7.4 noma ngaphezulu
- I-plugin ye-Ultimate Multisite (iyasebenza)
- Isiphetho se-API esihambisana ne-OpenAI (esisingathwa nguwe noma esivela enkampanini yangaphandle)

## Ukufakwa

1. Layisha amafayela esengezo enkombeni yakho ethi `/wp-content/plugins/`
2. Yenza i-plugin isebenze ngemenyu ethi 'Plugins' ku-WordPress
3. Zulazula uye ku-**Ultimate Multisite → AI Connector** ukuze ulungiselele isengezo

## Ukulungiselela

### Ukusetha Abahlinzeki Abaningi

Isikrini sokulungiselela sisebenzisa ukuhlelwa okusekelwe emakhadini lapho ikhadi ngalinye limelela umhlinzeki oyedwa we-AI. Ungangeza abahlinzeki abaningi ngendlela oyidingayo.

#### Ukwengeza Umhlinzeki

1. Iya ku-**Ultimate Multisite → AI Connector** kumlawuli wenethiwekhi
2. Chofoza **Engeza Umhlinzeki**
3. Ikhadi elisha lomhlininzeki liyavela futhi linwebeka ngokuzenzakalelayo
4. Gcwalisa imininingwane yomhlininzeki:
   - **Igama Lomhlininzeki** — ilebula yokuhlonza lo mhlinzeki (isb. "OpenAI", "Mistral", "Local Ollama")
   - **I-URL Yesiphetho** — i-URL eyisisekelo ye-API ehambisana ne-OpenAI (isb. `https://api.openai.com/v1`)
   - **Ukhiye we-API** — ukhiye wakho we-API walo mhlinzeki
   - **Imodeli Ezenzakalelayo** — isihlonzi semodeli esizosetshenziswa ngokuzenzakalelayo kulo mhlinzeki (isb. `gpt-4o`, `mistral-small`)
5. Chofoza **Londoloza Izilungiselelo**

#### Ukususa Umhlinzeki

Chofoza inkinobho ethi **Susa** kunoma yiliphi ikhadi lomhlininzeki ukuze ulisuse. Londoloza izilungiselelo ukuze usebenzise ushintsho.

#### Ukuhlela Kabusha Abahlinzeki

Hudula isithonjana sokubamba (⣿) ohlangothini lwesobunxele lwanoma yiliphi ikhadi lomhlininzeki ukuze ulihlele kabusha. Ukuhleleka kunquma ukubaluleka kokubuyela komunye umhlinzeki: izicelo zithunyelwa kumhlinzeki wokuqala bese zibuyela kubahlinzeki abalandelayo uma isicelo sehluleka.

### Ukuqondisa Ngokubuyela Komunye Umhlinzeki

Lapho isicelo kumhlinzeki oyinhloko sehluleka — ngenxa yokuphelelwa isikhathi, umkhawulo wezinga, noma ukuphazamiseka kwesevisi — isixhumi sizama futhi ngokuzenzakalelayo ngomhlininzeki olandelayo ohlwini. Lokhu kuyaqhubeka kuhlu kuze kube yilapho umhlinzeki ephumelela noma bonke abahlinzeki sebezanyiwe.

| Isimo | Indlela esebenza ngayo |
|----------|-----------|
| Umhlinzeki oyinhloko uyaphendula | Isicelo sisetshenzwa ngokujwayelekile |
| Umhlinzeki oyinhloko uyehluleka | Isicelo sizanywa futhi ngomhlininzeki olandelayo |
| Bonke abahlinzeki bayehluleka | Iphutha libuyiselwa kobizayo |

Ukubuyela komunye umhlinzeki kucacile ku-plugin noma itimu ebizayo — bathola impendulo ephumelele noma iphutha lokugcina, kungakhathaliseki ukuthi bangaki abahlinzeki abazanyiwe.

### Inkomba Yokulungiselela Umhlinzeki

| Inkambu | Iyadingeka | Incazelo |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Igama Lomhlininzeki | Yebo | Ilebula yokubonisa yalo mhlinzeki |
| I-URL Yesiphetho | Yebo | I-URL eyisisekelo ye-API ehambisana ne-OpenAI |
| Ukhiye we-API | Yebo | Ukhiye wokuqinisekisa walo mhlinzeki |
| Imodeli Ezenzakalelayo | Yebo | Isihlonzi semodeli esisetshenziswa lapho kungekho modeli ecacisiwe esicelweni |

## Ukuxazulula Izinkinga

### Abekho abahlinzeki abafakwe ohlwini

Uma uhlu lwabahlinzeki lungenalutho ngemva kokulondoloza, qinisekisa ukuthi:

- Uchofoze **Londoloza Izilungiselelo** ngemva kokwengeza abahlinzeki
- Isiphequluli sakho asivimbanga ikhasi lezilungiselelo ekulondolozeni (hlola ikhonsoli yesiphequluli ukuze uthole amaphutha e-JavaScript)
- I-plugin iyasebenza futhi usesikrinini somlawuli wenethiwekhi

### Izicelo ziyehluleka kubo bonke abahlinzeki

- Qinisekisa ukuthi i-URL yesiphetho yomhlininzeki ngamunye iyafinyeleleka kuseva yakho (hhayi kuphela esipheqululini sakho)
- Hlola ukuthi ukhiye we-API ngamunye uvumelekile futhi awukaphelelwa isikhathi
- Buyekeza umkhawulo wezinga womhlininzeki nesimo sesabelo ku-dashboard yabo
- Nika amandla ukuloga kokulungisa amaphutha kwe-WordPress (`WP_DEBUG_LOG`) ukuze uthwebule imilayezo yamaphutha enemininingwane evela kusixhumi

### Umhlinzeki akasetshenziselwa ukubuyela komunye umhlinzeki

Ukuhudula ukuze uhlele kabusha kushintsha ukuhleleka kokubuyela komunye umhlinzeki. Qinisekisa ukuthi umhlinzeki olindele ukuthi athole izicelo zokubuyela komunye umhlinzeki ubekwe kahle ohlwini nokuthi i-URL yesiphetho sakhe nokhiye we-API kulungile.

### Ikhadi elisha lomhlininzeki alinwebeki ngokuzenzakalelayo

Ukunwetshwa ngokuzenzakalelayo kudinga ukuthi i-JavaScript inikwe amandla esipheqululini sakho. Uma ikhadi linganwebeki lapho uchofoza, hlola ikhonsoli yesiphequluli ukuze uthole amaphutha. Okuqukethwe kwekhadi kuhlale kufinyeleleka ngokuchofoza unhlokweni wekhadi ngisho noma ukunwetshwa ngokuzenzakalelayo kungaqali.
