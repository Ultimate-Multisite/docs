# Mailster {#mailster}

Guqula inethiwekhi yakho ye-multisite ibe umshini onamandla wokwakha uhlu lwama-imeyili ngesengezo se-Ultimate Multisite Mailster Integration. Engeza ngokuzenzakalelayo amakhasimende amasha ohlwini lwakho lwama-imeyili e-Mailster lapho ebhalisa noma ethenga, udale ukuxhumana okushelelayo phakathi kwenkundla yakho yobulungu nesu lakho lokumaketha nge-imeyili.

## Izici Eziyinhloko {#key-features}

**Ukuvumelanisa Ababhalisile Okuzenzakalelayo**
* Engeza ngokuzenzakalelayo amakhasimende ohlwini lwe-Mailster lapho ebhalisa
* Khetha ukuvumelanisa ngokushesha lapho kubhaliswa noma ulinde ukuqinisekiswa kwenkokhelo
* Gcina uhlu lwakho lwama-imeyili lusha futhi lusesikhathini ngaphandle kokungenelela ngesandla

**Ukuphathwa Kohlu Okuguquguqukayo**
* Setha izinhlu ezizenzakalelayo zomhlaba wonke zawo wonke amakhasimende amasha
* Yeqa okuzenzakalelayo ngezinhlu eziqondene nomkhiqizo zemikhankaso eqondiswe kahle
* Phatha kalula ukunikezwa kwezinhlu ngesixhumi esibonakalayo esijwayelekile se-Mailster

**Izinketho Zokuvuma Ezihambisana ne-GDPR**
* Khetha phakathi kokubhalisa okuzenzakalelayo noma imvume esekelwe ebhokisini lokuhlola
* Nika amandla i-double opt-in yokubhalisa okuqinisekisiwe
* Bonisa/fihla ibhokisi lokuhlola lokuvuma ngokusekelwe ezidingweni zakho zokuhambisana

**Ukuqondanisa Idatha Okuhlakaniphile**
* Vumelanisa ngokuzenzakalelayo amagama amakhasimende nolwazi lokuxhumana
* Qondanisa amakheli okukhokhisa nezinkambu zababhalisile
* Landelela izinsuku zokubhalisa zemikhankaso eqondiswe kahle

**Ukulawula Ezingeni Lomkhiqizo**
* Nikeza izinhlu ezihlukene emikhiqizweni ehlukene
* Dala imikhankaso eqondiswe kahle ngokusekelwe kulokho amakhasimende akuthengayo
* Khubaza i-Mailster emikhiqizweni ethile uma kudingeka

**Okuhlangenwe Nakho Kobungcweti**
* Ukuhlanganiswa kwe-UI ye-Mailster yemvelo yokukhetha izinhlu
* Ukuloga okunemininingwane yokuxazulula izinkinga nokuqinisekisa
* Ukubuyekezwa okushelelayo kwababhalisile abakhona

## Ilungele {#perfect-for}

* Abanikazi bamasayithi obulungu abakha izinhlu zama-imeyili
* Izinkundla ze-SaaS ezifuna ukunakekela amakhasimende amasha
* Abadali bezifundo abahlukanisa abafundi ngokubhaliswa
* Noma ubani osebenzisa Ultimate Multisite ne-Mailster

## Isebenza Kanjani {#how-it-works}

1. Faka futhi wenze isengezo sisebenze kunethiwekhi yakho ye-Ultimate Multisite
2. Lungiselela izinhlu ezizenzakalelayo zomhlaba wonke kuzilungiselelo ze-Ultimate Multisite
3. Ngokuzithandela yeqa ngezinhlu eziqondene nomkhiqizo
4. Khetha imodi yakho yokuvuma nesikhathi sokubhalisa
5. Buka izinhlu zakho ze-Mailster zikhula ngokuzenzakalelayo!

## Ukufaka {#installation}

1. Layisha amafayela e-plugin kufolda yezingezo ye-Ultimate Multisite yakho
2. Qinisekisa ukuthi i-Mailster ifakiwe futhi iyasebenza kusayithi lakho eliyinhloko
3. Yenza isengezo sisebenze ngekhasi lezingezo le-Ultimate Multisite
4. Zulazulela kokuthi Izilungiselelo > Ukuhlanganiswa kwe-Mailster ukuze ulungiselele
5. Khetha izinhlu zakho ezizenzakalelayo futhi ulungiselele okuncamelayo kokuvuma

## Imibuzo Evame Ukubuzwa {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Ingabe lesi sengezo sisebenza ngaphandle kwe-Ultimate Multisite? {#does-this-addon-work-without-ultimate-multisite}

Cha, lesi sengezo sidinga i-Ultimate Multisite ukuze sisebenze. Siklanyelwe ngokukhethekile ukuhlanganisa i-Mailster nohlelo lwamakhasimende nemikhiqizo lwe-Ultimate Multisite.

## Ingabe ngidinga ukuthi i-Mailster ifakwe kuwo wonke amasayithi? {#do-i-need-mailster-installed-on-all-sites}

Cha, i-Mailster idinga kuphela ukufakwa futhi yenziwe isebenze kusayithi lakho eliyinhloko lenethiwekhi. Isengezo sizoxhuma kuyo ngokuzenzakalelayo sisuka kuwo wonke amasayithi angaphansi.

## Ngingakwazi ukunikeza izinhlu ezihlukene emikhiqizweni ehlukene? {#can-i-assign-different-lists-to-different-products}

Yebo! Ungasetha izinhlu ezizenzakalelayo zomhlaba wonke, bese uyeqa ngokomkhiqizo ngamunye. Lokhu kukuvumela ukuthi udale imikhankaso yama-imeyili eqondiswe kakhulu ngokusekelwe kulokho amakhasimende akuthengayo.

## Kwenzekani uma ikhasimende selivele likhona ku-Mailster? {#what-happens-if-a-customer-already-exists-in-mailster}

Ngokuzenzakalelayo, isengezo sizobuyekeza ababhalisile abakhona futhi sibangeze ezinhlu ezintsha. Ungakukhubaza lokhu kuzilungiselelo uma ukhetha ukweqa ababhalisile abakhona.

## Ingabe lokhu kuyahambisana ne-GDPR? {#is-this-gdpr-compliant}

Yebo! Isengezo sifaka imodi yokuvuma esekelwe ebhokisini lokuhlola lapho amakhasimende kufanele avume ngokusobala ukuthola ama-imeyili. Lokhu kuhlangabezana nezidingo ze-GDPR uma kuhlanganiswe nezinqubomgomo zobumfihlo ezifanele.

## Ngingakwazi ukusebenzisa i-double opt-in? {#can-i-use-double-opt-in}

Nakanjani! Nika amandla isilungiselelo se-double opt-in futhi i-Mailster izothumela ama-imeyili okuqinisekisa ngaphambi kokwenza ukubhalisa kusebenze.
