# Isihlungi: wu_connector_enforcement_option_keys

Hlunga uhlu lokhiye bezinketho zomhlinzeki we-AI abaphoqelelwa kusuka kusayithi eyinhloko.

Engeza okhiye bezinketho bama-plugin abahlinzeki be-AI enziwe ngokwezifiso noma avela kubahlinzeki bangaphandle ukuze izilungiselelo zawo nazo zizuzwe njengefa kusuka kusayithi eyinhloko kumasayithi angaphansi.

## Amapharamitha {#parameters}

| Igama | Uhlobo | Incazelo |
|------|------|-------------|
| $keys | `string[]` | Amagama okhiye bezinketho okufanele aphoqqelelwe (asevele afaka okhiye be-connector abatholwe ngokuzenzakalelayo kanye ne-EXTRA_PROVIDER_OPTIONS). |
