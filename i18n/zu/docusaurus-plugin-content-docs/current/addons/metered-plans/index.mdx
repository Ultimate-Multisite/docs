# Izinhlelo Ezilinganisiwe

I-addon ye-Ultimate Multisite: Izinhlelo Ezilinganisiwe iguqula indlela okhokhisa ngayo amakhasimende akho ngokwethula amakhono okukhokhisa aguquguqukayo asuselwe ekusetshenzisweni. Esikhundleni semikhawulo eqinile evimba abasebenzisi uma beyidlula, vumela izindleko zokweqisa ezikhokhisa amakhasimende ngokuzenzakalelayo ngokusetshenziswa okwengeziwe.

## Izici Eziyinhloko

* **Ukukhokhisa Okususelwe Ekusetshenzisweni**: Khokhisa amakhasimende ngokusekelwe ekusetshenzisweni kwangempela kunamazinga agxilile
* **Ukuphathwa Kokweqisa**: Khokhisa ngokuzenzakalelayo ukusetshenziswa okudlula imikhawulo yecebo
* **Ukulandelela Ukusetshenziswa**: Gada ukusetshenziswa kwezinsiza zekhasimende ngesikhathi sangempela
* **Amanani Aguquguqukayo**: Setha amanani angokwezifiso ezinsizeni ezahlukene (isitoreji, bandwidth, amasayithi, njll.)
* **Ukukhokhisa Ama-token e-AI**: Landela futhi ukhokhise ama-token okokufaka nawokukhipha ngokusetshenziswa kwe-AI kuwo wonke amasayithi angaphansi amakhasimende
* **Ukukhiqizwa Kwama-invoice Okuzenzakalelayo**: Khiqiza ama-invoice ngokuzenzakalelayo lapho imikhawulo yokusetshenziswa idlulwa
* **Imibiko Yokusetshenziswa**: Ukuhlaziywa okunemininingwane kokusetshenziswa nemibiko yamakhasimende nabaphathi

## Ifanele Kakhulu

* **Abahlinzeki Be-hosting**: Nikeza izinhlelo ezikhula nezidingo zekhasimende
* **Amapulatifomu e-SaaS**: Khokhisa ngokusekelwe ekusetshenzisweni kwangempela kwezinsiza
* **Amanethiwekhi Ama-ejensi**: Amamodeli entengo aguquguqukayo wamaphrojekthi amaklayenti
* **I-Reseller Hosting**: Vumela ozakwethu banikeze amanani asuselwe ekusetshenzisweni

## Isebenza Kanjani

1. Lungiselela izinsiza ezilinganisiwe (indawo yediski, bandwidth, inani lamasayithi, njll.)
2. Setha imikhawulo eyisisekelo yecebo namanani okweqisa
3. Uhlelo lulandelela ngokuzenzakalelayo ukusetshenziswa kuyo yonke inethiwekhi yakho
4. Lapho amakhasimende edlula imikhawulo, izindleko zokweqisa ziyabalwa
5. Ama-invoice akhiqizwa ngokuzenzakalelayo ngokusekelwe emjikelezweni wokukhokhisa
6. Amakhasimende angabuka ukusetshenziswa kwawo nezindleko ezizayo ngesikhathi sangempela

## Ukukhokhisa Ama-token e-AI

Inguqulo 1.1.0 yengeza ukukhokhisa ama-token e-AI kumanethiwekhi anikeza izici ze-AI nge-WordPress AI Client SDK. Izinhlelo Ezilinganisiwe zilalela izicelo eziqediwe zokukhiqiza nge-AI, zirekhode izibalo zama-token okokufaka nawokukhipha kusayithi elingaphansi lamanje, bese ziguqula lawo ma-token abe yizindleko ze-AI ezikhokhisekayo zesikhathi sokukhokhisa samanje sekhasimende.

Sebenzisa ukukhokhisa ama-token e-AI lapho ufuna ukunikeza amathuluzi e-AI kumasayithi amakhasimende ngaphandle kokuthwala izindleko zemodeli ezingalindelekile. Isicelo ngasinye sixhunywa kusayithi elingaphansi, ikhasimende, ubulungu, umhlinzeki, nemodeli ukuze abaphathi bakwazi ukuhlola ukusetshenziswa kuyilapho amakhasimende ebona ukuthi ukusetshenziswa kwawo kwe-AI kuyithinta kanjani i-account yawo.

### Lungiselela Amanani Nge-token Ngayinye

Amanani e-AI alungiselelwa ngemodeli ngayinye njengamanani ahlukene okokufaka nawokukhipha. Amanani abalwa ngama-token ayisigidi esingu-1, okuhambisana namakhasi entengo ajwayelekile abahlinzeki be-AI.

1. Ku-admin yenethiwekhi, vula **Multisite Ultimate → Settings → Metered Plans**.
2. Nika amandla ukulinganiswa kwezindleko ze-AI kumkhiqizo noma ubulungu okufanele kukhokhise ukusetshenziswa kwe-AI.
3. Engeza okufakiwe kwamanani emodeli usebenzisa ama-ID emodeli abuyiswa yi-connector yakho, njenge-`gpt-4o`, `gpt-4o-mini`, noma esinye isihlonzi semodeli ehambisana ne-OpenAI.
4. Faka amanani ahlukene e-**Input Rate** ne-**Output Rate** ngohlobo lwemali lwesayithi lakho ngama-token ayisigidi esingu-1.
5. Setha isabelo sezindleko ze-AI ezifakiwe zekhasimende nokuziphatha kokweqisa kwecebo.

Lapho i-ID yemodeli eqondile ingalungiselelwanga, Izinhlelo Ezilinganisiwe zingafanisa ngesandulelo semodeli. Isibonelo, inani elilungiselelwe i-`claude-sonnet-4` lingasebenza kuma-ID emodeli anezitembu zesikhathi aqala ngaleso sandulelo. Abathuthukisi bangakwazi futhi ukulungisa ithebula lokugcina lamanani ngesihlungi se-[`wu_ai_token_rates`](./hooks/Filters/wu_ai_token_rates).

### Ama-connector e-AI Asekelwayo

Ukukhokhisa ama-token e-AI kusebenza nama-connector akhipha imicimbi yokukhiqiza ye-WordPress AI Client SDK futhi afaka ukusetshenziswa kwama-token kudatha ye-metadata yempendulo. Lokhu kufaka umndeni wama-connector e-Ultimate Multisite AI obhalwe lapha:

* [I-Ultimate AI Connector Yama-endpoint Ahambisanayo](../ultimate-ai-connector-compatible-endpoints/) yama-API ahambisana ne-OpenAI, okuhlanganisa i-OpenAI, Mistral, amasango ahambisana ne-Ollama, nabanye abahlinzeki abadalula i-metadata yokusetshenziswa kwama-token ehambisanayo
* [I-Ultimate AI Connector ye-WebLLM](../ultimate-ai-connector-webllm/) yokusetshenziswa kwe-WebLLM esebenza kusiphequluli lapho izicelo zidluliselwa ngokugeleza komcimbi we-AI Client SDK
* Ama-connector abahlinzeki be-AI asetshenziswa yi-[Gratis AI Agent](../gratis-ai-agent/) lapho umhlinzeki okhethiwe ebika izibalo zama-token e-prompt nawokuqedela nge-SDK

Ama-connector angokwezifiso angabamba iqhaza ngokudubula imicimbi ejwayelekile ye-`wp_ai_client_before_generate_result` ne-`wp_ai_client_after_generate_result` ene-metadata yemodeli, umhlinzeki, nokusetshenziswa kwama-token. Izilungiselelo ze-connector ziphoqelelwa kusuka kusayithi eliyinhloko kuya kumasayithi angaphansi ukuze amasayithi amakhasimende asebenzise abahlinzeki nemikhawulo egunyazwe yinethiwekhi.

### Ukusetshenziswa Kwe-Account Yekhasimende

Amakhasimende abona ukusetshenziswa kwe-AI kanye nezinye izinsiza zawo ezilinganisiwe endaweni ye-account yawo. Umugqa wezindleko ze-AI ubonisa ingqikithi yesikhathi sokukhokhisa samanje etholakala kuma-token e-prompt nawokuqedela arekhodiwe, amanani emodeli alungiselelwe, nanoma yisiphi isabelo esifakiwe kucebo.

Uma ikhasimende linendlela yokukhokha efayelini, ukusetshenziswa okungaphezu kwesabelo esifakiwe kuthathwa njengokweqisa okukhokhisekayo. Uma icebo lilungiselelwe ukunqanyulwa okuqinile futhi ikhasimende lingenayo indlela yokukhokha etholakalayo, izicelo ze-AI zingavinjwa lapho isabelo sesifinyelelwe futhi ikhasimende licelwe ukuthi lengeze imininingwane yokukhokha ngaphambi kokuqhubeka.

## Izinsiza Ezisekelwayo

* Isitoreji sediski
* Bandwidth/Ukudluliswa kwedatha
* Inani lamasayithi
* Inani labasebenzisi
* Ukusetshenziswa kwama-token e-AI nezindleko ze-AI
* Izinhlobo zezinsiza zangokwezifiso

## Izidingo

* Multisite Ultimate 2.0.0 noma ngaphezulu
* Ukufakwa kwe-WordPress Multisite
* PHP 7.4 noma ngaphezulu

## Ukufakwa

1. Layisha amafayela e-plugin ku-`/wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite-metered-plans`
2. Yenza i-plugin isebenze ngesikrini se-'Plugins' ku-WordPress
3. Iya ku-Multisite Ultimate → Settings → Metered Plans
4. Lungiselela izinsiza zakho ezilinganisiwe namanani
5. Nika amandla ukukhokhisa okulinganisiwe kwemikhiqizo kusihleli somkhiqizo

## Imibuzo Evame Ukubuzwa

## Kwenzekani lapho ikhasimende lidlula umkhawulo walo?

Esikhundleni sokuvimba ukufinyelela, uhlelo lulandelela ukweqisa bese lukwengeza ku-invoice yalo elandelayo ngokusekelwe kumanani owalungiselele.

## Ngingakwazi ukusetha amanani okweqisa ahlukene emikhiqizweni ehlukene?

Yebo, umkhiqizo ngamunye ungaba nezinsiza ezilinganisiwe ezihlukile namanani okweqisa.

## Amakhasimende aziswa kanjani ngokusetshenziswa?

Amakhasimende angabuka ukusetshenziswa kwawo kwamanje ku-dashboard yawo futhi athole izaziso ozikhethela zona lapho esondela noma edlula imikhawulo.

## Ukusetshenziswa kwama-token e-AI kubekwa kanjani intengo?

Ukusetshenziswa kwama-token e-AI kubekwa intengo kusukela kumanani okokufaka nawokukhipha alungiselelwe emodeli ngama-token ayisigidi esingu-1. Izinhlelo Ezilinganisiwe ziphindaphinda izibalo zama-token e-prompt nawokuqedela zesicelo ngasinye ngalawo manani, zengeze umphumela ezindlekweni zamanje ze-AI zesikhathi sokukhokhisa sekhasimende, bese zisebenzisa isabelo secebo noma imithetho yokweqisa.

## Ngingakwazi yini ukuqhubeka nokusebenzisa imikhawulo eqinile?

Yebo, ukukhokhisa okulinganisiwe kungokuzikhethela ngomkhiqizo ngamunye. Ungaxuba imikhiqizo yendabuko enemikhawulo eqinile nemikhiqizo elinganisiwe.
