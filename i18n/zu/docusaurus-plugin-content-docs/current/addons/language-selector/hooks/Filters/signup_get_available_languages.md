# Isihlungi: signup_get_available_languages {#filter-signupgetavailablelanguages}

Sihluza uhlu lwezilimi ezitholakalayo zokubhaliswa kwamasayithi ohlangothini lwangaphambili.

Ukudlulisa i-array engenalutho kule hook kuzokhubaza ukukhishwa kwesilungiselelo kwifomu lokubhalisa, futhi ulimi oluzenzakalelayo luzosetshenziswa lapho kwakhiwa isayithi. Izilimi ezingakafakwa zizokhishwa.

## Amapharamitha {#parameters}

| Igama | Uhlobo | Incazelo |
|------|------|-------------|
| $available_languages | `array` | Izilimi ezitholakalayo. |
