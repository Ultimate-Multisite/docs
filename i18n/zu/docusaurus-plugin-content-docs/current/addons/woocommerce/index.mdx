# Ukuhlanganiswa kwe-WooCommerce {#woocommerce-integration}

Ukuhlanganiswa kwe-WooCommerce kusebenza ngendlela efana kakhulu nendlela i-Manual Gateway yethu esebenza ngayo. Kunikeza abasebenzisi bakho ukuguquguquka okukhulu, kubavumela ukuthi bakhethe noma yiliphi ifomu lokukhokha elitholakala ekufakweni kwakho kwe-WooCommerce. Futhi kulula: uma isikhathi sokukhokhiswa komsebenzisi sesiphela, ukubhalisa komsebenzisi kubekwa kulindile. Ngesikhathi esifanayo, i-oda lizodalwa ekufakweni kwe-WooCommerce kwesayithi eliyinhloko lenethiwekhi. Umsebenzisi ube esethola i-invoyisi ku-imeyili yakhe, enesixhumanisi sokwenza inkokhelo. Uma inkokhelo isitholakele, ukubhalisa kuyavuselelwa.

## Izici {#features}

- Ukuhlanganiswa okulula phakathi kwe-Ultimate Multisite ne-WooCommerce
- Izindlela zokukhokha eziningana ezitholakalayo
- Ukukhokhisa okuphindaphindayo okuzenzakalelayo kusetshenziswa ingqikithi yokubhalisa efakiwe, ngokuthuthukiswa okungakhethwa kwe-WooCommerce Subscriptions plugin
- Izamba ze-checkout zifaka izintela ze-Ultimate Multisite njengemigqa yemali ye-WooCommerce ehlukene, engakhokhiswa ntela ukuze amakhasimende abuyekeze amanani entela ngaphambi kokukhokha

## Imibhalo {#documentation}

**Ukusetha Ukuhlanganiswa kwe-WooCommerce**

**Ukwandisa Izinketho Zakho Zokukhokha nge-Ultimate Multisite: Ukuhlanganiswa kwe-WooCommerce**

## Ukufakwa {#installation}

1. Layisha 'ultimate-multisite-woocommerce' enkombeni ethi '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Yenza i-plugin isebenze ngemenyu ethi 'Plugins' ku-WordPress
3. Landela i-Wizard isinyathelo ngesinyathelo ukuze usethe i-plugin

## Umhlahlandlela Wokusebenzisa {#usage-guide}

_**INOTHI ELIBALULEKILE: Lesi sihloko sibhekisela ku-Ultimate Multisite version 2.x.**_

_**QAPHELA:** Ultimate Multisite: Ukuhlanganiswa kwe-WooCommerce kudinga ukuthi i-WooCommerce ivulwe okungenani kusayithi lakho eliyinhloko._

Siyaqonda ukuthi i-_Stripe_ ne-_PayPal_ azitholakali kwamanye amazwe, okukhawulela noma okuvimba abasebenzisi be-Ultimate Multisite ekusebenziseni i-plugin yethu ngempumelelo. Ngakho sidale **i-add-on yokuhlanganisa _WooCommerce_**.

_I-WooCommerce_ iyi-plugin ye-eCommerce ethandwa kakhulu. Onjiniyela emhlabeni wonke badale ama-add-on okuhlanganisa amasango okukhokha ahlukahlukene kuyo. Sisebenzise lokhu ukuze sandise amasango okukhokha ongawasebenzisa ohlelweni lokukhokhisa lwe-Ultimate Multisite.

## Ukuyisetha {#setting-it-up}

Okokuqala, udinga ukufaka i-add-on ye-WooCommerce Integration **emahhala kubasebenzisi abanamalayisense**. Ukuze uyifake, chofoza ku-Ultimate Multisite ku-Dashboard yakho ye-super admin bese ukhetha **Add-ons**.

![Ikhasi lama-Add-ons](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

Lapha ungathola wonke ama-add-on e-Ultimate Multisite. Chofoza ku-add-on ethi **Ultimate Multisite: WooCommerce Integration**.

![Ikhasi lama-Add-ons elibonisa i-WooCommerce Integration](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

Iwindi lizovela nemininingwane ye-add-on. Vele uchofoze ku-**Install Now** *.*

Ngemuva kokuthi ukufakwa sekuqediwe, uzoqondiswa ekhasini lama-plugins. Lapha, vele uchofoze ku-**Network Activate** futhi i-add-on ye-_WooCommerce_ izovulwa kunethiwekhi yakho.

Ngemuva kokuyivula, uma ungakabi nayo i-WooCommerce plugin efakiwe futhi evuliwe kuwebhusayithi yakho, **uzothola isikhumbuzi**.

Uma isifakiwe, uzobona inketho eyengeziwe ngaphansi kwezilungiselelo zokukhokha ze-Ultimate Multisite. Ungabe usuyakwazi **ukuvula** inketho ye-_WooCommerce_ lapho.

![Izilungiselelo zesango lokukhokha le-Ultimate Multisite ezine-WooCommerce evuliwe](/img/addons/woocommerce-payment-gateway.png)

Ungalungiselela ezinye izilungiselelo eziyisisekelo njengegama lokubonisa elizovela ngaphambili kanye nemiyalelo yokuqondisa abasebenzisi bakho ekugelezeni kwabo kwe-checkout. Bese ungaya **kuzilungiselelo ze-_WooCommerce_ zesayithi lakho eliyinhloko** ukuze uvule futhi ulungiselele isango lokukhokha olikhethayo ukulisebenzisa.

### Imigqa yemali yentela ye-checkout {#checkout-tax-fee-lines}

Uma i-checkout ye-Ultimate Multisite ifaka izintela, ukuhlanganiswa kwengeza inani lentela ngalinye le-Ultimate Multisite ku-checkout ye-WooCommerce njengomugqa wemali ohlukene, ongakhokhiswa ntela. Lokhu kugcina isamba sentela sibonakala ngaphambi kokukhokha kuyilapho kuvimbela i-WooCommerce ukuthi iphinde ikhokhise intela emalini yentela.

## Indlela yokuphatha inkokhelo {#how-to-manage-the-payment}

Ukuhlanganiswa kwe-WooCommerce kusebenza ngendlela efana kakhulu nendlela i-**Manual Gateway** yethu esebenza ngayo. Ububi ukuthi yonke inkokhelo kufanele ikhokhwe ngesandla ekupheleni kwesikhathi sokukhokhisa. Nansi indlela ukugeleza okusebenza ngayo:

  * Uma umjikelezo wokukhokhiswa komsebenzisi usuphela, ubulungu bubekwa **kulindile**. Ngesikhathi esifanayo, i-oda lizodalwa ekufakweni kwe-_WooCommerce_ kusayithi eliyinhloko lenethiwekhi yakho.

  * Abasebenzisi babe sebethola i-invoyisi ku-imeyili yabo, enesixhumanisi sokwenza inkokhelo (isixhumanisi siphinde sivele emlandweni wabo wokukhokhiswa ku-Dashboard yesayithi labo elingaphansi). Leso sixhumanisi siqondisa umsebenzisi ku-_**WooCommerce**_ **checkout** , lapho abasebenzisi bezokwazi ukukhetha elinye lama-**gateway e-_WooCommerce_ atholakalayo** ukuze bacubungule inkokhelo yabo.

  * Uma inkokhelo isitholakele, ubulungu **buyavuselelwa** , inkokhelo ibhaliswa ku-Ultimate Multisite futhi i-oda lisethwa ukuthi liphelele.

### Amanye Amanothi Abalulekile: {#some-important-notes}

  * Ukusebenza okuzenzakalelayo kwe-Ultimate Multisite kusasebenza nalolu hlobo lokuhlanganiswa. Isibonelo, **ukukhipha ukubuyiselwa kwemali** esikrinini se-**Subscription Management** kuzoxhumana ne-WooCommerce ukuze kukhishwe ukubuyiselwa kwemali kusetshenziswa ifomu lokukhokha elikhethiwe.

  * Ultimate Multisite: Ukuhlanganiswa kwe-WooCommerce kuncike ku-WP-Cron ukuze kusebenze. Lokhu kusebenza kahle kakhulu ezikhathini eziningi, kodwa uma isayithi linethrafikhi ephansi i-cron ayikhuthazwa ukuthi ihlole imicimbi ehleliwe futhi umcimbi uyaphuthelwa, okungabangela ukubambezeleka kokwenziwa.

## I-WooCommerce Subscriptions Plugin {#woocommerce-subscriptions-plugin}

Ukuqalisa inkokhelo ngesandla njalo ngenyanga akukuhle kubasebenzisi bokugcina ngoba ukubhalisa kulindeleke ukuthi kuphindaphindeke ngokuzenzakalelayo emjikelezweni ngamunye wokukhokhisa. I-add-on ye-WooCommerce Integration manje ifaka ikhodi eyingqikithi yokubhalisa, ngakho imikhiqizo ephindaphindayo ihlala isebenzisa ukubhalisa ekukhokhiseni okuphindaphindayo.

**Awudingi** ukuthenga noma ukufaka i-[WooCommerce Subscriptions plugin](https://woocommerce.com/products/woocommerce-subscriptions/) ehlukile ukuze uthengise imikhiqizo ephindaphindayo ngalokhu kuhlanganiswa. Uma leyo plugin ithengiwe, yafakwa, futhi itholakala kusayithi lakho eliyinhloko, i-Ultimate Multisite izoyisebenzisa futhi i-WooCommerce izoveza izici ezengeziwe zokuphatha ukubhalisa. Uma ingatholakali, ingqikithi yokubhalisa efakiwe isaqhubeka nokuphatha imikhiqizo ephindaphindayo.

![Izilungiselelo zokuhlanganiswa kokubhalisa kwe-WooCommerce](/img/addons/woocommerce-subscription-integration.png)

Futhi kuphelele lapho. Abasebenzisi bakho bokugcina ngeke besadinga ukwenza inkokhelo ngesandla ekupheleni komjikelezo wabo wokukhokhisa wemikhiqizo ephindaphindayo.

Ngaphandle kokuthi i-oda lidalwe kusayithi lakho eliyinhloko ngaphansi kwe-_WooCommerce_, kuzophinde kudale ukubhalisa kwe-Account ngayinye.

### Ukuziphatha kokumiswa uma ukuvuselelwa kwehlulekile {#failed-renewal-suspension-behavior}

Ngokuzenzakalelayo, ubulungu buhlala busebenza kuyilapho i-WooCommerce Subscriptions icubungula ishejuli yayo yokuzama futhi ngemuva kokwehluleka kokukhokha. Lokhu kugcina iwindi elivamile lomusa le-WooCommerce Subscriptions ukuze ukuvuselelwa okubuyisiwe kungabuyisela ukubhalisa nobulungu ekuvumelaneni ngaphandle kokuphazamisa ukufinyelela ngaphambi kwesikhathi.

Abaphathi abafuna ukulawula ukufinyelela okuqinile bangavula **Suspend Memberships Immediately on Failed Renewals** kuzilungiselelo ze-WooCommerce Integration. Uma lesi silungiselelo sokukhetha ukungena sivuliwe, i-Ultimate Multisite imisa ubulungu obuhlobene ngokushesha lapho inkokhelo yokuvuselela yehluleka esikhundleni sokulinda ukuthi ukuphatha ukuzama futhi kwe-WooCommerce Subscriptions kuqede.

Onjiniyela bangabhala phezu kwesinqumo esifanayo ngesihlungi esithi `wu_woo_suspend_on_payment_failure`. Amakhasi ereferensi yama-hook akhiqizwa emthonjeni we-add-on, ngakho lokhu kubuka konke kugcizelela kuphela igama lesihlungi ngezinqubomgomo zokumisa ezenziwe ngokwezifiso.
