# Ama-imeyili

I-addon ye-Ultimate Multisite: Emails ivumela abanikazi benethiwekhi ukuthi bahlinzeke futhi baphathe ama-akhawunti e-imeyili amakhasimende kanye namasayithi nobulungu. Inguqulo 1.1.0 yengeza ukuphathwa kwama-akhawunti e-imeyili okubhekiswe kumakhasimende, ukungena okukodwa ku-webmail, ukwesekwa kwama-forwarder e-CyberPanel, kanye nokuthuthukiswa kokuhlinzekwa ngesikhathi se-checkout.

## Izici Eziyinhloko

* **Iwijethi ye-akhawunti ye-imeyili yekhasimende**: Amakhasimende angadala futhi aphathe amabhokisi eposi ekhasini lawo le-akhawunti ngemva kokuba enobulungu be-imeyili obusebenzayo.
* **I-Webmail SSO nezixhumanisi zemilingo**: Amakhasimende angavula i-webmail ngokuqondile ekhasini le-akhawunti ngokugunyazwa kokuchofoza okukodwa uma umhlinzeki ekusekela.
* **Ukushintshwa kwamaphasiwedi**: Amakhasimende nabaphathi bangabuyekeza amaphasiwedi amabhokisi eposi kusuka kuwijethi yokuphatha i-imeyili.
* **Ukuhlinzekwa kwelungelo le-checkout**: I-checkout idala ubulungu be-imeyili, ibuxhumanise nedomeni lesayithi lekhasimende, futhi yenze amathuluzi ebhokisi leposi ekhasini le-akhawunti atholakale.
* **Ama-forwarder e-CyberPanel**: Amanethiwekhi asekelwa yi-CyberPanel angadala futhi aphathe ama-forwarder e-imeyili njengengxenye yokugeleza kwesevisi ye-imeyili.
* **Izilungiselelo eziqaphela umhlinzeki**: Ikhasi lezilungiselelo lihluza abahlinzeki libashiye ekuhlanganisweni okusebenzayo nokulungiselelwe ukuze abaphathi babone kuphela izinketho ezisebenzisekayo.

## Izimfuneko

* Ultimate Multisite 2.0.0 noma ngaphezulu
* Ukufakwa kwe-WordPress Multisite
* Okungenani ukuhlanganiswa okukodwa komhlinzeki we-imeyili okulungiselelwe
* Imininingwane ye-API yomhlini we-imeyili enemvume yokudala amabhokisi eposi, ukushintsha amaphasiwedi, nokukhipha izixhumanisi zokufinyelela ku-webmail lapho kusekelwa khona

## Ukufaka

1. Layisha amafayela e-plugin ku-`/wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite-emails`.
2. Yenza i-addon isebenze kunethiwekhi kusuka ku-**Plugins** kumphathi wenethiwekhi ye-WordPress.
3. Iya ku-**Ultimate Multisite** > **Izilungiselelo** bese uvula izilungiselelo ze-imeyili ze-addon.
4. Nika amandla ukuhlanganiswa kwabahlinzeki be-imeyili ofuna amakhasimende akusebenzise.
5. Londoloza izilungiselelo, bese uhlola ukudalwa kwe-akhawunti ngekhasimende elingelona elokukhiqiza noma isayithi le-staging.

## Indlela Ukugeleza Komthengisi We-imeyili Okusebenza Ngayo

Ukuthengisa i-imeyili kunezingqimba ezimbili ezihlukene:

1. **Ilungelo** — umkhiqizo/ubulungu be-imeyili be-Ultimate Multisite obuthi ikhasimende livunyelwe ukusebenzisa i-imeyili kudomeni lesayithi elithile, umhlinzeki, i-quota, nomkhawulo wama-akhawunti.
2. **Ukudalwa kwebhokisi leposi** — ibhokisi leposi langempela lomhlini, njengokuthi `info@example.com`, elidalwa lapho ikhasimende noma umphathi wenethiwekhi ekhetha ingxenye yasendaweni nephasiwedi.

Ngesikhathi se-checkout, i-addon idala noma ivuselele **ubulungu be-imeyili** futhi irekhode idomeni lesayithi lekhasimende nomhlinzeki kulobo bulungu. Ayizami ukuqagela igama lebhokisi leposi noma iphasiwedi. Ngemva kokuba ukuthenga sekuqediwe futhi umongo wesayithi/wedomeni usukhona, ikhasimende lingavula ikhasi le-akhawunti futhi lidale amagama amabhokisi eposi eliwafunayo.

Lokhu kusho ukuthi ukugeleza kwekhasimende okujwayelekile kungokulandelayo:

1. Ikhasimende lithenga uhlelo lwesayithi futhi lengeza umkhiqizo we-imeyili ngesikhathi se-checkout.
2. Ultimate Multisite idala ubulungu besayithi kanye nobulungu besibili be-imeyili.
3. I-addon ye-Emails ixhumanisa ubulungu be-imeyili nedomeni lesayithi lekhasimende nomhlinzeki.
4. Ikhasimende livula **Account** ku-dashboard yalo.
5. Iphaneli ye-**Ama-akhawunti E-imeyili** iyavela kulobo bulungu be-imeyili obusebenzayo.
6. Ikhasimende lichofoza **Dala I-akhawunti**, lifake ingxenye yasendaweni njengokuthi `info`, lisethe iphasiwedi, bese i-addon idala `info@their-domain.com` kumhlinzeki olungiselelwe.

## Lungiselela Umkhiqizo We-imeyili

Dala umkhiqizo owodwa we-imeyili ngephakheji ngayinye ye-imeyili ofuna ukuyithengisa noma ukuyinika mahhala. Uma umusha ekusetheni imikhiqizo, qala ngo-[Ukudala Umkhiqizo Wakho Wokuqala Wokubhalisa](../../user-guide/configuration/creating-your-first-subscription-product) bese ubuya lapha ukuze uthole izilungiselelo ezikhethekile ze-imeyili.

1. Iya ku-**Network Admin > Ultimate Multisite > Products > Add Product**.
2. Setha **Uhlobo Lomkhiqizo** ku-**Imeyili**.
3. Lungiselela amanani. Sebenzisa intengo ephindaphindayo ekhokhelwayo kuhlelo oluthengiswayo, noma usethe umkhiqizo ube mahhala/ube nentengo engu-zero uma kufanele ube uhlelo lwe-imeyili oluhambisana mahhala.
4. Vula isigaba se-**Izilungiselelo Ze-imeyili**.
5. Khetha **Umhlinzeki We-imeyili** olungiselelwe.
6. Setha **Inani Lama-akhawunti**, **I-Quota Yesitoreji (MB)**, imikhawulo yama-alias, ama-forwarder, nama-autoresponder njengoba kudingeka.
7. Londoloza umkhiqizo.

Abahlinzeki abanikwe amandla futhi abalungiselelwe ngokugcwele ekhasini le-**Integrations** kuphela abavela kusikhethi somhlinzeki. Uma isikhethi singenalutho, lungiselela futhi uhlole umhlinzeki kuqala.

## Engeza I-imeyili ku-Checkout

Imikhiqizo ye-imeyili ivamise ukunikezwa njengezengezo kuhlelo lwesayithi. Bona [Amafomu E-checkout](../../user-guide/configuration/checkout-forms) ukuze uthole ukugeleza komsebenzi okujwayelekile kwesihleli se-checkout.

1. Dala noma hlela uhlelo lwesayithi amakhasimende aluthenga kuqala.
2. Engeza umkhiqizo we-imeyili njengesengezo/i-order bump etholakalayo kulokho kugeleza kwe-checkout.
3. Qinisekisa ukuthi ifomu le-checkout lisafaka izinkambu zesayithi ezijwayelekile ezidingekayo, ikakhulukazi inkambu ye-URL/domeni lesayithi.
4. Qalisa i-checkout yokuhlola ngekhasimende le-staging.
5. Ngemva kwe-checkout, vula [ikhasi le-akhawunti](../../user-guide/client-management/account-page) lekhasimende futhi uqinisekise ukuthi iphaneli ye-**Ama-akhawunti E-imeyili** iyavela.

Idomeni le-imeyili lithathwa kudomeni lesayithi lekhasimende. Amakhasimende awafaki idomeni le-imeyili esinyathelweni somkhiqizo we-imeyili.

## Ukuphathwa Kwe-imeyili Ye-akhawunti Yekhasimende

Inguqulo 1.1.0 yengeza iwijethi yokuphatha i-imeyili ekhasini le-akhawunti yekhasimende. Yisebenzise lapho amakhasimende kufanele azisebenzele ngokwawo ekudaleni nasekugcineni amabhokisi eposi amasayithi awo.

Kusuka ekhasini le-akhawunti, amakhasimende anga:

1. Buka ama-akhawunti e-imeyili axhunywe kubulungu bawo bamanje be-imeyili/idomeni lesayithi.
2. Dala ibhokisi leposi elisha lapho umkhiqizo wawo we-imeyili uvumela ama-akhawunti angeziwe.
3. Shintsha iphasiwedi yebhokisi leposi elikhona.
4. Vula abahlinzeki be-webmail abasekelwayo nge-SSO noma ngokungena ngesixhumanisi semilingo.

Iwijethi isebenzisa uhlelo lwamanje lwekhasi le-akhawunti le-Ultimate Multisite, ngakho izenzo zikhawulelwe kumongo we-akhawunti ikhasimende eliwubukayo. Lokhu kuvimbela amakhasimende ekuphatheni ama-akhawunti e-imeyili angawobunye ubulungu noma isayithi.

Uma ikhasimende lingaliboni iphaneli le-imeyili, hlola ukuthi linabo **ubulungu bomkhiqizo we-imeyili obusebenzayo**, ukuthi ubulungu buxhunyaniswe nedomeni lesayithi, nokuthi umkhiqizo unomhlini olungiselelwe.

## Ukunika Ikhasimende Uhlelo Lwamahhala Lwe-imeyili

Ukuze unike ikhasimende i-imeyili ngaphandle kokulikhokhisa, nikeza **ilungelo le-imeyili** kuqala, bese udala noma uvumele ikhasimende lidale ibhokisi leposi.

Izinketho ezinconyiwe:

* **Dala umkhiqizo we-imeyili wamahhala**: setha intengo yomkhiqizo we-imeyili ibe mahhala/zero, bese wengeza ubulungu obusebenzayo balelo khasimende kusuka ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Memberships > Add Membership**. Khetha umkhiqizo we-imeyili nekhasimende. Ubulungu bamahhala buhlala impilo yonke kuze kushintshwe noma kukhanselwe. Uma ubulungu be-imeyili buhloselwe ukulandela isayithi elikhona, qiniseka ukuthi buxhunyaniswe nalowo mongo wesayithi/wedomeni.
* **Sebenzisa isephulelo esingu-100% noma isixhumanisi se-checkout samahhala**: vumela ikhasimende lidlule ku-checkout ukuze Ultimate Multisite idale ubulungu be-imeyili nerekhodi lokukhokha ngokuzenzakalelayo.
* **Umphathi adale ibhokisi leposi**: iya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Email Accounts > Add Email Account** ukuze udale ibhokisi leposi elithile kumhlinzeki lekhasimende. Lokhu kuyasiza ekusekeleni, kodwa akubuyiseli ubulungu bomkhiqizo we-imeyili obunika amandla amathuluzi ekhasimende okuzisebenzela ekhasini le-akhawunti.

Uma ufuna ikhasimende liphathe ibhokisi leposi ngokwalo kusuka ekhasini le-akhawunti, qiniseka ukuthi linobulungu be-imeyili obusebenzayo bedomeni. Ibhokisi leposi lomhlini elidalwe mathupha ngaphandle kwalobo bulungu lingaba khona, kodwa ikhasimende lingase lingaliboni iphaneli le-imeyili lokuzisebenzela lalo.

## Ama-akhawunti Adalwe Umphathi kanye nokuthi "Ayikho Inombolo Ye-oda"

Ikhasi lomphathi le-**Ama-akhawunti E-imeyili** lidala noma lihlele ibhokisi leposi lomhlini. Akusona isinyathelo sokuqala sokuthengisa noma ukunikeza uhlelo lwe-imeyili.

Lapho uhlela i-akhawunti ye-imeyili, inkambu ye-**Ubulungu** isesha ubulungu obukhona be-Ultimate Multisite ngenombolo yabo yereferensi/ye-oda. Uma uzama ukwabela i-akhawunti ngaphambi kokuba ikhasimende libe nobulungu be-imeyili, kungase kungabi nenombolo ye-oda ongayikhetha.

Sebenzisa lolu hlelo lwezinyathelo esikhundleni salokho:

1. Dala noma thola ubulungu be-imeyili bekhasimende. Ekuthengeni, i-checkout iyakwenza lokhu. Kuhlelo oluhambisana mahhala, dala ubulungu mathupha kusuka ku-[Ubulungu](../../user-guide/administration/managing-memberships) noma thumela ikhasimende ku-checkout yamahhala.
2. Qinisekisa ukuthi ubulungu buyasebenza futhi busebenzisa umkhiqizo we-Email.
3. Qinisekisa ukuthi ubulungu be-imeyili buxhunyaniswe nedomeni/umhlinzeki wesayithi.
4. Dala ibhokisi leposi kusuka ekhasini le-akhawunti yekhasimende noma kusuka ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Email Accounts**.
5. Uma kudingeka, hlela irekhodi lebhokisi leposi bese wabela ikhasimende, ubulungu, nesayithi.

Uma udinga kuphela ukudala ibhokisi leposi lomhlini lekhasimende futhi ungadingi ukuphathwa kokuzisebenzela ekhasini le-akhawunti, i-akhawunti edalwe umphathi inganele. Uma ufuna ikhasimende liyibone futhi liyiphathe ku-Ultimate Multisite, dala nobulungu be-imeyili futhi.

## I-Webmail SSO Nokungena Ngesixhumanisi Semilingo

I-Webmail SSO inika amakhasimende isenzo esiqondile se-**Vula i-webmail** kusuka ekhasini le-akhawunti. Lapho kusekelwa ngumhlini osebenzayo, i-addon icela i-URL yokufinyelela yesikhathi esifushane futhi iqondise ikhasimende ku-webmail ngaphandle kokulicela ukuthi likopishe imininingwane yebhokisi leposi.

Ukuziphatha komhlini kungahluka:

* Abahlinzeki abanama-API e-SSO omdabu bangadala iseshini ye-webmail eqinisekisiwe ngokuqondile.
* Abahlinzeki abanokwesekwa kwesixhumanisi semilingo bangakhiqiza isixhumanisi sokungena esinomkhawulo wesikhathi.
* Abahlinzeki abangenakho ukwesekwa kwe-SSO noma kwesixhumanisi semilingo kufanele basabonisa imininingwane yebhokisi leposi nezinyathelo zokuphatha iphasiwedi, kodwa kungabi nenkinobho ye-webmail yokuchofoza okukodwa.

Nika amandla ukufinyelela ku-webmail kuphela kubahlinzeki lapho ulungiselele khona imininingwane ye-API edingekayo. Uma isenzo singekho kumhlinzeki, qala uqinisekise ukuthi umhlinzeki uyasebenza, ulungiselelwe, futhi usekela izixhumanisi zokungena ku-webmail.

## Ukuhlinzekwa Kwe-checkout

I-addon ihlinzeka **umongo wobulungu** be-imeyili ngesikhathi se-checkout. Irekhoda umhlinzeki, umkhawulo wama-akhawunti, i-quota, nedomeni lesayithi ukuze amakhasimende adale amagama awo amabhokisi eposi kusuka ekhasini le-akhawunti ngemva kokuthenga.

Ukuqalisa okunconyiwe:

1. Lungiselela umhlinzeki nemikhawulo yomkhiqizo ngaphambi kokunikeza umkhiqizo we-imeyili.
2. Hlola ukuthenga ngomkhiqizo we-staging futhi uqinisekise ukuthi ubulungu be-imeyili buyadalwa.
3. Buyekeza ikhasi le-akhawunti yekhasimende ukuze uqinisekise ukuthi iwijethi ye-**Ama-akhawunti E-imeyili** iyavela kudomeni.
4. Dala ibhokisi leposi lokuhlola kusuka kuwijethi.
5. Qinisekisa izenzo zokushintsha iphasiwedi ne-webmail ngaphambi kokunika amandla ukugeleza kwemikhiqizo yokukhiqiza.

Uma ukuhlinzekwa kwehluleka, i-akhawunti yekhasimende nobulungu kusengaba khona kuyilapho ubulungu be-imeyili noma ibhokisi leposi lomhlini kudinga ukulandelwa ngumlawuli. Hlola amalogi omsebenzi we-Ultimate Multisite namalogi omhlini ngaphambi kokuzama futhi.

## Ama-forwarder E-imeyili e-CyberPanel

Kumanethiwekhi asekelwa yi-CyberPanel, inguqulo 1.1.0 yengeza ukwesekwa kwama-forwarder e-imeyili. Sebenzisa ama-forwarder lapho amakhasimende edinga amakheli afana nokuthi `info@customer-domain.test` ukuze aqondise kabusha imeyili kwenye inbox ngaphandle kokudala ibhokisi leposi eliphelele.

Ngaphambi kokunikeza ama-forwarder, qinisekisa ukuthi ukuhlanganiswa kwe-CyberPanel kulungiselelwe futhi kuyafinyeleleka. Bona umhlahlandlela we-[Ukuhlanganiswa kwe-CyberPanel](../../user-guide/administration/cyberpanel-integration) ukuze uthole i-host, igama lomsebenzisi, iphasiwedi, nama-constant ephakheji adingekayo.

## Imibuzo Evame Ukubuzwa

### Amakhasimende angakwazi ukudala ama-akhawunti awo e-imeyili?

Yebo. Uma ikhasimende linobulungu be-imeyili obusebenzayo be-domain futhi umkhiqizo uvumela ama-akhawunti engeziwe, amakhasimende angadala futhi aphathe ama-mailbox awo ekhasini le-akhawunti.

### Ingabe wonke umhlinzeki uyayisekela i-webmail SSO?

Cha. Ukusekelwa kwe-SSO ne-magic-link kuncike kumhlinzeki we-imeyili osebenzayo. Abahlinzeki abangenayo i-access-link API basengakwazi ukusekela ukudalwa kwe-mailbox nokushintshwa kwamaphasiwedi.

### Abalawuli bangakwazi ukudala ama-akhawunti e-imeyili amakhasimende?

Yebo. Abalawuli bangadala ama-akhawunti e-imeyili kusixhumi esibonakalayo somlawuli, okuhlanganisa nokusetha iphasiwedi yokuqala lapho kudalwa i-akhawunti. Ngokuphathwa kokuzisiza kwekhasimende, qiniseka futhi ukuthi ikhasimende linobulungu be-imeyili obusebenzayo be-domain.

### Ngingayinikeza i-akhawunti ye-imeyili mahhala?

Yebo. Dala umkhiqizo we-Email wamahhala/onenani elingu-zero bese uwungeza njengobulungu obusebenzayo bekhasimende, noma uvumele ikhasimende liqedele i-checkout ngesaphulelo esingu-100%. Ngemva kokuba ubulungu be-imeyili sebukhona, ikhasimende noma umlawuli angadala i-mailbox yangempela.

### Kungani ukwabela i-akhawunti ye-imeyili kucela inombolo ye-oda?

Inkambu ye-Membership ibheka ubulungu obukhona be-Ultimate Multisite ngenombolo yayo yereferensi/ye-oda. Uma ikhasimende lingathenganga umkhiqizo we-imeyili futhi ungakadalanga mathupha ubulungu be-imeyili, abukho ubulungu/i-oda le-imeyili ongakwabela. Dala ubulungu be-imeyili kuqala, bese udala noma wabela i-mailbox.

### Ingabe ama-forwarder ayafana nama-mailbox?

Cha. I-mailbox igcina imeyili futhi inemininingwane yokungena. I-forwarder iqondisa kabusha imeyili kwenye ikheli futhi ayinikezi i-inbox ehlukile.
