# Indlela Yokumisa Ukumataniswa Kwe-domain (v2)

_**INOTHI ELIBALULEKILE: Lesi sihloko sibhekisela ku-Ultimate Multisite inguqulo 2.x.**_

Esinye sezici ezinamandla kakhulu zenethiwekhi ye-premium yikhono lokunikeza amaklayenti ethu ithuba lokunamathisela i-domain yezinga eliphezulu kumasayithi awo. Phela, yikuphi okubukeka kunobungcweti kakhulu: [_**joesbikeshop.yournetwork.com**_](http://joesbikeshop.yournetwork.com) noma [_**joesbikeshop.com**_](http://joesbikeshop.com)? Yingakho i-Ultimate Multisite inikeza leso sici esakhelwe ngaphakathi, ngaphandle kwesidingo sokusebenzisa ama-plugin enkampani yangaphandle.

## Kuyini ukumataniswa kwe-domain?

Njengoba igama liphakamisa, ukumataniswa kwe-domain yikhono elinikezwa yi-Ultimate Multisite lokwamukela isicelo se-domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso bese limatanisa leso sicelo nesayithi elifanele kunethiwekhi elinale domain ethile enamathiselwe.

### Indlela yokusetha ukumataniswa kwe-domain ku-Ultimate Multisite Network yakho

Ukumataniswa kwe-domain kudinga ukusetha okuthile ngakuwena ukuze kusebenze. Ngenhlanhla, i-Ultimate Multisite izenzakalela umsebenzi onzima kuwe ukuze ukwazi ukuhlangabezana nezidingo kalula.

Ngesikhathi sokufakwa kwe-Ultimate Multisite, iwizadi izokopisha futhi ifake ngokuzenzakalelayo i-**sunrise.php** kufolda ebekiwe. **Iwizadi ngeke ikuvumele ukuthi uqhubeke kuze kube lesi sinyathelo siqediwe**.

<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: iwizadi yokufaka ye-Ultimate Multisite enesinyathelo se-sunrise.php -->

Lokhu kusho ukuthi uma iwizadi yokufaka ye-Ultimate Multisite isiqedile ukusetha inethiwekhi yakho, ungaqala ukumatanisa i-domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso khona manjalo.

Qaphela ukuthi ukumataniswa kwe-domain ku-Ultimate Multisite akuphoqelekile. Unenketho yokusebenzisa umsebenzi wokumataniswa kwe-domain womdabu we-WordPress Multisite noma noma yisiphi esinye isixazululo sokumataniswa kwe-domain.

Uma kudingeka ukhubaze ukumataniswa kwe-domain kwe-Ultimate Multisite ukuze unikeze indawo ezinye izixazululo zokumataniswa kwe-domain, ungakhubaza lesi sici ngaphansi kokuthi **Ultimate Multisite > Izilungiselelo > Ukumataniswa Kwe-domain**.

![Ikhasi lezilungiselelo Zokumataniswa Kwe-domain elibonisa ukuqondisa kabusha komlawuli, umlayezo wokumatanisa nezinketho ze-DNS](/img/config/domain-mapping-settings.png)

Ngaphansi ngqo kwale nketho, ungabona futhi inketho ethi **Phoqa Ukuqondisa Kabusha Komlawuli**. Le nketho ikuvumela ukuthi ulawule ukuthi amakhasimende akho azokwazi yini ukufinyelela i-dashboard yawo yokuphatha kokubili ku-domain yawo eyenziwe ngokwezifiso naku-subdomain noma kweyodwa kuphela yazo.

Uma ukhetha **Phoqa ukuqondisa kabusha ku-domain ematanisiwe** , amakhasimende akho azokwazi ukufinyelela i-dashboard yawo yokuphatha kuma-domain awo enziwe ngokwezifiso kuphela.

Inketho ethi **Phoqa ukuqondisa kabusha ku-** **domain yenethiwekhi** izokwenza okuphambene ncamashi - amakhasimende akho azovunyelwa kuphela ukufinyelela ama-dashboard awo ku-subdomain yawo, ngisho noma ezama ukungena ngemvume kuma-domain awo enziwe ngokwezifiso.

Futhi inketho ethi **Vumela ukufinyelela kumlawuli ngakho kokubili i-domain ematanisiwe ne-domain yenethiwekhi** ibavumela ukuthi bafinyelele ama-dashboard abo okuphatha kokubili ku-subdomain naku-domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso.

![Uhlu olwehlayo Lokuqondisa Kabusha Komlawuli oluvuliwe lubonisa izinketho ezintathu zokuqondisa kabusha](/img/config/domain-mapping-redirect-options.png)

Kunezindlela ezimbili zokumatanisa i-domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso. Eyokuqala iwukumatanisa igama le-domain kusuka ku-dashboard yokuphatha yenethiwekhi yakho njengomlawuli omkhulu futhi eyesibili iwukusebenzisa i-dashboard yokuphatha ye-subsite ngaphansi kwekhasi le-account.

Kodwa ngaphambi kokuthi uqale ukumatanisa i-domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso kwenye yama-subsite kunethiwekhi yakho, kuzodingeka uqinisekise ukuthi **izilungiselelo ze-DNS** zegama le-domain zimiswe kahle.

###

### Ukuqinisekisa ukuthi izilungiselelo ze-DNS ze-domain zimiswe kahle

Ukuze ukumatanisa kusebenze, kudingeka uqinisekise ukuthi i-domain ohlela ukuyimatanisa ikhomba ekhelini le-IP leNethiwekhi yakho. Qaphela ukuthi udinga ikheli le-IP leNethiwekhi - ikheli le-IP le-domain lapho i-Ultimate Multisite ifakiwe khona - hhayi ikheli le-IP le-domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso ofuna ukuyimatanisa. Ukusesha ikheli le-IP le-domain ethile, siphakamisa ukuya ku-[Site24x7](https://www.site24x7.com/find-ip-address-of-web-site.html), ngokwesibonelo.

Ukuze umatanise i-domain ngendlela efanele, kudingeka ungeze i-**A RECORD** ekumisweni kwakho kwe-**DNS** ekhomba kulelo **kheli le-IP**. Ukuphathwa kwe-DNS kwehluka kakhulu phakathi kwababhali be-domain abahlukene, kodwa kunezifundo eziningi ku-inthanethi ezimboza lokho uma usesha okuthi " _Creating A Record on XXXX_ " lapho u-XXXX kungumbhalisi we-domain yakho (isb.: " _Creating A Record on_ _GoDaddy_ ").

Uma uzithola unenkinga yokwenza lokhu kusebenze, **xhumana nokusekelwa kombhalisi we-domain yakho** futhi bazokwazi ukukusiza ngale ngxenye.

Uma uhlela ukuvumela amaklayenti akho ukuthi amatanise ama-domain awo ngokwawo, kuzodingeka enze umsebenzi kule ngxenye ngokwawo. Wakhombe ohlelweni lokusekelwa lombhalisi wawo uma ezithola engakwazi ukudala i-A Record.

### Ukumatanisa igama le-domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso njengo-Super Admin

Uma ungene ngemvume njengo-super admin kunethiwekhi yakho, ungakwazi ukwengeza nokuphatha kalula amagama ama-domain enziwe ngokwezifiso ngokuya ngaphansi kokuthi **Ultimate Multisite > Ama-domain**.

![Ikhasi lohlu Lwama-domain ku-Ultimate Multisite](/img/admin/domains-list.png)

Ngaphansi kwaleli khasi, ungachofoza inkinobho ethi **Engeza I-domain** phezulu futhi lokhu kuzovula iwindi le-modal lapho ungasetha futhi ugcwalise **igama le-domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso** , **i-subsite** ofisa ukusebenzisa kuyo igama le-domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso, futhi unqume ukuthi ufuna ukuyisetha njengegama **le-domain eyinhloko** noma cha (qaphela ukuthi ungamatanisa **amagama amaningi ama-domain ku-subsite eyodwa**).

![I-modal Yokwengeza I-domain enegama le-domain, isikhethi sesayithi neswishi ye-domain eyinhloko](/img/admin/domain-add-modal.png)

Ngemva kokufaka lonke ulwazi, ungachofoza inkinobho ethi **Engeza I-domain Ekhona** ngezansi.

Lokhu kuzoqala inqubo yokuqinisekisa nokulanda ulwazi lwe-DNS lwe-domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso. Uzobona futhi ilogi ngezansi kwekhasi ukuze ulandele inqubo edlula kuyo. Le nqubo ingase ithathe imizuzu embalwa ukuqedwa.

Ultimate Multisite v2.13.0 iphinde idale irekhodi langaphakathi le-domain ngokuzenzakalelayo lapho kudalwa isayithi entsha ku-host okufanele iphathwe njenge-domain yesayithi ngayinye. Uma i-host iyi-domain eyinhloko yenethiwekhi, noma ingenye yama-domain ayisisekelo efomu le-checkout abiwe amiswe enkambini ye-**Site URL**, irekhodi le-domain exhunyanisiwe elizenzakalelayo liyeqiwa ukuze leyo domain eyisisekelo eyabiwe ihlale itholakala kuwo wonke amasayithi ayisebenzisayo.

Lapho ikhasimende libhalisa i-domain entsha nge-Domain Seller v1.3.0 noma entsha, Ultimate Multisite ngokuzenzakalelayo ixhumanisa i-domain ebhalisiwe nesayithi yenethiwekhi yekhasimende ngokuzenzakalelayo. Abaphathi abasadingi ukwengeza irekhodi elihlukile le-domain exhunyanisiwe ngemva kokubhalisa ngempumelelo ngaphandle uma befuna ukulungisa izinketho ezifana nefulegi le-domain eyinhloko, isimo sokuvula, noma ukuphathwa kwe-SSL.

I-**Isigaba** noma isimo kufanele sishintshe sisuke ku-**Ihlola i-DNS** siye ku-**Silungile** uma konke kumiswe kahle.

<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: Umugqa we-domain obonisa isigaba se-Ihlola i-DNS ohlwini lwama-domain -->

<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: Umugqa we-domain obonisa isigaba se-Silungile nesibonisi sesimo esiluhlaza -->

Uma uchofoza egameni le-domain, uzokwazi ukubona ezinye izinketho ngaphakathi kuyo. Ake sizibheke ngokushesha:

![Ikhasi lemininingwane ye-domain elinokushintsha kwesigaba, isayithi, okusebenzayo, okuyinhloko kanye ne-SSL](/img/admin/domain-edit.png)

**Isigaba:** Lesi yisigaba i-domain ekuso. Lapho uqala ukwengeza i-domain, cishe izoba sesigabeni se-**Ihlola i-DNS**. Inqubo izohlola okufakiwe kwe-DNS futhi iqinisekise ukuthi kulungile. Bese, i-domain izobekwa esigabeni se-**Ihlola i-SSL**. Ultimate Multisite izohlola ukuthi i-domain ine-SSL noma cha bese ihlukanisa i-domain yakho njenge-**Silungile** noma **Silungile (ngaphandle kwe-SSL)**.

**Isayithi:** I-subdomain ehlotshaniswa nale domain. I-domain exhunyanisiwe izobonisa okuqukethwe kwale sayithi ethile.

**Kuyasebenza:** Ungavula noma uvale le nketho ukuze uvule noma uvale i-domain.

**Ingabe iyi-Domain Eyinhloko?:** Amakhasimende akho angaba ne-domain exhunyanisiwe engaphezu kweyodwa yesayithi ngayinye. Sebenzisa le nketho ukukhetha ukuthi lena yini i-domain eyinhloko yesayithi ethile.

**Ingabe Ivikelekile?:** Nakuba Ultimate Multisite ihlola ukuthi i-domain inesitifiketi se-SSL noma cha ngaphambi kokuyinika amandla, ungakhetha mathupha ukulayisha i-domain ngesitifiketi se-SSL noma ngaphandle kwaso. Qaphela ukuthi uma iwebhusayithi ingenayo isitifiketi se-SSL futhi uzama ukuyiphoqa ukuthi ilayishe nge-SSL, ingase ikunikeze amaphutha.

### Ukuxhumanisa igama le-domain elenziwe ngokwezifiso njengomsebenzisi we-Subsite

Abaphathi be-Subsite nabo bangaxhumanisa amagama ama-domain enziwe ngokwezifiso kusuka ku-Dashboard yokuphatha ye-subsite yabo.

Okokuqala, udinga ukuqinisekisa ukuthi unika amandla le nketho ngaphansi kwezilungiselelo ze-**Ukuxhumanisa i-domain**. Bona isithombe-skrini ngezansi.

<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: Izilungiselelo zokuxhumanisa i-domain ezivumela abasebenzisi be-subsite ukuthi baxhumanise ama-domain ngokushintsha kwe-Customer DNS Management -->

Ungasetha noma ulungise le nketho ngaphansi kwezinga le-**Uhlelo** noma izinketho zomkhiqizo ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Imikhiqizo**.

![Ingxenye yama-Custom Domains ekhasini lokuhlela umkhiqizo](/img/config/product-custom-domains.png)

Lapho noma yiziphi zalezo zinketho zinikwe amandla futhi umsebenzisi we-subsite evunyelwe ukuxhumanisa amagama ama-domain enziwe ngokwezifiso, umsebenzisi we-subsite kufanele abone i-metabox ngaphansi kwekhasi le-**Account** ebizwa ngokuthi **Ama-domain**.

<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: I-metabox yama-Domains ekhasini le-Account le-subsite enenkinobho ethi Add Domain -->

Umsebenzisi angachofoza inkinobho ethi **Engeza i-Domain** futhi izovula iwindi le-modal elineziqondiso ezithile.

<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: I-modal ethi Add Domain ebonisa iziqondiso ze-DNS A-record kubasebenzisi be-subsite -->

Umsebenzisi angachofoza ku-**Isinyathelo Esilandelayo** bese eqhubeka nokwengeza igama le-domain elenziwe ngokwezifiso. Bangakhetha futhi ukuthi lokhu kuzoba yi-domain eyinhloko noma cha.

<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: Ifomu le-Add Domain elinenkambu yegama le-domain elenziwe ngokwezifiso kanye nokushintsha kwe-domain eyinhloko -->

<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: Isinyathelo sokuqinisekisa se-Add Domain esiqalisa ukuqinisekiswa kwe-DNS -->

Ukuchofoza ku-**Engeza i-Domain** kuzoqala inqubo yokuqinisekisa nokulanda ulwazi lwe-DNS lwe-domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso.

### Mayelana Nokuvumelanisa i-Domain

Ukuvumelanisa i-Domain kuyinqubo lapho Ultimate Multisite yengeza khona igama le-domain elenziwe ngokwezifiso ku-akhawunti yakho yokusingatha njenge-domain eyengeziwe **ukuze ukuxhumanisa i-domain kusebenze**.

Ukuvumelanisa i-domain kwenzeka ngokuzenzakalelayo uma umhlinzeki wakho wokusingatha enokuhlanganiswa nesici sokuxhumanisa i-domain se-Ultimate Multisite. Njengamanje, laba bahlinzeki bokusingatha bango-_Runcloud, Closte, WP Engine, Gridpane, WPMU Dev, Cloudways,_ kanye no-_Cpanel._

Lapho ukuhlanganiswa komhlinzeki we-host kusebenza, Ultimate Multisite ingaphinde ifake kulayini umsebenzi wokudala i-DNS noma i-subdomain ohlangothini lomhlinzeki wamasayithi asanda kudalwa. Uma kungekho ukuhlanganiswa okulalele lowo msebenzi, umsebenzi wangemuva uyeqiwa ukuze kugwenywe okufakiwe kolayini okungenzi lutho. Ukuhlolwa kwe-DNS ne-SSL kwama-domain axhunyanisiwe kuyaqhubeka nokusebenza ngenqubo evamile yesigaba se-domain.

Uzodinga ukusebenzisa lokhu kuhlanganiswa kuzilungiselelo ze-Ultimate Multisite ngaphansi kwethebhu ye-**Ukuhlanganiswa**.

![Ithebhu ye-Integrations kuzilungiselelo ze-Ultimate Multisite ebonisa abahlinzeki bokusingatha](/img/config/integrations-tab.png)

<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: Izixhumanisi zokumisa umhlinzeki wokusingatha kuthebhu yezilungiselelo ze-Integrations -->

_Qaphela ukuthi uma umhlinzeki wakho wokusingatha engeyena omunye walabo bahlinzeki abashiwo ngenhla,**uzodinga ukuvumelanisa mathupha noma ukwengeza igama le-domain** ku-akhawunti yakho yokusingatha._
