# Ukugeleza Kokubhalisa (v2)

_**INOTHI ELIBALULEKILE: Lesi sihloko sibhekisela ku-Ultimate Multisite inguqulo 2.x.**_

Abasebenzisi bangabhalisa ngezindlela ezahlukene kunethiwekhi yakho. Bangasebenzisa ifomu lakho lokubhalisa noma isixhumanisi esabiwayo esiya ohlelweni olukhethwe kusengaphambili. Lapha sizokubonisa ukuthi amakhasimende akho angabhalisa kanjani kunethiwekhi yakho esebenzisa izindlela ezitholakalayo nokuthi kwenzekani ngemva kokubhalisa kunethiwekhi yakho.

## Ukusebenzisa Ifomu Lokubhalisa: {#using-the-registration-form}

Lena inqubo ejwayelekile yokubhalisa. Udala ikhasi lokubhalisa eline-**ifomu lokukhokha** futhi lokhu kuzoba lapho amakhasimende akho ezoya khona ukuze abhalise kunethiwekhi yakho futhi abhalisele uhlelo. Ungaba namakhasi amaningi okubhalisa, ngalinye linefomu lokubhalisa elihlukile uma ufuna.

Ikhasi elizenzakalelayo lokubhalisa ngu-[_**yourdomain.com/register**_](http://yourdomain.com/register), kodwa ungakushintsha lokhu nganoma yisiphi isikhathi ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Login & Registration > Default Registration Page**.

Ngemva kokuba umsebenzisi efike ekhasini lakho lokubhalisa (ngokuvamile ngokuchofoza inkinobho ethi **Ngena ngemvume** noma **Thenga manje**), bazobona ifomu lakho lokubhalisa lapho.

![Ifomu lokubhalisa eliboniswa ekhasini lokubhalisa](/img/frontend/registration-form.png)

Nasi isibonelo sefomu lokukhokha njengoba livela ku-frontend:

![Ifomu lokubhalisa lokukhokha le-frontend](/img/config/checkout-frontend-registration.png)

Konke okumele bakwenze ukugcwalisa zonke izinkambu eziyimpoqo - i-imeyili, igama lomsebenzisi, iphasiwedi, njll... - bese bakhokhela uhlelo noma baqinisekise ikheli labo le-imeyili uma bebhalisela uhlelo lwamahhala noma uhlelo olukhokhelwayo olunesikhathi sesivivinyo ngaphandle kolwazi lokukhokha.

Ekhasini elithi "Siyabonga", bazobona umlayezo obatshela ukuthi kudingeka yini baqinisekise ikheli labo le-imeyili noma ukuthi iwebhusayithi yabo isivele ivuliwe futhi bangaqala ukuyisebenzisa.

![Ikhasi elithi Siyabonga ngemva kokubhalisa](/img/frontend/registration-thank-you.png)

Uma kudingeka ukuqinisekiswa kwekheli le-imeyili, kuzodingeka baye kubhokisi labo lemiyalezo ye-imeyili bese bechofoza isixhumanisi sokuqinisekisa. Iwebhusayithi yabo ngeke ivulwe uma ikheli labo le-imeyili lingaqinisekiswanga.

Uma bebhalise ohlelweni olukhokhelwayo noma ukuqinisekiswa kwe-imeyili kungaphoqelekile kunethiwekhi yakho, iwebhusayithi yabo izovulwa ngokushesha ngemva kokukhokha futhi bazokhonjiswa isixhumanisi sokungena ngemvume ku-Dashboard yabo.

![Isayithi livuliwe nesixhumanisi sokungena ngemvume ku-Dashboard](/img/frontend/site-activated.png)

## Ukusebenzisa Isixhumanisi Esabiwayo: {#using-a-shareable-link}

Inqubo yokubhalisa usebenzisa isixhumanisi esabiwayo ngokuyisisekelo iyafana nefomu lokubhalisa, umehluko kuphela ukuthi ngokusebenzisa isixhumanisi esabiwayo, amakhasimende akho angaba nomkhiqizo noma isifanekiso sewebhusayithi esikhethwe kusengaphambili efomini lokukhokha (bheka isigaba esithi Ukukhetha imikhiqizo nezifanekiso kusengaphambili ngamapharamitha e-URL) noma mhlawumbe kufakwe ikhodi yekhuphoni (bheka isigaba esithi Ukusebenzisa Amapharamitha e-URL).

Inqubo yokubhalisa izofana: kuzodingeka bagcwalise igama labo, igama lomsebenzisi, ikheli le-imeyili, igama lewebhusayithi nesihloko, njll... kodwa uhlelo noma isifanekiso sesayithi kuzobe sekukhethwe kusengaphambili kubo.

### Ukubhalisa Usebenzisa Izinkokhelo Zangomathupha: {#registering-using-manual-payments}

Uma ungafuni ukusebenzisa i-PayPal, Stripe noma enye indlela yokukhokha enikezwa yi-Ultimate Multisite noma ukuhlanganiswa kwayo kwezengezo, ungasebenzisa izinkokhelo zangomathupha kumakhasimende akho. Ngale ndlela, ungabakhiqizela i-invoyisi ukuze bakhokhe kumcubunguli wakho wokukhokha owuncamelayo ngemva kokubhalisa kunethiwekhi yakho.

Inqubo yokubhalisa izofana ncamashi naleyo engenhla, kodwa ekhasini lokubhalisa amakhasimende akho azobona umlayezo othi azothola i-imeyili enemiyalelo eyengeziwe yokuqedela inkokhelo.

![Umlayezo wenkokhelo yangomathupha ngesikhathi sokubhalisa](/img/frontend/registration-manual-notice.png)

Futhi ngemva kokuba ukubhalisa sekuqediwe, bazobona imiyalelo yokukhokha oyimisile (futhi bayoyithola naku-imeyili yabo).

![Imiyalelo yokukhokha eboniswa ngemva kokubhalisa](/img/frontend/registration-payment-instructions.png)

Imiyalelo yokukhokha ingashintshwa ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Payments** ngemva kokuvula inketho yokukhokha ethi **Mathupha**:

![Ukushintsha inkokhelo yangomathupha nenkambu yemiyalelo yokukhokha](/img/config/manual-gateway-settings.png)

Ngemva kokuba amakhasimende akho eqede inkokhelo yangomathupha futhi ekuthumele ukuqinisekiswa, kudingeka **uqinisekise inkokhelo mathupha** ukuze uvule ubulungu bekhasimende newebhusayithi.

Ukuze wenze lokhu, iya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Payments** bese uthola inkokhelo yekhasimende. Kusafanele ibonise isimo esithi **Kusalindile**.

![Uhlu lwezinkokhelo olunenkokhelo yangomathupha esalindile](/img/admin/payments-list.png)

Chofoza inombolo yenkokhelo futhi uzokwazi ukushintsha isimo sayo sibe **Kuqediwe**.

![Ikhasi lemininingwane yenkokhelo](/img/admin/payment-edit.png)

![Ukushintsha isimo senkokhelo sibe Kuqediwe](/img/admin/payment-status-completed.png)

Ngemva kokushintsha isimo sayo sibe **Kuqediwe** , kufanele ubone umlayezo othi **Vula ubulungu**. Vula le nketho ibe **ivuliwe** ukuze uvule ubulungu newebhusayithi ehlotshaniswa naleli khasimende. Bese, chofoza ukuze **Londoloza Inkokhelo**.

![Ukushintsha kokuvula ubulungu nenkinobho ethi Londoloza Inkokhelo](/img/admin/payment-activate-membership.png)

Ikhasimende lakho manje kufanele likwazi ukufinyelela ku-Dashboard nazo zonke izici elizibhalisele.
