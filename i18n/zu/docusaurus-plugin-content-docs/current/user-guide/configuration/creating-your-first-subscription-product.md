# Ukudala Umkhiqizo wakho Wokuqala Wokubhaliselwe (v2)

_**INOTHI ELIBALULEKILE: Lesi sihloko esabasebenzisi be-Ultimate Multisite version 2.x kuphela. Uma usebenzisa version 1.x,**_ **_**bona lesi sihloko**_**.

Ukuze uqale ukusebenzisa inethiwekhi yakho futhi uqale ukuthengisa amasevisi akho kubasebenzisi abangaba khona, kudingeka ube nezinketho ezahlukene zokubhaliselwe. Uyidala kanjani le mikhiqizo? Yiziphi izinhlobo zemikhiqizo ongazinikeza? Kulesi sihloko, sizohlanganisa konke okudingeka ukwazi ngemikhiqizo.

## Uhlobo Lomkhiqizo {#product-type}

Nge-Ultimate Multisite unganikeza amaklayenti akho izigaba ezimbili zemikhiqizo: **izinhlelo** kanye **nezengezo** **(Order Bump)**. Izengezo zingahlukaniswa zibe izinhlobo ezimbili:**amaphakheji** kanye **namasevisi**. Sizobona umehluko nezici zazo ngokulandelayo.

  * **Izinhlelo** : umkhiqizo oyisisekelo we-Ultimate Multisite. Iklayenti lakho lingaba nobulungu kuphela uma bunamathiselwe ohlelweni. Uhlelo lunikeza amaklayenti akho isayithi elilodwa noma amaningi (kuya ngezilungiselelo zohlelo lwakho) nemikhawulo oyisetha ekhasini lokuhlela umkhiqizo wakho.

  * **Amaphakheji** : izengezo ezithinta ngokuqondile ukusebenza kwezinhlelo ze-Ultimate Multisite. Ziguqula imikhawulo noma zengeze izinsiza ezintsha, ama-plugin noma amatimu ohlelweni lokuqala iklayenti lakho eliluthengile. Isibonelo, uhlelo oluyisisekelo lungavumela ukuvakashelwa okungu-1,000 ngenyanga futhi ungenza kutholakale iphakheji elinweba le nombolo iye ku-10,000.

  * **Amasevisi:** izengezo ezingaguquli ukusebenza kwe-Ultimate Multisite. Yimisebenzi ozoyenzela iklayenti lakho ngaphezu kohlelo eliluthengile. Isibonelo, ikhasimende lakho lingathenga uhlelo oluvumela isayithi elilodwa futhi liphinde likhokhele isevisi eyengeziwe ezokwenza idizayini yaleli sayithi.

## Ukuphatha Imikhiqizo {#managing-products}

Kwabaningi ithebhu ethi **Imikhiqizo** ku-Ultimate Multisite **(Ultimate Multisite > Imikhiqizo)** ingafaniswa nezinhlelo endaweni evamile yokusingatha amasayithi.

Ngaphakathi kwe-Ultimate Multisite ithebhu ethi Imikhiqizo ichaza ukwakheka nemikhawulo esebenza kumkhiqizo noma isevisi ethile. Lokhu kwakheka kufinyelela encazelweni yomkhiqizo noma yesevisi, intengo, izintela, nezimvume.

Lesi sigaba sizokuhola ekuqondeni le nsika ebalulekile ye-Ultimate Multisite.

![Ikhasi lohlu lwemikhiqizo](/img/config/products-list.png)

## Ukwengeza Imikhiqizo {#adding-products}

Kungakhathaliseki ukuthi kuwuhlelo, iphakheji, noma isevisi, indawo yokuqala yokuchaza into entsha ingokusebenzisa **Ultimate Multisite > Imikhiqizo > Engeza Umkhiqizo**.

![Inkinobho ethi Engeza Umkhiqizo](/img/config/product-add-button.png)

Isixhumi esibonakalayo siqukethe izigaba ezimbili ezivelele. Ngakwesobunxele kukhona amathebhu amaningana asiza ekuchazeni umkhiqizo, kanti ngakwesokudla kunezigaba ezimbalwa zokuchaza intengo eyisisekelo yomkhiqizo, isimo sawo sokusebenza, nesithombe somkhiqizo.

![Ukubuka konke kwekhasi lokuhlela umkhiqizo](/img/config/product-edit-full.png)

### Incazelo {#description}

Ulwazi oluyisisekelo lomkhiqizo lungachazwa ngokunikeza igama lomkhiqizo nencazelo. Lezi zikhombi ziboniswa noma kuphi lapho kudingeka khona ulwazi lomkhiqizo, njengokukhethwa kohlelo nentengo, ama-invoice, ukuthuthukiswa, nokunye okufanayo.

![Isigaba sencazelo yomkhiqizo](/img/config/product-description.png)

### Uhlobo Lwentengo {#pricing-type}

Ngakwesokudla kwesixhumi esibonakalayo, intengo eyisisekelo ingachazwa.

![Isigaba sentengo nokulondoloza](/img/config/product-pricing-save.png)

I-Ultimate Multisite isekela izinhlobo ezintathu ezahlukene zentengo. Inketho ethi **okukhokhelwayo** icela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ulwazi mayelana nentengo yomkhiqizo nobuningi bokukhokhiswa.

### Intengo {#pricing}

Ingxenye yentengo ichaza intengo eyisisekelo yomkhiqizo nesikhawu sokukhokhiswa.

![Isigaba sentengo nokulondoloza](/img/config/product-pricing-save.png)

ngakho isibonelo sentengo engu-$29.99 enesilungiselelo senyanga engu-1 sizokhokhisa u-$29.99 inyanga ngayinye. Ngokufanayo, intengo engu-$89.97 enesilungiselelo sezinyanga ezi-3 izokhokhisa lelo nani njalo ngekota.

### Imijikelezo Yokukhokhisa {#billing-cycles}

Isigaba semijikelezo yokukhokhisa sicacisa ubuningi besikhawu sokukhokhisa esishiwo ngenhla futhi ngokuvamile siqondwa ngokwemibandela yezinkontileka noma izikhathi ezimisiwe.

![Isigaba sentengo nokulondoloza](/img/config/product-pricing-save.png)

Isibonelo, intengo yomkhiqizo engu-$29.99 enesikhawu senyanga engu-1 nemijikelezo yokukhokhisa engu-12 izokhokhisa u-$29.99 ngenyanga ngomkhiqizo ezinyangeni eziyi-12 ezilandelayo. Ngamanye amazwi, isilungiselelo esinjalo singasungula isikhathi esinentengo engaguquki engu-$29.99 ngenyanga izinyanga eziyi-12 bese simisa ukukhokhisa.

### Isikhathi Sokuzama {#trial-period}

Ukunika amandla iswishi yokunikeza isikhathi sokuzama kuvumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuthi achaze isikhathi sokuzama somkhiqizo.

![Isigaba sentengo nokulondoloza](/img/config/product-pricing-save.png)

Ngesikhathi sokuzama, amakhasimende akhululekile ukusebenzisa umkhiqizo futhi ngeke akhokhiswe kuze kuphele isikhathi sokuzama.

### Imali Yokusetha {#setup-fee}

Ungase futhi usebenzise imali yokusetha ohlelweni lwakho.

![Isigaba sentengo nokulondoloza](/img/config/product-pricing-save.png)

Lokhu kusho ukuthi iklayenti lakho lizokhokha inani elengeziwe ekukhokhisweni kokuqala (ngaphezu kwentengo yohlelo) elihambisana nemali oyichazile kulesi sigaba.

### Kuyasebenza {#active}

Iswishi yokusebenza ichaza ngokuphumelelayo ukuthi umkhiqizo uyatholakala yini kumakhasimende ukuze abhalise okusha.

![Iswishi yokusebenza](/img/config/product-active.png)

Uma kukhona amakhasimende akhona kulolu hlelo, ukusetha iswishi esimweni sayo sokukhutshazwa kugcina ngokuphumelelayo uhlelo lwamakhasimende akhona kuyilapho kulususa ekubhalisweni kwesikhathi esizayo. **Amakhasimende akhona ohlelweni azoqhubeka nokukhokhiswa** kuze kube yilapho edluliselwa ohlelweni olusha noma esuswa ohlelweni.

### Isithombe Somkhiqizo {#product-image}

Inkinobho ethi **Layisha Isithombe** ivumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuthi asebenzise ilabhulali yemidiya ukuze akhethe noma alayishe isithombe somkhiqizo.

![Isigaba sesithombe somkhiqizo](/img/config/product-image.png)

### Susa {#delete}

Inkinobho ethi **Susa Umkhiqizo** isusa umkhiqizo ohlelweni. Ivela uma umkhiqizo usushicilelwe.

![Isigaba sokususa umkhiqizo](/img/config/product-delete.png)

Ngokungafani nokunye ukususa, umkhiqizo awufakwa kunoma yisiphi isimo sikadoti. Ngakho uma usususiwe isenzo asinakuhlehliswa.

### Izinketho Zomkhiqizo {#product-options}

Uma ulwazi lomkhiqizo olusezingeni eliyisisekelo seluchaziwe, izinketho zomkhiqizo zisiza umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuthi achaze kabanzi izici ezithile zomkhiqizo.

#### Okuvamile {#general}

Ithebhu ethi **Okuvamile** ichaza izici ezivamile zomkhiqizo ezingasebenzi kunoma yimaphi amanye amathebhu akhethekile omkhiqizo.

![Ithebhu ethi Okuvamile](/img/config/product-general-tab.png)

I-**slug yomkhiqizo** ezichazayo ichaza i-slug umkhiqizo okhonjwa ngayo kuma-URL nakwezinye izindawo ze-Ultimate Multisite.

Ultimate Multisite isekela izinhlobo zemikhiqizo eziningana okuyi-Uhlelo, Iphakheji, neSevisi. Amathebhu ethi **Izinketho Zomkhiqizo** alungiswa ngokuzenzakalelayo kuye ngohlobo lomkhiqizo olushiwo.

I-**Indima Yekhasimende** icacisa indima ikhasimende elinikezwa yona lapho isayithi lidalwa. Ngokuvamile kubalawuli abaningi benethiwekhi, lokhu kuzoba okuzenzakalelayo kwe-Ultimate Multisite noma uMlawuli. Indima ezenzakalelayo ye-Ultimate Multisite ingasethwa ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Login & Registration**.

![Izilungiselelo zendima yekhasimende](/img/config/product-customer-role-settings.png)

#### Ukunyuselwa Nokwehliselwa Izinga {#up--downgrades}

Le thebhu icacisa izindlela zokunyuselwa nokwehliselwa izinga ezitholakala kukhasimende ngaphakathi kwezinga lalo elithile.

Ukuze uqonde lo mqondo cabanga ngesibonelo lapho ukufakwa kwe-Ultimate Multisite okuqondiswe emkhakheni othile kunikeza amakhasimende ako izixazululo zokuphatha ukufunda. Ukuze kufezwe lokhu kuchazwa izinhlelo ezintathu (Basic, Plus, ne-Premium) futhi ama-plugin athile avulwa kuhlelo ngalunye (bheka kamuva kulesi sigaba ukuze uthole imiyalelo yokuthi uwavula kanjani ama-plugin).

Uma ukufakwa kwe-Ultimate Multisite futhi kusebenzela amawebhusayithi ebhizinisi noma amawebhusayithi okuhweba nge-inthanethi, lezo zinhlelo zingadinga ama-plugin ahlukile ukuthi afakwe futhi avulwe.

Ngalesi sizathu, bekungeke kufiseleke futhi kungadala izinkinga ukuvumela amakhasimende okufunda nge-inthanethi ukuthi adlulele ezinhlelweni zokuhweba nge-inthanethi njengoba lezi zinhlelo, amanani, nemikhawulo kungase kungafanelani kahle nawo.

Ngakho-ke ukuze kukhawulelwe indlela yekhasimende futhi kuvinjelwe izehlakalo, umlawuli wenethiwekhi angachaza iqembu lohlelo futhi ngaphakathi kwalelo qembu acacise izinhlelo ikhasimende elingadlulela kuzo.

![Ithebhu Yokunyuselwa Nokwehliselwa Izinga](/img/config/product-upgrades.png)

Ukuze uchaze iqembu lohlelo cacisa izinhlelo ezihambisanayo ohlwini lwe-**iqembu lohlelo**. **Ukuhleleka komkhiqizo** kunquma ukuthi izinhlelo zihlelwa futhi ziboniswa kanjani kusukela kwesezansi kakhulu kuya kwesiphezulu kakhulu.

Ultimate Multisite futhi ifaka isici se-**order bump** lapho imikhiqizo nezinsizakalo ezingeziwe ezifanele zingangezwa ezinhlelweni. Lokhu kunikezwa ikhasimende njengezinto ezengeziwe ezingangezwa ezinhlelweni ngesikhathi sokukhokha noma ngesikhathi sokunyuselwa izinga.

#### Ukuhlukahluka Kwamanani {#price-variations}

Ukuhlukahluka kwamanani kuvumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuthi acacise amazinga entengo ahlukile kuye ngobude besikhathi. Lesi silungiselelo senza kube nokwenzeka ukuthi kunikezwe isikhathi sokukhokhiswa sanyanga zonke, sekota, sonyaka, noma esinye isikhathi sokukhokhisa sanoma yiluphi uhlobo kumkhiqizo ofanayo. Isibonelo, ungasetha umkhiqizo ku-$29.99/ngenyanga ngenketho yonyaka enesaphulelo ku-$249.99/ngonyaka.

![Ithebhu Yokuhlukahluka Kwamanani](/img/config/product-price-variations-tab.png)

Ukuze usungule ukuhlukahluka kwamanani, setha iswishi ye-**Vumela Ukuhlukahluka Kwamanani** ukuthi isebenze bese uchofoza inkinobho ethi **Engeza Ukuhlukahluka Kwamanani okusha**.

![Ukuhlukahluka kwamanani omkhiqizo](/img/config/product-price-variations.png)

Ukuze ufake ukuhlukahluka, setha ubude besikhathi, isikhathi, nenani lokuhlukahluka. Ukuhlukahluka okwengeziwe kungafakwa ngokuchofoza inkinobho futhi.

Isibonelo, uma intengo yomkhiqizo wakho oyisisekelo ingu-$29.99 ngenyanga, ungangeza:

  * **Izinyanga ezi-3** ngo-$79.99 (isaphulelo esincane uma kuqhathaniswa nesanyanga zonke)
  * **Unyaka o-1** ngo-$249.99 (isaphulelo esikhulu sokuzibophezela konyaka)

#### Izintela {#taxes}

Ithebhu ethi **Izintela** ihambisana nezilungiselelo zentela ezicaciswe ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Taxes** futhi ikakhulukazi amanani entela achaziwe. Ukuze uvumele izintela futhi uchaze amanani entela asebenzayo sicela ubheke imibhalo ku-**Ultimate Multisite: Settings**

![Ithebhu ethi Izintela](/img/config/product-taxes.png)

Esibonelweni esedlule, sichaze inani lentela lendawo elingu-7.25% elisebenza kumakhasimende ase-California (United States of America).

Uma inani lentela selichaziwe ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Manage Tax Rates** liyakhetheka ezingeni lomkhiqizo.

![Ithebhu ethi Izintela](/img/config/product-taxes.png)

Ukuze ubonise ukuthi umkhiqizo uyinto ekhokhiswa intela, setha iswishi ethi **Iyakhokhiswa Intela** ukuthi isebenze bese ukhetha inani lentela elisebenzayo kumenyu eyehlayo yeSigaba Sentela.

#### Izifanekiso Zamasayithi {#site-templates}

Ngokuyisisekelo, izifanekiso zamasayithi zingamawebhusayithi e-WordPress aphelele akopishwa kusayithi lekhasimende ekuqaleni kokubhalisa kwalo.

![Ithebhu Yezifanekiso Zamasayithi](/img/config/product-site-templates.png)

Umlawuli wenethiwekhi udala futhi alungiselele isayithi lesifanekiso njengendawo evamile ye-WordPress enamatimu, ama-plugin, nokuqukethwe okuvuliwe futhi okulungiselelwe. Isayithi lesifanekiso likopishwa njengoba linjalo kumakhasimende.

Le thebhu ivumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuthi acacise ukuziphatha kwezifanekiso zamasayithi lapho kuba nokubhalisa okusha. Ukuze usebenzise izifanekiso zamasayithi zalolu hlelo, setha iswishi ethi **Vumela Izifanekiso Zamasayithi** ibe sesimweni sayo esisebenzayo.

Uma **Vumela Izifanekiso Zamasayithi** kukhutshaziwe, amakhasimende akulolu hlelo awakwazi ukukhetha izifanekiso noma ngabe ifomu lokukhokha, isixhumanisi esabelanekayo, noma ipharamitha ye-URL kungenjalo bekungenza izifanekiso zitholakale. Ultimate Multisite manje iphoqelela lo mkhawulo ngochungechunge lokubuyela emuva kuwo wonke amaphuzu okungena atholakalayo: izilungiselelo zohlelo zihlolwa kuqala, bese kuba izilungiselelo zezifanekiso zefomu lokukhokha, bese kuba izifanekiso ezikhethwe kusengaphambili noma ezinikezwe nge-URL. Lokhu kugcina imikhawulo yohlelo ingaguquki futhi kuvimbela izifanekiso ukuthi zingaveli emikhiqizweni okungafanele izinikeze.

**Imodi yokukhetha isifanekiso sesayithi** ichaza ukuziphatha kwezifanekiso zamasayithi ngesikhathi senqubo yokubhalisa.

Isilungiselelo **O** **kuzenzakalelayo** silandela izinyathelo efomini lokukhokha. Uma umlawuli wenethiwekhi echaze isinyathelo sokukhetha isifanekiso enqubweni yokukhokha futhi leso sinyathelo sichazwe ngezifanekiso, lesi silungiselelo sizohlonipha imiyalelo esungulwe esinyathelweni sokukhokha.

Ukucacisa **N** **ikeza Isifanekiso Sesayithi** kuphoqa ukukhethwa kwesifanekiso esicacisiwe. Ngenxa yalokho, noma yiziphi izinyathelo zokukhetha isifanekiso enqubweni yokukhokha ziyasuswa.

Okokugcina, **K** **hetha Izifanekiso Zamasayithi Ezitholakalayo** kweqa izifanekiso ezicaciswe esinyathelweni sokukhokha ngezifanekiso ezikhethwe kulesi silungiselelo. Isifanekiso esikhethwe kusengaphambili singachazwa futhi ukusiza ikhasimende ekukhetheni.

Ekugcineni uma umlawuli wenethiwekhi efuna ukukhethwa kwesifanekiso kwenzeke ezinyathelweni zokukhokha, isilungiselelo esithi ' _okuzenzakalelayo_ ' sizokwanela. Kungenjalo, ukususa nokukhiya ukukhethwa kwesifanekiso bese kudluliselwa ukukhetha kuzilungiselelo zohlelo, izinketho ezithi ' _nikeza isifanekiso esisha_ ' noma ' _khetha izifanekiso zamasayithi ezitholakalayo_ ' zingafiseleka.

#### Amasayithi {#sites}

Ithebhu ethi **Amasayithi** iyingxenye yokusebenza kwemikhawulo ye-Ultimate Multisite.

![Ithebhu yamasayithi](/img/config/product-sites.png)

Lesi silungiselelo sicacisa inani eliphezulu lamasayithi ikhasimende elingawakha ngaphansi kobulungu balo.

Ukuze unike amandla umkhawulo, setha iswishi ethi **khawulela amasayithi** esimweni sayo esisebenzayo bese ucacisa inani eliphezulu lamasayithi enkambini ethi **isabelo sesayithi**.

#### Ukuvakashelwa {#visits}

Ithebhu ethi **Ukuvakashelwa** iyingxenye eyengeziwe yohlelo lwemikhawulo ye-Ultimate Multisite. Lesi silungiselelo sivumela ukubalwa nokuncishiswa okulandelayo kwezivakashi ezihlukile esizeni sekhasimende.

![Ithebhu yokuvakashelwa](/img/config/product-visits.png)

Ngokombono wokumaketha wenethiwekhi, abalawuli bangasebenzisa lesi silungiselelo njengendlela yokukhuthaza amakhasimende ukuthi athuthukisele uhlelo lwawo uma imikhawulo isifinyelelwe. Lesi silungiselelo singasiza futhi umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukunciphisa nokuvimbela ithrafikhi eyeqile kumasayithi ukuze kugcinwe izinsiza zesistimu.

Ukuze usebenzise lesi sici, setha iswishi ethi **khawulela ukuvakashelwa okuhlukile** esimweni sayo esisebenzayo bese ucacisa inani eliphezulu lezivakashi ezihlukile enkambini ethi **isabelo sokuvakashelwa okuhlukile**.

Uma lo mkhawulo usufinyelelwe, i-Ultimate Multisite izoyeka ukunikeza isayithi lekhasimende esikhundleni sokubonisa umlayezo okhombisa ukuthi imikhawulo yeqiwe.

#### Abasebenzisi {#users}

Imikhawulo ethi 'Abasebenzisi' ye-Ultimate Multisite ivumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuthi abeke imikhawulo enanini labasebenzisi abangadalwa futhi banikezwe izindima.

![Ithebhu yabasebenzisi](/img/config/product-users.png)

Ukuze unike amandla isici semikhawulo, setha iswishi ethi **khawulela umsebenzisi** esimweni sayo esisebenzayo ngokuyislayida iye kwesokudla.

Okulandelayo, endimeni ngayinye ezokhawulelwa, setha iswishi eseduze nayo esimweni esisebenzayo bese uchaza umkhawulo ophezulu omkhulu enkambini efanele.

#### Izinhlobo Zokuthunyelwe {#post-types}

Ithebhu ethi **Izinhlobo Zokuthunyelwe** ivumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuthi abeke imikhawulo enemininingwane kuqoqo elibanzi lezinhlobo zokuthunyelwe ngaphakathi kwe-WordPress.

![Ithebhu yezinhlobo zokuthunyelwe](/img/config/product-post-types.png)

Ngenxa yokwakheka kwe-WordPress, okuthunyelwe nezinhlobo zokuthunyelwe kuyizingxenye ezibalulekile zokusebenza kwayo okuyisisekelo, ngakho uhlelo lwemikhawulo ye-Ultimate Multisite luklanyelwe ukusiza umlawuli wenethiwekhi ekusunguleni nasekugcineni imikhawulo.

Ukuze unike amandla lolu hlelo olungaphansi lwemikhawulo, setha iswishi ethi **khawulela izinhlobo zokuthunyelwe** esimweni sayo esisebenzayo ngokuyislayida iye kwesokudla.

Okulandelayo, kuhlobo ngalunye lokuthunyelwe oluzokhawulelwa, luvule ngokuyislayida iye kwesokudla bese ucacisa umkhawulo ophezulu omkhulu enkambini efanele.

#### Isikhala Sediski {#disk-space}

Ithebhu ethi **Isikhala Sediski** ivumela abalawuli benethiwekhi ukuthi bakhawulele isikhala esisetshenziswa amakhasimende.

![Ithebhu yesikhala sediski](/img/config/product-disk-space.png)

Ngokuvamile ku-WordPress multisite amafayela ayisisekelo abiwa phakathi kwawo wonke amasayithi futhi kudalwa amadirectory ngamanye amafayela emidiya nokulayishiwe, lapho lezi zilungiselelo nemikhawulo kusebenza khona.

Ukuze unike amandla umkhawulo wokusetshenziswa kwediski, setha iswishi ethi **khawulela usayizi wediski ngesayithi ngalinye** esimweni sayo esisebenzayo ngokuyislayida iye kwesokudla.

Okulandelayo, cacisa umkhawulo ophezulu omkhulu ngamamegabhayithi enkambini ethi **isabelo sesikhala sediski**.

Ngokushintsha le nketho ungavumela izizinda ezenziwe ngokwezifiso kulolu hlelo ngokuqondile.

![Ithebhu yezizinda ezenziwe ngokwezifiso](/img/config/product-custom-domains.png)

#### Amatimu {#themes}

Ithebhu ethi **Amatimu** ngaphakathi kwezinketho zomkhiqizo ivumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuthi enze amatimu atholakale kumakhasimende ukuze awakhethe futhi ngokuzikhethela aphoqe isimo setimu.

![Ithebhu yamatimu](/img/config/product-themes.png)

_**Qaphela: Ukuze amatimu enziwe atholakale kumakhasimende kufanele anikwe amandla kunethiwekhi ngumlawuli wenethiwekhi.**_

![Ikhasi lamatimu enethiwekhi](/img/config/product-themes-network-enabled.png)

Inketho ethi **ukubonakala** ichaza ukuthi le timu iyabonakala yini noma cha ekhasimendeni lapho libuka ithebhu yalo ethi **Appearance > Themes** ngaphakathi kwesayithi lalo. Ukusetha le nketho ku-**Kufihliwe** kususa itimu ekubukweni futhi ngaleyo ndlela kukhawulele ikhono lokuyikhetha nokuyisebenzisa.

![Ithebhu yamatimu](/img/config/product-themes.png)

Ukukhetha **ukuziphatha** kuvumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuthi achaze isimo setimu lapho kudalwa isayithi lekhasimende.

Esimweni esithi **K** **uyatholakala** itimu yenziwa itholakale ekhasimendeni ukuze lizisebenzisele yona. Ngokuphambene, isimo esithi **Akutholakali** sisusa ekhasimendeni ikhono lokusebenzisa itimu. Okokugcina, inketho ethi **Phoqa Ukusebenzisa** iphoqa ukukhethwa nokusetshenziswa kwetimu ngaleyo ndlela iyimise njengokuzenzakalelayo lapho kudalwa isayithi.

#### Ama-plugin {#plugins}

Ngokufanayo nethebhu yama-Themes, i-Ultimate Multisite ivumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuthi achaze ukubonakala kwama-plugin kumakhasimende kanye nesimo sawo lapho kudalwa isayithi elisha.

![Ithebhu yama-plugin](/img/config/product-plugins.png)

Imenyu eyehlayo ethi **ukubonakala** ivumela i-plugin ukuthi ibonakale noma ifihlwe ekhasimendeni lapho ibukwa kusayithi lalo ngenketho yemenyu yama-Plugins.

Umphathi wenethiwekhi angaqhubeka nokulawula ukuziphatha kwama-plugin ngokusebenzisa izinketho eziku-drop-down yokuziphatha.

![Ithebhu yama-plugin](/img/config/product-plugins.png)

Ukukhetha **Default** kuhlonipha isimo se-plugin esichazwe kuthempulethi yesayithi ekhethwe ikhasimende. Ngakho ama-plugin avuliwe ngaphakathi kwethempulethi azohlala evuliwe lapho ithempulethi iklonwa kusayithi lekhasimende.

I-**Force Activate** ibeka i-plugin esimweni esisebenzayo lapho kudalwa isayithi, kanti ngokuphambene i-**Force Inactivate** iyicisha i-plugin lapho kudalwa isayithi. Kuzo zombili lezi zimo, isimo se-plugin singashintshwa ngesandla yikhasimende ngemenyu yalo ye-WordPress Plugins.

Isilungiselelo se-**Force Activate & Lock** sisebenza ngendlela efanayo kodwa sivimbela isimo se-plugin ukuthi sishintshwe yikhasimende. Ngakho isilungiselelo se-Force Activate and Lock sizophoqa i-plugin esimweni sayo esisebenzayo futhi sivimbele ikhasimende ukuthi liyicime. Ngokufanayo, isilungiselelo se-**Force Inactivate & Lock** sizophoqa i-plugin esimweni sayo esingasebenzi futhi sivimbele umsebenzisi ukuthi avule i-plugin.

Umphathi wenethiwekhi angase afise ukucabangela izilungiselelo ze-Force Activate & Lock kanye ne-Force Inactivate & Lock ngokuhambisana namathempulethi esayithi, njengoba ama-plugin nezimo zama-plugin ngaphakathi kwamathempulethi kungase kuthintwe yilezi zilungiselelo uma zikhethiwe.

#### Setha Kabusha Imikhawulo {#reset-limitations}

Ithebhu ye-**Reset Limitations** isetha kabusha yonke imikhawulo yangokwezifiso echazwe kumkhiqizo. Ukuze usethe kabusha imikhawulo chofoza inkinobho ethi **reset limitations**.

![Ithebhu ye-Reset Limitations](/img/config/product-reset-limitations.png)

Ukuze uqinisekise isenzo, slayida i-toggle ethi **confirm reset** iye esimweni sayo esisebenzayo ngakwesokudla bese uchofoza inkinobho ethi **reset limitations**.

![Ithebhu ye-Reset Limitations](/img/config/product-reset-limitations.png)
