# Amafomu Okukhokha

Amafomu Okukhokha ayindlela elula futhi eguquguqukayo yokuzama izindlela ezahlukene lapho uzama ukuguqula amakhasimende amasha.

Ultimate Multisite 2.0 inikeza isihleli seFomu Lokukhokha esikuvumela ukuthi udale amafomu amaningi ngendlela ofuna ngayo, anezinkambu ezahlukene, imikhiqizo enikezwayo, njll.

Ukuze ufinyelele lesi sici, iya kumenyu yamaFomu Okukhokha, kubha eseceleni kwesobunxele.

![Uhlu lwamaFomu Okukhokha](/img/config/checkout-forms-list.png)

Kuleli khasi, ungabona wonke amafomu okukhokha onawo.

Ithebula lohlu lifaka ikholomu ethi **Isimo** ukuze uqinisekise ukuthi ifomu ngalinye liyatholakala yini kumakhasimende njengamanje:

| Isimo | Incazelo |
|---|---|
| **Kuyasebenza** | Ifomu lingasetshenziswa noma kuphi lapho kushicilelwe khona i-shortcode yalo noma ikhasi lokubhalisa. |
| **Akusebenzi** | Ifomu ligciniwe kodwa likhutshaziwe. Amakhasimende awakwazi ukuqedela ukukhokha ngalo kuze kube yilapho ulivumela futhi. |

Sebenzisa ikholomu yesimo ngaphambi kokuhlela ukugeleza kokubhalisa okusesidlangalaleni, ikakhulukazi lapho ugcina amafomu okukhokha angakabi bikho noma awesizini eceleni kwamafomu akho asebenzayo.

Uma ufuna ukudala elisha, vele uchofoze okuthi Engeza Ifomu Lokukhokha phezulu ekhasini.

Ungakhetha okukodwa kulezi zinketho ezintathu njengendawo yakho yokuqala: isinyathelo esisodwa, izinyathelo eziningi noma okungenalutho. Bese, uchofoze ukuze Uye Esihleli.

Lapho ukhetha **isinyathelo esisodwa** noma **izinyathelo eziningi** njengendawo yakho yokuqala, isifanekiso sefomu manje sifaka inkambu ethi **Ukukhethwa Kwesifanekiso** ngokuzenzakalelayo. Le nkambu ivumela amakhasimende akho ukuthi akhethe isifanekiso sesayithi ngesikhathi senqubo yokubhalisa. Ungayishiya lapho ikhona, uyisuse, noma uyibeke kwenye indawo njengayo yonke enye inkambu esihleli.

![Isihleli seFomu Lokukhokha](/img/config/checkout-form-editor.png)

Kungenjalo, ungahlela noma uphinde wenze ikhophi yamafomu osuvele unawo ngokuchofoza izinketho ezingezansi kwegama lawo. Lapho, uzothola futhi izinketho zokukopisha i-shortcode yefomu noma ukususa ifomu.

![Izenzo zefomu lokukhokha lapho uhambisa igundane phezu kwalo](/img/config/checkout-form-hover-actions.png)

#### Ukubuka Konke Kwesihleli seFomu Lokukhokha {#checkout-form-editor-overview}

Isihleli sefomu lokukhokha sinikeza isixhumi esibonakalayo esiphelele sokwakha amafomu akho okubhalisa. Nakhu ukubuka konke kokwakheka kwesihleli:

![Ukubuka konke kwesihleli seFomu Lokukhokha](/img/config/checkout-form-editor-overview.png)

### Ukuhlela Ifomu Lokukhokha {#editing-a-checkout-form}

Ungakha amafomu okukhokha ngezinhloso ezahlukene. Kulesi sibonelo sizosebenza ngefomu lokubhalisa.

Ngemva kokuzulazula uye kusihleli sefomu lokukhokha, nikeza ifomu lakho igama (elizosetshenziselwa inkomba yangaphakathi kuphela) kanye ne-slug (esetshenziswa ukudala ama-shortcode, ngokwesibonelo).

![Isihleli seFomu Lokukhokha](/img/config/checkout-form-editor.png)

Amafomu akhiwa izinyathelo nezinkambu. Ungangeza isinyathelo esisha ngokuchofoza okuthi Engeza Isinyathelo Esisha Sokukhokha.

![Engeza Isinyathelo Esisha Sokukhokha](/img/config/checkout-form-add-step.png)

Kuthebhu yokuqala yewindi le-modal, gcwalisa okuqukethwe kwesinyathelo sefomu lakho. Linikeze i-ID, igama nencazelo. Lezi zinto zisetshenziswa kakhulu ngaphakathi.

![Ithebhu yokuqukethwe kwesinyathelo sefomu lokukhokha](/img/config/checkout-form-step-content.png)

Okulandelayo, setha ukubonakala kwesinyathelo. Ungakhetha phakathi kokuthi "Bonisa njalo", "Bonisa kuphela kubasebenzisi abangene ngemvume" noma "Bonisa kuphela kuzivakashi".

![Ithebhu yokubonakala kwesinyathelo sefomu lokukhokha](/img/config/checkout-form-step-visibility.png)

Ekugcineni, lungiselela isitayela sesinyathelo. Lezi yizinkambu ongazikhetha.

![Ithebhu yesitayela sesinyathelo sefomu lokukhokha](/img/config/checkout-form-step-style.png)

Manje, sekuyisikhathi sokwengeza izinkambu esinyathelweni sethu sokuqala. Vele uchofoze ukuze Engeza Inkambu Entsha bese ukhetha uhlobo lwesigaba osifunayo.

![Isinyathelo sefomu lokukhokha esinezinkambu](/img/config/checkout-form-step-with-fields.png)

Inkambu ngayinye inamapharamitha ahlukene okufanele agcwaliswe. Kulokhu kungena kokuqala, sizokhetha inkambu ethi "Username".

![Imenyu eyehlayo yokukhetha uhlobo lwenkambu](/img/config/checkout-form-field-type-dropdown.png)

![Izilungiselelo zokuqukethwe kwenkambu ye-Username](/img/config/checkout-form-username-content.png)

![Izilungiselelo zokubonakala kwenkambu ye-Username](/img/config/checkout-form-username-visibility.png)

![Izilungiselelo zesitayela senkambu ye-Username](/img/config/checkout-form-username-style.png)

Ungangeza izinyathelo nezinkambu eziningi ngendlela oyidingayo. Ukuze ubonise imikhiqizo yakho ukuze amakhasimende akho akhethe eyodwa, sebenzisa inkambu yeThebula Lamanani. Uma ufuna ukuvumela amaklayenti akho akhethe isifanekiso, engeza inkambu ethi Ukukhethwa Kwesifanekiso. Njalo njalo.

![Ingxoxo yokwengeza Inkambu Entsha](/img/config/checkout-form-add-field-dialog.png)

_**Qaphela:** username, email, password, isihloko sesayithi, i-URL yesayithi, isifinyezo se-oda, ukukhokha, nenkinobho yokuthumela yizinkambu eziphoqelekile ukuze kudalwe ifomu lokukhokha._

Ngesikhathi usebenza ngefomu lakho lokukhokha, ungasebenzisa njalo inkinobho yokubuka kuqala ukuze ubone ukuthi amaklayenti akho azolibona kanjani ifomu. Ungashintsha futhi phakathi kokubuka njengomsebenzisi okhona noma isivakashi

![Inkinobho yokubuka kuqala iFomu Lokukhokha](/img/config/checkout-form-preview-button.png)

![I-modal yokubuka kuqala iFomu Lokukhokha](/img/config/checkout-form-preview-modal.png)

Ekugcineni, kokuthi Izinketho Ezithuthukile ungakwazi ukulungiselela umlayezo wekhasi elithi "Ngiyabonga", wengeze ama-snippet okulandelela ukuguqulwa, wengeze i-CSS yangokwezifiso kufomu lakho lokukhokha noma ulibekele umkhawulo emazweni athile.

![Izinketho Ezithuthukile](/img/config/checkout-form-advanced.png)

Ungakwazi futhi ukuvumela noma ukukhubaza mathupha ifomu lakho lokukhokha ngokushintsha le nketho kukholomu engakwesokudla, noma ususe ifomu unomphela.

![Iswishi yokusebenza](/img/config/checkout-form-active.png)

Ukuze ususe ifomu, chofoza inketho yokususa ezenzweni zefomu:

![Inketho yokususa ifomu lokukhokha](/img/config/checkout-form-delete.png)

Ungakhohlwa ukugcina ifomu lakho lokukhokha!

![Inkinobho yokugcina](/img/config/checkout-form-save.png)

Ukuze uthole i-shortcode yefomu lakho chofoza okuthi Khiqiza I-shortcode bese ukopisha umphumela oboniswe efasiteleni le-modal.

![Inkinobho yokugcina ene-shortcode](/img/config/checkout-form-save.png)

### Inkambu yeThebula Lamanani {#the-pricing-table-field}

Inkambu ethi **Ithebula Lamanani** ibonisa imikhiqizo yakho efomini lokukhokha ukuze amakhasimende akhethe uhlelo. Lapho uhlela le nkambu, ungalungiselela izinketho eziningana:

![Izilungiselelo zenkambu yeThebula Lamanani](/img/config/pricing-table-field-settings.png)

Nansi indlela ithebula lamanani elivela ngayo efomini lokubhalisa le-frontend:

![Ithebula lamanani le-frontend checkout](/img/config/frontend-checkout-pricing-table.png)

  * **Imikhiqizo**: Khetha ukuthi yimiphi imikhiqizo ezoboniswa nokulandelana ezovela ngakho.
  * **Phoqa Izikhathi Ezihlukile**: Uma kunikwe amandla, yonke imikhiqizo iyaboniswa kungakhathaliseki ukuthi inenguqulo yentengo ehambisana nesikhathi sokukhokhisa esikhethiwe manje. Uma kukhutshaziwe (okuzenzakalelayo), imikhiqizo engenanguqulo yesikhathi esikhethiwe iyafihlwa.
    ![Inketho yokuphoqa izikhathi kuthebula lamanani](/img/config/pricing-table-force-durations.png)
  * **Fihla uma Kukhethwe Kusengaphambili**: Ifihla ithebula lamanani lapho uhlelo selukhethiwe kakade nge-URL (isb. `/register/premium`).
  * **Isifanekiso Sethebula Lamanani**: Khetha isifanekiso esibonakalayo sethebula lamanani (Uhlu Olulula, Legacy, njll.).

Uma wengeza umkhiqizo kuThebula Lamanani ngaphambi kokuthi ifomu lifake izinkambu ezidingekayo ukuqedela ukugeleza kwe-checkout kwalowo mkhiqizo, umhleli manje ubonisa isexwayiso. Sebenzisa isexwayiso ukwengeza inkambu edingekayo engekho ngaphambi kokushicilela noma ukulondoloza izinguquko zefomu lokubhalisa elibukhoma.

### Ukwengeza Iswishi Yokukhetha Isikhathi {#adding-a-period-selection-toggle}

Uma ulungiselele [Izinguqulo Zentengo](creating-your-first-subscription-product#price-variations) emikhiqizweni yakho (isb. amanani enyanga nonyaka), ungangeza inkambu **Yokukhetha Isikhathi** efomini lakho le-checkout. Le nkambu ibonisa iswishi evumela amakhasimende ukuthi ashintshe phakathi kwezikhathi zokukhokhisa, futhi ithebula lamanani libuyekezwa ngokuguquguqukayo ngesikhathi sangempela.

#### Isinyathelo 1: Setha Izinguqulo Zentengo Emikhiqizweni Yakho {#step-1-set-up-price-variations-on-your-products}

Ngaphambi kokwengeza inkambu Yokukhetha Isikhathi, qiniseka ukuthi imikhiqizo yakho inezinguqulo zentengo ezilungisiwe. Iya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Imikhiqizo**, hlela umkhiqizo, bese uya kuthebhu ethi **Izinguqulo Zentengo** ukuze wengeze ezinye izikhathi zokukhokhisa (isb. Okwaminyaka yonke ngentengo eyehlisiwe).

![Ithebhu Yezinguqulo Zentengo kumkhiqizo](/img/config/product-price-variations-tab.png)

#### Isinyathelo 2: Engeza Inkambu Yokukhetha Isikhathi Efomini Lakho le-Checkout {#step-2-add-the-period-selection-field-to-your-checkout-form}

1. Iya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Amafomu e-Checkout** bese uhlela ifomu lakho le-checkout.

2. Skrolela phansi esinyathelweni esiqukethe inkambu yakho **Yethebula Lamanani** bese uchofoza **Engeza Inkambu entsha**.

3. Engxoxweni yokukhetha uhlobo lwenkambu, chofoza **Khetha Isikhathi**.

![Ingxoxo ethi Engeza Inkambu Entsha ebonisa Khetha Isikhathi](/img/config/checkout-form-add-field-dialog.png)

4. Lungiselela izinketho zesikhathi. Inketho ngayinye idinga:
   * **Ubude besikhathi**: Inombolo (isb. `1`)
   * **Iyunithi yobude besikhathi**: Uhlobo lwesikhathi (Izinsuku, Amaviki, Izinyanga, noma Iminyaka)
   * **Ilebula**: Umbhalo amakhasimende azowubona (isb. "Nyanga zonke", "Njalo ngonyaka")

5. Chofoza **+ Engeza Inketho** ukuze wengeze ezinye izinketho zesikhathi. Lezi zinketho kufanele zihambisane nezinguqulo zentengo ozilungiselele emikhiqizweni yakho.

![Izilungiselelo zenkambu Yokukhetha Isikhathi](/img/config/period-selection-field-settings.png)

6. Khetha **Isifanekiso Sesikhethi Sesikhathi** (Clean iyona ezenzakalelayo, ekhipha isikhethi esilula esinesitayela esilungele i-CSS yangokwezifiso).

7. Chofoza **Londoloza Inkambu**.

#### Isinyathelo 3: Beka Inkambu Ngaphezulu Kwethebula Lamanani {#step-3-position-the-field-above-the-pricing-table}

Ukuze umsebenzisi abe nolwazi olungcono kakhulu, qiniseka ukuthi inkambu Yokukhetha Isikhathi ivela **ngaphambi** kwenkambu Yethebula Lamanani esinyathelweni sakho se-checkout. Ungahudula izinkambu ukuze uzihlele kabusha kumhleli wefomu le-checkout. Ngale ndlela, amakhasimende aqala ngokukhetha isikhathi sokukhokhisa bese ebona amanani alesosikhathi.

![Umhleli wefomu le-checkout obonisa ukulandelana kwezinkambu](/img/config/checkout-form-editor-with-fields.png)

#### Kusebenza Kanjani ku-Frontend {#how-it-works-on-the-frontend}

Uma sekulungisiwe, amakhasimende avakashela ikhasi lakho lokubhalisa azobona isikhethi sesikhathi ngaphezulu kwethebula lamanani. Uma echofoza isikhathi sokukhokhisa esihlukile:

  * Ithebula lamanani libuyekezwa ngokushesha ukuze libonise amanani esikhathi esikhethiwe (akudingeki ukulayisha kabusha ikhasi).
  * Uma **Phoqa Izikhathi Ezihlukile** kukhutshaziwe enkambini Yethebula Lamanani, imikhiqizo engenanguqulo yentengo yesikhathi esikhethiwe izofihlwa.
  * Uma **Phoqa Izikhathi Ezihlukile** kunikwe amandla, yonke imikhiqizo ihlala ibonakala ngisho noma ingenanguqulo yesikhathi esikhethiwe (izobonisa intengo yayo ezenzakalelayo).

#### Ukukhetha Kusengaphambili Isikhathi Sokukhokhisa nge-URL {#pre-selecting-a-billing-period-via-url}

Ungaphinde ukhethe kusengaphambili umkhiqizo nesikhathi sokukhokhisa nge-URL. Ultimate Multisite isekela lawa maphethini e-URL:

  * `/register/premium` — Ikhetha kusengaphambili umkhiqizo "Premium" kuphela
  * `/register/premium/12` — Ikhetha kusengaphambili umkhiqizo nobude besikhathi bezinyanga eziyi-12
  * `/register/premium/1/year` — Ikhetha kusengaphambili umkhiqizo onobude besikhathi bonyaka o-1

### Inkambu Yokukhetha Isifanekiso {#the-template-selection-field}

Inkambu **Yokukhetha Isifanekiso** ivumela amakhasimende ukuthi akhethe isifanekiso sesayithi ngesikhathi se-checkout. Manje ifakiwe ngokuzenzakalelayo kuzifanekiso zefomu le-checkout **zesinyathelo esisodwa** kanye **nezinyathelo eziningi** ezengezwe ku-Ultimate Multisite v2.6.1.

#### Ukwengeza inkambu mathupha {#adding-the-field-manually}

Uma usebenza ngefomu eladalwa ngaphambi kwe-v2.6.1, noma eliqale kusifanekiso esingenalutho:

1. Iya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Amafomu e-Checkout** bese uhlela ifomu lakho le-checkout.
2. Esinyathelweni lapho kuqoqwa khona imininingwane yesayithi, chofoza **Engeza Inkambu entsha**.
3. Khetha **Ukukhetha Isifanekiso** engxoxweni yohlobo lwenkambu.
4. Lungiselela inkambu:
   - **Ilebula** — Isihloko amakhasimende asibona ngaphezulu kwegridi yezifanekiso (isb. "Khetha isifanekiso sesayithi").
   - **Kuyadingeka** — Ukuthi amakhasimende kufanele yini akhethe isifanekiso ngaphambi kokuqhubeka.

#### Kusebenza kanjani {#how-it-works}

Lapho ikhasimende likhetha isifanekiso ngesikhathi se-checkout, Ultimate Multisite isisebenzisa lapho ihlinzeka ngesayithi labo elisha. Izifanekiso eziboniswayo zivela ohlwini lwakho lwe-**Izifanekiso Zesayithi** (**Ultimate Multisite > Izifanekiso Zesayithi**). Izifanekiso ezimakwe njengezitholakala kumakhasimende kuphela ezivela lapha.

### Izizinda eziyisisekelo zefomu le-checkout {#checkout-form-base-domains}

Ultimate Multisite v2.13.0 iphatha izizinda ezilungiselelwe ezinkambini **ze-URL Yesayithi** zefomu le-checkout njengezizinda eziyisisekelo zenethiwekhi. Sebenzisa izilungiselelo zezizinda ezitholakalayo zenkambu lapho ufuna amakhasimende adale amasayithi ngaphansi kwesizinda sokubhalisa esabiwe esisodwa noma ezingaphezu kwesisodwa, njenge-`example.com` ne-`sites.example.com`.

Ama-domain ayisisekelo e-checkout-form okwabelwana ngawo awathathwa njengokumataniswa kwama-domain wangokwezifiso esayithi ngayinye. Lapho ikhasimende lidala isayithi le-subdirectory kwesinye salezo zisekelo, Ultimate Multisite ayidali irekhodi le-mapped-domain elingenza i-host okwabelwana ngayo ibe ngeyaleyo sayithi eyodwa kuphela. I-host okwabelwana ngayo ihlala itholakala kumasayithi angodadewabo asebenzisa isisekelo se-checkout form esifanayo.

Gcina ama-domain wangokwezifiso ama-host amatanisiwe ekhasimende ngalinye, njenge-`customer-example.com`. Gcina ama-domain ayisisekelo e-checkout-form njengama-host okubhalisa okwabelwana ngawo amasayithi amaningi angawasebenzisa.

#### Ukususa inkambu {#removing-the-field}

Uma unganikezi izifanekiso zesayithi, susa inkambu ethi Template Selection efomini lakho. Amakhasimende azobe esethola noma yisiphi isifanekiso esizenzakalelayo esilungiselelwe ngaphansi kokuthi **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Site Templates**.
