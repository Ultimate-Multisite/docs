# Ukwenza ifomu lakho lokubhalisa ngokwezifiso

Ukuze wenze inethiwekhi yakho ibukeke ihlukile kuwo wonke amanye ama-SaaS akhiwe ku-WordPress platform, Ultimate Multisite ikuvumela ukuthi wenze amakhasi akho okubhalisa nokungena ngemvume ngokwezifiso ngesici sethu se-**Checkout Forms**.

Nakuba kuyindlela elula futhi eguquguqukayo yokuzama izindlela ezahlukene lapho uzama ukuguqula amakhasimende amasha, asetshenziswa kakhulu ukudala amafomu okubhalisa enziwe abe ngawomuntu siqu. Lesi sihloko sihlose ukukubonisa ukuthi ungakwenza kanjani.

## Amakhasi okungena ngemvume nawokubhalisa:

Lapho ufaka i-Ultimate Multisite, idala ngokuzenzakalelayo amakhasi angokwezifiso okungena ngemvume nawokubhalisa esizeni sakho esiyinhloko. Ungashintsha la makhasi azenzakalelayo nganoma yisiphi isikhathi ngokuya ekhasini lakho le-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Login & Registration**.

![Ikhasi lezilungiselelo zokungena ngemvume nokubhalisa](/img/config/settings-general.png)

Nanku umbono ogcwele wekhasi lezilungiselelo zokungena ngemvume nokubhalisa:

![Ikhasi eligcwele lezilungiselelo zokungena ngemvume nokubhalisa](/img/config/settings-login-registration-full.png)

Ake sibheke ngayinye yezinketho ongazenza ngokwezifiso ekhasini le-**Login & Registration**:

  * **Nika amandla ukubhalisa:** Le nketho izonika amandla noma ikhubaze ukubhalisa kunethiwekhi yakho. Uma icishiwe, amakhasimende akho ngeke akwazi ukubhalisa nokubhalisela imikhiqizo yakho.

  * **Nika amandla ukuqinisekiswa kwe-imeyili:** Uma le nketho ivuliwe, amakhasimende abhalisela uhlelo lwamahhala noma uhlelo olukhokhelwayo olunenkathi yesivivinyo azothola i-imeyili yokuqinisekisa futhi kuzodingeka achofoze isixhumanisi sokuqinisekisa ukuze amawebhusayithi awo adalwe.

  * **Ikhasi lokubhalisa elizenzakalelayo:** Leli yikhasi elizenzakalelayo lokubhalisa. Leli khasi kudingeka lishicilelwe kuwebhusayithi yakho futhi libe nefomu lokubhalisa (elaziwa nangokuthi ifomu lokukhokha) - lapho amakhasimende akho ezobhalisela khona imikhiqizo yakho. Ungadala amakhasi amaningi okubhalisa namafomu okukhokha ngangokuthanda kwakho, khumbula nje ukubeka i-shortcode yefomu lokukhokha ekhasini lokubhalisa, kungenjalo ngeke ivele.

  * **Sebenzisa ikhasi lokungena ngemvume elingokwezifiso:** Le nketho ikuvumela ukuthi usebenzise ikhasi lokungena ngemvume elenziwe ngokwezifiso, ngaphandle kwekhasi elizenzakalelayo le-wp-login.php. Uma le nketho ivuliwe, ungakhetha ukuthi yiliphi ikhasi elizosetshenziswa ukungena ngemvume enkethweni ethi **Default login page** (ngqo ngezansi).

  * **Fihla i-url yokungena ngemvume yasekuqaleni (wp-login.php)** : Uma ufuna ukufihla i-URL yokungena ngemvume yasekuqaleni, ungavula le nketho. Lokhu kuwusizo ukuvimbela ukuhlasela kwe-brute-force. Uma le nketho inikwe amandla, Ultimate Multisite izobonisa iphutha le-404 lapho umsebenzisi ezama ukufinyelela isixhumanisi sokuqala se-wp-login.php

  * **Phoqa ukushicilelwa kwesiza okuhambisanayo:** Ngemva kokuba ikhasimende libhalisele umkhiqizo kunethiwekhi, isiza esisha esisalindile kudingeka siguqulwe sibe isiza sangempela senethiwekhi. Inqubo yokushicilela yenzeka nge-Job Queue, ngokungahambisani. Nika amandla le nketho ukuze uphoqe ukushicilelwa ukuthi kwenzeke esicelweni esifanayo nokubhalisa.

Manje, ake sibone ezinye izinketho ezisahlobene nenqubo yokungena ngemvume nokubhalisa. Zingaphansi ngqo kwe-**Other options** ekhasini elifanayo le-Login & registration:

  * **Indima ezenzakalelayo:** Lena indima amakhasimende akho azoba nayo kuwebhusayithi yawo ngemva kwenqubo yokubhalisa.

  * **Nika amandla i-Jumper:** Inika amandla isinqamuleli se-Jumper endaweni yokuphatha. I-Jumper ivumela abaphathi ukuthi bagxumele ngokushesha ezikrinini ze-Ultimate Multisite, ezintweni zenethiwekhi, nakwezinye izindawo ezisekelwayo ngaphandle kokuphequlula yonke imenyu. Yivale uma ukhetha ukufihla leli thuluzi lokuzulazula ngokushesha kusixhumi esibonakalayo sokuphatha.

  * **Engeza abasebenzisi esizeni esiyinhloko futhi:** Ukunika amandla le nketho kuzongeza nomsebenzisi esizeni esiyinhloko senethiwekhi yakho ngemva kwenqubo yokubhalisa. Uma unika amandla le nketho, inketho yokusetha **indima ezenzakalelayo** yalaba basebenzisi kuwebhusayithi yakho nayo izovela ngqo ngezansi.

  * **Nika amandla ama-akhawunti amaningi:** Vumela abasebenzisi babe nama-akhawunti kumasayithi ahlukene enethiwekhi yakho ngekheli le-imeyili elifanayo. Uma le nketho icishiwe, amakhasimende akho ngeke akwazi ukudala i-akhawunti kwamanye amawebhusayithi asebenza kunethiwekhi yakho ngekheli le-imeyili elifanayo.

Futhi yizo zonke izinketho ezihlobene nokungena ngemvume nokubhalisa ongazenza ngokwezifiso! Ungakhohlwa ukulondoloza izilungiselelo zakho ngemva kokuqeda ukuzihlela.

## Ukusebenzisa amafomu okubhalisa amaningi:

Ultimate Multisite 2.0 inikeza umhleli wefomu lokukhokha okuvumela ukuthi udale amafomu amaningi ngangokuthanda kwakho, anezinkambu ezahlukene, imikhiqizo enikezwayo, njll.

Womabili amakhasi okungena ngemvume nokubhalisa afakwe ngama-shortcode: **[wu_login_form]** ekhasini lokungena ngemvume kanye**[wu_checkout]** ekhasini lokubhalisa. Ungaqhubeka wenze ikhasi lokubhalisa ngokwezifiso ngokwakha noma ngokudala amafomu okukhokha.

Ukuze ufinyelele lesi sici, iya kumenyu ye-**Checkout Forms**, ohlangothini lwesobunxele.

![Imenyu ye-Checkout Forms ku-sidebar](/img/config/checkout-forms-list.png)

Kuleli khasi, ungabona wonke amafomu okukhokha onawo.

Uma ufuna ukudala elisha, mane uchofoze ku-**Add Checkout Form** phezulu kwekhasi.

Ungakhetha eyodwa yalezi zinketho ezintathu njengendawo yakho yokuqala: isinyathelo esisodwa, izinyathelo eziningi noma okungenalutho. Bese, uchofoze ku-**Go to the Editor**.

![Engeza i-Checkout Form ngezinketho zesinyathelo esisodwa, izinyathelo eziningi, noma okungenalutho](/img/config/checkout-forms-list.png)

Ngokunye, ungahlela noma uphindaphinde amafomu osuvele unawo ngokuchofoza izinketho ezingezansi kwegama lawo. Lapho, uzothola nezinketho zokukopisha i-shortcode yefomu noma ukususa ifomu.

![Izenzo ze-hover zefomu lokukhokha ezine-edit, duplicate, ne-delete](/img/config/checkout-form-hover-actions.png)

Uma ukhetha isinyathelo esisodwa noma izinyathelo eziningi, ifomu lokukhokha lizobe seligcwaliswe kusengaphambili ngezinyathelo eziyisisekelo ukuze lisebenze. Bese, uma ufuna, ungangeza izinyathelo ezengeziwe kulo.

### Ukuhlela i-Checkout Form:

Njengoba sishilo ngaphambili, ungadala amafomu okukhokha ngezinhloso ezahlukene. Kulesi sibonelo sizosebenza ngefomu lokubhalisa.

Ngemva kokuya kumhleli wefomu lokukhokha, nika ifomu lakho igama (elizosetshenziselwa ukubhekisela kwangaphakathi kuphela) kanye ne-slug (esetshenziselwa ukudala ama-shortcode, ngokwesibonelo).

![Umhleli wefomu le-checkout onezinkambu zegama ne-slug](/img/config/checkout-form-name-slug.png)

Amafomu akhiwa ngezinyathelo nezinkambu. Ungangeza isinyathelo esisha ngokuchofoza ku-**Add New Checkout Step**.

![Inkinobho ethi Add New Checkout Step](/img/config/checkout-form-add-step.png)

Kuthebhu yokuqala yewindi le-modal, gcwalisa okuqukethwe kwesinyathelo sefomu lakho. Linike i-ID, igama nencazelo. Lezi zinto zisetshenziswa kakhulu ngaphakathi.

![Ithebhu yokuqukethwe kwesinyathelo se-checkout ene-ID, igama nencazelo](/img/config/checkout-form-step-content.png)

Okulandelayo, setha ukubonakala kwesinyathelo. Ungakhetha phakathi kuka-**Always show** , **Only show for logged in users** noma **Only show for guests**.

![Izinketho zokubonakala kwesinyathelo se-checkout](/img/config/checkout-form-step-visibility.png)

Ekugcineni, lungiselela isitayela sesinyathelo. Lezi yizinkambu ongazigcwalisa noma ungazigcwalisi.

![Ukulungiselelwa kwesitayela sesinyathelo se-checkout](/img/config/checkout-form-step-style.png)

Manje, sekuyisikhathi sokwengeza izinkambu esinyathelweni sethu sokuqala. Vele uchofoze ku-**Add New Field** bese ukhetha uhlobo lwesigaba olufunayo.

![Inkinobho ethi Add New Field](/img/config/checkout-form-add-field-button.png)![Uhlu olwehlayo lokukhetha uhlobo lwenkambu](/img/config/checkout-form-field-type-dropdown.png)

Inkambu ngayinye inamapharamitha ahlukene okufanele agcwaliswe. Kulokhu kuqala, sizokhetha inkambu ye-**Username**.

![Ukulungiselelwa kwenkambu ye-Username](/img/config/checkout-form-username-content.png)![Amapharamitha enkambu ye-Username](/img/config/checkout-form-username-visibility.png)![Izilungiselelo ezengeziwe zenkambu ye-Username](/img/config/checkout-form-username-style.png)

Ungangeza izinyathelo nezinkambu eziningi njengoba udinga. Ukuze ubonise imikhiqizo yakho ukuze amakhasimende akho akhethe eyodwa, sebenzisa inkambu ye-Pricing Table. Uma ufuna ukuvumela amaklayenti akho akhethe isifanekiso, engeza inkambu ye-Template Selection. Kanjalo njalo.

![Umhleli wefomu le-checkout onenkambu yokukhetha isifanekiso](/img/config/checkout-form-with-template-field.png)

_**Qaphela:** Uma udala umkhiqizo ngemva kokudala ifomu lakho le-checkout, kuzodingeka wengeze umkhiqizo esigabeni se-Pricing table. Uma ungawengezi, umkhiqizo ngeke ubonakale kumakhasimende akho ekhasini lokubhalisa._

_**Qaphela 2:** username, email, password, site title, site URL, order summary, payment, kanye nenkinobho yokuthumela kuyizinkambu eziphoqelekile ukuze udale ifomu le-checkout._

Ngesikhathi usebenza efomini lakho le-checkout, ungasebenzisa njalo inkinobho ye-Preview ukuze ubone ukuthi amaklayenti akho azolibona kanjani ifomu. Ungaphinde ushintshe phakathi kokubuka njengomsebenzisi okhona noma njengesivakashi.

![Inkinobho ye-Preview kumhleli wefomu le-checkout](/img/config/checkout-form-preview-button.png)![Ukubuka kuqala kwefomu le-checkout njengesivakashi noma umsebenzisi okhona](/img/config/checkout-form-preview-modal.png)

Ekugcineni, ku-**Advanced Options** ungalungiselela umlayezo wekhasi le-**Thank You**, wengeze ama-snippet okulandelela ukuguqulwa, wengeze i-CSS yangokwezifiso efomini lakho le-checkout noma ulivimbele emazweni athile.

![Advanced Options enekhasi le-Thank You, ukulandelela ukuguqulwa, ne-CSS yangokwezifiso](/img/config/checkout-form-advanced.png)

Ungaphinde uvule noma uvale ifomu lakho le-checkout ngesandla ngokushintsha le nketho kukholomu engakwesokudla, noma ususe ifomu unomphela.

![Iswishi ye-Active nenketho yokususa yefomu le-checkout](/img/config/checkout-form-active.png)

Ungakhohlwa ukulondoloza ifomu lakho le-checkout!

![Inkinobho ethi Save Checkout Form](/img/config/checkout-form-save.png)

Ukuze uthole i-shortcode yefomu lakho chofoza ku-**Generate Shortcode** bese ukopisha umphumela okhonjiswe ewindi le-modal.

![I-modal ye-Generate Shortcode ene-shortcode okufanele ikopishwe](/img/config/checkout-form-editor.png)

_**Qaphela:** Kuzodingeka wengeze le shortcode ekhasini lakho lokubhalisa ukuze leli fomu le-checkout lengezwe kulo._

## Ukukhetha kusengaphambili imikhiqizo nezifanekiso ngamapharamitha e-URL:

Uma ufuna ukudala amatafula entengo enziwe ngokwezifiso emikhiqizo yakho futhi ukhethe kusengaphambili efomini le-checkout umkhiqizo noma isifanekiso ikhasimende lakho elisikhethayo etafuleni lakho lentengo noma ekhasini lezifanekiso, ungasebenzisa amapharamitha e-URL kulokhu.

### **Okwezinhlelo:**

Iya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Products > Select a plan**. Kufanele ubone inkinobho ethi **Click to copy Shareable Link** phezulu ekhasini. Lesi isixhumanisi ongasisebenzisa ukukhetha kusengaphambili lolu hlelo oluthile efomini lakho le-checkout.

![Ikhasi lomkhiqizo elinenkinobho yesixhumanisi esabelwana ngaso](/img/config/products-list.png)

Qaphela ukuthi lesi sixhumanisi esabelwana ngaso sisebenza kuphela ku-**Plans**. Awukwazi ukusebenzisa izixhumanisi ezabelwana ngazo kumaphakheji noma amasevisi.

### Okwezifanekiso:

Uma ufuna ukukhetha kusengaphambili izifanekiso zesayithi efomini lakho le-checkout, ungasebenzisa ipharamitha: **?template_id=X** ku-URL yekhasi lakho lokubhalisa. U-"X" udinga ukushintshwa ngenombolo ye-**site template ID number**. Ukuze uthole le nombolo, iya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Sites**.

Chofoza ku-**Manage** ngaphansi ngqo kwesifanekiso sesayithi ofuna ukusisebenzisa. Uzobona inombolo ye-SITE ID. Vele usebenzise le nombolo yalesi sifanekiso sesayithi esithile ukuze sikhethwe kusengaphambili efomini lakho le-checkout. Esimweni sethu lapha, ipharamitha ye-URL izoba ngu-**?template_id=2**.

![Uhlu lwamasayithi olubonisa i-ID yesifanekiso sesayithi](/img/config/site-templates-list.png)

Ake sithi iwebhusayithi yenethiwekhi yethu ngu-[**www.mynetwork.com**](http://www.mynetwork.com) futhi ikhasi lethu lokubhalisa elinefomu lethu le-checkout litholakala ekhasini le-**/register**. I-URL yonke enalesi sifanekiso sesayithi esikhethwe kusengaphambili izobukeka kanje [**www.mynetwork.com/register/?template**](http://www.mynetwork.com/register/?template)**_id=2**.

Futhi uma ufuna, ungakhetha kusengaphambili kokubili imikhiqizo nezifanekiso efomini lakho le-checkout. Okudingeka ukwenze ukukopisha isixhumanisi esabelwana ngaso sohlelo bese unamathisela ipharamitha yesifanekiso ekugcineni. Izobukeka kanje [**www.mynetwork.com/register/premium-plan/?template**](http://www.mynetwork.com/register/premium-plan/?template)**_id=2**.
