# Ukuphatha Amakhasimende {#managing-customers}

Ikhasi elithi **Amakhasimende** libonisa uhlu lwamalungu abhaliswe kunethiwekhi yakho. Leli khasi libonisa ulwazi oluyisisekelo lomsebenzisi oluhlanganisa igama, ikheli le-imeyili, ukungena kokugcina, kanye nesixhumanisi esithi **shintshela ku** esikuvumela ukuthi ungene kusayithi engaphansi yomsebenzisi njengomlawuli wesayithi.

![Ikhasi lohlu lwamakhasimende](/img/admin/customers-list.png)

Chofoza **Phatha** ngezansi kwekhadi lekhasimende ukuze ubone ulwazi olunemininingwane ngalelo khasimende.

Ekhasini lemininingwane yekhasimende, ibhulokhi ethi **Ngokubuka nje** ibonisa i-ID yekhasimende, isikhathi sokungena sokugcina, kanye nesamba semali esichithwe yileli khasimende.

Ibhulokhi ethi **Izinketho Zekhasimende** ikuvumela ukuthi usethe leli khasimende njenge-**VIP** (ithegi yokwenza kube lula ukulithola). Ithebhu ethi **Ulwazi Lokukhokhisa** ibonisa ulwazi lokukhokhisa olufana ne-imeyili, ikheli, nezwe.

Ibhulokhi ethi **Izinkambu** ibonisa igama lomsebenzisi lekhasimende, i-imeyili, futhi ikuvumela ukuthi ushintshe isimo se-**Ukuqinisekiswa Kwe-imeyili**.

Ngezansi kwebhulokhi ethi Izinketho Zekhasimende uzothola amabhulokhi amane engeziwe:

- **Ubulungu** — Okubhaliselwe kwamanje ikhasimende elibhalisele kukho
- **Izinkokhelo** — Umlando wezinkokhelo zekhasimende
- **Amasayithi** — Amasayithi aphethwe yileli khasimende
- **Imicimbi** — Imicimbi ehlobene naleli khasimende (ukuqinisekiswa kwe-imeyili, izinguquko zokukhokhisa, njll.)

Chofoza isithonjana somcibisholo (→) eduze kwanoma iyiphi into ukuze uye ekhasini layo lemininingwane. Isibonelo, ukuchofoza umcibisholo eduze kwesayithi kukuyisa ekhasini lokuhlela isayithi lapho ungashintsha khona ukuhlotshaniswa kobulungu, uhlobo lwesayithi, nesimo sokusebenza.

Okufanayo kuyasebenza kubulungu nasezinkokhelweni — ngakunye kuxhumanisa namakhasi emininingwane lapho ungahlela khona isimo sobulungu, ikhasimende elihlotshanisiwe, imikhiqizo, izilungiselelo zesango lokukhokha, futhi uze ucubungule nezimbuyiselo zemali.

Ngakwesokudla sekhasi lekhasimende, uzothola:

- **Ukungena Kokugcina & Ama-IP** — Isikhathi sokungena sokugcina, ikheli le-IP, nezwe
- **Izitembu zesikhathi** — Lapho i-Account yekhasimende yadalwa khona nalapho yagcina ukushintshwa khona
- **Susa** — Inketho yokususa ikhasimende unomphela

:::warning
Ukususa ikhasimende kuhlala unomphela futhi akunakuhlehliswa.
:::
