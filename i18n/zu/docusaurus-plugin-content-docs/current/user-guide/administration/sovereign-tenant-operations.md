# Imisebenzi Yabaqashi Abazimele

Ultimate Multisite: Multi-Tenancy 1.2.0 yengeza amathuluzi okusebenza abaqashi abazimele: amasayithi angaphansi asebenza nesizindalwazi sawo, impande yesistimu yamafayela, nomongo wokurutha kuyilapho esabonakala kumphathi wenethiwekhi.

Sebenzisa leli khasi lapho uphatha amasayithi amakhasimende ahlukanisiwe, ukunikezelwa kwamasayithi akude, noma ukuthutha okudlulisa isayithi elingaphansi elijwayelekile liye kwingqalasizinda ezimele.

## Okushintshayo kubaphathi

- **Ukungena ngokuzenzakalelayo komqashi okungenasimo** — Abaphathi benethiwekhi bangavakashela umqashi ozimele ngaphandle kokuncika esimweni seseshini esabiwe sesikhathi eside. Ithokheni ye-SSO inobubanzi benhloso, iboshelwe kumsuka, ivikelwe ekuphindweni, futhi ikhawulelwe efasiteleni elifushane lokuphelelwa yisikhathi.
- **Ukurutha okuqaphela ukuzimela** — Amanethiwekhi ahlukanisiwe amadala nabaqashi abazimele baxazululwa ngendlela efanayo yomzila wesayithi, kunciphisa umehluko wokuqalisa phakathi kokufakwa okudala nokusha okuhlukanisiwe.
- **Isimo sokuthutha esiqinisekisiwe** — Ukuqinisekiswa kokuthutha kuhlola ukuhlinzekwa kwabasebenzisi, izimvume zombhali wesizindalwazi, isimo sokuphela komugqa, nokungabikho kwamathebula amadala ngaphambi kokuba umqashi athathwe njengoqediwe.
- **Ukudilizwa okuphephile** — Ukudilizwa kokuzimela manje kususa imininingwane yokuqinisekisa yomqashi ngokuhlanzekile ukuze abaqashi abasusiwe bangashiyi ukufinyelela kwesizindalwazi okuphelelwe yisikhathi ngemuva.

## Ukuvakashela umqashi ozimele

1. Vula **Umphathi Wenethiwekhi > Ultimate Multisite > Amasayithi**.
2. Khetha umqashi ozimele.
3. Sebenzisa **Vakashela (SSO)** uma kutholakala esikhundleni sokukopisha amaphasiwedi noma ukudala ama-akhawunti omphathi wesikhashana.

Ukugeleza kokuvakasha kudala ithokheni yokungena yesikhathi esifushane yalowo mqashi futhi kurekhoda umcimbi we-SSO emkhondweni wokucwaninga womqashi. Uma inkinobho yehluleka, hlola ukuthi isizinda somqashi sixazululeka ekufakweni okulindelekile nokuthi umqashi angafinyelela iphoyinti lokugcina le-SSO ohlangothini lwenethiwekhi.

## Uhlu lokuhlola lwemisebenzi yesayithi elikude

Ngaphambi kokushintsha umqashi ozimele noma okude, qinisekisa:

- Isizinda somqashi sikhomba kumsingathi ophethe isistimu yamafayela yomqashi.
- Umsingathi wesizindalwazi somqashi uhambisana nokubophezela komsingathi okulungiselelwe lokho kufakwa.
- Imiyalo yokuqinisekiswa kokuthutha iyaphumelela kumqashi.
- Imigqa yokuthutha engavumelanisiwe iphelile ngaphambi kokwenza izinguquko ze-DNS noma zobunikazi.
- Umsebenzisi womphathi womqashi wahlinzekwa ngesikhathi sokuthutha futhi angangena nge-SSO.

## Ukususa abaqashi abazimele

Ukususa umqashi ozimele kuyonakalisa. Qinisekisa isimo sekhophi eyisipele nokuthekelisa kuqala, bese ususa esikrinini sokuphathwa kwesayithi. Ukugeleza kokudiliza kwe-1.2.0 kususa imininingwane yokuqinisekisa yesizindalwazi somqashi njengengxenye yokuhlanza, kodwa abaphathi kufanele basaqinisekise ukuthi abasebenzisi besizindalwazi bezinga lomsingathi namafolda sekususiwe lapho kusetshenziswa amaphaneli angaphandle okusingatha.

:::warning
Ungamsusi umqashi ozimele ngesikhathi ukuqinisekiswa kokuthutha kusasebenza noma ngesikhathi imisebenzi yokuphusha engavumelanisiwe isemgqeni. Linda ukuqinisekiswa kuqede ukuze ukudiliza kungasusi imininingwane yokuqinisekisa edingwa yimisebenzi esalindile.
:::
