# Imiqondo Eyisisekelo

Kumsebenzisi omusha we-WordPress Multisite nomuntu osanda kuqala ukusebenzisa i-Ultimate Multisite, kungaba namagama nemishwana eminingi emisha okufanele ayiqonde ekuqaleni. Ukuyifunda kuwumsebenzi obalulekile ngoba uzodinga ukuqonda inkundla nokuthi isebenza kanjani iyonke.

Kulesi sihloko, sizozama ukuchaza nokucacisa eminye yemiqondo esemqoka ku-WordPress. Eminye yayo ibaluleke kakhulu kubasebenzisi, eminye kubathuthukisi, kanti eminye kubo bobabili.

## WordPress Multisite

I-WordPress **Multisite** iwuhlobo lokufakwa kwe-WordPress olukuvumela ukuthi udale futhi uphathe inethiwekhi yamawebhusayithi amaningi kusuka ku-dashboard eyodwa ye-WordPress. Ungaphatha yonke into okuhlanganisa inani lamasayithi, izici, themes, nezindima zabasebenzisi. Kungenzeka ukuphatha amakhulu nezinkulungwane zamasayithi.

## Inethiwekhi

Ngokwemigomo ye-WordPress, inethiwekhi ye-multisite yilapho inani lama-**subsites** lingaphathwa khona kusuka ku-dashboard eyodwa. Nakuba ukudala inethiwekhi ye-multisite kwehluka phakathi kwabahlinzeki be-hosting, umphumela wokugcina uvamise ukuba imiyalelo embalwa eyengeziwe kufayela le-**wp-config.php** ukuze kwaziswe i-WordPress ukuthi isebenza kule modi ethile.

Kunezinto eziningi ezihlukile phakathi kwenethiwekhi ye-multisite nokufakwa kwe-WordPress okuzimele esizokuxoxa kafushane.

## Isizindalwazi

Isizindalwazi siyisethi yedatha ehlelekile futhi ehlelwe kahle. Emagameni ekhompyutha, isizindalwazi sibhekisela ku-software esetshenziswa ukugcina nokuhlela idatha. Cabanga ngaso njengekhabethe lamafayela lapho ugcina khona idatha ezigabeni ezahlukene ezibizwa ngokuthi amathebula.

I-WordPress Multisite isebenzisa isizindalwazi esisodwa futhi i-subsite ngayinye ithola amathebula ayo ane-blog id ku-prefix, ngakho uma usufake ukufakwa kwenethiwekhi yesizindalwazi sakho futhi udale i-subsite, kufanele ube nalawa mathebula:

_wp_1_options_ \- ithebula lezinketho le-subsite yokuqala

_wp_2_options_ \- ithebula lezinketho le-subsite yesibili

## Umhlinzeki we-hosting

Umhlinzeki we-hosting yinkampani eyenza amabhizinisi nabantu ngabanye bakwazi ukwenza amawebhusayithi abo atholakale nge-World Wide Web. Izinsizakalo ezinikezwa abahlinzeki be-web hosting zizohluka kodwa ngokuvamile zihlanganisa ukwakhiwa kwewebhusayithi, indawo yokugcina kumphathi, nokuxhumeka ku-inthanethi.

## I-domain

Igama le-domain liyikheli abantu abalisebenzisayo ukuvakashela isayithi lakho. Litshela isiphequluli sewebhu ukuthi sibheke kuphi isayithi lakho. Njengekheli lomgwaqo, i-domain yindlela abantu abavakashela ngayo iwebhusayithi yakho ku-inthanethi. Futhi, kufana nokuba nophawu phambi kwesitolo sakho. Uma ufisa ukuvakashela iwebhusayithi yethu, kuzodingeka uthayiphe ikheli lethu lewebhu ekhelini lesiphequluli sakho elingu-[_www.ultimatemultisite.com_](http://www.ultimatemultisite.com) _,_ lapho [**ultimatemultisite.com**](http://ultimatemultisite.com) kuyigama le-domain.

## I-subdomain

I-subdomain iwuhlobo lohlelo lokuhleleka kwewebhusayithi ngaphansi kwe-domain enkulu, kodwa esikhundleni sokusebenzisa amafolda ukuhlela okuqukethwe kuwebhusayithi, icishe ithole iwebhusayithi yayo. Ivezwa njenge-[**https://site1.domain.com/**](https://site1.domain.com/) lapho _site1_ kuyigama le-subdomain futhi [_domain.com_](http://domain.com) kuyi-domain enkulu.

## I-subdirectory

I-subdirectory iwuhlobo lohlelo lokuhleleka kwewebhusayithi ngaphansi kwe-root domain olusebenzisa amafolda ukuhlela okuqukethwe kuwebhusayithi. I-subdirectory iyafana ne-subfolder futhi amagama angasetshenziswa ngokushintshana. Ivezwa njenge-[**https://domain.com/site1**](https://domain.com/site1) lapho _site1_ kuyigama le-subdirectory futhi [_domain.com_](http://domain.com) kuyi-domain enkulu.

## I-subsite

I-subsite iyisayithi elingaphansi olidalayo kunethiwekhi ye-Multisite. Lingaba yi-**subdomain** noma **subdirectory** kuye ngokuthi ukufakwa kwakho kwe-WordPress Multisite kulungiselelwe kanjani.

## Super Admin

I-WordPress Super Admin iyindima yomsebenzisi enamakhono aphelele okuphatha wonke ama-subsites kunethiwekhi ye-Multisite. Kubasebenzisi be-Multisite, iyona **izinga eliphezulu kakhulu lokufinyelela** ongalinikeza ekufakweni kwakho kwe-WordPress.

## I-plugin

Ngokuvamile, i-plugin iyisethi yekhodi engeza ukusebenza okwengeziwe kusayithi lakho le-WordPress. Lokhu kungaba lula njengokushintsha ilogo yokungena ngemvume noma kube yinkimbinkimbi njengokwengeza ukusebenza kwe-e-commerce. _Woocommerce and Contact Form_ ziyizibonelo ze-plugin.

Ku-WordPress Multisite, ama-plugin angafakwa kuphela kusuka ku-dashboard yomlawuli wenethiwekhi yi-Super Admin. Abalawuli be-subsite bangasebenza kuphela futhi bacime ama-plugin ngaphakathi kwe-subsite yabo.

## Themes

I-theme ye-WordPress iyiqembu lamafayela (_izithombe, ama-style sheets, nekhodi_) elinquma ukubukeka okuphelele kwesayithi. Ihlinzeka ngakho konke ukuhlobisa kwe-front-end njengokuhlelwa kwamafonti, ukuhlelwa kwekhasi, imibala, njll.

Njengama-plugin, themes ku-WordPress Multisite zingafakwa kuphela yi-Super Admin futhi zingasebenza ezingeni le-subsite ngabaphathi be-subsite.

## Isifanekiso Sesayithi

**Isifanekiso Sesayithi** yisayithi le-boilerplate elingasetshenziswa njengesisekelo lapho kudalwa amasayithi amasha kunethiwekhi yakho.

Lokhu kusho ukuthi ungadala isayithi lesisekelo, usebenzise ama-plugin ahlukene, usethe i-theme esebenzayo, futhi uyilungise nganoma iyiphi indlela oyithandayo. Bese, lapho ikhasimende lakho lidala i-akhawunti entsha, esikhundleni sokuthola isayithi le-WordPress elizenzakalelayo elingenakho okuqukethwe okunenjongo ngaphakathi kwalo, lizothola ikhophi yesayithi lakho lesisekelo elinakho konke ukulungiselelwa nokuqukethwe sekusekhona.

## Ukuqondanisa I-domain

**Ukuqondanisa i-domain** nge-WordPress kuyindlela yokuqondisa kabusha abasebenzisi kumphathi ofanele, ngokusebenzisa ikheli lewebhusayithi. Ku-WordPress Multisite, ama-subsites adalwa kusetshenziswa i-subdirectory noma i-subdomain. Okwenziwa ukuqondanisa i-domain ukuthi kuvumela abasebenzisi be-subsite ukuthi basebenzise i-domain yezinga eliphezulu efana ne-[**joesbikeshop.com**](http://joesbikeshop.com) ukuze benze ikheli lesayithi labo libukeke linobungcweti kakhulu.

## SSL

SSL imele **Secure Sockets Layer**. Yisitifiketi sedijithali esiqinisekisa ubunikazi besayithi yewebhu futhi sivumela ukuxhumana okubethelwe. Namuhla isetshenziswa njengobuchwepheshe obujwayelekile bokugcina ukuxhumana kwe-inthanethi kuphephile nokuvikela noma iyiphi idatha ebucayi ethunyelwa phakathi kwezinhlelo ezimbili, ivimbele izigebengu ukuthi zifunde futhi ziguqule noma yiluphi ulwazi oludlulisiwe, kuhlanganise nemininingwane yomuntu siqu engaba khona. Iziphequluli zesimanje zidinga i-SSL, okwenza ibe semqoka lapho kwakhiwa futhi kuqhutshwa iwebhusayithi.

## Imidiya

Imidiya izithombe, umsindo, ividiyo, namanye amafayela akha iwebhusayithi.

Amasayithi enethiwekhi abelana ngedathabheyisi eyodwa ku-WordPress Multisite, agcina izindlela ezihlukene ohlelweni lwamafayela zamafayela emidiya.

Indawo ejwayelekile ye-WordPress (wp-content/uploads) iyasala; nokho, indlela yayo iyashintshwa ukuze ibonise i-ID eyingqayizivele yesayithi yenethiwekhi. Ngenxa yalokho amafayela emidiya esayithi yenethiwekhi avela njenge-wp-contents/uploads/site/[id].

## Permalinks

Ama-Permalinks ama-URL angunaphakade okuthunyelwe kwebhulogi yakho ngakunye noma ikhasi ngaphakathi kwesayithi yakho. Ama-Permalinks abuye abizwe ngokuthi **pretty links**. Ngokuzenzakalelayo, ama-URL e-WordPress asebenzisa ifomethi yeyunithi yombuzo ebukeka kanje:

[**http://www.example.com/registration**](http://www.example.com/registration)

## Ultimate Multisite

Ultimate Multisite iyi-plugin ye-WordPress, eyenzelwe ukufakwa kwe-WordPress Multisite, eguqula ukufakwa kwakho kwe-WordPress kube inethiwekhi yamaphrimiyamu yamasayithi – njenge-[WordPress.com](https://WordPress.com) – evumela amaklayenti ukuthi akhe amasayithi ngezinkokhelo zanyanga zonke, zekota, noma zonyaka (ungakha nezinhlelo zamahhala).

## Ifomu le-Checkout

Ifomu le-Checkout liyifomu le-oda lesinyathelo esisodwa noma lezinyathelo eziningi elibandakanya ukwakhiwa kwe-subsite, ubulungu, nama-akhawunti omsebenzisi ngokubhalisa kwe-Ultimate Multisite. Liqukethe izinkambu ezahlukene namafomu okukhokha umsebenzisi okufanele awathumele ngesikhathi senqubo yokubhalisa.

## Webhook

I-webhook (ebuye ibizwe ngokuthi i-web callback noma i-HTTP push API) iyindlela yokuthi uhlelo lokusebenza lunikeze ezinye izinhlelo zokusebenza ngolwazi lwesikhathi sangempela. I-webhook iletha idatha kwezinye izinhlelo zokusebenza njengoba kwenzeka, okusho ukuthi uthola idatha ngokushesha.

**Ama-webhook e-Ultimate Multisite** avula amathuba angenamkhawulo, evumela abaphathi benethiwekhi ukuthi benze zonke izinhlobo zokuhlanganiswa eziphambene-kodwa-eziwusizo, ikakhulukazi uma esetshenziswa ngokuhlanganyela nezinsizakalo ezifana no-_Zapier and IFTTT_.

## Izehlakalo

Isehlakalo isenzo esenzeka ngenxa yomsebenzisi noma esinye isenzo somthombo, njengokuchofoza igundane. Ultimate Multisite igcina irekhodi lazo zonke izehlakalo namalogi okwenzeka ngaphakathi kwenethiwekhi yakho yonke. Ilandelela imisebenzi ehlukahlukene eyenzeka ku-multisite yakho, njengokushintsha kwezinhlelo.
