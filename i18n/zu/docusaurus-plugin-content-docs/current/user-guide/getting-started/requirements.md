# Izimfuneko ze-Ultimate Multisite

Into yokuqala ongase uzibuze yona ngaphambi kokuthenga i-Ultimate Multisite ukuthi yiziphi izimfuneko zokuqalisa i-plugin yethu. Nokho, ngenhlanhla, kunezimfuneko ezimbalwa kuphela eziyisisekelo zokuqalisa i-Ultimate Multisite: **igama lesizinda** , **umhlinzeki wokusingatha** omuhle kanye nokufakwa kwe-WordPress **Multisite**. Yilokho kuphela okudingayo!

## Igama Lesizinda

Okokuqala, ake sikhulume ngegama lesizinda.

Lapho ukhetha igama lesizinda, khumbula ukuthi iyiphi indawo yakho ethile futhi yini ozoyakha nge-Ultimate Multisite: Ingabe ufuna ukudala i-ejensi? Ukuthengisa amawebhusayithi afana nezitolo? Noma mhlawumbe ukusingathwa kwewebhu kwe-WordPress? Konke kungenzeka nge-Ultimate Multisite.

I-plugin yethu inikeza izinsizakalo zokubhaliselwe, ukuqoqwa kwezinkokhelo, amafomu okukhokha, amavawusha esaphulelo kanye nokuxhumana namakhasimende. Ingxenye enkulu yomsebenzi odingekayo ukufaka, ukumisa nokunakekela i-WordPress Multisite yenziwa ibe lula yi-Ultimate Multisite.

Noma iyiphi indawo oyikhethayo ingaphathwa kusetshenziswa i-plugin yethu inqobo nje uma uthengisa amawebhusayithi njengesevisi! Qiniseka ukuthi ukhetha igama lesizinda elihambisana nohlelo lwakho lwebhizinisi.

## Umhlinzeki Wokusingatha

Manje njengoba usunegama lesizinda elihehayo elimele indawo yakho nebhizinisi lakho, uzodinga ukukhetha inkampani enhle yokusingatha iwebhu ukuze isingathe inethiwekhi yakho nge-Ultimate Multisite. Kukhona izinketho ezinhle emakethe. Abanye abahlinzeki bokusingatha bahlanganiswa kahle ne-Ultimate Multisite, okungaba yisici esinqumayo kuye ngokuthi yini ohlela ukuyakha.

I-Ultimate Multisite kufanele isebenze kunoma yimuphi umhlinzeki wokusingatha. Umehluko kuphela ukuthi ezinye izinkampani zokusingatha ezifana ne-_WPMU DEV Hosting_ , _WP Engine_ , _Closte_ , _Cloudways_ , _Gridpane_ kanye ne-_Runcloud_ zinokuhlanganiswa okuqinile kakhulu kokumataniswa kwezizinda. Kokunye ukusingatha, kungase kudingeke umatanise izizinda ezenziwe ngokwezifiso ngesandla. Kuwumqondo omuhle futhi ukuthola umhlinzeki wokusingatha oseka i-AutoSSL. _Closte_ , _Runcloud_ kanye ne-_cPanel_ bayayisekela.

Mayelana nemininingwane yeseva, ayikho ifomula ethile njengoba kuzoncika ezicini nasemisebenzini ozoyinikeza kanye nosayizi wenethiwekhi yakho. Ukusingatha okwabiwayo kungaba ukuqala okuhle, kodwa lapho nje inethiwekhi yakho iqala ukukhula, cishe uzodinga i-VPS ezinikele.

## WordPress Multisite

Manje, ake sikhulume kancane ngokufakwa kwe-WordPress Multisite.

I-WordPress Multisite ikuvumela ukuba ube nenethiwekhi yamasayithi ekufakweni okukodwa. Lesi isici esakhelwe ngaphakathi, kodwa asisebenzi ngokuzenzakalelayo. Udinga ukunika lesi sici amandla ngaphakathi kokufakwa kwakho kwe-WordPress nephaneli yokulawula yokusingatha iwebhu.

Ukuba ne-WordPress ene-Multisite enikwe amandla **kuyisisekelo se-Ultimate Multisite**. Njengoba ukusetshenziswa okuyinhloko kwe-Ultimate Multisite kuwukusiza abasebenzisi ukuphatha nokuthengisa amawebhusayithi njengesevisi, lokhu kudinga ukwenziwa ku-WordPress ene-Multisite enikwe amandla.

Sine-**athikili ejulile** efundisa isinyathelo ngesinyathelo lokho okudingeka kwenziwe ukuze kunikwe amandla isici se-Multisite ekufakweni kwakho kwe-WordPress.

Ngemva kokuba usunike amandla i-Multisite ku-WordPress, sekuyisikhathi sokufaka i-Ultimate Multisite. Ukuze ufake kahle i-Ultimate Multisite, kufanele ube nalokhu:

  * WordPress: v5.3+ (Kunconyiwe: Inguqulo yokugcina ezinzile)

  * PHP: 7.4.x (ukusekelwa kwe-> 8.0 kuyeza maduze)

  * MySQL: v5+ (Kunconyiwe: 5.6, uma ungenayo i-8.0 etholakalayo kumhlinzeki wakho wokusingatha)

Lezi ukuphela kwezimfuneko zesofthiwe ze-Ultimate Multisite.

Khumbula ukuthi i-Ultimate Multisite ingase isebenze nge-PHP 8.0 kodwa sincoma ukuyiqalisa nge-PHP 7.4.x.

Futhi, sincoma ukuyifaka **ezizindeni eziyinhloko** , hhayi ezizindeni ezingaphansi noma ezinkombandleleni ezingaphansi. Ingase isebenze ezizindeni ezingaphansi/ezinkombandleleni ezingaphansi, kodwa ingase iveze amaphutha athile.

Ukuze uthole umhlahlandlela onemininingwane wokuthi ungayifaka kanjani i-Ultimate Multisite, **chofoza lapha**.
