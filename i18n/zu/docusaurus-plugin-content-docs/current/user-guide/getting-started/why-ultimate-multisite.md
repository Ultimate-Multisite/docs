# Kungani Ultimate Multisite

Uma ufike kulesi sihloko, kungenzeka uzwile ukuthi isandiso sethu singaba yisixazululo sokwenza umqondo wakho webhizinisi ufezeke. Sithemba ukuthi uzoba nezimpendulo ozidingayo ekupheleni kwalesi siqephu.

## Iyini i-Ultimate Multisite? {#what-is-ultimate-multisite}

Ultimate Multisite isandiso se-WordPress Multisite esikuvumela ukuthi unikeze amakhasimende i-WaaS (i-Website as a Service). Ngesandiso sethu, ungaba namazinga ahlukahlukene okubhalisa futhi wenze amakhasimende akukhokhele imali ephindaphindayo ukuze abe nesayithi elisingathwe kunethiwekhi yakho ye-Multisite.

Ultimate Multisite ikuvumela ukuthi wamukele izinkokhelo zakho ngezindlela zokukhokha ezahlukene (njenge-WooCommerce noma i-Stripe), udale izifanekiso zamasayithi ukuze wenze impilo yakho ibe lula kakhulu lapho umsebenzisi omusha ebhalisa kunethiwekhi yakho, uxhume ama-domain, futhi wenze amafomu akho okubhalisa abe ngawomuntu siqu ukuze udale isipiliyoni esenziwe ngokwezifiso kumakhasimende akho.

Futhi lokhu kuseyisiqalo kuphela ngoba ungakwazi ukunweba imisebenzi yayo ngezingezo nama-webhook.

## Ezinye izici {#other-features}

Ultimate Multisite iphinde inikeze:

  * Ukuhlanganiswa kwe-Domain Mapping nabahlinzeki bokusingatha be-Managed WordPress: Ultimate Multisite yengeza ngokuzenzakalelayo ama-domain axhunyiwe kumaphaneli okuphatha e-WP Engine, Cloudways, cPanel, RunCloud, Closte, nokunye lapho i-domain entsha ixhunyiwe, ngakho akudingeki ukwenze mathupha. Kwezinye izimo, sizokuvusela ngisho nokulanda isitifiketi se-SSL.

  * Ama-webhook: Ultimate Multisite inesistimu ye-Webhook enamandla ekuvumela ukuthi uhlanganise inethiwekhi yakho nezinsizakalo eziningi ezahlukene. Sinikeza ngisho nokuhlanganiswa okuqondile ne-Zapier, okuvula inethiwekhi yakho kuzinhlelo zokusebenza nezinsizakalo ezingaphezu kuka-1000 ezitholakala endaweni yabo.

  * Amakhasi Okuphatha Anamandla okusiza amakhasimende akho esebenzisa umakhi wamakhasi owuthandayo: Ultimate Multisite inesengezo esiwusizo esibizwa ngokuthi Admin Pages Creator, esikuvumela ngokuyisisekelo ukuthi wengeze Amakhasi Okuphatha enziwe ngokwezifiso ngokuphelele ku-dashboard yamakhasimende akho usebenzisa izifanekiso ze-Elementor, Beaver Builder, ne-Brizy.

  * Ama-Admin Themes amabili ahlukene ukwenza isipiliyoni se-dashboard samakhasimende akho sibe ngesomuntu siqu: Bheka i-Material WP ne-PRO.

Nokunye okuthuthuke kakhulu:

  * Ukusekelwa kwe-Template Overrides: Nge-Ultimate Multisite, ungashintsha izifanekiso ze-HTML ezizenzakalelayo zokugeleza kokubhalisa ngezinguqulo zakho ngaphandle kokukhathazeka ngokushintsha amafayela ayisisekelo esandiso.

  * Search and Replace API: Ungaphinde uphawule izifanekiso zamasayithi ngezibambi zendawo ezifana ne-{{site_name}} futhi usebenzise i-Search and Replace API ukugcwalisa ngokuzenzakalelayo lezo zibambi zendawo ngolwazi olunikezwe ikhasimende lakho ngokushesha ngemva kokubhalisa.

## Izimo zokusetshenziswa {#use-cases}

Manje sekuyisikhathi sokubona ezinye zezimo zokusetshenziswa ezingaba khona ze-Ultimate Multisite.

### Isimo 1: I-ejensi {#case-1-an-agency}

Ngokuvamile amakhono ayisisekelo e-ejensi asekwakhiweni kwamawebhusayithi, kanti izici ezifana nokusingathwa kwawo noma ukumakethwa kwawo zibalwa njengezinsizakalo ezengeziwe.

Kuma-ejensi, Ultimate Multisite iletha isiphakamiso senani esingakholeki ekukwazini kwayo ukusingatha nokuphatha amawebhusayithi amaningi endaweni eyodwa. Ngisho nangaphezulu, ama-ejensi ajwayelanisa imiklamo yawo namatimu athile afana ne-GeneratePress, Astra, OceanWP, noma amanye angasebenzisa amakhono e-Ultimate Multisite okuvula ngokuzenzakalelayo la matimu kusayithi ngayinye entsha.

Ukulawula okuqinile phezu kwezandiso namatimu kugcinwa ngokusekelwe kumkhiqizo ngamunye ngokusebenzisa izilungiselelo ze-Ultimate Multisite, okuvumela izandiso namatimu ukuthi zenziwe zitholakale noma zifihlwe kanye nesimo sazo sokuvulwa lapho kwakhiwa isayithi entsha.

Ama-ejensi azothola ukuthula kwengqondo nge-Ultimate Multisite ebavumela ukuthi benze abakwenza kangcono kakhulu - baklame amawebhusayithi avelele.

### Isimo 2: Umhlinzeki We-niche {#case-2-niche-provider}

Kukhona isisho esidala esithi, “Yenza into eyodwa futhi uyiphathe kahle”. Kochwepheshe abaningi, lokhu kusho ukudala umkhiqizo noma isevisi ezungeze umqondo owodwa oyisisekelo.

Uma ugxile ekuthuthukiseni inethiwekhi ye-niche yamasayithi, cishe ungafuna ukunikeza izinsizakalo ezisekelwe kuhlaka oluvamile nendawo eyodwa. Yilapho izifanekiso zamasayithi zingasiza khona!

Esinye sezici ezintsha ze-Ultimate Multisite, isayithi yesifanekiso yilapho itimu ifakiwe futhi yavulwa, izandiso ezidingekayo zafakwa futhi zavulwa, futhi amaposi noma amakhasi esampula adalwa. Lapho ikhasimende lidala isayithi entsha esekelwe esifanekisweni, okuqukethwe nezilungiselelo zesifanekiso kukopishwa kusayithi esanda kudalwa.

Kumhlinzeki wamasayithi nezinsizakalo ze-niche, lokhu kunikeza inzuzo engenakuqhathaniswa ekukwazini ukudala ngokushesha isayithi elilungele ukusetshenziswa ngezandiso nomklamo owenziwe ngokwezifiso. Ikhasimende lidinga kuphela ukunikeza okokufaka okuncane ukuze liqedele isevisi.

### Isimo 3: Ukusingathwa Kwewebhu kwe-WordPress {#case-3-wordpress-web-hosting}

Kunezindlela eziningi kakhulu zokusingatha amasayithi e-WordPress kodwa akuvamile ukuthi kube lula njengokunikeza ikhasimende indawo yewebhu enenguqulo ye-WordPress efakwe kusengaphambili. Lokhu kungenxa yokuthi izinqumo nokucatshangelwa okuningi kudinga ukuhlangana ukuze kunikezwe isevisi enencazelo.

Ultimate Multisite iphumelela kakhulu kule ndawo ngokunikeza isixazululo esiphelele esilungele ukusetshenziswa sokusingathwa kwamasayithi e-WordPress. Kufakwe esixazululweni izindlela eziyisisekelo zokunikeza izinsizakalo zokubhalisa, ukuqoqwa kwezinkokhelo, amafomu e-checkout, amavawusha esaphulelo, nokuxhumana namakhasimende.

Umsebenzi omningi obalulekile odingekayo ukufaka, ukumisa, nokugcina i-WordPress Multisite ngendlela efanele wenziwa ube lula yi-Ultimate Multisite kuze kufike ezingeni lapho abaphathi benethiwekhi bedinga kuphela ukucabangela izici njengoba zihlobene nesevisi yabo noma i-niche yabo, njengamazinga emikhiqizo, intengo, nokunikezwa kwezinsizakalo.

Kubathuthukisi abafisa ukuhlanganisa ne-Ultimate Multisite, isixazululo siphinde sinikeze i-RESTful API ephelele nama-Webhooks ezaziso zemicimbi.

Ngaphandle kokuncika ezandisweni namalayisense amaningi angaphandle, Ultimate Multisite inikeza isixazululo esicebile ngezici futhi esiqhathanisekayo nese-Wix, Squarespace, [WordPress.com](https://WordPress.com), nabanye.
