# Ukuthumela Ama-imeyili Nokusebenzisa Ukusakaza (v2)

_**INOTHI ELIBALULEKILE: Le athikili ibhekisela ku-Ultimate Multisite inguqulo 2.x.**_

Ultimate Multisite iza nesici esizokuvumela ukuthi uxhumane namakhasimende akho ngokuthumela i-imeyili kumsebenzisi oqondisiwe noma eqenjini labasebenzisi, kanye nokuthumela izaziso ku-admin dashboard yabo ukuze usakaze izimemezelo

## Engeza izaziso ze-admin ku-dashboard yamakhasimende akho nge-Ukusakaza {#add-admin-notices-to-your-customers-dashboard-with-broadcasts}

Usebenzisa isici sokusakaza se-Ultimate Multisite, ungakwazi ukwengeza **izaziso ze-admin** ku-admin dashboard ye-subsite yomsebenzisi wakho.

Lokhu kusiza kakhulu uma kudingeka wenze isimemezelo njengokulungiswa kohlelo noma ukunikeza imikhiqizo noma izinsizakalo ezintsha kubasebenzisi bakho abakhona. Nansi indlela isaziso se-admin esizobukeka ngayo ku-dashboard yomsebenzisi wakho.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Admin notice broadcast shown on a customer's subsite dashboard -->

Ukuqala isaziso se-admin, iya ku-network admin dashboard yakho futhi ngaphansi kwemenyu ye-**Ultimate Multisite**, uzothola inketho ethi **Ukusakaza**.

![Ikhasi lohlu lokusakaza ku-admin ye-Ultimate Multisite](/img/admin/broadcasts-list.png)

Ungakwazi futhi ukuhlela ukusakaza okukhona:

![Isixhumi esibonakalayo sokuhlela ukusakaza](/img/admin/broadcast-edit.png)

Kuleli khasi, chofoza inkinobho ethi **Engeza Ukusakaza** phezulu.

Lokhu kuzovula iwindi le-modal lokwengeza ukusakaza lapho ungakhetha khona uhlobo lokusakaza ofisa ukukuthumela.

Qhubeka bese ukhetha **Umlayezo** bese uchofoza inkinobho ethi **Isinyathelo Esilandelayo**.

![I-modal yokwengeza ukusakaza enenketho ethi Umlayezo ekhethiwe](/img/admin/broadcast-add-message.png)

Iwindi elilandelayo lizokubuza noma **Ikhasimende eliqondisiwe** noma **Umkhiqizo oqondisiwe**. Qaphela ukuthi ungakhetha abasebenzisi abangaphezu koyedwa noma imikhiqizo engaphezu kowodwa.

Ukuze useshe i-akhawunti yomsebenzisi noma umkhiqizo, kudingeka uqale ukuthayipha igama eliyisihluthulelo ngaphakathi kwenkambu.

Ngaphansi kwenkambu ethi **Uhlobo lomlayezo**, ungakhetha umbala wesaziso. Lokhu kuzogcizelela ukuphuthuma komlayezo wakho.

Ungabe usuchofoza **Isinyathelo Esilandelayo**.

![Amakhasimende aqondisiwe, umkhiqizo oqondisiwe nezinkambu zohlobo lomlayezo zokusakaza Umlayezo](/img/admin/broadcast-message-targets.png)

Iwindi elilandelayo yilapho ungaqala khona ukubhala umlayezo wakho ngokufaka isihloko kanye nokuqukethwe/umlayezo ofisa ukuwusakaza kubasebenzisi.

![Isihloko somlayezo wokusakaza nomhleli wokuqukethwe esinyathelweni sokubhala](/img/admin/broadcast-edit.png)

Ngemva kokudala umlayezo wakho, ungabe usucindezela inkinobho ethi **Thumela**.

Futhi yilokho. Isaziso se-admin kufanele sivele ngokushesha ku-dashboard yomsebenzisi wakho.

## Thumela ama-imeyili kumakhasimende akho {#send-emails-to-your-customers}

Usebenzisa isici sokusakaza se-Ultimate Multisite, ungathumela i-imeyili kubasebenzisi bakho. Unenketho yokuthumela i-imeyili kubasebenzisi abathile kuphela noma ukuqondisa iqembu elithile labasebenzisi ngokusekelwe kumkhiqizo noma ku-plan ababhalise ngaphansi kwayo.

Ukuqala ukusakaza kwe-imeyili, iya ku-network admin dashboard yakho futhi ngaphansi kwemenyu ye-Ultimate Multisite, uzothola inketho yokusakaza.

![Ikhasi lohlu lokusakaza elisetshenziswa njengesiqalo sokusakaza i-imeyili](/img/admin/broadcasts-list.png)

Kuleli khasi, chofoza inkinobho ethi **Engeza ukusakaza** phezulu.

Lokhu kuzovula iwindi le-modal lokwengeza ukusakaza lapho ungakhetha khona uhlobo lokusakaza ofisa ukukuthumela. Qhubeka bese ukhetha **I-imeyili** bese uchofoza inkinobho ethi **Isinyathelo Esilandelayo**.

![I-modal yokwengeza ukusakaza enenketho ethi I-imeyili ekhethiwe](/img/admin/broadcast-add-email.png)

Iwindi elilandelayo lizokubuza noma **Ikhasimende eliqondisiwe** noma **Umkhiqiz** wa oqondisiwe. Qaphela ukuthi ungakhetha abasebenzisi abangaphezu koyedwa noma imikhiqizo engaphezu kowodwa.

Ukuze useshe i-akhawunti yomsebenzisi noma umkhiqizo, kudingeka uqale ukuthayipha igama eliyisihluthulelo ngaphakathi kwenkambu.

Uma izethameli zakho eziqondisiwe sezikhethiwe, ungachofoza **Isinyathelo Esilandelayo**.

![Ukukhethwa kwamakhasimende aqondisiwe nomkhiqizo oqondisiwe kokusakaza I-imeyili](/img/admin/broadcast-email-targets.png)

Iwindi elilandelayo yilapho ungaqala khona ukubhala i-imeyili yakho ngokufaka isihloko kanye nokuqukethwe/umlayezo ofisa ukuwuthumela kubasebenzisi.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Email broadcast subject and content editor on the compose step -->

Ngemva kokudala umlayezo wakho, ungacindezela inkinobho ethi **Thumela**.

Futhi kulula kanjalo ukuthumela i-imeyili kubasebenzisi bakho bokugcina usebenzisa isici sokusakaza.

## Ama-imeyili ohlelo {#system-emails}

Ama-imeyili ohlelo ku-Ultimate Multisite yilawo **maziso azenzakalelayo** athunyelwa uhlelo ngemva kwezenzo ezithile njengokubhalisa, inkokhelo, ukuhlanganisa i-domain, njll. Lawa ma-imeyili angahlelwa noma alungiswe ezilungiselelweni ze-Ultimate Multisite. Iza futhi nesici esizokuvumela ukuthi usethe kabusha futhi ungenise izilungiselelo ezikhona kusuka kokunye ukufakwa kwe-Ultimate Multisite.

### Ukusetha kabusha nokungenisa {#resetting--importing}

Izinhlobo ezintsha ze-Ultimate Multisite, kanye nama-add-on, zingakwazi futhi zizobhalisa ama-imeyili amasha ngezikhathi ezithile.

Ukuvimbela ukungqubuzana nezinye izinkinga, **ngeke sengeze ama-template amasha e-imeyili njengama-imeyili ohlelo ekufakweni kwakho ngokuzenzakalelayo** , ngaphandle uma ebalulekile ekusebenzeni okulungile kwesici esithile.

Nokho, ama-super admin nama-agent angangenisa lawa ma-imeyili asanda kubhaliswa ngethuluzi lokungenisa. Leyo nqubo izodala i-imeyili entsha yohlelo enokuqukethwe nokumiswa kwe-template entsha ye-imeyili, ivumele i-super admin ukuthi yenze noma yiziphi izinguquko ezifisayo noma iwagcine njengoba enjalo.

#### Indlela yokungenisa ama-imeyili ohlelo {#how-to-import-system-emails}

Iya ekhasini lakho le-Ultimate Multisite Settings bese uya kuthebhu ethi **Ama-imeyili**.

![Ithebhu yama-imeyili kuzilungiselelo ze-Ultimate Multisite ekhombisa isigaba sama-imeyili ohlelo](/img/config/settings-emails-tab.png)

Bese, ku-sidebar, chofoza inkinobho ethi **Yenza Ngokwezifiso Ama-imeyili Ohlelo**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Customize System Emails button on the System Emails sidebar panel -->

Ekhasini lama-imeyili ohlelo, uzobona inkinobho yesenzo ethi **Setha Kabusha & Ngenisa** phezulu. Ukuchofoza leyo nkinobho kufanele kuvule iwindi le-modal lokungenisa nokusetha kabusha.

![Inkinobho yesenzo Sokusetha Kabusha noma Ukungenisa ekhasini le-admin lama-imeyili ohlelo](/img/admin/system-emails-reset-import.png)

Bese, ungaguqula izinketho zokungenisa ama-imeyili ukuze ubone ukuthi yimaphi ama-imeyili ohlelo atholakalayo ukuze angeniswe.

<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: Imodali Yokubuyisela Nokungenisa enezinketho Zokungenisa Ama-email ezinwetshiwe -->

#### Ukubuyisela Ama-email Esistimu {#reseting-system-emails}

Ngezinye izikhathi, uzobona ukuthi izinguquko ozenzile kusifanekiso se-email esithile azisakusebenzeli futhi ungathanda ukusibuyisela **esimweni saso sokuzenzakalelayo**.

Ezimweni ezinjalo, unezinketho ezimbili: ungamane ususe i-email yesistimu bese uyingenisa futhi (usebenzisa imiyalelo engenhla) - okuzosula amamethrikhi okuthumela nezinye izinto, okwenza le ndlela inganconywa kakhulu.

Noma ungasebenzisa **ithuluzi Lokubuyisela & Nokungenisa** ukuze ubuyisele leso sifanekiso se-email.

Ukuze ubuyisele isifanekiso se-email, ungalandela izinyathelo ezingenhla uze ufinyelele ethuluzini Lokubuyisela & Nokungenisa, bese uvula inketho ethi **Buyisela** futhi ukhethe ama-email ofuna ukuwabuyisela kokuqukethwe kwawo okuzenzakalelayo.
