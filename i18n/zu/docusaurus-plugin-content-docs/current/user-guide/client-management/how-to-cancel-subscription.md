# Ngingakukhansela kanjani ukubhalisa kwami?

Uma unelayisensi ye-Ultimate Multisite, ungakhansela ukuvuselelwa kwayo nganoma yisiphi isikhathi osifunayo. Landela nje izinyathelo ezingezansi:


<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: i-imeyili yekhasi le-Account enesixhumanisi sokufinyelela -->

Sebenzisa i-imeyili oyinikezile ngesikhathi sokuthenga kanye nephasiwedi yakho.

<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: ikhasi lokungena elinezinkambu ze-imeyili nephasiwedi -->

Lesi isikrini sokuqala sekhasi lakho le-Account:

<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: isikrini sokuqala sekhasi le-Account esibonisa imininingwane yokubhalisa -->

Ukuze ukhansela ukubhalisa kwakho, kumenyu engakwesobunxele, iya ku-_Renewing & Billing._

<!-- Isithombe-skrini asitholakali: ikhasi le-Renewing & Billing elinenketho yokukhansela -->

Chofoza umcibisholo ongakwesokudla ukuze uvule iwindi eliseceleni. Bese, kufanele ukhethe inketho ezokhansela i-**auto-renew**.

Uhlelo luzokubonisa umlayezo wokuqinisekisa.

Uma uqinisekile ukuthi ufuna ukukhansela ukubhalisa kwakho, chofoza nje inkinobho ethi _**Cancel Renewals**_. Ngemva kwalesi senzo, uzocelwa ukuthi uphendule inhlolovo emfushane.

Sekuqediwe! Ukubhalisa kwakho ngeke kuvuselelwe ngokuzenzakalelayo.

**Uzoba nokhiye osebenzayo kuze kube yilapho ukubhalisa kwakho kuphelelwa yisikhathi**. Uma ufuna ukuvuselela ukubhalisa kwakho, kuzodingeka ukwenze mathupha.
