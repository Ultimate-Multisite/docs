# Ukuthuthukisa Uhlelo (v2)

_**INOTHI ELIBALULEKILE: Lesi sihloko sibhekisela ku-Ultimate Multisite version 2.x.**_

Amakhasimende akho angakwazi ukuthuthukisa izinhlelo zawo nganoma yisiphi isikhathi. Angathuthukisela kolunye uhlelo noma athenge noma yiziphi izinsizakalo noma amaphakheji angeziwe owanikezayo kunethiwekhi yakho.

Kulesi sifundo sizochaza ukuthi angaluthuthukisa kanjani uhlelo lwawo nokuthi kwenzekani ngemva kwenqubo yokuthuthukisa.

Ukuze athuthukise uhlelo lwawo, amakhasimende akho kufanele afinyelele ku-Dashboard yawo bese eya ekhasini le-**Account**.

![I-Dashboard yesizindana sekhasimende enesixhumanisi semenyu ye-Account esibonakalayo](/img/account-page/account-menu.png)

Ekhasini le-Account, azoboniswa ubulungu bawo bamanje kanye nohlelo oluhambisana nabo. Ukuze athuthukisele kolunye uhlelo, kufanele achofoze ku-**Change** ekhoneni eliphezulu kwesokudla lesigaba esithi **Your Membership**.

![Ikhadi elithi Your Membership ekhasini le-Account elinenkinobho ethi Change](/img/account-page/membership-change-button.png)

Azodluliselwa efomini lokukhokha lapho zonke izinhlelo ezitholakalayo zizovezwa khona.

Azokwazi futhi ukubona **izinsizakalo namaphakheji atholakalayo ohlelweni lwawo lwamanje** , uma nje efuna ukuthenga insizakalo noma iphakheji ethile (njengokuvakasha okungenamkhawulo noma indawo yediski esibonelweni sethu lapha), hhayi ukuthuthukisa uhlelo.

![Isikhethi sokuthuthukisa esibonisa izinhlelo namaphakheji atholakalayo ohlangothini lwekhasimende](/img/account-page/upgrade-picker.png)

Ngemva kokukhetha umkhiqizo afuna ukuwuthenga, azobona ukuthi kudingeka akhokhe malini manje - kungafaki noma yisiphi isikweletu esivele sikhona - nokuthi azokhokhiswa malini ngosuku olulandelayo lokukhokhiswa.

Ngokuvamile, uma umkhiqizo ungolunye uhlelo futhi inkokhelo izokwenziwa phakathi kokukhokhiswa kobulungu, azokwamukela isikweletu senani elikhokhwe ohlelweni lokuqala.

![Isifinyezo senkokhelo yokuthuthukisa esibonisa isikweletu esisetshenzisiwe nenani elilandelayo lokukhokhiswa](/img/account-page/upgrade-summary.png)

Uma ekhetha uhlelo noma iphakheji engeke ishintshe lutho kokubhaliselwe kwamanje, azobona umlayezo ochaza lokho.

![Isaziso lapho uhlelo olukhethiwe lungakushintshi okubhaliselwe](/img/account-page/upgrade-no-change.png)

Ngemva kokuba ukukhokha sekuqediwe, umkhiqizo/imikhiqizo emisha izongezwa ku-akhawunti yamakhasimende akho futhi yonke imikhawulo noma izici zomkhiqizo/zemikhiqizo emisha zizongezwa kuyo ngokushesha: ukuvakasha, indawo yediski, okuthunyelwe, njll...

## Izindlela Zokuthuthukisa Nokwehlisa

Kumkhiqizo ngamunye wakho, uzoba nethebhu ethi **Up & Downgrades**. Inketho yokuqala kuleyo thebhu iyinkambu ebizwa ngokuthi **Plan Group**.

**Amaqembu ezinhlelo** yiwo avumela ukuthi wazise i-Ultimate Multisite ukuthi izinhlelo ezithile zingezomndeni ofanayo, ngakho kufanele zisetshenziselwe ukwakha izinketho zezindlela zokuthuthukisa/ukwehlisa.

![Ithebhu ye-Up and Downgrades yokuhlela umkhiqizo enenkambu ye-Plan Group](/img/config/product-upgrades-plan-group.png)

Isibonelo, une-**uhlelo lwamahhala** , **Uhlelo Oluyisisekelo** kanye **noHlelo Lwe-Premium** okutholakalayo. Ufuna abasebenzisi ababhalise ngaphansi kwe-**Free Plan** bakwazi ukuthuthukisela kuphela ku-**Premium Plan** futhi awufuni babone i-"Basic Plan" njengenketho yokuthuthukisa. Okudingeka ukwenze nje ukwabela igama elifanayo leqembu lohlelo kuzo zombili izinhlelo ze-Free ne-Premium njengoba kubonisiwe ezithombeni-skrini ezingezansi.

![Ikhasi lomkhiqizo le-Free Plan elinikezwe iqembu lohlelo le-High End](/img/config/product-upgrades-free.png)

![Ikhasi lomkhiqizo le-Premium Plan elinikezwe iqembu lohlelo le-High End](/img/config/product-upgrades-premium.png)

Lokhu okufanele kwenziwe ukutshela i-Ultimate Multisite ukuthi kuno "mndeni" wezinhlelo kunethiwekhi obizwa ngokuthi **High End**. Uma kunikezwa ukuthuthukisa noma ukwehlisa, izinhlelo ezivela emndenini ofanayo kuphela ezizokwethulwa njengenketho yomsebenzisi.
