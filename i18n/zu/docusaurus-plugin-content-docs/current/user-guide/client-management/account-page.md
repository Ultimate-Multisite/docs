# Ikhasi le-Account Leklayenti Lakho (v2)

_**INOTHI ELIBALULEKILE: Lesi sihloko sibhekisela ku-Ultimate Multisite version 2.x.**_

Lapho amakhasimende ebhalisela iplani kunethiwekhi yakho, athola ukufinyelela kuwebhusayithi kanye ne-dashboard yayo enolwazi olubalulekile mayelana nezinkokhelo zawo, ubulungu, izizinda, imikhawulo yeplani, njll...

Kulesi sifundo, sizokuqondisa ekhasini le-account lekhasimende futhi uzobona lokho amakhasimende akho angakubona futhi akwenze ngaphakathi kwalo.

## Ikhasi le-Account

Ikhasi le-account lifinyeleleka ngokuchofoza ku-**Account** ngaphakathi kwe-dashboard yekhasimende lakho.

![Imenyu ye-Account ku-dashboard yekhasimende](/img/account-page/account-menu.png)

Kumanethiwekhi ama-tenant azimele, i-Ultimate Multisite v2.13.0 igcina lokhu okuhlangenwe nakho kokuphatha amakhasimende kusayithi eliyinhloko. Uma ikhasimende livula izenzo ze-account, checkout, billing, invoice, ukuphathwa kwesayithi, ukushintsha ithempulethi, noma ukuxhumanisa isizinda lisuka ku-tenant ezimele, isenzo sikhomba emuva kuphaneli yekhasimende lesayithi eliyinhloko ukuze amarekhodi okukhokhisa kwenethiwekhi nobulungu ahlale egunyaziwe.

Lapho ikhasimende lifika lisuka ku-tenant ezimele, iphaneli yekhasimende lesayithi eliyinhloko ingafaka isixhumanisi sokubuyela emuva kusayithi le-tenant. Isixhumanisi sokubuyela emuva siboniswa kuphela lapho i-Ultimate Multisite ingaqinisekisa indawo yokubuyela njengelinye lamasayithi ekhasimende, okuvimbela ukuqondiswa kabusha okungahleliwe kuyilapho kugcinwa ukugeleza komsebenzi kwe-tenant.

![Ukubuka konke kwekhasi le-account lekhasimende](/img/account-page/overview.png)

Ngemva kokuba ikhasimende lichofoze kukho, lizobona ukubuka konke kobulungu balo, ikheli lokukhokhisa, ama-invoyisi, izizinda, imikhawulo yesayithi, futhi lizokwazi futhi ukushintsha i-****Thempulethi Yesayithi** (uma kuvunyelwe kunethiwekhi yakho)**.

Bangakwazi futhi ukushintsha ubulungu bube kwenye iplani, noma bathenge elinye iphakheji noma isevisi oyinikezayo. Ake sibheke isigaba ngasinye ngokwehlukana.

### Ukubuka Konke Kobulungu Bakho:

Ibhulokhi yokuqala ngaphansi nje kwegama lewebhusayithi yamakhasimende akho ibonisa ukubuka konke kweplani yawo yamanje kanye nezinsizakalo/amaphakheji athengwe nayo. Ibhulokhi iphinde ibonise inombolo yobulungu, inani lokuqala elikhokhelwe yona, ukuthi iplani nanoma iyiphi isevisi/iphakheji kubiza malini nokuthi bakhokhiswe kangaki ngalobu bulungu. Bangabona futhi ukuthi ubulungu **Buyasebenza** , **Buphelelwe Isikhathi** noma **Bukhanseliwe**.

![Ukubuka konke kobulungu okubonisa iplani, inani, nemininingwane yokukhokhisa](/img/account-page/membership-card.png)

Ngaphansi nje kwale bhulokhi, amakhasimende akho angabona amabhulokhi okuthi **Mayelana Nalesi Sayithi** kanye nokuthi **Imikhawulo Yesayithi**. Lawa mabhulokhi awabonisa yonke imikhawulo ehambisana neplani yawo: indawo yediski, okuthunyelwe, amakhasi, ukuvakasha, njll... Le mikhawulo ingalungiselelwa ekhasini leplani ngayinye ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Imikhiqizo**.

![Amabhulokhi okuthi Mayelana Nalesi Sayithi kanye Nemikhawulo Yesayithi abonisa imikhawulo yeplani](/img/account-page/site-limits.png)

Ngakwesokudla kwe-**Ubulungu Bakho** , amakhasimende angachofoza ku-**Shintsha**. Lokhu kuzowabonisa wonke amapulani atholakalayo namaphakheji/izinsizakalo. Uma ekhetha enye iplani, imikhawulo yaleyo plani izosebenza esikhundleni semikhawulo yamanje yobulungu - akunandaba ukuthi ayehlisa noma ayakhuphula.

Manje, uma amakhasimende akho ekhetha ukuthenga amaphakheji noma izinsizakalo zalobu bulungu bamanje - njengendawo yediski eyengeziwe noma ukuvakasha - ubulungu bamanje ngeke bushintshwe kodwa kuzongezwa kuphela amaphakheji amasha kubo.

Qaphela ukuthi amakhodi amakhuphoni awakwazi ukungezwa kuleli khasi lokushintsha ubulungu. Uma ikhasimende lisebenzise ikhodi yekhuphoni ekuthengeni kokuqala kobulungu, ikhodi izosebenza nakulobu bulungu obusha.

### Ukubuyekeza Ikheli Lokukhokhisa:

Ekhasini le-account, amakhasimende akho angabuyekeza futhi ikheli lawo lokukhokhisa. Adinga nje ukuchofoza ku-**Buyekeza** eduze kwe-_Ikheli Lokukhokhisa_.

![Isigaba Sekheli Lokukhokhisa esinenkinobho ethi Buyekeza](/img/account-page/billing-address.png)

Iwindi elisha lizovela ekhasimendeni lakho. Konke okudingeka likwenze ukugcwalisa ikheli elisha bese lichofoza ku-_Londoloza Izinguquko_.

![Ifomu lokubuyekeza ikheli lokukhokhisa](/img/account-page/billing-address-form.png)

### Ukushintsha Ithempulethi Yesayithi:

Ukuze uvumele amakhasimende akho ukuthi ashintshe amathempulethi amasayithi awo, kudingeka uye ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Izilungiselelo > Amasayithi** bese uvula inketho ethi **Vumela Ukushintsha Ithempulethi**.

Futhi, ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Imikhiqizo**, khetha amapulani akho bese uya kuthebhu ethi **Amathempulethi Esayithi**. Qiniseka ukuthi inketho ethi **Vumela Amathempulethi Esayithi** ivuliwe futhi ku-**Imodi Yokukhetha Ithempulethi Yesayithi** , inketho ethi **Khetha Amathempulethi Esayithi Atholakalayo** ikhethiwe.

![Ithebhu yamathempulethi esayithi omkhiqizo enemodi yokukhetha ithempulethi](/img/config/product-site-templates.png)

Uzokwazi ukubona wonke amathempulethi esayithi atholakalayo kuwebhusayithi yakho. Khetha ukuthi yimaphi ofuna ukuwenza atholakale nokuthi yimaphi ongafuni atholakale kumakhasimende akho abhalise ngaphansi kwale plani. Qaphela ukuthi lezi zinketho zithinta futhi ifomu le-checkout, ngakho noma iyiphi ithempulethi ekhethwe njenge-**Ayitholakali** ngeke ivele ekhasini lokubhalisa lale plani.

Manje amakhasimende akho angachofoza ku-**Shintsha Ithempulethi Yesayithi** ngaphakathi kwekhasi lawo le-account.

![Inkinobho ethi Shintsha Ithempulethi Yesayithi ekhasini le-account](/img/account-page/change-template-button.png)

I-Ultimate Multisite 2.10.0 ibonisa iphaneli yokushintsha ithempulethi eklanywe kabusha. Iphaneli iqala nge-**khadi lethempulethi yamanje** ukuze amakhasimende abone ukuthi iyiphi ithempulethi esebenzayo ngaphambi kokukhetha esikhundleni sayo.

Igridi eqhubekayo yamathempulethi esayithi atholakalayo ihlala ibonakala ngenkathi amakhasimende ebuyekeza izinketho zawo. Lokhu kuyawasiza aqhathanise amathempulethi avunyelwe eplani yawo ngaphandle kokulahlekelwa ukubona ukukhetha kwamanje.

![Uhlu lwamathempulethi esayithi atholakalayo eplani](/img/config/site-templates-list.png)

Ngemva kokukhetha leyo afuna ukushintshela kuyo, azocelwa ukuthi aqinisekise ushintsho.

![Ibhokisi lengxoxo yokuqinisekisa ukushintshwa kwethempulethi yesayithi](/img/account-page/template-switch-confirm.png)

Ngemva kokuvula isiqinisekiso nokuchofoza ku-**Qalisa Ukushintsha** , ithempulethi yesayithi entsha izosetshenziswa kuwebhusayithi yekhasimende lakho.

Amakhasimende angasebenzisa futhi **Reset current template** kuleli phaneli lapho edinga ukusetha kabusha isayithi libuyele kuthempulethi elabelwe njengamanje. Njengokushintshela kwenye ithempulethi, ukusetha kabusha ithempulethi kungabhala phezu kokuqukethwe kwesayithi, ngakho amakhasimende kufanele akuqinisekise kuphela lapho eqonda isenzo sokusetha kabusha.

### Ukwengeza Ama-domain Enziwe Ngokwezifiso:

Amakhasimende akho azophinde abe nenketho yokwengeza i-domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso yalolu hlelo ekhasini lawo le-akhawunti. Ukuvumela amakhasimende akho asebenzise ama-domain enziwe ngokwezifiso, iya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings >** **Domain Mapping**.

Vula inketho ethi **Enable Domain Mapping**. Lokhu kuzovumela amakhasimende akho ukuthi asebenzise ama-domain enziwe ngokwezifiso ezingeni lenethiwekhi.

Ungakhohlwa futhi ukuhlola ukuthi i-domain mapping ivunyelwe yini ngokomkhiqizo - ngoba ungakhawulela umkhiqizo ukuze ungavumeli amakhasimende akho asebenzise ama-domain enziwe ngokwezifiso.

Iya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Products**. Khetha uhlelo oluthandayo bese uya kuthebhu ethi **Custom Domains**. Vula inketho ethi **Allow Custom Domains**.

![Ithebhu ethi Custom Domains eneswishi ethi Allow Custom Domains](/img/config/product-custom-domains.png)

Lokhu kuzovumela wonke amakhasimende abhalisele lolu hlelo oluthile ukuthi asebenzise ama-domain enziwe ngokwezifiso. Manje, ekhasini le-Account, amakhasimende akho angangeza i-domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso ngokuchofoza ku-**Add Domain**.

![Inkinobho ethi Add Domain ekhasini le-akhawunti](/img/account-page/add-domain-button.png)

Iwindi lokuqala elivulekayo lizobonisa amakhasimende akho umlayezo owawayalelayo ukuthi angawabuyekeza kanjani amarekhodi awo e-DNS ukuze le domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso isebenze kunethiwekhi yakho.

![Imiyalelo ye-DNS eboniswa lapho kwengezwa i-domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso](/img/account-page/add-domain-dns.png)

Lo mlayezo ungahlelwa (nguwe) ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Domain Mapping > Add New Domain Instructions**.

![Isilungiselelo se-Add New Domain Instructions ku-Domain Mapping](/img/config/settings-domain-mapping.png)

Nanku umbono ogcwele wekhasi lezilungiselelo ze-domain mapping:

![Ikhasi eligcwele lezilungiselelo ze-domain mapping](/img/config/settings-domain-mapping-full.png)

Ngemva kokuchofoza ku-**Next Step** , amakhasimende akho angangeza igama le-domain yawo eyenziwe ngokwezifiso futhi akhethe ukuthi le domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso izoba eyinhloko yini. Qaphela ukuthi amakhasimende akho angasebenzisa i-domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso engaphezu kweyodwa kumawebhusayithi awo, ngakho angakhetha ukuthi iyiphi ezoba eyinhloko.

![Ukufakwa kwegama le-domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso nenketho ye-domain eyinhloko](/img/account-page/add-domain-primary.png)

Ngemva kokuchofoza ku-**Add Domain** , i-domain izongezwa ku-akhawunti yekhasimende lakho. Konke okudingeka bakwenze manje ukushintsha amarekhodi e-DNS ale domain eyenziwe ngokwezifiso kumregistrari wabo we-domain.

### Ukushintsha Iphasiwedi:

Ngaphakathi kwe-dashboard ye-akhawunti, amakhasimende akho angashintsha futhi iphasiwedi yawo ngokuchofoza ku-**Change Password**.

![Inkinobho ethi Change Password ekhasini le-akhawunti](/img/account-page/change-password-button.png)

Lokhu kuzobonisa iwindi elisha lapho amakhasimende akho ezodinga ukugcwalisa iphasiwedi yawo yamanje bese egcwalisa iphasiwedi entsha afuna ukuyisebenzisa.

![Ifomu lokushintsha iphasiwedi elinezinkambu zephasiwedi yamanje nentsha](/img/account-page/change-password-form.png)

### Indawo Eyingozi:

Sinezinketho ezimbili futhi eziboniswa engxenyeni ethi **Danger Zone**: **Delete Site** kanye ne-**Delete Account**. Zombili zikwingxenye ye-Danger Zone ngoba lezi zenzo ezimbili azikwazi ukubuyiselwa emuva. Uma amakhasimende akho esusa iwebhusayithi yawo noma i-akhawunti yawo, ngeke akwazi ukuzibuyisela.

![Danger Zone enezinketho ze-Delete Site ne-Delete Account](/img/account-page/danger-zone.png)

Uma amakhasimende akho echofoza kunoma iyiphi yalezi zinketho ezimbili, azoboniswa iwindi lapho ezodinga ukuvula inketho yokususa iwebhusayithi noma i-akhawunti futhi azoxwayiswa ukuthi lesi senzo asinakuhlehliswa.

![Ibhokisi lokuqinisekisa i-Delete Site](/img/account-page/delete-site-confirm.png)

![Ibhokisi lokuqinisekisa i-Delete Account](/img/account-page/delete-account-confirm.png)

Uma besusa iwebhusayithi yabo, i-akhawunti yabo nobulungu babo kuzohlala kungathintwanga. Bazolahlekelwa kuphela yikho konke okuqukethwe kuwebhusayithi yabo. Uma besusa i-akhawunti yabo, wonke amawebhusayithi, ubulungu kanye nolwazi olumayelana nale akhawunti kuzolahleka.
