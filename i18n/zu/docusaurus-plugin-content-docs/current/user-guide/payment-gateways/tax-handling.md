# Ukuphathwa Kwentela

Ultimate Multisite inemojuli yokuqoqwa kwentela eyakhelwe ku-plugin yethu eyinhloko, ngakho uma udinga ukuqoqa izintela zokuthengisa ezinhlelweni, emaphakhejini nasezinsizeni zakho, ungakwenza kalula ngaphandle kokuphendukela ekufakeni noma yimaphi ama-add-ons.

Ezinkampanini ezise-Europe, sinikeza **i-add-on** enezela amathuluzi nezici ukuze **isekele i-VAT** compliance kangcono.

Ultimate Multisite ayifayili noma ayithumeli izintela egameni lakho kuhulumeni; simane sikusize uqoqe izintela ezifanele ngesikhathi sokwenziwa komsebenzi. **Usazodinga ukuthumela izintela ngokwakho.**

## Ukunika Amandla Ukuqoqwa Kwentela {#enabling-tax-collection}

Ukuqoqwa kwentela akunikiwe amandla ngokuzenzakalelayo. Ukuze ukunike amandla, udinga ukuya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Taxes** bese ushintsha ukuze unike amandla isethingi ethi Enable Taxes.

![Iswishi ye-Enable Taxes phezulu ekhasini lezilungiselelo ze-Tax](/img/config/settings-taxes-enable.png)

Nanku umbono ogcwele wekhasi lezilungiselelo zentela:

![Ikhasi eligcwele lezilungiselelo ze-Tax](/img/config/settings-taxes-full.png)

Ungabuka futhi izilungiselelo zentela zemikhiqizo ngayinye:

![Izilungiselelo ze-Tax zemikhiqizo](/img/config/settings-taxes.png)

### Intela engafakiwe vs. Intela efakiwe {#tax-excluded-vs-tax-included}

Ngokuzenzakalelayo, wonke amanani emikhiqizo yakho awafaki intela, okusho ukuthi izintela **azifakiwe** enanini lomkhiqizo. Uma sinquma ukuthi ikhasimende kufanele likhokhe izintela ekuthengeni okuthile, sizongeza izintela **ngaphezulu** kwe-subtotal.

Uma ukhetha ukuthi izintela zifakwe enanini lomkhiqizo wakho, ungakwenza lokho ngokunika amandla isethingi ye-**Inclusive Tax**.

![Umugqa weswishi ye-Inclusive Tax ngaphansi kwesethingi ye-Enable Taxes](/img/config/settings-taxes-inclusive.png)

Ungakhohlwa **ukulondoloza** izinguquko ozenzile.

###

## Ukudala Amanani Entela {#creating-tax-rates}

Ngemva kokunika amandla Ukuqoqwa Kwentela, uzodinga ukudala amanani entela ezindawo ezithile usebenzisa umhleli wethu wamanani entela.

Ungafinyelela kumhleli ngokuchofoza inkinobho ethi **Manage Tax Rates** ku-sidebar yekhasi lezilungiselelo ze-Tax.

![Isixhumanisi se-Manage Tax Rates kuphaneli ye-Tax Rates ekhasini lezilungiselelo](/img/config/settings-taxes-manage-rates.png)

Ekhasini lomhleli wamanani entela, ungakwazi ukwengeza ama-Tax Rates amasha ngokuchofoza inkinobho ethi **Add new Row**.

![Ithebula lomhleli wamanani entela elinenkinobho ethi Add new Row phezulu](/img/config/tax-rates-editor.png)

Uzodinga ukunikeza izinga lentela ngalinye **isihloko** (esisetshenziswa kuma-invoices). Bese ungakhetha **izwe** (okudingekayo), **isifundazwe,** kanye **nedolobha** (kokubili kuyakhethwa) lapho le ntela izokhokhiswa khona. Ekugcineni, engeza **izinga lentela ngamaphesenti**.

### Izigaba Zentela {#tax-categories}

Ungakwazi futhi ukudala Izigaba Zentela eziningi, ukuze ungeze amanani entela ahlukene ezinhlotsheni ezahlukene zemikhiqizo.

Chofoza ukuze **Add new Tax Category** , bese ubhala igama lesigaba sakho bese ucindezela **Create**.

![Inkinobho ethi Add new Tax Category phezulu komhleli wamanani entela](/img/config/tax-categories-add.png)

![Inkambu yokufaka ye-Tax Category Name ku-modal yokudala isigaba](/img/config/tax-categories-create-modal.png)

Ukuze uphequlule izigaba, chofoza **Switch** bese ukhetha isigaba ofuna ukwengeza kuso izintela ezintsha.

![Inkinobho ye-dropdown ethi Switch ukuze ushintshe phakathi kwezigaba zentela](/img/config/tax-categories-switch.png)

![I-dropdown yokukhetha isigaba sentela ekhombisa izigaba ezitholakalayo](/img/config/tax-categories-select.png)

Ungasetha isigaba sentela somkhiqizo othile ngokuya ku-**Product edit page** bese uya kuthebhu ethi Taxes.

![Ithebhu ye-Product taxes enesigaba sentela neswishi yokuthi iyakhokhiswa intela](/img/config/product-taxes.png)

Kuleso sikrini esifanayo, ungacisha iswishi ethi **Is Taxable?** ukuze wazise Ultimate Multisite ukuthi akufanele iqoqe izintela kulowo mkhiqizo onikeziwe.

## Ukusekelwa kwe-European VAT {#european-vat-support}

Njengoba kushiwo ngaphambili, sine-add-on etholakalayo kumakhasimende ase-EU anezidingo ezengeziwe ngenxa yemithethonqubo ye-European VAT.

Amathuluzi ethu e-VAT asiza ngezinto ezimbalwa ezibalulekile:

  * Ukulayishwa okulula kwamanani e-EU VAT;

  * Ukuqoqwa nokuqinisekiswa kwe-VAT Number - kanye nokukhokhisa okuhlehlisiwe kwezinhlangano ezikhululiwe ku-VAT (njengezinkampani ezinama-VAT numbers avumelekile);

Ukuze ufake leyo add-on, iya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings** bese uchofoza isixhumanisi se-sidebar esithi **Check our Add-ons**.

![I-sidebar yekhasi le-Settings enesixhumanisi esithi Check our Add-ons](/img/config/settings-taxes-addons-link.png)

Uzoqondiswa kabusha ekhasini lethu lama-add-ons. Lapho, ungasesha i-**Ultimate Multisite VAT add-on** bese uyifaka.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Ithayili le-VAT add-on ekhasini lama-Add-ons -->

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Ingxoxo ethi VAT add-on Install Now -->

Bese, uya ku-**Network Admin > Plugins** futhi wenze leyo add-on isebenze kuyo yonke inethiwekhi.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Isenzo se-Network Activate se-VAT add-on ekhasini le-Plugins -->

Uma ubuyela ku-**Tax Settings tab** , uzobona izinketho ezintsha ezitholakalayo. Shintsha inketho ethi **Enable VAT Support** ukuze unike amandla amathuluzi amasha e-VAT. Ungakhohlwa **ukulondoloza** izilungiselelo zakho!

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Iswishi ye-Enable VAT Support kuzilungiselelo ze-Tax ngemva kokuvulwa kwe-add-on -->

### Ukudonsa Amanani Entela ye-VAT {#pulling-on-vat-tax-rates}

Elinye lamathuluzi elengezwa yi-integration yethu yikhono lokulayisha amanani entela amazwe angamalungu e-EU. Lokho kungenziwa ngokuvakashela ikhasi lomhleli wamanani entela ngemva kokunika amandla ukusekelwa kwe-EU VAT.

Phansi kwekhasi, uzobona izinketho zokudonsa i-VAT. Ukukhetha uhlobo lwezinga bese uchofoza inkinobho ethi **Update EU VAT Rates** kuzodonsa futhi kugcwalise ngokuzenzakalelayo ithebula ngamanani entela ezwe ngalinye eliyilungu le-EU. Bese, udinga kuphela ukukulondoloza.

![Inkinobho ethi Update EU VAT Rates phansi komhleli wamanani entela](/img/config/tax-rates-vat-pull.png)

Ungakwazi futhi ukuhlela amanani ngemva kokuwadonsa. Ukuze ukwenze, mane uhlele umugqa wethebula owudingayo bese uchofoza ukulondoloza amanani amasha.

### Ukuqinisekiswa kwe-VAT {#vat-validation}

Uma ukusekelwa kwe-VAT kunikwe amandla, Ultimate Multisite izongeza inkambu eyengeziwe kufomu le-checkout, ngaphansi kwenkambu yekheli lokukhokhisa. Inkambu izovela kuphela kumakhasimende ase-EU.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Inkambu ye-VAT Number kufomu le-frontend checkout ngaphansi kwekheli lokukhokhisa -->

Ultimate Multisite izobe isiqinisekisa i-VAT Number, futhi uma ibuya njengevumelekile, indlela ye-reverse charge iyasetshenziswa bese izinga lentela lisethwa ku-0% kulelo oda.
