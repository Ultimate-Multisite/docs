# Isifundo 1: Kungani I-Niche Iwina {#lesson-1-why-niche-wins}

Ngaphambi kokuthi sithinte noma iyiphi isoftware, kudingeka uqonde ukuthi kungani ukwakhela izethameli ezithile kuyisinqumo esisodwa esibaluleke kakhulu ozosithatha.

## Inkinga Ngokuthengisa Amawebhusayithi Ajwayelekile {#the-problem-with-selling-generic-websites}

Uma unikeza "amawebhusayithi awo wonke umuntu," uncintisana no:

- Wix, Squarespace, kanye ne-WordPress.com ekuzisebenzeleni
- Izinkulungwane zama-freelancer ekwakheni ngokwezifiso
- Fiverr ne-Upwork ngentengo

Awukwazi ukunqoba ngentengo. Awukwazi ukunqoba ngezici. Imakethe yamawebhusayithi ajwayelekile ingumjaho oya phansi.

## Okushintshayo Uma Ukhetha I-Niche {#what-changes-when-you-pick-a-niche}

Uma wakha inkundla ngokukhethekile yamastudio okujima (noma iyiphi i-niche), konke kuyashintsha kube ngakwakho:

### Uxazulula Izinkinga Zangempela {#you-solve-real-problems}

Umnikazi westudio sokujima akafuni "iwebhusayithi." Ufuna:

- Uhlelo lwamakilasi oluzibuyekeza ngokuzenzakalelayo
- Indlela yokuthi amalungu abhukhe izikhathi ku-inthanethi
- Amaphrofayela abaqeqeshi abukeka ngobungcweti
- Ukuhlanganiswa nohlelo lwabo lokubhuka
- Isayithi elibukeka sengathi lingele-brand yokujima, hhayi ithempulethi ejwayelekile

Uma inkundla yakho ixazulula lezi zinkinga ezithile zisuka nje, awusathengisi iwebhusayithi. Uthengisa isixazululo.

### Ungakhokhisa Okuningi {#you-can-charge-more}

Isayithi le-WordPress elijwayelekile lingathengiswa ngo-$500-$2,000 njengephrojekthi yesikhathi esisodwa. Inkundla ye-niche exazulula izinkinga eziqondene nomkhakha ingakhokhisa u-$49-$199/ngenyanga njenge-subscription ephindaphindayo ngoba inani liyaqhubeka futhi liqondile.

### Uba Uchwepheshe {#you-become-the-expert}

Uma ugxila embonini eyodwa, ufunda ulimi lwayo, amaphuzu ayo obuhlungu, namaphethini ayo okuthenga. Abanikazi bamastudio okujima bayakhuluma bodwa. Uma omunye ethola inkundla esebenzayo, utshela abanye. Ukudluliselwa ngomlomo ku-niche kunamandla ngoba umphakathi usondelene.

### Ukumaketha Kwakho Kuba Lula {#your-marketing-gets-easier}

Esikhundleni sokuthi "sakhela amabhizinisi amawebhusayithi," uthi "sakhela amastudio okujima amawebhusayithi." Ikhasi lakho lokufika likhuluma ngqo nabanikazi bama-gym. Amathempulethi akho abukeka njengezindawo zokujima. Ubufakazi bakho buvela kubaqeqeshi nabanikazi bamastudio. Yonke ingxenye yokumaketha ishaya ngamandla kakhulu ngoba iqondile.

## Ithuba le-FitSite {#the-fitsite-opportunity}

Ake sibheke imboni yokujima ngale ndlela:

### Usayizi Wemakethe {#market-size}

- Kunezikhungo zokujima ezingaphezu kuka-100,000 e-United States kuphela
- Iningi lazo lingamabhizinisi amancane azimele
- Amaningi awo awanayo iwebhusayithi, anayo embi kakhulu, noma anayo ebiza ngokweqile abangakwazi ukuyibuyekeza ngokwabo

### Izixazululo Zamanje {#current-solutions}

- **Abakhi abajwayelekile** (Wix, Squarespace): Azikho izici eziqondene nokujima, abanikazi bamastudio bayazabalaza ukwakha abakudingayo
- **Ukuthuthukisa ngokwezifiso**: Kuyabiza, kwenziwa kanye kuphela, kunzima ukukugcina
- **Izinkundla eziqondene nomkhakha**: Zimbalwa ezikhona, futhi eziningi ziboshelwe ezinhlelweni zokubhuka kunokunikeza ukuba khona okuphelele kuwebhu

### Igebe {#the-gap}

Kukhona igebe elicacile phakathi "komakhi wewebhusayithi ojwayelekile" kanye "nokuthuthukisa ngokwezifiso okubizayo" kumabhizinisi okujima. Inkundla enikeza abanikazi bamastudio iwebhusayithi yobungcweti, eqondene nokujima, enezici abazidingayo -- ngentengo enengqondo ebhizinisini elincane -- igcwalisa lelo gebe.

Leli yithuba i-FitSite ezolihlose.

## Okubalulekile Okumele Ukuthathe {#key-takeaway}

Ukukhetha i-niche akukhona ukuzikhawulela. Kumayelana nokugxilisa umzamo wakho lapho unomthelela omkhulu khona. Inkundla ye-niche esiza amakhasimende angu-500 ngo-$99/ngenyanga iyibhizinisi lika-$600,000/ngonyaka. Awudingi izigidi zamakhasimende. Udinga afanele.

## Esikwakhile Kulesi Sifundo {#what-we-built-this-lesson}

Akukho msebenzi wobuchwepheshe okwamanje -- kodwa manje usunesisekelo sesu:

- **Kungani i-niche iwina**: ukuqondisa okuthile kunqoba okujwayelekile enanini, entengo, ekumaketheni, nasekudluliselweni ngomlomo
- **Umqondo we-FitSite**: inkundla yewebhusayithi eyakhelwe ngokukhethekile amastudio okujima
- **Ithuba lemakethe**: imakethe enkulu engafinyelelwa, engasizwanga ngokwanele, igebe elicacile
