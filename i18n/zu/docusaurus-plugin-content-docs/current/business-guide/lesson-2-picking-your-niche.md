# Isifundo 2: Ukukhetha I-niche Yakho

Esifundweni 1 sathola ukuthi kungani i-niche inqoba okujwayelekile. Manje kudingeka ukhethe eyakho. Lesi sifundo sikunikeza uhlaka lokuthola nokuqinisekisa i-niche ngaphambi kokuba utshale isikhathi uyakhela yona.

## Lapho Esagcina Khona

Sachaza umqondo we-FitSite: inkundla yewebhusayithi yezitudiyo zokuzivocavoca. Kodwa safika kanjani ezitudiyweni zokuzivocavoca ngokuqondile? Lesi sifundo sihamba ngenqubo ukuze ukwazi ukuyisebenzisa ukuthola i-niche yakho.

## Imibandela Emithathu

I-niche enhle yenkundla yewebhusayithi ihlangabezana nezimo ezintathu:

### 1. Badinga Amawebhusayithi Kodwa Bayahluleka Ukuthola Amahle

Bheka izimboni lapho:

- Amabhizinisi kulindeleke ukuthi abe khona kuwebhu
- Amawebhusayithi amaningi akhona manje embonini esezingeni eliphansi
- Abakhi abajwayelekile bengaxazululi izidingo zabo ezithile
- Ukuthuthukiswa ngokwezifiso kubiza kakhulu ngosayizi webhizinisi ojwayelekile

**Izitudiyo zokuzivocavoca ziyafaneleka**: amalungu alindele ukuthola amashejuli amakilasi, amanani, nokubhuka ku-inthanethi. Amawebhusayithi amaningi ezitudiyo aphelelwe yisikhathi noma akhiwe ngezifanekiso ezijwayelekile ezingafezi injongo.

### 2. Amabhizinisi Angakwazi Ukukhokhela Isubscription Sanyanga Zonke

Amakhasimende owaqondisayo kudingeka abe:

- Esezinzile ngokwanele ukuba abe nesabelomali sokumaketha
- Ejwayele ukukhokhela ama-subscription esoftware (amasistimu okubhuka, ukucubungula izinkokhelo, njll.)
- Esebenza ngezinga lapho u-$49-$199/ngenyanga kuyizindleko zebhizinisi ezifanele

**Izitudiyo zokuzivocavoca ziyafaneleka**: sezivele zikhokhela isoftware yokubhuka, ukucubungula izinkokhelo, namathuluzi okumaketha. I-subscription yewebhusayithi iyisengezo esingokwemvelo.

### 3. Bayakhuluma Bodwa

Ama-niche angcono kakhulu anokulandelayo:

- Izinhlangano zemboni, izingqungquthela, noma imibukiso yezohwebo
- Imiphakathi esebenzayo ku-inthanethi (amaqembu e-Facebook, amaforamu, ama-subreddit)
- Amanethiwekhi endawo lapho abanikazi bamabhizinisi babelana ngezincomo

**Izitudiyo zokuzivocavoca ziyafaneleka**: abanikazi bezitudiyo baya emicimbini yemboni yezokuzivocavoca, babamba iqhaza emiphakathini eku-inthanethi, futhi bavame ukwabelana ngamathuluzi nezinsizakalo nozakwabo.

## Indlela Yokuthola Abangaba Ama-niche

### Qala Ngalokho Okwaziyo

Okuhlangenwe nakho kwakho okukhona kuyinzuzo. Cabanga ngalokhu:

- Izimboni oke wasebenza kuzo noma wazakhela amawebhusayithi
- Izinto ozithandayo noma izintshisekelo lapho uqonda khona umphakathi
- Amanethiwekhi ochwepheshe osuvele ungowawo

### Bheka Izimpawu Zobuhlungu

Sesha ubufakazi bokuthi amabhizinisi ku-niche ahlushwa amawebhusayithi:

- Google "[industry] website builder" bese ubona okukhona
- Phequlula amaforamu emboni ukuze uthole izikhalazo ngobukhona kuwebhu
- Bheka amawebhusayithi angempela ku-niche -- ingabe amaningi awo mabi?
- Hlola ukuthi bakhona yini abancintisana nawe nokuthi bayisebenzela kahle kangakanani imakethe

### Hlola Ezomnotho

Ku-niche ngayinye engaba khona, linganisa:

- **Usayizi wemakethe**: Mangaki amabhizinisi akhona kule niche? (Qala ngezwe/isifunda sakho)
- **Ukuzimisela ukukhokha**: Bachitha malini njengamanje kumathuluzi afanayo?
- **Amandla enani lesikhathi sokuphila**: Ingabe la mabhizinisi azinzile, noma ayashintsha ngokushesha?

## Ukuqinisekisa I-niche Yakho

Ngaphambi kokwakha noma yini, qinisekisa isidingo:

### Khuluma Namakhasimende Angaba Khona

Xhumana nabanikazi bamabhizinisi abangu-10-15 ku-niche oyiqondisayo. Buza:

- Wayithola kanjani iwebhusayithi yakho yamanje?
- Yini ofisa ukuthi ikwazi ukuyenza kodwa engakwazi?
- Uyikhokhela malini njengamanje?
- Uma bekukhona inkundla eyakhelwe ngokuqondile [i-niche yakho], ubungaba nentshisekelo?

Awukadayisi okwamanje. Uyalalela.

### Hlola Isimo Sabancintisana Nawe

- **Abekho abancintisana nawe**: Kungasho ukuthi asikho isidingo, noma kungasho ithuba elingakathintwa. Izingxoxo namakhasimende zizokutshela ukuthi yikuphi.
- **Abancintisana nawe abambalwa**: Uphawu oluhle. Kuqinisekisa isidingo. Funda lokho abakwenza kahle nalapho besilela khona.
- **Abancintisana nawe abaningi**: Kunzima kakhulu ukungena, kodwa kusenokwenzeka uma ungazihlukanisa ngokucacile.

### Yakha Ikhasi Lokufika

Ngaphambi kokwakha inkundla, dala ikhasi lokufika elilula elichaza isixazululo sakho se-niche bese uqoqa ukubhalisa nge-imeyili. Sebenzisa inani elincane lokukhangisa okuqondisiwe ukuze uhlole intshisekelo. Uma abantu bebhalisa, usunokuqinisekiswa.

## Ukusebenzisa Lokhu ku-FitSite

Nansi indlela i-FitSite ethole ngayo amaphuzu uma iqhathaniswa nemibandela:

| Umbandela | Ukuhlola |
|-----------|------------|
| Badinga amawebhusayithi kodwa bayahluleka | Yebo -- amasayithi amaningi ezitudiyo ajwayelekile noma aphelelwe yisikhathi |
| Bangakwazi ukukhokhela i-subscription | Yebo -- sebeyakhokha u-$50-$200/ngenyanga ngesoftware yokubhuka |
| Bayakhuluma bodwa | Yebo -- imiphakathi esebenzayo, imicimbi yemboni, amanethiwekhi endawo |
| Usayizi wemakethe | Izikhungo ezingu-100,000+ e-US kuphela |
| Isimo sabancintisana nawe | Zimbalwa izinkundla zewebhusayithi eziqondene ne-niche ezikhona |

Ukuqinisekiswa kuqinisekise ithuba. Manje siyakha.

## Imibono Yama-niche Ongayicabangela

Uma ezokuzivocavoca zingeyona imakethe yakho, nazi ezinye ama-niche ahlangabezana nemibandela:

- **Izindawo zokudlela namakhefi** -- amamenyu, ukubhukha amatafula, amahora, ukuhlanganiswa kokulethwa
- **Ama-ejenti ezindlu** -- izinhlu zezindlu, amaphrofayela ama-ejenti, imihlahlandlela yezindawo
- **Amafemu omthetho** -- izindawo zokusebenza, ama-bio abameli, ukubhukha ukubonisana
- **Amasonto nezinhlangano ezingenzi nzuzo** -- imicimbi, izintshumayelo, iminikelo, umphakathi
- **Osonkontileka bezohwebo** (abasebenza ngamapayipi, abasebenza ngogesi, HVAC) -- izindawo zesevisi, ukubhukha, izibuyekezo
- **Amasaluni nama-spa** -- izinsizakalo, ukubhukha, amaphrofayela ama-stylist, amagalari
- **Imitholampilo yezilwane** -- izinsizakalo, ama-portal ezilwane ezifuywayo, ukubhukha ama-aphoyintimenti
- **Izikole zomculo** -- amaphrofayela abafundisi, ukuhlela izifundo, ulwazi lwemibukiso

Ngayinye yalezi inezici ezifanayo: izidingo zewebhusayithi ezicacile, ikhono lokukhokha, nokusabalala kwezwi ngomphakathi.

## Esikwakhile Kulesi Sifundo

- **Uhlaka lokukhetha i-niche**: imibandela emithathu yokuhlola noma iyiphi i-niche
- **Izindlela zokuqinisekisa**: izingxoxo namakhasimende, ukuhlaziywa kwabancintisana nawe, ukuhlola ikhasi lokufika
- **Ukuqinisekiswa kwe-FitSite**: kuqinisekisiwe ukuthi izitudiyo zokuzivocavoca zihlangabezana nayo yonke imibandela
- **Ama-niche ahlukile**: uhlu lweminye imikhakha ongasebenzisa kuyo lolu hlaka
