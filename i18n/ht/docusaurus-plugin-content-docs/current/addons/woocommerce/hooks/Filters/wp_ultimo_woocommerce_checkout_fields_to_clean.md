# Filtè: wp_ultimo_woocommerce_checkout_fields_to_clean

Pèmèt devlopè yo filtre chan adrès fakti WooCommerce defo yo.

Piske Ultimate Multisite gen chan adrès fakti, li ta ka fè sans pou retire chan defo WooCommerce montre sou fòm checkout la. Pa defo, pa gen okenn valè ki pase nan lis sa a, kidonk tout chan ki konfigire sou WooCommerce pou afiche yo kenbe.

### Depi

- 2.0.0
### Sous

Defini nan [`inc/gateways/class-woocommerce-gateway.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite-woocommerce/blob/main/inc/gateways/class-woocommerce-gateway.php#L277) nan liy 277


## Retounen
Lis chan pou retire yo. Valè ki disponib yo gen ladan billing_first_name, billing_last_name, billing_company, billing_country, billing_address_1, billing_address_2, billing_city, billing_state, billing_postcode, billing_email, ak billing_phone.
