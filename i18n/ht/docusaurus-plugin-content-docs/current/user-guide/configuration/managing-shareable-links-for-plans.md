# Jeremanje Liyen Liens Ki Shareable pou Plan yo {#managing-shareable-links-for-plans}

Ultimate Multisite bay ou opsyon pou ou pataje liens plan dirèkt ak kliyan ou la. Pou fè sa, ale nan nenpòt paj edisyon plan ou epi klike sou bouton _Click to copy the Shareable Link_.

![Paj edisyon pwodwi ak bouton link shareable](/img/config/product-edit-full.png)

_Sou Paj Edisyon Plan an, ou ka jwenn bouton pou kreye liens shareable_

Fè atansyon ke mekanis sa a itilize frekans fakturi dekouvè (default billing frequency) la pou bati link la. Sa vle di si fakturi default ou se chanjman chak mwa, pa egzanp, nan fen an, ou pral gen yon link tankou sa: [_**https://network.com/register/premium/**_](https://network.com/register/premium/)

Si ou vle link shareable a devan pou li quarterly (chak 3 mwa), ou dwe ajoute 3 la nan fen:

[_**https://network.com/register/premium/3**_](https://network.com/register/premium/3)

Pou chwazi plan _premium_ sou yon chèdoul an annè, ou pral ajoute 1 epi mo a sa a:

[_**https://network.com/register/premium/1/year**_](https://network.com/register/premium/1/year)

Kenbe nan tèt ou ke ou pa ka gen sèl link pou diferan frekans fakturi. Ou dwe pataje yon link espesifik pou chak frekans fakturi ou genyen.

Yon fwa ou pataje link la ak kliyan ou, li pral redireksyon yo nan premye etap koupe anrejistre (signup flow) ou a.
