### Leano la Tlhaloso la Preso (The Pricing Table Field) {#the-pricing-table-field}

Leano la **Pricing Table** le bo iketsopeletse preso ea hau ka form ya checkout ho ba leboho ba tloha ho ba leboho ba ntlha. Ho feta leano la hau, u ka ho setso litlhahlo tse fapaneng:

![Pricing Table field settings](/img/config/pricing-table-field-settings.png)

Kea leano la pricing table le leba mo form ya registration ea frontend (frontend):

![Frontend checkout pricing table](/img/config/frontend-checkout-pricing-table.png)

  * **Products**: U fana ho utloha preso ebebe ba u batla ho bonahale le bohlokoa ba li tla ba leboho ka morao o tla ba.
  * **Force Different Durations** (Ho fetola tsela ea morao): Ho feta le seo, ha se a fana ka litlhahlo tse fapaneng tsa preso hantle ho morao o tla ba leboho. Ha e fana (default), preso ebebe e nang le morao o tla ba leboho e ne e fana ho ba leboho.
    ![Pricing table force durations option](/img/config/pricing-table-force-durations.png)
  * **Hide when Pre-Selected** (Ho fana ha u fetola): Ho fana ha leano la pricing table ha morao o tla fetola ka URL (e.a, /register/premium).
  * **Pricing Table Template**: U utloha template ea bonolo ebebe ba leano la pricing table (Simple List, Legacy, le ho hlaha).

Ha u fana preso ebebe mo Pricing Table ha form e nang litlhahlo tse hlokang ho feta morao oa preso ya hau, editor o tla bona tšepo. U sebelisa tšepo ho fana litlhahlo tse hlokang tsa bohlokoa ha u fetola ka tsamaiso (publish) kapa ho fana litlhahlo tse hlokang ho feta ho ba leboho ya registration e le live.

### Go Dirha Ho Fikiso Ho Hoho (Adding a Period Selection Toggle) {#adding-a-period-selection-toggle}

Ha u na le ho setea [Price Variations](creating-your-first-subscription-product#price-variations) ka baaka (e.g. monthly and annual pricing) tsa lona products, u ka ntsa **Period Selection** field (tsebo ya Hoho haholo) mo checkout form la lona. Le tseo, se fa toggle se ntse se leba ba bang ba ka fana ka ho tsamaya le periods tsa ho tsamaea, le table ya prices e tla fetoga ka nako ka nako.

#### Step 1: Setea Price Variations mo Lona Products {#step-1-set-up-price-variations-on-your-products}

Ha o tsamaisa ho ntsa Period Selection field, u leboha hore lona products le ba setea price variations. La goba **Ultimate Multisite > Products**, etsa product e le, le fana ka tab la **Price Variations** ho ntsa periods tsa ho tsamaea (e.g. Annual le li-discounted).

#### Step 3: Posisha le Field Lehlelo Phethwa Pele le Table ya Preso {#step-3-position-the-field-above-the-pricing-table}

Ho ba leboho le fetoga kae, kea leba le Period Selection field leba pele la Pricing Table field mo step ya checkout ya lona. Ke ka leba fields ho re-order mo checkout form editor. Ka leka le sehlopha sa ba, ba tla leba period ya billing pele ba bona preso ya that period.

![Checkout form editor showing field order](/img/config/checkout-form-editor-with-fields.png)

#### Kea sehlopha ka Frontend {#how-it-works-on-the-frontend}

Ha e lehlelo, ba ba ba leboho ba tla bona selector ya period leba pele la pricing table mo page ya lona ya registration. Ha ba leba period ya billing efe:

  * Pricing table e tla fetoga ka tsela kae ho bona preso ya that period (ha ho hlokomisa reload ya page).
  * Ha **Force Different Durations** e le disabled mo Pricing Table field, products ha ho na variation ya preso efe sa that period ha tla ba leba.
  * Ha **Force Different Durations** e le enabled, sehlopha sa products se tla ba leba hantle ha ho na variation ya preso efe sa that period (se tla bona preso ya default ya bone).

#### Ho leba Period ya Billing ka URL {#pre-selecting-a-billing-period-via-url}

Ke ka leba product le period ya billing pele ka URL. Ultimate Multisite e leboho sehlopha sa URLs tse di fetoga:

  * `/register/premium` — Ho leba product "Premium" haholo
  * `/register/premium/12` — Ho leba product le duration ya 12 months
  * `/register/premium/1/year` — Ho leba product le duration ya 1-year

### Lehlelo la Field La Selection ya Template {#the-template-selection-field}

Field la **Template Selection** le ba ba leboho ba tla leba site template mo checkout. Kea le ka fihla ka default mo single step le multi-step checkout form templates tse di fetoga mo Ultimate Multisite v2.6.1.

#### Ho leba field manually {#adding-the-field-manually}

Ha u le fa fōma a e a fetang ka a fetang ho v2.6.1 a fetang ka a fetang ho template a ntlha:

1. La **Ultimate Multisite > Checkout Forms** la, a fetola fōma a checkout.
2. Ho tsela la step a o le fa a e fa'aiga o le site (site details) a fa'aoga, a klik **Add new Field**.
3. I le dialog o le field type, selek **Template Selection**.
4. Fa'amalosia le field:
   - **Label** — O le fa'aiga o le fa'atupu (heading) o le fa'aiga o le template a e fa'aoga i le fa'aiga o le grid o le template (o le a fa'aiga, e.g., "Choose a site template").
   - **Required** — O le a e tatau ona selek se template i le fa'atupu a le fa'aiga a le fa'aoga i le fa'aiga o le fa'amalosia (before proceeding).

#### O le a lelei lava {#how-it-works}

O le a e le fa'aiga o le customer a selek se template i le checkout, Ultimate Multisite o la fa'aoga lea pe a o ia fa'aoga le site a ia fa'aoga. O le mga template a fa'aoga e fa'aoga mai i o lo **Site Templates** list (**Ultimate Multisite > Site Templates**). O le mga template a tatau ona fa'aoga mo le customer fa'aoga i lona.

### Checkout-form base domains {#checkout-form-base-domains}

Ultimate Multisite v2.13.0 o la fa'aoga le domains a fa'aoga i le checkout form **Site URL** e pei o network base domains. Fa'aoga le settings o le available-domain o le field lea lava mo le taimi e te te mana'o o le customer fa'aoga site i le a fa'amalosia (provisioning) i le base domains a fa'aoga i le tasi po'o se tatau.

O le mga checkout-form base domains a fa'aoga e le fa'aoga mo per-site custom domain mappings. O le taimi e o le customer fa'aoga site i subdirectory a leiri i o base domains e tatau, Ultimate Multisite o la fa'aoga se mapped-domain record ae o le a fa'amalosia (shared host) o le fa'aoga lea mo le site tasi. O le shared host o le fa'aoga lea o le fa'aoga lea mo sibling sites e fa'aoga le checkout form base a laemalo.

Fa'aoga le custom domains mo per-customer mapped hosts, e pei `customer-example.com`. Fa'aoga le checkout-form base domains mo shared registration hosts ae o le a fa'aoga i le site fa'aoga lea.

#### A lelei lava le field {#removing-the-field}



Ha u le ho ba le kaofatso ea template, hlakisa sebo ea Template Selection ho fana'a. Ba tla sebelisa template e e fapaneng eo e le e fapaneng ho setsoe ka **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Site Templates**.
