### Kea le ho fetola project ka Codeable? {#how-to-create-a-project-in-codeable}

Mopola, ula [Codeable](https://codeable.io/partners/wpultimo/?ref=7wHwE) le faka bohotse **Start a Project** (Ho fetola project).

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Codeable homepage with Start a Project button -->

Ho fana ka tšoa sebope, u thabela le lapa la lefa ho **Start a Project**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Lapa la tsamaiso tsa project le lapa la Start a Project -->

Ha o le "I need help with", u uthole **Customizing** :

Ha o "My", uba **Plugin**:

Uba ka sefane sa "My" uba **Plugin**:

Lekete le **Continue**.

<!-- Screenshot a ne a tsela: Button ea ho tsamaea le project form e e hlophisoa -->

Le nako e ntse e tla ba la ho hlalosa project ea hau le ho hlalosa hore o na le litlhoko tse fapaneng. Le tšoa ho ba le lipale tse fapaneng!

<!-- Screenshot e ne a tšoa: Sebope seo sebope sa morao wa tshedi (project description form) -->

Go leboha dikgwathlo tse di fetang ho thusa ka tshedi.

<!-- Screenshot e ne a tšoa: Dikgwathlo tsa tshedi tsa tshedi (project pricing questions) -->

Ha o ka se a le [Codeable](https://codeable.io/partners/wpultimo/?ref=7wHwE) account? A re tšoa e, a re tla etsa e le, a re kopa le **Publish My Project**.

<!-- Screenshot e ne ea ho lehleletso: Ho hlalosa ho fetola taba ea akaobo ea codeable le sebopeho sa "Publish My Project" -->

Ha o ne u dirile ho hlalosa (publish) tsela ya hau ya [Codeable](https://codeable.io/partners/wpultimo/?ref=7wHwE), le platform e tla u tsamaisa le ba thuto ba ba fetang ba ba fetang ka sehlopha se se hlophisetseng ho hau.

Rea le faofong haholo ho utloa morao o nang le sehlopha se tshepa ka WordPress le Ultimate Multisite!

Tshupo ya Codeable e le $70–$120 USD ka mase. Ho fana fa ho fetoga ka tsela e leng le maikutlo a morao a morero, bohlokoa ba morero, le hore bo nang le tsotso.

Ha o ne u le le tšepo ea hau, u tla u ka hloba mo-developer o o fapaneng le ho fetoga haholo, u le u ka laola sebopeho sa hau!
