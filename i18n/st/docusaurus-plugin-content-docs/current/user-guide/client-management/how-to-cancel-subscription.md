# Ke ba ka fana ka tšhobo? (How can I cancel my subscription?)

Ha o na Ultimate Multisite license, u ka fana ho fana haholo ka nako e tsoalo ea hau. A re le sehlopha seletse sefa:

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Account page email with access link -->

U sebelisa email ea hau seo u phethileng ka lebaka la khathollo (purchase) le password ea hau.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Login page with email and password fields -->

Ke ea liti la hau la Account Page lehle:

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Account page initial screen showing subscription details -->

Ho fana ho fana haholo, ka menu ea lehlopha lehlopha (left menu), u laela _Renewing & Billing._

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Renewing & Billing page with cancel option -->

Hloka arrow e hloka ka lina lehlopha (right side) ho bua le window seletse. Ho feta moo, u ka utloi opshoni ea ho fana haholo (auto-renew).

Ho ba le message ea ho tsamaea (confirmation message).

Ha u re le u batla ho fana haholo, u ka hloka ho tsamaea ka bohlokoa _**Cancel Renewals**_ button. Ha e le nako ea hau, u tla tšoa survey e le teng.

Ho feta! Ho fana haholo ha o tla fana haholo.

**O tla ba le key e fetang ka nako ea hau ea ho fana.** Ha u batla ho fana haholo ha u tla hloka ho etsa ka monate.
