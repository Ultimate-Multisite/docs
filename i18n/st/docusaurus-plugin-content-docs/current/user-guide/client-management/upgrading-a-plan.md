# Goala ho Hoho (v2)

_**LIPOTSE E KOTSI: Le tsela e thisa e le seo se Ultimate Multisite v2.x.**_

Ba ntlha ba hau ba ka fetola (upgrade) plans ea hau ka nako fa hau. Ba ka fetola ho plan e le efe, kapa ba ka utloi litšomo tsa boikotso (services) kapa packages tse ling tse o tloang ka hau ka network ea hau.

Ina le tutorial ena re tla lekanya hore na ba ka fetola plan ea bona le eng e tla dirang ka batho ba ba fetolela.

Ho fetola plan ea bona, ba ba bang ba ba ntlha ho ba le dashboard ea bona le ba la **Account** page.

![Customer subsite dashboard with Account menu link visible](/img/account-page/account-menu.png)

Ka page ea Account, ba tla ba leba boikotso ba bona ba lehle le plan e tloang ho bone. Ho fetola ho plan e le efe, ba hloka ba tsamaetsa **Change** ka mofuta o tla tloha (top-right corner) ha sebahala sa **Your Membership**.

![Account page Your Membership card with Change button](/img/account-page/membership-change-button.png)

Ba tla fetola ho checkout form e leng le ba tla bona plans ka tsela e tsoang.

Ba ka bona tseba **services le packages tse tloang ho plan ea bona e le efe**, ha ba ba batla fa ba batla utloi hantle (like unlimited visits) kapa disk space sa hau (ka sefalo sa hau), kapa ha ba batla fetola plan.

![Upgrade picker showing available plans and packages on the customer side](/img/account-page/upgrade-picker.png)

Ha ba tsamaetsa mokgwa o tloang oa ho utloi, ba tla bona hore na ba tla hloka ho rata (pay) ka nako ea lona - ha ba leba credit e le efe e le efe, kapa na ba tla rata fa nako ea boikotso (billing date).

Ha sefalo, ha mokgwa o tloang ke plan e le efe le ho rata ho rata ho rata (payment) ho fetola ka boikotso ba plan e le efe, ba tla bona credit e le efe e le efe ea nako ea lona.

![Upgrade payment summary showing credit applied and next billing amount](/img/account-page/upgrade-summary.png)

Ha ba leona e fanoa ea tšepo ea tšepetso (credit) le tšepo ea ho tsamaea haholo (next billing amount).

Ha ba ba utloi plan kapa package e tla fetola ntho e le e sa fetoleng ka tsela ea liti, ba tla bona litšepo tse bontšang hore na le se.

![Notice when the selected plan does not change the subscription](/img/account-page/upgrade-no-change.png)

Ha ufeha ho feta (checkout) haholo, ntho e ntlha (new product(s)) e tla fana ka tsela ea tšepo ea baeba le litšepo le riti tsa ntho e ntlha (visits, disk space, posts, etc...) e tla tsamaea ka tsela e le e le sa le.

## Tsela ea ho fetola le ho fana (Upgrade and Downgrade Paths) {#upgrade-and-downgrade-paths}

Ka nako ea ntho ea hau ka nako ea hau, o tla bona tab e ntse e bolelang **Up & Downgrades**. Opoto oa ntlha ea tab eohle ke sehlopso se na le le bitso **Plan Group**.

**Plan groups** ke se se etsa hore Ultimate Multisite se tsebe hore plan tse ling ba ba ka "famili" e le, le hore ba tla sebelisoa ho hlalosa tsela ea ho fetola kapa ho fana (upgrade/downgrade path options).

![Product edit Up and Downgrades tab with Plan Group field](/img/config/product-upgrades-plan-group.png)

Ho na la sefalo, o na **Free plan**, **Basic Plan** le **Premium Plan**. O batla ba ba ba tsamaea ka **Free Plan** ba tla ba ka ho fana (upgrade) fa **Premium Plan** le o batla hore ba sa bona "Basic Plan" e le tsela ea ho fana. Ho na la sehlopso seo u batlang ke ho rata ho tloha le bitso tsa plan tse ling ka leina le le le leng ka ntho e ntse e le e le le leng ka nako ea screenshots tse bontšang below.

![Free Plan product page with High End plan group assigned](/img/config/product-upgrades-free.png)

![Premium Plan product page with High End plan group assigned](/img/config/product-upgrades-premium.png)

O le fa'āuga lea e le fa'aalia i Ultimate Multisite o le fa'aalo o fa'aalo ua fa'aopoopo i le network, o se fa'aalo ua fa'aopoopo i le **High End**. Pe a oe e fa'aalia ai upgrades (fa'amalosia) pe downgrades (fa'atatau), o se fa'aalo uma e fa'aopoopo i le fa'aalo o le tasi o le fa'aalo ua fa'aopoopo i le network o le a fa'aopoopo ma fa'aopoopo.
