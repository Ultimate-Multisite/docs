![Menu ea Account mo dashboard ea li-ntaba](/img/account-page/account-menu.png)

Ha morero o fihla ho tloha mo tenant ea sovereign, panel ea li-ntaba ea motheo e ka ba le link ea ho tsamaisa ho tloha mo site ea tenant. Link ea ho tsamaisa e tla bona ha Ultimate Multisite e ka ba le ho ba leba mokhoa oa ho fetola target ea ho tsamaisa se le sefapeng sa morero oa li-ntaba, seo se fana ka ho a tloha arbitrarily (ho fetola ho batho) haholo, mme e fana ka ho ba le mokhoa o tla etsoang mo tenant.

### Le Tlhaloso la Membership ea hau:

Block e lehle ho hlaha mmele oa website ea ba bang ba ba khale ho ntlha ho bona tsela ea plan ea bona le litšomo/packages tse di fetileng tse di fetileng. Block ena e bontša e le ntlha ea membership, sebope sa ho tšoa haholo, eng e fanaang ka plan le setšhaba/package le eng e fanaang, le hore e fanaang eng ka madi a bona ho baile le membership ena. Ho tla tseba ho e le **Active** (e fetileng), **Expired** (e fetileng) kapa **Canceled** (e fetileng).

![Membership overview showing plan, amount, and billing details](/img/account-page/membership-card.png)

Mmele o lehle ho hlaha mmele oa hau, ba bang ba bona litšomo tsa **About This Site** le tse di fetileng tsa **Site Limits**. Mele moena e bontša tsela hantle ea liphatso tse fapaneng tse di fetileng tsa plan ea bona: sebope sa disk space, posts, pages, visits, le hore. Liphatso tse di fetileng haholo ka plan e fetileng e tla ba le ho setsoe ka **Ultimate Multisite > Products**.

![About This Site and Site Limits blocks showing plan limitations](/img/account-page/site-limits.png)

Ka lehlakeng la **Your Membership**, ba bang ba kaqetela ho tsamaea ka **Change**. Sena se tla bona litšomo tse hloekileng tsa plans le packages/services tse fetileng. Ha re tšoa plan e fetileng, liphatso tsa plan e fetileng li tla ba le ho fanaang haholo ha liphatso tsa membership e fetileng — haholo ha u fetola (downgrade) kapa u tsamaea (upgrade).

Ha u tsamaea ho tšoa packages kapa services tse di fetileng ho membership ena, joalo ka sebope sa disk space le visits tse fapaneng — membership e fetileng e tla tsamaea haholo empa ke packages tse ntlha tse ntse di fetileng tse tla ba le.

A re tseba hore coupon codes haholo haholo haholo ho tšoa mmele ena ea change. Ha u sebelisang coupon code ka tsela ea ho tsamaea membership e fetileng, code e tla fanaang haholo haholo haholo ho membership ena e fetileng.

### Ho Fetola Leletso la Madi a Baile:

Morale ea lekhotso la tšepo (account page), ba bo ba ka fetola leletso la ho tsamaea haholo (billing address). Ba ke ba hloka fa ba thobela **Update** e leng ho _Billing Address_.

![Billing Address section with Update button](/img/account-page/billing-address.png)

Mohlodi o tla ba leba le mohlodi oa lisetso la bona. Re ea fa ba hloka fa ba tsamaisa leletso la ntlha (new address) le ba thobela ho tsamaea **Save Changes**.

![Update billing address form](/img/account-page/billing-address-form.png)

### Ho fetola Template ea Lisetso:

Ho ba ba ka fetola template ea lisetso la bona, u hloka fa u ea kwa **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Sites** le ho fana ka (toggle on) bonako ea **Allow Template Switching**.

Ena le, eona **Ultimate Multisite > Products**, seba plans ea hau le ea ea kwa tab ea **Site Templates**. Re tloha hore bonako ea **Allow Site Templates** e fana ka (toggled on) le ho bonako ea **Site Template Selection Mode**, bonako ea **Choose Available Site Templates** e ba etsoile.

![Product site templates tab with template selection mode](/img/config/product-site-templates.png)

O tla ba le bohlokoa ho bona template ka tsela e fapaneng tsa hau. Reba hore u tsame se sehlopha seo u batlang ho ba etsoile le seo u batlang se se hloka ho ba etsoile ho ba leba leba ba baetsoang ka plan ena. Se tseba hore bonako lena li fetola eona tabla ea checkout, ka sekolong sebaka se se etsoile se tla ba leba leba sa ho bona ka lereka la tsamaiso (registration page) la plan ena.

Lumela, ba ba ka tsamaea **Change Site Template** mo lekhotso la tšepo la bona.

![Change Site Template button on account page](/img/account-page/change-template-button.png)

Ultimate Multisite 2.10.0 e bontša panel ea ho fetola template e fetoa (redesigned). Panel e tla qala le **current-template card** seo ba ba ka bona template e etsoile hore ba tsame se setso se etsoile ha ba ba tsamaea ho ba leba se se fetoa.

Ho faka a le tsela lafaka la ho site templates a fetang ka ho hlakileng, ho fana ka ba bang ho tsamaetsa ho ba ntsaetsi ba bona ba tsela tsa ho site. Sena se ba thusa ho ba ba batla ho ba tsamaetsa le tsela e fetileng e ntse e le teng, ha ba a hlakileng le ho fana ka tsela ea ba ba e fetang.

![List la tsela tsa ho site templates tse fetangong ho plan](/img/config/site-templates-list.png)

Ha ba tsamaetsa yo o batlang ho fetola, ba tla ba tsamaiswa ho ba le ka ho ba leka ho fetola.

![Dialog la ho tsamaetsa tsela tsa site template](/img/account-page/template-switch-confirm.png)

Ha u fana ka ho tsamaetsa (toggle on) confirmation le ho tsamaetsa **Process Switch**, tsela e ntlha ea site template e fetileng e tla ba etsa ho website ea ba ba ba bang.

Ba ba ka ba u sebelisa **Reset current template** ho fetola se le sefetang sa site ho se le teng ha ba batla ho fana ka ho fetola tsela, ha ho tsamaetsa tsela e fetileng, ho reset tsela e ka fetola bohlokoa ba site. Ka lebaka la hau, ba ba ba ka ba tsamaiswa haholo ha ba ba tseba hore o tla etsa se se fetang sa reset.

Tsamaetsa ho tsamaetsa option ea **Enable Domain Mapping**. Sena se tla ba leka ho ba le ka ho sebelisa domain e fetileng ka leano la network.

E ntse a ntsa ho tseba hore domain mapping e fetang eng ka tsela ea produk—ha ho ka fana ka ho hlokomela produk ha ho ba le ka ho fana ka ho sebelisa domain e fetileng.
