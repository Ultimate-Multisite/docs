# Go-boetsoho (v2) {#canceling-a-membership-v2}

_**SEKOLOSHO: Le tsela efe seo sefalo sa Ultimate Multisite v2.x.**_

Go-boetsoho (membership) ka morao wa ba ba ka ba ntlha ke sefalo sefalo.

Ha le user a batla go go-boetsoho, ba ba tlwaela ka ntlha ho **Account** page ya hae.

Ha ntlha eona, ba ba tla tsamaya ka head ho **Danger Zone**, le ba tla tsamaya ka **Delete Account**.

![Account page Danger Zone with Delete Account button](/img/account-page/danger-zone.png)

Module o tla bua go batla confirmation ya hae. Ba ba tla tsamaya ka toggle option ya **Confirm Account Deletion** le ba tla tsamaya ka **Delete Account**.

![Confirm Account Deletion dialog](/img/account-page/delete-account-confirm.png)

E tla ba ntlha ho site le go go-boetsoho wa hae ka netefana ya hau, le e tla faka site ya hae ka netefana ya hau.

![Logged out after account deletion](/img/account-page/logged-out-after-delete.png)

![Membership canceled confirmation](/img/account-page/membership-canceled.png)

**SEKOLOSHO:** Re sefalo, tsela ena e tla ba le go faka morao wa ba ka netefana ya hau, ka ntlha sefalo ka morao o tseba le morao o ne a fihla.

![Customer still exists in the network after cancellation](/img/admin/customer-after-cancel.png)

![Customer with no active memberships](/img/admin/memberships-customer-empty.png)
