# Ultimate Multisite {#translating-ultimate-multisite}

Ultimate Multisite o fetse ka ho fana ka English, empa plugin e le fetang ka ho fetola haholo, le re re tšoa ka ho ba le bone tsa fetola.

## Fetolao ea Plugin E Hlaloso {#core-plugin-translations}

Plugin e hlaloso ea Ultimate Multisite e fetola ka morao oa **WordPress.org translation platform**:

**[translate.wordpress.org/projects/wp-plugins/ultimate-multisite/](https://translate.wordpress.org/projects/wp-plugins/ultimate-multisite/)**

Kea se le setso sa fetolao sa WordPress e le sebetsi. Ha fetolao tsa fetola ho ba le tsela ea ho tsamaisa, ha di fetola ka morao o tla fana ka bone ka bohlokoa ba Ultimate Multisite ka mokhoa oa updates ea plugin ea WordPress.

**Re hloka thuso!** Ha u utloi le lero la English, re re bolela ka ho ba le bone tsa fetolao. Ho fetola litaba tse fapaneng ha li fetola haholo. Ho haosa:

1. Leha [translate.wordpress.org](https://translate.wordpress.org/projects/wp-plugins/ultimate-multisite/)
2. Uba le lero la hau
3. Thaba "Contribute Translation" (Fetola Fetolao)
4. O tla hloka account e fana ka WordPress.org

Ha u batla ho ba le bone tsa fetolao ea hau (o na le bohlokoa ba fetola), re re bolela ka [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite/discussions).

Ha u batla ho fetola tšobo ka lona le lona, ha ho batla ho fana ka mofuta o tla fana ka lona, ho na le dikgomo tse pedi:

### Ho sebelisa Poedit {#addon-translations}

U ka sebelisa application ea desktop **[Poedit](https://poedit.net)** ho fetola faili ea `.pot`:

1. Download Poedit ho [poedit.net](https://poedit.net)
2. Opana faili ea `.pot` e teng `wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite/lang/`
3. Fetola (translate) strings ka sehlopha sa Poedit
4. Ho hla -- Poedit o fetola faili ea `.po` le `.mo`

5. Setseba faili tse fetileng ka folder ea `lang`

### Ho sebelisa Loco Translate {#local-translations}

Plugin ea WordPress e ntse e teng **[Loco Translate](https://wordpress.org/plugins/loco-translate/)** e re ka ho fetola ka lona ka sehlopha sa admin haholo, ha u batla dikotla tsa desktop. E re ka ho fana le ho fetola le ho hlalosa (compile) faili ea translation ka lona.
