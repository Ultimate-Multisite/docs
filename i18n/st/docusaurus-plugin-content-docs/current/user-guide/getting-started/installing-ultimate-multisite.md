# Ho Kenya Ultimate Multisite {#installing-ultimate-multisite}

:::note
Tlhahlobo ena e nka hore o se kentse le ho hlophisa WordPress Multisite. Ho ithuta kamoo, sheba [tlhahlobo ena](https://www.wpbeginner.com/glossary/multisite/) ke WP Beginner.
:::

## Ho Kena Plugin {#installing-the-plugin}

Ultimate Multisite e fumaneha mahala ho [WordPress.org](https://wordpress.org/plugins/ultimate-multisite/).

Ho tloha ho **Network Admin Dashboard** ya hao, ea ho **Plugins → Add New Plugin**.

![Network Admin Add New Plugin page](/img/installation/add-new-plugin.png)

Batla "Ultimate Multisite" (ka liphaposi bakeng sa ho batana hantle) mme e tla hlaha e le sephetho sa pele. Tobetsa **Install Now**.

![Search results showing Ultimate Multisite](/img/installation/search-ultimate-multisite.png)

Hang ha e kentse, tobetsa **Network Activate** ho kenya plugin kaofela ho netwekh ya hao.

![Plugin installed with Network Activate button](/img/installation/plugin-installed.png)

Kamora hore e kene, o tla leqhoa ho Setup Wizard.

![Plugin activated and redirected to wizard](/img/installation/plugin-activated.png)

## Setup Wizard {#setup-wizard}

Setup Wizard e tla u tataisa ho hlophisa Ultimate Multisite ka metsotso e 10.

### Ho Amoheloa {#welcome}

Tobetsa **Get Started** ho qala.

![Setup Wizard welcome screen](/img/installation/wizard-welcome.png)

### Pre-install Checks {#pre-install-checks}

Mohato ona o hlahloba tlhaahloliso ya sistimi le ho kenya WordPress ho netefatsa hore e fihlela litlhoko tsa Ultimate Multisite. Haeba ntho tsohle di lokile, tobetsa **Go to the Next Step**.

![Pre-install checks showing system requirements](/img/installation/wizard-pre-install-checks.png)

:::note Network Activate button (v2.6.1+)
Haeba Ultimate Multisite e kentse empa ha e sa kenngoe netwekhong — mohlala, haeba o tobetsitse **Activate** (sebaka se le seng) ho fapana le **Network Activate** ho skrine ya dipheihine tsa netwekh, mohato wa Pre-install Checks o tla bona sena mme o bontshe konopo ya **Network Activate**.

Ho tobetsa **Network Activate** ho kenya plugin kaofela ho netwekh ya hao. Hang ha e kentse, wizard e tswela pele ka tsela e tlwaelehileng ho mohato wa ho kena. Ha ho hlokahale hore o tsoe ho wizard ho lokisa boemo ba ho kenya.
:::

### Ho Kena {#installation}

Ho kenya ho tla theha ditafole tsa database tse hlokolosetseng le ho kenya faele ya `sunrise.php` e leng se Ultimate Multisite e hlokang ho sebetsa. Tobetsa **Install** ho tswela pele.

![Installation step showing database tables and sunrise.php](/img/installation/wizard-installation.png)

### Khamphani Ya Hao {#your-company}

Tlatsa tlhaahloliso ya khamphani ya hao mme o beha chelete ya hao e loketsweng. Tlhaahloliso ena e tla sebeliswa kaofela ho setefala sa hao sa WaaS. Tobetsa **Continue** ha o qetile.

![Your Company configuration step](/img/installation/wizard-your-company.png)

### Litaba tsa Ho Qala {#default-content}

Mohato ona o u lumella hore o kenye dithemphele tse hlokolosetseng, dihlahiswa le litaba tse ling tsa ho qala. Hona ke tsela e ntle ya ho ithuta ka ditshebediso tsa Ultimate Multisite. Tobetsa **Install** ho eketsa ditaba tsa ho qala, kapa o tlohele mohato ona haeba o batla ho qala ho tswa ho zero.

![Default content installation step](/img/installation/wizard-default-content.png)

### Dipheihine tse Reketsoeng {#recommended-plugins}

O ka kenya dipheihine tsa motsoalle tse reketsweng. Tobetsa **Install** ho li eketsa kapa o tlohele ho tswela pele.

![Recommended plugins step](/img/installation/wizard-recommended-plugins.png)

### E Sebelisitse! {#ready}

Ke yona! Ho kenya Ultimate Multisite ha hao ho phethilwe. O ka qala ho haha setefala sa hao sa Website as a Service ho tloha ho **Network Admin Dashboard**.

![Setup complete - Ready screen](/img/installation/wizard-ready.png)

![Network Admin Dashboard with Ultimate Multisite active](/img/installation/network-dashboard.png)

Tsamaea, o ka qala ho sebetsa!
