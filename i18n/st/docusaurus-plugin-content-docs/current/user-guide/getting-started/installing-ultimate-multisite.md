# Ho Hloboha oa Ultimate Multisite

:::note
O lekhotso le sehlese sa tšoa hore o na le WordPress Multisite e ntsitsoe le e fana'a. Ho utloa hore o ka etsa hore, utloi [lekhotso le sehlese](https://www.wpbeginner.com/glossary/multisite/) ho lula le WP Beginner.
:::

## Hoholo ea Plugin

Ultimate Multisite e fana ka boholuo le [WordPress.org](https://wordpress.org/plugins/ultimate-multisite/).

Ho ba le **Network Admin Dashboard** ea hau, phela ho **Plugins → Add New Plugin**.

![Network Admin Add New Plugin page](/img/installation/add-new-plugin.png)

Hlola **"Ultimate Multisite"** (ho fana ka ho fetoha ho tsoalo) le e tla ba molao wa tsamaiso. Le tla tsamaea **Install Now**.

![Tlhahlobo ya tsamaiso e ntse e le ho ntlha ea Ultimate Multisite](/img/installation/search-ultimate-multisite.png)

Ha o se a tsoalo, u thabela **Network Activate** ho ba le ka ho fana ka plugin oa hau ka nako ya mofuta oa hau ka tsela efe.

![Plugin e ne tsohliswe le boikotso ba Network Activate](/img/installation/plugin-installed.png)

Ha o ikotso, ho tla go tsamaisa go bona Setup Wizard.

![Plugin e ne e tsohliswe le e ne e tsamaiswe ka wizard](/img/installation/plugin-activated.png)

## Setso ea Ho Setso (Setup Wizard)

Setso ea Ho Setso o tla u le tloha ho fana ka ho setso Ultimate Multisite ka nako e leng 10.

### Ho Tumelise (Welcome)

Hloka **Get Started** ho haholo ho haholo.

![Setup Wizard welcome screen](/img/installation/wizard-welcome.png)

### Lekalo la Ho Setso (Pre-install Checks)

Tse tsela tseo e fana ka ho leba litlhahiselo tsa sefapeng sa hau le ho installa WordPress ho bone hore li fetang bonnum ba Ultimate Multisite. Ha se ho bona hore ntho e ntse e teng, u thabela **Go to the Next Step**.

![Tšepo ya pele ho ba leboho bo hlophisoa ea tshedi e bontšang tsela e e hlophisang ea tsena](https://example.com/images/installation/wizard-pre-install-checks.png)

:::note Le butji la Network Activate (v2.6.1+)
Ha o ne Ultimate Multisite o ntswengwe, empa **ho ntse ho fihla le ho baetsoa ho network-activate** — re teng, ha u phethileng **Activate** (single-site) empa e ne u phethileng **Network Activate** ho se tsoalo ka morao o ntswengwe wa plugins — bo step la Pre-install Checks bo tla bona le bo tla fana ka butji la **Network Activate**.

Ho fana ka **Network Activate**, ho fana ka plugin ka tsela efe ea liti la hau lehle lehle (multisite network) ka tsela efe. Ha o fana, sefaka se fetoha sefetso se fetoha ka tsela efe. Ha u hloka ho tsamaea ka tsela efe ho abala sefetso sa fana.

### Ho lala (Installation)

Bo-installer o tla fana ka taba tsa database tse hlokang le ho ntsa lafile la `sunrise.php` le le hlophiso la Ultimate Multisite le ho ba le bonako. Leela **Install** ho laola.

![Tšepo ya ho faka (Installation step showing database tables and sunrise.php)](/img/installation/wizard-installation.png)

### Lopa ea lapa la hau (Your Company)

A re le fa motho ea lina la rona le ho fana ka mofuta oa tšepo ea lina. Lefa la hau le tla fana ka tsela efe ka sefapaneng sa WaaS platform ea hau. Le tla tsamaetsa **Continue** ha u feta.

![Your Company configuration step](/img/installation/wizard-your-company.png)

### Litsoetsoetso

Kea leboha la ho fana ka litsoetsoetso.

Tse tsela se se fana ka ho ntsa templates, products, le boikotso ba ho ntsa. Ke tsela e teng ho u rata fa u utloile litlhahlo tsa Ultimate Multisite. Leha u tsamisa **Install** ho ntsa boikotso ba bohlokoa, kapa u tsamae ka tsela ena ha u batla ho ntsa se se fana ka ho ntsa.

![Tlhahlobo ya tšhobo ya tshediso](/img/installation/wizard-default-content.png)

### Plugini e tlamehang

Ho na le bohloko, ulela plugini tse di tlamehang ho ba le. Leela **Install** ho li fana ka kapa o se a leka ho fola ka ho tsamaea.

![Step ya boitshwaro ba plugin a fetang](/img/installation/wizard-recommended-plugins.png)

### A re!

Kea! Ho fihla! Ho fihla lehle ho fihla la Ultimate Multisite haholo. U ka sahlola ho fana ka Website as a Service platform oa hau ka **Network Admin Dashboard**.

![Setup complete - Ready screen](/img/installation/wizard-ready.png)

![Dashboard ea Network Admin le Ultimate Multisite e le lehle](/img/installation/network-dashboard.png)

Letse ho fana ka bonako!
