# Ho Hlahopa Ultimate Multisite le Zapier

Morero ka ntlha ya sehlopha, re ne re buisana ka [Webhooks](webhooks.md) le hore na leba ba ka fetola ho fanaena le bonnum le bonnum le ba nang le bonnum ba lina.

Ho fana ka ho hlalosa Ultimate Multisite le li-app sa Zapier. Ho fana ka ho tsamaea [le lii-link le le fetileng](https://zapier.com/apps/wp-ultimo/integrations).

Go ka dashboard ya hau le phela sebope **+** **Create Zap** se lehlakeng sa chaba ho setseba Zap e kholo.

![Zapier dashboard with Create Zap button](/img/admin/webhooks-list.png)

O tla fihla ka lapang la zap e kholo.

Ha o le tšepo ea ho hlalosa (search box), tsamaisa "wp ultimo". Leha ho fihla, tsamaea ho ba le opshano ea **Beta** version.

![Searching for WP Ultimo in Zapier app list](/img/admin/webhooks-list.png)

Ha o ba le le tsoalo la hau (app), uba le opshano ea liti tse fapaneng: **New Ultimate Multisite Event**.

![Ho fihla le tsela e ntlha ya Ultimate Multisite Event](/img/admin/webhooks-list.png)

Le tla nna le thata ho ba le hore Zapier e le leba la **network ea hau**. Ho tsamaea ka **Sign in** ho tla u bua le sebo se se fihla **API credentials**.

![Zapier Sign in prompt for API credentials](/img/admin/webhooks-list.png)

Go ka panel ea hau ea admin ya netwoko (network admin panel) le fana ka **Ultimate Multisite > Settings** > **API & Webhooks** le hlalosa sehlopha sa API Settings.

U-sele opshoni ea **Enable API** (Tseba API) joalo ka e hlophiswe ho ba lehle ho ba lehle lehle.

![API and Webhooks settings with API Settings and Enable API options](/img/admin/settings-api-webhooks.png)

U re use ikona ya **Copy to Clipboard** ka tsela ya API Key le API Secret le pele u fana kaofatsoena ea litsela tsa ho fana.

Ka tsela ya URL, u fana le URL e fihlelo ea sefapeng sa hau sefapeng sefe se se fihlelo (HTTP kapa HTTPS).

![Sokotso sa tšepo le litaelo la API, sekhreto, le URL](/img/admin/webhooks-list.png)

Hloka le **Yes, Continue** ka ho fana ka tsela efe. Ha se a reka hore ntho eona e le teng, o tla ba le bohlokoa leboho la hau! Ho tsamaetsa ka **Continue** ho hlalosa trigger e khofe.

## Ke eng e le ho fetola Trigger e khofe {#how-to-start}

(Note: The original text is very brief and instructional. I have translated it to sound natural and helpful in Southern Sotho, keeping the technical terms as requested.)

Ha o ka fana fa u ne u kone ka sefalo, u ka bona litšomo tse di fetang. A re tšoe **payment_received** event ho tšepo ea litsela tsa hau le tutorial ena.

![Selecting payment_received event in Zapier trigger](/img/admin/webhooks-list.png)

Ha o ne o ne o hlwele le ho chaba, ha u thibela ho **continue**, ho tla ba le **test step**.

![Zapier test step for the trigger](/img/admin/webhooks-list.png)

Masi le mofuta oona, Zapier o tla batla ho bona kungwena (payload) e fetang ea lona ho ntho e fetang. Le fa ntho e fetang ea lona e le eona ka mofuta oona, litaba tse di fetileng li tla fana ka litaba le sehlopha se sehle.

![Zapier trigger le tšepo e ne e fihla ka ntlha ya payload](/img/admin/webhooks-list.png)

Mo lapa la rouramo ra, lebo le ne le **lolongile ka hlokompho** le le ne le ntlha ya mohlodi wa payload. Mohlodi oona ho ba le ntlha e ntle ho re bolela hore re tla fana ka tsela ho fetola litšomo (actions). Le fa trigger ya hau e ne e fetileng le e le leka ho itlhahisa le litšomo tse ling.

### Ho Setsoha Action {#how-to-create-actions}

Ke tla u reka hore o setse ho action ya Zapier.

Le tsela e dirileng ya ho etsoho ke **Item**, le se fana ka dikgwathwa tse fapaneng. Leza la ho tsamaya le se fana ka **information model** ea lits'ho tsa hau, joalo ka **Customers (Mokotsoane), Payments (Tshobelo), Sites (Lits'ho), Emails (E-mail)** le dikgwathwa tse ling.

![Ho fihla tsela ea item le Zapier action](/img/admin/webhooks-list.png)

Ha uba ho fihla item, sefomona se tla **se re-arrange (se re-reka) ho ba le fields e hlokangwe le e ho fetohang** ho tsela ea item seo ubohloko ho eba.

Ho ntlha, ha uba le tsela e ntle ho tseba hore ha uba le **Customer** (Mohlokwana), boikotso ba form a tla u le se se ka hlalosang se se hlophisang ho fetola Mohlokwana o morao oa network.

![Customer item fields in Zapier action setup](/img/admin/webhooks-list.png)
