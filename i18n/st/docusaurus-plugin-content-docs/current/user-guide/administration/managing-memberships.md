# Ho Pele Mofumo (Managing Memberships)

Ke Ultimate Multisite, u ka itse le mofumo (memberships) le ho baithoa ka tsela efe ea setso sa hau sehle. U ka hlalosa le ho lula tafatsa tsa mofumo (subscriptions) joalo ka plans le products, sefapeng sa sites, sefapeng sa tšepo (billing amount) le tšepo (frequency), litšomo tsa tšepo (payment history), le timestamps.

Ho ba ka ho fihla pele le lapa la **Memberships** le menu ea Ultimate Multisite.

![Memberships list page](/img/admin/memberships-list.png)

Ka lapa la Memberships, u ka bona litšomo tsa boikotso (customers) le products joalo le mofumo joalo le accounts ea bona. U ka fana ka ho fihla mofumo ka tsela efe: active (ho baetso), pending (ho baithoa), on hold (ho baithoa), expired (ho baetsi), kapa cancelled (ho baithoa).

Mofumo oa boikotso o kholo (Free memberships) o baithoa ka mofumo oa **lifetime** (ho le tšepo ea ho fihla). Ha e na le lapa la ho baetso le ha e baetsi ka ho automatically. Ha plan e kholo e tla fihla ha sefapeng sa ho baetso kapa nako e fetang, u ka u sebelisa product e kholo e na le trial, u ka u baithoa mofumo ka ho manually (manually cancel the membership), kapa u ka u mala boikotso oa hau ka plan efe ha access ea hae e fetoha.

U ka clique ka mofumo o kholo ho fihla pele le lapa le ka ho hlalosa litšomo tsa mofumo — lapa la tšepo (billing info), sefapeng sa site kapa limits tsa boikotso, le ba le tseba.

![Membership edit interface](/img/admin/membership-edit.png)

Kea le ho bona ka ho fihla pele la lapa la ho hlalosa mofumo:

![Membership edit full interface](/img/admin/membership-edit-full.png)

U ka u mala mofumo tse ntlha joalo le boikotso ba ba baithoa ha ba ba baithoa ka tsela efe ea hau. U ka clique **Add Membership** (Ba mala Mofumo) le ho hlalosa litšomo tsa bona.

![Add Membership modal](/img/admin/membership-add-modal.png)

Kea le ho clique **Create Membership** (Baala Mofumo). Mofumo o ntlha o tla ba fihla ka lapa la mofumo.
