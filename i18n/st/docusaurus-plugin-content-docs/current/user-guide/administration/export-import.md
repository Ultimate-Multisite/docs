# Ho Hloboha le Ho Fetola (Export & Import)

Kea re bua ka hore o fetolela le ho hloba data. Kea se tsoalo kae e le eng, ke tla go reka ka lina le lina.

**Ho Hloba Data (Export)**

Ho hloba data ho batla hore u bone data ea hau ka morao. Ho ba le ntlha eeng e ntle ho hloba data:

*   U batla hore u fetole la WordPress ka sefalo se sefalo (backup).
*   U batla hore u tsamaisa data ea hau ka platform a hau e fapaneng.

Ho hloba data ke lefa le lehlopha ho ba le lehle:

1.  **WordPress Backup:** Kea re fetola WordPress ea hau ka sefalo se sefalo (backup).
2.  **Data Export:** Ho hloba data ka lina le lina, u tla bona e le eng ka morao.

**Ho Fetola Data (Import)**

Ho fetola data ho batla hore u tsamaisa data ea hau ka platform a hau e fapaneng. Kea se tsoalo kae e le eng:

*   U batla hore u tsamaisa data ea hau ka WooCommerce kapa platform a hau e fapaneng.
*   U batla hore u fetole la WordPress ka data e fetileng.

Ho fetola data ke lefa le lehlopha ho ba le lehle:

1.  **Data Import:** Ho tsamaisa data ea hau ka WooCommerce kapa platform a hau e fapaneng.
2.  **WordPress Data Restore:** Ho fetole la WordPress ka data e fetileng.

***

### Ho Hloba Data (Export) {#required-permissions}

Ho hloba data ho batla hore u bone data ea hau ka morao. Kea se tsoalo kae e le eng, ke tla go reka ka lina le lina.

**1. Ho Hloba WordPress Backup:**
Kea re fetola WordPress ea hau ka sefalo se sefalo (backup). Sena se fana ka ho ba le lehle le ho tsamaisa data ka morao.

**2. Ho Hloba Data Export:**
Ho hloba data ka lina le lina, u tla bona e le eng ka morao. Kea se tsoalo kae e le eng, ke tla go reka ka lina le lina.

***

### Ho Fetola Data (Import) {#exporting-a-site-to-a-zip}

Ho fetola data ho batla hore u tsamaisa data ea hau ka platform a hau e fapaneng. Kea se tsoalo kae e le eng:

**1. Data Import:**
Ho tsamaisa data ea hau ka WooCommerce kapa platform a hau e fapaneng.

**2. WordPress Data Restore:**
Ho fetole la WordPress ka data e fetileng.

Ultimate Multisite 2.9.0 e ntse le **Export & Import** (Hlaka le Hlobo) se se fapaneng ka morao wa **Tools > Export & Import**. U sebelisa lehle seo u hloka ho ba leba site ya WordPress ka ZIP file, ho phela (restore) ZIP eona, kapa ho hloba site ho a mathata ka lefatshe la Ultimate Multisite le single-site WordPress.

## Tlhaloso ea tsela e hlophisang {#what-the-export-includes}

Kea lebo la ho ba le litšomo tse hlophisang (Required permissions). Kea leba lehlopha la ho ba le litšomo tse hlophisang.

Ho hloka ho fana ka tsamaiso ea admin o ka ba lehle menu ea **Tools** ea WordPress ea sehlopha se tla export kapa import. Ho leka multisite, u sebelisa account ea admin ea boleng (network administrator) ha u ba lehle ho export kapa import subsites ho tsoalo la Ultimate Multisite tools ea boleng.

1. Morero ka admin ya WordPress ea se o batlang ho kopanya, u leka **Tools > Export & Import**.
2. A lebisa sebo sa export (export area) u utloi sebo seo u batlang ho ba lehle.

3. Ho fana ka tsela e fetang le seo u ka ba le ho fana ka molao, ka ntlha le mofuta o fana ka molao, le mofuta o fana ka molao, ha ho fana.

4. Laola ho fana le ho tsamaea le ho tloha ho fihla hore se se fihle. Sites tse dintsi mo thuto a ka na le ho fihla ho fihla ho thuto ha ZIP a ka ba le ho fihla.

5. Leha ZIP efe le fetileng ho tšoa ho lona sebopeletso (exports list).

Ke fana ka ZIP le lona ka leka le lefetso. Ke hloka ho e tsamisa ka leka le lefetso le lefetso, le lefetso la sebopeletso, litaba tsa media, le code a tšoa.

## Le fa ZIP efe le fetileng {#importing-a-site-from-a-zip}

ZIP efe le fetileng ho ba le:

Mofuta oa database le bohlokoa ba sefapaneng.
Lihlobo la media le li-file tse di-uploadileng ha ho fana ka tsela efe.
Plugins le themes ha ho fana ka tsela efe ha ho fanoa.

Ho fana ka metadata e o fetang ho fetola le site ea hau ka molao o tla hlokomela ho tsamaea ka installation ea tsoalo.

Sebaka sa lero la ZIP se fetoga ka lebaka la media, plugins le themes seo u fetang, le lero la tables ea database ea site.
