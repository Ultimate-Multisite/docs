# Le Tlhaloso ho Dashboard ea Ultimate Multisite {#understanding-the-ultimate-multisite-dashboard}

Ha re lehle ka ho fana ka tsela e fetang le litlhahisaho tsa Ultimate Multisite, ha re lehle ka dashboard.

Joalo ka plugins ea WordPress ba ba ba bang, Ultimate Multisite e fetola entry ea menu ea level ea network e ntse e bolelang **Ultimate Multisite** le setlo la sub-menu sefalo.

## Widgets ea Dashboard ea Network {#network-dashboard-widgets}

Ultimate Multisite e fana ka widgets tse dintsi tse noe tsa dashboard ea network. Dashboard ena e le ho hlokomisa ka tsela ea **My Sites → Network Admin → Dashboard**. Widgets e fetang ho fana ka ho ba le ho ba le ho ba leho la litsela le litlhahisaho tse kholo ho admin ea network.

![Network Dashboard with Ultimate Multisite widgets](/img/admin/network-dashboard-full.png)

### Setso sa Ho Hlae (First Steps) {#first-steps}

![First Steps widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-first-steps.png)

Widget ena e le ho ba le ka tsela ea ho hlae ea Ultimate Multisite le e uena ho re admin ea network a se fetile ho ba le litšepo tse ling, joalo ka ho setsoha metheo ea tshepo le ho fetola account ea leresetso.

### Summary (Motsamaisi) {#summary}

![Summary widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-summary.png)

Widget ea Summary e rekaetsa sehlopha sa signups le litšepo tsa bona tse fapaneng ka mofuta oa shamoei. MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) e rekaetsa tsela ea litsela tse nts'o ho ba leho leboha le bohlokoa ba li-customer le litšepo tse fetang.

### Activity Stream (Lekalo la Ho Ba Le) {#activity-stream}

![Activity Stream widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-activity-stream.png)

Widget ena e rekaetsa litšepo tse fapaneng tsa Ultimate Multisite. Litšepo tse bao ka ho ba leho, joalo ka signups, cancellations, site creation, deletion, le litšepo tse ling.

Ho hlokomisa sections ea Events ea documentation ho bona lehlopha la ho rekaetsa.

### Ho Ba Le (Right Now) {#right-now}

![Right Now widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-right-now.png)

Le widget o this utility e tla ntlha le tsamaiso seletso sa botshelo ba batho le sites ka network. Le tšho tsa mofuta a tšho tse di tšoa ka teng li le tšho ya go dira sites of users ka one-click.

### News and Discussions (Dikarolo le Ditšepo) {#news-and-discussions}

![News and Discussions widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-news.png)

Le widget o thisa le o tšoa dikarolo tsa Ultimate Multisite tse ntlha. Le tšho ya go tsamaetsa ka le widget eona ho bona litlhahlo tsa tšhobo, bugs tse di fetang, le diketso tsa bohlokoa.

## Ultimate Multisite Dashboard (Tšho ya Dashboard ea Ultimate Multisite) {#ultimate-multisite-dashboard}

Ho ntlha le dashboard ea network e tšoa litlhahlo tse fapaneng ka network, dashboard ea Ultimate Multisite (e tšoa ka morao o tla ea menu ea Ultimate Multisite) e tšoa litlhahlo tsa sehlopha.

![Ultimate Multisite Dashboard](/img/admin/um-dashboard-top.png)

### Monthly Recurring Revenue Growth (Tšho ya Tšhobo e Tšoa Ka Mele) {#monthly-recurring-revenue-growth}

Chart ea MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) e tšoa le tšoa ea tšhobo ka mele ka nako ea calendar ea 12 months.

![MRR Growth chart](/img/admin/um-dashboard-mrr.png)

Tšhobo e ntlha e tsamaiswa ka blue, le diketso di fetang di tsamaiswa ka red.

### Date-range Support (Supeletso ea Nako) {#date-range-support}

Ka tsela e fapaneng ea batho ba kholo le ho etsa injamelo, statistics e ka leba. Supeletso ea nako e tšoa ka dashboard e tšoa ka mofuta o tla a tšoa litlhahlo le nako tse hlokang.

![Date range selector and stats](/img/admin/um-dashboard-date-range.png)

Supeletso ea nako e tsamaiswa le nako ea litlhahlo tse di tšoa ke widgets tse fapaneng ka bona, ho fihla tšhobo, injamelo tse ntlha, injamelo tsa nansa, injamelo tsa lethu, le sites tse di leboha.

![Dashboard statistics widgets](/img/admin/um-dashboard-stats.png)

:::tip
Ho ba lehle le widget ea **Most Visited Sites**, tsamaetsa ho etsa visit counting ka **Ultimate Multisite → Settings → Sites → Enable Visits Limitation and Counting**.
:::
