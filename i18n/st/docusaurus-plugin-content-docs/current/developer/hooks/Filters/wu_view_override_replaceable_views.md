# Sefe: wu_view_override_replaceable_views {#filter-wuviewoverridereplaceableviews}

E lumella bahlahisi ho eketsa difoldara tse ding lenaneng le ka nkelwang sebaka.

E-ba hlokolosi, hobane ho dumella difoldara tse ding ho ka etsa hore dikopi tse seng di fetilwe ke nako di jarwe ho ena le mefuta ya Ultimate Multisite.

## Dipharamitha {#parameters}

| Lebitso | Mofuta | Tlhaloso |
|------|------|-------------|
| $replaceable_views | `array` | Lenane la difoldara tse dumelletsweng. |

- 2.0.0
