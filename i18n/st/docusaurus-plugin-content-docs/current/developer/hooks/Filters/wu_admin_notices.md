# Sefo: wu_admin_notices

Dumella bahlahisi ho sefa ditsebiso tsa admin tse ekeditsweng ke Ultimate Multisite.

## Dipharamitha

| Lebitso | Mofuta | Tlhaloso |
|------|------|-------------|
| $notices | `array` | Lenane la ditsebiso bakeng sa phanele eo e itseng. |
| $all_notices | `array` | Lenane la ditsebiso tse ekeditsweng, tse arolletsweng ka phanele. |
| $panel | `string` | Phanele eo ho ka fumanwang ditsebiso ho yona. |
| $filter | `string` | Haeba ditsebiso tse ka tloswang di se di sefilwe. |
| $dismissed_messages | `array` | Lenane la dinotlolo tsa ditsebiso tse tlositsweng. |

### Ho tloha

- 2.0.0
### Mohlodi

E hlalositswe ho [`inc/class-admin-notices.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite/blob/main/inc/class-admin-notices.php#L121) moleng wa 121
