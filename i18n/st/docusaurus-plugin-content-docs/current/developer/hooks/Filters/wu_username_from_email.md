# Sefe: wu_username_from_email {#filter-wuusernamefromemail}

Sefa lebitso la mosebedisi la moreki e motjha.

## Dipharaemitha {#parameters}

| Lebitso | Mofuta | Tlhaloso |
|------|------|-------------|
| $username | `string` | Lebitso la mosebedisi la moreki. |
| $email | `string` | Aterese ya email ya moreki e motjha. |
| $new_user_args | `array` | Array ya args tsa mosebedisi e motjha, mohlomong e kenyeletsa mabitso a pele le a ho qetela. |
| $suffix | `string` | Kenya string qetellong ya lebitso la mosebedisi ho le etsa le ikgethileng. |
