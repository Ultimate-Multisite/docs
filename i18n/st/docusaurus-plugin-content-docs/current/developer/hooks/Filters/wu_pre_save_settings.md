# Sefe: wu_pre_save_settings

Dumella bahlahisi ho sefa ditlhophiso pele di bolokwa ke Ultimate Multisite.

## Dipharametha {#parameters}

| Lebitso | Mofuta | Tlhaloso |
|------|------|-------------|
| $settings | `array` | Ditlhophiso tse tla bolokwa. |
| $settings_to_save | `array` | Ditlhophiso tse ntjha tse tla eketswa. |
| $saved_settings | `array` | Ditlhophiso tsa hajwale tse bolokilweng. |

### Ho tloha ka {#since}

- 2.0.18
