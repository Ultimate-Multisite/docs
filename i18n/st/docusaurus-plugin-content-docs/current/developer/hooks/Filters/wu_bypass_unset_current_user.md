# Filter: wu_bypass_unset_current_user {#filter-wubypassunsetcurrentuser}

Dumella bahlahisi ho potoloha khoutu ya ho hlakola mosebedisi wa jwale.

Ho kgutlisa eng kapa eng ntle le null ho tla potoloha ho hlakolwa ha mosebedisi wa jwale ya keneng. Sena se ka ba molemo maemong a mang, ka mohlala, ha ho sebetsanwa le dibaka tse ka tlasa tse sebediswang e le diphanele tsa taolo.

## Dipharamitha {#parameters}

| Lebitso | Mofuta | Tlhaloso |
|------|------|-------------|
| $null_or_bypass | `mixed` | Null ho tswela pele, eng kapa eng e nngwe ho e potoloha. |
| $current_user | `false\|\WP_User` | Ntho ya mosebedisi wa jwale. |
