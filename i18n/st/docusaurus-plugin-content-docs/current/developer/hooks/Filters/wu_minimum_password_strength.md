# Sefa: wu_minimum_password_strength

Sefa matla a tlase a phasewete a hlokahalang (lintlha tsa zxcvbn).

Maemo a matla: - 0, 1: E fokola haholo - 2: E fokola - 3: E mahareng - 4: E matla (ya kamehla)

## Dipharamitha {#parameters}

| Lebitso | Mofuta | Tlhaloso |
|------|------|-------------|
| $min_strength | `int` | Boemo ba matla bo tlase bo hlokahalang. |
| $strength_setting | `string` | Boleng ba tlhophiso ya admin (medium, strong, super_strong). |
