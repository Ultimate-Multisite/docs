# Sefe: wu_minimum_password_length

Sefa bolelele bo bonyenyane ba phasewete.

E qobelloa feela ha wu_enforce_password_rules e le true.

## Dipharamitha {#parameters}

| Lebitso | Mofuta | Tlhaloso |
|------|------|-------------|
| $min_length | `int` | Bolelele bo bonyenyane ba phasewete. Default 12 (e tsamaisana le Defender Pro). |
| $defender_active | `bool` | Hore na Defender Pro Strong Password e sebetsa. |

### Ho tloha ka {#since}

- 2.4.0
