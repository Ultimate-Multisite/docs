# Sefe: wu_checkout_form_base_domains

E sefa didomain tsa motheo tse arolelanoang tsa checkout-form tse sa lokelang ho fetoha lirekoto tsa mapped-domain.

Sebelisa sefe ena ha integration e fana ka didomain tse ling tsa motheo bakeng sa masimo a **Site URL** a foromo ea checkout. Didomain tse khutlisitsoeng ke sefe ena li tšoaroa e le baamoheli ba ngoliso ba arolelanoang ho e-na le didomain tse ikhethileng tsa site ka ’ngoe.

## Dipharametha {#parameters}

| Lebitso | Mofuta | Tlhaloso |
|------|------|-------------|
| $domains | `array` | Didomain tsa motheo tse arolelanoang tse bokelletsoeng ho tsoa ho tlhophiso ea foromo ea checkout. |

### Ho tloha {#since}

- 2.13.0

### Mohloli {#source}

E hlalositsoe ho `inc/functions/domain.php`.


## Dikgutliso {#returns}

Array ea didomain tsa motheo tsa checkout-form tse tloaelehileng.
