# Sefe: wu_get_site_domain_and_path

Dumella bahlahisi ho fetola dipara tsa domain/tsela.

Sena se ka ba molemo bakeng sa dintho tse mmalwa, jwalo ka ho kenya tshebetsong tharollo e itseng ya staging, di-server tse fapaneng, jj.

## Dipharamitha {#parameters}

| Lebitso | Mofuta | Tlhaloso |
|------|------|-------------|
| $d | `object` | Object ya jwale e nang le dinotlolo tsa domain le tsela. |
| $path_or_subdomain | `string` | Tsela/subdomain ya pele e fetisitsweng ho function. |

### Ho tloha {#since}

- 2.0.0
### Mohlodi {#source}

E hlalositswe ho [`inc/functions/site.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite/blob/main/inc/functions/site.php#L235) moleng wa 235


## E kgutlisa {#returns}
Object e nang le dinotlolo tsa domain le tsela.
