# Sefe: wu_sso_enabled

Kgontsha/tima bokgoni ba single-sign-on pakeng tsa domain.

Sefa boleng bona ho tima single-sign-on ka botlalo, kapa ho e kgontsha ka maemo ho e-na le hoo.

## Dipharamitha {#parameters}

| Lebitso | Mofuta | Tlhaloso |
|------|------|-------------|
| $enabled | `bool` | Na SSO e lokela ho kgontshwa? True bakeng sa ho bulela, false-ish bakeng sa ho tima. |

### Ho tloha {#since}

- 2.0.11
### Mohlodi {#source}

E hlalositsoe ho [`inc/sso/class-sso.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite/blob/main/inc/sso/class-sso.php#L110) moleng wa 110


## Tse kgutliswang {#returns}
Hore na SSO e kgontshitswe kapa tjhe.
