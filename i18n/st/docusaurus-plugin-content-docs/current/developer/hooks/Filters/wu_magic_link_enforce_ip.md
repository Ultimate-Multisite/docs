# Sefe: wu_magic_link_enforce_ip {#filter-wumagiclinkenforceip}

Sefa hore na ho tiisetsoa netefatso ya aterese ya IP.

Beha ho false ho dumella tokens ho sebetsa ho tswa dinetwekeng tse fapaneng. Sena se fokotsa tshireletso empa se eketsa bonolo ba tshebediso (mohl., bakeng sa basebedisi ba mobile ba fetolang dinetweke).

## Dipharamitha {#parameters}

| Lebitso | Mofuta | Tlhaloso |
|------|------|-------------|
| $enforce | `bool` | Hore na ho tiisetsoa ho tshwana ha aterese ya IP. |

- 2.0.0
