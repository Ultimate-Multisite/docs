# Sefe: wu_stripe_generate_idempotency_key

E sefa boleng ba idempotency_key bo romelloang le dikgetho tsa tefiso ya Stripe.

## Dipharamitha {#parameters}

| Lebitso | Mofuta | Tlhaloso |
|------|------|-------------|
| $idempotency_key | `string` | Boleng ba senotlolo sa idempotency. |
| $args | `array` | Diargument tse sebediswang ho thusa ho hlahisa senotlolo. |
| $context | `string` | Moelelo oo senotlolo sa idempotency se hlahiswang tlasa wona. |
