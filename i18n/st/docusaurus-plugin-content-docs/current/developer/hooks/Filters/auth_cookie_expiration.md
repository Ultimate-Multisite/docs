# Sefe: auth_cookie_expiration

E sefa bolelele ba nako ya ho fela ha cookie ya netefatso.

## Dipharamitha {#parameters}

| Lebitso | Mofuta | Tlhaloso |
|------|------|-------------|
| $length | `int` | Bolelele ba nako ya ho fela ka metsotswana. |
| $user_id | `int` | ID ya mosebedisi. |
| $remember | `bool` | Hore na ho hopolwe ho kena ha mosebedisi. Ya kamehla ke false. |

### Ho tloha ka {#since}

- 2.8.0
