# Sefe: wu_get_checkout_variables

Dumella bahlahisi ba ditlatsetso ho sefa disete tsa pele tsa leqephe la ho lefa.

Hlokomela, dinotlolo tse haellang di ka senya ka ho feletseng tshebetso ya ho lefa karolong e ka pele.

## Dipharaemetha {#parameters}

| Lebitso | Mofuta | Tlhaloso |
|------|------|-------------|
| $variables | `array` | Diphetoho tse lokalaisitsweng. |
| $checkout | `\Checkout` | Sehlopha sa ho lefa. |

### Ho tloha ka {#since}

- 2.0.0
### Mohlodi {#source}

E hlalositswe ho [`inc/checkout/class-checkout.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite/blob/main/inc/checkout/class-checkout.php#L1970) moleng wa 1970


## E kgutlisa {#returns}
