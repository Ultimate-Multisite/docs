# Material WP {#material-wp}

Sebopeho sa tsamaiso sa WordPress se bile se tšoana ka nako e telele. Empa na o kile oa ipotsa hore na mofuta oa Material Design oa sebopeho sa tsamaiso o ka shebahala joang? 

Material WP ke theme ea tsamaiso e ntlafalitsoeng ho u fa ponahalo le maikutlo ao, e u thusa ho fa bareki ba hao Dashboard e ikhethileng haholoanyane.

## Likarolo {#features}

- Tšehetso ea Custom CSS
- Litlhophiso tsa setaele tse bonolo
- Ho hlophisa lenane hape
- Ho kenya/ho romela ha bonolo

## Litokomane {#documentation}

**Ho iketsetsa Admin Panel ea hao ka Material WP**

## Ho kenya {#installation}

1. Kenya 'material-wp' bukeng ea '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Kenya tshebetsong plugin ka lenane la 'Li-plugin' ho WordPress
