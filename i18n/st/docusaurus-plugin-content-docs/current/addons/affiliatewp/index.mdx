# AffiliateWP {#affiliatewp}

Multisite Ultimate: Kopanyo ya AffiliateWP

Sebedisa AffiliateWP e matla ho hodisa setsi sa bareki sa Ultimo Network ya hao!

## Ho kenya {#installation}

1. Kenya 'ultimate-multisite-git ' ho directory ya '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Kenya se-plugin tshebetsong ka menu ya 'Li-plugin' ho WordPress
3. Latela Wizard mohato ka mohato ho seta se-plugin

## Tataiso ya Tshebediso {#usage-guide}

[AffiliateWP](https://affiliatewp.com/) e se e le tharollo e sebediswang haholo ha ho ahuwa lenaneo la affiliate bakeng sa kgwebo ya hao ya inthaneteng. Ultimate Multisite: AffiliateWP ke borokgo bo tla o nolofalletsa ho aha ntho e tshwanang bakeng sa marangrang a hao a maemo a hodimo.

## Ho qala {#getting-started}

Pele ho tsohle, o **hloka ho ba le** [**AffiliateWP**](https://affiliatewp.com/) **e sebetsang**! O ke ke wa kgona ho sebedisa tlatsetso ya rona haeba o sena **se-plugin se kopanang le yona**. Haeba o sa rera ho dumella bareki ba hao ho e sebedisa disaeteng tsa bona le bona, AffiliateWP e lokela ho sebetsa feela saeteng ya hao e ka sehloohong.

Hape, o tla hloka hore **Ultimate Multisite** le **Ultimate Multisite: AffiliateWP** ka bobedi di **kenngwe tshebetsong** **marangrang ohle**.

## Ho eketsa Ultimate Multisite jwalo ka Kopanyo ya AffiliateWP {#adding-ultimate-multisite-as-an-affiliatewp-integration}

Saeteng ya hao e ka sehloohong, eya ho Di-setting tsa Affiliates, mme tabeng ya Dikopanyo, tshwaya Ultimate Multisite. Sena se tla bolella [AffiliateWP](https://affiliatewp.com/) hore e qale ho latela diketelo le diphetoho tse tswang mokgweng wa ho ingodisa wa Ultimate Multisite.

<!-- Screenshot ha e fumanehe: tab ya di-setting tsa Dikopanyo tsa AffiliateWP e nang le lebokose la ho tshwaya la Ultimate Multisite -->

_Tshwaya lebokose la Ultimate Multisite tlase mme o boloke_

Ke hoo feela hoo ho hlokahalang. Diketelo tse tswang mahokelong a referral di tla latelwa, mme ha mosebedisi eo hamorao a etsa tefo ya hae ya pele, phetoho e tla thehwa mme khomishene e tla behellwa ka thoko bakeng sa affiliate ya ikarabellang bakeng sa phetoho eo.

Leha ho le jwalo, ho tla latelwa feela tefo ya pele e amohetsweng ho tswa ho mosebedisi ya sa tswa thehwa. Ho nolofalletsa ditefo tsa referral tse iphetang, o tla hloka [AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals Add-on](https://affiliatewp.com/add-ons/pro/recurring-referrals/)

## Ho nolofalletsa Di-referral tse Iphetang {#enabling-recurring-referrals}

Kamora ho kenya [AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals Add-on](https://affiliatewp.com/add-ons/pro/recurring-referrals/), eya tabeng ya Di-setting tsa Affiliates tsa Recurring Referrals, mme o nolofalletse tshebediso ya di-referral tse iphetang.

<!-- Screenshot ha e fumanehe: di-setting tsa AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals tse nang le switch ya ho bulela Enable Recurring Referrals -->

_Ho bonolo jwalo: Bulela Recurring Referrals mme tsohle di lokile!_

Hape, ke hoo feela ho hlokahalang!

Jwale, nako le nako ha tefo e ntjha e amohelwa akhaonteng e rometsweng ka referral, affiliate ya ikarabellang e tla fumana karolo ya yona ka boyona!
