# VAT

Multisite Ultimate: VAT ya Yuropa

E eketsa dikarolo le disebediswa tse ding ho tshwaro ya kamehla ya makgetho ya Multisite Ultimate ho tshehetsa VAT ya Yuropa hamolemo.
E eketsa ka boiketsetso ditefello tsa VAT tsa EU leqepheng la Ditefello tsa Lekgetho ho tloha

## Ho kenya {#installation}

1. Kenya 'ultimate-multisite-vat' ho directory ya '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Kenya polaka tshebetsong ka menu ya 'Plugins' ho WordPress
3. Eya ho Ultimate Multisite > Ditlhophiso > Makgetho mme o bulele EU VAT.
4. Tobetsa ho Ditefello tsa Lekgetho mme o Ntjhafatse Ditefello tsa VAT tsa EU tlase, ebe o Boloka Ditefello tsa Lekgetho.
5. Ho fedile!
