# Sefe: wu_connector_enforcement_option_keys

Sefa lenane la linotlolo tsa khetho tsa mofani oa AI tse qobelloang ho tsoa sebakeng se seholo.

Kenya linotlolo tsa khetho bakeng sa plugin tsa bafani ba AI ba tloaelitsoeng kapa ba mokha oa boraro hore litlhophiso tsa tsona le tsona li futswe ho tsoa sebakeng se seholo ho li-subsite.

## Dipharamitha {#parameters}

| Lebitso | Mofuta | Tlhaloso |
|------|------|-------------|
| $keys | `string[]` | Mabitso a linotlolo tsa khetho a lokelang ho qobelloa (a se a kenyelletsa linotlolo tsa connector tse fumanoeng ka boiketsetso le EXTRA_PROVIDER_OPTIONS). |

### Ho tloha ka {#since}

- 1.2.0
