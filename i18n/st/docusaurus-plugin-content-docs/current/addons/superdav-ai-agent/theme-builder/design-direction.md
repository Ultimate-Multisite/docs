# Tataiso ea Moralo {#design-direction}

Mohato oa **Tataiso ea Moralo** o u lumella ho hlahloba, ho ntlafatsa, le ho phethela moralo oa pono oa theme ea hao pele Theme Builder e hlahisa theme e feletseng.

## Kakaretso {#overview}

Kamora ho qeta Puisano ea Ho Sibolla, Theme Builder e hlahisa tataiso ea hao ea moralo ka **pontšo ea ponelopele ea desktop le mobile**. Sena se u lumella ho bona hantle hore na moralo oa hao o tla shebahala joang lisebelisoa tse fapaneng pele u itlama ho theme.

## Likarolo tsa Tataiso ea Moralo {#design-direction-components}

### 1. Palete ea Mebala {#1-color-palette}

Sekema sa mebala sa theme ea hao, ho kenyeletsa:

- **Mmala oa mantlha** — mmala o moholo oa lets'oao o sebelisoang bakeng sa likonopo, lihokelo, le lintlha tse hatelletsoeng
- **Mmala oa bobeli** — mmala o tlatsanang bakeng sa phapang
- **Mmala oa kgatello** — mmala o hlahellisang bakeng sa likarolo tsa bohlokoa
- **Mebala e sa nke lehlakore** — bohlooho le bosoeu bakeng sa limelo le mongolo
- **Mmala oa mongolo** — mmala oa mantlha oa mongolo bakeng sa ho baleha habonolo

### 2. Thaepografi {#2-typography}

Likhetho tsa fonte bakeng sa:

- **Fonte ea lihlooho** — e sebelisoang bakeng sa lihlooho tsa maqephe le lihlooho tsa likarolo
- **Fonte ea 'mele** — e sebelisoang bakeng sa mongolo oa lirapa le dikahare
- **Boholo ba fonte** — boholo bo arabelang bakeng sa boholo bo fapaneng ba skrine
- **Bophahamo ba mola** — sebaka pakeng tsa mela bakeng sa ho baleha habonolo
- **Boima ba fonte** — mefuta e fapaneng ea botenya, e tloaelehileng, le e bobebe

### 3. Peakanyo le Sebaka {#3-layout-and-spacing}

- **Bophara ba setshelo** — bophara bo boholo ka ho fetisisa bakeng sa dikahare
- **Padding le margins** — sebaka se potolohileng likarolo
- **Sistimi ea gridi** — peakanyo ea dikholomo bakeng sa moralo o arabelang
- **Sebaka sa dikarolo** — sebaka pakeng tsa likarolo tsa UI

### 4. Likarolo tsa Pono {#4-visual-elements}

- **Mekhoa ea likonopo** — meralo ea likonopo tsa mantlha, tsa bobeli, le tsa boraro
- **Meralo ea likarete** — dipeakanyo bakeng sa diboloko tsa dikahare
- **Matshwao** — mokgwa le boholo ba matshwao
- **Meedi le meriti** — botebo bo bonolo ba pono
- **Ditlamorao tsa hover** — setaele sa boemo bo sebelisanang

## Pontšo ea Ponelopele {#preview-rendering}

### Ponelopele ea Desktop {#desktop-preview}

Ponelopele ea desktop e bontša moralo oa hao ka bophara bo feletseng (hangata 1200px kapa ho feta):

- Menyu e feletseng ea ho tsamaya
- Peakanyo e feletseng ka dikholomo tsohle
- Thaepografi e kgolo le sebaka se seholo
- Likarolo tsohle tsa pono ka boholo bo feletseng

### Ponelopele ea Mobile {#mobile-preview}

Ponelopele ea mobile e bontša moralo oa hao ka bophara ba mobile (hangata 375px):

- Ho tsamaya ho arabelang (menyu ea hamburger)
- Peakanyo ea kholomo e le nngwe
- Thaepografi le sebaka tse fetotsoeng
- Boholo ba likonopo bo loketseng ho thetswa

### Dibreakpoint tse Arabelang {#responsive-breakpoints}

Theme Builder e hlahisa diponelopele ho dibreakpoint tsena:

| Sesebediswa | Bophara | Ponelopele |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Mobile** | 375px | Mohala o otlolohileng |
| **Tablet** | 768px | Tablet e rapameng |
| **Desktop** | 1200px | Desktop ea bophara bo feletseng |
| **Desktop e Kgolo** | 1920px | Pontsho e sephara haholo |

## Ho Ntlafatsa Moralo oa Hao {#refining-your-design}

### Ho Etsa Diphetoho {#making-adjustments}

O ka ntlafatsa tataiso ea hao ea moralo ka ho:

1. **Ho fetola mebala** — fetola mmala ofe kapa ofe paleteng
2. **Ho fetola difonte** — kgetha difonti tse fapaneng
3. **Ho fetola sebaka** — fetola padding le margins
4. **Ho ntlafatsa peakanyo** — fetola bophara ba setshelo le dikholomo tsa gridi
5. **Ho iketsetsa likarolo** — fetola mekhoa ea likonopo, meralo ea likarete, jj.

### Dintlafatso tsa Ponelopele {#preview-updates}

Diphetoho di bonahala ka nako ya nnete:

- Ponelopele ea desktop e ntlafatswa hanghang
- Ponelopele ea mobile e ntlafatswa hanghang
- Dibreakpoint tsohle tse arabelang di ntlafatswa
- O ka fetoha pakeng tsa diponelopele ho netefatsa diphetoho

### Pono ea Papiso {#comparison-view}

Bapisa tataiso ea hao ea moralo le:

- **Moralo oa pele** — bona se fetohileng
- **Meralo ea bahlolisani** — bapisa le disete tsa tshusumetso
- **Dikhatiso tse fetileng** — kgutlela ditataisong tsa pele tsa moralo

## Kamohelo ea Tataiso ea Moralo {#design-direction-approval}

Hang ha o kgotsofetse ka moralo oa hao:

1. **Hlahloba diponelopele ka bobedi** — netefatsa hore desktop le mobile di shebahala hantle
2. **Lekola mebala yohle** — netefatsa phapang le phihlello
3. **Leka thaepografi** — netefatsa ho baleha habonolo ka boholo bohle
4. **Netefatsa peakanyo** — lekola sebaka le ho lekana
5. **Amohela moralo** — tswela pele ho hlahisoeng ha theme

## Mehato e Latelang {#next-steps}

Kamora ho amohela tataiso ea hao ea moralo:

1. Theme Builder e hlahisa theme ea hao e feletseng
2. Theme e kenngwa ho site ea hao ea WordPress
3. O ka iketsetsa ho feta o sebelisa WordPress customizer
4. Tswela pele ho [Dimenyu tsa Hospitality](./hospitality-menus.md) kapa ditshebetso tse ding

## Mekgwa e Metle ka ho Fetisisa {#best-practices}

- **Leka ho lisebelisoa tsa nnete** — sebedisa mehala le ditablet tsa nnete haeba ho kgoneha
- **Lekola ho baleha habonolo** — netefatsa hore mongolo o baleha ka boholo bohle
- **Netefatsa phapang** — sebedisa [Netefatsa Phapang ea Palete](../abilities/validate-palette-contrast.md) bakeng sa phihlello
- **Nahana ka tshebetso** — ntlafatsa ditshwantsho le difonte bakeng sa lebelo
- **Rera bakeng sa dikahare** — netefatsa hore peakanyo e sebetsa le dikahare tsa hao tsa nnete

## Ho Rarolla Mathata {#troubleshooting}

### Ponelopele ha e Ntlafatswe {#preview-not-updating}

- Ntjhafatsa leqephe
- Hlakola cache ea sebatli
- Leka sebatli se fapaneng
- Lekola kgokahano ya inthanete

### Mebala e Shebahala e Fapane {#colors-look-different}

- Lekola di-setting tsa mmala tsa monitor
- Leka ho lisebelisoa tse fapaneng
- Netefatsa phapang ea mmala ka dithulusi tsa phihlello
- Nahana ka disimulator tsa bofofu ba mebala

### Mathata a Thaepografi {#typography-issues}

- Netefatsa hore difaele tsa fonte di a laiswa
- Lekola boholo ba fonte ho dibreakpoint tse fapaneng
- Leka ka dikahare tsa nnete
- Nahana ka bolelele ba mola bakeng sa ho baleha habonolo
