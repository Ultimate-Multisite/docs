# Puisano ea Tlhahlobo {#discovery-interview}

**Puisano ea Tlhahlobo** ke mohato oa pele ts'ebetsong ea moralo ea Theme Builder. E bokella tlhahisoleseling ka sebaka sa hau sa marang-rang, khoebo ea hau, le likhetho tsa hau tsa moralo ho etsa theme e etselitsoeng uena.

## Kakaretso {#overview}

Puisano ea Tlhahlobo e u tataisa letotong la lipotso ho utloisisa lebitso la hau la khoebo, bamameli bao u ba shebileng, le lipakane tsa moralo. Mofuteng oa 1.16.0, puisano joale e kenyelletsa **mohato oa ho kenya foto** o u lumellang ho fana ka tšusumetso ea pono bakeng sa moralo oa hau.

## Mehato ea Puisano {#interview-steps}

### 1. Tlhahisoleseling ea Khoebo {#1-business-information}

- Lebitso la sebaka sa marang-rang le tagline
- Mofuta oa khoebo kapa indasteri
- Lipakane tsa mantlha tsa khoebo
- Tlhaloso ea bamameli bao u ba shebileng

### 2. Likhetho tsa Moralo {#2-design-preferences}

- Sekema sa mebala se ratoang
- Setaele sa moralo (sa sejoale-joale, sa khale, se bonolo, se bapalang, jj.)
- Liwebsaete kapa mabitso a khoebo a fanang ka tšusumetso
- Likarolo tsa moralo tse tlamehang ho ba teng

### 3. Ho Kenya Foto (E ncha ho v1.16.0) {#3-photo-upload-new-in-v1160}

Kenya foto e le 'ngoe kapa ho feta ho susumetsa tataiso ea moralo:

#### Libopeho tse Amoheloang {#accepted-formats}

| Sebopeho | Katoloso | Lintlha |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| **JPEG** | `.jpg`, `.jpeg` | E khothaletsoa bakeng sa linepe |
| **PNG** | `.png` | E tšehetsa ponaletso |
| **WebP** | `.webp` | Sebopeho sa sejoale-joale, boholo ba faele bo bonyenyane |
| **GIF** | `.gif` | Litšoantšo tse sa sisinyeheng feela (ha ho animation) |

#### Litlhoko tsa Faele {#file-requirements}

- **Boholo bo boholo ka ho fetisisa ba faele:** 10 MB
- **Litekanyo tse nyenyane ka ho fetisisa:** 400 × 300 lipikselse
- **Litekanyo tse khothaletsoang:** 1200 × 800 lipikselse kapa ho feta
- **Karolelano ea mahlakore:** Karolelano efe kapa efe ea amoheleha

#### Kamoo Linepe li Susumetsang Tataiso ea Moralo {#how-photos-influence-design-direction}

Linepe tseo u li kenyang li thusa Theme Builder ho utloisisa:

- **Phalete ea mebala** — mebala e hlahelletseng ea ntšoa 'me ea sebelisoa ho theme
- **Setaele sa pono** — sa sejoale-joale, sa mahaeng, se bonolo, se khanyang, jj.
- **Maikutlo le sepakapaka** — sa botsebi, sa boiqapelo, se botsoalle, sa mabothobotho, jj.
- **Likarolo tsa moralo** — lipaterone, masela a ponahalo, le litaele tsa popeho
- **Tšusumetso ea typography** — litaele tsa mongolo tse tlatsanang le theme ea pono

### 4. Sebopeho sa Litaba {#4-content-structure}

- Maqephe a mantlha a hlokahalang (Home, About, Services, Contact, jj.)
- Karolo ea blog kapa litaba e hlokahalang
- Ts'ebetso ea khoebo ea inthanete e hlokahalang
- Likarolo tse khethehileng kapa likopanyo

### 5. Tlhahlobo le Netefatso {#5-review-and-confirmation}

- Kakaretso ea tlhahisoleseling eohle e fanoeng
- Netefatso pele tlhahiso ea moralo e qala

## Mekhoa e Molemo ea ho Kenya Foto {#photo-upload-best-practices}

### Seo U ka se Kenyang {#what-to-upload}

- **Tšusumetso ea lebitso la khoebo** — li-logo, disampole tsa mebala, kapa litataiso tsa lebitso la khoebo
- **Liwebsaete tsa bahlolisani** — li-screenshot tsa libaka tsa marang-rang tseo u li ratang
- **Mood boards** — pokello ea litšoantšo tse emelang botle boo u bo batlang
- **Linepe tsa lihlahisoa** — haeba u na le lihlahisoa tseo u lokelang ho li bontša
- **Linepe tsa sehlopha kapa ofisi** — bakeng sa boitsebiso ba botho kapa ba botsebi
- **Litšoantšo tsa mokhoa oa bophelo** — tse emelang bamameli bao u ba shebileng

### Seo U Lokelang ho se Qoba {#what-to-avoid}

- **Litšoantšo tsa boleng bo tlase** — linepe tse fifetseng kapa tse nang le lipikselse tse bonahalang
- **Litšoantšo tse sa amaneng** — linepe tse sa emeleng lebitso la hau la khoebo
- **Litšoantšo tse nang le mongolo o mongata** — li-screenshot tse nang le mongolo o mongata
- **Lisebelisoa tse sirelelitsoeng ke copyright** — netefatsa hore u na le litokelo tsa ho sebelisa litšoantšo
- **Li-GIF tse sisinyehang** — ho sebetsoa feela litšoantšo tse sa sisinyeheng

### Malebela bakeng sa Liphetho tse Molemohali {#tips-for-best-results}

1. **Kenya litšoantšo tse 2-3** — litšoantšo tse ngata li fana ka moelelo o betere
2. **Sebelisa linepe tsa boleng bo holimo** — litšoantšo tse hlakileng, tse bonesitsoeng hantle li sebetsa hantle ka ho fetisisa
3. **Kenyelletsa mefuta-futa** — motsoako oa mebala, setaele, le taba ea setšoantšo
4. **Fana ka moelelo** — hlalosa ka bokhutšoanyane hore na ke hobane'ng ha u khethile setšoantšo ka seng
5. **Totobatsa ka ho hlaka** — haeba u rata karolo e itseng, e bontše

## Sephetho sa Tataiso ea Moralo {#design-direction-output}

Ka mor'a Puisano ea Tlhahlobo, Theme Builder e hlahisa:

- **Phalete ea mebala** — mebala ea mantlha, ea bobeli, le ea khatello
- **Typography** — malapa a lifonti bakeng sa lihlooho le mongolo oa 'mele
- **Setaele sa layout** — likheo, tekanyo, le tlhophiso ea likarolo
- **Likarolo tsa pono** — li-icon, lipaterone, le likarolo tsa mokhabiso
- **Mood board** — pontšo ea pono ea tataiso ea moralo

## Mehato e Latelang {#next-steps}

Ka mor'a ho qeta Puisano ea Tlhahlobo:

1. Hlahloba tataiso ea moralo e hlahisitsoeng
2. Fana ka maikutlo kapa u kope liphetoho
3. Tsoela pele mohato oa [Tataiso ea Moralo](./design-direction.md)
4. Theme Builder e etsa theme ea hau e ikhethileng
