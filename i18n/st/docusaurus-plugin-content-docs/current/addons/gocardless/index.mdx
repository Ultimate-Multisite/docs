# GoCardless

Multisite Ultimate: Tefo ya GoCardless

Dumella bareki ba hao ho lefella botho le dihlahiswa tsa Multisite Ultimate ka Direct Debit ba sebedisa GoCardless!

## Ho kenya {#installation}

1. Kenya 'ultimate-multisite-gocardless' foldareng ya '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Kenya katoloso tshebetsong ka menu ya 'Dikatoloso' ho WordPress
3. Latela Wizard ya mohato ka mohato ho seta katoloso

## Tataiso ya Tshebediso {#usage-guide}

_GoCardless_ ke kgoro ya tefo e fumanehang **Yuropa le UK** e dumellang ditefo tsa **Direct Debit**.

**Ultimate Multisite: GoCardless Gateway** e eketsa tshehetso ya _GoCardless_ ho Ultimate Multisite, e o dumellang ho lefisa bareki ba hao sebakeng seo ka Direct Debit.

Thuto ena e tla akaretsa kamoo o ka setang _GoCardless_ kateng hore o qale ho amohela ditefo ka yona marangrang a hao a Ultimate Multisite.

_**Tlhokomeliso: GoCardless e sebetsa feela ka ditjhelete tse latelang:**_ **EUR, GBP, AUD, SEK, le DKK.**

## Ho kenya Tlatsetso {#installing-the-add-on}

Pele ho tsohle, o tla hloka ho kenya tlatsetso ya GoCardless bakeng sa Ultimate Multisite. O ka etsa seo ka ho ya ho **Ultimate Multisite > Dipeakanyo > Ditlatsetso (ka bareng ya lehlakore)**. Ebe o kgetha tlatsetso ya GoCardless lenaneng leo.

Kamora hore tlatsetso e kengwe le ho kenngwa tshebetsong, ya ho **Ultimate Multisite > Dipeakanyo > Ditefo**, bulela kgetho ya GoCardless, mme o boloke.

![Dipeakanyo tsa kgoro ya tefo](/img/config/settings-payment-gateways.png)

## Ho fumana Access Token {#getting-an-access-token}

Theha akhaonto ho _GoCardless_ (kapa ho sandbox ya _GoCardless_, haeba o batla ho **e leka pele** , e leng se kgothaletswang). Jwale, kamora ho kena, ya nthong ya menu ya **Developers**.

<!-- Setshwantsho ha se fumanehe: dashboard ya GoCardless e bontsha sebaka sa menu ya Developers -->

Leqepheng la **Developers**, tobetsa konopo ya **Create** hukung e ka hodimo ka ho le letona ya skrine, ebe o tobetsa kgetho ya **Access Token**.

<!-- Setshwantsho ha se fumanehe: leqephe la GoCardless Developers le nang le konopo ya Create le kgetho ya Access Token -->

Sena se tla bula fensetere e ntjha ya modal, se o dumella ho theha token e ntjha. E fe lebitso le lemohuwang mme o netefatse hore o kgetha kgetho ya **Read-write access** ho menu ya **Scope**.

<!-- Setshwantsho ha se fumanehe: modal ya GoCardless ya ho theha access token e ntjha ka scope ya Read-write -->

Access token e tla hlaha skrineng. E kopitse mme o e manamise ho menu ya hao ya **Ultimate Multisite > Dipeakanyo > Ditefo > GoCardless**.

Netefatsa hore o e beha lebaleng le nepahetseng. Haeba e le **live token** , tima **mokgwa wa sandbox** pele o manamisa token.

![Dipeakanyo tsa kgoro ya tefo ka GoCardless](/img/config/settings-payment-gateways.png)

<!-- Setshwantsho ha se fumanehe: mabala a token ya GoCardless dipeakanyong tsa tefo tsa Ultimate Multisite -->

Ke phetho bakeng sa mohato wa pele. E latelang, o hloka ho seta momamedi wa webhook.

## Ho seta Momamedi wa Webhook {#setting-up-the-webhook-listener}

Ha ho sebetsanwa le dipeeletso, Ultimate Multisite e hloka ho utlwa ka diphetoho boemong ba peeletso, ditefo tse ntjha tse etswang, le dintho tse ding tse jwalo.

Tsela eo dikgoro tsa tefo di tsebisang Ultimate Multisite ka diphetoho tseo ka yona ke ho sebedisa **mehala ya webhook**. Ka lebaka leo, hore kopanyo e sebetse 100%, re hloka ho bolella _GoCardless_ hore na e bitse URL efe ka tlhahisoleseding eo yohle.

Kgutlela phaneleng ya hao ya **GoCardless > Developers** mme o tobetse konopo ya Create hape, empa lekgetlong lena, kgetha kgetho ya **Webhook endpoint**.

<!-- Setshwantsho ha se fumanehe: leqephe la GoCardless Developers le nang le kgetho ya Webhook endpoint -->Fensetere e ntjha e tla bula ka dikgetho tsa webhook endpoint. E kopa webhook URL, kahoo kgutlela ho **dipeakanyo tsa Ultimate Multisite > Ditefo > GoCardless** mme o kopitse webhook URL e thathamisitsweng moo:

<!-- Setshwantsho ha se fumanehe: dipeakanyo tsa Ultimate Multisite GoCardless tse bontshang webhook URL -->

Ho feta moo, o ka kenya **webhook secret** e tla sebediswa ho saena mehala ya hao ya webhook. Eng kapa eng eo o e kenyang lebaleng leo, o tla hloka ho e kenya le ho **lebala la Ultimate Multisite Webhook Secret**.

<!-- Setshwantsho ha se fumanehe: foromo ya tlhophiso ya GoCardless webhook endpoint e nang le mabala a URL le secret -->

Qetella ka ho boloka endpoint ka ho tobetsa konopo ya **Create webhook endpoint** ebe o **boloka le dipeakanyo tsa hao tsa Ultimate Multisite**.

Jwale o lokela ho be o loketse ho amohela _GoCardless_!
