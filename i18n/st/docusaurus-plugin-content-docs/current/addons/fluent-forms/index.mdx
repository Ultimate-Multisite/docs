# Fluent Forms

Tlatsetso ena e etsa hore ho khonehe ho eketsa meedi ho Fluent Forms ho Multisite Ultimate ka har'a lihlahisoa, botho kapa libaka.
Fluent Forms e sebelisa litafole tse ikhethileng ho e-na le mefuta ea poso e ikhethileng, kahoo likhetho tse tloaelehileng tsa ho beha meedi ha li sebetse le Fluent Forms.

## Likarolo {#features}

- Eketsa meedi bakeng sa Fluent Forms kapa Conversational Fluent Forms
- Bareki ba fumana molaetsa o mosa o ba khothalletsang ho ntlafatsa moralo oa bona ha ba fihla moeding
