# Lenane la Liphetoho la Multi-Network {#multi-network-changelog}

### 1.3.0 {#130}
* E ncha: Seboni sa thempleite ea netweke — bala le ho shebella thempleite ea netweke ka phanele e phelang pele o reka.
* E ncha: Sekgethi sa thempleite ea netweke ha ho lefshoa — bareki ba ka khetha thempleite ea netweke nakong ea ts'ebetso ea ho ingolisa le ho lefa.
* E ncha: SSO ea sehokelo sa boselamose pakeng tsa dinetweke — bareki ba ka fihlella dinetweke tse ka tlase ka bonolo ka netefatso ea magic-link.
* E ncha: Ho kopitsa media ea netweke — media ea site e kopitsoa ka nepo ha ho kopitsoa dinetweke ho tsoa thempleiteng.
* Tokiso: Ho kopitsa netweke joale ho sebetsa ntle le addon ea multi-tenancy; litokelo tsa super admin li fuoa hape kamora clone.
* Tokiso: Ho bopa netweke joale ho lumellana le WooCommerce le diheke tsohle tsa tefo.
* Tokiso: Nomoro ea port joale e kenyelelitsoe tlhahisong ea domain ea fallback.
* Tokiso: CSS ea sekgethi sa thempleite e falliselitsoe ho stylesheet e kentsoeng moleng bakeng sa ponahatso e nepahetseng ho themes tsohle.
* Tokiso: Sireletsa khahlanong le senotlolo sa `network_id` se sa hlalosoang ka har'a sebaka sa potso ea site.
* Tokiso: Tlhahlobo ea tumello ea netweke pele ho fetoha ha moelelo e thibela liphoso tsa thepa ea tafole ea `wpdb`.
