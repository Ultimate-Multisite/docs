# Kgetho ya Puo

Ultimate Multisite: Kgetho ya Puo ha ho Ingodiswa

Kenya sebaka sa kgetho ya puo phallong ya ho ingodisa.

## Ho kenya {#installation}

1. Kenya 'ultimate-multisite-language-selector' foldareng ya '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Kenya tlatsetso tshebetsong ka menu ya 'Ditlatsetso' ho WordPress Network Admin ya hao

## Tataiso ya Tshebediso {#usage-guide}

Ka tlatsetso ena ya mahala o ka dumella basebedisi ba hao ho kgetha puo eo ba ka ratang ho e bona phallong ya ho ingodisa le leqepheng la ho kena.

Ka mora [ho kenya laesense ya hao tshebetsong](../../user-guide/getting-started/installing-ultimate-multisite.md) o tla kgona ho kenya ditlatsetso tsa Ultimate Multisite tseo laesense ya hao e o fang phihlello ho tsona.

Eya leqepheng la disetting tsa Ultimate Multisite mme ebe, ho menu e ka lehlakoreng le letshehadi, tobetsa leibole ya _Ditlatsetso_.

![Leqephe la ditlatsetso](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

_Eya leqepheng la Ditlatsetso ho qala ho kenya Kgetho ya Puo_

Moo, o tla fumana ditlatsetso tsohle tseo re fanang ka tsona.

Ditlatsetso tsa mahala di fumaneha ho bareki bohle ba rona, ho sa tsotellehe moralo wa phano oo ba ingodisitseng ho ona.

Tobetsa konopo ya _E kenye Hona Jwale!_ ka tlase ho setshwantsho se senyane sa tlatsetso. Ebe o e kenya tshebetsong leqepheng la Ditlatsetso.

![Leqephe la ditlatsetso le konopo ya ho kenya](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

_Tobetsa konopo ho kenya tlatsetso_

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: WP Plugins page showing Network Activate button for Language Selector addon -->

_E kenye tshebetsong leqepheng la hao la Ditlatsetso_

Jwale, eya ho menu ya Disetting tsa Ultimate Multisite mme o kene tabeng ya _Disetting tsa Network_. Moo, botlaaseng ba leqephe, o tla fumana karolo e ntjha e bitswang _Dipuo_.

![Leqephe la disetting](/img/config/settings-general.png)

_Karolo e ntjha ka ho felletseng e a hlaha!_

O ka e tlohela e se na letho ho bontsha dipuo tsohle e le dikgetho kapa wa kgetha tseo o ka ratang ho di etsa hore di fumanehe. Dipuo ke tseo o di kentseng tlhophisong ya hao ya WordPress.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Language selection dropdown showing available languages on WP install -->

_Lenane la dipuo tse fumanehang kenyong ena ya WP_

Ka mora ho boloka diphetoho tseo o di entseng, menu ya kgetho e nang le dipuo tseo o di kgethileng e tla bontshwa leqepheng la Ho Kena mmoho le Phallong ya ho Ingodisa.

![Disetting tsa ho kena le ho ingodisa](/img/config/settings-login-registration.png)

_Kgetho ya Puo Leqepheng la Ho Kena_

![Leqephe la ho ingodisa](/img/config/registration-page.png)

_Kgetho ya Puo Phallong ya ho Ingodisa_

## Ho seta Puo ka URL {#setting-the-language-via-the-url}

O ka boela wa fetola puo ya leqephe ka ho fetisa parameter ya locale ho URL. Haeba o batla ho lebisa hape leqepheng la ho ingodisa ka Setaliana, mohlala, kenya ?locale=it_IT ho URL.

[https://yournetwork.com/wp-signup.php?locale=it_IT](https://yournetwork.com/wp-signup.php?locale=it%5FIT)

Puo e tlameha ho ba e kentswe ho network ya hao ya WordPress hore sena se sebetse.
