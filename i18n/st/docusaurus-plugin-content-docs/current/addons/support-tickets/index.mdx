# Litekete tsa Tšehetso

**Litekete tsa Tšehetso** ke addon e matla bakeng sa Ultimate Multisite e eketsang tsamaiso e felletseng ea litekete tsa tšehetso ho netweke ea hau ea WordPress. E loketse ka ho phethahetseng liforomo tsa SaaS, bafani ba WaaS (Website as a Service), le dinetweke tsa multisite tse hlokang ho fana ka tšehetso ya bareki maemong a netweke le a sebaka ka seng.

## Hobaneng o Khetha Litekete tsa Tšehetso? {#why-choose-support-tickets}

* **Tšehetso ya Maemo a Mangata**: Batsamaisi ba netweke ba sebetsana le litekete tsa netweke ka bophara ha beng ba dibaka ba laola tšehetso ya bareki ba bona
* **Tshebetso ya Setsebi**: Taolo e felletseng ya potoloho ya bophelo ba tekete ka dintho tse tlang pele, maemo, le dikabelo
* **Boiphihlelo ba Moreki**: Sebopeho se setle, se arabelang se nolofalletsang bareki ho fumana thuso
* **E Sireletsehileng & e Tshepehang**: E hahilwe ka mekgwa e metle ya tshireletso ya WordPress mme e ntlafaditswe bakeng sa tshebetso ya database
* **Kopanyo e se Nang Mathata**: E sebetsa ka botlalo le taolo ya bareki le dibaka ya Ultimate Multisite

## Dikarolo tsa Bohlokwa {#key-features}

**Taolo e Felletseng ya Litekete**

* Sistimi e ikgethang ya dinomoro tsa litekete bakeng sa tshupiso e bonolo
* Maemo a dintho tse tlang pele a ka hlophiswang (Tlase, Tlwaelehileng, Hodimo, Bohlokwa Haholo)
* Tshebetso e felletseng ya maemo (E Bulehile, E Tswela Pele, E Emetse, E Rarollotswe, E Kwetswe)
* Ho arola litekete ka mekgahlelo (Kakaretso, Tekheniki, Tefiso, Kopo ya Karolo, Tlaleho ya Phoso)
* Abela baemedi ba tšehetso litekete ka kgetho ya kabo ya boiketsetso

**Dipuisano tse Latelanang**

* Ho latellana ho felletseng ha puisano bakeng sa dikarabo tsa tekete
* Setaele se fapaneng bakeng sa dikarabo tsa basebetsi le bareki
* Dinoutu tsa poraefete tsa basebetsi tseo bareki ba ke keng ba di bona
* Sebopeho sa nako ya nnete se tsamaiswang ke AJAX
* Mohlophisi wa mongolo wa sejwalejwale ka dikgetho tsa ho fomata

**Dikamathiso tsa Difaele**

* Ho kenya faele ka polokeho ka tšehetso ya ho hula-le-ho lahlela
* Dithibelo tsa mofuta wa faele le boholo tse ka hlophiswang
* Tshireletso e tsoetseng pele ka ho lemoha difaele tse kotsi
* Dintho tse jarollwang tse sireleditsweng ka ditaolo tsa phihlello

**Ditsebiso tse Bohlale**

* Ditemoso tsa imeile tsa boiketsetso bakeng sa litekete tse ntjha, dikarabo, le diphetoho tsa maemo
* Dithempleite tsa imeile tse ka ikgethelwang
* Ditsebiso tse thehilweng ho karolo bakeng sa basebetsi le bareki
* Diphetoho tsa thempleite tse fetohang bakeng sa melaetsa e etseditsweng motho ka mong

**Sebopeho se Matla sa Motsamaisi**

* Lenane le tsoetseng pele la litekete ka ho hlophisa le ho sefa
* Tokiso e potlakileng bakeng sa maemo, dintho tse tlang pele, le dikabelo
* Diketso tsa bongata bakeng sa taolo e sebetsang hantle
* Dashboard ya dipalopalo ka dimetriki tsa tshebetso
* Phanele e felletseng ya di-setting

**Portal e Ntle ya Bareki**

* Sebopeho se hlwekileng, se arabelang disebedisweng tsohle
* Moralo o qalang ka mobile
* Patlo e tsoetseng pele le ho sefa
* Mohlophisi wa sejwalejwale ka toolbar ya ho fomata
* Diforomo tsa ho romela tekete tse bonolo ho di sebedisa

**Kopanyo e Feto-fetohang**

* Sebedisa shortcodes kapa dielemente tsa UI tsa Ultimate Multisite
* Bontsha diforomo tsa litekete le manane kae kapa kae sebakeng sa hau
* E sebetsa ka bonolo le sebopeho sa netweke ya multisite
* E atoloseha ka hooks le filters

## E Loketse {#perfect-for}

* Liforomo tsa SaaS tse hlokang tšehetso ya bareki
* Bafani ba WaaS ba laolang dibaka tse ngata tsa bareki
* Dibaka tsa botho tse nang le tšehetso ya maemo
* Batsamaisi ba netweke ba laolang mabitso a kgwebo a mangata
* Beng ba dibaka ba fanang ka tshebeletso ya bareki

## Ho Kenya {#installation}

1. Netefatsa hore plugin ya Ultimate Multisite e kentsoe mme e buletswe netwekeng ya hau ya WordPress
2. Kenya difaele tsa addon bukeng ya hao ya `/wp-content/plugins/`
3. Kenya plugin maemong a netweke ka menu ya 'Plugins' ho WordPress
4. Eya ho Network Admin > Support Tickets ho hlophisa di-setting tsa hao

## Dipotso tse Botswang Hangata {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Na sena se hloka Ultimate Multisite? {#does-this-require-ultimate-multisite}

Ee, addon ena e hloka hore Ultimate Multisite e kenngwe mme e sebetse. E hokahana ka botebo le dikarolo tsa taolo ya bareki le dibaka tsa Ultimate Multisite.

## Na beng ba dibaka ba ka sebetsana le tšehetso ya bareki ba bona? {#can-site-owners-handle-their-own-customer-support}

Ka sebele! Beng ba dibaka ba ka sebedisa sistimi ya litekete ho fana ka tšehetso ho bareki ba bona, ha batsamaisi ba netweke ba ka sebetsana le litekete tsa tšehetso tsa netweke ka bophara.

## Bareki ba romela litekete jwang? {#how-do-customers-submit-tickets}

O ka eketsa diforomo tsa ho romela litekete kae kapa kae sebakeng sa hau o sebedisa shortcodes kapa dielemente tsa UI tsa Ultimate Multisite. Bareki ba tlatsa foromo feela mme ba romele kopo ya bona.

## Na nka ikgethela maemo le dintho tse tlang pele tsa litekete? {#can-i-customize-the-ticket-statuses-and-priorities}

Ee! O ka ikgethela ka botlalo maemo a dintho tse tlang pele, maemo, le mefuta ya litekete di-setting tsa motsamaisi wa netweke ho tsamaisana le tshebetso ya hao ya tšehetso.

## Na e tshehetsa dikamathiso tsa difaele? {#does-it-support-file-attachments}

Ee, bareki le basebetsi ka bobedi ba ka hokela difaele ho litekete le dikarabo. O na le taolo e felletseng hodima mefuta ya difaele tse dumelletsweng le meedi ya boholo bakeng sa tshireletso.

## Ditsebiso di sebetswa jwang? {#how-are-notifications-handled}

Sistimi e romela ka boiketsetso ditsebiso tsa imeile bakeng sa litekete tse ntjha, dikarabo, le diphetoho tsa maemo. O ka ikgethela dithempleite tsohle tsa imeile di-setting.

## Na nka abela baemedi ba itseng ba tšehetso litekete? {#can-i-assign-tickets-to-specific-support-agents}

Ee, o ka abela baemedi litekete ka letsoho kapa wa bulela kabo ya boiketsetso ho aba litekete ka boiketsetso.

## Na ho na le dinoutu tsa poraefete bakeng sa basebetsi? {#are-there-private-notes-for-staff}

Ee, ditho tsa basebetsi di ka eketsa dikarabo tsa poraefete tse bonahalang feela ho ditho tse ding tsa basebetsi, eseng ho bareki.

## Na e sebetsa hantle ho mobile? {#is-it-mobile-friendly}

Ka sebele! Sebopeho sohle se hahilwe ka mokgwa wa ho qala ka mobile mme se sebetsa hantle disebedisweng tsohle.

## Na nka bontsha manane a litekete le diforomo ka ho sebedisa shortcodes? {#can-i-display-ticket-lists-and-forms-using-shortcodes}

Ee! Shortcodes tse fumanehang:
* `[wu_support_tickets]` - Bontsha lenane la litekete tsa moreki
* `[wu_support_ticket_form]` - Bontsha foromo ya ho romela tekete e ntjha
* `[wu_support_ticket_view ticket_id="123"]` - Bontsha tekete e itseng

## Ke mefuta efe ya difaele e ka kenngwang? {#what-file-types-can-be-uploaded}

O ka hlophisa mefuta ya difaele tse dumelletsweng di-setting. Sistimi e kenyelletsa tlhahlobo e tsoetseng pele ya tshireletso ho sireletsa kgahlanong le difaele tse kotsi.

## Na nka romela data ya litekete kantle? {#can-i-export-ticket-data}

Ee, addon e kenyelletsa bokgoni ba ho romela kantle data ya litekete le dipalopalo.
