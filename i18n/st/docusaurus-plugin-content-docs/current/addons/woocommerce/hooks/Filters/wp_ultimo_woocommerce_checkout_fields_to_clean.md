# Sesefe: wp_ultimo_woocommerce_checkout_fields_to_clean

E lumella bahlahisi ho sefa masimo a kamehla a aterese ea billing ea WooCommerce.

Kaha Ultimate Multisite e na le masimo a aterese ea billing, ho ka utloahala ho tlosa masimo a kamehla a bontšitsoeng ke WooCommerce foromong ea checkout. Ka kamehla, ha ho boleng bo fetisetsoang lenaneng lena, kahoo masimo ohle a hlophisitsoeng ho WooCommerce hore a bontšoe a bolokoa.

### Ho tloha

- 2.0.0
### Mohloli

E hlalositsoe ho [`inc/gateways/class-woocommerce-gateway.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite-woocommerce/blob/main/inc/gateways/class-woocommerce-gateway.php#L277) moleng oa 277


## E khutlisa
Lenane la masimo a lokelang ho tlosoa. Litekanyetso tse fumanehang li kenyelletsa billing_first_name, billing_last_name, billing_company, billing_country, billing_address_1, billing_address_2, billing_city, billing_state, billing_postcode, billing_email, le billing_phone.
