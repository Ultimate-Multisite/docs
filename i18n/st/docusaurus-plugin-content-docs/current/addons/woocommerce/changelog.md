# Lenane la Diphetoho la Kopanyo ya WooCommerce

Mofuta 2.2.0 - E lokollotswe ka 2026-07-01
* E ntjha: Dipalo tsa lekgetho tsa Ultimate Multisite jwale di hlaha e le mela e arohaneng ya ditefiso tsa WooCommerce nakong ya checkout, ho etsa hore kakaretso ya lekgetho e hlake haholoanyane pele ho tefo.
* E ntjha: Ho kentswe setting ya boikgethelo ya "Emisa Memberships Hanghang ha Renewals di Hlotsoe" le filter ya `wu_woo_suspend_on_payment_failure` bakeng sa disaete tse batlang ho emiswa ka lebaka la failed-renewal nakong ya fensetere ya ho leka hape ya WooCommerce Subscriptions.
* Tokiso: Ho bapantswe maemo a WooCommerce Subscription a neng a ka sala a sa tsamaisane le memberships tsa Ultimate Multisite ka mora renewals tse hlotsoeng kapa tse hlaphohetsweng.
* Tokiso: Ho kentswe tjhelete ya lebenkele la WooCommerce lenaneng la ditjhelete la Ultimate Multisite ha e ne e le siyo.
* Tokiso: Ho bolokilwe dintlha tsa tefiso tsa moreki ha ho lebiswa subscribers ho WooCommerce checkout.
* Ho ntlafaditswe: Ho kentswe tshebedisano le Jetpack Autoloader 5.
* Ho ntlafaditswe: Ho hlwekisitswe tlhahiso ya sephutheloana sa tokollo hore di-zip tsa GitHub le marketplace di qobe directories tsa staging tse keneng kahare le difaele tsa ntshetsopele.
