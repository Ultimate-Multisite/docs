# Filtr: wp_ultimo_skip_network_active_check {#filter-wpultimoskipnetworkactivecheck}

Dasturchilarga tarmoq faollashtirish tekshiruvini muddatidan oldin to‘xtatishga imkon beradi.

Bu composer asosidagi va boshqa maxsus sozlamalardan foydalanganda foydali, masalan, Bedrock kabi, bunda pluginlarni mu-plugins sifatida ishlatish odatiy hisoblanadi.

## Parametrlar {#parameters}

| Nomi | Turi | Tavsif |
|------|------|-------------|
| $skip_network_activation_check | `bool` | Tekshiruvni o‘tkazib yuborishimiz kerakmi yoki yo‘qmi; standart qiymati false. |

### Versiyadan beri {#since}

- 2.0.0
### Manba {#source}

[`inc/class-requirements.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite-woocommerce/blob/main/inc/class-requirements.php#L272) faylida, 272-qatorda aniqlangan


## Qaytaradi {#returns}
Agar tekshiruvni o‘tkazib yubormoqchi bo‘lsangiz true, aks holda false.
