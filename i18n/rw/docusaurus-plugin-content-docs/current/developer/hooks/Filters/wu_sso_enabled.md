# Muyunguruzi: wu_sso_enabled

Fungura/funga ubushobozi bwa single-sign-on hagati ya domain.

Yungurura aka gaciro kugira ngo uzimye single-sign-on burundu, cyangwa uyifungure hashingiwe ku bisabwa.

## Ibipimo

| Izina | Ubwoko | Ibisobanuro |
|------|------|-------------|
| $enabled | `bool` | SSO ikwiye kuba ifunguye? True bisobanuye ko ifunguye, false-ish bisobanuye ko ifunze. |

### Kuva kuri

- 2.0.11
### Inkomoko

Byasobanuwe muri [`inc/sso/class-sso.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite/blob/main/inc/sso/class-sso.php#L110) ku murongo wa 110


## Ibisubizwa
Niba SSO ifunguye cyangwa itafunguye.
