# Akayunguruzo: wu_view_override_replaceable_views {#filter-wuviewoverridereplaceableviews}

Byemerera abatunganya porogaramu kongeramo ubundi bubiko ku rutonde rushobora gusimburwa.

Witondere, kuko kwemera ubundi bubiko bishobora gutuma kopi zitakijyanye n’igihe zipakirwa aho gukoresha verisiyo za Ultimate Multisite.

## Ibipimo {#parameters}

| Izina | Ubwoko | Ibisobanuro |
|------|------|-------------|
| $replaceable_views | `array` | Urutonde rw’ububiko bwemewe. |

### Kuva {#since}

- 2.0.0
### Inkomoko {#source}

Byasobanuwe muri [`inc/functions/template.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite/blob/main/inc/functions/template.php#L50) ku murongo wa 50
