# Multi-Network

Inyongera ya Ultimate Multisite: Multinetwork yongerera urubuga rwawe rwa Ultimate Multisite ubushobozi bukomeye bwa multinetwork. Iyi nyongera ituma abakiriya bawe bashobora kugura no gucunga insobe zose za WordPress multisite, aho kuba imbuga ku giti cyazo gusa.

## Ibiranga by'ingenzi

* **Gutanga urusobe**: Kora kandi utunganye mu buryo bwikora insobe zuzuye za WordPress multisite ku bakiriya bawe
* **Ingero z'insobe**: Reka abakiriya barebe, bagerageze kureba mbere, kandi bahitemo urugero rw'urusobe rwateguwe mbere yo kwiyandikisha
* **Gucunga urusobe**: Ubushobozi bwuzuye bwo gucunga ibidukikije bya multinetwork
* **Insobe zitandukanye**: Buri rusobe rw'umukiriya rukora rwigenga rufite imbonerahamwe zarwo za database n'igenamiterere ryarwo
* **Cross-Network SSO**: Ha abakiriya uburyo bwo kugera ku nsobe zabo z'inyongera nta nkomyi ukoresheje kwemeza ukoresheje magic-link
* **Imiterere ishobora kwaguka**: Cunga insobe nyinshi neza ukoresheje igabanywa ry'ibikoresho ryanozwemo
* **Kwinjiza hamwe**: Yinjirana nta nkomyi na sisitemu za Ultimate Multisite zo kwiyandikisha no kwishyuza

## Aho bikoreshwa

* **White Label Hosting**: Ha abakiriya insobe zabo za multisite zifite ikirango cyabo
* **Ibisubizo by'amasosiyete y'abahuza serivisi**: Fasha amasosiyete gucunga insobe nyinshi z'abakiriya avuye ku rubuga rumwe
* **Ikoreshwa mu bigo binini**: Shyigikira imiryango minini ifite ibisabwa bikomeye bya multi-network
* **Imbuga z'uburezi**: Kora insobe zitandukanye ku mashuri cyangwa amashami atandukanye

## Ibisabwa

* Ultimate Multisite 2.0.0 cyangwa hejuru yayo
* Ishyirwaho rya WordPress Multisite
* PHP 7.4 cyangwa hejuru yayo
* MySQL 5.6 cyangwa hejuru yayo

## Gushyiraho

1. Shyira amadosiye y'icomekwaho muri bubiko bwa `/wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite-multinetwork`
2. Koresha icyo comeho unyuze kuri ecran ya 'Ibicomekwaho' muri WordPress
3. Tunganya igenamiterere rya multinetwork unyuze kuri Ultimate Multisite → Settings

## Ingero z'insobe

Verisiyo 1.3.0 yongeramo uburyo bwo kuvumbura no guhitamo urugero ku bakiriya bagura insobe nshya. Koresha ingero z'insobe igihe ushaka ko buri rusobe rushya rw'umukiriya rutangirira ku miterere, igishushanyo, isomero ry'ibikubiyemo, cyangwa igenamiterere ryihariye ry'icyiciro byateguwe.

### Uwerekana urugero mbere

Uwerekana urugero rw'urusobe mbere afasha abakiriya kureba ingero zihari mbere yo kwishyura. Buri rugero rushobora gufungurwa mu gice cyo kururebera imbonankubone, bityo abakiriya bakagereranya imiterere n'ibikubiyemo mbere y'uko bagura urusobe.

Koresha uwerekana mbere kugira ngo ufashe abakiriya:

* Gusuzuma isura n'imiterere ya buri rugero rw'urusobe ruhari
* Kugereranya ingero nyinshi batavuye mu nzira yo kugura
* Guhitamo urugero ruhuye neza n'icyo bashaka kurukoresha mbere yo kwiyandikisha

Komeza amazina n'ibisobanuro by'ingero byorohere abakiriya. Uwerekana mbere agira akamaro cyane iyo abakiriya bashobora kumva icyo rugenewe gukoreshwa, nko ku nsobe z'amasosiyete, insobe z'amashuri, insobe za franchise, cyangwa amapaki ya white-label hosting.

### Uhitamo urugero igihe cyo kwishyura

Uhitamo urugero ashobora kongerwa mu nzira yo kwiyandikisha no kwishyura nk'umwanya. Iyo byatunganyijwe, abakiriya bahitamo urugero rw'urusobe bashaka mu gihe cyo kwishyura, kandi urugero rwatoranyijwe ni rwo rukoreshwa igihe urusobe rwabo rushya rutanzwe.

Kugira ngo ukoreshe uhitamo mu nzira yo kwishyura:

1. Tegura insobe ushaka gutanga nk'ingero.
2. Koresha umwanya wo guhitamo urugero rwa Multinetwork mu ifishi ijyanye no kwiyandikisha cyangwa kwishyura.
3. Hitamo ingero zigomba kuboneka kuri iyo nzira yo kwishyura.
4. Tangaza inzira yo kwishyura maze ugerageze kwiyandikisha kugira ngo wemeze ko urugero rwatoranyijwe rukoreshwa ku rusobe rushya.

Niba inzira yo kwishyura itanga igicuruzwa kimwe gusa cy'urusobe, uhitamo ashobora gukomeza kuba ingirakamaro nk'intambwe iyobora mu gutangira. Ku miyoboro yo kugurisha ifite gahunda nyinshi, erekana gusa ingero zijyanye n'igicuruzwa cyangwa gahunda byatoranyijwe.

## Cross-Network Magic Link SSO

Cross-network magic link SSO ifasha abakiriya kuva hagati y'ahantu h'Account yabo y'ibanze n'insobe z'inyongera batiriwe bongera kwinjira n'intoki. Iyo umukiriya akurikiye link yo kugera kuri cross-network, Ultimate Multisite ikora magic link ifite igihe ntarengwa maze ikemeza umukiriya mu rusobe rugenewe.

Ibi bitezimbere ubunararibonye ku bakiriya bacunga urusobe rurenze rumwe cyangwa bakeneye kugera ku nsobe z'ingero zakoporowe vuba nyuma yo kugura.

Urutonde rwo kugenzura rw'umuyobozi:

* Emeza ko inyongera ya Multinetwork ikora hose mu ishyirwaho rya WordPress multisite.
* Menya neza ko abakiriya bafite uburenganzira bukwiye bwo kugera ku rusobe mbere y'uko bahabwa link za cross-network.
* Komeza indangarubuga z'insobe n'indangarubuga zahujwe bitunganyijwe neza kugira ngo magic links zijye ku rusobe rugenewe.
* Gerageza customer dashboard na buto zose zo kugera nyuma yo kwishyura nyuma yo guhindura igenamiterere ry'urusobe, indangarubuga, cyangwa kwishyura.

Magic links zigomba gufatwa nka link zo kwinjira. Zateguriwe gutuma abakiriya bagera kuri serivisi nta nkomyi kandi ntizigomba koherezwa abandi cyangwa kongera gukoreshwa hanze y'isomo ryagenewe.

## Ibibazo bikunze kubazwa

### Multinetwork ni iki?

Multinetwork ni ikiranga cya WordPress kigufasha gukoresha insobe nyinshi za multisite zigenga ku ishyirwaho rimwe rya WordPress.

### Ibi bitandukaniye he na multisite isanzwe?

Multisite isanzwe yemera imbuga nyinshi imbere mu rusobe rumwe. Multinetwork yemera insobe nyinshi, buri rumwe rufite imbuga zarwo, zose imbere mu ishyirwaho rimwe rya WordPress.

### Ese ibi bisaba igenamiterere ryihariye rya server?

Yego, multinetwork isaba itegurwa n'igenamiterere ryihariye rya server. Nyamuneka reba inyandiko z'ubufasha kugira ngo ubone amabwiriza arambuye yo kubitunganya.
