# Ikiganiro cyo Kumenya Iby'ibanze {#discovery-interview}

**Ikiganiro cyo Kumenya Iby'ibanze** ni intambwe ya mbere mu nzira y'igishushanyo ya Theme Builder. Gikusanya amakuru yerekeye urubuga rwawe, ubucuruzi bwawe, n'ibyo ukunda mu gishushanyo kugira ngo hakorwe theme yihariye.

## Incamake {#overview}

Ikiganiro cyo Kumenya Iby'ibanze kikuyobora mu ruhererekane rw'ibibazo kugira ngo humvikane ikirango cyawe, abo ugamije kugeraho, n'intego z'igishushanyo. Muri verisiyo 1.16.0, ikiganiro ubu kirimo **intambwe yo kohereza ifoto** igufasha gutanga icyitegererezo kigaragara cyafasha igishushanyo cyawe.

## Intambwe z'Ikiganiro {#interview-steps}

### 1. Amakuru y'Ubucuruzi {#1-business-information}

- Izina ry'urubuga n'interuro iruranga
- Ubwoko bw'ubucuruzi cyangwa urwego rukorerwamo
- Intego z'ibanze z'ubucuruzi
- Ibisobanuro by'abo ugamije kugeraho

### 2. Ibyo Ukunda mu Gishushanyo {#2-design-preferences}

- Uruhererekane rw'amabara ukunda
- Imiterere y'igishushanyo (igezweho, iya kera, yoroshye cyane, ishimishije, n'ibindi)
- Imbuga cyangwa ibirango bigutera inganzo
- Ibice by'igishushanyo bigomba kubamo byanze bikunze

### 3. Kohereza Ifoto (Bishya muri v1.16.0) {#3-photo-upload-new-in-v1160}

Ohereza ifoto imwe cyangwa nyinshi kugira ngo zigire uruhare mu cyerekezo cy'igishushanyo:

#### Imiterere Yemewe {#accepted-formats}

| Imiterere | Umugereka | Ibisobanuro |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| **JPEG** | `.jpg`, `.jpeg` | Isabwa ku mafoto |
| **PNG** | `.png` | Ishyigikira kugaragara mu buryo bubonerana |
| **WebP** | `.webp` | Imiterere igezweho, ingano ntoya ya dosiye |
| **GIF** | `.gif` | Amashusho adahinduka gusa (nta kugenda) |

#### Ibisabwa kuri Dosiye {#file-requirements}

- **Ingano ntarengwa ya dosiye:** 10 MB
- **Ibipimo bito ntarengwa:** 400 × 300 pixels
- **Ibipimo bisabwa:** 1200 × 800 pixels cyangwa binini kurushaho
- **Ikigereranyo cy'impande:** Ikigereranyo icyo ari cyo cyose kiremewe

#### Uko Amafoto Agira Uruhare mu Cyerekezo cy'Igishushanyo {#how-photos-influence-design-direction}

Amafoto wohereza afasha Theme Builder gusobanukirwa:

- **Urutonde rw'amabara** — amabara yiganjemo akurwamo kandi agakoreshwa muri theme
- **Imiterere igaragara** — igezweho, ya cyaro, yoroshye cyane, ifite imbaraga, n'ibindi.
- **Imyiyumvire n'umwuka** — by'umwuga, bihanga, bya gicuti, bihenze, n'ibindi.
- **Ibice by'igishushanyo** — imitako, imiterere y'ibikoresho, n'imisusire y'imitunganyirize
- **Icyitegererezo cy'inyuguti** — imiterere y'imyandikire ijyana na theme igaragara

### 4. Imiterere y'Ibirimo {#4-content-structure}

- Paji nyamukuru zikenewe (Ahabanza, Ibyerekeye, Serivisi, Aho Tubarizwa, n'ibindi)
- Igice cya blog cyangwa amakuru gikenewe
- Imikorere ya e-commerce ikenewe
- Ibikorwa byihariye cyangwa integrations

### 5. Gusuzuma no Kwemeza {#5-review-and-confirmation}

- Incamake y'amakuru yose yatanzwe
- Kwemeza mbere y'uko gukora igishushanyo bitangira

## Imyitozo Myiza yo Kohereza Amafoto {#photo-upload-best-practices}

### Ibyo Wakohereza {#what-to-upload}

- **Icyitegererezo cy'ikirango** — ibirango, ingero z'amabara, cyangwa amabwiriza y'ikirango
- **Imbuga z'abo mupiganwa** — amafoto ya ecran y'imbuga ushima
- **Mood boards** — ikusanyirizo ry'amashusho agaragaza ubwiza wifuza
- **Amafoto y'ibicuruzwa** — niba ufite ibicuruzwa byo kwerekana
- **Amafoto y'itsinda cyangwa ibiro** — ku kumenyekanisha umuntu ku giti cye cyangwa k'umwuga
- **Amashusho y'imibereho** — agaragaza abo ugamije kugeraho

### Ibyo Wakwirinda {#what-to-avoid}

- **Amashusho adafite ireme** — amafoto atagaragara neza cyangwa afite pixels zigaragara
- **Amashusho adafitanye isano** — amafoto atagaragaza ikirango cyawe
- **Amashusho arimo inyandiko nyinshi** — amafoto ya ecran arimo inyandiko nyinshi
- **Ibikoresho bifite uburenganzira bw'umuhanzi** — menya neza ko ufite uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amashusho
- **GIF zigenda** — amashusho adahinduka gusa ni yo atunganywa

### Inama zo Kubona Ibisubizo Byiza {#tips-for-best-results}

1. **Ohereza amashusho 2-3** — amashusho menshi atanga imiterere yumvikana kurushaho
2. **Koresha amafoto yo ku rwego rwo hejuru** — amashusho agaragara neza kandi afite urumuri ruhagije ni yo akora neza
3. **Shyiramo ubudasa** — vanga amabara, imiterere, n'ikivugwaho
4. **Tanga imiterere** — sobanura muri make impamvu wahisemo buri shusho
5. **Vuga neza** — niba ukunda igice runaka, kigaragaze

## Ibisohoka by'Icyerekezo cy'Igishushanyo {#design-direction-output}

Nyuma y'Ikiganiro cyo Kumenya Iby'ibanze, Theme Builder itanga:

- **Urutonde rw'amabara** — amabara y'ibanze, ayunganira, n'ay'inyongera agaragaza
- **Typography** — imiryango y'imyandikire y'imitwe n'inyandiko y'umubiri
- **Imiterere y'itunganyirizwa** — intera, guhuza, n'imiterere y'ibice
- **Ibice bigaragara** — udushushanyo, imitako, n'ibice by'imitako
- **Mood board** — ishusho igaragaza icyerekezo cy'igishushanyo

## Intambwe Zikurikira {#next-steps}

Nyuma yo kurangiza Ikiganiro cyo Kumenya Iby'ibanze:

1. Suzuma icyerekezo cy'igishushanyo cyakozwe
2. Tanga ibitekerezo cyangwa usabe ko hagira ibihindurwa
3. Komeza ku ntambwe ya [Icyerekezo cy'Igishushanyo](./design-direction.md)
4. Theme Builder ikora theme yawe yihariye
