# Filter: wu_connector_enforcement_option_keys {#filter-wuconnectorenforcementoptionkeys}

Yungurura urutonde rw’amazina y’amahitamo y’abatanga AI akurikizwa avuye ku rubuga nyamukuru.

Ongeramo amazina y’amahitamo ya plugin z’abatanga AI zihariye cyangwa z’abandi bantu kugira ngo igenamiterere ryazo naryo rizaragwe ku rubuga nyamukuru ku mbuga zishamikiyeho.

## Ibipimo {#parameters}

| Izina | Ubwoko | Ibisobanuro |
|------|------|-------------|
| $keys | `string[]` | Amazina y’amahitamo agomba gukurikizwa (asanzwe arimo amazina ya connector yabonetse mu buryo bwikora na EXTRA_PROVIDER_OPTIONS). |
