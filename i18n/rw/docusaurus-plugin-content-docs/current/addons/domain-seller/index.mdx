# Ugurisha Domeni

**Multisite Ultimate: Ugurisha Domeni** ihindura umuyoboro wawe wa WordPress Multisite ukaba urubuga rukomeye rwo kugurishirizaho domeni. Reka abakiriya biyandikishe domeni zihariye ako kanya mu gihe cyo kwiyandikisha cyangwa bari muri dashboard yabo, hamwe no kugenzura niba ziboneka ako kanya n’ibiciro bisobanutse.

### Kuki Ugurisha Domeni? {#why-domain-seller}

Kwiyandikisha domeni ni kimwe mu bicuruzwa by’inyongera bisabwa cyane ku rubuga urwo ari rwo rwose rwa WaaS (Urubuga nka Serivisi). Aho kohereza abakiriya bawe ku muyandikishaji wo hanze, ushobora gutanga domeni ako kanya — ukagumana inyungu nyinshi mu muyoboro wawe kandi ugaha abakiriya ubunararibonye butarimo inzitizi.

### Ibintu by’ingenzi biranga {#key-features}

#### Gushyigikira Abayandikishaji ba Domeni Benshi {#multiple-domain-registrar-support}
* **OpenSRS** — Kwiyandikisha, kongerera igihe, no gucunga byuzuye
* **Namecheap** — Ihuzabikorwa ryuzuye na WhoisGuard n’igerageza rya sandbox
* **HostAfrica** — Gushakisha domeni, ibiciro, kwiyandikisha, kwimura, DNS, n’uburinzi bwa ID binyuze kuri Domains Reseller API
* **Openprovider** — Ibiciro by’abacuruzi bongeye kugurisha, kwiyandikisha, kongerera igihe, kwimura, DNS, ubwirinzi bw’ibanga bwa WHOIS, no guhuza TLD
* **Hostinger** — Gushakisha no kwiyandikisha domeni ukoresheje shared Hostinger integration token
* **Imiterere yaguka** — Yubakiwe ku buryo bwa Integration Registry pattern, bigatuma byoroha kongeramo abandi batanga serivisi
* **Igerageza ry’ihuza ririmo imbere** — Emeza ibiranga umutanga serivisi n’uko ihuza rikora mbere yo kujya live

#### Umuyobozi wo Gushyiraho Ugufasha {#guided-setup-wizard}
* **Gutangira ukoresheje gukanda rimwe** — Ikuyobora mu igenamiterere ry’umutanga serivisi, kuzana TLD, no gukora igicuruzwa cya domeni
* **Guhuza TLD mu buryo bwikora** — Izana TLD zose ziboneka n’ibiciro bya wholesale bivuye ku batanga serivisi wahuje
* **Gukora igicuruzwa gisanzwe** — Ikora igicuruzwa cya domeni cyiteguye gukoreshwa gifite ibyemezo bya markup bifatika

#### Ibiciro bya Domeni Bihinduka {#flexible-domain-pricing}
* **Uburyo butatu bwa markup** — Markup y’ijanisha, markup ihamye, cyangwa igiciro gihamye kuri buri TLD
* **Ibicuruzwa byihariye bya TLD** — Kora ibicuruzwa bya domeni bitandukanye ku matsinda ya TLD atandukanye (.com/.net, .io, kode z’ibihugu, n’ibindi)
* **Ibiciro byo gutangira** — Tanga igiciro kigabanyijwe cy’umwaka wa mbere kugira ngo ukurure abiyandikisha bashya
* **Ibiciro byo mu gihe nyacyo** — Ibiciro bya wholesale biva ku batanga serivisi live hamwe na caching ifite ubwenge
* **Gushyigikira coupon** — Gukorana byuzuye na kode z’igabanyirizwa za Multisite Ultimate

#### Uburinzi bw’Ibanga bwa WHOIS {#whois-privacy-protection}
* **Igenamiterere kuri buri gicuruzwa** — Shyira ubwirinzi bw’ibanga bwa WHOIS kuri Byahagaritswe, Bihora Birimo, cyangwa Guhitamo k’Umukiriya
* **Umukiriya abyemera ku bushake** — Iyo byashyizwe kuri Guhitamo k’Umukiriya, akazu ko gushyiraho ikimenyetso muri checkout kemerera abakiriya kongeramo uburinzi bw’ibanga
* **Ibiciro bishobora kugenwa** — Shyiraho igiciro cy’umwaka ku nyongera y’ubwirinzi bw’ibanga, cyangwa uyitange ku buntu
* **Ihuzabikorwa n’umwandikishaji** — Ihita ikoresha WhoisGuard (Namecheap) cyangwa ubwirinzi bw’ibanga bwa WHOIS (OpenSRS) mu gihe cyo kwiyandikisha

#### Ubunararibonye bwa Checkout Buhurijwe Hamwe {#unified-checkout-experience}
* **Uburyo butatu bwa domeni** — Abakiriya bahitamo hagati ya subdomain (y’ubuntu), kwiyandikisha domeni nshya, cyangwa guhuza domeni isanzwe basanzwe bafite
* **Gushakisha domeni mu gihe nyacyo** — Kugenzura niba iboneka bikoresha AJAX nta gusubizaho paji
* **Ibyifuzo by’ubwenge** — Ibyifuzo bya TLD zindi iyo domeni yifujwe yafashwe
* **Ibiciro bisobanutse** — Kwerekana neza igiciro cyo kwiyandikisha, igiciro cyo kongerera igihe, n’amafaranga y’ubwirinzi bw’ibanga bwa WHOIS atari ngombwa
* **Imirima y’amakuru y’umwandikishwa** — Ikusanya amakuru asabwa y’umwandikishwa (izina, aderesi, telefone) aho nyine muri checkout
* **URL y’urubuga ikorwa mu buryo bwikora** — Aderesi y’urubuga ihita ikurwa kuri domeni yahiswemo

#### Sisitemu yo Kongerera Igihe mu Buryo Bwikora {#auto-renewal-system}
* **Kongera igihe bifitanye isano n’ubunyamuryango** — Domeni zongerwa igihe mu buryo bwikora igihe ubunyamuryango bw’umukiriya bugikora
* **Gahunda y’ubwenge** — Imirimo yo kongerera igihe ishyirwa ku murongo hashingiwe ku matariki domeni zirangiriraho
* **Logique yo kongera kugerageza** — Kongera kugerageza mu buryo bw’ubwenge igihe kugerageza kongerera igihe byanze
* **Imenyesha rya email** — Integuza zishobora kugenwa ku bijyanye no kongerera igihe biri hafi, kongerera igihe byagenze neza, kunanirwa, no kurangira

#### Gucunga DNS k’Umukiriya {#customer-dns-management}
* **DNS yikorera** — Abakiriya bashobora kongeramo, guhindura, no gusiba DNS records (A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, TXT) muri dashboard yabo
* **Imiterere ya domeni** — Imiterere yo kwiyandikisha mu gihe nyacyo, amatariki yo kurangiriraho, n’amakuru ya nameserver
* **Guhuza domeni mu buryo bwikora** — Domeni ziyandikishijwe cyangwa izisanzwe zihita zihuzwa n’urubuga rw’umukiriya

#### Ibikoresho bya Admin {#admin-tools}
* **Kwiyandikisha domeni n’intoki** — Iyandikishe domeni mu izina ry’abakiriya uhereye muri admin panel
* **Gukurikirana domeni** — Reba domeni zose z’abakiriya, imiterere yazo, n’amatariki yo kurangiriraho uhereye kuri paji imwe
* **Logging yihariye ku mutanga serivisi** — Imiyoboro ya log itandukanye kuri buri mwandikishaji (urugero, domain-seller-namecheap.log)
* **Ibisobanuro byo kwiyandikisha** — Reba amakuru y’umwandikishwa n’amakuru yo kwiyandikisha kuri paji nyamukuru yo guhindura domeni

### Abatanga Serivisi Bashyigikiwe {#supported-providers}

* **OpenSRS** — Ihuzabikorwa ryuzuye ryo kwiyandikisha, kongerera igihe, gucunga DNS, n’ubwirinzi bw’ibanga bwa WHOIS
* **Namecheap** — Ihuzabikorwa ryuzuye ryo kwiyandikisha, kongerera igihe, kugenzura kuboneka mu byiciro, na WhoisGuard y’ubuntu
* **HostAfrica** — Kwiyandikisha, kongerera igihe, kwimura, gucunga DNS, kubona kode ya EPP, registrar lock, n’uburinzi bwa ID
* **Openprovider** — Kwiyandikisha, kongerera igihe, kwimura, gucunga DNS, kubona kode ya EPP, registrar lock, ubwirinzi bw’ibanga bwa WHOIS, no guhuza TLD z’abacuruzi bongeye kugurisha
* **Hostinger** — Kwiyandikisha, gushakisha domeni, kuvugurura nameserver, registrar lock, n’ubwirinzi bw’ibanga bwa WHOIS; kongerera igihe bikorwa na Hostinger auto-renew
* **GoDaddy** — Kwiyandikisha no kongerera igihe
* **ResellerClub** — Kwiyandikisha, kongerera igihe, no gucunga DNS
* **NameSilo** — Kwiyandikisha no kongerera igihe
* **Enom** — Kwiyandikisha, kongerera igihe, no gucunga DNS

### Ibisobanuro bya Tekiniki {#technical-details}

* **Bisaba:** PHP 7.4+, Multisite Ultimate 2.4.12+
* **Imiterere:** Integration Registry pattern hamwe na Domain_Selling_Capability interface
* **Autoloading:** Jetpack autoloader hamwe na PSR-4 class mapping
* **Ibipimo ngenderwaho:** WordPress coding standards, strict typing, prepared statements

## Gushyiraho {#installation}

1. Menya neza ko **Multisite Ultimate v2.4.12+** yashyizweho kandi yakorewe activation ku rwego rwa network
2. Ohereza plugin kuri `/wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite-domain-seller/`
3. Kora network-activation ya plugin uhereye muri **Plugins** muri WordPress Network Admin
4. **Setup Wizard** itangira ku buryo bwikora ku activation ya mbere — kurikiza intambwe kugira ngo:
   * Uhuze domain registrar yawe
   * Ugerageze ihuza
   * Winjize TLDs ziboneka n’ibiciro bya wholesale
   * Ureme domain product yawe ya mbere ifite markup y’ibiciro
5. Ongeraho field ya **Domain Selection** kuri checkout form yawe muri **Ultimate Multisite > Checkout Forms**

## Ibibazo Bikunze Kubazwa {#frequently-asked-questions}

### Ni verisiyo iyihe ya Multisite Ultimate ikenewe? {#what-version-of-multisite-ultimate-is-required}

Verisiyo 2.4.12 cyangwa irenze. Addon ntabwo ijyana na WP Ultimo v1.x cyangwa verisiyo za Ultimate Multisite ziri munsi ya 2.4.12.

### Ni domain providers abahe bashyigikiwe? {#which-domain-providers-are-supported}

Registrars icyenda zirashyigikiwe: **OpenSRS**, **Namecheap**, **HostAfrica**, **Openprovider**, **Hostinger**, **GoDaddy**, **ResellerClub**, **NameSilo**, na **Enom**. Ubushobozi buratandukana bitewe na provider kuko registrar APIs zitanga ibikorwa bitandukanye; reba setup guide kugira ngo ubone incamake ya provider ku wundi. Imiterere ya Integration Registry ya addon ituma kongeramo custom providers byoroha — reba [developer guide](/developer/integration-guide/custom-registrar).

### Nshobora gutanga WHOIS privacy protection? {#can-i-offer-whois-privacy-protection}

Yego. Buri domain product ifite igenamiterere rya WHOIS Privacy rifite uburyo butatu: Disabled, Always Included, cyangwa Customer Choice. Iyo yashyizwe kuri Customer Choice, customers babona checkbox muri checkout iriho igiciro (cyangwa "Free" kuri WhoisGuard ya Namecheap). Privacy flag ihita yoherezwa kuri registrar igihe cyo kwandikisha.

### Nshobora gushyiraho ibiciro bitandukanye kuri TLDs zitandukanye? {#can-i-set-different-pricing-for-different-tlds}

Yego. Rema domain products zitandukanye ku matsinda atandukanye ya TLD. Buri product ishobora kugira markup type yayo (percentage, fixed markup, cyangwa fixed price), TLDs zishyigikiwe, n’ibiciro by’intangiriro.

### Ni checkout options izihe customers babona? {#what-checkout-options-do-customers-see}

Field ya Domain Selection iha customers tabs eshatu: **Subdomain** (koresha subdomain y’ubuntu kuri network yawe), **Register New Domain** (shaka kandi wandikishe domain nshya), na **Existing Domain** (huza domain basanzwe bafite). Uburyo washoboje gusa ni bwo buzagaragara.

### Ishyigikira automatic renewals? {#does-it-support-automatic-renewals}

Yego. Domains zishobora gushyirwa kuri auto-renew kandi zihuzwa na membership status ya customer. Renewal payments zitunganywa binyuze muri payment gateway isanzwe ya customer.

### Customers bashobora gucunga DNS records zabo? {#can-customers-manage-their-dns-records}

Yego. Customers babona DNS management interface muri dashboard yabo kugira ngo bongereho, bahindure, kandi basibe records za A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, na TXT. DNS management iboneka kuri OpenSRS, ResellerClub, Enom, HostAfrica, na Openprovider. Hostinger DNS kuri domains zakiriwe icungwa n’ihuzwa rya domain-mapping rya Hostinger ry’ibanze.

### Hari setup wizard? {#is-there-a-setup-wizard}

Yego. Setup wizard ikora ku buryo bwikora ku activation ya mbere kandi ikakunyura mu guhuza registrar, kwinjiza TLDs, no kurema domain product yawe ya mbere. Ushobora kandi kongera gukoresha wizard uhereye mu igenamiterere rya addon.
