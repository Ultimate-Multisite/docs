# Ihuzwa rya WooCommerce

Ihuzwa rya WooCommerce rikora mu buryo busa cyane n'uko Irembo ryacu ry'ubwishyu rikorwa n'intoki rikora. Riha abakoresha bawe ubwisanzure bwinshi, rikabemerera guhitamo uburyo ubwo ari bwo bwose bwo kwishyura buboneka ku ishyirwaho rya WooCommerce ryawe. Kandi riroroshye: igihe cyo kwishyurira umukoresha nikirangira, ifatabuguzi ry'umukoresha rishyirwa mu gihagaritswe. Muri icyo gihe, itegeko rizakorwa ku ishyirwaho rya WooCommerce ry'urubuga nyamukuru rw'urusobe. Hanyuma umukoresha akakira inyemezabwishyu muri email ye, irimo link yo gukora ubwishyu. Ubwishyu nibumara kwakirwa, ifatabuguzi riravugururwa.

## Ibiranga

- Ihuzwa ryoroshye hagati ya Ultimate Multisite na WooCommerce
- Uburyo bwinshi bwo kwishyura buraboneka
- Kwishyuza gusubiramo byikoresha hakoreshejwe core y'amafatabuguzi irimo, hamwe n'ibyongeweho by'umugereka wa WooCommerce Subscriptions ku buryo butari itegeko
- Igiteranyo cyo kwishyura kirimo imisoro ya Ultimate Multisite nk'imirongo y'amafaranga ya WooCommerce itandukanye kandi idasoreshwa, kugira ngo abakiriya bashobore gusuzuma ingano y'imisoro mbere yo kwishyura

## Inyandiko mfashanyigisho

**Gushyiraho Ihuzwa rya WooCommerce**

**Kwagura amahitamo yawe yo kwishyura ukoresheje Ultimate Multisite: Ihuzwa rya WooCommerce**

## Gushyiraho

1. Ohereza 'ultimate-multisite-woocommerce' muri directory ya '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Koresha umugereka unyuze muri menu ya 'Imigereka' muri WordPress
3. Kurikiza Wizard intambwe ku yindi kugira ngo ushyireho umugereka

## Imfashanyigisho yo gukoresha

_**ICYITONDERWA GIKOMEYE: Iyi ngingo ivuga kuri Ultimate Multisite version 2.x.**_

_**ICYITONDERWA:** Ultimate Multisite: Ihuzwa rya WooCommerce risaba ko WooCommerce iba ikoreshejwe nibura ku rubuga rwawe nyamukuru._

Twumva ko _Stripe_ na _PayPal_ bitaboneka mu bihugu bimwe na bimwe, bikagabanya cyangwa bikabangamira abakoresha Ultimate Multisite gukoresha neza umugereka wacu. Ni yo mpamvu twakoze **add-on yo guhuzamo _WooCommerce_**.

_WooCommerce_ ni umugereka wa eCommerce ukunzwe cyane. Abakora porogaramu ku isi hose bakoze add-ons zo guhuzamo amarembo atandukanye y'ubwishyu. Twabyungukiyemo kugira ngo twagure amarembo y'ubwishyu ushobora gukoresha muri sisitemu yo kwishyuza ya Ultimate Multisite.

### Imirongo y'amafaranga y'umusoro mu kwishyura

Iyo kwishyura kwa Ultimate Multisite kurimo imisoro, ihuzwa ryongerera buri ngano y'umusoro wa Ultimate Multisite muri kwishyura kwa WooCommerce nk'umurongo w'amafaranga utandukanye kandi udasoreshwa. Ibi bituma igiteranyo cy'umusoro kigaragara mbere yo kwishyura, kandi bikabuza WooCommerce kongera gusoresha amafaranga y'umusoro.

## Uko wacunga ubwishyu

Ihuzwa rya WooCommerce rikora mu buryo busa cyane n'uko **Irembo ryacu ry'ubwishyu rikorwa n'intoki** rikora. Inenge ni uko buri bwishyu bugomba kwishyurwa n'intoki ku iherezo ry'igihe cyo kwishyura. Dore uko inzira ikora:

  * Igihe cy'umukoresha cyo kwishyurirwamo nikirangira, ubunyamuryango bushyirwa **mu gihagaritswe**. Muri icyo gihe, itegeko rizakorwa ku ishyirwaho rya _WooCommerce_ riri ku rubuga nyamukuru rw'urusobe rwawe.

  * Hanyuma abakoresha bakakira inyemezabwishyu muri email yabo, irimo link yo gukora ubwishyu (iyo link inagaragara mu mateka yabo yo kwishyura avuye kuri dashboard y'urubuga rwabo ruto). Iyo link yohereza umukoresha kuri _**WooCommerce**_ **kwishyura** , aho abakoresha bazashobora guhitamo rimwe mu **marembo ya _WooCommerce_ aboneka** kugira ngo batunganye ubwishyu bwabo.

  * Ubwishyu nibumara kwakirwa, ubunyamuryango **buravugururwa** , ubwishyu bwandikwa muri Ultimate Multisite kandi itegeko rigashyirwaho ko ryuzuye.

### Icyitonderwa cy'ingenzi:

  * Imikorere isanzwe ya Ultimate Multisite iracyakorana n'ubu buryo bw'ihuzwa. Urugero, **gutanga refunds** uhereye kuri screen ya **Gucunga Ifatabuguzi** bizavugana na WooCommerce kugira ngo bitange refund hakoreshejwe uburyo bw'ubwishyu bwatoranyijwe.

  * Ultimate Multisite: Ihuzwa rya WooCommerce ryishingikiriza kuri WP-Cron kugira ngo rikore. Ibi bikora neza cyane kenshi, ariko niba urubuga rufite traffic nkeya, cron ntibwirwa kugenzura events zateganyijwe kandi event igasibwa, bishobora gutera gutinda mu gukora.

## Umugereka wa WooCommerce Subscriptions

Gutangira ubwishyu buri kwezi ukoresheje intoki si byiza ku bakoresha ba nyuma, kuko abonnements ziba zitezwe ko zisubiramo mu buryo bwikora kuri buri billing cycle. WooCommerce Integration add-on ubu izana subscriptions code y’ibanze, bityo recurring products buri gihe zikoresha subscriptions ku recurring billing.

**Ntukeneye** kugura cyangwa gushyiraho [WooCommerce Subscriptions plugin](https://woocommerce.com/products/woocommerce-subscriptions/) yihariye kugira ngo ugurishe recurring products ukoresheje iyi integration. Niba iyo plugin iguzwe, igashyirwaho, kandi ikaboneka kuri site yawe nyamukuru, Ultimate Multisite izayikoresha kandi WooCommerce izagaragaza ibindi bintu byo gucunga subscriptions. Iyo itaboneka, subscriptions core izanye na yo ikomeza gucunga recurring products.

![Igenamiterere rya WooCommerce subscription integration](/img/addons/woocommerce-subscription-integration.png)

Kandi ibyo birahagije. Abakoresha bawe ba nyuma ntibazongera gukenera gukora ubwishyu n’intoki ku mpera ya billing cycle yabo kuri recurring products.

Uretse order ikorwa kuri site yawe nyamukuru munsi ya _WooCommerce_, izanakora subscription kuri buri Account.

### Imyitwarire yo guhagarika by’agateganyo iyo renewal yananiwe

Mu buryo busanzwe, memberships zikomeza gukora igihe WooCommerce Subscriptions itunganya failed-payment retry schedule yayo. Ibi bigumana grace window isanzwe ya WooCommerce Subscriptions kugira ngo renewal yongeye gukorwa neza ishobore gusubiza subscription na membership ku murongo umwe itabanje guhagarika access imburagihe.

Administrators bifuza access control ikaze kurushaho bashobora gufungura **Suspend Memberships Immediately on Failed Renewals** mu igenamiterere rya WooCommerce Integration. Iyo iri genamiterere ryo guhitamo rifunguye, Ultimate Multisite ihagarika by’agateganyo membership ifitanye isano ako kanya renewal payment ikimara kunanirwa aho gutegereza ko WooCommerce Subscriptions retry handling irangira.

Developers bashobora gusimbura icyo cyemezo kimwe bakoresheje filter `wu_woo_suspend_on_payment_failure`. Hook reference pages zikorerwa muri source ya add-on, bityo iyi ncamake ivuga gusa izina rya filter ku custom suspension policies.
