# Muyunguruzi: wp_ultimo_woocommerce_checkout_fields_to_clean {#filter-wpultimowoocommercecheckoutfieldstoclean}

Yemerera abatezimbere kuyungurura ibice bisanzwe by’aderesi yo kwishyura ya WooCommerce.

Kubera ko Ultimate Multisite ifite ibice by’aderesi yo kwishyura, bishobora kuba byumvikana gukuraho ibice bisanzwe bigaragazwa na WooCommerce ku ifishi ya checkout. Ku buryo busanzwe, nta gaciro gahabwa uru rutonde, bityo ibice byose byashyizweho kuri WooCommerce kugira ngo bigaragazwe biragumaho.

### Kuva kuri {#since}

- 2.0.0
### Inkomoko {#source}

Bisobanuwe muri [`inc/gateways/class-woocommerce-gateway.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite-woocommerce/blob/main/inc/gateways/class-woocommerce-gateway.php#L277) ku murongo wa 277


## Ibisubizwa {#returns}
Urutonde rw’ibice byo gukuraho. Indangagaciro ziboneka zirimo billing_first_name, billing_last_name, billing_company, billing_country, billing_address_1, billing_address_2, billing_city, billing_state, billing_postcode, billing_email, na billing_phone.
