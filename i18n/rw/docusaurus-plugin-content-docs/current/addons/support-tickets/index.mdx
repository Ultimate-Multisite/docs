# Amatike y'Ubufasha

**Amatike y'Ubufasha** ni addon ikomeye ya Ultimate Multisite yongeramo sisitemu yuzuye y'amatike y'ubufasha muri network yawe ya WordPress. Iboneye ku mbuga za SaaS, abatanga WaaS (Urubuga nka Serivisi), na network za multisite zikeneye gutanga ubufasha ku bakiriya haba ku rwego rwa network no ku rwego rwa buri rubuga.

## Kuki Wahitamo Amatike y'Ubufasha?

* **Ubufasha ku Nzego Nyinshi**: Abayobozi ba network bakemura amatike areba network yose, mu gihe ba nyiri imbuga bayobora ubufasha bw'abakiriya babo
* **Imigendekere y'Akazi ya Kinyamwuga**: Ubuyobozi bwuzuye bw'ubuzima bwose bw'itike burimo ibyihutirwa, imimerere, n'abo zahawe
* **Uburambe bw'Umukiriya**: Interineti nziza, yisubiza neza ku bikoresho bitandukanye, ituma abakiriya babona ubufasha bitagoranye
* **Itekanye kandi Yizewe**: Yubatswe hakurikijwe uburyo bwiza bw'umutekano bwa WordPress kandi inoze imikorere ya database
* **Kwihuza Bidakoma**: Ikorana neza n'ubuyobozi bw'abakiriya n'imbuga bwa Ultimate Multisite

## Ibiranga by'Ingenzi

**Ubuyobozi Bwuzuye bw'Amatike**

* Sisitemu yihariye yo guha amatike nimero kugira ngo byoroshye kuyerekezaho
* Inzego z'ibyihutirwa zishobora kugenwa (Hasi, Bisanzwe, Hejuru, Bikomeye)
* Imigendekere yuzuye y'imimerere (Ifunguye, Irimo Gukorwaho, Itegereje, Yakemuwe, Yafunzwe)
* Gushyira amatike mu byiciro (Rusange, Tekiniki, Kwishyuza, Gusaba Ikiranga, Raporo y'Ikosa)
* Guha amatike abakozi b'ubufasha hamwe n'uburyo bwo kuyatanga mu buryo bwikora

**Ibiganiro Bifite Imirongo**

* Gukurikiranya ibiganiro byuzuye ku bisubizo by'amatike
* Imiterere itandukanye ku bisubizo by'abakozi n'iby'abakiriya
* Inyandiko z'abakozi z'ibanga abakiriya badashobora kubona
* Interineti ikoreshwa na AJAX mu gihe nyacyo
* Muhinduzi w'inyandiko ugezweho ufite amahitamo yo gutunganya imiterere

**Imigereka ya Dosiyeri**

* Kohereza dosiyeri mu mutekano hamwe n'ubufasha bwa gukurura-ukarekura
* Amabwiriza ashobora kugenwa ku bwoko bwa dosiyeri n'ingano
* Umutekano wateye imbere ufite gutahura dosiyeri mbi
* Gukurura dosiyeri kurinzwe hakoreshejwe igenzura ry'uburenganzira bwo kugera

**Amamenyesha Yubwenge**

* Imenyesha rya email ryikora ku matike mashya, ibisubizo, n'impinduka z'imimerere
* Inyandikorugero za email zishobora guhindurwa
* Amamenyesha ashingiye ku nshingano ku bakozi n'abakiriya
* Impinduragaciro z'inyandikorugero zihinduka ku butumwa bwihariye

**Interineti Ikomeye y'Ubuyobozi**

* Urutonde rw'amatike rwateye imbere rufite gutondeka no kuyungurura
* Guhindura vuba imimerere, icyihutirwa, n'abo amatike yahawe
* Ibikorwa byinshi icyarimwe ku buyobozi bukora neza
* Dashboard y'imibare ifite ibipimo by'imikorere
* Panel yuzuye y'amagenamiterere

**Portal Nziza y'Abakiriya**

* Interineti isukuye, yisubiza neza ku bikoresho byose
* Igishushanyo kibanza kuri mobile
* Gushakisha no kuyungurura byateye imbere
* Muhinduzi ugezweho ufite umurongo w'ibikoresho byo gutunganya imiterere
* Amafishi yo kohereza amatike yoroshye gukoresha

**Kwihuza Guhinduka**

* Koresha shortcodes cyangwa ibice bya UI bya Ultimate Multisite
* Erekana amafishi y'amatike n'urutonde aho ari ho hose ku rubuga rwawe
* Ikorana neza n'imiterere ya network ya multisite
* Ishobora kwagurwa hakoreshejwe hooks na filters

## Iboneye kuri

* Imbuga za SaaS zikeneye ubufasha bw'abakiriya
* Abatanga WaaS bayobora imbuga nyinshi z'abakiriya
* Imbuga z'ubunyamuryango zifite ubufasha bugabanyijwe mu nzego
* Abayobozi ba network bayobora brands nyinshi
* Ba nyiri imbuga batanga serivisi ku bakiriya

## Kwinjiza

1. Menya neza ko plugin ya Ultimate Multisite yinjijwe kandi ikora kuri network yawe ya WordPress
2. Ohereza dosiyeri za addon muri directory yawe ya `/wp-content/plugins/`
3. Koresha plugin kuri network unyuze muri menu ya 'Amacomekwamo' muri WordPress
4. Jya kuri Ubuyobozi bwa Network > Amatike y'Ubufasha kugira ngo ugene amagenamiterere yawe

## Ibibazo Bikunze Kubazwa

## Ese ibi bisaba Ultimate Multisite?

Yego, iyi addon isaba ko Ultimate Multisite iba yarinjijwe kandi ikora. Yihuza byimbitse n'ibiranga ubuyobozi bw'abakiriya n'imbuga bya Ultimate Multisite.

## Ese ba nyiri imbuga bashobora gukemura ubufasha bw'abakiriya babo?

Rwose! Ba nyiri imbuga bashobora gukoresha sisitemu y'amatike kugira ngo batange ubufasha ku bakiriya babo, mu gihe abayobozi ba network bashobora gukemura amatike y'ubufasha areba network yose.

## Abakiriya bohereza bate amatike?

Ushobora kongera amafishi yo kohereza amatike aho ari ho hose ku rubuga rwawe ukoresheje shortcodes cyangwa ibice bya UI bya Ultimate Multisite. Abakiriya buzuza ifishi gusa hanyuma bagatanga icyifuzo cyabo.

## Nshobora guhindura imimerere n'ibyihutirwa by'amatike?

Yego! Ushobora guhindura byuzuye inzego z'ibyihutirwa, imimerere, n'ubwoko bw'amatike mu migenamiterere y'ubuyobozi bwa network kugira ngo bihure n'imigendekere y'ubufasha bwawe.

## Ese ishyigikira imigereka ya dosiyeri?

Yego, abakiriya n'abakozi bashobora kongera dosiyeri ku matike no ku bisubizo. Ufite igenzura ryuzuye ku bwoko bwa dosiyeri zemewe n'imbibi z'ingano ku bw'umutekano.

## Amamenyesha akemurwa ate?

Sisitemu yohereza mu buryo bwikora amamenyesha ya email ku matike mashya, ibisubizo, n'impinduka z'imimerere. Ushobora guhindura inyandikorugero zose za email mu migenamiterere.

## Nshobora guha amatike abakozi b'ubufasha runaka?

Yego, ushobora guha amatike abakozi intoki cyangwa ugakoresha gutanga mu buryo bwikora kugira ngo amatike atangwe mu buryo bwikora.

## Ese hari inyandiko z'ibanga z'abakozi?

Yego, abakozi bashobora kongeramo ibisubizo by'ibanga bigaragarira abandi bakozi gusa, bitagaragarira abakiriya.

## Ese ikorana neza na mobile?

Rwose! Interineti yose yubatswe hakurikijwe uburyo bubanza kuri mobile kandi ikora neza ku bikoresho byose.

## Nshobora kwerekana urutonde rw'amatike n'amafishi nkoresheje shortcodes?

Yego! Shortcodes ziboneka:
* `[wu_support_tickets]` - Erekana urutonde rw'amatike y'umukiriya
* `[wu_support_ticket_form]` - Erekana ifishi yo kohereza itike nshya
* `[wu_support_ticket_view ticket_id="123"]` - Erekana itike runaka

## Ni bwoko ki bwa dosiyeri bushobora koherezwa?

Ushobora kugena ubwoko bwa dosiyeri bwemewe mu migenamiterere. Sisitemu irimo gusuzuma umutekano kwateye imbere kugira ngo irinde dosiyeri mbi.

## Nshobora kohereza hanze amakuru y'amatike?

Yego, iyi addon irimo ubushobozi bwo kohereza hanze amakuru y'amatike n'imibare.
