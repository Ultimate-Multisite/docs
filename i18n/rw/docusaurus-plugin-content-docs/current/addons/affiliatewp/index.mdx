# AffiliateWP

Multisite Ultimate: Guhuza na AffiliateWP

Koresha AffiliateWP ifite imbaraga kugira ngo wongere umubare w’abakiriya ba Ultimo Network yawe!

## Kwinjiza

1. Ohereza 'ultimate-multisite-git ' muri dosiye '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Koresha ubwomekwa unyuze muri menu ya 'Ubwomekwa' muri WordPress
3. Kurikiza Wizard intambwe ku yindi kugira ngo ushyireho ubwomekwa

## Uko Bikoreshwa

[AffiliateWP](https://affiliatewp.com/) yabaye igisubizo gikunzwe cyane iyo hubakwa porogaramu y’abafatanyabikorwa ku bucuruzi bwawe bwo kuri interineti. Ultimate Multisite: AffiliateWP ni ikiraro kizagushoboza kubaka ikintu nk’icyo ku ihuriro ryawe ryishyurwa.

## Gutangira

Mbere na mbere, **ugomba kuba ufite** [**AffiliateWP**](https://affiliatewp.com/) **ikora**! Ntuzashobora gukoresha inyongera yacu niba udafite **ubwomekwa bujyana nayo**. Niba udateganya kwemera ko abakiriya bawe na bo bayikoresha ku mbuga zabo, AffiliateWP igomba kuba ikora ku rubuga rwawe nyamukuru gusa.

Nanone, uzakenera ko **Ultimate Multisite** na **Ultimate Multisite: AffiliateWP** byombi **bikoreshwa** **ku rwego rw’urusobe rwose**.

## Kongera Ultimate Multisite nk’ihuzwa rya AffiliateWP

Ku rubuga rwawe nyamukuru, jya muri Igenamiterere rya Affiliates, hanyuma kuri tab ya Integrations, shyira akamenyetso kuri Ultimate Multisite. Ibi bizabwira [AffiliateWP](https://affiliatewp.com/) gutangira gukurikirana gusura n’impinduka zituruka mu nzira yo kwiyandikisha ya Ultimate Multisite.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: AffiliateWP Integrations settings tab with Ultimate Multisite checkbox -->

_Shira akamenyetso mu gasanduku ka Ultimate Multisite kari hasi hanyuma ubike_

Ibyo ni byo hafi ya byose bisabwa. Gusura guturuka ku mahuza yohereza abandi bizakurikiranywa, maze igihe uwo mukoresha azaba akoze ubwishyu bwe bwa mbere, impinduka izaremwa kandi komisiyo ishyirwe ku ruhande igenerwe umufatanyabikorwa wagize uruhare muri iyo mpinduka.

Icyakora, ubwishyu bwa mbere gusa bwakiriwe buturutse ku mukoresha mushya waremewe ni bwo buzakurikiranwa. Kugira ngo ushoboze ubwishyu busubiramo bwo kohereza abandi, uzakenera [AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals Add-on](https://affiliatewp.com/add-ons/pro/recurring-referrals/)

## Gushoboza Kohereza Abandi Bisubiramo

Nyuma yo kwinjiza [AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals Add-on](https://affiliatewp.com/add-ons/pro/recurring-referrals/), jya muri tab ya Affiliates Settings Recurring Referrals, hanyuma ushoboze ikoreshwa ryo kohereza abandi bisubiramo.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals settings with Enable Recurring Referrals toggle -->

_Byoroshye uko bishoboka: Koresha Enable Recurring Referrals maze byose bibe biratunganye!_

Nanone, ibyo ni byo byose bisabwa!

Ubu, igihe cyose ubwishyu bushya bwakiriwe kuri konti yoherejwe n’undi, umufatanyabikorwa wabigizemo uruhare azahita abona umugabane we!
