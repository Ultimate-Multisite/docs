# Tutor Multisite Compatibility

Umugereka wa WordPress wongeramo gukosora imikoranire ya Tutor LMS mu bidukikije by'imbuga nyinshi.

## Ibisobanuro {#description}

Uyu mugereka utanga gukosora kw'ingenzi kw'imikoranire kugira ngo Tutor LMS ikore ku miyoboro ya WordPress y'imbuga nyinshi. Ukemura kwiyandikisha kw'abakoresha, gucunga abigisha, imipaka y'amasomo, n'indi mikorere yihariye ku mbuga nyinshi.

## Ibiranga {#features}

- **Gucunga Imbonerahamwe z'Ububikoshingiro**: Ihita ibona kandi igakora imbonerahamwe za Tutor LMS zibura zitashoboye gukorwa kubera inzitizi z'imfunguzo mvamahanga mu mbuga nyinshi
- **Kwiyandikisha kw'Abakoresha ku Mbuga Nyinshi**: Abakemura AJAX b'ihariye bo gucunga kwiyandikisha kw'abakoresha ku mbuga zo mu muyoboro
- **Gucunga Uruhare rw'Umwigisha**: Gutanga no gucunga uruhare rw'umwigisha mu buryo bwikora mu bidukikije by'imbuga nyinshi
- **Imipaka y'Amasomo**: Kwihuza na Ultimate Multisite mu gucunga imipaka yo gukora amasomo na funnel hashingiwe kuri gahunda z'urubuga
- **Amagaragaza y'Admin Yihariye**: Urutonde rw'abigisha n'imbonezamikorere yo kubacunga byumva imbuga nyinshi
- **Ibikoresho bya Admin**: Imbonezamikorere yo gucunga ububikoshingiro ku bayobozi b'umuyoboro n'ab'urubuga

## Ibisabwa {#requirements}

- WordPress 5.0+
- PHP 7.4+
- Umugereka wa Tutor LMS (ushyirwa mu bikorwa mu buryo bwikora binyuze ku mutwe wa `Requires Plugins`)
- Ishyirwaho rya WordPress ry'imbuga nyinshi
- Umugereka wa Ultimate Multisite (ku mikorere y'imipaka y'amasomo)

## Gushyiraho {#installation}

1. Ohereza dosiye z'umugereka muri `/wp-content/plugins/tutor-multisite-compatibility/`
2. Menya neza ko Tutor LMS yashyizweho kandi yakorejwe
3. Koresha umugereka unyuze muri menu ya 'Plugins' muri WordPress
4. Umugereka uzahita wihuza na Tutor LMS mu buryo bwikora

## Imikorere Yimuwe Ivanywe muri MU Plugin {#functionality-moved-from-mu-plugin}

Uyu mugereka urimo imikorere yari isanzwe muri `wp-content/mu-plugins/daves.php`:

- Imipaka yo guhindura imiterere y'isomo
- Imipaka yo gukora isomo
- Kwihuza na CartFlows n'imipaka
- Gucunga uruhare rw'umwigisha wa Tutor
- Gukemura kwiyandikisha kw'abakoresha ku mbuga nyinshi
- Kugaragaza ID y'igicuruzwa cy'isomo

## Gucunga Ububikoshingiro {#database-management}

### Ikibazo {#the-problem}

Mu bidukikije bya WordPress by'imbuga nyinshi, gukora imbonerahamwe za Tutor LMS birananirana ku mbonerahamwe zifite inzitizi z'imfunguzo mvamahanga zijya ku mbonerahamwe y'abakoresha. Ibi bibaho kubera ko:

- Imbonerahamwe zihariye z'urubuga zikoresha intangiriro nka `wp_2_tutor_carts`
- Imfunguzo mvamahanga zerekeza kuri `wp_2_users` (itabaho)
- Imbonerahamwe nyakuri y'abakoresha ni `wp_users` (isangiwe mu muyoboro wose)

### Igisubizo {#the-solution}

Uyu mugereka uhita:

1. **Ubona Imbonerahamwe Zibura**: Ugenzura imbonerahamwe zitashoboye gukorwa kubera inzitizi z'imfunguzo mvamahanga
2. **Ukora Imbonerahamwe zifite Aho Zerekeza Hakwiye**: Ukoresha izina rikwiye ry'imbonerahamwe y'abakoresha (`wp_users` mu mbuga nyinshi)
3. **Ukemura Ibihe Bitandukanye**:
   - Gukoresha/kongera gukoresha umugereka
   - Gukora urubuga rushya mu muyoboro
   - Kugenzura imbonerahamwe intoki binyuze mu mbonezamikorere ya admin

### Imbonerahamwe Zirebwa {#affected-tables}

- `tutor_coupon_usages` - Irimo urufunguzo mvamahanga rujya ku mbonerahamwe y'abakoresha
- `tutor_carts` - Irimo urufunguzo mvamahanga rujya ku mbonerahamwe y'abakoresha
- `tutor_notification_preferences` - Imbonerahamwe ya TutorPro ifite urufunguzo mvamahanga rujya ku mbonerahamwe y'abakoresha

### Imbonezamikorere ya Admin {#admin-interface}

Gera ku bikoresho byo gucunga ububikoshingiro kuri:

- **Network Admin**: Settings → Tutor Multisite DB
- **Site Admin**: Tools → Tutor Multisite DB

Imbonezamikorere ikwemerera:
- Kureba imbonerahamwe zibura ku mbuga zose
- Gutangiza gukora imbonerahamwe intoki
- Kugenzura iboneza ry'ububikoshingiro
- Kureba amakuru arambuye y'imiterere
