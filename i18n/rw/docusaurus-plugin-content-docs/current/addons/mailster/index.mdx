# Mailster {#mailster}

Hindura urusobe rwawe rwa multisite rube imashini ikomeye yo kubaka urutonde rwa email ukoresheje addon ya Ultimate Multisite Mailster Integration. Ongeraho abakiriya bashya mu ntonde za email za Mailster mu buryo bwikora igihe biyandikishije cyangwa bakoze kugura, bityo ushyireho ihuzabikorwa ridacika hagati y’urubuga rwawe rw’ubunyamuryango n’ingamba zawe zo kwamamaza kuri email.

## Ibiranga by’ingenzi {#key-features}

**Guhuza Abiyandikishije mu Buryo Bwikora**
* Ongeraho abakiriya ku ntonde za Mailster mu buryo bwikora igihe biyandikishije
* Hitamo guhuza ako kanya igihe cyo kwiyandikisha cyangwa gutegereza icyemezo cy’ubwishyu
* Komeza intonde zawe za email ari nshya kandi zivuguruye utabigizemo uruhare n’intoki

**Imicungire y’Intonde Ihinduka**
* Shyiraho intonde zisanzwe rusange ku bakiriya bashya bose
* Subiramo ibisanzwe ukoresheje intonde zijyanye n’ibicuruzwa runaka ku bukangurambaga bugamije itsinda ryihariye
* Cunga byoroshye ishyirwaho ry’intonde ukoresheje interface imenyerewe ya Mailster

**Amahitamo ya Opt-In Yubahiriza GDPR**
* Hitamo hagati yo kwiyandikisha mu buryo bwikora cyangwa ukwemererwa gushingiye kuri checkbox
* Koresha double opt-in ku kwiyandikisha kwemejwe
* Erekana/hisha checkbox ya opt-in ukurikije ibisabwa byo kubahiriza amategeko

**Guhuza Amakuru mu Buryo Buclever**
* Huza mu buryo bwikora amazina y’abakiriya n’amakuru yo kubavugisha
* Huza aderesi zo kwishyuriraho n’imyanya y’abiyandikishije
* Kurikirana amatariki yo kwiyandikisha ku bukangurambaga bugamije itsinda ryihariye

**Igenzura ku Rwego rw’Igicuruzwa**
* Shyira intonde zitandukanye ku bicuruzwa bitandukanye
* Kora ubukangurambaga bugamije itsinda ryihariye hashingiwe ku byo abakiriya bagura
* Zimya Mailster ku bicuruzwa runaka igihe bikenewe

**Ubunararibonye bwa Kinyamwuga**
* Ihuzabikorwa rya UI ya Mailster kavukire ryo guhitamo intonde
* Kwandika logs birambuye byo gukemura ibibazo no kugenzura
* Kuvugurura abiyandikishije basanzwe mu buryo budacika

## Bibereye {#perfect-for}

* Ba nyiri urubuga rw’ubunyamuryango bubaka intonde za email
* Amahuriro ya SaaS ashaka kwita ku bakiriya bashya
* Abakora amasomo batandukanya abanyeshuri hashingiwe ku kwiyandikisha mu masomo
* Umuntu wese ukoresha Ultimate Multisite hamwe na Mailster

## Uko Bikora {#how-it-works}

1. Shyiramo kandi ukore addon ku rusobe rwawe rwa Ultimate Multisite
2. Tunganya intonde zisanzwe rusange mu igenamiterere rya Ultimate Multisite
3. Ushobora guhitamo kubisimbuza intonde zijyanye n’ibicuruzwa runaka
4. Hitamo uburyo bwa opt-in n’igihe cyo kwiyandikisha
5. Reba uko intonde zawe za Mailster zikura mu buryo bwikora!

## Gushyiramo {#installation}

1. Ohereza dosiye za plugin mu bubiko bwa addons bwa Ultimate Multisite
2. Menya neza ko Mailster yashyizweho kandi ikora ku rubuga rwawe nyamukuru
3. Koresha addon unyuze ku rupapuro rwa addons rwa Ultimate Multisite
4. Jya kuri Settings > Mailster Integration kugira ngo utunganye
5. Hitamo intonde zawe zisanzwe kandi utunganye ibyifuzo bya opt-in

## Ibibazo Bikunze Kubazwa {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Ese iyi addon ikora idafite Ultimate Multisite? {#does-this-addon-work-without-ultimate-multisite}

Oya, iyi addon isaba Ultimate Multisite kugira ngo ikore. Yakozwe by’umwihariko kugira ngo ihuze Mailster na sisitemu y’abakiriya n’ibicuruzwa ya Ultimate Multisite.

## Ese nkeneye ko Mailster ishyirwa ku mbuga zose? {#do-i-need-mailster-installed-on-all-sites}

Oya, Mailster ikeneye gusa gushyirwa no gukorerwa ku rubuga nyamukuru rw’urusobe rwawe. Addon izayihuza mu buryo bwikora ivuye ku mbuga nto zose.

## Ese nshobora gushyira intonde zitandukanye ku bicuruzwa bitandukanye? {#can-i-assign-different-lists-to-different-products}

Yego! Ushobora gushyiraho intonde zisanzwe rusange, hanyuma ukazisimbuza ku rwego rwa buri gicuruzwa. Ibi bigufasha gukora ubukangurambaga bwa email bugamije cyane hashingiwe ku byo abakiriya bagura.

## Bigenda bite iyo umukiriya asanzwe ari muri Mailster? {#what-happens-if-a-customer-already-exists-in-mailster}

Mu buryo busanzwe, addon izavugurura abiyandikishije basanzwe kandi ibongere ku ntonde nshya. Ushobora kubihagarika mu igenamiterere niba wifuza gusimbuka abiyandikishije basanzwe.

## Ese ibi byubahiriza GDPR? {#is-this-gdpr-compliant}

Yego! Addon irimo uburyo bwa opt-in bushingiye kuri checkbox aho abakiriya bagomba kwemera mu buryo bweruye kwakira email. Ibi byujuje ibisabwa na GDPR iyo bihujwe na politiki ziboneye z’ibanga.

## Ese nshobora gukoresha double opt-in? {#can-i-use-double-opt-in}

Rwose! Koresha igenamiterere rya double opt-in maze Mailster izohereze email zo kwemeza mbere yo gukora kwiyandikisha.
