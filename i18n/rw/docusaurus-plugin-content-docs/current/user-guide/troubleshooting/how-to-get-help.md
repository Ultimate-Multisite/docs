## Guhura na Ubufasha (Contact Support) {#contact-support}

Iyo utagira ikibazo kandi nta gisubizo cyo kugera ku help site yacu, ushobora guhura na team yawe ya ubufasha binyuze mu gukoresha speech bubble icon yakurikije ku cyerekezo cy'website yacu cyangwa binyuze mu gunguru (email) ku support@ultimatemultisite.com.

Iyo utagira ibibazo by'itekniki, ushobora kugira ubufasha ugomba kwerekana uburyo mwiza bw'ibibazo runaka n'amashyirahamwe yacu (screenshot), amakuru y'ibibazo (error messages), cyangwa ibindi byo gushyize abantu b'ubwujgwa bwo kugira urugero rwiza kandi kurinda uburyo mwiza bwo gukora ibibazo. Ugiye gufasha n'License key yawe. Gusa abantu bashobora gukoresha License key zafite ubufasha.

## Community Site {#community-site}

[community site](https://community.ultimatemultisite.com/) yacu ni umuryango w'Ultimate Multisite abantu banyamukira mu gutandukanya n'ibindi. Ni uburyo bwiza bwo gushyize amakuru aho abantu bashobora guhagarika, kwandikira ibyo babigaga, gushyize ubufasha, gukora umuryango (collaborate), cyangwa gushyize inama z'abantu. Ugiye kugira ngo wumva License key yawe y'Ultimate Multisite kugira ngo utangire amahamya/pages zitandukanye.
