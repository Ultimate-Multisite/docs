# Uburyo mu Komuniti ya Ultimate Multisite

Ultimate Multisite Community ni indwara uyuho utera umubiri n'abandi b'umuntu abashobora gukora Ultimate Multisite, kandi wishobora kwishandana ibyo mwiza, uburyo mwe yashoboye gukora, n'ibindi. Kugira ngo ushambire mu Ultimate Multisite Community, ugenda ku https://community.ultimatemultisite.com/home.

Ku mypage ya welcome, ugenda kuri link " **gukora aka kanya uburyo bwawe** ".

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Mypage ya welcome na link y'uko wumva -->

Ushobora guhindurwa ku page ya registration yawe. Uyu munsi, ugomba gukoresha username (izina ry'ubwoko), email yawe n'ibyo ugiye mu gihe cyo kwandika password. Yemeza Captcha kandi ukoresha button " **Register** ".

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Mypage ya registration na byereka y'username, email, no password -->

Nyuma yo gukorera (register), ni gihe cyo gukora profile yawe. Ku page iyi, uba mushobora gukoresha iyo foto yawe, kugira ngo ushije izina ry'ubwoko ryawe, ugire bio y'umwihariko kandi n'ibindi. Uba mushobora kandi gufata links zawe z'abantu b'umwebwe (social media) no gukora uburyo bwiza bwo kugira uburenganzira ku account yawe.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Mypage ya gukora profile na byereka foto, bio -->

Uyu munsi, uba mu channel "Introductions" (Guhangira), aho ushobora guhagarara kandi wumva ibyo abandi b'abantu bashobora gukora. Uba mushobora gukora post yawe binyuze mu gukoresha button " **New Post** ".

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Channel ya Introductions na button New Post -->

Channels ku Lounge section n'channel "Dev Logs" ni yizera abantu bose bashobora gufata. Ariko, uza kubyara uburyo bwiza kugira ngo ushambire mu channels z'ibindi mu community.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Channels z'community zishobora gushobora Lounge section n'channel Dev Logs -->

Uburyo bwo kwishyura ubu bwurenganzira uzahindurwa iyo utwita umukono wowe (license key) wa Ultimate Multisite. Iyo uba mushobora gukoresha, ugenda ku link y'channel yose yizera kandi ukoresha button " **Unlock** ".

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Channel ya private na button ya Unlock -->

Ushakubika ku mudasobora wacu wa ubuchifushi bwa access. Shika kinyume cyo (Enter) ukuri License key ya Ultimate Multisite yanyu kandi ugushyire butaka "Unlock Access" (Kusubiza Access):

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Channel ya community ikoresha input ya license key -->

License yawe izakwibajwe kandi uza kubona access ku channels zishya. Icyo cyo kera kera uziheraho mu gihe cyo gukora, ugushyira butaka link kugira ngo urere mu community kandi wumve ibindi by'ubwoko bwo gukora twakora igikorwa cyacu.
