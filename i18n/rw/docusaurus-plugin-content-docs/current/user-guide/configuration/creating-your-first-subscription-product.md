# Gukora Icyemezo Cyako Cy'Uruhuriro (v2)

_**IKUNDI KUGUCUZE: Urugero uyu ari ukwiko kwisi Ultimate Multisite version 2.x. Uziyo ugiye n'version 1.x,**_ **__gukora uyu wese***_ **._

Kugenzura ukugenzura uburyo bwa network yakwa no kumenya imbere imbere ubucuruzi bwawe ku bashobora bafite, ushobora kuba n'ubwoko bw'ibintu bwa subscription. Ugiye gutera ibintu byo gukora ibi? Ubwoko bw'ibintu w'ibyo mushobora kubona? Mu gihe cyacu, twagira uburyo bwawe bose bwo kumenya ibintu by'ibintu.

## Ubwoko bw'Icyemezo (Product Type) {#product-type}

N'Ultimate Multisite, ushobora gukoresha ibintu bibiri mu buryo bwa clienta wako: **plans** n'**add-ons (Order Bump)**. Add-ons mushobora guhindurwa mu byiciro bibiri: **packages** n'**services**. Twagira uburyo bwabo kandi twakwibona ibyo bifite.

  * **Plans** : Icyemezo cyiza cyane cy'Ultimate Multisite. Umuco wako ashobora kuba umukoresha (membership) gusa niba yari yakwiye n'plan. Plan igira imbere muco yawe uyu mukozi (ikiye kishobora guhindurwa ku buryo bwo kubyara) n'ibintu byo kwizera ku gihe cyo gukoresha ibyo bwa product editing page.

  * **Packages** : Add-ons y'ibintu bifite urugero mu gutera imbere uburyo bwa plan ya Ultimate Multisite. Bishobora guhindura imbere n'ubwoko bw'ibintu cyangwa gukoresha ibintu by'ingenzi (resources), plugins cyangwa themes zishobora kubyara ku plan y'umukozi wako yakwiriye. Urugero, plan ya mbere ishobora gufasha uwo muntu kugira umubare wa ibitabo 1,000 mu gihe cyo gukoresha, kandi ushobora gutera imbere package yishobora gufasha uwo nshya umubare kugeza 10,000.

  * **Services:** Add-ons atari ibintu byogera imbere ubucuruzi bwa Ultimate Multisite. Ni ibintu byo gukora ku mukozi wako kandi yari yakwiye n'plan yashobanye. Urugero, umukoresha wako ashobora gukora plan igira site runaka cyangwa yari yakwiye gukora service ikomeye cyo kubyara uwo site.

## Gukoma Icyemezo (Managing Products) {#managing-products}

Buriwe, uyu **Products** tab mu Ultimate Multisite **(Ultimate Multisite > Products)** mushobwa n'ubwoko bw'ibizigirwa (plans) mu gihe cyo gukoresha hosting w'umunsi.

Mu Ultimate Multisite, tab ya Products igaragaza uburyo bwo gukora kandi ibindi byiza bikorwa ku bijyanye n'ibizigirwa cyangwa ibikorwa by'ubwoko runaka. Ibi byiza byitera ku gukoresha ibisubizo by'ibizigirwa, uburo mu gihe cyo gukora, amafaranga, uburyo bwo gukoresha (permissions), n'ibindi.

Iki cyiciro kizabigize inama ku kumenya uburyo bwo gukoresha uburyo buhitamo mu Ultimate Multisite.

![Products list page](/img/config/products-list.png)

## Gufasha Ibibizigirwa (Adding Products) {#adding-products}

Icyiza cyangwa ibyiza, plan, package, cyangwa ikintu runaka kumenya uburyo bwo gukora ibiri mu gihe cyo gushyira umuntu ku bijyanye n'ikintu runaka ni **Ultimate Multisite > Products > Add Product**.

![Add Product button](/img/config/product-add-button.png)

Icyemezo kishobora gukoresha ibiri mu gihe cyo gushyira umuntu ku bijyanye n'ikintu runaka. Muwe, hari tabs nyinshi zishobora gufasha mu gukora ikintu runaka, kandi mu gihe kera hari sections nyinshi zishobora gukoresha mu gukora amafaranga y'ibizigirwa by'ikintu runaka, uburyo bwo kuba ari mu gihe cyiza (active state), n'isali ya ikintu runaka.

### Icyemezo cy'Icyemezo (Product Options) {#product-options}

#### Icyemezo Cy'Uburyo (General) {#general}

Icyemezo cy'Iburyo (**General**) bituma ibintu byo gushyirwa mu gihe bishobora gukoreshwa mu muryango w'ibintu byo mu kintu, ntabwo bifite mu gihe cyo kuboneka mu gihe cyo guhindura ibyo byo mu kintu by'ibintu byo mu kintu.

![General tab](/img/config/product-general-tab.png)

Ultimate Multisite yashobora gushyira ibintu byo mu kintu bishobora kuba ari Plan, Package, na Service. Icyemezo cy'Icyemezo (**Product Options**) bituma bifite mu gihe cyo guhindura bifite mu buryo bwo mu kintu wiboneka ku muryango uyu.

Icyemezo cy'Uburyo (Customer Role) bituma umuryango w'abantu bakoresha mu gihe uburyo bwo kuboneka mu gihe cyo gukora site. Mu gihe cyo guhindura, niba hari abantu benshi bafite ku muryango w'ibintu byo mu kintu, izwiho itangwa mu gihe cyo guhindura (default) cyangwa Administrator. #### Kumenyi no Gukoresha n'Ukurusha (Up & Downgrades) {#up--downgrades}

Icyo ubutabashwa bishobora gukoresha n'ukoresha (upgrade and downgrade paths) byo mu kundenye umuntu muri uburenganzira bwe.

Kugushyira ngo wumve uburyo bwo kuyitondera ibyo, gusoma uburyo bw'ibyo: uburyo umuntu wumva uburyo n'ukoresha (upgrade and downgrade) mu kintu cyiza cyane cyane. Umeze gukoresha uburyo bwo kugushyira ngo umuntu yitondera inyigisho z'ubwumvikane (learning management solutions) ku bumwe bw'ibyo: mu gihe cyose, ibi bitandukanye (Basic, Plus, n'Premium) bigenzwa kandi ibyerekeye uburyo bwo gukoresha plugins by'ingenzi kuri buri gihe (bura mu gihe cyane muri iki cyiciro, uza kumenya inama ku buryo bwo gukoresha plugins).

Niba Ultimate Multisite yitondera ibyo bikoreshwa no mu bikorwa by'ubizina cyangwa eCommerce websites, ibi gihe bigenzwa bitewe n'ibindi plugins byerekana kandi byerekana.

Mu gihe cyo, bitabwire kandi bitera ikibazo gukora umuntu wumva uburyo bwo guhagarara ku gihe cy'eCommerce (eLearning) kuko ibi gihe bigenzwa n'ubwoko bw'ikoresho, uburo mu gihe, n'ibindi byiza bitabwire.

Bityo kugira ngo twizige umuntu kandi twihagarire ikibazo, umunyamakuru w'uburenganzira (network administrator) ashobora gukora "plan group" kandi mu gihe cyo kumenya ibi plan byose umuntu yashobora guhagarara.

![Up and Downgrades tab](/img/config/product-upgrades.png)

Kugenzura "plan group", uburyo bwo gukoresha ibi plan byose muri listi ya **plan group**. Uburyo bwo gutangira (product order) bishobora kwerekana uko ibi plan bigenzwa kandi bigenzwa ku buryo bw'ibyo cyiza cyane, kuva mu gihe cyinhinduro kugeza mu gihe cyinhinduro.

Ultimate Multisite yitondera n'ubwoko bwo "order bump" (order bump feature) aho ibindi byiza bitandukanye byashobora gushyirwa kuri plan. Ibi bitangwa umuntu nk'ibindi byiza bigenzwa ku gihe cyo guhagarara cyangwa mu gihe cyinhinduro.

#### Ubwoko bw'Icyemezo (Price Variations) {#price-variations}

Urugero mu gihe cy'umwihariko, umunyarwanda w'ubwumvikane y'urwego rwa WordPress.

Urugero mu gihe cy'ubwujobozi bwo gukoresha ubwoko bw'ibyo (Price variations) bishobora guhindura uburyo bwo kwerekana imyera ya imyera ku muntu w'uburyo, aho umukozi w'urwego rwa WordPress yashobora gukoresha ibyo mu gihe cyose. Icyo kishobora kugira ngo umwe mu gihe (monthly), mu gihe cy'umwuka (quarterly), mu gihe cy'umwuka w'intaka (annual), cyangwa uburyo bw'ibindi byiza binyuze mu gihe cyose. Urugero, ushobora gukoresha duka imyera ya duka ku gihe cy'umwuka (monthly) yari $29.99, kandi ukoresha duka imyera ya intaka (annual) yari $249.99 yari yashyira umutera w'intaka.

Kugira ngo uburyo bwo gukoresha ubwoko bw'ibyo (variation), ugomba gukora ibi: gukora gihe cyangwa umwuka, igihe, n'ubwoko bw'imyera ya ubwoko. Ushobora gukoresha ubwoko bukwirwa mu gihe cyose binyuze mu gihe cyo gukora **Add new Price Variation**.

![Disk Space tab](/img/config/product-disk-space.png)

Mu gihe cyane mu WordPress multisite, ibyerekezo by'uburyo (core files) byari yihagaragaje ku nshingano yawe yose kandi ibintu by'ubwoko bwa media n'ibyo byo gukoreshwa byari bifite uburyo bwo kwizera ibi bikorwa.

Kugira ngo ushake gukora uburyo bwo kwizera ubwoko, wumva **limit disk size per site** uburyo bwo gukoresha mu gihe cy'ubwoko (toggle) kandi ubufashe ku kinyagurura ku gice cy'ibyo byiza.

Kandi, ushaka gukora uburyo bwo kwizera uburyo bwawe bwawe mu **disk space allowance** uburyo bwo gukoresha megabytes (MB).

#### Custom Domain (Urururo Rw'Ubwoko Bw'Umuryango) {#custom-domain}

Binyuze mu gihe cy'ubwoko (toggle), ushaka gukora uburyo bwo kwizera ibi byiza ku ruhande (custom domains) kuri iyo plan igomba.

![Custom Domains tab](/img/config/product-custom-domains.png)

#### Themes (Urururo Rw'Icyemezo) {#themes}

Tabu ya **Themes** mu gihe cyo gukora ibyo byiza ku product ituma umuntu wese uburyo bwo kwizera ibi byiza ku ruhande, kandi ushaka gukora uburyo bwo kwizera uburyo bwawe.

![Themes tab](/img/config/product-themes.png)

_**Urugero: Iyo themes zishobora gufite umuntu wese uburyo bwo kwizera, abashobozi b'uburyo (network administrator) bashobora gufite ibyo byiza ku ruhande.**_

![Network themes page](/img/config/product-themes-network-enabled.png)

Uburyo bwa **visibility** buvura uburyo bwo gukoresha iyo theme kuri umuntu wese uburyo bwo kwizera, mu gihe yisobanura ibyo byiza (Appearance > Themes) mu site yawe. Kugira ngo ushake gukora uburyo bwo kwizera **Hidden**, ubufashe ku kuzuza iyo theme kandi ubwoko bwawe bwo gukoresha.

![Themes tab](/img/config/product-themes.png)

Uburyo bwa **behavior** buvura umuntu wese uburyo bwo gukora ibyo byiza mu gihe cyo gukira uburyo bwa theme ku gihe cyo gukoresha site yawe.

Mu gihe **A** **vuba** n'ubwo, uburyo bwo theme ni mu gihe cyashobora kugabanyijwe umukoresha w'umuryango (customer) kugira ngo yisobanure mu buryo bw'ibyo. Mu gihe **Nta vuba**, ibyo byitera umukoresha w'umuryango ubushobozi bwo gukorera theme. Mu gihe **Gukora Gukora** (Force Activate), uburyo bwa "Force Activate" rishobora gutera ko theme yisobanura kandi yirashobora kugira umwanya mu gihe cyerekana ku site.

#### Plugins {#plugins}

Nko mu gihe cy'Themes, Ultimate Multisite yishobora guhindurira umunyamakuru w'umuryango (network administrator) gukora ibyo plugin byitera umukoresha w'umuryango kugira ngo yisobanure kandi yirashobora kuba mu gihe cyo gukora site yashyirwe.

![Plugins tab](/img/config/product-plugins.png)

Ururu wa **visibility** (ubwumvikane) rishobora gutera ko plugin yitera umukoresha w'umuryango kugira ngo yerekane cyangwa yisobanure mu gihe yerekana ku site yashyirwe binyuze mu menu ya Plugins.

Umunyamakuru w'umuryango yashobora gukora ibyo plugin byitera umuntu kandi binyuze mu ruru wa **behavior** (uburyo bw'ibyo) kugira ngo yisobanure cyangwa yishobora guhindurwa.

![Plugins tab](/img/config/product-plugins.png)

Ururu wa **Default** rishobora gukoresha uburyo bwo plugin byitera mu gihe cyo site yashyirwe (site template) umukoresha w'umuryango yashyiraho. Bityo plugins zikoreshwa mu template yisobanura zizishobora kuze kwibuka mu site ya umukoresha w'umuryango yashyiramoho, ntabwo zishobora guhindurwa iyo template yitera (cloned) mu site ya umukoresha w'umuryango.

Ururu wa **Force Activate** rishobora gukora plugin yisobanura mu gihe cyo site yashyirwe, kandi mu gihe **Force Inactivate**, rishobora guhindura ko plugin yisobanure mu gihe cyo site yashyirwe. Mu gihe cyo gukora ibyo, umukoresha w'umuryango yashobora guhindura isobanuro ry'plugin mu menu ya WordPress Plugins.

Icyo **Force Activate & Lock** ni ifite nko mu buryo bwa mbere, ariko yituma uburyo bw'ibyo plugin cyangwa umenye bwa customer bishobora guhindura. Bityo ibyo setting ya Force Activate and Lock ituma plugin ikomeye kandi ituma customer atagushobora gukomeza (deactivate) uko. Uburyo bwa Force Inactivate & Lock, niho, ituma plugin ikomeye kandi ituma umuntu atagushobora gukomeza (activate).

Umuvugizi w'ubwoko bw'ubwoko (network administrator) yashobora gutera ku byo Force Activate & Lock n'Force Inactivate & Lock mu buryo bwa site templates. Iyo uziho, ibyo plugin cyangwa umenye bwa plugin mu templates bishobora guhindurwa n'ibyo setting bituma ari byo bikorwa.

#### Guhindura Uburyo Bwa Gukomeza (Reset Limitations) {#reset-limitations}

Icyo tab ya **Reset Limitations** ituma uburyo bwose bwa mbere bwa product bishobora gushyirwa mu gihe cy'ibyo plugin cyangwa ibindi byo. Kugira ngo utone ibyo, ugomba gukora igikorwa cy'ubwoko **reset limitations**.

![Reset Limitations tab](/img/config/product-reset-limitations.png)

Kugira ngo utone ikoranabuhindura, ugomba gukoresha toggle ya **confirm reset** kugira ngo itegeze mu gihe cy'ibyo (active state) ku kera kandi ukoreshe **reset limitations**.

![Reset Limitations tab](/img/config/product-reset-limitations.png)
