# Icyo ya Akaunti ya Mteja (v2)

_**KUMBUKA MUHIMU: Hii habari inahusu Ultimate Multisite toleo la 2.x.**_

Wakati wateja wanapochopoa mpango mmoja kwenye mtandao wako, wanapata ufikiaji wa tovuti na dashboard yake yenye taarifa muhimu kuhusu malipo yao, usajili (memberships), maeneo ya tovuti (domains), mipaka ya mpango, n.k...

Katika mwongozo huu, tutakuelekeza kupitia ukurasa wa akaunti ya mteja na utaona kile ambacho wateja wanaweza kuona na kufanya ndani yake.

## Ukurasa wa Akaunti (The Account Page) {#the-account-page}

Ukurasa wa akaunti unaweza kufikiwa kwa kukumbatia **Account** ndani ya dashboard ya mteja wako.

![Menu ya Akaunti kwenye dashboard ya mteja](/img/account-page/account-menu.png)

Katika mitandao ya sovereign tenant, Ultimate Multisite v2.13.0 inahifadhi uzoefu huu wa usimamizi wa wateja kwenye tovuti kuu. Ikiwa mteja anafungua akaunti, checkout, malipo (billing), risiti (invoice), usimamizi wa tovuti (site-management), kubadilisha template, au upatanishi wa domain kutoka kwa sovereign tenant, hatua hizo zinarejea kwenye paneli ya wateja ya tovuti kuu ili kumbukumbu za malipo na usajili wa mtandao zibaki kuwa sahihi.

Wakati mteja anapofika kutoka kwa sovereign tenant, paneli ya wateja ya tovuti kuu inaweza kujumuisha kiungo cha kurudisha (return link) kwenda kwenye tovuti ya tenant. Kiungo cha kurudisha kinawonekana tu wakati Ultimate Multisite inaweza kuthibitisha lengo la kurudisha kama moja kati ya maeneo ya wateja, jambo linalozuia ubadilishanaji wa kiwango chochote huku likihifadhi mtiririko wa kazi wa tenant.

![Muonekano wa ukurasa wa akaunti ya mteja](/img/account-page/overview.png)

Baada ya mteja kukumbatia, ataona muhtasari wa usajili wake, anwani ya malipo (billing address), risiti zake, domain, mipaka ya tovuti, na pia atakuwa na uwezo wa kubadilisha **Site Template** (ikiwa inaruhusiwa kwenye mtandao wako).

Anaweza pia kubadilisha usajili kwenda mpango mwingine, au kununua pakiti au huduma nyingine unayotoa. Tuchukulie kila sehemu kwa mguso tofauti.

### Guhindura Site Template: {#updating-the-billing-address}

Kugira ngo abantu bwawe bashobore guhindura amafoto y'ibigo byo gukoresha (site templates), ugomba kwifashisha **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Sites** kandi ugomba gukora option **Allow Template Switching**.

Ntabwo, ku **Ultimate Multisite > Products**, wumva uburyo bwawe bw'ibigo byo gukoresha (select your plans) kandi ugomba kwifashisha tab ya **Site Templates**. Kugira ngo umuntu yose ashobore guhindura amafoto y'ibigo byo gukoresha, ugomba kumenya ko option **Allow Site Templates** iri mu gihe cyo kubone. Kera ku **Site Template Selection Mode**, ugomba gukora option **Choose Available Site Templates**.

![Product site templates tab with template selection mode](/img/config/product-site-templates.png)

Uziwe ko uza kubona amafoto yose y'ibigo byo gukoresha kuri website yawe. Ufite ubushobozi gukora ibyo wifuza kugira ngo bishobore guhindurwa kandi ibyo wifuza abantu bafite uburyo bw'ibigo byo gukoresha (which ones you want to make available) kandi ibyo wifuza abantu bafite uburyo bw'ibigo byo gukoresha (and which ones you want to not be available to your customers subscribed under this plan). Uzi ko ibyo byo mu gihe cyo kubona byo gukora (these options) bitera ntabwo ku form y'ubwumvikane (checkout form), kandi amafoto yose yashoborwa guhindurwa (any template that is chosen as **Not Available**) azabone ntabwo kuri pejo ya gukoresha (registration page) y'ibyo byo gukoresha.

Ubu, abantu bwawe bashobora gukora **Change Site Template** mu gihe cyo kubona ibigo byo gukoresha (inside their account page).

![Change Site Template button on account page](/img/account-page/change-template-button.png)

Ultimate Multisite 2.10.0 yifashisha panel y'ibigo byo gukoresha (template switch panel) yagize uburyo bwawe bw'ibigo byo gukoresha. Iki gihe cyo kubone kigize **current-template card** kugira ngo abantu bashobore kumenya amafoto yose y'ibigo byo gukoresha (so customers can see which template is active) mbere y'uko bafite uburyo bw'ibigo byo gukoresha (before they choose a replacement).

Urugero rurimi rw'ubwoko rwa site rishobora kuba ari mu gukoresha, kandi bishobora kwibonwa mu gihe abakoresha bafite uburyo bwo gushaka. Ibi bikorera kubasha kugira ngo bashobore guhindura ibyo bafite ku plan yabo nta kumenya ibyo bafite mu gihe cyose.

![Urugero rwa site rishobora kuba ari mu gukoresha, kandi bishobora kwibonwa mu gihe abakoresha bafite uburyo bwo gushaka](/img/config/site-templates-list.png)

Nyuma y'uko bashobora gushyira umwe wumva guhindura, bazakubwira ko bakwibona kandi bakwibona ngo bahindure.

![Urugero rwa site rishobora kuba ari mu gukoresha, kandi bishobora kwibonwa mu gihe abakoresha bafite uburyo bwo gushaka](/img/account-page/template-switch-confirm.png)

Nyuma y'uko wamukoreshe ubwoko rwa confirmation kandi wamukoreshe **Process Switch**, umwe site template uyu mwe uzakoreshwa ku website ya abakoresha bacyo.

Abakoresha bashobora gusezeranya **Reset current template** ku panel uyu mu gihe bafite uburyo bwo gushyira site mu dore y'ubwoko rwa mbere. Nko mu gihe bafite uburyo bwo gushyira umwe site template, gushyira umwe template mu dore y'ubwoko rwa mbere bishobora guhindura ibyo mu website, kandi abakoresha bashobora kwibona kandi bakwibona ngo bahindure.

### Gufasha Custom Domains: {#changing-the-site-template}

Abakoresha bacyo bashobora gushyira uburyo bwo gutanga custom domain ku plan yabo ku page ya akazi yabo. Kugira ngo abakoresha bacyo bashobore gukoresha custom domains, gukora **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Domain Mapping**.

Gusubiza (Toggle on) uburyo bwo gukoresha **Enable Domain Mapping**. Ibi bikorera abakoresha bacyo bashobora gukoresha custom domains ku network.

Nta kugira ngo mufite ubwoko rwa gutanga domain mapping ku product cyangwa mu gihe runaka - kuko mushobora kwibonya umwe product ko nta akazi yabo ishobora gukoresha custom domains.

Gukora **Ultimate Multisite > Products**. Sezera (Select) plan uyu mwe kandi gukora ku tab ya **Custom Domains**. Gusubiza (Toggle on) uburyo bwo gukoresha **Allow Custom Domains**.

![Tab ya Custom Domains n'ubwoko rwa Allow Custom Domains](/img/config/product-custom-domains.png)

Ibibihindurawekoze ko abagahutiwe bose bakoresha uburyo bwa plan uyu bashobora gukoresha domain cyangwa custom domains. Ubu, ku myaka ya Account, abagahutiwe n'abakoresha banyashobora gushyira custom domain bicya bikoreshwa binyuze mu gukoresha **Add Domain**.

![Add Domain button on the account page](/img/account-page/add-domain-button.png)

Icyo cyo mbere cyo kigaragara cyo mu cyarwo, kigisha abagahutiwe n'abakoresha banyashobora gushyira uburyo bwa DNS (DNS records) kugira ngo ubutumwa bw'custom domain bukurirwe ku rurimi rwawe.

![DNS instructions shown when adding a custom domain](/img/account-page/add-domain-dns.png)

Icyo cyo mbere cyashobora guhindurwa (n'ubwo wowe) mu **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Domain Mapping > Add New Domain Instructions**.

![Add New Domain Instructions setting in Domain Mapping](/img/config/settings-domain-mapping.png)

Uruhu rwose rwa settings ya domain mapping:

![Domain mapping settings full page](/img/config/settings-domain-mapping-full.png)

Nyuma yuko wamukoreshe **Next Step**, abagahutiwe n'abakoresha banyashobora gushyira izina ry'custom domain cyangwa gushyira uburyo bwa primary (primary one). Nyirinda ko abagahutiwe n'abakoresha banyashobora gukoresha custom domains nyinshi ku myaka yabo, kandi bashobora gukoresha izo zikurikira kugira ngo bashobore gutanga uburyo bwa primary.

![Custom domain name entry with primary domain option](/img/account-page/add-domain-primary.png)

Nyuma yuko wamukoreshe **Add Domain**, domain izo zishobora gushyirwa mu myaka ya abagahutiwe n'abakoresha banyashobora gukora. Uko bashobora gukora uburyo bwa DNS bwa custom domain ku rurimi rw'domain registrar (domain registrar) ryabo.

### Guhindura Password: {#adding-custom-domains}

Mu dashboard ya account, abagahutiwe n'abakoresha banyashobora guhindura password yabo binyuze mu gukoresha **Change Password**.

![Change Password button on account page](/img/account-page/change-password-button.png)

Iyiho cyo gushobora kugaragaza igice gishya aho abakoresha bako bashobora gufata uburyo bwo kwiga (current password) kandi bashobora gufata uburyo bwo kwiga gishya.

![Change password form with current and new password fields](/img/account-page/change-password-form.png)

### Icyo gushobora kugira ngo ubone ibyo: {#changing-password}

Dufite n'ibintu bibiri byo bifite mu **Danger Zone**: **Gukoresha Site** (Delete Site) no **Gukoresha Aka** (Delete Account). Bicyo bibiri byo bifite mu Danger Zone kuko bishobora gukorwa kandi bitagushoboka kurinda. Niba abakoresha bako bashobora gufata website cyangwa aka, batagushobora gutera ku gihe cy'ibyo.

![Danger Zone with Delete Site and Delete Account options](/img/account-page/danger-zone.png)

Niba abakoresha bako bashobora gukorwa mu mu gihe runaka uburyo bwo kwiga (current password), bazabone igice gishya aho bazashobora gukoresha uburyo bwo kwiga gishya bashaka gufata.

![Delete Site confirmation dialog](/img/account-page/delete-site-confirm.png)

![Delete Account confirmation dialog](/img/account-page/delete-account-confirm.png)

Niba batakoresha gukoresha site, aka kandi ubushobozi bako bishobora gufata bishobora kwibuka. Batagushobora gutera ku gihe cy'ibyo. Niba batakoresha gukoresha aka, website yose, ubushobozi bose n'ubwujgwa bw'uko aka kigishobora gufata bishobora gutera ku gihe cyo.
