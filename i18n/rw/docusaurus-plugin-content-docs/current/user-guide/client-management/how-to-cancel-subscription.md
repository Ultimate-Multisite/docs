# Uburyo ubutshobora w'ubwumvikane bwawe? {#how-can-i-cancel-my-subscription}

Iyo ufite license ya Ultimate Multisite, ubutshobora kubyara ubwumvikane bwawe mu gihe cyose uvuga. Ibyo ni ibyako mu buryo bwo gukora:

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Account page email with access link -->

Gukoresha email wari ukagize ku gihe ugiye gutangira, kandi umwanya (password) wowe.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Login page with email and password fields -->

Iki ni igice cyo gukoresha Icyemezo (Account Page) cyowe:

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Account page initial screen showing subscription details -->

Kugira ngo ubire ubwumvikane bwawe, ku menu ya mbere kuri buri gice kera, gukora uburyo bwo gukoresha _Renewing & Billing._

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Renewing & Billing page with cancel option -->

Gukoresha inda (arrow) y'ibere mu gihe cyo gukoresha ibindi byo. Nyuma, ugomba gukora uburyo bwo gukoresha uburyo bwawe bwo gukemura ubwumvikane _auto-renew_.

Inzego izwiho ubutshobora gukoresha umugambi wowe.

Iyo ufite ubushobozi bwo gukemura ubwumvikane, ugomba gukora uburyo bwo gukoresha uburyo bwawe bwo gukoresha _**Cancel Renewals**_. Nyuma y'ibyo, uzabaye mu gihe cyo gutanga umusanzuro wowe.

Wari wumvikane wowe utagira ubwumvikane bw'auto-renew.

**Uziho ubutshobora gukoresha key (key) itera ku gihe ubwumvikane bwawe bwo guteka.** Iyo ufite ubushobozi bwo gukemura ubwumvikane, uzabaye mu gihe cyo gukora ibyo wowe.
