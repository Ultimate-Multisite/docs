# Kumenya Icyemezo (v2)

_**Icyemezo: Inyandiko irereye ku Ultimate Multisite version 2.x.**_

Abakoresha bako abakoresha bishobora gukunda icyemezo cyo kubara mu gihe runaka. Bishobora gukunda icyemezo cyo kubara mu gihe runaka cyangwa gukoresha ibindi by'ubwujumbura cyangwa ibindi by'ubwujumbura bishobora kubyara ku rugendo rwawe.

Mu tutorial uyu, twagira kwerekana uko bashobora gukunda icyemezo cyo kubara mu gihe runaka kandi iki kigira kwitwa nyuma y'icyemezo cyo kubara.

Kugunda icyemezo cyo kubara, abakoresha bako bashobora kugera ku dashboard yabo kandi bakora ku rubuga rwa **Account**.

![Dashboard ya subsite y'umukoresha n'ubwoko bw'Account wifashwe](/img/account-page/account-menu.png)

Ku rubuga rwa Account, bishobora kubona uburyo bwo kuba umukoresha wako kandi icyemezo cyo kera ku yisobanuye. Kumenya icyemezo cyo kubara mu gihe runaka, bashobora gukunda **Change** kuri **Your Membership** ku cyerekezo cy'ibindi by'ubwujumbura.

![Rubuga rwa Account Your Membership card n'ibyo Change bitwa](/img/account-page/membership-change-button.png)

Bishobora gukoreshwa mu gihe runaka ku rubuga rwa checkout aho bose ibindi by'ubwujumbura byose bigaragara.

Bishobora kandi kubona **ibindi by'ubwujumbura n'ibindi by'ubwujumbura bisobanuye ku icyemezo cyo kera cyako**, mu gihe hari ugiye gukunda ibindi by'ubwujumbura cyangwa ibindi by'ubwujumbura (nk'uko twari dushobora kubyara uburenganzira cyangwa disk space mu gihe twari dushobora kubyara), kandi si gukunda icyemezo cyo kubara.

![Upgrade picker ifite ibindi by'ubwujumbura n'ibindi by'ubwujumbura bigaragara kuri uukoresha](/img/account-page/upgrade-picker.png)

Nyuma y'uko bashobora gukunda ikintu cyo kubyara, bishobora kumenya uko bakwiriye kwishyura uburyo bwo kuyishyura ubu - mu gihe hari ibindi by'ubwujumbura bishobora kuba bafite ubushobozi (credit) runaka kandi uko bakwiriye kwishyura ku itariki y'ibindi.

Gusa, niba ikintu cyo kubyara ni icyemezo cyo kubara kandi kwishyura kera mu gihe hari ibindi by'ubwujumbura, bashobora gukoresha ubushobozi (credit) ku miktar yashyiramo ku icyemezo cyo mbere.

![Upgrade payment summary showing credit applied and next billing amount](/img/account-page/upgrade-summary.png)

Ntabwo bash batuma cyangwa package utera umuntu ubanze mu gihe cy'ubwujisoro, azabona uburyo bwo kubyitangira.

![Notice when the selected plan does not change the subscription](/img/account-page/upgrade-no-change.png)

Nyuma yuko checkout yishobye, ibintu by'umutera (products) by'ibyo bishobye byizera mu gihe cy'ubwujisoro uwo mutera kandi imyitwarire n'ibyo byiza byo by'ibyo byizera byizera mu gihe cy'ubwujisoro yabo: ibi cyane ni ibyo bishobye, ubwoko bw'amashyirahamwe (visits), ubwoko bw'umutera (disk space), impamvu zikoreshwa (posts), n'ibindi.

## Uburyo bwo gukora Upgrade na Downgrade {#upgrade-and-downgrade-paths}

Ku gihe cy'ubwujisoro w'ibyo byizera, uza kubona tab ya **Up & Downgrades** kuri buri mutera. Icyo cyo mbere ku tab ni ikoranabuhinduzi cyo mu gihe cy'ubwujisoro (Plan Group).

**Plan groups** ni iki kigira ngo umuntu ashobore kumenya Ultimate Multisite ko ibyo byiza byizera byishobye bishobora gukoreshwa mu "umuryango" wowe, kandi bishobora gukoreshwa kugira ngo bashobore gutera upgrade cyangwa kwifisha (downgrade).

![Product edit Up and Downgrades tab with Plan Group field](/img/config/product-upgrades-plan-group.png)

Urugero, uza kuba ufite **Free plan**, **Basic Plan** n'**Premium Plan**. Ufite ubushobozi ko abantu bafite umutera mu **Free Plan** bashobore gukora upgrade gusa ku **Premium Plan** kandi ushaka ko basobanura "Basic Plan" nk'umwihariko (upgrade option). Icyo uza gukora ni gukoresha igice cyo Plan Group cyiza cyane kuri Free plan n'Premium Plan nk'uko byari mu zandira.

![Free Plan product page with High End plan group assigned](/img/config/product-upgrades-free.png)

![Premium Plan product page with High End plan group assigned](/img/config/product-upgrades-premium.png)

Ibi byumva ni ukwikozo ku Ultimate Multisite ko hari "family" ya plan mu giti cy'ubwoko (network) itewe **High End**. Iyo uba ugenda kubyara cyangwa kugabanya (upgrades or downgrades), uba umuntu w'ibyo bishobora kuba ari iplan mu gihe cy'ibyo byo mu family yashya.
