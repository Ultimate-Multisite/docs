# Nini Ubuto mu Ultimate Multisite

Uziye yifite ubutumwa bwo kumenya ko plugin yetu yashobora kuba ari usobanuro kugira ngo ubucuruzi bwawe bukurirwe. Turakoze ko wari ufite ibyo ukeneye ku gikorwa cy'ibindi byo mu gihe cy'ikiwe.

## Icyo ni Ultimate Multisite?

Ultimate Multisite ni plugin ya WordPress Multisite itanga uburyo bwo kugira umuntu yose ubushobozi bwo gutanga abakiriya WaaS (Website as a Service). Ndiho, n'ubwoko bw'ibinyabiko by'umukono kandi wiyumva umuntu atangira amafaranga menshi mu gihe cy'uko ufungura site yawe mu network ya Multisite.

Ultimate Multisite yifashisha ubushobozi bwo gutanga amafaranga ku buryo bushobora (nko WooCommerce cyangwa Stripe), gukora templates za site kugira ngo ibyo byari byiza kandi bikorera mu gihe umuntu w'umunsi w'ubwoko rwawe, gukoresha domain mapping, no gutera umufugurura w'umunsi w'ubwoko kugira ngo umuntu atangire uburyo bwo gukora cyiza kandi kumenya.

Kindi, iki ni igice cyiza kandi hari uburyo bwo kwerekana ibyo byari byiza n'ibindi byo mu gihe cy'ikiwe nk'uko wumva ko hari add-ons no webhooks.

## Icyo gusa (Other features)

Ultimate Multisite yifashisha:

  * **Domain Mapping integration** na abanyamrukire bwo WordPress hosting: Ultimate Multisite yitera domain mapping mu admin panels za WP Engine, Cloudways, cPanel, RunCloud, Closte, n'ibindi. Iyo umunsi w'umunsi w'ubwoko ufungurwa, inyandiko z'domain zishobora zikoreshwa mu gihe cy'uko nta kumenya. Mu gihe runaka, yitera SSL certificate fetching kandi ku muntu.

  * **Webhooks**: Ultimate Multisite yifashisha Webhook system itanga ubushobozi bwo gukoresha network yawe n'ibindi services nyinshi. Turakoze uburyo bwo gutanga integration na Zapier, ibyo bitanga network yawe kugira ngo ikoresheye n'ibindi apps no services izindi zikurikira 1000+ kuri platform yayo.

* Admin Pages zishobora kwizera abakoresha w'umwe mu gukoresha page builder yashobora wamukunda: Ultimate Multisite yashobora uburyo bwo kugufasha kugira Admin Pages zikoreshwa n'abakoresha wanyu ku dashboard yabo, uzi gukoresha Elementor, Beaver Builder, na Brizy templates.

* Urugero rwa Admin Themes runaka hariyo kugira ngo mwishobore kugira umwihariko mu gushyira umwihariko wa dashboard ya abakoresha wanyu: Check Material WP na PRO.

Na ibindi by'ingenzi cyane:

* Gusobanura Template Overrides (Template Overrides): Na Ultimate Multisite, mwe mushobora guhindura HTML templates zikoreshwa mu gukoresha signup flow n'ubwo mwishobora gushyira inyandiko zanyu nta kuyibanda ku file za plugin ya mbere.

* Search and Replace API: Mwe mushobora kandi gushyira inyandiko (placeholders) nk'{{site_name}} mu templates za site na gukoresha Search and Replace API kugira ngo yishobore gutanga inama zo mu gihe cyo gukoreshwa n'ubwo abakoresha wanyu bashobora gutanga inama zikoresho mu gihe cyo guhagarika, byiza kandi byiza.

## Uburyo bwo gukoresha (Use cases)

Urugero rwa ibyo Ultimate Multisite yashobora gukoreshwa.

### Icyiciro 1: Agency

Gusa, uburyo bw'ubwoko bw'agency buri gihe bishobora kuba ari ugukoresha inyandiko zikoresho mu gushyira umwihariko wa website n'ibindi byo nk'uko yashobora guhagarika cyangwa gukoreshwa mu marketing.

Kuva kuva, Ultimate Multisite yishobora gutanga uburyo bwiza cyane mu gushyira umwihariko wa websites nyinshi ku platformi ya mbere. Icyiciro cy'agency cyane cyane cyane cyashobora gukoresha ubushobozi bwa Ultimate Multisite kugira ngo yishobore guhagarika na gukoma websites nyinshi mu gihe cyo gukoreshwa.

Urugero rwa gukemura inkingi z'plugins n'themes runaka hariyo ku buryo bw'umwihariko wa Ultimate Multisite, uzi ko plugins na themes zishobora guhagarika cyangwa guhagarika mu gihe cyo gushyira umwihariko w'site yashobora kuba ari uko byashobora kubaho cyangwa ntabwo mu gihe cyo gukoreshwa.

Agencies zizera urugero n'ubwoba ku Ultimate Multisite, aho bafasha gukora ibyo babayeho cyane - kwiga uburyo bwiza mu byo gukora websites.

### Urugero 2: Umuntu wumva (Niche Provider)

Hari igice cy'ubwujyu cyumva cyane kigaga ko, “Gukora ikintu cyiza kandi cyiza.” Ku rugero rwa abantu bashobora bafasha, ibyo bikunda byera gukora ikintu cyangwa uburyo bwiza mu gihe runaka.

Niba wumva ubwoba mu gutegura umuryango w'websites z'ubwujyu (niche), uzafasha kumenya ko ugomba gukoresha uburyo bwa framework n'isangaro ry'ibyo. Icyo niho template sites (site templates) zishobora kukufasha!

Icyo cyiza mu gihe cy'umuntu wumva umuryango w'websites n'ibyo babayeho, niho uzafasha gukora website yawe mu gihe runaka kandi ikoresha plugins n'ubwujyu. Icyo kera umuntu yenda ibyo atazwi (minimal input) kugira ngo yikoreshe ubuzima bwawe.

### Urugero 3: Hosting ya WordPress

Hari uburyo bwinshi bwo gukora hosting ya WordPress, ariko cyane cyane ni urugero rwiza kumenya ko ni gukoresha amafaranga ku muntu kandi ukamara WordPress yari yashyiramo. Icyo ni cyiza kubera ko hari ibindi byinshi bikwiriye kwibanda kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gukora umuryango w'ibyo bishobora gutanga.

Ultimate Multisite ikora neza kuri iki kuko yitanga uburyo bwiza bwo gukoresha hosting ya WordPress mu gihe runaka (turnkey solution). Icyo cyitanga ni ibintu byose bikwiriye kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gutanga ubuzima bwawe, nk'ibyo:

*   Uburyo bwo gukora subscription services.
*   Gukoresha amafaranga (payment collection).
*   Checkout forms.
*   Discount vouchers.
*   Kugera ku umuntu (customer communications).

Gukoresha ny asa lehibe ilaina mba hasezerana, hategana, ary fitaziana WordPress Multisite tsara dia atao ho sahaza na Ultimate Multisite. Izany no mahatonga ny mpandrindra ny tambajotra (network administrators) hahitana zavatra toa ny antontan-taratasy momba ny serivisy na ny sehatra manokana azy, toy ny tondro vokatra (product tiers), vidiny, ary tolotra serivisy.

Ho an'ireo mpandrindra (developers) izay te-hifandray amin'ny Ultimate Multisite, ny vahaolana dia manome API RESTful sy Webhooks feno ho an'ny famantarana zava-nitranga (event notification).

Tsy mila miankina amin'ny plugin sy lisansy maro ivelany ianao, satria ny Ultimate Multisite dia manome vahaolana be mpitondra tsiambaratelo ary mitovy amin'izay omen'i Wix, Squarespace, [WordPress.com](https://WordPress.com), ary hafa.
