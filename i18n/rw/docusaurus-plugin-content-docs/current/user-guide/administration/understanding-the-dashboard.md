# Kumenya Dashboard ya Ultimate Multisite {#understanding-the-ultimate-multisite-dashboard}

Tazama kabla ya kujitahidi zaidi katika chaguzi za ziada za mipangilio na vipengele vya utendaji vya Ultimate Multisite, tuanze kwa kutazama dashboard.

Kama kama plugin nyingi za WordPress, Ultimate Multisite inaunda kipengele cha menyu cha kiwanda (network-level menu entry) kinachoitwa **Ultimate Multisite** na vitu vingi vya menyu vinavyoonekana chini yake.

## Widgets za Dashboard ya Mtandao (Network Dashboard Widgets) {#network-dashboard-widgets}

Ultimate Multisite inaweka widgets kadhaa muhimu kwenye dashboard ya mtandao. Unaweza kupata dashboard hii kwa kwenda **My Sites → Network Admin → Dashboard**. Widgets hizo zimeundwa kutoa ufikiaji rahisi wa habari na shughuli za kawaida kwa msimamizi wa mtandao (network administrator).

![Network Dashboard with Ultimate Multisite widgets](/img/admin/network-dashboard-full.png)

### Hatua za Kwanza (First Steps) {#first-steps}

![First Steps widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-first-steps.png)

Widget hii inajitokeza kwenye usakinishaji mpya wa Ultimate Multisite na inakumbusha msimamizi wa mtandao kukamilisha majukumu mengine kama vile kurekebisha njia ya malipo (payment method) na kuunda akaunti ya majaribio.

### Muhtasari (Summary) {#summary}

![Summary widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-summary.png)

Widget ya Muhtasari inatoa ripoti kuhusu idadi ya watu waliosajiliwa na mapato yao kwa siku. MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) inaonyesha jumla iliyotarajiwa kwa wateja wenye usajili unaozalisha mapato.

### Mtiririko wa Shughuli (Activity Stream) {#activity-stream}

![Activity Stream widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-activity-stream.png)

Widget hii inatoa ripoti kuhusu matukio yote yanayotokea kwenye Ultimate Multisite. Matukio haya yanajumuisha usajili, kufutwa kwa akaunti, kuunda tovuti, kufuta tovuti, na shughuli nyinginezo.

Tafadhali rejelea sehemu ya Events (Matukio) ya documentation kwa maelezo zaidi.

### Sasa (Right Now) {#right-now}

![Right Now widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-right-now.png)

Icyo ubutumwe ubutumwa bwo kuwumva umuntu w'ubwumelera n'ubwumelera mu gihugu. Uburyo bwawe bukurinda ubutumwa cyangwa ubwumelera bw'ibyo mu gihugu. Icyo kishobora guhinduka mu gihe cyiza ku gice cy'ibyo gishobora gukoreshwa (shortcuts) ku gice cy'ibanze, kugira ngo ushobore gukora ubutumwa run cyangwa umuntu run cyangwa ubwumelera runye mu gihe cyo gukora.

### Nyaho n'Ubwishuvura {#news-and-discussions}

![News and Discussions widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-news.png)

Icyo kishobora gutanga no gushobora gushyira umunsi w'ibyo nyaho mu Ultimate Multisite. Kugira ngo ushije ku cyerekeza ubutumwa bwo guhinduka, ibintu by'ingenzi by'ubwoba n'ibyo byerekana mu gihe cyo gukora.

## Dashboard ya Ultimate Multisite {#ultimate-multisite-dashboard}

Ho, aho dashboard y'ibyo mu gihugu itanga amakuru menshi ku butumwa bwose bwo gihugu, dashboard ya Ultimate Multisite (ikiho cyitwa mu gice cyibanze cy'Ultimate Multisite) itanga ibyerekeye uburyo bwo gukoresha icyo gikorwa.

![Ultimate Multisite Dashboard](/img/admin/um-dashboard-top.png)

### Ubwumelera bwo Gice Cy'Icyiciro (Date-range Support) {#date-range-support}

Kugira ngo umuntu yashobore kwumva ibyo byerekana, ibintu by'ingenzi n'ibyo byerekana mu gihe cyo gukoresha imyaka 12 byishobora kuba byari bitanga. Ubwumelera bwo guhinduka (date-range support) bwawe bukurinda umukozi w'ubwumelera ko yashobora kwirinda ibyo byerekana mu gihe cyo gukoresha imyaka 12, kugira ngo yishobore gukoresha gusa ibyo byifashishijwe kandi igihe cyo guhinduka.

![Date range selector and stats](/img/admin/um-dashboard-date-range.png)

Ubwumelera bwo guhinduka bukurinda igihe cyo gukoreshwa mu gihe cyo gukoresha ibyo byerekana mu gihe cyo gukoresha uburyo bwo gukoresha imyaka 12, ukurikije ibindi byose, nk'ubwoko bw'ibintu (revenue), ubwoko bukurikirana (new memberships), ubutumwa bwo guhagarika mu gihugu (signups by country), ubutumwa bwo guhagarika mu gihe cyo gukoresha (signups by form), n'ibyo byerekana ku siti ryiza cyane.

![Dashboard statistics widgets](/img/admin/um-dashboard-stats.png)
