# Isihluzi: wu_sso_enabled

Yenza okanye ukhubaze isakhono sokungena kube kanye kwiidomeyini ezahlukeneyo.

Hluza eli xabiso ukucima ukungena kube kanye ngokupheleleyo, okanye endaweni yoko ukwenze kusebenze ngokwemeko.

## Iiparamitha

| Igama | Uhlobo | Inkcazo |
|------|------|-------------|
| $enabled | `bool` | Ngaba i-SSO kufuneka yenziwe isebenze? True xa ivuliwe, false-ish xa icinyiwe. |
