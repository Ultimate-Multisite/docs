# Iingcinga Ezisisiseko {#basic-concepts}

Kumsebenzisi omtsha we-WordPress Multisite nomntu osandula ukuqala ukusebenzisa i-Ultimate Multisite, kusenokubakho amagama namabinzana amaninzi amatsha ekufuneka uwaqonde ekuqaleni. Ukuwafunda ngumsebenzi obalulekileyo kuba kuya kufuneka uqonde iqonga nendlela elisebenza ngayo xa lilonke.

Kweli nqaku, siza kuzama ukuchaza nokucacisa ezinye zeengcinga eziphambili kwi-WordPress. Ezinye kwezi zifaneleka ngakumbi kubasebenzisi, ezinye kubaphuhlisi, kwaye ezinye kubo bobabini.

## WordPress Multisite {#wordpress-multisite}

WordPress **Multisite** luhlobo lofakelo lwe-WordPress olukuvumela ukuba wenze kwaye ulawule uthungelwano lweewebhusayithi ezininzi ukusuka kwi-dashboard enye ye-WordPress. Ungalawula yonke into kuquka inani leesayithi, iimpawu, imixholo, kunye neendima zabasebenzisi. Kuyenzeka ukulawula amakhulu namawaka eesayithi.

## Uthungelwano {#network}

Ngokwe-WordPress, uthungelwano lwe-multisite yindawo apho inani lee-**subsites** linokulawulwa ukusuka kwi-dashboard enye. Nangona ukwenza uthungelwano lwe-multisite kwahluka phakathi kwababoneleli be-hosting, isiphumo sokugqibela siqhele ukuba yimiyalelo embalwa eyongeziweyo kwifayile ye-**wp-config.php** ukwazisa i-WordPress ukuba isebenza kule modi ikhethekileyo.

Kukho umahluko ocacileyo omninzi phakathi kothungelwano lwe-multisite kunye nofakelo lwe-WordPress oluzimeleyo esiza kuwuxoxa ngokufutshane.

## Isiseko sedatha {#database}

Isiseko sedatha siseti yedatha eyakhiwe kakuhle, elungelelanisiweyo. Kwigama lekhompyutha, isiseko sedatha sibhekisa kwisoftware esetyenziselwa ukugcina nokulungelelanisa idatha. Yicinge njengekhabhathi yeefayile apho ugcina idatha kumacandelo ahlukeneyo abizwa ngokuba ziitheyibhile.

WordPress Multisite isebenzisa isiseko sedatha esinye kwaye i-subsite nganye ifumana iitheyibhile zayo nge-blog id kwisimaphambili, ngoko xa sele ufakele ufakelo lothungelwano kwisiseko sakho sedatha kwaye wenze i-subsite, kufuneka ube nezi theyibhile:

_wp_1_options_ \- itheyibhile yeenketho ye-subsite yokuqala

_wp_2_options_ \- itheyibhile yeenketho ye-subsite yesibini

## Umboneleli we-hosting {#hosting-provider}

Umboneleli we-hosting yinkampani eyenza ukuba amashishini nabantu ngabanye benze iiwebhusayithi zabo zifumaneke nge-World Wide Web. Iinkonzo ezinikezelwa ngababoneleli be-web hosting ziya kwahluka kodwa ziqhele ukuquka uyilo lwewebhusayithi, indawo yokugcina kwi-host, kunye noqhagamshelo kwi-Internet.

## Domain {#domain}

Igama le-domain yidilesi abantu abayisebenzisayo ukutyelela isayithi yakho. Lixelela isikhangeli sewebhu apho kufuneka sikhangele khona isayithi yakho. Njengedilesi yesitalato, i-domain yindlela abantu abatyelela ngayo iwebhusayithi yakho kwi-intanethi. Kwaye, kufana nokuba nophawu phambi kwevenkile yakho. Ukuba unqwenela ukutyelela iwebhusayithi yethu, kuya kufuneka uchwetheze idilesi yethu yewebhu kwidilesi yesikhangeli sakho engu-[_www.ultimatemultisite.com_](http://www.ultimatemultisite.com) _,_ apho [**ultimatemultisite.com**](http://ultimatemultisite.com) ligama le-domain.

## Subdomain {#subdomain}

I-subdomain luhlobo lwesakhiwo soluhlu lwewebhusayithi phantsi kwe-domain ephambili, kodwa endaweni yokusebenzisa iifolda ukulungelelanisa umxholo kwiwebhusayithi, iphantse ifumane iwebhusayithi yayo. Iboniswa njenge [**https://site1.domain.com/**](https://site1.domain.com/) apho _site1_ ligama le-subdomain kwaye [_domain.com_](http://domain.com) yi-domain ephambili.

## Subdirectory {#subdirectory}

I-subdirectory luhlobo lwesakhiwo soluhlu lwewebhusayithi phantsi kwe-domain yengcambu esebenzisa iifolda ukulungelelanisa umxholo kwiwebhusayithi. I-subdirectory iyafana ne-subfolder kwaye amagama anokusetyenziswa ngokutshintshanayo. Iboniswa njenge [**https://domain.com/site1**](https://domain.com/site1) apho _site1_ ligama le-subdirectory kwaye [_domain.com_](http://domain.com) yi-domain ephambili.

## Subsite {#subsite}

I-Subsite yisayithi engumntwana oyenzayo kuthungelwano lwe-Multisite. Inokuba yi-**subdomain** okanye **subdirectory** kuxhomekeke kwindlela ufakelo lwakho lwe-WordPress Multisite oluqwalaselwe ngayo.

## Super Admin {#super-admin}

I-WordPress Super Admin yindima yomsebenzisi enezakhono ezipheleleyo zokulawula zonke ii-subsites kuthungelwano lwe-Multisite. Kubasebenzisi be-Multisite, le yeyona **nqanaba liphezulu lokufikelela** onokulinika kufakelo lwakho lwe-WordPress.

## Plugin {#plugin}

Ngokubanzi, i-plugin yiseti yekhowudi eyongeza ukusebenza okongezelelweyo kwisayithi yakho ye-WordPress. Oku kunokuba lula njengokutshintsha ilogo yokungena okanye kube nzima njengokongeza ukusebenza kwe-e-commerce. _Woocommerce and Contact Form_ yimizekelo ye-plugin.

Kwi-WordPress Multisite, ii-plugin zinokufakelwa kuphela ukusuka kwi-dashboard yomlawuli wothungelwano ngu-Super Admin. Abalawuli be-subsite banokwenza kusebenze okanye bangasebenzisi ii-plugin kuphela ngaphakathi kwe-subsite yabo.

## Imixholo {#themes}

Umxholo we-WordPress liqela leefayile (_imizobo, amaphepha esitayile, kunye nekhowudi_) elilawula inkangeleko iyonke yesayithi. Linika zonke izitayile zecala elingaphambili ezifana nesitayile sefonti, ulungiselelo lwephepha, imibala, njl.

Njengee-plugin, imixholo kwi-WordPress Multisite inokufakelwa kuphela ngu-Super Admin kwaye inokwenziwa isebenze kwinqanaba le-subsite ngabalawuli be-subsite.

## Site Template {#site-template}

**Site Template** yisayithi esisiseko esele ilungiselelwe enokusetyenziswa njengesiseko xa kusenziwa iisayithi ezintsha kuthungelwano lwakho.

Oku kuthetha ukuba ungakha isayithi esisiseko, wenze kusebenze ii-plugin ezahlukeneyo, usete umxholo osebenzayo, kwaye uyilungiselele ngayo nayiphi na indlela oyithandayo. Emva koko, xa umthengi wakho esenza i-akhawunti entsha, endaweni yokufumana isayithi ye-WordPress engagqibekanga engenawo umxholo onentsingiselo ngaphakathi kuyo, baya kufumana ikopi yesayithi yakho esisiseko enazo zonke izilungiselelo ezikhethekileyo kunye nemixholo sele ikhona.

## Domain Mapping {#domain-mapping}

**Domain mapping** nge-WordPress yindlela yokuthumela abasebenzisi kumamkeli ochanekileyo, ngokusebenzisa idilesi yewebhusayithi. Kwi-WordPress Multisite, ii-subsites zenziwa kusetyenziswa nokuba yi-subdirectory okanye yi-subdomain. Into eyenziwa yi-domain mapping kukuvumela abasebenzisi be-subsite ukuba basebenzise i-domain yenqanaba eliphezulu efana ne-[**joesbikeshop.com**](http://joesbikeshop.com) ukwenza idilesi yesayithi yabo ibonakale inobungcali ngakumbi.

## SSL {#ssl}

SSL imele **Secure Sockets Layer**. Sisatifikethi sedijithali esiqinisekisa isazisi sewebhusayithi kwaye sivumela uqhagamshelwano olufihliweyo. Kule mihla isetyenziswa njengetekhnoloji esemgangathweni yokugcina uqhagamshelwano lwe-intanethi lukhuselekile nokukhusela nayiphi na idatha ebuthathaka ethunyelwa phakathi kweenkqubo ezimbini, ithintele abaphuli-mthetho ekufundeni nasekutshintsheni naluphi na ulwazi oludlulisiweyo, kuquka neenkcukacha zobuqu ezinokubakho. Iibhrawuza zale mihla zifuna i-SSL, nto leyo eyenza ibaluleke xa kusenziwa kwaye kuqhutywa iwebhusayithi.

## Imidiya {#media}

Imidiya yimifanekiso, iaudio, ividiyo, kunye nezinye iifayile ezenza iwebhusayithi.

Iisayithi zenethiwekhi zabelana ngedathabheyisi enye kwi-WordPress Multisite, zigcina iindlela ezahlukileyo kwinkqubo yeefayile zeefayile zemidiya.

Indawo esemgangathweni ye-WordPress (wp-content/uploads) ihlala injalo; nangona kunjalo, indlela yayo itshintshiwe ukuze ibonise i-ID eyodwa yesayithi yenethiwekhi. Ngenxa yoko iifayile zemidiya zesayithi yenethiwekhi zivela njenge-wp-contents/uploads/site/[id].

## Iipermalinks {#permalinks}

Iipermalinks zii-URL ezisisigxina zeposti yebhlog okanye iphepha ngalinye ngaphakathi kwesayithi yakho. Iipermalinks zikwabizwa ngokuba **pretty links**. Ngokungagqibekanga, ii-URL ze-WordPress zisebenzisa ifomathi yomtya wombuzo ekhangeleka ngolu hlobo:

[**http://www.example.com/registration**](http://www.example.com/registration)

## Ultimate Multisite {#ultimate-multisite}

Ultimate Multisite yi-plugin ye-WordPress, eyenzelwe ukufakelwa kwe-WordPress Multisite, eguqula ukufakelwa kwakho kwe-WordPress kube yinethiwekhi esemgangathweni ephezulu yeesayithi – njenge [WordPress.com](https://WordPress.com) – ivumela abathengi ukuba benze iisayithi ngeentlawulo zenyanga, zekota, okanye zonyaka (ungakwazi nokwenza izicwangciso zasimahla).

## Ifomu yeCheckout {#checkout-form}

Ifomu yeCheckout yifomu yeodolo enenyathelo elinye okanye amaninzi ebandakanya ukudalwa kwesubsite, membership, kunye neeakhawunti zabasebenzisi ngobhaliso lwe-Ultimate Multisite. Iquka iindawo ezahlukeneyo kunye neefomu zentlawulo ekufuneka umsebenzisi azingenise ngexesha lenkqubo yokubhalisa.

## Webhook {#webhook}

I-webhook (ekwabizwa ngokuba yi-web callback okanye HTTP push API) yindlela yokuba i-app inike ezinye ii-aplikeshini ulwazi lwexesha lokwenyani. I-webhook ihambisa idatha kwezinye ii-aplikeshini njengoko isenzeka, oko kuthetha ukuba ufumana idatha ngoko nangoko.

**Ultimate Multisite webhooks** zivula amathuba angenasiphelo, zivumela abalawuli benethiwekhi ukuba benze zonke iintlobo zokudityaniswa ezingaqhelekanga-kodwa-eziluncedo, ngakumbi xa zisetyenziswa kunye neenkonzo ezifana no _Zapier and IFTTT_.

## Iziganeko {#events}

Isiganeko sisenzo esenzeka ngenxa yomsebenzisi okanye isenzo somnye umthombo, njengokucofa imawusi. Ultimate Multisite igcina irekhodi lazo zonke iziganeko kunye neelog ezenzekayo kuyo yonke inethiwekhi yakho. Ilandelela imisebenzi eyahlukeneyo eyenzeka kwi-multisite yakho, njengotshintsho lwezicwangciso.
