# Iimfuno ze-Ultimate Multisite

Into yokuqala onokuzibuza yona phambi kokuthenga Ultimate Multisite kukuba zithini iimfuno zokuqhuba isongezo sethu. Ewe, ngethamsanqa, kukho kuphela iimfuno ezimbalwa ezisisiseko zokuqhuba Ultimate Multisite: **igama le-domain** , **umboneleli we-hosting** olungileyo kunye nofakelo lwe-WordPress **Multisite**. Yiyo yonke into oyifunayo!

## Igama le-Domain {#domain-name}

Okokuqala, masithethe ngegama le-domain.

Xa ukhetha igama le-domain, gcina engqondweni ukuba yintoni indawo yakho ethile yeshishini kwaye uza kudala ntoni nge-Ultimate Multisite: Ngaba ufuna ukudala iarhente? Ukuthengisa iiwebhusayithi ezifana neevenkile? Okanye mhlawumbi i-WordPress web hosting? Yonke into inokwenzeka nge-Ultimate Multisite.

Isongezo sethu sibonelela ngeenkonzo zemirhumo, ukuqokelelwa kweentlawulo, iifom zokugqiba ukuthenga, iivawutsha zesaphulelo kunye nonxibelelwano nabathengi. Inxalenye enkulu yomsebenzi ofunekayo ukufaka, ukuqwalasela nokugcina i-WordPress Multisite yenziwa lula yi-Ultimate Multisite.

Nayiphi na indawo yeshishini oyikhethayo ingalawulwa usebenzisa isongezo sethu ukuba nje uthengisa iiwebhusayithi njengenkonzo! Qiniseka ukuba ukhetha igama le-domain elihambelana nesicwangciso sakho seshishini.

## Umboneleli we-Hosting {#hosting-provider}

Ngoku ukuba unegama le-domain elitsalayo elimele indawo yakho yeshishini kunye neshishini lakho, kuya kufuneka ukhethe inkampani elungileyo ye-web hosting ukuze ibambe inethiwekhi yakho nge-Ultimate Multisite. Kukho ezinye iindlela ezilungileyo kwimarike. Abanye ababoneleli be-hosting badibana kakuhle ne-Ultimate Multisite, nto leyo enokuba yinto ethatha isigqibo kuxhomekeke kwinto oceba ukuyakha.

Ultimate Multisite kufuneka isebenze nakuwuphi na umboneleli we-hosting. Umahluko kuphela kukuba ezinye iinkampani ze-hosting ezifana ne-_WPMU DEV Hosting_ , _WP Engine_ , _Closte_ , _Cloudways_ , _Gridpane_ kunye ne-_Runcloud_ zinodibaniso olusondeleyo ngakumbi lokumataniswa kwe-domain. Kwezinye ii-hosting, kusenokufuneka umatanise ii-domain zesiko ngesandla. Kukwalicebo elihle ukufumana umboneleli we-hosting oxhasa i-AutoSSL. _Closte_ , _Runcloud_ kunye ne-_cPanel_ bayayixhasa.

Xa kuziwa kwiinkcukacha ze-server, akukho fomyula ithile kuba kuya kuxhomekeka kwiimpawu nemisebenzi oza kuyinikezela kunye nobukhulu benethiwekhi yakho. I-shared hosting inokuba sisiqalo esihle, kodwa nje ukuba inethiwekhi yakho iqale ukukhula, mhlawumbi uya kufuna i-VPS ezinikeleyo.

## WordPress Multisite {#wordpress-multisite}

Ngoku, masithethe kancinci ngofakelo lwe-WordPress Multisite.

WordPress Multisite ikuvumela ukuba ube nenethiwekhi yeesayithi kufakelo olunye. Le yimpawu eyakhelwe ngaphakathi, kodwa ayisebenzi ngokungagqibekanga. Kufuneka uvule le mpawu ngaphakathi kufakelo lwakho lwe-WordPress nakwiphaneli yolawulo ye-web hosting.

Ukuba ne-WordPress ene-Multisite evuliweyo **sisiseko se-Ultimate Multisite**. Kuba ukusetyenziswa okuphambili kwe-Ultimate Multisite kukunceda abasebenzisi balawule kwaye bathengise iiwebhusayithi njengenkonzo, oku kufuneka kwenziwe kwi-WordPress ene-Multisite evuliweyo.

Sine **inqaku elinzulu** elifundisa inyathelo ngenyathelo into ekufuneka yenziwe ukuvula impawu ye-Multisite kufakelo lwakho lwe-WordPress.

Emva kokuba uvule i-Multisite kwi-WordPress, lixesha lokufaka Ultimate Multisite. Ukuze ufake Ultimate Multisite ngokuchanekileyo, kufuneka ube nale:

  * WordPress: v5.3+ (Kuyacetyiswa: Inguqulelo yokugqibela ezinzileyo)

  * PHP: 7.4.x (inkxaso ye-> 8.0 iyeza kungekudala)

  * MySQL: v5+ (Kuyacetyiswa: 5.6, ukuba awunayo i-8.0 ekhoyo kumbooneleli wakho we-hosting)

Ezi kuphela kweemfuno zesoftware ze-Ultimate Multisite.

Gcina engqondweni ukuba Ultimate Multisite inokusebenza nge-PHP 8.0 kodwa sicebisa ukuyiqhuba nge-PHP 7.4.x.

Kwakhona, sicebisa ukuyifaka kwii-**domain eziphambili** , hayi kwii-subdomain okanye kwii-subdirectory. Isenokusebenza kwii-subdomain/subdirectory, kodwa isenokubonisa ezinye iimpazamo.

Ngesikhokelo esineenkcukacha sokuba ungayifaka njani Ultimate Multisite, **cofa apha**.
