# Isalathiso Seesetingi {#settings-reference}

Eli phepha lilandelela iisetingi ezichaphazela ulawulo lwemihla ngemihla kunye notshintsho lwamva nje lokuziphatha kwi-Ultimate Multisite.

## Ezinye Iinketho {#other-options}

Indawo ethi **Ezinye Iinketho** ibonakala phantsi kwe-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Login & Registration**.

| Isetingi | Inkcazelo |
|---|---|
| **Enable Jumper** | Ibonisa isixhobo sokuhamba ngokukhawuleza se-Jumper kwindawo yolawulo. Yisebenzise ukutsibela ngqo kwizikrini ze-Ultimate Multisite, izinto zenethiwekhi, kunye neendawo zolawulo ezixhaswayo. Yicime ukuba awufuni ukuba le ndlela imfutshane ibonakale. |

## Ingxelo yeempazamo kunye ne-telemetry {#error-reporting-and-telemetry}

Isetingi yangaphambili yokuzikhethela ukunika ingxelo yeempazamo isusiwe kwiphepha leesetingi. I-telemetry engachazwanga igama ivaliwe kwaye akukho UI toggle yokuyivula.

Ukuba ugcina ii-runbook zangaphakathi okanye iiscreenshot zephepha leesetingi, susa izikhombisi kwifield endala yokuzikhethela ingxelo yeempazamo ukuze abalawuli bangakhangeli isetingi engasekhoyo.

## Iisetingi zokungenisa/zokuthumela ngaphandle {#importexport-settings}

Ithebhu yeesetingi ze-**Import/Export** ichaza ukuba zeziphi iisetingi ezilawulayo kwaye iqhagamshela ngqo ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Site Export** yeendawo kunye nee-archive zenethiwekhi. Sebenzisa ithebhu yeesetingi kuqwalaselo lokungenisa/lokuthumela ngaphandle, sebenzisa **Tools > Export & Import** kwindlela yokusebenza yokuthumela ngaphandle/yokungenisa yesayithi enye, kwaye usebenzise isixhobo se-Site Export xa ufuna i-archive epheleleyo ye-Network Export.

## Isilumkiso sebhalansi ye-Domain Seller HostAfrica {#domain-seller-hostafrica-balance-warning}

Xa i-addon ye-Domain Seller iqhagamshelwe kwi-HostAfrica, abalawuli benethiwekhi ngoku babona isilumkiso esinokurhoxiswa sebhalansi ephantsi xa ibhalansi ye-account yomthengisi iphantsi kakhulu ukuze kusetyenzwe ngokuthembekileyo ubhaliso okanye uhlaziyo lweedomain.

Phatha esi saziso njengesilumkiso sokusebenza: yongeza ibhalansi yomthengisi we-HostAfrica ngaphambi kokwamkela ubhaliso oluninzi lweedomain ezihlawulelwayo, emva koko ubuyele kwiisetingi ze-Domain Seller okanye kwisikrini sokubeka iliso kwiidomain ukuqinisekisa ukuba ubhaliso kunye nohlaziyo lunokuqhubeka njengesiqhelo.

## Iisetingi ze-AI provider connector {#ai-provider-connector-settings}

Iisetingi ze-AI provider connector ngoku ziveza kuphela ii-pool zee-account ze-OAuth ezixhaswayo:

| Provider | Inkqubo yokuseta |
|---|---|
| **Anthropic Max** | Qhagamshela i-account enye okanye ezingaphezulu ze-Anthropic Max ngeqhosha le-OAuth. Sebenzisa indlela yokubuyela umva ye-OAuth eyenziwa ngesandla xa ibrawuza ekwi-sandbox ingakwazi ukugqiba i-redirect ngokuzenzekelayo. |
| **OpenAI ChatGPT/Codex** | Qhagamshela ii-account ze-ChatGPT ngenkqubo efanayo ye-OAuth pool. Imisebenzi exhaswa yi-connector ingasebenzisa iicall zezixhobo ze-ChatGPT Codex emva kokuba i-account iqhagamshelwe. |
| **Google AI Pro** | Qhagamshela ii-account ze-Google AI Pro nge-OAuth, emva koko uhlaziye i-connector ukuba uluhlu lwee-account aluhlaziyeki kwangoko. |

I-Cursor Pro ayisenguye provider oxhaswayo. Susa iiscreenshot zangaphakathi ezindala, ii-runbook, okanye amanyathelo okuqalisa akhankanya iifield zokuseta ze-Cursor Pro okanye iindlela ze-connector.

Xa usongeza okanye ususa ii-account ze-provider, faka idilesi ye-imeyile esebenzayo ye-account ehlaziywayo okanye ecinywayo kwaye ugcine iisetingi ze-provider ngaphambi kokuvavanya imisebenzi exhaswa yi-connector.
