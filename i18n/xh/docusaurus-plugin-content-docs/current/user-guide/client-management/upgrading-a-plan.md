# Ukunyusa Isicwangciso (v2)

_**INQAKU ELIBALULEKILEYO: Eli nqaku libhekisa ku-Ultimate Multisite version 2.x.**_

Abathengi bakho bayakwazi ukunyusa izicwangciso zabo nangaliphi na ixesha. Banokunyusela kwesinye isicwangciso okanye bathenge naziphi na iinkonzo okanye iipakethe ezongezelelweyo ozinikezelayo kwinethiwekhi yakho.

Kule tutorial siza kugubungela indlela abanokunyusa ngayo isicwangciso sabo nokuba kwenzeka ntoni emva kwenkqubo yokunyusa.

Ukuze banyuse isicwangciso sabo, abathengi bakho kufuneka bafikelele kwi-Dashboard yabo baze baye kwiphepha le-**Account**.

![I-Dashboard yesayithi engaphantsi yomthengi enekhonkco lemenyu ye-Account elibonakalayo](/img/account-page/account-menu.png)

Kwiphepha le-Account, baya kuboniswa ubulungu babo bangoku kunye nesicwangciso esinxulumene nabo. Ukuze banyusele kwesinye isicwangciso, kufuneka bacofe ku-**Guqula** kwikona ephezulu ekunene yecandelo le-**Ubulungu Bakho**.

![Iphepha le-Account ikhadi loBulungu Bakho elineqhosha elithi Guqula](/img/account-page/membership-change-button.png)

Baya kuthunyelwa kwifomu yokugqibezela intlawulo apho zonke izicwangciso ezikhoyo ziya kuboniswa.

Baya kuba nakho ukubona **iinkonzo neepakethe ezikhoyo kwisicwangciso sabo sangoku** , ukuba bafuna nje ukuthenga inkonzo ethile okanye iphakheji (njengotyelelo olungenamda okanye indawo yediski kumzekelo wethu apha), benganyusi isicwangciso.

![Umkhethi wokunyusa obonisa izicwangciso neepakethe ezikhoyo kwicala lomthengi](/img/account-page/upgrade-picker.png)

Emva kokuba bekhethe imveliso abafuna ukuyithenga, baya kubona ukuba kufuneka bahlawule malini ngoku - ngaphandle kwalo naliphi na ityala elikhoyo - kwaye baya kuhlawuliswa malini ngomhla olandelayo wentlawulo.

Ngokuqhelekileyo, ukuba imveliso sesinye isicwangciso kwaye intlawulo iza kwenziwa phakathi kwentlawulo yobulungu, baya kufumana ikhredithi yesixa esihlawulwe kwisicwangciso sokuqala.

![Isishwankathelo sentlawulo yokunyusa esibonisa ikhredithi esetyenzisiweyo kunye nesixa sentlawulo elandelayo](/img/account-page/upgrade-summary.png)

Ukuba bakhetha isicwangciso okanye iphakheji engayi kutshintsha nto kumrhumo wangoku, baya kubona umyalezo ochaza oko.

![Isaziso xa isicwangciso esikhethiweyo singawutshintshi umrhumo](/img/account-page/upgrade-no-change.png)

Emva kokuba ukugqibezela intlawulo kugqityiwe, imveliso/imveliso ezintsha ziya kongezwa kwi-akhawunti yabathengi bakho kwaye yonke imida okanye iimpawu zemveliso/imveliso ezintsha ziya kongezwa kuyo ngoko nangoko: utyelelo, indawo yediski, izithuba, njl...

## Iindlela Zokunyusa Nokuthoba {#upgrade-and-downgrade-paths}

Kwimveliso nganye yakho, uya kuba nethebhu ethi **Ukunyusa Nokuthoba**. Ukhetho lokuqala kuloo thebhu yintsimi ebizwa **Iqela Lesicwangciso**.

**Amaqela ezicwangciso** yinto ekuvumela ukuba uxelele Ultimate Multisite ukuba izicwangciso ezithile zezentsapho enye, kwaye ngenxa yoko kufuneka zisetyenziselwe ukwakha iinketho zendlela yokunyusa/yokuthoba.

![Ukuhlela imveliso ithebhu ethi Ukunyusa Nokuthoba enentsimi yeQela Lesicwangciso](/img/config/product-upgrades-plan-group.png)

Umzekelo, une-**sicwangciso Sasimahla** , **iSicwangciso Esisisiseko** kunye **neSicwangciso sePremium** ezikhoyo. Ufuna abasebenzisi ababhalise phantsi kwe-**Sicwangciso Sasimahla** bakwazi ukunyusela kuphela kwi-**Sicwangciso sePremium** kwaye awufuni babone "iSicwangciso Esisisiseko" njengokhetho lokunyusa. Konke okufuneka ukwenze kukwabela igama elifanayo leqela lesicwangciso kuzo zombini izicwangciso Sasimahla nePremium njengoko kubonisiwe kwizikrini ezingezantsi.

![Iphepha lemveliso yeSicwangciso Sasimahla elineqela lesicwangciso seHigh End elabelweyo](/img/config/product-upgrades-free.png)

![Iphepha lemveliso yeSicwangciso sePremium elineqela lesicwangciso seHigh End elabelweyo](/img/config/product-upgrades-premium.png)

Oko oku kufanele ukukwenza kukuxelela Ultimate Multisite ukuba kukho "intsapho" yezicwangciso kwinethiwekhi ebizwa **High End**. Xa kunikezelwa ukunyusa okanye ukuthoba, kuphela izicwangciso ezivela kwintsapho enye eziya kuboniswa njengokhetho kumsebenzisi.
