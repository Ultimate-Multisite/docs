# Iphepha le-Account loMthengi Wakho (v2)

_**INQAKU ELIBALULEKILEYO: Eli nqaku libhekisa ku-Ultimate Multisite inguqulelo 2.x.**_

Xa abathengi bebhalisela isicwangciso kwinethiwekhi yakho, bafumana ukufikelela kwiwebhusayithi kunye ne-Dashboard yayo enolwazi olubalulekileyo malunga neentlawulo zabo, ubulungu, imimandla, imida yesicwangciso, njl...

Kule tutorial, siza kukukhokela kwiphepha le-account lomthengi kwaye uza kubona oko abathengi bakho banokukubona nokukwenza ngaphakathi kulo.

## Iphepha le-Account

Iphepha le-account liyafikeleleka ngokucofa ku-**Account** ngaphakathi kwi-Dashboard yomthengi wakho.

![Imenyu ye-Account kwi-Dashboard yomthengi](/img/account-page/account-menu.png)

Kwiinethiwekhi ze-tenant ezizimeleyo, Ultimate Multisite v2.13.0 igcina la mava olawulo lwabathengi kwisiza esikhulu. Ukuba umthengi uvula izenzo ze-account, checkout, billing, invoice, ulawulo lwesiza, ukutshintsha i-template, okanye ukudibanisa i-domain ukusuka kwi-tenant ezimeleyo, isenzo sibuyela kwiphaneli yomthengi yesiza esikhulu ukuze iirekhodi ze-billing yenethiwekhi nobulungu zihlale zinegunya.

Xa umthengi efika evela kwi-tenant ezimeleyo, iphaneli yomthengi yesiza esikhulu inokubandakanya ikhonkco lokubuyela kwisiza se-tenant. Ikhonkco lokubuyela liboniswa kuphela xa Ultimate Multisite inokungqinisisa indawo ekubuyelwa kuyo njengenye yeziza zomthengi, nto leyo ethintela ukuhanjiswa okungacwangciswanga ngelixa kugcinwa ukuhamba komsebenzi kwe-tenant.

![Isishwankathelo sephepha le-account yomthengi](/img/account-page/overview.png)

Emva kokuba umthengi ecofe kuyo, uza kubona isishwankathelo sobulungu bakhe, idilesi ye-billing, ii-invoice, imimandla, imida yesiza, kwaye uya kukwazi nokutshintsha i-****Template yeSayithi** (ukuba kuvunyelwe kwinethiwekhi yakho)**.

Banokutshintsha nobulungu baye kwesinye isicwangciso, okanye bathenge enye iphakheji okanye inkonzo oyinikezelayo. Makhe sijonge icandelo ngalinye ngokwahlukeneyo.

### Isishwankathelo Sobulungu Bakho:

Ibhloko yokuqala ngqo phantsi kwegama lewebhusayithi yabathengi bakho ibonisa isishwankathelo sesicwangciso sabo sangoku kunye neenkonzo/iipakheji ezathengwa kunye naso. Le bhloko ikwabonisa inombolo yobulungu, isixa sokuqala esahlawulwa ngaso, ixabiso lesicwangciso kunye nayo nayiphi na inkonzo/ipakheji, kwanokuba bahlawuliswa kangaphi kobu bulungu. Banokubona nokuba ubulungu bu-**Active** , **Expired** okanye **Canceled**.

![Isishwankathelo sobulungu esibonisa isicwangciso, isixa, kunye neenkcukacha ze-billing](/img/account-page/membership-card.png)

Ngqo phantsi kwale bhloko, abathengi bakho banokubona iibhloko ze-**Malunga Nesi Siza** kunye ne-**Mida Yesiza**. Ezi bhloko zibabonisa yonke imida ehambelana nesicwangciso sabo: indawo yediski, izithuba, amaphepha, utyelelo, njl... Le mida inokulungiswa kwiphepha lesicwangciso ngasinye ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Iimveliso**.

![Iibhloko zikaMalunga Nesi Siza kunye neMida Yesiza ezibonisa imida yesicwangciso](/img/account-page/site-limits.png)

Kwicala lasekunene lika-**Ubulungu Bakho** , abathengi banokucofa ku-**Tshintsha**. Oku kuya kubabonisa zonke izicwangciso ezikhoyo kunye neepakheji/iinkonzo. Ukuba bakhetha esinye isicwangciso, imida yesicwangciso iya kusebenza endaweni yemida yangoku yobulungu - akunamsebenzi nokuba bayasinciphisa okanye bayasiphucula.

Ngoku, ukuba abathengi bakho bakhetha ukuthenga iipakheji okanye iinkonzo zobu bulungu bangoku - njengendawo yediski engaphezulu okanye utyelelo - ubulungu bangoku abuyi kutshintshwa kodwa kuphela iipakheji ezintsha ziya kongezwa kubo.

Qaphela ukuba iikhowudi ze-coupon azinakongezwa kweli phepha lokutshintsha ubulungu. Ukuba umthengi usebenzise ikhowudi ye-coupon ekuthengweni kobulungu bokuqala, ikhowudi iya kusebenza nakobu bulungu butsha.

### Ukuhlaziya Idilesi ye-Billing:

Kwiphepha le-account, abathengi bakho banokuhlaziya idilesi yabo ye-billing. Kufuneka nje bacofe ku-**Hlaziya** ecaleni kwe-_Dilesi ye-Billing_.

![Icandelo leDilesi ye-Billing elineqhosha elithi Hlaziya](/img/account-page/billing-address.png)

Ifestile entsha iza kuvela kumthengi wakho. Konke ekufuneka ekwenzile kukugcwalisa idilesi entsha aze acofe ku-_Gcina Utshintsho_.

![Ifomu yokuhlaziya idilesi ye-billing](/img/account-page/billing-address-form.png)

### Ukutshintsha i-Template yeSayithi:

Ukuvumela abathengi bakho ukuba batshintshe ii-template zeziza zabo, kufuneka uye ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Izicwangciso > Iziza** uze uvule ukhetho **Vumela Ukutshintsha i-Template**.

Kwakhona, ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Iimveliso**, khetha izicwangciso zakho uye kwi-tab ethi **Ii-Template zeSayithi**. Qinisekisa ukuba ukhetho **Vumela ii-Template zeSayithi** luvuliwe kwaye ku-**Imowudi yoKhetho lwe-Template yeSayithi** , ukhetho **Khetha ii-Template zeSayithi Ezikhoyo** lukhethiwe.

![I-tab yee-template zesiza semveliso enemowudi yokukhetha i-template](/img/config/product-site-templates.png)

Uya kukwazi ukubona zonke ii-template zesiza ezikhoyo kwiwebhusayithi yakho. Khetha ezo ufuna ukuzenza zifumaneke kunye nezo ungafuni ukuba zifumaneke kubathengi bakho ababhalise phantsi kwesi sicwangciso. Qaphela ukuba olu khetho lukwachaphazela ifomu ye-checkout, ngoko nayiphi na i-template ekhethwe njenge-**Ayifumaneki** ayiyi kuvela kwiphepha lobhaliso lesi sicwangciso.

Ngoku abathengi bakho banokucofa ku-**Tshintsha i-Template yeSayithi** ngaphakathi kwiphepha labo le-account.

![Iqhosha elithi Tshintsha i-Template yeSayithi kwiphepha le-account](/img/account-page/change-template-button.png)

Ultimate Multisite 2.10.0 ibonisa iphaneli yokutshintsha i-template eyilwe ngokutsha. Iphaneli iqala nge-**khadi le-template yangoku** ukuze abathengi babone ukuba yeyiphi i-template esebenzayo ngaphambi kokuba bakhethe enye endaweni yayo.

Igridi esisigxina yee-template zesiza ezikhoyo ihlala ibonakala ngelixa abathengi bephonononga iinketho zabo. Oku kubanceda bathelekise ii-template ezivunyelweyo kwisicwangciso sabo ngaphandle kokuphulukana nokubona ukhetho lwangoku.

![Uluhlu lwee-template zesiza ezikhoyo kwisicwangciso](/img/config/site-templates-list.png)

Emva kokukhetha leyo bafuna ukutshintshela kuyo, baya kucelwa ukuba baqinisekise utshintsho.

![Incoko yokuqinisekisa ukutshintsha i-template yesiza](/img/account-page/template-switch-confirm.png)

Emva kokuvula uqinisekiso nokucofa ku-**Qhuba Utshintsho** , i-template yesiza entsha iya kusetyenziswa kwiwebhusayithi yomthengi wakho.

Abathengi banokusebenzisa kwakhona **Reset current template** kule panel xa kufuneka babuyisele isayithi kwi-template eyabelwe ngoku. Njengokutshintshela kwenye i-template, ukubuyisela i-template kunokubhala ngaphezulu umxholo wesayithi, ngoko abathengi kufuneka bakuqinisekise kuphela xa besiqonda isenzo sokubuyisela.

### Ukongeza ii-Domain zesiko:

Abathengi bakho baya kuba nenketho yokongeza i-domain yesiko yale plan kwiphepha labo le-account. Ukuvumela abathengi bakho basebenzise ii-domain zesiko, yiya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings >** **Domain Mapping**.

Vula ukhetho **Enable Domain Mapping**. Oku kuya kuvumela abathengi bakho basebenzise ii-domain zesiko kwinqanaba le-network.

Ungalibali kwakhona ukujonga ukuba domain mapping ivuliwe ngokwemveliso - kuba unokunqanda imveliso ingavumeli abathengi bakho basebenzise ii-domain zesiko.

Yiya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Products**. Khetha i-plan oyifunayo uze uye kwi-tab **Custom Domains**. Vula ukhetho **Allow Custom Domains**.

![I-tab yee-Domain zesiko enetoggle ye-Allow Custom Domains](/img/config/product-custom-domains.png)

Oku kuya kuvumela bonke abathengi ababhalisele le plan ethile ukuba basebenzise ii-domain zesiko. Ngoku, kwiphepha le-Account, abathengi bakho banokongeza i-domain yesiko ngokucofa ku-**Add Domain**.

![Iqhosha le-Add Domain kwiphepha le-account](/img/account-page/add-domain-button.png)

Ifestile yokuqala evulekayo iya kubonisa abathengi bakho umyalezo obayalela indlela yokuhlaziya iirekhodi zabo ze-DNS ukuze le domain yesiko isebenze kwi-network yakho.

![Imiyalelo ye-DNS eboniswa xa kongezwa i-domain yesiko](/img/account-page/add-domain-dns.png)

Lo myalezo unokuhlelwa (nguwe) ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Domain Mapping > Add New Domain Instructions**.

![Useto lwe-Add New Domain Instructions kwi-Domain Mapping](/img/config/settings-domain-mapping.png)

Nanku umbono opheleleyo wephepha lezicwangciso ze-domain mapping:

![Iphepha elipheleleyo lezicwangciso ze-domain mapping](/img/config/settings-domain-mapping-full.png)

Emva kokucofa **Next Step** , abathengi bakho banokongeza igama le-domain yabo yesiko baze bakhethe ukuba le domain yesiko iya kuba yeyona iphambili. Qaphela ukuba abathengi bakho banokusebenzisa ngaphezu kwe-domain enye yesiko kwiiwebhusayithi zabo, ngoko banokukhetha ukuba yeyiphi eya kuba yeyona iphambili.

![Ungeniso lwegama le-domain yesiko kunye nokhetho lwe-domain ephambili](/img/account-page/add-domain-primary.png)

Emva kokucofa ku-**Add Domain** , i-domain iya kongezwa kwi-account yomthengi wakho. Into ekufuneka bayenze ngoku kukutshintsha iirekhodi ze-DNS zale domain yesiko kumregistrar we-domain wabo.

### Ukutshintsha iPassword:

Ngaphakathi kwi-account dashboard, abathengi bakho banokutshintsha kwakhona i-password yabo ngokucofa ku-**Change Password**.

![Iqhosha le-Change Password kwiphepha le-account](/img/account-page/change-password-button.png)

Oku kuya kubonisa ifestile entsha apho abathengi bakho kuya kufuneka bagcwalise i-password yabo yangoku baze emva koko bagcwalise i-password entsha abafuna ukuyisebenzisa.

![Ifomu yokutshintsha i-password enamasimi e-password yangoku kunye nentsha](/img/account-page/change-password-form.png)

### Indawo Yengozi:

Sineenketho ezimbini eziboniswa kwicandelo le-**Danger Zone**: **Delete Site** kunye ne-**Delete Account**. Zombini zikwicandelo le-Danger Zone kuba ezi zenzo zimbini azinakubuyiselwa umva. Ukuba abathengi bakho bayayicima iwebhusayithi yabo okanye i-account yabo, abanakuzibuyisela.

![Danger Zone eneenketho ze-Delete Site kunye ne-Delete Account](/img/account-page/danger-zone.png)

Ukuba abathengi bakho bacofa kuyo nayiphi na kwezi nketho zimbini, baya kuboniswa ifestile apho kuya kufuneka bavule ukhetho lokususa iwebhusayithi okanye i-account kwaye baya kulunyukiswa ukuba esi senzo asinakwenziwa singabikho.

![Incoko yokuqinisekisa i-Delete Site](/img/account-page/delete-site-confirm.png)

![Incoko yokuqinisekisa i-Delete Account](/img/account-page/delete-account-confirm.png)

Ukuba bayayicima iwebhusayithi yabo, i-account yabo kunye nobulungu babo zisaza kuhlala zingachaphazelekanga. Baza kuphulukana nje nawo wonke umxholo okwiwebhusayithi yabo. Ukuba bayayicima i-account yabo, zonke iiwebhusayithi, ubulungu kunye nolwazi olunxulumene nale account ziya kulahleka.
