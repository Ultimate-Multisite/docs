# Imibuzo Ebuzwa Rhoqo

Nantsi eminye **Imibuzo Ebuzwa Rhoqo** edla ngokuvela ngexesha lezicelo zenkxaso.

  * **Yintoni i-Ultimate Multisite?**

Ultimate Multisite yi-WordPress plugin, eyenzelwe ufakelo lwe-WordPress multisite, eguqula ufakelo lwakho lwe-WordPress lube yinethiwekhi yeepremiyamu yeendawo – njenge [WordPress.com](https://WordPress.com) – ivumela abathengi ukuba benze iindawo ngeentlawulo zenyanga, zekota, okanye zonyaka (ungakwazi nokwenza iiplani zasimahla).

  * **Ngaba i-Ultimate Multisite iza kusebenza ngaphandle kwe-Multisite?**

Hayi. Ultimate Multisite ifuna i-WordPress multisite, subdirectory, okanye subdomain.

  * **Zeziphi iimfuno ze-Ultimate Multisite?**

Ultimate Multisite ifuna:

**Uguqulelo lwe-WordPress** : v5.3+ (Kucetyiswa: Uguqulelo Oluzinzileyo Lokugqibela)
**Uguqulelo lwe-PHP** : 7.4.x (Inkxaso ukuya ku > 8.0 iza kungekudala)
**Uguqulelo lwe-MySQL** : v5+ (Kucetyiswa: Uguqulelo 5.6, ukuba awunayo i-8.0 ekhoyo kumboneleli wakho wokusingatha)

_I-Multisite (subdomain okanye subdirectory) nayo kufuneka ivulwe._ [Indlela yokufaka i-WordPress Multisite](/user-guide/getting-started/how-to-install-wordpress-multisite).

  * **Ngaba i-Ultimate Multisite iza kusebenza ne-Multisite ene-Subdirectories?**

Ewe. Ultimate Multisite ixhasa zombini ufakelo lwe-subdirectory kunye nofakelo lwe-subdirectory.

  * **Ngaba i-Ultimate Multisite iza kusebenza ne-Multisite ene-Subdomains?**

Ewe. Ultimate Multisite ixhasa zombini ufakelo lwe-subdirectory kunye nofakelo lwe-subdomain.

  * **Ndingayisebenzisa i-Ultimate Multisite ukunciphisa ukuba zeziphi iiPlugins neeThemes abasebenzisi abanokuzisebenzisa?**

Ewe. Nge-Ultimate Multisite ungakhetha ukuba zeziphi ii-plugins nee-themes ezifumanekayo kwiplani nganye kwinethiwekhi yakho.

  * **Ndingayisebenzisa i-Ultimate Multisite ukunciphisa inani leeposti umsebenzisi anokuzidala kwisiza sakhe?**

Ewe. Nge-Ultimate Multisite unokumisela imida yeeposti ku **lonke uhlobo lweposti olukhoyo** kwinethiwekhi yakho. Unganciphisa kwakhona inani labasebenzisi umlawuli anokubamema, **isixa sendawo yediski,** nokunye okuninzi.

  * **Ngaba i-Ultimate Multisite ixhasa iiTemplates zeSite?**

Ewe. Nge-Ultimate Multisite ungadala iiSite Templates ezininzi kangangoko ufuna. IiSite Templates azizizo ii-themes, ziziza eziqhelekileyo kwinethiwekhi yakho. Ungavula ii-plugins, ii-themes, kwaye ulungise loo Site Template nangayiphi na indlela oyifunayo. Ukuba umsebenzisi ukhetha loo template, isiza sakhe siya kudalwa njengekopi yaloo template, nazo zonke iinguqulelo ezenzelwe wena zikho.

Unokumisela kwakhona itemplate ethile yeplani. Ngokwenza oko, wonke umsebenzisi obhalisa kuloo plani uya kuba nesiza sakhe sidalwe njengekopi efanayo yaloo Site Template.

  * **Ngaba i-Ultimate Multisite idityaniswa ne-WooCommerce?**

Ewe!

Ukuba ufuna abasebenzisi benethiwekhi yakho babe ne-WooCommerce ekhoyo kubo, ubavumele bamise iivenkile, oko kunokwenzeka usebenzisa i-Ultimate Multisite. Unganciphisa nokuba linani elingakanani leemveliso abathengi bakho abaya kukwazi ukuzidala kwinqanaba ngalinye leplani.

Ngokumalunga neentlawulo zobhaliso ze-Ultimate Multisite, ungasebenzisa i-[Ultimate Multisite: WooCommerce Integration Add-on](https://ultimatemultisite.com/addons/wp-ultimo-woocommerce/) ukuvumela abathengi bakho be-Ultimate Multisite ukuba bahlawule besebenzisa ii-gateways ze-WooCommerce kwisiza sakho esiphambili. Ngale ndlela, ungasebenzisa ilayibrari enkulu yee-gateways zentlawulo eziphuhliselwe i-WooCommerce kwaye uzisebenzise ukubiza abathengi bakho be-Ultimate Multisite.

  * **Ikhona indlela yokufudusa ubhaliso ukusuka kwi-WPMUDEV Pro Sites?**

Ewe, Ultimate Multisite ngoku ikunikeza [isixhobo esiluncedo sokukunceda ufuduse](https://ultimatemultisite.com/addons/wp-ultimo-pro-sites-migrator/) inethiwekhi yakho ye-Pro Sites.

  * **Kutheni ithebhu ethi “Allowed Plugins” ingenanto kwiPhepha lam lokuHlela iPlani?**

Ultimate Multisite ikuvumela kuphela ukuba uvumele okanye wale ukufikelela kwii-plugins ezingavulwanga kwinethiwekhi yonke kwinethiwekhi yakho. Xa i-plugin ivulwe kwinethiwekhi yonke, ngokwendalo yayo, iya kulayishwa ngokuzenzekelayo kuyo yonke indawo yenethiwekhi yakho.

Ukuba awuboni naziphi na ii-plugins zidweliswe kwithebhu ethi “Allowed Plugins”, jonga ukuba i-plugin oyifunayo isebenza kwinethiwekhi yonke okanye hayi.

  * **Ndingayisebenzisa uyilo lwam lweTheyibhile yamaXabiso?**

Ewe. Ukususela kuguqulelo 1.4.0, Ultimate Multisite ixhasa ukutshintshwa kweetemplate.

  * **Ngaba i-Ultimate Multisite isebenza ne-WP Engine?**

Ewe. Ultimate Multisite yayisoloko ixhaswa yi-WP Engine, kodwa ukwenza i-domain mapping isebenze kwakufuna amanyathelo ongezelelweyo ezenziwa ngesandla ngumlawuli (ukongeza i-domain kwiphaneli ye-WP Engine). **Oku akusenjalo ukususela kuguqulelo 1.6.0.** Ultimate Multisite idityaniswa ngqo ne-WP Engine API kwaye ii-domain zongezwa kwiphaneli ngokuzenzekelayo emva kokumapping.

  * **Ngowuphi umboneleli wokusingatha omcebisayo?**

Ultimate Multisite kufuneka isebenze nawo nawuphi na umboneleli wokusingatha. Umahluko kuphela kukuba ezinye iinkampani zokusingatha ezifana ne-Closte, Serverpilot, WPMUDEV, WP Engine, Cloudways, Gridpane kunye ne-[Runcloud.io](http://Runcloud.io) zinodityaniso olusondeleyo ngakumbi kwizakhono ze-domain mapping ukuze zisebenze kwangoko. Kwezinye iinkonzo zokusingatha, kusenokufuneka umisele i-domain mapping ngesandla.
