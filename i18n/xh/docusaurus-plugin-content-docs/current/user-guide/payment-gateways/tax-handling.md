# Ukuphathwa kweRhafu

Ultimate Multisite inemodyuli yokuqokelela irhafu eyakhelwe kwi-plugin yethu engundoqo, ngoko ukuba kufuneka uqokelele iirhafu zentengiso kwizicwangciso zakho, iipakethe kunye neenkonzo, ungakwenza oko ngokulula ngaphandle kokubhenela ekufakeni naziphi na ii-add-ons.

Kwiinkampani eziseYurophu, sinikezela nge-**add-on** eyongeza izixhobo kunye neempawu zokuxhasa ngcono ukuthotyelwa kwe-**VAT**.

Ultimate Multisite ayifayili okanye ayithumeli iirhafu egameni lakho kurhulumente; sikunceda nje uqokelele iirhafu ezifanelekileyo ngexesha lentengiselwano. **Kusafuneka uthumele iirhafu ngokwakho.**

## Ukwenza Ukuqokelelwa Kwerhafu Kusebenze

Ukuqokelelwa kwerhafu akusebenzi ngokungagqibekanga. Ukuze ukwenze kusebenze, kufuneka uye ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Taxes** uze utshintshe ukuze wenze isethingi ethi Enable Taxes isebenze.

![Isitshintshi se-Enable Taxes phezulu kwiphepha leesethingi zeRhafu](/img/config/settings-taxes-enable.png)

Nanku umbono opheleleyo wephepha leesethingi zerhafu:

![Iphepha elipheleleyo leesethingi zerhafu](/img/config/settings-taxes-full.png)

Unokujonga kwakhona iisethingi zerhafu zeemveliso ezizodwa:

![Iisethingi zerhafu zeemveliso](/img/config/settings-taxes.png)

### Irhafu engafakwanga vs. Irhafu efakiweyo

Ngokungagqibekanga, onke amaxabiso eemveliso zakho awabandakanyi rhafu, okuthetha ukuba iirhafu **azifakwanga** kwixabiso lemveliso. Ukuba sigqiba ukuba umthengi kufuneka ahlawule iirhafu kwintengo ethile, siya kongeza iirhafu **ngaphezulu** kwesixa esingaphambi kwerhafu.

Ukuba ukhetha ukuba iirhafu zifakwe kwixabiso lemveliso yakho, ungakwenza oko ngokwenza isethingi ye-**Inclusive Tax** isebenze.

![Umqolo wesitshintshi se-Inclusive Tax ngezantsi kwesethingi ye-Enable Taxes](/img/config/settings-taxes-inclusive.png)

Ungalibali **ukugcina** utshintsho olwenzileyo.

###

## Ukwenza Amaxabiso Erhafu

Emva kokuba wenze Ukuqokelelwa Kwerhafu kusebenze, kuya kufuneka wenze amaxabiso erhafu kwiindawo ezithile usebenzisa umhleli wethu wamaxabiso erhafu.

Ungafikelela kumhleli ngokucofa iqhosha elithi **Manage Tax Rates** kwibar esecaleni yephepha leesethingi zeRhafu.

![Ikhonkco le-Manage Tax Rates kwiphaneli ye-Tax Rates kwiphepha leesethingi](/img/config/settings-taxes-manage-rates.png)

Kwiphepha lomhleli wamaxabiso erhafu, ungongeza Amaxabiso Erhafu amatsha ngokucofa iqhosha elithi **Add new Row**.

![Itheyibhile yomhleli wamaxabiso erhafu eneqhosha le-Add new Row phezulu](/img/config/tax-rates-editor.png)

Kuya kufuneka unike ixabiso ngalinye lerhafu **isihloko** (esisetyenziswa kwii-invoices). Emva koko ungakhetha **ilizwe** (liyafuneka), **iphondo,** kunye **nesixeko** (zombini ziyakhethwa) apho le rhafu iza kuhlawuliswa khona. Ekugqibeleni, yongeza **ixabiso lerhafu ngeepesenti**.

### Iindidi Zerhafu

Unokwenza kwakhona Iindidi Zerhafu ezininzi, ukuze wongeze amaxabiso erhafu ahlukeneyo kwiintlobo ezahlukeneyo zeemveliso.

Cofa ukuze **Add new Tax Category** , emva koko ubhale igama lodidi lwakho uze ucinezele **Create**.

![Iqhosha le-Add new Tax Category phezulu kumhleli wamaxabiso erhafu](/img/config/tax-categories-add.png)

![Indawo yegalelo ye-Tax Category Name kwimodal yokwenza udidi](/img/config/tax-categories-create-modal.png)

Ukujonga phakathi kweendidi, cofa **Switch** uze ukhethe udidi ofuna ukongeza kulo iirhafu ezintsha.

![Iqhosha le-dropdown le-Switch lokutshintsha phakathi kweendidi zerhafu](/img/config/tax-categories-switch.png)

![I-dropdown yokukhetha udidi lwerhafu ebonisa iindidi ezikhoyo](/img/config/tax-categories-select.png)

Ungaseta udidi lwerhafu lwemveliso ethile ngokuya ku-**Product edit page** emva koko uye kwithebhu ye-Taxes.

![Ithebhu yeerhafu zemveliso enodidi lwerhafu kunye nesitshintshi sokuba iyarhafiswa](/img/config/product-taxes.png)

Kweso sikrini sinye, ungacima isitshintshi se-**Is Taxable?** ukuze wazise Ultimate Multisite ukuba ayifanele iqokelele iirhafu kuloo mveliso inikiweyo.

## Inkxaso ye-VAT yaseYurophu

Njengoko kukhankanyiwe ngaphambili, sine-add-on ekhoyo kumthengi ose-EU oneemfuno ezongezelelweyo ngenxa yemimiselo ye-VAT yaseYurophu.

Izixhobo zethu ze-VAT zinceda kwizinto ezimbalwa ezibalulekileyo:

  * Ukulayishwa okulula kwamaxabiso e-VAT e-EU;

  * Ukuqokelelwa nokuqinisekiswa kweNombolo ye-VAT - kunye nokuhlawulisa ngokubuyisela umva kumaqumrhu angahlawuliswa VAT (njengeenkampani ezineenombolo ze-VAT ezisemthethweni);

Ukuze ufake loo add-on, yiya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings** uze ucofe ikhonkco lebar esecaleni elithi **Check our Add-ons**.

![Ibar esecaleni yephepha leesethingi enekhonkco le-Check our Add-ons](/img/config/settings-taxes-addons-link.png)

Uya kuthunyelwa kwiphepha lethu lee-add-ons. Apho, ungakhangela i-**Ultimate Multisite VAT add-on** uze uyifake.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: VAT add-on tile on the Add-ons page -->

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: VAT add-on Install Now dialog -->

Emva koko, yiya ku-**Network Admin > Plugins** uze wenze loo add-on isebenze kuyo yonke inethiwekhi.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Network Activate action for the VAT add-on on the Plugins page -->

Ukuba ubuyela ku-**Tax Settings tab** , uya kubona iinketho ezintsha ezikhoyo. Tshintsha inketho ethi **Enable VAT Support** ukuze wenze izixhobo ezintsha ze-VAT zisebenze. Ungalibali **ukugcina** iisethingi zakho!

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Enable VAT Support toggle in the Tax settings after add-on activation -->

### Ukutsala Amaxabiso Erhafu ye-VAT

Esinye sezixhobo ezongezwa ludibaniso lwethu kukukwazi ukulayisha amaxabiso erhafu kumazwe angamalungu e-EU. Oko kunokwenziwa ngokundwendwela iphepha lomhleli wamaxabiso erhafu emva kokwenza inkxaso ye-EU VAT isebenze.

Emazantsi ephepha, uya kubona iinketho zokutsala i-VAT. Ukukhetha uhlobo lwexabiso nokucofa iqhosha elithi **Update EU VAT Rates** kuya kutsala kuze kuzalise ngokuzenzekelayo itheyibhile ngamaxabiso erhafu elizwe ngalinye elililungu le-EU. Emva koko, kufuneka uyigcine kuphela.

![Iqhosha le-Update EU VAT Rates emazantsi omhleli wamaxabiso erhafu](/img/config/tax-rates-vat-pull.png)

Ungawahlela kwakhona amaxabiso emva kokuwatsala. Ukuze wenze oko, hlela nje umgca wetheyibhile owudingayo uze ucofe ukugcina amaxabiso amatsha.

### Ukuqinisekiswa kwe-VAT

Xa inkxaso ye-VAT yenziwe yasebenza, Ultimate Multisite iya kongeza indawo eyongezelelweyo kwifomu ye-checkout, ngaphantsi kwendawo yedilesi yokuhlawula. Le ndawo iya kubonakala kuphela kubathengi abakwi-EU.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: VAT Number field on the frontend checkout form below the billing address -->

Ultimate Multisite iya kuthi emva koko iqinisekise iNombolo ye-VAT, kwaye ukuba ibuya njengenyanisekileyo, indlela ye-reverse charge iyasetyenziswa kwaye izinga lerhafu lisetwa ku-0% kuloo odolo.
