# Indlela yokujoyina uluntu lwe-Ultimate Multisite {#how-to-join-ultimate-multisite-community}

Uluntu lwe-Ultimate Multisite yindawo apho unokunxibelelana nabanye abasebenzisi be-Ultimate Multisite uze wabelane ngeengcebiso, amava nokunye. Ukufikelela kuluntu lwe-Ultimate Multisite yiya ku-https://community.ultimatemultisite.com/home.

Kwiphepha lokwamkela yiya kwikhonkco elithi “**ukudala i-account yakho apha** ”.

<!-- Umfanekiso-skrini awufumaneki: Iphepha lokwamkela loluntu elinekhonkco lokudala i-account yakho -->

Uya kuthunyelwa kwiphepha lethu lokubhalisa. Ngoku kufuneka ufake igama lomsebenzisi, i-email yakho kunye nephasiwedi. Yenza i-Captcha uze ucofe iqhosha elithi **Bhalisa**.

<!-- Umfanekiso-skrini awufumaneki: Iphepha lokubhalisa loluntu elinemihlaba yegama lomsebenzisi, i-email, iphasiwedi kunye ne-Captcha -->

Emva kokubhalisa lixesha lokudala iprofayile yakho. Kweli phepha ungafaka ifoto yakho, ufake igama lakho elipheleleyo, ubhale i-bio emfutshane njalo njalo. Kwakhona, yongeza amakhonkco akho e-social media uze usete iimvume ze-account yakho.

<!-- Umfanekiso-skrini awufumaneki: Iphepha lokudala iprofayile yoluntu elinokulayishwa kwefoto kunye nemihlaba ye-bio -->

Ngoku ukwijelo elithi “Iintshayelelo”, apho ungazazisa khona uze wazi kancinci ngamanye amalungu. Ungadala iposti yakho ngokucofa iqhosha elithi **Iposti Entsha**.

<!-- Umfanekiso-skrini awufumaneki: Ijelo le-Iintshayelelo loluntu elineqhosha le-Iposti Entsha -->

Amajelo akwicandelo le-Lounge kunye nejelo elithi “Dev Logs” avulelekile kuye wonke umntu. Nangona kunjalo uya kudinga imvume ekhethekileyo ukuze ufikelele kwamanye amajelo kuluntu.

<!-- Umfanekiso-skrini awufumaneki: Amajelo oluntu abonisa icandelo le-Lounge kunye nejelo le-Dev Logs -->

Olu fikelelo luya kunikwa xa usinike isitshixo sakho selayisensi ye-Ultimate Multisite esisebenzayo. Ukwenza oko cofa nakweliphi na ikhonkco lejelo labucala uze ucofe iqhosha elithi **Vula**.

<!-- Umfanekiso-skrini awufumaneki: Ijelo labucala loluntu elineqhosha le-Vula -->

Uya kuthunyelwa kwiphepha lethu lokuvula ufikelelo. Faka isitshixo sakho selayisensi ye-Ultimate Multisite uze ucofe iqhosha elithi Vula Ufikelelo:

<!-- Umfanekiso-skrini awufumaneki: Iphepha lokuvula ufikelelo loluntu elinendawo yokufaka isitshixo selayisensi -->

Ilayisensi yakho iya kuqinisekiswa kwaye uya kukwazi ukufikelela kumajelo abucala. Le nkqubo ingathatha ukuya kwiiyure ezingama-24, ngoko cofa ikhonkco ukuze ubuyele kuluntu uze wonwabele ezinye iindawo ngelixa siqhuba isicelo sakho.
