# Ukudala iiKhowudi zeZaphulelo (v2)

_**INQAKU ELIBALULEKILEYO: Eli nqaku libhekisa kwi-Ultimate Multisite inguqulelo 2.x.**_

Nge-Ultimate Multisite ungadala iikhowudi zezaphulelo ukuze unike abathengi bakho izaphulelo kwizirhumo zabo. Kwaye ukuzidala kulula!

## Ukudala nokuHlela iiKhowudi zeZaphulelo {#creating-and-editing-discount-codes}

Ukudala okanye ukuhlela ikhowudi yesaphulelo, yiya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Iikhowudi zeZaphulelo**.

![Uluhlu lweekhowudi zezaphulelo — imeko engenanto ngaphambi kokuba kubekho naziphi na iikhowudi](/img/config/discount-codes-empty.png)

Apho uya kuba noluhlu lweekhowudi zezaphulelo osele uzidalile.

Ungacofa ku-**Yongeza iSaphulelo** **Ikhowudi** ukuze udale ikhuphoni elitsha okanye ungahlela ezo unazo ngokuhambisa isalathisi phezu kwazo uze ucofe **Hlela**.

![Uluhlu lweekhowudi zezaphulelo olunemisebenzi evela xa kusondelwa ngaphezulu, lubonisa amakhonkco okuHlela nokuCima](/img/config/discount-codes-list-hover.png)

![Iqhosha lokongeza iKhowudi yeSaphulelo kwisihloko sephepha](/img/config/discount-codes-add-button.png)

Uya kuthunyelwa kwiphepha apho uza kudala okanye uhlele ikhowudi yekhuphoni yakho. Kulo mzekelo siza kudala entsha.

![Iphepha lokuhlela ikhowudi yesaphulelo elibonisa onke amacandelo](/img/config/discount-code-edit.png)

Masijonge izicwangciso ezifumanekayo apha:

**Faka iKhowudi yeSaphulelo:** Eli ligama nje lekhowudi yakho yesaphulelo. Le asiyiyo ikhowudi abathengi bakho abaya kuyidinga ukuyisebenzisa kwifom yokuhlawula.

**Inkcazelo:** Apha, ungachaza ngokufutshane ukuba le khuphoni yeyantoni.

![Iindawo zegama nekcazelo yekhowudi yesaphulelo phezulu kwiphepha lokuhlela](/img/config/discount-code-description.png)

Ungayijonga kwakhona ikhowudi yesaphulelo njengesebenzayo okanye engasebenziyo:

![Imeko esebenzayo yekhowudi yesaphulelo](/img/config/discount-code-active.png)

**Ikhowudi yekhuphoni:** Apha kulapho uchaza khona ikhowudi abathengi bakho abaya kuyidinga ukuyifaka ngexesha lokuhlawula.

![Indawo yekhowudi yekhuphoni apho abathengi bafaka ikhowudi xa behlawula](/img/config/discount-code-coupon-field.png)

**Isaphulelo:** Apha, ungaseta nokuba yi-**pesenti** okanye **isixa esiqingqiweyo** semali sekhowudi yakho yesaphulelo.

![Isicwangciso sesixa sesaphulelo esinepesenti okanye uluhlu lokukhetha isixa esiqingqiweyo](/img/config/discount-code-amount.png)

**Sebenzisa kuhlaziyo:** Ukuba olu khetho lucinyiwe, le khowudi yesaphulelo iya kusetyenziswa kuphela **kwintlawulo yokuqala**. Zonke ezinye iintlawulo aziyi kuba nasaphulelo. Ukuba olu khetho luvuliwe, ikhowudi yesaphulelo iya kusebenza kuzo zonke iintlawulo zexesha elizayo.

**Isaphulelo sentlawulo yokumisela:** Ukuba olu khetho lucinyiwe, ikhowudi yekhuphoni **ayiyi kunika nasiphi na isaphulelo kwintlawulo yokumisela** yeodolo. Ukuba olu khetho luvuliwe, ungaseta isaphulelo (ipesenti okanye isixa esiqingqiweyo) esiza kusetyenziswa yile khowudi yekhuphoni kwintlawulo yokumisela yezicwangciso zakho.

![Iinketho zokuvula nokuvala ukusetyenziswa kuhlaziyo nesaphulelo sentlawulo yokumisela](/img/config/discount-code-renewals.png)

**Iyasebenza:** Yenza le khowudi yekhuphoni isebenze okanye ingasebenzi ngesandla.

![Iswitshi yokusebenza ukwenza ikhowudi yesaphulelo isebenze okanye ingasebenzi ngesandla](/img/config/discount-code-active.png)

Phantsi kwe-**Iinketho eziPhambili**, sinezi zicwangciso zilandelayo:

![Iinketho eziphambili zekhowudi yesaphulelo](/img/config/discount-code-advanced.png)

**Nciphisa ukusetyenziswa:**

  * **Ukusetyenziswa:** Apha, ungabona ukuba mangaphi amaxesha ikhowudi yesaphulelo esetyenzisiweyo.

  * **Elona nani liphezulu lokusetyenziswa:** Oku kuya kunciphisa inani lamaxesha abasebenzisi abanokusebenzisa ngawo le khowudi yesaphulelo. Umzekelo, ukuba ubeka u-10 apha, ikhuphoni ingasetyenziswa kuphela amaxesha ali-10. Emva kwalo mda, ikhowudi yekhuphoni ayinakuphinda isetyenziswe.

![Isicwangciso sokunciphisa ukusetyenziswa esinenani langoku lokusetyenziswa nendawo yelona nani liphezulu lokusetyenziswa](/img/config/discount-code-limit-uses.png)

**Imihla yokuqalisa & yokuphelelwa:** Apha uya kuba nokhetho lokongeza umhla wokuqalisa kunye/okanye umhla wokuphelelwa kwikhuphoni yakho.

![Iindawo zomhla wokuqalisa nowokuphelelwa zokucwangcisa ikhowudi yesaphulelo](/img/config/discount-code-dates.png)

**Nciphisa iimveliso:** Ukuba uvula **Khetha iimveliso**, zonke iimveliso zakho ziya kuboniswa kuwe. Uya kuba nokhetho lokukhetha ngesandla (ngokuvula okanye ngokuvala) ukuba yeyiphi imveliso enokwamkela le khowudi yekhuphoni. Iimveliso ezicinyiweyo apha aziyi kubonisa naluphi na utshintsho ukuba abathengi bakho bazama ukusebenzisa le khowudi yekhuphoni kuzo.

![Icandelo lokunciphisa iimveliso elineeswitshi zokuvula nokuvala kwimveliso nganye](/img/config/discount-code-limit-products.png)

Emva kokuseta zonke ezi nketho, cofa ku-**Gcina iKhowudi yeSaphulelo** ukuze ugcine ikhuphoni yakho, kwaye kugqityiwe!

![Iqhosha lokuGcina iKhowudi yeSaphulelo emazantsi ephepha lokuhlela](/img/config/discount-code-save.png)

Ikhuphoni ngoku ikuluhlu lwakho kwaye, ukusuka apho, ungacofa ukuze **uyihlele okanye uyicime**.

![Umqolo wekhowudi yesaphulelo kuluhlu onezenzo zokuHlela nokuCima ezivela xa kusondelwa ngaphezulu](/img/config/discount-codes-list-hover.png)

### Ukusebenzisa iiParamitha ze-URL: {#using-url-parameters}

Ukuba ufuna ukwenza iitheyibhile zakho zamaxabiso zihambelane neemfuno zakho okanye wakhe iphepha elihle lekhowudi yekhuphoni lewebhusayithi yakho kwaye ufuna ukusebenzisa ikhowudi yesaphulelo kwifom yakho yokuhlawula ngokuzenzekelayo, ungakwenza oku ngeeparamitha ze-URL.

Okokuqala, kufuneka ufumane ikhonkco elinokwabelwana ngalo lesicwangciso sakho. Ukwenza oku, yiya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Iimveliso** uze ukhethe isicwangciso.

Cofa kwiqhosha elithi **Cofa ukuze ukope iKhonkco eliNokwabelwana ngalo**. Oku kuya kukunika ikhonkco elinokwabelwana ngalo lesi sicwangciso sikhethekileyo. Kwimeko yethu, ikhonkco elinokwabelwana ngalo esalinikiweyo laliyi-[_**mynetworkdomain.com/register/premium/**_](http://mynetworkdomain.com/register/premium/)_._

![Iphepha lemveliso elineqhosha lekhonkco elinokwabelwana ngalo](/img/config/products-list.png)

Ukusebenzisa ikhowudi yakho yesaphulelo kwesi sicwangciso sikhethekileyo, yongeza nje iparamitha **?discount_code=XXX** kwi-URL. Apho **XXX** iyikhowudi yekhuphoni.

Kumzekelo wethu apha, siza kusebenzisa ikhowudi yekhuphoni **50OFF** kule mveliso ikhethekileyo.

I-URL yesi sicwangciso sikhethekileyo, kunye nekhowudi yesaphulelo 50OFF esetyenzisiweyo, iya kubonakala ngolu hlobo: [_**mynetworkdomain.com/register/premium/**_](http://mynetworkdomain.com/register/premium/) _**?discount_code=50OFF**_.
