# Iifomu zokugqibezela ukuthenga

Iifomu zokugqibezela ukuthenga ziyindlela elula neguquguqukayo yokuzama iindlela ezahlukeneyo xa uzama ukuguqula abathengi abatsha.

Ultimate Multisite 2.0 inikezela ngomhleli wefomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga okuvumela ukuba wenze iifomu ezininzi kangangoko ufuna, ngeefield ezahlukeneyo, iimveliso ezinikezelwayo, njl.

Ukufikelela kweli nqaku, yiya kwimenyu yeeFomu zokugqibezela ukuthenga, kwicala lasekhohlo.

![Uluhlu lweeFomu zokugqibezela ukuthenga](/img/config/checkout-forms-list.png)

Kweli phepha, ungabona zonke iifomu zokugqibezela ukuthenga onazo.

Itheyibhile yoluhlu iquka ikholamu ye-**Isimo** ukuze uqinisekise ukuba ifomu nganye iyafumaneka ngoku kubathengi:

| Isimo | Intsingiselo |
|---|---|
| **Iyasebenza** | Ifomu ingasetyenziswa naphi na apho i-shortcode yayo okanye iphepha lobhaliso lipapashwe khona. |
| **Ayisebenzi** | Ifomu igciniwe kodwa ikhubazekile. Abathengi abanakugqibezela ukuthenga ngayo ude uyivule kwakhona. |

Sebenzisa ikholamu yesimo ngaphambi kokuhlela ukuhamba kobhaliso lukawonke-wonke, ngakumbi xa ugcina iifomu zokugqibezela ukuthenga eziseyidrafti okanye zexesha elithile ecaleni kweefomu zakho eziphilayo.

Ukuba ufuna ukwenza entsha, cofa nje ku-Add Checkout Form phezulu ephepheni.

Ungakhetha enye kwezi ndlela zintathu njengendawo yakho yokuqalisa: inyathelo elinye, amanyathelo amaninzi okanye engenanto. Emva koko, cofa ukuze uye kuMhleli.

Xa ukhetha **inyathelo elinye** okanye **amanyathelo amaninzi** njengendawo yakho yokuqalisa, itemplate yefomu ngoku iquka ifield ye-**Ukhetho lweTemplate** ngokungagqibekanga. Le field ivumela abathengi bakho ukuba bakhethe itemplate yesayithi ngexesha lenkqubo yobhaliso. Ungayishiya apho ikhoyo, uyisuse, okanye uyibeke kwenye indawo njengayo nayiphi na enye field kumhleli.

![Umhleli weFomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga](/img/config/checkout-form-editor.png)

Kungenjalo, ungahlela okanye uphindaphinde iifomu osele unazo ngokucofa kwiinketho ezingezantsi kwegama layo. Apho, uya kufumana neenketho zokukopa i-shortcode yefomu okanye ukucima ifomu.

![Izenzo ezibonakala xa uhambisa impuku phezu kwefomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga](/img/config/checkout-form-hover-actions.png)

#### Isishwankathelo soMhleli weFomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga {#checkout-form-editor-overview}

Umhleli wefomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga unikezela ngojongano olubanzi lokwakha iifomu zakho zobhaliso. Nasi isishwankathelo sobume bomhleli:

![Isishwankathelo somhleli weFomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga](/img/config/checkout-form-editor-overview.png)

### Ukuhlela iFomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga {#editing-a-checkout-form}

Ungenza iifomu zokugqibezela ukuthenga ngeenjongo ezahlukeneyo. Kulo mzekelo siza kusebenza kwifomu yobhaliso.

Emva kokuhamba uye kumhleli wefomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga, nika ifomu yakho igama (eliza kusetyenziselwa isalathiso sangaphakathi kuphela) kunye ne-slug (esetyenziselwa ukwenza ii-shortcakes, umzekelo).

![Umhleli weFomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga](/img/config/checkout-form-editor.png)

Iifomu zenziwe ngamanyathelo neefield. Ungongeza inyathelo elitsha ngokucofa ku-Add New Checkout Step.

![Yongeza iNyathelo elitsha lokugqibezela ukuthenga](/img/config/checkout-form-add-step.png)

Kwithebhu yokuqala yefestile ye-modal, gcwalisa umxholo wenyathelo lefomu yakho. Yinike i-ID, igama kunye nenkcazelo. Ezi zinto zisetyenziswa kakhulu ngaphakathi.

![Ithebhu yomxholo wenyathelo lefomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga](/img/config/checkout-form-step-content.png)

Okulandelayo, seta ukubonakala kwenyathelo. Ungakhetha phakathi kuka-"Soloko ubonisa", "Bonisa kuphela kubasebenzisi abangene ngemvume" okanye "Bonisa kuphela kwiindwendwe".

![Ithebhu yokubonakala kwenyathelo lefomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga](/img/config/checkout-form-step-visibility.png)

Ekugqibeleni, qwalasela isimbo senyathelo. Ezi ziifield ozikhethayo.

![Ithebhu yesimbo senyathelo lefomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga](/img/config/checkout-form-step-style.png)

Ngoku, lixesha lokongeza iifield kwinyathelo lethu lokuqala. Cofa nje ku-Add New Field uze ukhethe uhlobo lwecandelo olufunayo.

![Inyathelo lefomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga elineefield](/img/config/checkout-form-step-with-fields.png)

Ifield nganye ineeparamitha ezahlukeneyo ekufuneka zigcwaliswe. Kolu ngeniso lokuqala, siza kukhetha ifield ethi "Igama lomsebenzisi".

![Imenyu eyehlayo yokukhetha uhlobo lwefield](/img/config/checkout-form-field-type-dropdown.png)

![Iisetingi zomxholo wefield yeGama lomsebenzisi](/img/config/checkout-form-username-content.png)

![Iisetingi zokubonakala kwefield yeGama lomsebenzisi](/img/config/checkout-form-username-visibility.png)

![Iisetingi zesimbo sefield yeGama lomsebenzisi](/img/config/checkout-form-username-style.png)

Ungongeza amanyathelo neefield ezininzi kangangoko uzifunayo. Ukubonisa iimveliso zakho ukuze abathengi bakho bakhethe enye, sebenzisa ifield yeTheyibhile yamaxabiso. Ukuba ufuna ukuvumela abathengi bakho bakhethe itemplate, yongeza ifield yoKhetho lweTemplate. Kwaye njalo njalo.

![Incoko yokongeza iField entsha](/img/config/checkout-form-add-field-dialog.png)

_**Qaphela:** igama lomsebenzisi, i-imeyile, ipassword, isihloko sesayithi, i-URL yesayithi, isishwankathelo se-odolo, intlawulo, kunye neqhosha lokuthumela ziifield ezinyanzelekileyo ukwenza ifomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga._

Ngelixa usebenza kwifomu yakho yokugqibezela ukuthenga, ungasoloko usebenzisa iqhosha lePreview ukubona indlela abathengi bakho abaza kuyibona ngayo ifomu. Ungatshintshatshintsha phakathi kokuyijonga njengomsebenzisi okhoyo okanye njengondwendwe

![Iqhosha lePreview leFomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga](/img/config/checkout-form-preview-button.png)

![I-modal yePreview yeFomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga](/img/config/checkout-form-preview-modal.png)

Ekugqibeleni, kwi-Advanced Options ungaqwalasela umyalezo wephepha elithi "Enkosi", wongeze ii-snippet zokulandelela ukuguqulwa, wongeze i-CSS yesiko kwifomu yakho yokugqibezela ukuthenga okanye uyithintele kumazwe athile.

![Iinketho eziPhambili](/img/config/checkout-form-advanced.png)

Ungayivula okanye uyikhubaze ngesandla ifomu yakho yokugqibezela ukuthenga ngokutshintsha le nketho kwikholamu yasekunene, okanye ucime ifomu ngokusisigxina.

![Ukutshintsha kokusebenza](/img/config/checkout-form-active.png)

Ukucima ifomu, cofa inketho yokucima kwiizenzo zefomu:

![Inketho yokucima ifomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga](/img/config/checkout-form-delete.png)

Ungalibali ukugcina ifomu yakho yokugqibezela ukuthenga!

![Iqhosha lokugcina](/img/config/checkout-form-save.png)

Ukufumana i-shortcode yefomu yakho cofa ku-Generate Shortcode uze ukope isiphumo esiboniswe kwifestile ye-modal.

![Iqhosha lokugcina eline-shortcode](/img/config/checkout-form-save.png)

### Ifield yeTheyibhile yamaxabiso {#the-pricing-table-field}

Ifield ye-**Theyibhile yamaxabiso** ibonisa iimveliso zakho kwifomu yokugqibezela ukuthenga ukuze abathengi bakhethe iplan. Xa uhlela le field, ungaqwalasela iinketho ezininzi:

![Iisetingi zefield yeTheyibhile yamaxabiso](/img/config/pricing-table-field-settings.png)

Nantsi indlela itheyibhile yamaxabiso ebonakala ngayo kwifomu yobhaliso ye-frontend:

![Itheyibhile yamaxabiso yokuhlawula ye-frontend](/img/config/frontend-checkout-pricing-table.png)

  * **Iimveliso**: Khetha ukuba zeziphi iimveliso eziza kuboniswa kunye nolandelelwano ezivela ngalo.
  * **Nyanzelisa Amaxesha Ahlukeneyo**: Xa yenziwe yasebenza, zonke iimveliso ziyaboniswa kungakhathaliseki ukuba zinenguqulelo yexabiso ehambelana nexesha lokuhlawula elikhethiweyo ngoku. Xa ingasebenzi (okungagqibekanga), iimveliso ezingenayo inguqulelo yexesha elikhethiweyo ziyafihlwa.
    ![Ukhetho lokunyanzelisa amaxesha kwitheyibhile yamaxabiso](/img/config/pricing-table-force-durations.png)
  * **Fihla xa Ikhethwe Kwangaphambili**: Ifihla itheyibhile yamaxabiso xa isicwangciso sele sikhethwe nge-URL (umz. `/register/premium`).
  * **Itemplate yeTheyibhile Yamaxabiso**: Khetha itemplate ebonakalayo yetheyibhile yamaxabiso (Uluhlu Olulula, Legacy, njl.).

Ukuba wongeza imveliso kwiTheyibhile Yamaxabiso ngaphambi kokuba ifomu ibandakanye amasimi afunekayo ukugqibezela ukuhamba kokuhlawula kwaloo mveliso, umhleli ngoku ubonisa isilumkiso. Sebenzisa isilumkiso ukongeza intsimi efunekayo engekhoyo ngaphambi kokupapasha okanye ukugcina utshintsho kwifomu yobhaliso ephilayo.

### Ukongeza Isitshintshi Sokukhetha Ixesha {#adding-a-period-selection-toggle}

Ukuba uqwalasele [Iinguqulelo Zamaxabiso](creating-your-first-subscription-product#price-variations) kwiimveliso zakho (umz. amaxabiso enyanga nonyaka), ungongeza intsimi ye-**Ukukhetha Ixesha** kwifomu yakho yokuhlawula. Le ntsimi ibonisa isitshintshi esivumela abathengi ukuba batshintshe phakathi kwamaxesha okuhlawuliswa, kwaye itheyibhile yamaxabiso ihlaziyeka ngokuguquguqukayo ngexesha lokwenyani.

#### Inyathelo 1: Seta Iinguqulelo Zamaxabiso kwiimveliso zakho {#step-1-set-up-price-variations-on-your-products}

Ngaphambi kokongeza intsimi ye-Ukukhetha Ixesha, qinisekisa ukuba iimveliso zakho zineenguqulelo zamaxabiso eziqwalaselweyo. Yiya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Iimveliso**, hlela imveliso, uze uye kwithebhu ye-**Iinguqulelo Zamaxabiso** ukongeza amaxesha okuhlawuliswa angamanye (umz. Yonyaka ngexabiso elithotyiweyo).

![Ithebhu yeeNguqulelo Zamaxabiso kwimveliso](/img/config/product-price-variations-tab.png)

#### Inyathelo 2: Yongeza Intsimi ye-Ukukhetha Ixesha kwiFomu yakho yokuHlawula {#step-2-add-the-period-selection-field-to-your-checkout-form}

1. Yiya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Iifomu zokuHlawula** uze uhlele ifomu yakho yokuhlawula.

2. Skrolela ezantsi kwinyathelo eliqulethe intsimi yakho ye-**Theyibhile Yamaxabiso** uze ucofe **Yongeza Intsimi entsha**.

3. Kwidayalog yokukhetha uhlobo lwentsimi, cofa **Khetha Ixesha**.

![Idayalog ka-Yongeza Intsimi Entsha ebonisa u-Khetha Ixesha](/img/config/checkout-form-add-field-dialog.png)

4. Qwalasela iinketho zexesha. Inketho nganye ifuna:
   * **Ubude bexesha**: Inani (umz. `1`)
   * **Iyunithi yobude bexesha**: Uhlobo lwexesha (Iintsuku, Iiveki, Iinyanga, okanye Iminyaka)
   * **Ileyibhile**: Umbhalo abathengi abaza kuwubona (umz. "Ngenyanga", "Ngonyaka")

5. Cofa **+ Yongeza Ukhetho** ukongeza ezinye iinketho zexesha. Ezi nketho kufuneka zihambelane neenguqulelo zamaxabiso oziqwalasele kwiimveliso zakho.

![Iisetingi zentsimi ye-Ukukhetha Ixesha](/img/config/period-selection-field-settings.png)

6. Khetha **Itemplate yoMkhethi weXesha** (Clean yeyona ingagqibekanga, enika umkhethi olula onesitayile olungele i-CSS yesiko).

7. Cofa **Gcina Intsimi**.

#### Inyathelo 3: Beka Intsimi Ngaphezulu kweTheyibhile Yamaxabiso {#step-3-position-the-field-above-the-pricing-table}

Ukuze abasebenzisi bafumane amava angcono, qinisekisa ukuba intsimi ye-Ukukhetha Ixesha ivela **ngaphambi** kwentsimi yeTheyibhile Yamaxabiso kwinyathelo lakho lokuhlawula. Ungatsala amasimi ukuze uwacwangcise ngokutsha kumhleli wefomu yokuhlawula. Ngale ndlela, abathengi kuqala bakhetha ixesha lokuhlawuliswa baze emva koko babone amaxabiso elo xesha.

![Umhleli wefomu yokuhlawula obonisa ulandelelwano lwamasimi](/img/config/checkout-form-editor-with-fields.png)

#### Indlela Esebenza Ngayo kwi-Frontend {#how-it-works-on-the-frontend}

Nje ukuba kuqwalaselwe, abathengi abatyelela iphepha lakho lobhaliso baza kubona umkhethi wexesha ngaphezulu kwetheyibhile yamaxabiso. Xa becofa ixesha lokuhlawuliswa elahlukileyo:

  * Itheyibhile yamaxabiso ihlaziyeka ngoko nangoko ukubonisa amaxabiso exesha elikhethiweyo (akufuneki kulayishwe iphepha kwakhona).
  * Ukuba **Nyanzelisa Amaxesha Ahlukeneyo** ingasebenzi kwintsimi yeTheyibhile Yamaxabiso, iimveliso ezingenayo inguqulelo yexabiso yexesha elikhethiweyo ziya kufihlwa.
  * Ukuba **Nyanzelisa Amaxesha Ahlukeneyo** yenziwe yasebenza, zonke iimveliso zihlala zibonakala nokuba azinanguqulelo yexesha elikhethiweyo (ziya kubonisa ixabiso lazo elingagqibekanga).

#### Ukukhetha Kwangaphambili Ixesha lokuHlawuliswa nge-URL {#pre-selecting-a-billing-period-via-url}

Ungakwazi kwakhona ukukhetha kwangaphambili imveliso kunye nexesha lokuhlawuliswa nge-URL. Ultimate Multisite ixhasa ezi patheni ze-URL:

  * `/register/premium` — Ikhetha kwangaphambili imveliso ethi "Premium" kuphela
  * `/register/premium/12` — Ikhetha kwangaphambili imveliso kunye nobude beenyanga ezili-12
  * `/register/premium/1/year` — Ikhetha kwangaphambili imveliso enobude bonyaka om-1

### Intsimi yoKhetho lweTemplate {#the-template-selection-field}

Intsimi ye-**Ukukhetha iTemplate** ivumela abathengi ukuba bakhethe itemplate yesayithi ngexesha lokuhlawula. Ngoku ibandakanyiwe ngokungagqibekanga kwiitemplate zeefomu zokuhlawula ze-**nyathelo elinye** kunye **namanyathelo amaninzi** ezongezwe kwi-Ultimate Multisite v2.6.1.

#### Ukongeza intsimi ngesandla {#adding-the-field-manually}

Ukuba usebenza ngefomu eyadalwa ngaphambi kwe-v2.6.1, okanye uqale kwitemplate engenanto:

1. Yiya ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Iifomu zokuHlawula** uze uhlele ifomu yakho yokuhlawula.
2. Kwinyathelo apho kuqokelelwa iinkcukacha zesayithi, cofa **Yongeza Intsimi entsha**.
3. Khetha **Ukukhetha iTemplate** kwidayalog yohlobo lwentsimi.
4. Qwalasela intsimi:
   - **Ileyibhile** — Isihloko abathengi abasibona ngaphezulu kwegridi yeetemplate (umz. "Khetha itemplate yesayithi").
   - **Iyafuneka** — Ukuba abathengi kufuneka bakhethe itemplate ngaphambi kokuqhubeka.

#### Indlela esebenza ngayo {#how-it-works}

Xa umthengi ekhetha itemplate ngexesha lokuhlawula, Ultimate Multisite iyayisebenzisa xa isenza isayithi yakhe entsha. Iitemplate eziboniswayo zivela kuluhlu lwakho lwe-**Site Templates** (**Ultimate Multisite > Site Templates**). Kuphela ziitemplate eziphawulwe njengezifumanekayo kubathengi ezivela apha.

### Iidomeyini ezisisiseko zeefomu zokuhlawula {#checkout-form-base-domains}

Ultimate Multisite v2.13.0 iphatha iidomeyini eziqwalaselwe kwiintsimi ze-**URL yeSayithi** zefomu yokuhlawula njengeedomeyini ezisisiseko zenethiwekhi. Sebenzisa iisetingi zedomeyini ezifumanekayo zentsimi xa ufuna abathengi benze iisayithi phantsi kwedomeyini enye okanye ezingaphezulu zobhaliso ekwabelwana ngazo, ezifana no-`example.com` kunye no-`sites.example.com`.

Iidomeyini ezisisiseko ze-checkout-form ekwabelwana ngazo aziphathwa njengemephu yeedomeyini zesiko zesayithi nganye. Xa umthengi esenza isayithi yesubdirectory kwenye yezo ziseko, Ultimate Multisite ayenzi rekhodi ye-mapped-domain eya kwenza ukuba i-host ekwabelwana ngayo ibe yeyaloo sayithi enye kuphela. I-host ekwabelwana ngayo ihlala ifumaneka kwiisayithi ezingoodade ezisebenzisa isiseko esifanayo sefom ye-checkout.

Gcina iidomeyini zesiko zee-host ezimephiweyo zomthengi ngamnye, ezifana ne-`customer-example.com`. Gcina iidomeyini ezisisiseko ze-checkout-form zee-host zobhaliso ekwabelwana ngazo ezinokusetyenziswa ziisayithi ezininzi.

#### Ukususa intsimi {#removing-the-field}

Ukuba awuboneleli ngeethempleyithi zesayithi, susa intsimi ye-Template Selection kwifom yakho. Abathengi baya kufumana nayiphi na ithempleyithi engagqibekanga eqwalaselwe phantsi kwe-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Site Templates**.
