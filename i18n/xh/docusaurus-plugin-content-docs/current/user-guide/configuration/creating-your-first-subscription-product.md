# Ukwenza Imveliso yakho yokuqala yoMrhumo (v2) {#creating-your-first-subscription-product-v2}

_**INQAKU ELIBALULEKILEYO: Eli nqaku lelama abasebenzisi be-Ultimate Multisite inguqulelo 2.x kuphela. Ukuba usebenzisa inguqulelo 1.x,**_ **_**jonga eli nqaku**_**.

Ukuqala ukuqhuba inethiwekhi yakho uze uqalise ukuthengisa iinkonzo zakho kubasebenzisi abanokubakho, kufuneka ube neendlela ezahlukeneyo zorhumo. Uzenza njani ezi mveliso? Zeziphi iintlobo zeemveliso onokuzinika? Kweli nqaku, siza kugubungela yonke into ekufuneka uyazi ngeemveliso.

## Uhlobo Lwemveliso {#product-type}

Nge-Ultimate Multisite unokunika abaxhasi bakho iindidi ezimbini zeemveliso: **izicwangciso** kunye **nezongezo** **(Order Bump)**. Izongezo zingahlulwa zibe ziintlobo ezimbini:**iipakethe** kunye **neenkonzo**. Siza kubona umahluko wazo neempawu zazo ezithile ngokulandelayo.

  * **Izicwangciso** : imveliso esisiseko ye-Ultimate Multisite. Umxhasi wakho unokuba nobulungu kuphela ukuba buqhagamshelwe kwisicwangciso. Isicwangciso sinika abaxhasi bakho isiza esinye okanye ezingaphezulu (kuxhomekeke kuqwalaselo lwesicwangciso sakho) kunye nemida oyibekayo kwiphepha lakho lokuhlela imveliso.

  * **Iipakethe** : izongezo ezichaphazela ngokuthe ngqo ukusebenza kwezicwangciso ze-Ultimate Multisite. Zitshintsha imida okanye zongeza izixhobo ezitsha, izandiso okanye imixholo kwisicwangciso sokuqala esithengwe ngumxhasi wakho. Umzekelo, isicwangciso esisisiseko sinokuvumela utyelelo olu-1,000 ngenyanga kwaye unokwenza kufumaneke iphakethe elandisa eli nani libe li-10,000.

  * **Iinkonzo:** izongezo ezingatshintshi ukusebenza kwe-Ultimate Multisite. Zizabelo oza kuzenzela umxhasi wakho ukongeza kwisicwangciso asithengileyo. Umzekelo, umthengi wakho angathenga isicwangciso esivumela isiza esinye aze ahlawule nenkonzo eyongezelelweyo eza kwenza uyilo lwesi siza.

## Ukulawula Iimveliso {#managing-products}

Kwabaninzi ithebhu ethi **Iimveliso** kwi-Ultimate Multisite **(Ultimate Multisite > Iimveliso)** inokulinganiswa nezicwangciso kwimeko yesiqhelo yokubamba iisayithi.

Ngaphakathi kwe-Ultimate Multisite ithebhu ethi Iimveliso ichaza isakhiwo kunye nemida esebenzayo kwimveliso okanye inkonzo ethile. Ezo zakhiwo ziquka inkcazo yemveliso okanye yenkonzo, ixabiso, iirhafu, kunye neemvume.

Eli candelo liza kukukhokela ekuqondeni eli litye lembombo libalulekileyo le-Ultimate Multisite.

![Iphepha loluhlu lweemveliso](/img/config/products-list.png)

## Ukongeza Iimveliso {#adding-products}

Nokuba sisicwangciso, iphakethe, okanye inkonzo, indawo yokuqala yokuchaza into entsha ikwi-**Ultimate Multisite > Iimveliso > Yongeza Imveliso**.

![Iqhosha lokongeza imveliso](/img/config/product-add-button.png)

Ujongano luqulethe amacandelo amabini aphambili. Ngasekhohlo kukho iithebhu ezininzi ezinceda ekuchazeni imveliso, kwaye ngasekunene kukho amacandelo ambalwa okuchaza ixabiso lesiseko lemveliso, imeko yayo yokusebenza, kunye nomfanekiso wemveliso.

![Ushwankathelo lwephepha lokuhlela imveliso](/img/config/product-edit-full.png)

### Inkcazo {#description}

Ulwazi lwesiseko lwemveliso lunokuchazwa ngokubonelela ngegama lemveliso kunye nenkcazo. Ezi zazisi ziboniswa naphi na apho ulwazi lwemveliso lufunekayo, njengokukhethwa kwesicwangciso namaxabiso, ii-invoice, uphuculo, kunye nezinye ezinjalo.

![Icandelo lenkcazo yemveliso](/img/config/product-description.png)

### Uhlobo Lwamaxabiso {#pricing-type}

Kwicala lasekunene lojongano, amaxabiso esiseko anokuchazwa.

![Icandelo lamaxabiso nokugcina](/img/config/product-pricing-save.png)

Ultimate Multisite ixhasa iintlobo ezintathu ezahlukeneyo zamaxabiso. Ukhetho **oluhlawulwayo** lucela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuba anike ulwazi malunga nexabiso lemveliso kunye nokuphindaphinda kokuhlawulisa.

### Amaxabiso {#pricing}

Icandelo lexabiso lichaza ixabiso lesiseko lemveliso kunye nesithuba sokuhlawulisa.

![Icandelo lamaxabiso nokugcina](/img/config/product-pricing-save.png)

ngoko umzekelo wexabiso le-$29.99 kunye noseto lwenyanga e-1 uya kuhlawulisa i-$29.99 nyanga nganye. Ngokufanayo, ixabiso le-$89.97 kunye noseto lweenyanga ezi-3 liya kuhlawulisa elo xabiso qho ngekota.

### Imijikelo Yokuhlawulisa {#billing-cycles}

Icandelo lemijikelo yokuhlawulisa lichaza ukuphindaphinda kwesithuba sokuhlawulisa esikhankanywe ngasentla kwaye ngokubanzi liqondwa ngokwemvumelwano okanye imiqathango emiselweyo.

![Icandelo lamaxabiso nokugcina](/img/config/product-pricing-save.png)

Umzekelo, ixabiso lemveliso le-$29.99 elinesithuba senyanga e-1 kunye nemijikelo yokuhlawulisa eli-12 liya kuhlawulisa i-$29.99 ngenyanga ngemveliso kwiinyanga ezili-12 ezilandelayo. Ngamanye amazwi, olo seto luya kuseka ixesha lexabiso elimiselweyo le-$29.99 ngenyanga kangangeenyanga ezili-12, emva koko liyeke ukuhlawulisa.

### Ixesha Lovavanyo {#trial-period}

Ukuvula iswitshi yokunikezela ngovavanyo kuvumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuba achaze ixesha lovavanyo lemveliso.

![Icandelo lamaxabiso nokugcina](/img/config/product-pricing-save.png)

Ngexesha lovavanyo, abathengi bakhululekile ukusebenzisa imveliso kwaye abayi kuhlawuliswa de ixesha lovavanyo liphele.

### Umrhumo Wokuseta {#setup-fee}

Ungafaka nomrhumo wokuseta kwisicwangciso sakho.

![Icandelo lamaxabiso nokugcina](/img/config/product-pricing-save.png)

Oku kuthetha ukuba umxhasi wakho uya kuhlawula isixa esongezelelweyo kwintlawulo yokuqala (ukongeza kwixabiso lesicwangciso) esihambelana nomrhumo owuchazile kweli candelo.

### Iyasebenza {#active}

Iswitshi yokusebenza ichaza ngokusebenzayo ukuba imveliso iyafumaneka na kubathengi ukuze babhalise ngokutsha.

![Iswitshi yokusebenza](/img/config/product-active.png)

Ukuba kukho abathengi abakhoyo kwesi sicwangciso, ukuseta iswitshi kwimeko yayo evaliweyo kusenza ngokusebenzayo esi sicwangciso sigcinelwe abo sele besikuso, kusisusa kubhaliso lwexesha elizayo. **Abathengi abakhoyo kwisicwangciso baya kuqhubeka behlawuliswa** de batshintshelwe kwisicwangciso esitsha okanye basuswe kwisicwangciso.

### Umfanekiso Wemveliso {#product-image}

Iqhosha elithi **Layisha Umfanekiso** livumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuba asebenzise ilayibrari yemidiya ukukhetha okanye ukulayisha umfanekiso wemveliso.

![Icandelo lomfanekiso wemveliso](/img/config/product-image.png)

### Cima {#delete}

Iqhosha elithi **Cima Imveliso** licima imveliso kwisistim. Livela xa imveliso sele ipapashiwe.

![Icandelo lokucima imveliso](/img/config/product-delete.png)

Ngokungafaniyo nokunye ukucima, imveliso ayibekwa nakweyiphi imeko yenkunkuma. Ngoko ke xa sele icinyiwe, isenzo asinakubuyiselwa umva.

### Iinketho Zemveliso {#product-options}

Nje ukuba ulwazi lwemveliso olukwinqanaba lesiseko luchaziwe, iinketho zemveliso zinceda umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuba achaze ngakumbi iimpawu ezithile zemveliso.

#### Ngokubanzi {#general}

I-tab ethi **Ngokubanzi** ichaza iimpawu ngokubanzi zemveliso ezingasebenziyo nakweyiphi na enye i-tab ethile yemveliso.

![I-tab Ngokubanzi](/img/config/product-general-tab.png)

I-**product slug** ezicacisayo ngokwayo ichaza i-slug esetyenziswa ukuchonga imveliso kwii-URLs nakwezinye iindawo ze-Ultimate Multisite.

Ultimate Multisite ixhasa iintlobo ezininzi zeemveliso, ezizezi Isicwangciso, Iphakheji, kunye neNkonzo. Ii-tab ze-**Iinketho Zemveliso** zilungiswa ngokuzenzekelayo ngokohlobo lwemveliso oluchaziweyo.

I-**Indima Yomthengi** ichaza indima eyabelwa umthengi xa isayithi yenziwe. Ngokuqhelekileyo kubalawuli benethiwekhi abaninzi, oku kuya kuba sisigqibo esingagqibekanga se-Ultimate Multisite okanye Administrator. Indima engagqibekanga ye-Ultimate Multisite inokusetwa ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Login & Registration**.

![Iisetingi zendima yomthengi](/img/config/product-customer-role-settings.png)

#### Ukunyusa & Nokwehlisa {#up--downgrades}

Le tab ichaza iindlela zokunyusa nokwehlisa ezifumanekayo kumthengi ngaphakathi kwinqanaba lakhe elithile.

Ukuze uqonde le ngcamango cinga ngomzekelo apho ufakelo lwe-Ultimate Multisite olugxile kwindawo ethile lubonelela abathengi balo ngezisombululo zolawulo lokufunda. Ukufezekisa oku kuchazwa izicwangciso ezintathu (Basic, Plus, kunye nePremium) kwaye plugins ezithile ziyavulwa kwisicwangciso ngasinye (jonga kamva kweli candelo imiyalelo yokuvula plugins).

Ukuba ufakelo lwe-Ultimate Multisite lukwasebenzela iiwebhusayithi zeshishini okanye iiwebhusayithi ze-eCommerce, ezo zicwangciso zisenokufuna plugins ezahlukileyo ukuba zifakwe kwaye zivulwe.

Ngale ndlela, kuya kuba yinto engathandekiyo nengxaki ukuvumela abathengi be-eLearning ukuba batshintshele kwizicwangciso ze-eCommerce kuba ezi zicwangciso, amaxabiso, kunye nemida zisenokungangqinelani kakuhle nabo.

Ngoko ke ukuze kuthintelwe indlela yomthengi nokuthintela iziganeko, umlawuli wenethiwekhi angachaza iqela lesicwangciso kwaye ngaphakathi kwelo qela achaze izicwangciso umthengi anokutshintshela kuzo.

![I-tab Ukunyusa nokwehlisa](/img/config/product-upgrades.png)

Ukuchaza iqela lesicwangciso, chaza izicwangciso ezihambelanayo kuluhlu lwe-**iqela lesicwangciso**. **Ulandelelwano lwemveliso** lumisela indlela izicwangciso ezilandelelana ngayo kwaye ziboniswe ukusuka kwelisezantsi ukuya kweliphezulu.

Ultimate Multisite ikwaquka uphawu lwe-**order bump** apho iimveliso nezinkonzo ezongezelelekileyo ezifanelekileyo zinokongezwa kwizicwangciso. Ezi zinikezelwa kumthengi njengezinto ezongezelelekileyo ezinokongezwa kwizicwangciso ngexesha le-checkout okanye ngexesha lokunyusa.

#### Iinguqu Zamaxabiso {#price-variations}

Iinguqu zamaxabiso zivumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuba achaze amanqanaba amaxabiso angamanye ngokuxhomekeke kubude bexesha. Le setingi yenza kube nokwenzeka ukuba ixesha lokubhatala lenyanga, lekota, lonyaka, okanye naliphi na elinye linikezelwe kwimveliso efanayo. Umzekelo, unokuseta imveliso nge-$29.99/ngenyanga kunye nenketho yonyaka enesaphulelo nge-$249.99/ngonyaka.

![I-tab Iinguqu Zamaxabiso](/img/config/product-price-variations-tab.png)

Ukuseka iinguqu zamaxabiso, seta i-toggle ethi **Vula Iinguqu Zamaxabiso** ukuba isebenze uze ucofe iqhosha elithi **Yongeza Inguqu Entsha Yexabiso**.

![Iinguqu zamaxabiso emveliso](/img/config/product-price-variations.png)

Ukufaka inguqu, seta ubude bexesha, ixesha, kunye nexabiso lenguqu. Iinguqu ezongezelelekileyo zinokufakwa ngokuphinda ucofe iqhosha.

Umzekelo, ukuba ixabiso lemveliso yakho esisiseko yi-$29.99 ngenyanga, unokongeza:

  * **Iinyanga ezi-3** nge-$79.99 (isaphulelo esincinane ngaphezu kwenyanga nenyanga)
  * **Unyaka o-1** nge-$249.99 (isaphulelo esikhulu sokuzibophelela konyaka)

:::tip Ukubonisa i-Toggle Yexesha Lokubhatala kwi-Frontend

Iinguqu zamaxabiso zodwa azongezi i-toggle okanye iswitshi kwi-frontend checkout. Ukuvumela abathengi batshintshe phakathi kwamaxesha okubhatala (umz. Ngenyanga / Ngonyaka), kufuneka wongeze ifildi ye-**Ukukhetha Ixesha** kwifomu yakho ye-checkout. Jonga [Iifomu ze-Checkout: Ukongeza i-Toggle Yokukhetha Ixesha](checkout-forms#adding-a-period-selection-toggle) ukuze ufumane imiyalelo yenyathelo ngenyathelo.
:::

#### Iirhafu {#taxes}

I-tab ethi **Iirhafu** ihambelana neesetingi zerhafu ezichazwe ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Taxes** kwaye ngakumbi amanqanaba erhafu achaziweyo. Ukuvula iirhafu nokuchaza amanqanaba erhafu asebenzayo nceda ubone amaxwebhu ku-**Ultimate Multisite: Settings**

![I-tab Iirhafu](/img/config/product-taxes.png)

Kumzekelo ongaphambili, sichaze izinga lerhafu yendawo le-7.25% elisebenzayo kubathengi baseCalifornia (United States of America).

Nje ukuba izinga lerhafu lichaziwe ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Manage Tax Rates** liyakhethwa kwinqanaba lemveliso.

![I-tab Iirhafu](/img/config/product-taxes.png)

Ukubonisa ukuba imveliso yinto erhafiswayo, seta i-toggle ethi **Iyahlawuliswa Irhafu** ukuba isebenze uze ukhethe izinga lerhafu elisebenzayo kwi-dropdown yeCandelo Lerhafu.

#### Iitemplate Zesayithi {#site-templates}

Ngokwenene, iitemplate zesayithi ziwebhusayithi ze-WordPress ezipheleleyo ezikhutshelwa kwisayithi yomthengi ekuqaleni kobhaliso lwakhe.

![I-tab Iitemplate Zesayithi](/img/config/product-site-templates.png)

Umlawuli wenethiwekhi udala aze aqwalasele isayithi yetemplate njengesayithi ye-WordPress eqhelekileyo enee-themes, plugins, kunye nomxholo ovuliweyo noqwalaselweyo. Isayithi yetemplate ikhutshelwa kumthengi njengoko injalo.

Le tab ivumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuba achaze indlela iitemplate zesayithi eziza kuziphatha ngayo xa kukho ubhaliso olutsha. Ukuze usebenzise iitemplate zesayithi kwesi sicwangciso, seta i-toggle ethi **Vumela Iitemplate Zesayithi** kwimeko yayo esebenzayo.

Xa **Vumela Iitemplate Zesayithi** ikhubaziwe, abathengi abakwisicwangciso abakwazi ukukhetha iitemplate nokuba ifomu ye-checkout, ikhonkco ekwabelwana ngalo, okanye iparamitha ye-URL ibinokwenza iitemplate zifumaneke. Ultimate Multisite ngoku inyanzelisa lo mda ngothotho lokubuyela umva kwiindawo zokungena ezikhoyo: iisetingi zesicwangciso zijongwa kuqala, emva koko iisetingi zetemplate yefomu ye-checkout, emva koko iitemplate ezikhethwe kwangaphambili okanye ezibonelelwe nge-URL. Oku kugcina imida yesicwangciso ihambelana kwaye kuthintela iitemplate ekubonakaleni kwiimveliso ezingafanele ukuzinikezela.

Imowudi **yokukhetha itemplate yesayithi** ichaza indlela iitemplate zesayithi eziziphatha ngayo ngexesha lenkqubo yomrhumo.

Useto **O** **kuhlala kukho** lulandela amanyathelo kwifomu yokuhlawula. Ukuba umlawuli wenethiwekhi uchaze inyathelo lokukhetha itemplate kwinkqubo yokuhlawula kwaye inyathelo lichazwe ngeetemplate, olu seto luya kuthobela imiyalelo esekwe kwinyathelo lokuhlawula.

Ukuchaza **Y** **abela Itemplate Yesayithi** kunyanzelisa ukukhethwa kwetemplate echaziweyo. Ngenxa yoko, nawaphi na amanyathelo okukhetha itemplate kwinkqubo yokuhlawula ayasuswa.

Okokugqibela, **K** **hetha Iitemplate Zesayithi Ezikhoyo** kudlula iitemplate ezichazwe kwinyathelo lokuhlawula ngeetemplate ezikhethwe kolu seto. Itemplate ekhethwe kwangaphambili nayo ingachazwa ukunceda umthengi ekukhetheni.

Ekugqibeleni, ukuba umlawuli wenethiwekhi ufuna ukuba ukukhethwa kwetemplate kwenzeke kumanyathelo okuhlawula, useto luka-' _default_ ' luya kwanela. Kungenjalo, ukususa nokutshixa ukukhethwa kwetemplate nokudlulisela ukukhetha kuseto lwesicwangciso, iinketho ze-' _assign new template_ ' okanye ze-' _choose available site templates_ ' zinokunqweneleka.

#### Iisayithi {#sites}

Ithebhu ethi **Iisayithi** iyinxalenye yokusebenza kwemida kwe-Ultimate Multisite.

![Ithebhu yeesayithi](/img/config/product-sites.png)

Olu seto luchaza elona nani liphezulu leesayithi umthengi anokuzidala phantsi kobulungu bakhe.

Ukuvumela umda, seta iswitshi ye-**misa umda kwiisayithi** kwimo esebenzayo uze uchaze elona nani liphezulu leesayithi kwi-field ye-**isibonelelo sesayithi**.

#### Utyelelo {#visits}

Ithebhu ethi **Utyelelo** yenye inxalenye yenkqubo yemida ye-Ultimate Multisite. Olu seto luvumela ukubalwa kunye nokuthotywa okulandelayo kweendwendwe ezizodwa kwisayithi yomthengi.

![Ithebhu yotyelelo](/img/config/product-visits.png)

Ngokombono wentengiso, abalawuli benethiwekhi banokusebenzisa olu seto njengendlela yokukhuthaza abathengi ukuba banyuse isicwangciso sabo xa imida ifikelelwe. Olu seto lunokunceda umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukunciphisa nokuthintela itrafikhi egqithisileyo kwiisayithi ukuze kugcinwe izixhobo zenkqubo.

Ukuze usebenzise olu phawu, seta iswitshi ye-**misa umda kutyelelo oluzodwa** kwimo esebenzayo uze uchaze elona nani liphezulu leendwendwe ezizodwa kwi-field ye-**isabelo sotyelelo oluzodwa**.

Xa lo mda ufikelelwe, Ultimate Multisite iya kuyeka ukusebenzela isayithi yomthengi endaweni yokubonisa umyalezo obonisa ukuba imida igqithiwe.

#### Abasebenzisi {#users}

Imida ye-'Abasebenzisi' ye-Ultimate Multisite ivumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuba abeke imida kwinani labasebenzisi abanokudalwa baze babelwe iindima.

![Ithebhu yabasebenzisi](/img/config/product-users.png)

Ukuvumela uphawu lwemida, seta iswitshi ye-**misa umda kumsebenzisi** kwimo esebenzayo ngokuyityibilikisa ukuya ekunene.

Okulandelayo, kwindima nganye eza kumiselwa umda, seta iswitshi esecaleni kwayo kwimo esebenzayo uze uchaze owona mda uphezulu kwi-field efanelekileyo.

#### Iintlobo Zeposti {#post-types}

Ithebhu ethi **Iintlobo Zeposti** ivumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuba abeke imida eneenkcukacha kuluhlu olubanzi lweentlobo zeposti ngaphakathi kwe-WordPress.

![Ithebhu yeentlobo zeposti](/img/config/product-post-types.png)

Ngenxa yokwakhiwa kwe-WordPress, iiposti kunye neentlobo zeposti ziyinxalenye ebalulekileyo yomsebenzi wayo ongundoqo, kwaye ngoko inkqubo yemida ye-Ultimate Multisite yenzelwe ukunceda umlawuli wenethiwekhi ekusekeni nasekugcineni imida.

Ukuvumela le nkqubo engaphantsi yemida, seta iswitshi ye-**misa umda kwiintlobo zeposti** kwimo esebenzayo ngokuyityibilikisa ukuya ekunene.

Okulandelayo, kuhlobo ngalunye lweposti oluza kumiselwa umda, luvule ngokulutyibilikisa ukuya ekunene uze uchaze owona mda uphezulu kwi-field efanelekileyo.

#### Isithuba Sediski {#disk-space}

Ithebhu ethi **Isithuba Sediski** ivumela abalawuli benethiwekhi ukuba banciphise isithuba esisetyenziswa ngabathengi.

![Ithebhu yesithuba sediski](/img/config/product-disk-space.png)

Ngokuqhelekileyo kwi-WordPress multisite iifayile ezingundoqo zabelwana phakathi kwazo zonke iisayithi, kwaye kwenziwa iidirayirekthari ezizimeleyo zeefayile zemidiya kunye nokulayishwa apho olu seto kunye nemida zisebenza khona.

Ukuvumela umda wokusetyenziswa kwediski, seta iswitshi ye-**misa umda kubungakanani bediski ngesayithi nganye** kwimo esebenzayo ngokuyityibilikisa ukuya ekunene.

Okulandelayo, chaza owona mda uphezulu ngeemegabhayithi kwi-field ye-**isibonelelo sesithuba sediski**.

#### Imixholo {#themes}

Ithebhu ethi **Imixholo** ngaphakathi kweenketho zemveliso ivumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuba enze imixholo ifumaneke kubathengi ukuze bayikhethe kwaye, ngokuzikhethela, anyanzelise imo yomxholo.

![Ithebhu yemixholo](/img/config/product-themes.png)

_**Qaphela: Ukuze imixholo yenziwe ifumaneke kubathengi kufuneka yenziwe isebenze kwinethiwekhi ngumlawuli wenethiwekhi.**_

![Iphepha lemixholo yenethiwekhi](/img/config/product-themes-network-enabled.png)

Inketho ye-**visibility** ichaza ukuba lo mxholo uyabonakala okanye awubonakali kumthengi xa ejonga ithebhu yakhe ethi **Inkangeleko > Imixholo** ngaphakathi kwesayithi yakhe. Ukuseta olu khetho ku-**Hidden** kususa umxholo ekubonakaleni kwaye ngaloo ndlela kuthintela amandla okuwukhetha nokuwuvula.

![Ithebhu yemixholo](/img/config/product-themes.png)

Ukhetho lwe-**behavior** luvumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuba achaze imo yomxholo xa kusenziwa isayithi yomthengi.

Kwimo **E** **khoyo** umxholo wenziwa ufumaneke kumthengi ukuze azivulele ngokwakhe. Ngokuchaseneyo, imo ethi **Ayifumaneki** isusa kumthengi amandla okuvula umxholo. Okokugqibela, inketho ethi **Nyanzelisa Ukuvula** inyanzelisa ukukhethwa nokuvulwa komxholo, ngaloo ndlela iwuseta njengowokuqala xa kusenziwa isayithi.

#### Iiplagi {#plugins}

Ngokufanayo nethebhu yeMixholo, Ultimate Multisite ivumela umlawuli wenethiwekhi ukuba achaze ukubonakala kweeplagi kubathengi kwakunye nemeko yazo xa kusenziwa isayithi entsha.

![Ithebhu yeeplagi](/img/config/product-plugins.png)

Uluhlu oluhlayo lwe-**visibility** luvumela iplagi ukuba ibonakale okanye ifihlwe kumthengi xa ijongwa kwisayithi yakhe ngenketho yemenyu yeePlagi.

Umlawuli wenethiwekhi angaphinda alawule indlela eziziphatha ngayo iiplagin ngokusebenzisa iinketho ezikuluhlu olwehlayo lokuziphatha.

![Ithebhu yeeplagin](/img/config/product-plugins.png)

Ukhetho **Oluqhelekileyo** luhlonipha imeko yeplagin echazwe kwitempleyithi yesayithi ekhethwe ngumthengi. Ngoko ke iiplagin ezenziwe zasebenza ngaphakathi kwitempleyithi ziya kuhlala zisebenza xa itempleyithi ikhutshelwa kwisayithi yomthengi.

Ukhetho **Nyanzela ukuSebenza** lubeka iplagin kwimeko esebenzayo xa kusenziwa isayithi, kwaye ngokuchaseneyo **Nyanzela ukungaSebenzi** lwenza iplagin ingasebenzi xa kusenziwa isayithi. Kuzo zombini ezi meko, imeko yeplagin inokutshintshwa ngesandla ngumthengi ngemenu yabo yeeplagin ze-WordPress.

Useto **Nyanzela ukuSebenza & Tshixa** lusebenza ngendlela efanayo kodwa luthintela imeko yeplagin ekubeni itshintshwe ngumthengi. Ngoko useto oluthi Nyanzela ukuSebenza & Tshixa luya kunyanzela iplagin ukuba ibe kwimeko yayo esebenzayo kwaye luthintele umthengi ekuyenzeni ingasebenzi. Ngokufanayo, useto **Nyanzela ukungaSebenzi & Tshixa** luya kunyanzela iplagin ukuba ibe kwimeko yayo engasebenziyo kwaye luthintele umsebenzisi ekuyenzeni isebenze.

Umlawuli wenethiwekhi unokufuna ukuqwalasela useto lweNyanzela ukuSebenza & Tshixa kunye neNyanzela ukungaSebenzi & Tshixa ngokudibeneyo neetempleyithi zesayithi, njengoko iiplagin kunye neemeko zeeplagin ngaphakathi kweetempleyithi zinokuchaphazeleka zezi seto ukuba zikhethiwe.

#### Seta kwakhona iziThintelo {#reset-limitations}

Ithebhu **Seta kwakhona iziThintelo** iseta kwakhona yonke imida eyenziwe ngokwezifiso echazwe kwimveliso. Ukuseta kwakhona izithintelo cofa iqhosha **seta kwakhona izithintelo**.

![Ithebhu yokuseta kwakhona izithintelo](/img/config/product-reset-limitations.png)

Ukuqinisekisa isenzo, tyibilikisa isitshintsheli **qinisekisa ukuseta kwakhona** kwimeko yaso esebenzayo ngasekunene uze ucofe iqhosha **seta kwakhona izithintelo**.

![Ithebhu yokuseta kwakhona izithintelo](/img/config/product-reset-limitations.png)
