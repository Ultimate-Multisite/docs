# Udibaniso lwe-WooCommerce

Udibaniso lwe-WooCommerce lusebenza ngendlela efana kakhulu nendlela esebenza ngayo i-Manual Gateway yethu. Lunika abasebenzisi bakho ukuguquguquka okuninzi, lubavumela ukuba bakhethe nayiphi na ifom yentlawulo ekhoyo kufakelo lwakho lwe-WooCommerce. Kwaye lulula: xa ixesha lokubizwa komsebenzisi liphela, umrhumo womsebenzisi ubekwa ekubanjweni. Kwangaxeshanye, iodolo iya kudalwa kufakelo lwe-WooCommerce lwesayithi ephambili yenethiwekhi. Umsebenzisi emva koko ufumana i-invoyisi kwi-imeyile yakhe, enekhonkco lokwenza intlawulo. Nje ukuba intlawulo ifunyenwe, umrhumo uyahlaziywa.

## Iimpawu {#features}

- Udibaniso olulula phakathi kwe-Ultimate Multisite ne-WooCommerce
- Iindlela ezininzi zentlawulo ziyafumaneka
- Ukubizwa okuphindaphindayo okuzenzekelayo kusetyenziswa undoqo wemirhumo obandakanyiweyo, kunye nophuculo olukhethwayo lwe-WooCommerce Subscriptions isongezo
- Iitotali zokuhlawula ziquka iirhafu ze-Ultimate Multisite njengemigca yemirhumo ye-WooCommerce eyahlukileyo, engarhafelwayo ukuze abathengi bakwazi ukuphonononga izixa zerhafu phambi kwentlawulo

## Amaxwebhu {#documentation}

**Ukuseta Udibaniso lwe-WooCommerce**

**Ukwandisa Iinketho zakho Zentlawulo nge-Ultimate Multisite: Udibaniso lwe-WooCommerce**

## Ufakelo {#installation}

1. Layisha 'ultimate-multisite-woocommerce' kulawulo lwe-'/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Sebenzisa isongezo ngemenu ye-'Plugins' kwi-WordPress
3. Landela i-Wizard inyathelo ngenyathelo ukuze usete isongezo

## Isikhokelo Sokusetyenziswa {#usage-guide}

_**INQAKU ELIBALULEKILEYO: Eli nqaku libhekisa kuhlobo lwe-Ultimate Multisite 2.x.**_

_**QAPHELA:** Ultimate Multisite: Udibaniso lwe-WooCommerce lufuna ukuba i-WooCommerce isebenze ubuncinane kwisayithi yakho ephambili._

Siyaqonda ukuba _Stripe_ ne-_PayPal_ azifumaneki kwamanye amazwe nto leyo enciphisa okanye ithintele abasebenzisi be-Ultimate Multisite ekusebenziseni isongezo sethu ngokusebenzayo. Ngoko sidale **isongezo sokudibanisa _WooCommerce_**.

_I-WooCommerce_ sisiyongezo se-eCommerce esithandwa kakhulu. Abaphuhlisi kwihlabathi lonke badale izongezo zokudibanisa iigateway zentlawulo ezahlukeneyo kuyo. Sisebenzise oku ukwandisa iigateway zentlawulo onokuzisebenzisa kwinkqubo yokubiza ye-Ultimate Multisite.

Emva koko ungaya kwiisetingi ze-**_WooCommerce_ zesayithi yakho ephambili** ukuze uvule kwaye umisele igateway yentlawulo okhetha ukuyisebenzisa.

### Imigca yemirhumo yerhafu yokuhlawula {#checkout-tax-fee-lines}

Xa ukuhlawula kwe-Ultimate Multisite kuquka iirhafu, udibaniso longeza isixa serhafu ngasinye se-Ultimate Multisite kukuhlawula kwe-WooCommerce njengomgca womrhumo owahlukileyo, ongarhafelwayo. Oku kugcina itotali yerhafu ibonakala phambi kwentlawulo ngoxa kuthintela i-WooCommerce ekurhafiseni umrhumo werhafu kwakhona.

## Indlela yokulawula intlawulo {#how-to-manage-the-payment}

Udibaniso lwe-WooCommerce lusebenza ngendlela efana kakhulu nendlela esebenza ngayo **Manual Gateway** yethu. Ingxaki kukuba intlawulo nganye kufuneka ihlawulwe ngesandla ekupheleni kwexesha lokubizwa. Nantsi indlela esebenza ngayo le nkqubo:

  * Nje ukuba umjikelo wokubizwa komsebenzisi uphele, ubulungu bubekwa **ekubanjweni**. Kwangaxeshanye, iodolo iya kudalwa kufakelo lwe-_WooCommerce_ kwisayithi ephambili yenethiwekhi yakho.

  * Abasebenzisi emva koko bafumana i-invoyisi kwi-imeyile yabo, enekhonkco lokwenza intlawulo (ikhonkco likwavela kwimbali yabo yokubizwa kwi-Dashboard yesayithi yabo engaphantsi). Elo khonkco lithumela umsebenzisi kwi-**ndawo yokuhlawula** ye-_**WooCommerce**_ , apho abasebenzisi baya kukwazi ukukhetha enye yee-**gateway ze-_WooCommerce_ ezifumanekayo** ukuze kuqhutyekwe intlawulo yabo.

  * Nje ukuba intlawulo ifunyenwe, ubulungu **buyahlaziywa** , intlawulo irekhodwa kwi-Ultimate Multisite kwaye iodolo isetwa njengogqityiweyo.

### Amanye Amanqaku Abalulekileyo: {#some-important-notes}

  * Umsebenzi ongagqibekanga we-Ultimate Multisite usasebenza nale fomu yodibaniso. Umzekelo, **ukukhupha ukubuyiselwa kwemali** kwiscreen se-**Ulawulo Lomrhumo** kuya kunxibelelana ne-WooCommerce ukukhupha ukubuyiselwa kwemali kusetyenziswa ifom yentlawulo ekhethiweyo.

  * Ultimate Multisite: Udibaniso lwe-WooCommerce luxhomekeke kwi-WP-Cron ukuze lusebenze. Oku kusebenza kakuhle kakhulu kumaxesha amaninzi, kodwa ukuba isayithi inetraffikhi ephantsi ngoko i-cron ayikhuthazwa ukuba ijongisise iziganeko ezicwangcisiweyo kwaye isiganeko siyaphoswa, nto leyo enokubangela ukulibaziseka kokuqhuba.

## Isiyongezo se-WooCommerce Subscriptions {#woocommerce-subscriptions-plugin}

Ukuqalisa intlawulo ngesandla nyanga nganye akufanelekanga kubasebenzisi bokugqibela kuba imirhumo kulindeleke ukuba iphinde yenzeke ngokuzenzekelayo kumjikelo ngamnye wokubizwa. Isongezo se-WooCommerce Integration ngoku sibandakanya ikhowudi engundoqo yemirhumo, ngoko iimveliso eziphindaphindayo zihlala zisebenzisa imirhumo kubizo oluphindaphindayo.

**Akudingeki** ukuba uthenge okanye ufake isongezo esahlukileyo se-[WooCommerce Subscriptions](https://woocommerce.com/products/woocommerce-subscriptions/) ukuze uthengise iimveliso eziphindaphindayo ngolu dityaniso. Ukuba eso songezo sithengiwe, safakwa, kwaye siyafumaneka kwisayithi yakho ephambili, i-Ultimate Multisite iya kusisebenzisa kwaye i-WooCommerce iya kubonisa iimpawu ezongezelelweyo zolawulo lwemirhumo. Xa singafumaneki, undoqo wemirhumo obandakanyiweyo usaqhuba iimveliso eziphindaphindayo.

![Iisetingi zodibaniso lomrhumo we-WooCommerce](/img/addons/woocommerce-subscription-integration.png)

Kwaye kuphelele apho. Abasebenzisi bakho bokugqibela abasayi kusafuna ukwenza intlawulo ngesandla ekupheleni komjikelo wabo wokubizwa kwiimveliso eziphindaphindayo.

Ngaphandle kokuba iodolo idalwe kwisayithi yakho ephambili phantsi kwe-_WooCommerce_, iya kudala nomrhumo kwi-akhawunti nganye.

### Indlela yokunqunyanyiswa xa uhlaziyo lusilele {#failed-renewal-suspension-behavior}

Ngokungagqibekanga, ubulungu buhlala busebenza ngoxa i-WooCommerce Subscriptions iqhuba ishedyuli yayo yokuzama kwakhona intlawulo esileleyo. Oku kugcina ixesha eliqhelekileyo lobabalo le-WooCommerce Subscriptions ukuze uhlaziyo olubuyiselweyo lukwazi ukubuyisela umrhumo nobulungu ekuvumelaneni ngaphandle kokuphazamisa ufikelelo ngaphambi kwexesha.

Abalawuli abafuna ulawulo lofikelelo olungqongqo ngakumbi banokuvula **Nqumamisa Ubulungu Ngoko Nangoko xa Uhlaziyo Lusilele** kwiisetingi ze-WooCommerce Integration. Xa esi setingi sokukhetha singenelelwa sivuliwe, i-Ultimate Multisite inqumamisa ubulungu obunxulumeneyo ngokukhawuleza nje ukuba intlawulo yohlaziyo isilele endaweni yokulinda ukuba ukuphathwa kokuzama kwakhona kwe-WooCommerce Subscriptions kugqitywe.

Abaphuhlisi banokutshintsha isigqibo esifanayo ngesihluzi se-`wu_woo_suspend_on_payment_failure`. Amaphepha esalathiso se-hook aveliswa kumthombo wesongezo, ngoko esi sishwankathelo sikhankanya kuphela igama lesihluzi kwimigaqo-nkqubo yesiko yokunqunyanyiswa.
