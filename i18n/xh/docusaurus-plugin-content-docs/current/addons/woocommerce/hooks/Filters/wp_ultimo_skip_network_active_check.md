# Isihluzi: wp_ultimo_skip_network_active_check

Vumela abaphuhlisi ukuba banqumamise kwangoko uvavanyo lokusebenza kwinethiwekhi.

Oku kuluncedo xa kusetyenziswa ulungiselelo olusekelwe kwi-composer kunye nolunye ulungiselelo olwenzelwe wena, njengakwi-Bedrock, umzekelo, apho ukusetyenziswa kwee-plugin njenge-mu-plugins kuyinto eqhelekileyo.

## Iiparamitha {#parameters}

| Igama | Uhlobo | Inkcazelo |
|------|------|-------------|
| $skip_network_activation_check | `bool` | Ukuba kufuneka silutsibe uvavanyo okanye hayi, ixabiso elisisiseko ngu-false. |

### Ukususela {#since}

- 2.0.0
