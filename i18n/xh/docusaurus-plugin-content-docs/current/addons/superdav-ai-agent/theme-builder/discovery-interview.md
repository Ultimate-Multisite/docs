# Udliwano-ndlebe Lokufumanisa

I-**Discovery Interview** linyathelo lokuqala kwinkqubo yoyilo ye-Theme Builder. Liqokelela ulwazi malunga nesayithi yakho, ishishini lakho, kunye nezinto ozikhethayo kuyilo ukuze lenze i-theme elungiselelwe wena.

## Isishwankathelo

I-Discovery Interview ikukhokela ngothotho lwemibuzo ukuze iqonde uphawu lwakho, abaphulaphuli ojolise kubo, kunye neenjongo zoyilo. Kuguqulelo 1.16.0, udliwano-ndlebe ngoku luquka **inyathelo lokulayisha ifoto** elikuvumela ukuba unike inkuthazo ebonakalayo kuyilo lwakho.

## Amanyathelo Odliwano-ndlebe

### 1. Ulwazi Lweshishini

- Igama lesayithi kunye nesivakalisi esichazayo
- Uhlobo lweshishini okanye icandelo loshishino
- Iinjongo eziphambili zeshishini
- Inkcazelo yabaphulaphuli ojolise kubo

### 2. Izinto Ozithandayo Kuyilo

- Iskimu semibala osikhethayo
- Isimbo soyilo (sanamhlanje, sakudala, esilula, esidlalayo, njl.)
- Iiwebhusayithi okanye iibrendi zenkuthazo
- Izinto zoyilo ekufuneka zibekho

### 3. Ukulayisha Ifoto (Entsha kwi-v1.16.0)

Layisha ifoto enye okanye ezingaphezulu ukuze ziphembelele indlela yoyilo:

#### Iifomathi Ezamkelwayo

| Ifomathi | Ulwandiso | Iinkcukacha |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| **JPEG** | `.jpg`, `.jpeg` | Iyacetyiswa kwiifoto |
| **PNG** | `.png` | Ixhasa ukungafihli |
| **WebP** | `.webp` | Ifomathi yanamhlanje, ubungakanani befayile obuncinci |
| **GIF** | `.gif` | Imifanekiso emileyo kuphela (akukho ntshukumo) |

#### Iimfuno Zefayile

- **Obona bungakanani befayile buphezulu:** 10 MB
- **Obona bukhulu buncinane:** 400 × 300 pixels
- **Ubungakanani obucetyiswayo:** 1200 × 800 pixels okanye ngaphezulu
- **Umlinganiselo wamacala:** Nawuphi na umlinganiselo wamkelekile

#### Indlela Iifoto Eziphembelela Ngayo Indlela Yoyilo

Iifoto ozilayishayo zinceda Theme Builder iqonde:

- **Iphalethi yemibala** — imibala elawula umfanekiso iyatsalwa kwaye isetyenziswe kwi-theme
- **Isimbo esibonakalayo** — sanamhlanje, selali, esilula, esiqaqambileyo, njl.
- **Imvakalelo nomoya** — yobuchwephesha, enobuchule, enobuhlobo, enobunewunewu, njl.
- **Izinto zoyilo** — iipateni, imisonto, kunye nezimbo zokuhlanganisa
- **Inkuthazo yetypography** — izimbo zefonti ezihambelana ne-theme ebonakalayo

### 4. Isakhiwo Somxholo

- Amaphepha aphambili afunekayo (Ekhaya, Malunga, Iinkonzo, Uqhagamshelwano, njl.)
- Icandelo lebhlog okanye leendaba liyafuneka
- Ukusebenza kwe-e-commerce kuyafuneka
- Iimpawu ezikhethekileyo okanye udibaniso

### 5. Uphononongo Nokuqinisekisa

- Isishwankathelo solwazi lonke olunikiweyo
- Ukuqinisekisa phambi kokuba ukuveliswa koyilo kuqale

## Iindlela Ezilungileyo Zokulayisha Iifoto

### Into Yokulayisha

- **Inkuthazo ye-brand** — iilogo, iiswotshi zemibala, okanye izikhokelo ze-brand
- **Iisayithi zabakhuphisana nawe** — ii-screenshot zeesayithi ozithandayo
- **Iibhodi zemvakalelo** — iingqokelela zemifanekiso emele inkangeleko oyinqwenelayo
- **Iifoto zeemveliso** — ukuba uneemveliso ofuna ukuzibonisa
- **Iifoto zeqela okanye zeofisi** — ukwenzela i-branding yomntu okanye yobuchwephesha
- **Imifanekiso yendlela yokuphila** — emele abaphulaphuli ojolise kubo

### Into Yokuphepha

- **Imifanekiso ekumgangatho ophantsi** — iifoto ezingacacanga okanye ezineepikseli ezibonakalayo
- **Imifanekiso engahambelaniyo** — iifoto ezingamele i-brand yakho
- **Imifanekiso enombhalo omninzi** — ii-screenshot ezinombhalo omninzi
- **Izinto ezinamalungelo okukopa** — qinisekisa ukuba unamalungelo okusebenzisa imifanekiso
- **Ii-GIF ezihambayo** — kusetyenzwa kuphela imifanekiso emileyo

### Iingcebiso Zeziphumo Ezilungileyo

1. **Layisha imifanekiso emi-2-3** — imifanekiso emininzi inika umxholo ongcono
2. **Sebenzisa iifoto ezikumgangatho ophezulu** — imifanekiso ecacileyo, ekhanyiswe kakuhle isebenza ngcono
3. **Faka iintlobo ezahlukeneyo** — umxube wemibala, isimbo, kunye nomxholo
4. **Nika umxholo** — chaza ngokufutshane ukuba kutheni ukhethe umfanekiso ngamnye
5. **Cacisa ngokuthe ngqo** — ukuba uthanda into ethile, yibonise

## Isiphumo Sendlela Yoyilo

Emva kwe-Discovery Interview, Theme Builder ivelisa:

- **Iphalethi yemibala** — imibala ephambili, yesibini, kunye neyokugxininisa
- **Typography** — iintsapho zefonti zeentloko kunye nombhalo womzimba
- **Isimbo solwakhiwo** — izithuba, ukulungelelanisa, kunye nolungiselelo lwamacandelo
- **Izinto ezibonakalayo** — ii-icon, iipateni, kunye nezinto zokuhombisa
- **Ibhodi yemvakalelo** — umboniso obonakalayo wendlela yoyilo

## Amanyathelo Alandelayo

Emva kokugqiba i-Discovery Interview:
