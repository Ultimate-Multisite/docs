# Izicwangciso Ezilinganiselweyo

I-Ultimate Multisite: isongezo se-Izicwangciso Ezilinganiselweyo sitshintsha indlela obabiza ngayo abathengi bakho ngokuzisa amandla okubiza aguquguqukayo asekelwe kusetyenziso. Endaweni yemida engqongqo evala abasebenzisi xa begqithisile, yenza iintlawulo zokusetyenziswa okugqithisileyo ezibiza abathengi ngokuzenzekelayo ngosetyenziso olongezelelekileyo.

## Iimpawu Ezibalulekileyo {#key-features}

* **Ukubiza Okulinganiselweyo**: Biza abathengi ngokusekelwe kusetyenziso lokwenene endaweni yamanqanaba amiselweyo
* **Ulawulo Lokusetyenziswa Okugqithisileyo**: Biza ngokuzenzekelayo ngosetyenziso olugqitha imida yesicwangciso
* **Ukulandelela Usetyenziso**: Jonga ukusetyenziswa kwezixhobo zabathengi ngexesha lokwenyani
* **Amaxabiso Aguquguqukayo**: Misela amaxabiso akho kwizixhobo ezahlukeneyo (ugcino, bandwidth, iisayithi, njl.)
* **Ukubiza ii-Token ze-AI**: Landela uze ubize ii-token zokufaka nezokukhupha zosetyenziso lwe-AI kwiisayithi ezingaphantsi zabathengi
* **Ukuvelisa ii-Invoyisi Ngokuzenzekelayo**: Velisa ii-invoyisi ngokuzenzekelayo xa imigangatho yosetyenziso igqithisiwe
* **Iingxelo Zosetyenziso**: Uhlalutyo oluneenkcukacha losetyenziso kunye neengxelo zabathengi nabalawuli

## Ifanele Ngokugqibeleleyo {#perfect-for}

* **Ababoneleli be-Hosting**: Nikezela ngezicwangciso ezinokukhula ezikhula neemfuno zabathengi
* **Amaqonga e-SaaS**: Biza ngokusekelwe kusetyenziso lokwenene lwezixhobo
* **Iinethiwekhi zee-Arhente**: Iimodeli zamaxabiso aguquguqukayo zeeprojekthi zabathengi
* **I-Reseller Hosting**: Yenza amaqabane akwazi ukunikezela ngamaxabiso asekelwe kusetyenziso

## Indlela Esebenza Ngayo {#how-it-works}

1. Qwalasela izixhobo ezilinganiselweyo (indawo yediski, bandwidth, inani leesayithi, njl.)
2. Misela imida yesicwangciso esisisiseko kunye namaxabiso okusetyenziswa okugqithisileyo
3. Inkqubo ilandelela ngokuzenzekelayo usetyenziso kuyo yonke inethiwekhi yakho
4. Xa abathengi begqitha imida, iintlawulo zokusetyenziswa okugqithisileyo ziyabalwa
5. Ii-invoyisi ziveliswa ngokuzenzekelayo ngokusekelwe kumjikelo wokubiza
6. Abathengi banokujonga usetyenziso lwabo kunye neentlawulo ezizayo ngexesha lokwenyani

## Ukubiza ii-Token ze-AI {#ai-token-billing}

Inguqulelo 1.1.0 yongeza ukubizwa kwee-token ze-AI kwiinenethiwekhi ezibonelela ngeempawu ze-AI nge-WordPress AI Client SDK. Izicwangciso Ezilinganiselweyo zimamela izicelo zokugqitywa kokuvelisa kwe-AI, zirekhode amanani ee-token zokufaka nezokukhupha zesayithi engaphantsi yangoku, zize ziguqule ezo token zibe yinkcitho ye-AI enokubizwa kwixesha langoku lokubiza lomthengi.

Sebenzisa ukubizwa kwee-token ze-AI xa ufuna ukunikezela ngezixhobo ze-AI kwiisayithi zabathengi ngaphandle kokuthwala iindleko zemodeli ezingalindelekanga. Isicelo ngasinye sinxulunyaniswa nesayithi engaphantsi, umthengi, ubulungu, umboneleli, kunye nemodeli ukuze abalawuli bakwazi ukuphicotha usetyenziso ngelixa abathengi bebona indlela ukusetyenziswa kwabo kwe-AI okuchaphazela ngayo iakhawunti yabo.

### Qwalasela Amaxabiso nge-Token Nganye {#configure-per-token-rates}

Amaxabiso e-AI aqwalaselwa ngemodeli nganye njengamaxabiso ahlukeneyo okufaka nawokukhupha. Amaxabiso abalelwa kwi-1 million token, nto leyo ehambelana namaphepha aqhelekileyo amaxabiso ababoneleli be-AI.

1. Kumlawuli wenethiwekhi, vula **Multisite Ultimate → Iisetingi → Izicwangciso Ezilinganiselweyo**.
2. Yenza ukulinganiswa kwenkcitho ye-AI kusebenze kwimveliso okanye kubulungu obumele bubize usetyenziso lwe-AI.
3. Yongeza amangeniso amaxabiso emodeli usebenzisa ii-ID zemodeli ezibuyiswa sisidibanisi sakho, ezifana no-`gpt-4o`, `gpt-4o-mini`, okanye esinye isazisi semodeli esihambelana ne-OpenAI.
4. Faka amaxabiso ahlukeneyo **Ixabiso Lokufaka** kunye **Nexabiso Lokukhupha** ngemali yesayithi yakho kwi-1 million token.
5. Misela isibonelelo senkcitho ye-AI ebandakanyiweyo somthengi kunye nokuziphatha kosetyenziso olugqithisileyo kwisicwangciso.

Xa i-ID yemodeli echanekileyo ingaqwalaselwanga, Izicwangciso Ezilinganiselweyo zinokuthelekisa ngesimaphambili semodeli. Umzekelo, ixabiso eliqwalaselwe ku-`claude-sonnet-4` lingasebenza kwii-ID zemodeli ezinesitampu sexesha eziqala ngeso simaphambili. Abaphuhlisi banokuhlengahlengisa itheyibhile yokugqibela yamaxabiso ngesihluzi se-[`wu_ai_token_rates`](./hooks/Filters/wu_ai_token_rates).

### Izidibanisi ze-AI Ezixhaswayo {#supported-ai-connectors}

Ukubiza ii-token ze-AI kusebenza nezidibanisi ezikhupha iziganeko zokuvelisa ze-WordPress AI Client SDK kwaye ziquka usetyenziso lwee-token kwimethadatha yempendulo. Oku kuquka usapho lwesidibanisi se-Ultimate Multisite AI olubhalwe apha:

* [Isidibanisi se-Ultimate AI see-Endpoint Ezihambelanayo](../ultimate-ai-connector-compatible-endpoints/) see-API ezihambelana ne-OpenAI, kuquka i-OpenAI, Mistral, amasango ahambelana ne-Ollama, kunye nabanye ababoneleli abaveza imethadatha yosetyenziso lwee-token ehambelanayo
* [Isidibanisi se-Ultimate AI se-WebLLM](../ultimate-ai-connector-webllm/) sosetyenziso lwe-WebLLM yemveli yesikhangeli xa izicelo zihanjiswa ngokuhamba kweziganeko ze-AI Client SDK
* Izidibanisi zababoneleli be-AI ezisetyenziswa yi-[Gratis AI Agent](../gratis-ai-agent/) xa umboneleli okhethiweyo exela amanani ee-token ze-prompt nezokugqitywa nge-SDK

Izidibanisi ezenzelwe wena zinokuthatha inxaxheba ngokudubula iziganeko eziqhelekileyo `wp_ai_client_before_generate_result` kunye `wp_ai_client_after_generate_result` ngemodeli, umboneleli, kunye nemethadatha yosetyenziso lwee-token. Iisetingi zezidibanisi zinyanzeliswa ukusuka kwisayithi ephambili ukuya kwiisayithi ezingaphantsi ukuze iisayithi zabathengi zisebenzise ababoneleli nemida evunyiweyo yinethiwekhi.

### Usetyenziso lwe-Akhawunti yoMthengi {#customer-account-usage}

Abathengi babona usetyenziso lwe-AI kunye nezinye izixhobo zabo ezilinganiselweyo kwindawo yeakhawunti yabo. Umqolo wenkcitho ye-AI ubonisa itotali yangoku yexesha lokubiza evela kwii-token ze-prompt nezokugqitywa ezirekhodiweyo, amaxabiso emodeli aqwalaselweyo, kunye naso nasiphi na isibonelelo esibandakanyiweyo kwisicwangciso.

Ukuba umthengi unendlela yokuhlawula efayilini, usetyenziso olungaphezu kwesibonelelo esibandakanyiweyo luthathwa njengosetyenziso olugqithisileyo olunokubizwa. Ukuba isicwangciso siqwalaselwe ukunqunyanyiswa okungqongqo kwaye umthengi akanayo indlela yokuhlawula ekhoyo, izicelo ze-AI zinokuvalwa xa isibonelelo sifikelelwe kwaye umthengi acelwe ukuba ongeze iinkcukacha zokuhlawula ngaphambi kokuqhubeka.

## Izixhobo Ezixhaswayo {#supported-resources}

* Ugcino lwediski
* Bandwidth/Udluliselo lwedatha
* Inani leesayithi
* Inani labasebenzisi
* Usetyenziso lwee-token ze-AI kunye nenkcitho ye-AI
* Iintlobo zezixhobo ezenzelwe wena

## Iimfuno {#requirements}

* Multisite Ultimate 2.0.0 okanye ngaphezulu
* Ufakelo lwe-WordPress Multisite
* PHP 7.4 okanye ngaphezulu

## Ufakelo {#installation}

1. Layisha iifayile ze-plugin ku-`/wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite-metered-plans`
2. Yenza i-plugin isebenze ngesikrini se-'Plugins' kwi-WordPress
3. Yiya ku-Multisite Ultimate → Iisetingi → Izicwangciso Ezilinganiselweyo
4. Qwalasela izixhobo zakho ezilinganiselweyo kunye namaxabiso
5. Yenza ukubiza okulinganiselweyo kusebenze kwiimveliso kumhleli wemveliso

## Imibuzo Ebuzwa Rhoqo {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Kwenzeka ntoni xa umthengi egqitha umda wakhe? {#what-happens-when-a-customer-exceeds-their-limit}

Endaweni yokuvala ukufikelela, inkqubo ilandelela ukusetyenziswa okugqithisileyo ize ikongeze kwi-invoyisi yakhe elandelayo ngokusekelwe kumaxabiso owaqwalaseleyo.

## Ndingamisela amaxabiso ahlukeneyo okusetyenziswa okugqithisileyo kwiimveliso ezahlukeneyo? {#can-i-set-different-overage-rates-for-different-products}

Ewe, imveliso nganye inokuthi ibe nezixhobo ezilinganiselweyo ezizodwa kunye namaxabiso okusetyenziswa okugqithisileyo.

## Abathengi baziswa njani malunga nosetyenziso? {#how-are-customers-notified-about-usage}

Abathengi banokujonga usetyenziso lwabo lwangoku kwi-dashboard yabo kwaye bafumane izaziso abazikhethayo xa besondela okanye begqitha imida.

## Usetyenziso lwee-token ze-AI lubizwa njani ngexabiso? {#how-is-ai-token-usage-priced}

Usetyenziso lwee-token ze-AI lubekwa ixabiso ukusuka kumaxabiso aqwalaselweyo okufaka nawokukhupha emodeli kwi-1 million token. Izicwangciso Ezilinganiselweyo ziphinda-phinda amanani ee-token ze-prompt nezokugqitywa zesicelo ngasinye ngaloo maxabiso, zongeze isiphumo kwinkcitho ye-AI yexesha langoku lokubiza lomthengi, zize zisebenzise isibonelelo sesicwangciso okanye imithetho yosetyenziso olugqithisileyo.

## Ndingakwazi na ukusebenzisa imida engqongqo? {#can-i-still-use-hard-limits}

Ewe, ukubiza okulinganiselweyo kuyakhethwa kwimveliso nganye. Unokudibanisa iimveliso zemveli ezinemida engqongqo kunye neemveliso ezilinganiselweyo.
