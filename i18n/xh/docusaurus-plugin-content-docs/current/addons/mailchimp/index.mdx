# Mailchimp

Multisite Ultimate: Udibaniso lwe-Mailchimp

Dibanisa inethiwekhi yakho ye-Multisite Ultimate ne-Mailchimp yakho. Ungaqhagamshela iiplani ezithile kuLuhlu lwe-Mailchimp kwaye wongeze ababhalisi ngokuzenzekelayo kolo luhlu xa bebhalisa.

## Ufakelo {#installation}

1. Layisha 'ultimate-multisite-mailchimp' kwidirekthri ethi '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Vula i-plugin ngemenyu ethi 'Ii-plugin' kuLawulo lweNethiwekhi lwe-WordPress yakho

## Isikhokelo sokuSebenzisa {#usage-guide}

Ukuvumelanisa ii-imeyile zababhalisi noluhlu lwakho lwe-MailChimp kulula kakhulu ngesongezo sethu se-Ultimate Multisite: MailChimp Integration. Esi sifundo sijolise ekukuncedeni ulungise yonke into!

## Ukufumana isitshixo se-API {#getting-an-api-key}

Phambi kokuba siqale ukungena kwiisetingi ezahlukeneyo ezikhoyo, sifuna isitshixo se-MailChimp API. Oku kuya kuvumela Ultimate Multisite ukuba ithethe ne-MailChimp API ukuze ifumane uluhlu, amaqela, kwaye yongeze iidilesi ze-imeyile kolo luhlu nakumaqela xa abasebenzisi bebhalisa.

Landela esi sifundo ukuze ufumane esakho isitshixo se-MailChimp API: [Malunga nezitshixo ze-API | MailChimp](https://kb.mailchimp.com/integrations/api-integrations/about-api-keys)

<!-- Umfanekiso wesikrini awufumaneki: iisetingi zesitshixo se-MailChimp API kwiisetingi zesongezo se-Ultimate Multisite -->

_Yongeza isitshixo sakho se-MailChimp API ku_ _**Ultimate Multisite Settings - > Add-on Settings -> MailChimp**_

## Imo yoDibaniso {#integration-mode}

Isongezo soDibaniso lwe-MailChimp sinika “iimo zodibaniso” ezimbini, okuthetha ukuba kukho iindlela ezimbini ezahlukeneyo onokusebenzisa ngazo isongezo ukuvumelanisa iidilesi ze-imeyile.

<!-- Umfanekiso wesikrini awufumaneki: ukhetho lwemo yodibaniso lwe-MailChimp olunokhetho loLuhlu oluNinzi noLuhlu olunye -->

_Iimo ezimbini zodibaniso ukuze kubekho ukuguquguquka okukhulu!_

### Imo 1: Uluhlu oluNinzi {#mode-1-multiple-lists}

Imo emiselweyo isebenzisa **Uluhlu oluNinzi**. Le mo iya kukuvumela ukuba ukhethe uluhlu oluninzi lwe-MailChimp kwiplani nganye. Olu lolona khetho lulungileyo ukuba wahlula abasebenzisi bakho kuluhlu olwahlukeneyo.

Umzekelo, masicinge ukuba unoluhlu olu-3 olwahlukeneyo kwi-Account yakho ye-MailChimp: **Newsletter** , **Plan A** , kunye **Plan B**. Ufuna bonke abasebenzisi, nokuba banayiphi na iplani, nabo bafakwe kuluhlu lwe-Newsletter, ukuze bangaphoswa ziingxelo zakho ezintsha.

Ukufezekisa oko, ungasebenzisa imo yoLuhlu oluNinzi ukongeza zombini uluhlu **Newsletter** kunye **Plan A** kwiisetingi ze-Plan A MailChimp, kunye **Newsletter** kunye **Plan B** kwiisetingi ze-Plan B MailChimp.

<!-- Umfanekiso wesikrini awufumaneki: ukhetho loluhlu olumiselweyo lwe-MailChimp lwee-akhawunti ezintsha nee-akhawunti ezirhoxisiweyo -->

_Khetha uluhlu olumiselweyo lwee-akhawunti ezintsha kunye nee-akhawunti ezirhoxisiweyo_

### Imo 2: Uluhlu olunye + Amaqela amaninzi {#mode-2-single-list--multiple-groups}

I-MailChimp ikuvumela ukuba wahlule uluhlu lwakho lube ngamaqela amancinci abasebenzisi abizwa ngokuba **Amaqela**. Oku kukuvumela ukuba ube nolawulo olungakumbi kuba ungathumela iphulo kwiqela elithile lababhalisi bakho ngaphandle kokudala iikopi ezininzi zephulo elifanayo kwi-MailChimp (into eyenza kube nzima ukuhlalutya iimetriki zelo phulo kamva).

Umzekelo, unokuba noluhlu olunye olubizwa **Ababhalisi beNethiwekhi Yam,** kwaye ngaphakathi kulo ube namaqela e-**Plan A** kunye ne-**Plan B**.

<!-- Umfanekiso wesikrini awufumaneki: ukhetho loluhlu olunye lwe-MailChimp namaqela ololuhlu olumiselweyo -->

_Khetha uluhlu olumiselweyo namaqela, ukuba usebenzisa imo yodibaniso ethi “uluhlu olunye + amaqela amaninzi”_

## Uluhlu namaQela aMiselweyo {#default-lists-and-groups}

Emva kokuba ukhethe ukuba yeyiphi imo yodibaniso enengqiqo ngakumbi kwimeko yakho yokusetyenziswa ethile, lixesha lokukhetha uluhlu olumiselweyo (namaqela, kuxhomekeke kwimowudi). Uluhlu olumiselweyo luya kusetyenziswa xa iplani ethile ingenalo uLuhlu oluthile olukhethiweyo.

Ungakhetha noluhlu olumiselweyo lwee-akhawunti ezirhoxisiweyo. Nanini na xa umsebenzisi ephelisa umrhumo wakhe aze asuse i-akhawunti yakhe kwinethiwekhi yakho, idilesi yakhe ye-imeyile iya kususwa isiwe kuluhlu olusetelwe ii-akhawunti ezirhoxisiweyo.

## Ukuvumela uMrhumo oPhindwe Kabini {#enabling-double-subscription}

Kwiphepha elifanayo leesetingi, uya kukwazi ukuvumela **uMrhumo oPhindwe Kabini**. Olu khetho luya kongeza umsebenzisi kuluhlu olumiselweyo olukhethiweyo KUNYE noluhlu olukhethiweyo lweplani yaloo msebenzisi. Ukuba olu khetho aluvunyelwanga, umsebenzisi uya kongezwa kuphela kuluhlu olukhethiweyo lweplani yomsebenzisi. Ukuba akukho nto imiselweyo kuloo plani ithile, ngoko umsebenzisi uya kongezwa kuluhlu olumiselweyo olukhethiweyo.

<!-- Umfanekiso wesikrini awufumaneki: isetingi yokuvula nokuvala umrhumo ophindwe kabini we-MailChimp -->

_Ungenza abasebenzisi bakho babhaliswe kuluhlu olumiselweyo nakuluhlu lweplani xa bebhalisa_

## Ukukhetha uLuhlu namaQela kwiplani nganye {#selecting-lists-and-groups-for-each-of-the-plans}

Yiya kwi-**Plans** zakho uze ukhethe iplani ofuna ukuyihlela. Qaphela ukuba kwi-meta-box ye-**Advanced Options**, ithebhu entsha ebizwa MailChimp iya kufumaneka.

Kuloo thebhu, uya kukwazi ukukhetha uluhlu oluninzi okanye uluhlu olunye namaqela amaninzi (kuxhomekeke kwimowudi yodibaniso) yaloo plani.

<!-- Umfanekiso wesikrini awufumaneki: ithebhu ye-MailChimp kwiphepha lokuhlela iplani ebonisa ukhetho loluhlu namaqela -->

_Khetha uLuhlu namaQela ofuna ukuwasebenzisa kwiplani nganye yakho_

Xa umsebenzisi omtsha ebhalisela loo plani, idilesi yakhe ye-imeyile iya kongezwa kuluhlu nakumaqela akhethiweyo!
