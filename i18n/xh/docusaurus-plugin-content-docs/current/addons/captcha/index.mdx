# Captcha

**Misa iiBots. Khusela ingeniso. Akukho ngxaki yoqwalaselo.**

Uqhuba inethiwekhi ye-multisite eneedomayini ezilungiselelweyo? Uyayazi intlungu: Google reCAPTCHA ne-hCaptcha zifuna ubhalise *idomayini nganye* kwikhonsoli yazo yolawulo. Wongeza isiza somthengi omtsha? Hlaziya uqwalaselo lwe-captcha. Umapha idomayini elungiselelweyo? Hlaziya kwakhona. Ayipheli.

**Ultimate Multisite: Captcha itshintsha yonke into.**

## Cap Captcha: Ukhuseleko olusingathwa nguwe olusebenza nje {#cap-captcha-self-hosted-protection-that-just-works}

Umboneleli wethu omtsha we-**Cap Captcha** utshintsha umdlalo kwiintanethi ze-multisite:

* **Akukho Qwalaselo** — Sebenzisa i-addon kwaye ukhuselekile. Akukho zitshixo ze-API, akukho akhawunti kaGoogle, akukho dashboard ye-hCaptcha, akukho kuvunyelwa kweedomayini kuluhlu olumhlophe.
* **Isebenza kuzo Zonke Iidomayini Ngokuzenzekelayo** — Ii-subdomain, iidomayini ezimaphwe, iidomayini zabathengi—zonke zikhuselwa ngoko nangoko.
* **Isingathwa Nguwe & Iyimfihlo** — Idatha yakho ayisoze ishiye iseva yakho. Ithobela ngokupheleleyo i-GDPR ngaphandle kokulandela umkhondo ngumntu wesithathu.
* **Ubungqina Bomsebenzi Obungabonakaliyo** — Abasebenzisi basombulula iphazili yekhompyutha ngasemva. Akukho kucofa izicimi-mlilo, akukho zigride zemifanekiso ezicaphukisayo.
* **Ikhaphukhaphu** — Yi-20KB kuphela. Ayizukucothisa i-checkout yakho.

## Ukhuseleko lwe-WooCommerce Kuvavanyo Lwamakhadi {#woocommerce-card-testing-protection}

Uhlaselo lokuvavanya amakhadi luxabisa abarhwebi iibhiliyoni minyaka le. Abenzi bobuqhetseba basebenzisa ii-bots ukuqinisekisa amakhadi etyala abiwe ngokuchasene ne-WooCommerce checkout yakho—wena uhlawula iimali zokuqhuba intlawulo, bona bafumana iinombolo zamakhadi ezisebenzayo.

**Sikhusela apho kubaluleke khona kakhulu:**

* **Ukhuseleko lwe-Store API** — Luthintela uhlaselo lwe-bot ku-`/wp-json/wc/store/v1/checkout` oludlula ii-captcha zeefom zemveli
* **Iyahambelana ne-PayPal Payments** — Isebenza kakuhle ne-WooCommerce PayPal Payments
* **Izibalo Zexesha Lokwenyani** — Bona kanye ukuba mangaphi uhlaselo oluthintelayo

## Usafuna i-Google reCAPTCHA okanye i-hCaptcha? {#still-need-google-recaptcha-or-hcaptcha}

Sikugubungele. Inkxaso epheleleyo ye:

* Google reCAPTCHA v2 (Checkbox)
* Google reCAPTCHA v2 (Invisible)
* Google reCAPTCHA v3 (Score-based)
* hCaptcha (Standard)
* hCaptcha (Invisible)

Tshintsha phakathi kwababoneleli nangaliphi na ixesha kwiisetingi zenethiwekhi yakho.

## Yakhelwe Ukukhula {#built-for-scale}

* Iisetingi zenethiwekhi yonke ezinokugqithiswa kwisiza ngasinye
* Dashboard yezibalo yokubeka iliso kukhuseleko
* Amanqanaba okhuseleko ahlengahlengiswayo (Fast, Medium, Maximum)
* Ilungele abaphuhlisi ngoyilo lomboneleli olunokwandiswa

## Ufakelo {#installation}

1. Layisha `ultimate-multisite-captcha` kwidirekthri yakho ye-`/wp-content/plugins/`
2. Yenza i-Network Activate ye-plugin
3. **Kuphelele apho. Ukhuselekile.**

Cap Captcha isebenza ngokuzenzekelayo—akukho zisetingi zokuqwalasela, akukho zitshixo ze-API zokuzifaka. Yonke endpoint exhaswayo kuyo yonke inethiwekhi yakho ikhuselwe mzuzu uyivula.

**Ngokuzikhethela:** Ukusebenzisa i-Google reCAPTCHA okanye i-hCaptcha endaweni yayo, yiya ku-**Ultimate Multisite → Settings → Captcha** uze ukhethe umboneleli omthandayo.

## Imibuzo Ebuzwa Rhoqo {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Ngaba kufuneka ndiqwalasele nantoni na? {#do-i-need-to-configure-anything}

Hayi! Cap Captcha iyavulwa ngokuzenzekelayo xa uvula i-addon. Inethiwekhi yakho yonke ikhuselekile kwangoko—akukho zisetingi zokuqwalasela, akukho zitshixo ze-API zokuzifaka.

## Ngaba ndifuna izitshixo ze-API ze-Cap Captcha? {#do-i-need-api-keys-for-cap-captcha}

Hayi. Cap Captcha isingathwa nguwe ngokupheleleyo. Akukho akhawunti zangaphandle, akukho kubhaliswa kweedomayini, akukho zintlungu zoqwalaselo.

## Ngaba oku kuza kucothisa i-checkout yam? {#will-this-slow-down-my-checkout}

Cap Captcha yi-20KB kuphela kwaye iqhuba imingeni yobungqina bomsebenzi ngasemva. Abathengi bakho abayi kuqaphela nakuphi na ukulibaziseka.

## Ngaba oku kukhusela kuhlaselo lokuvavanya amakhadi? {#does-this-protect-against-card-testing-attacks}

Ewe! Ngokungafaniyo nee-captcha zemveli ezikhusela kuphela iifom ze-HTML, udibaniso lwethu lwe-WooCommerce luxhuma ngqo kwi-Store API ukuthintela uhlaselo lwe-bot kwinqanaba le-API—apho uninzi lohlaselo lokuvavanya amakhadi lwenzeka khona.

## Ndingasebenzisa ababoneleli be-captcha abahlukeneyo kwiziza ezahlukeneyo? {#can-i-use-different-captcha-providers-on-different-sites}

Umboneleli we-captcha usetwa kwinethiwekhi yonke, kodwa unokuhlengahlengisa iisetingi zobunzima ngokusekelwe kwiimfuno zakho.

## Ukulinganiselwa Kwezinga {#rate-limiting}

Ultimate Multisite: Captcha v1.5.0 yazisa umlinganisi wezinga omisa ngokupheleleyo okhusela inethiwekhi yakho kuhlaselo lwe-brute-force nolokuvavanya amakhadi.

### Isebenza Njani {#how-it-works}

Umlinganisi wezinga ubala sonke isicelo se-GET ne-POST kwiindawo ezikhuselwe yi-captcha:

* **Ii-endpoint zokungena ze-WordPress** — wp-login, register, lost-password, comments
* **WooCommerce checkout** — my-account, checkout, pay-for-order
* **Ultimate Multisite** — checkout, inline-login

Xa undwendwe ludlula umda wezinga, i-plugin iphendula nge:

* **HTTP 429 (Too Many Requests)** ikhowudi yemeko
* **Retry-After** header ebonisa ukuba kuphinde kuzanywe nini
* **Ukulala kwe-tarpit okungahleliwe** (imizuzwana eyi-1–5 ngokungagqibekanga, kuvalelwe ngokuqinileyo kwimizuzwana eyi-15) ukucothisa abahlaseli

### Uqwalaselo {#configuration}

#### Ixesha le-Tarpit {#tarpit-timing}

Lawula ukulibaziseka okungahleliwe okusetyenziswa kwizicelo ezilinganiselwe ngezinga:

* **`cap_rate_limit_tarpit_min`** — Ubude obuncinane bokulala ngemizuzwana (okungagqibekanga: 1)
* **`cap_rate_limit_tarpit_max`** — Ubude obukhulu bokulala ngemizuzwana (okungagqibekanga: 5, kuvalelwe ngokuqinileyo ku-15)

Ezi setingi ziyafumaneka ku-**Ultimate Multisite → Settings → Captcha**.

### Ukwandiswa {#extensibility}

#### Isihluzo Soluhlu Olumhlophe lwe-IP {#ip-whitelist-filter}

Khulula uluhlu lwe-IP oluthembekileyo ekulinganisweni kwezinga:

```php
add_filter( 'wu_cap_rate_limit_whitelist_ip', function( $is_whitelisted, $ip ) {
    // Whitelist your monitoring service
    if ( $ip === '203.0.113.42' ) {
        return true;
    }
    return $is_whitelisted;
}, 10, 2 );
```

#### Isenzo Sangaphambi Kokuvala {#pre-block-action}

Qhuba ilojikhi elungiselelweyo ngokukhawuleza phambi kokuba ukuvalwa komlinganiselo wezinga kuthunyelwe:

```php
add_action( 'wu_cap_rate_limit_will_block', function( $ip, $surface ) {
    // Log the block, notify admins, or update custom metrics
    error_log( "Rate limit triggered for $ip on $surface" );
}, 10, 2 );
```

## Ukufunyanwa kwe-IP Yeklayenti & Ii-Proxy Ezithembekileyo {#client-ip-detection--trusted-proxies}

Ultimate Multisite: Captcha v1.5.0 yazisa ukufunyanwa kwe-client-IP okuxhathisa ukukhohliswa. Indlela ye-`Captcha_Core::get_client_ip()` ngoku ngumthombo omnye wenyaniso wokunxulumanisa i-IP yondwendwe kuyo yonke:

* Izitshixo zeebhakethi zomlinganiselo wezinga
* Umboneleli we-Captcha `remoteip` (reCAPTCHA, hCaptcha `siteverify`)
* Iihashi ze-IP zezibalo

### Imodeli Yokuthemba {#trust-model}

I-plugin inyanzelisa uluhlu olungqongqo lokuthemba i-IP:

1. **REMOTE_ADDR** — Ihlala ithembekile (i-IP yeqabane elikufutshane)
2. **CF-Connecting-IP** — Ihlonitshwa kuphela xa:
   - Iqabane elikufutshane lingaphakathi koluhlu lwangoku lwe-IP ye-Cloudflare
   - `cap_trust_cloudflare_headers` ivuliwe (okungagqibekanga: OFF)
3. **X-Forwarded-For** — Ihlonitshwa kuphela xa:
   - Iqabane elikufutshane likuluhlu lwakho lwe-trusted-proxy oluqwalaselwe ngumlawuli
   - I-header ihlalutywa ukusuka ekunene ukuya ekhohlo, kutsitywa ii-hop ezithembekileyo/ze-Cloudflare

### Uqwalaselo {#configuration-1}

#### Ukuthembela kwi-Header ye-Cloudflare {#cloudflare-header-trust}

Vumela ukuthembela kwi-header ye-Cloudflare `CF-Connecting-IP`:

* **`cap_trust_cloudflare_headers`** — Seta ku-ON ukuze ungene ekuthembeleni ku-`CF-Connecting-IP`
  - Okungagqibekanga: OFF (ikhubaziwe)
  - I-plugin iza ne-snapshot ye-Cloudflare CIDR ehlanganisiweyo
  - Uluhlu lwe-CIDR luhlaziywa ngeveki nge-wp-cron
  - Ugcino oluhlanganisiweyo luyasetyenziswa ukuba uhlaziyo luyasilela

#### Uqwalaselo lwe-Proxy Ethembekileyo {#trusted-proxy-configuration}

Chaza ii-proxy zakho zangaphambili kunye nee-load-balancer:

* **`cap_trusted_proxies`** — I-textarea yee-CIDR okanye ii-IP ezingenanto (enye kumgca ngamnye)
  - Izimvo zivumelekile (imigca eqala ngo-`#`)
  - Ngaphandle kwesi setingi, `X-Forwarded-For` iyahoywa nokuba umlinganisi wezinga uvuliwe
  - Umzekelo:
    ```
    # Our load balancer
    192.0.2.0/24

    # Backup proxy
    198.51.100.50
    ```

#### Ukufunyanwa Ngokuzenzekelayo Xa Kuvulwa Okokuqala {#first-enable-auto-detection}

Xa uqala ukuvula umlinganisi wezinga, i-plugin ifumanisa imeko yakho enokwenzeka ye-Cloudflare ne-proxy kwaye ibonise isaziso solawulo esicofwa kube kanye esithi "Sebenzisa iisetingi ezifunyenweyo".

* I-plugin **ayisoze ibhale ngaphezulu** amaxabiso akho agciniweyo
* Ukuba itrafikhi elandelayo ibonisa ukuba uqwalaselo lwakho alusangqamani nenyani (umz., Cloudflare itshintshe uluhlu lwe-CIDR), isaziso sokungangqinelani esingenakucinywa sibonisa uhlaziyo olucetyiswayo

### Kutheni Oku Kubalulekile {#why-this-matters}

Imodeli engqongqo yokuthemba i-IP ivala indlela ebalulekileyo yokukhohlisa: izicelo ezithe ngqo kwiseva yemvelaphi eziphethe i-header ye-`CF-Connecting-IP` eyenziwe ngobuxoki ngaphambili beziza kufakwa kwibhakethi nge-IP ekhohlisiweyo endaweni yeqabane lokwenyani. Oku kubaluleke ngakumbi kwi:

* **Ukulinganiselwa kwezinga** — Abahlaseli abanakudlula imida ngokukhohlisa ii-IP
* **Izibalo** — Iimetriki zakho zohlaselo zibonisa ii-IP zondwendwe zokwenyani, hayi ezenziwe ngobuxoki
* **Ukuqinisekiswa kwe-Captcha** — Ababoneleli bafumana i-IP yondwendwe echanekileyo kuvavanyo lomngcipheko
