# Amatikiti Enkxaso

**Amatikiti Enkxaso** sisongezo esinamandla se-Ultimate Multisite esongeza inkqubo ebanzi yamatikiti enkxaso kwinethiwekhi yakho ye-WordPress. Silungele amaqonga e-SaaS, ababoneleli be-WaaS (Website as a Service), kunye neenethiwekhi ze-multisite ezifuna ukubonelela ngenkxaso yabathengi kumanqanaba omabini: inethiwekhi kunye nesayithi nganye.

## Kutheni Ukhetha Amatikiti Enkxaso? {#why-choose-support-tickets}

* **Inkxaso Yamanqanaba Amaninzi**: Abalawuli benethiwekhi baphatha amatikiti achaphazela yonke inethiwekhi ngelixa abanini beesayithi belawula inkxaso yabathengi babo
* **Ukuhamba Komsebenzi Kobungcali**: Ulawulo olupheleleyo lomjikelo wobomi betikiti olunemigangatho ephambili, iimeko, kunye nokwabiwa
* **Amava Omthengi**: Ujongano oluhle, oluphendulayo, olwenza kube lula kubathengi ukufumana uncedo
* **Ikhuselekile & Ithembekile**: Yakhiwe ngemigaqo engcono yokhuseleko lwe-WordPress kwaye yaphuculwa ukusebenza kwedatabase
* **Udibaniso Olungenamthungo**: Isebenza ngokugqibeleleyo nolawulo lwabathengi kunye nolweesayithi lwe-Ultimate Multisite

## Iimpawu Ezibalulekileyo {#key-features}

**Ulawulo Olupheleleyo Lwamatikiti**

* Inkqubo yokunika amanani awodwa kumatikiti ukuze kube lula ukubhekisa kuwo
* Amanqanaba obaluleko anokulungiselelwa (Phantsi, Eqhelekileyo, Ephezulu, Engxamisekileyo)
* Ukuhamba komsebenzi okubanzi kweemeko (Livuliwe, Liyaqhubeka, Lilindile, Lisonjululiwe, Livaliwe)
* Ukuhlelwa kwamatikiti ngokweendidi (Ngokubanzi, Ezobugcisa, Intlawulo, Isicelo Sophawu, Ingxelo Yempazamo)
* Yabela amatikiti kwii-arhente zenkxaso ngokhetho lokwabela ngokuzenzekelayo

**Iincoko Ezinemisonto**

* Ukulandelelana okupheleleyo kwencoko yeempendulo zamatikiti
* Isitayile esahlukileyo seempendulo zabasebenzi kunye nezabathengi
* Amanqaku abucala abasebenzi abangabonwa ngabathengi
* Ujongano lwexesha lokwenyani oluxhaswa yi-AJAX
* Umhleli wombhalo wale mihla oneenketho zokufomatha

**Izinamathiselo Zeefayile**

* Ukulayishwa kweefayile okukhuselekileyo okunenkxaso yokutsala nokulahla
* Izithintelo zohlobo lwefayile nobungakanani ezinokulungiselelwa
* Ukhuseleko oluphambili ngokubhaqwa kweefayile eziyingozi
* Ukukhutshelwa okukhuselweyo ngolawulo lokufikelela

**Izaziso Ezihlakaniphile**

* Izilumkiso ze-imeyile ezizenzekelayo zamatikiti amatsha, iimpendulo, kunye notshintsho lweemeko
* Iitempleyithi ze-imeyile ezinokwenziwa ngokwezifiso
* Izaziso ezisekelwe kwiindima zabasebenzi nabathengi
* Iivariyabhile zeethempleyithi eziguqukayo zemiyalezo eyenzelwe umntu

**Ujongano Lolawulo Olunamandla**

* Uluhlu oluphambili lwamatikiti olunokuhlelwa nokuhluzwa
* Ukuhlela ngokukhawuleza imeko, ubaluleko, kunye nokwabiwa
* Izenzo ezininzi ngaxeshanye zolawulo olusebenzayo
* Dashboard yeenkcukacha-manani enemilinganiselo yokusebenza
* Iphaneli ebanzi yeesethingi

**Iphothali Entle Yabathengi**

* Ujongano olucocekileyo noluphendulayo kuzo zonke izixhobo
* Uyilo oluqala ngeselfowuni
* Ukukhangela nokuhluzwa okuphambili
* Umhleli wale mihla onebar yezixhobo zokufomatha
* Iifomu zokungenisa amatikiti ekulula ukuzisebenzisa

**Udibaniso Oluguquguqukayo**

* Sebenzisa iikhowudi ezimfutshane okanye iziqalelo ze-UI ze-Ultimate Multisite
* Bonisa iifomu zamatikiti kunye noluhlu naphi na kwisayithi yakho
* Isebenza ngokungenamthungo nesakhiwo senethiwekhi ye-multisite
* Iyandiswa ngee-hooks kunye nee-filters

## Ilungele {#perfect-for}

* Amaqonga e-SaaS afuna inkxaso yabathengi
* Ababoneleli be-WaaS abalawula iisayithi ezininzi zabaxumi
* Iisayithi zobulungu ezinenkxaso enamanqanaba
* Abalawuli beenethiwekhi abalawula iibrendi ezininzi
* Abanini beesayithi ababonelela ngenkonzo yabathengi

## Ufakelo {#installation}

1. Qinisekisa ukuba iplagin ye-Ultimate Multisite ifakiwe kwaye iyasebenza kwinethiwekhi yakho ye-WordPress
2. Layisha iifayile zesongezo kwidirekthri yakho ethi `/wp-content/plugins/`
3. Yenza iplagin isebenze kwinethiwekhi ngemenyu ethi 'Plugins' kwi-WordPress
4. Yiya ku-Network Admin > Support Tickets ukuze ulungiselele iisethingi zakho

## Imibuzo Ebuzwa Rhoqo {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Ngaba oku kufuna i-Ultimate Multisite? {#does-this-require-ultimate-multisite}

Ewe, esi songezo sifuna ukuba i-Ultimate Multisite ifakiwe kwaye isebenza. Sidibana nzulu neempawu zolawulo lwabathengi kunye neesayithi ze-Ultimate Multisite.

## Ngaba abanini beesayithi bangaphatha inkxaso yabathengi babo? {#can-site-owners-handle-their-own-customer-support}

Ngokuqinisekileyo! Abanini beesayithi bangasebenzisa inkqubo yamatikiti ukubonelela ngenkxaso kubathengi babo, ngelixa abalawuli benethiwekhi bengaphatha amatikiti enkxaso achaphazela yonke inethiwekhi.

## Abathengi bawangenisa njani amatikiti? {#how-do-customers-submit-tickets}

Ungongeza iifomu zokungenisa amatikiti naphi na kwisayithi yakho usebenzisa iikhowudi ezimfutshane okanye iziqalelo ze-UI ze-Ultimate Multisite. Abathengi bagcwalisa nje ifomu baze bangenise isicelo sabo.

## Ndingazenza ngokwezifiso iimeko nobaluleko lwamatikiti? {#can-i-customize-the-ticket-statuses-and-priorities}

Ewe! Ungalungiselela ngokupheleleyo amanqanaba obaluleko, iimeko, kunye neentlobo zamatikiti kwiisethingi zolawulo lwenethiwekhi ukuze zihambelane nokuhamba komsebenzi wenkxaso yakho.

## Ngaba ixhasa izinamathiselo zeefayile? {#does-it-support-file-attachments}

Ewe, bobabini abathengi nabasebenzi banokuncamathisela iifayile kumatikiti nakwiimpendulo. Unolawulo olupheleleyo kwiintlobo zeefayile ezivunyelweyo kunye nemida yobungakanani ukuze kugcinwe ukhuseleko.

## Izaziso ziphathwa njani? {#how-are-notifications-handled}

Inkqubo ithumela ngokuzenzekelayo izaziso ze-imeyile zamatikiti amatsha, iimpendulo, kunye notshintsho lweemeko. Ungazenza ngokwezifiso zonke iitempleyithi ze-imeyile kwiisethingi.

## Ndingawabela amatikiti kwii-arhente ezithile zenkxaso? {#can-i-assign-tickets-to-specific-support-agents}

Ewe, ungawabela ngesandla amatikiti kwii-arhente okanye uvule ukwabiwa okuzenzekelayo ukuze amatikiti asasazwe ngokuzenzekelayo.

## Ngaba kukho amanqaku abucala abasebenzi? {#are-there-private-notes-for-staff}

Ewe, amalungu abasebenzi anokongeza iimpendulo zabucala ezibonwa kuphela ngamanye amalungu abasebenzi, hayi ngabathengi.

## Ngaba ilungele iselfowuni? {#is-it-mobile-friendly}

Ngokuqinisekileyo! Ujongano lonke lwakhiwe ngendlela eqala ngeselfowuni kwaye lusebenza kakuhle kuzo zonke izixhobo.

## Ndingabonisa uluhlu lweamatikiti kunye neefomu ndisebenzisa iikhowudi ezimfutshane? {#can-i-display-ticket-lists-and-forms-using-shortcodes}

Ewe! Iikhowudi ezimfutshane ezifumanekayo:
* `[wu_support_tickets]` - Bonisa uluhlu lwamatikiti omthengi
* `[wu_support_ticket_form]` - Bonisa ifomu yokungenisa itikiti elitsha
* `[wu_support_ticket_view ticket_id="123"]` - Bonisa itikiti elithile

## Zeziphi iintlobo zeefayile ezinokulayishwa? {#what-file-types-can-be-uploaded}

Ungalungiselela iintlobo zeefayile ezivunyelweyo kwiisethingi. Inkqubo iquka ukuskena kokhuseleko okuphambili ukuze ikhusele kwiifayile eziyingozi.

## Ndingayithumela ngaphandle idatha yamatikiti? {#can-i-export-ticket-data}

Ewe, esi songezo siquka amandla okuthumela ngaphandle idatha yamatikiti kunye neenkcukacha-manani.
