# Iinyani ngoChuck Norris {#chuck-norris-facts}

Yongeza uburharha obuncinane kwimisebenzi yakho yemihla ngemihla yolawulo lwe-WordPress nge-addon **Ultimate Multisite - Chuck Norris Facts**. Xa ivuliwe, uza kubona inyani engakhethwanga kwangaphambili ngoChuck Norris iboniswa kwikona ephezulu yasekunene yephepha ngalinye lolawulo.

Ifanelekile ukuzisa uncumo kwiqela lakho, ukunciphisa ubunzima bomoya ngexesha leeseshoni zolawulo ezinde, okanye nje ukubhiyozela ubungangamsha obudumileyo obunguChuck Norris.

## Kutheni Iinyani ngoChuck Norris? {#why-chuck-norris-facts}

* **Yandisa Umdla Weqela**: Yongeza into eyonwabisayo kulawulo lwenethiwekhi yakho
* **Qalisa Incoko**: Isiqalo esihle sencoko kumalungu amatsha eqela
* **Ukwandisa Imveliso**: Ukuhleka ngokukhawuleza kunokuphucula ukugxila nemveliso
* **Uburharha Obungaphelelwa Lixesha**: Iinyani ngoChuck Norris azigugi

## Iimpawu Ezibalulekileyo {#key-features}

**Iinyani Ezidumileyo ezingaphezu kwe-110**
Ingqokelela ebanzi yamahlaya neenyani zakudala ngoChuck Norris ezibhiyozela intsomi ngokwayo.

**Umboniso Ongaphazamisiyo**
Iinyani zivela kwibar yolawulo ngendlela engaphazamisi ukuhamba komsebenzi wakho.

**Ilungele Ukuguqulelwa**
Zonke iinyani zisongelwe kwimisebenzi yokuguqulela, nto leyo eyenza kube lula ukuzenza zifanele ulwimi lwakho okanye ukudala iinyani ezenzelwe iqela lakho.

**Iphendula Ngokupheleleyo**
Iinyani ziboniswa ngokufanelekileyo kwizikrini zekhompyutha, tablet, kunye neefowuni eziphathwayo - uChuck Norris uyamangalisa kuzo zonke izixhobo.

**Inkxaso ye-RTL**
Inkxaso epheleleyo yeelwimi ezibhalwa ukusuka ekunene ukuya ekhohlo kuba uChuck Norris udlula imiqobo yolwimi.

**Iyahambelana noMhleli Weebhloko**
Iinyani zifihlwa ngobukrelekrele kumhleli weebhloko ukunqanda ukungqubana kojongano.

**Ijolise Kufikeleleko**
Iquka umbhalo ofanelekileyo wesifundi-skrini ukuze ihambelane ngokupheleleyo neemfuno zofikeleleko.

## Ifanelekile Ku {#perfect-for}

* Abalawuli benethiwekhi ye-multisite abafuna ukongeza ubuntu
* Amaqela ophuhliso axabisa uburharha obuhle
* Nabani na ofuna idosi yemihla ngemihla yobungangamsha bukaChuck Norris
* Iisayithi ezifuna ukudala indawo yokusebenza eyonwabisayo nenethemba

## Ufakelo {#installation}

1. Qinisekisa ukuba i-plugin ye-Ultimate Multisite ifakiwe kwaye ivuliwe kwinethiwekhi yakho ye-WordPress
2. Layisha iifayile ze-addon kulawulo lwakho lwe-`/wp-content/plugins/`
3. Vula i-plugin kwinethiwekhi ngokusebenzisa imenyu ethi 'Plugins' kwi-WordPress
4. Yonwabela iinyani ezikhethwa ngokungacwangciswanga ngoChuck Norris kwiphepha ngalinye lolawulo!

Akukho lungiselelo lufunekayo - yivule nje uze uncume!

## Imibuzo Ebuzwa Rhoqo {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Ngaba oku kufuna Ultimate Multisite? {#does-this-require-ultimate-multisite}

Nangona iyilwe njenge-addon ye-Ultimate Multisite, i-plugin isebenza ngokuzimela kwaye inokusebenza nakuluphi na ufakelo lwe-WordPress.

## Ndingongeza ezam iinyani? {#can-i-add-my-own-facts}

Ewe! Iinyani zilungele ukuguqulelwa. Ungadala ifayile yakho ye-`.po` eneinyani ezenzelwe wena uze uyibeke kulawulo lwe-`/lang`. Okanye ungahluza uluhlu lwezinyani ngokwenkqubo.

## Ngaba oku kuyacothisa ulawulo lwam? {#does-this-slow-down-my-admin}

Hayi kwaphela! I-plugin ilula kakhulu kwaye ayinampembelelo ekusebenzeni. Ibonisa umbhalo kuphela - akukho minxeba ye-API yangaphandle okanye inkqubo enzima.

## Ndingayicima kumaphepha athile? {#can-i-disable-it-on-certain-pages}

Iinyani zifihlwa ngokuzenzekelayo kumaphepha apho zinokuphazamisa khona (njengomhleli weebhloko). Unokusebenzisa ii-hook ze-WordPress ukwenza ngokwezifiso apho iinyani zivela khona.

## Ngaba iinyani zilungele bonke abaphulaphuli? {#are-the-facts-appropriate-for-all-audiences}

Ewe! Zonke iinyani zicocekile, zinoburharha obulungele usapho, kwaye zibhiyozela isikhundla sikaChuck Norris esidumileyo.

## Ndingaziguqulela iinyani kolunye ulwimi? {#can-i-translate-the-facts-to-another-language}

Ngokuqinisekileyo! Zonke iinyani zisebenzisa imisebenzi yokuguqulela ye-WordPress. Dala ifayile yokuguqulela yolwimi lwakho uze uyibeke kulawulo lwe-`/lang`. Text domain: `ultimate-multisite-chuck-norris-facts`

## Ngaba isebenza ne-multisite? {#does-it-work-with-multisite}

Ewe! Yenzelwe ngokukodwa iinethiwekhi ze-multisite kwaye inokwenziwa isebenze kwinethiwekhi.
