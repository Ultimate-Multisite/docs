### Inguqulelo 1.1.0 - Ikhutshwe nge-2025-12-31 {#version-110---released-on-2025-12-31}
* Entsha: Inkxaso yokuvumelanisa zonke iintlobo zezithuba (izithuba, amaphepha, iintlobo zezithuba ezenzelwe wena)
* Entsha: Ukuhluza uhlobo lwesithuba kwinyathelo lokukhetha umxholo
* Entsha: Inyathelo lokukhetha isayithi yomthombo - vumelanisa nakweyiphi na isayithi, hayi isayithi ephambili kuphela
* Entsha: Ukukhethwa kwangaphambili kwesayithi engumntwana kwiisayithi zeetemplate
* Entsha: Ukukhutshwa kwemidiya kwi-`post_content` kuzo zonke iintlobo zezithuba
* Entsha: Ukuvumelanisa i-taxonomy (iindidi, iithegi, ii-taxonomy ezenzelwe wena)
* Kutshintshiwe: Igama litshintshiwe ukusuka ku-"Elementor Template Sync" laba ngu-"Content Sync"
* Kutshintshiwe: Inyathelo elithi "Select Templates" lithiywe ngokutsha laba ngu-"Select Data"
* Kuphuculwe: Ikhowudi ethile ye-Elementor ngoku isebenza kuphela kuhlobo lwesithuba `elementor_library`
