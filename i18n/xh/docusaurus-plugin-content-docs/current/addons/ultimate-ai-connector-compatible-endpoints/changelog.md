## Version 2.0.0 — Ikhutshwe nge-2026-04-24

* Entsha: Inkxaso yababoneleli abaninzi — qwalasela iziphelo ze-AI ezininzi uze uthumele izicelo ngokubuyela ngokuzenzekelayo phakathi kwababoneleli.
* Lungiso: Udibaniso lwe-SDK lwababoneleli abaninzi olunee-ID zomboneleli ezichanekileyo, ii-URL zobhaliso, kunye nokumiselwa komda kwesihluzo se-HTTP kumboneleli ngamnye.
* Lungiso: Ikhadi elitsha lomboneleli ngoku liyazandisa ngokuzenzekelayo xa longeziwe; ukophulwa kwe-cache yeskripthi kuhlaziyo lweplagi.
* Lungiso: I-namespace yeklasi yomboneleli eguqukayo ye-eval() kunye nokuziphatha kokuzandisa ngokuzenzekelayo kubaboneleli abasandul’ ukongezwa.
* Lungiso: Isebenzisa amacandelo azinzileyo e-Card/CardBody/CardHeader/CardDivider (awasekho kuvavanyo) ukuze ihambelane ne-WordPress 6.9+.
* Lungiso: I-DragHandle engachazwanga ithathelwe indawo yi-icon ye-unicode grip ukuze kutsalwe umboneleli kulungiswe ulungelelwano.
* Lungiso: Isicelo se-HTTP esivimbayo esasiqalisa kulo lonke ulayisho lwephepha sisusiwe.
* Kuphuculwe: Iinkqubo zomsebenzi ze-GitHub Actions zinyuselwe kwi-Node.js 24.
