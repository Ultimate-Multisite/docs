# Ultimate AI Connector yeZziphelo Ezihambelanayo

I-Ultimate AI Connector yeZziphelo Ezihambelanayo idibanisa ii-API zababoneleli be-AI abahambelana ne-OpenAI nenethiwekhi yakho ye-WordPress multisite. Qwalasela ababoneleli be-AI abaninzi — ngamnye ene-URL yesiphelo sakhe, isitshixo se-API, kunye nemodeli engagqibekanga — uze uhambise izicelo ngokubuyela ngokuzenzekelayo komnye umboneleli xa umboneleli engafumaneki.

## Iimpawu Ezibalulekileyo

- **Inkxaso yababoneleli abaninzi**: Yongeza amakhadi ababoneleli be-AI abaninzi, ngalinye line-URL yesiphelo salo, isitshixo se-API, kunye nemodeli engagqibekanga
- **Tsala-ukuze-ulungelelanise ngokutsha**: Lungisa ababoneleli ngokokubaluleka usebenzisa isibambo sokubamba kwikhadi ngalinye; izicelo zisebenzisa ababoneleli ngokolandelelwano
- **Ukuhanjiswa kokubuyela komnye ngokuzenzekelayo**: Ukuba umboneleli ophambili akafumaneki, izicelo zibuyela ngokuzenzekelayo kumboneleli olandelayo oqwalaselweyo
- **Uqwalaselo lomnikezeli ngamnye**: Umboneleli ngamnye uqwalaselwa ngokuzimeleyo ngeenkcukacha zakhe zokungena kunye nokhetho lwemodeli
- **I-UI yeesetingi esekelwe kumakhadi**: Yakhelwe kumacandelo azinzileyo e-WordPress 6.9+ Card (CardBody, CardHeader, CardDivider)
- **Ukwandiswa ngokuzenzekelayo xa wongeza**: Amakhadi amatsha ababoneleli ayanda ngokuzenzekelayo ukuze ugcwalise iinkcukacha kwangoko
- **Ukuphelisa i-cache yeskripthi**: Izikripthi zababoneleli zikhutshwa kwi-cache xa plugin ihlaziywa ukuqinisekisa ukuba inguqulelo yamva nje iyalayishwa

## Iimfuno

- WordPress 5.3 okanye ngaphezulu
- PHP 7.4 okanye ngaphezulu
- Ultimate Multisite plugin (esebenzayo)
- Isiphelo se-API esihambelana ne-OpenAI (esibanjwe nguwe okanye ngumntu wesithathu)

## Ufakelo

1. Layisha iifayile ze-addon kulawulo lwakho lwe-`/wp-content/plugins/`
2. Vula i-plugin kwimenyu ethi 'Plugins' ku-WordPress
3. Yiya ku-**Ultimate Multisite → AI Connector** ukuze uqwalasele i-addon

## Uqwalaselo

### Useto Lwaboneleli Abaninzi

Isikrini soqwalaselo sisebenzisa uyilo olusekelwe kumakhadi apho ikhadi ngalinye limele umboneleli omnye we-AI. Ungongeza ababoneleli abaninzi kangangoko ufuna.

#### Ukongeza Umboneleli

1. Yiya ku-**Ultimate Multisite → AI Connector** kumlawuli wenethiwekhi
2. Cofa **Yongeza Umboneleli**
3. Ikhadi elitsha lomboneleli liyavela kwaye landa ngokuzenzekelayo
4. Gcwalisa iinkcukacha zomboneleli:
   - **Igama Lomboneleli** — ileyibhile yokuchonga lo mboneleli (umz. "OpenAI", "Mistral", "Local Ollama")
   - **I-URL Yesiphelo** — i-URL esisiseko ye-API ehambelana ne-OpenAI (umz. `https://api.openai.com/v1`)
   - **Isitshixo se-API** — isitshixo sakho se-API salo mboneleli
   - **Imodeli Engagqibekanga** — isichongi semodeli esiza kusetyenziswa ngokungagqibekanga kulo mboneleli (umz. `gpt-4o`, `mistral-small`)
5. Cofa **Gcina Iisetingi**

#### Ukususa Umboneleli

Cofa iqhosha elithi **Susa** nakweliphi na ikhadi lomboneleli ukuze ulicime. Gcina iisetingi ukuze usebenzise utshintsho.

#### Ukulungelelanisa Ababoneleli Ngokutsha

Tsala i-ayikhoni yokubamba (⣿) kwicala lasekhohlo lalo naliphi na ikhadi lomboneleli ukuze ulilungelelanise ngokutsha. Ulandelelwano lumisela ukubaluleka kokubuyela komnye: izicelo zithunyelwa kumboneleli wokuqala zize zibuyele kubaboneleli abalandelayo ukuba isicelo siyasilela.

### Ukuhanjiswa Kokubuyela Komnye

Xa isicelo kumboneleli ophambili sisilela — ngenxa yokuphelelwa lixesha, umda wesantya, okanye ukuphazamiseka kwenkonzo — isidibanisi sizama kwakhona ngokuzenzekelayo ngomboneleli olandelayo kuluhlu. Oku kuqhubeka kuluhlu de umboneleli aphumelele okanye bonke ababoneleli bazanywe.

| Imeko | Ukuziphatha |
|----------|-----------|
| Umboneleli ophambili uyaphendula | Isicelo sinikezelwa njengesiqhelo |
| Umboneleli ophambili uyasilela | Isicelo sizanywa kwakhona ngomboneleli olandelayo |
| Bonke ababoneleli bayasilela | Impazamo ibuyiselwa kumbizi |

Ukubuyela komnye kubonakala ngokungacacanga kwi-plugin okanye theme ebizayo — bafumana impendulo ephumeleleyo okanye impazamo yokugqibela, kungakhathaliseki ukuba bangaphi ababoneleli abazanyiweyo.

### Isalathiso Soqwalaselo Lomboneleli

| Indawo | Iyafuneka | Inkcazo |

| I-URL Yesiphelo | Ewe | I-URL esisiseko ye-API ehambelana ne-OpenAI |
| Isitshixo se-API | Ewe | Isitshixo soqinisekiso salo mboneleli |

## Ukusombulula Iingxaki

### Akukho baboneleli badwelisiweyo

Ukuba uluhlu lwababoneleli alunanto emva kokugcina, qinisekisa ukuba:

- Ucofe **Gcina Iisetingi** emva kokongeza ababoneleli
- Isikhangeli sakho asithintelanga iphepha leesetingi ekugcineni (jonga i-console yesikhangeli kwiimpazamo ze-JavaScript)
- I-plugin iyasebenza kwaye ukwisikrini somlawuli wenethiwekhi

### Izicelo ziyasilela kubo bonke ababoneleli

- Qinisekisa ukuba i-URL yesiphelo somboneleli ngamnye iyafikeleleka kwiseva yakho (hayi kwisikhangeli sakho kuphela)
- Jonga ukuba isitshixo ngasinye se-API sisebenza kwaye asiphelelwanga lixesha
- Phonononga imeko yomda wesantya kunye ne-quota yomboneleli kwi-dashboard yabo
- Vumela ukurekhodwa kwe-debug ye-WordPress (`WP_DEBUG_LOG`) ukuze kubanjwe imiyalezo yeempazamo eneenkcukacha evela kwisidibanisi

### Umboneleli akasetyenziswa ekubuyeleni komnye

Ukutsala-ukuze-ulungelelanise ngokutsha kutshintsha ulandelelwano lokubuyela komnye. Qinisekisa ukuba umboneleli olindele ukuba afumane izicelo zokubuyela komnye ubekwe ngokuchanekileyo kuluhlu kwaye i-URL yesiphelo sakhe kunye nesitshixo se-API zichanekile.

### Ikhadi elitsha lomboneleli alandi ngokuzenzekelayo

Ukwandiswa ngokuzenzekelayo kufuna ukuba i-JavaScript ivulwe kwisikhangeli sakho. Ukuba ikhadi alandi xa ucofa, jonga i-console yesikhangeli ukuze ubone iimpazamo. Imixholo yekhadi isoloko ifikeleleka ngokucofa isihloko sekhadi nokuba ukwandiswa ngokuzenzekelayo akuqalisi.
