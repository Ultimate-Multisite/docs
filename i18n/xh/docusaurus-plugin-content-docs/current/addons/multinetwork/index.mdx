# Multi-Network {#multi-network}

I-addon ye-Ultimate Multisite: Multinetwork yandisa iqonga lakho le-Ultimate Multisite ngomsebenzi onamandla we-multinetwork. Le addon ivumela abathengi bakho ukuba bathenge baze balawule iinethiwekhi ezipheleleyo ze-WordPress multisite, hayi iisayithi ezizimeleyo kuphela.

## Iimpawu Ezibalulekileyo {#key-features}

* **Ukunikezelwa Kwenethiwekhi**: Yenza uze uqwalasele ngokuzenzekelayo iinethiwekhi ezipheleleyo ze-WordPress multisite zabathengi bakho
* **Iitemplate Zenethiwekhi**: Vumela abathengi bakhangele, bajonge kwangaphambili, baze bakhethe itemplate yenethiwekhi esele yakhiwe ngaphambi kokubhalisa
* **Ulawulo Lwenethiwekhi**: Amandla apheleleyo olawulo eemeko ze-multinetwork
* **Iinethiwekhi Ezizimeleyo**: Inethiwekhi nganye yomthengi isebenza ngokuzimeleyo ngeetheyibhile zayo zedatabase noqwalaselo lwayo
* **I-SSO Phakathi Kweenethiwekhi**: Nika abathengi ukufikelela okungenamthungo kwiisub-network zabo ngokuqinisekiswa ngekhonkco lomlingo
* **Uyilo Olunokwandiswa**: Phatha iinethiwekhi ezininzi ngokufanelekileyo ngolwabiwo lwezixhobo oluphuculweyo
* **Umdibaniso**: Idibana ngaphandle komthungo neenkqubo zobhaliso nezokuhlawulisa ze-Ultimate Multisite

## Iimeko Zokusetyenziswa {#use-cases}

* **Ukusingathwa kwe-White Label**: Nika abathengi iinethiwekhi zabo ze-multisite ezinebrand yabo
* **Izisombululo Zee-arhente**: Vumela ii-arhente zilawule iinethiwekhi ezininzi zabathengi zisuka kwiqonga elinye
* **Ukusasazwa Kwamashishini Amakhulu**: Xhasa imibutho emikhulu eneemfuno ezintsonkothileyo zeenethiwekhi ezininzi
* **Amaqonga Emfundo**: Yenza iinethiwekhi ezahlukileyo zezikolo okanye amasebe ahlukeneyo

## Iimfuno {#requirements}

* Ultimate Multisite 2.0.0 okanye ngaphezulu
* Ufakelo lwe-WordPress Multisite
* PHP 7.4 okanye ngaphezulu
* MySQL 5.6 okanye ngaphezulu

## Ufakelo {#installation}

1. Layisha iifayile ze-plugin kwidirectory ethi `/wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite-multinetwork`
2. Vula i-plugin ngesikrini se-'Plugins' kwi-WordPress
3. Qwalasela iisetingi ze-multinetwork ngo-Ultimate Multisite → Settings

## Iitemplate Zenethiwekhi {#network-templates}

Inguqulelo 1.3.0 yongeza ukuhamba kokufunyanwa nokukhethwa kwetemplate kubathengi abathenga iinethiwekhi ezintsha. Sebenzisa iitemplate zenethiwekhi xa ufuna inethiwekhi entsha nganye yomthengi iqale kwisakhiwo esilungisiweyo, uyilo, ithala leencwadi lomxholo, okanye uqwalaselo olujolise kwicandelo elithile.

### Umjongi wangaphambili wetemplate {#template-previewer}

Umjongi wangaphambili wetemplate yenethiwekhi uvumela abathengi bakhangele iitemplate ezikhoyo ngaphambi kwe-checkout. Itemplate nganye ingavulwa kwipaneli yokujonga kwangaphambili ephilayo, ukuze abathengi bathelekise ubume nomxholo ngaphambi kokuba bathenge inethiwekhi.

Sebenzisa umjongi wangaphambili ukunceda abathengi:

* Bahlole inkangeleko nesakhiwo setemplate nganye yenethiwekhi ekhoyo
* Bathelekise iitemplate ezininzi bengashiyi ukuhamba kokuthenga
* Bakhethe itemplate ehambelana kakuhle kakhulu nemeko yabo yokusetyenziswa ngaphambi kokubhalisa

Gcina amagama neenkcazelo zeetemplate zilungele abathengi. Umjongi wangaphambili uluncedo kakhulu xa abathengi benokuqonda imeko yokusetyenziswa ecetyiweyo, efana neenethiwekhi zee-arhente, iinethiwekhi zezikolo, iinethiwekhi zeefranchise, okanye iibhanti zokusingathwa kwe-white-label.

### Umkhethi wetemplate kwi-checkout {#template-picker-at-checkout}

Umkhethi wetemplate unokongezwa ekuhambeni kwe-checkout yokubhalisa njengentsimi. Xa uqwalaselwe, abathengi bakhetha itemplate yenethiwekhi abayifunayo ngexesha le-checkout, kwaye itemplate ekhethiweyo isetyenziswa xa inethiwekhi yabo entsha inikezelwa.

Ukusebenzisa umkhethi ekuhambeni kwe-checkout:

1. Lungiselela iinethiwekhi ofuna ukuzinikezela njengeetemplate.
2. Vula intsimi yokukhetha itemplate ye-Multinetwork kwifom efanelekileyo yokubhalisa okanye ye-checkout.
3. Khetha ukuba zeziphi iitemplate ekufuneka zifumaneke kolo hambo lwe-checkout.
4. Papasha ukuhamba kwe-checkout uze uvavanye ukubhalisa ukuqinisekisa ukuba itemplate ekhethiweyo isetyenziswa kwinethiwekhi entsha.

Ukuba ukuhamba kwe-checkout kunika imveliso yenethiwekhi enye kuphela, umkhethi usenokuba luncedo njengenyathelo lokuqalisa elikhokelwayo. Kwiifaneli ezinezicwangciso ezininzi, bonisa kuphela iitemplate ezihambelana nemveliso okanye isicwangciso esikhethiweyo.

## I-Cross-Network Magic Link SSO {#cross-network-magic-link-sso}

I-Cross-network magic link SSO ivumela abathengi bahambe phakathi kwendawo yabo ephambili ye-akhawunti kunye neesub-network ngaphandle kokungena ngesandla kwakhona. Xa umthengi elandela ikhonkco lokufikelela phakathi kweenethiwekhi, Ultimate Multisite ivelisa ikhonkco lomlingo elinomda wexesha kwaye iqinisekisa umthengi kwinethiwekhi ekuyiwa kuyo.

Oku kuphucula amava kubathengi abalawula ngaphezu kwenethiwekhi enye okanye abafuna ukufikelela kwiinethiwekhi zeetemplate ezisandula ukuklonwa ngoko nangoko emva kokuthenga.

Uluhlu lokuhlola lomlawuli:

* Qinisekisa ukuba i-addon ye-Multinetwork iyasebenza kulo lonke ufakelo lwe-WordPress multisite.
* Qinisekisa ukuba abathengi banofikelelo oluchanekileyo lwenethiwekhi ngaphambi kokuba banikwe amakhonkco phakathi kweenethiwekhi.
* Gcina iidomain zenethiwekhi kunye needomain ezimaphuweyo ziqwalaselwe ngokuchanekileyo ukuze amakhonkco omlingo asombulule kwinethiwekhi ekujoliswe kuyo.
* Vavanya idashboard yomthengi kunye nawo nawaphi na amaqhosha okufikelela emva kwe-checkout emva kokutshintsha iisetingi zenethiwekhi, idomain, okanye checkout.

Amakhonkco omlingo kufuneka aphathwe njengamakhonkco okungena. Ayilelwe ukufikelela okungenamthungo kwabathengi kwaye akufanele athunyelwe kwabanye okanye asetyenziswe kwakhona ngaphandle kweseshoni ecetyiweyo.

## Imibuzo Ebuzwa Rhoqo {#frequently-asked-questions}

### Yintoni i-multinetwork? {#what-is-multinetwork}

I-multinetwork luphawu lwe-WordPress olukuvumela ukuba uqhube iinethiwekhi ezininzi ezizimeleyo ze-multisite kufakelo olunye lwe-WordPress.

### Oku kwahluke njani kwi-multisite eqhelekileyo? {#how-is-this-different-from-regular-multisite}

I-multisite eqhelekileyo ivumela iisayithi ezininzi ngaphakathi kwenethiwekhi enye. I-multinetwork ivumela iinethiwekhi ezininzi, nganye ineesayithi zayo, zonke ngaphakathi kofakelo olunye lwe-WordPress.

### Ngaba oku kufuna uqwalaselo olukhethekileyo lweseva? {#does-this-require-special-server-configuration}

Ewe, i-multinetwork ifuna ukuseta noqwalaselo oluthile lweseva. Nceda ujonge uxwebhu ukuze ufumane imiyalelo eneenkcukacha yokuseta.
