# Umthumeli Wesayithi

## Umxholo {#content}

Siyakuqonda imfuneko yokukwazi ukuhambisa iisayithi ezingaphantsi ngaphakathi kwenethiwekhi yakho uye kufakelo lwesayithi enye, nangokuphambeneyo, ukuba umthengi wakho agqibe ekubeni afudusele iwebhusayithi yakhe. Isongezelelo somthumeli nomngenisi wesayithi senza lo msebenzi ube lula. Esi sixhobo sithumela ngaphandle kwaye singenisa iisayithi sisebenzisa iifayile ze-zip ezinokusebenza kwakhona njengekopi yogcino.

## Iimpawu {#features}

* Thumela ngaphandle kwaye Ngenisa Iisayithi

* Ujongano olulula ukulusebenzisa

* Yenza ikopi yogcino yesayithi

## Amaxwebhu {#documentation}
**Indlela yokuThumela ngaphandle nokuNgenisa iSayithi**

## Ufakelo {#installation}

1. Layisha **ultimate-multisite-site-exporter** kulawulo lwe-**/wp-content/plugins/**
2. Yenza isongezelelo sisebenze ngemenyu ye-**Izongezelelo** kwi-WordPress
3. Landela iWizard inyathelo ngenyathelo ukuseta isongezelelo

## Isikhokelo Sokusetyenziswa {#usage-guide}

Nge-Ultimate Multisite ungathumela ngaphandle isayithi kwinethiwekhi yakho okanye ungenise isayithi kufakelo lwakho lwe-multisite okanye lwesayithi enye.

![Iphepha lezongezelelo elinokhetho lokufaka](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

## Indlela yokuthumela ngaphandle isayithi {#how-to-export-a-site}

Ukuthumela ngaphandle isayithi kwinethiwekhi yakho, yiya ku-**Thumela ngaphandle & Ngenisa** kwimenyu ye-Ultimate Multisite kwaye, kwithebhu ethi **Thumela Iisayithi Ngaphandle**, cofa **Yenza uThumelo olutsha lweSayithi**.

<!-- Umfanekiso wesikrini awukho: ithebhu yokuThumela Iisayithi Ngaphandle eneqhosha elithi Yenza uThumelo olutsha lweSayithi -->

Khangela isayithi ofuna ukuyithumela ngaphandle uze ukhethe izinto ofuna ukuzibandakanya kwifayile ethunyelwe ngaphandle. Ungabandakanya imixholo, izongezelelo, kunye nokulayishiweyo. Ukuba isayithi yakho inkulu kakhulu, ungakhetha ukuqhuba inkqubo yokuthumela ngaphandle ngasemva.<!-- Umfanekiso wesikrini awukho: iinketho zokuthumela ngaphandle isayithi ezinokukhangela isayithi, iibhokisi zokukhetha ukubandakanya imixholo/izongezelelo/okulayishiweyo -->

Cofa iqhosha elithi **Thumela Isayithi Ngaphandle**. Isayithi yakho ethunyelwe ngaphandle iya kufumaneka kuluhlu lwe-**Uthumelo Olukhoyo**. Cofa iqhosha elithi **Khuphela i-ZIP** ukukhuphela ifayile. Ii-ZIP ezithunyelwe ngaphandle zibandakanya `index.php` eziqalisa ngokwazo kunye ne-`readme.txt`, ukuze zifakwe kumamkeli omtsha ngaphandle kokufaka kuqala isongezelelo somngenisi esahlukileyo.<!-- Umfanekiso wesikrini awukho: uluhlu loThumelo Olukhoyo olunegqhosha elithi Khuphela i-ZIP -->

## Indlela yokuthumela ngaphandle inethiwekhi yonke {#how-to-export-the-whole-network}

Ukuthumela ngaphandle zonke iisayithi ezingaphantsi kunye, yiya ku-**Thumela ngaphandle & Ngenisa** kwimenyu ye-Ultimate Multisite uze usebenzise ukhetho lwe-**Thumela iNethiwekhi Ngaphandle** kwisikrini sokuthumela ngaphandle. I-akhayivu eyenziweyo iqulethe zonke iisayithi ezingaphantsi kwi-ZIP enye ukwenzela ukufuduswa kwabamkeli, ukwakhiwa kwakhona kweendawo zovavanyo, okanye iikopi zogcino zenqanaba lenethiwekhi.

Uthumelo lwenethiwekhi ngaphandle lunokuba lukhulu. Luqhube xa itrafikhi iphantsi uze uqinisekise ukuba iseva inendawo yokugcina eyaneleyo ye-akhayivu kunye nako nakuphi na okulayishiweyo, izongezelelo, kunye nemixholo ebandakanyiweyo.

## Indlela yokungenisa isayithi {#how-to-import-a-site}

Ukungenisa isayithi, yiya kwithebhu ethi **Ngenisa iSayithi** uze ulayishe ifayile ye-.zip yesayithi ofuna ukuyingenisa. Ungatshintsha i-URL iye kwentsha okanye ugcine i-URL endala ngokushiya isithuba singenanto. Kwakhona, ungakhetha ibhokisi yokucima ifayile ye-.zip kufakelo lwakho emva kokungenisa. Emva koko cofa **Qalisa Ukungenisa**.

<!-- Umfanekiso wesikrini awukho: ithebhu ethi Ngenisa iSayithi enendawo yokulayisha i-zip kunye neqhosha elithi Qalisa Ukungenisa -->

Inkqubo inokuthatha ixeshana. Uya kubona ukungenisa okulindileyo kuluhlu olungezantsi komngenisi.

<!-- Umfanekiso wesikrini awukho: ukungenisa okulindileyo kuluhlu olungezantsi komngenisi -->

Ungayimisa inkqubo ngokucofa **Rhoxisa Ukungenisa**.

<!-- Umfanekiso wesikrini awukho: iqhosha elithi Rhoxisa Ukungenisa ukumisa inkqubo yokungenisa -->

Emva kokuba inkqubo igqityiwe, isayithi yakho sele ifumaneka kwinethiwekhi yakho.

## Indlela yokungenisa isayithi kufakelo olunye {#how-to-import-a-site-on-a-single-install}

Ukungenisa isayithi kufakelo lwakho olunye, qala ufake kwaye wenze umngenisi asebenze kwisayithi yakho. Ungafumana ikhonkco lokuyikhuphela kwithebhu yomthumeli.

<!-- Umfanekiso wesikrini awukho: ithebhu yomthumeli enekhonkco lokukhuphela umngenisi wesayithi enye -->

Ungafumana umngenisi kwimenyu ye-**Izixhobo**, ngasekhohlo, kwinto engaphantsi ethi **Thumela ngaphandle & Ngenisa**.

<!-- Umfanekiso wesikrini awukho: imenyu yeZixhobo yesayithi enye ibonisa imenyu engaphantsi yokuThumela ngaphandle nokuNgenisa -->

Layisha ifayile ye-.zip yesayithi ofuna ukuyingenisa. Ungatshintsha i-URL iye kwentsha okanye ugcine i-URL endala ngokushiya isithuba singenanto. Kwakhona, ungakhetha ibhokisi yokucima ifayile ye-.zip kufakelo lwakho emva kokungenisa. Emva koko cofa **Qalisa Ukungenisa**.

<!-- Umfanekiso wesikrini awukho: ifomu yokungenisa yesayithi enye enokulayisha i-zip kunye neqhosha elithi Qalisa Ukungenisa -->

Inkqubo inokuthatha ixeshana. Uya kubona ukungenisa okulindileyo kuluhlu olungezantsi komngenisi.

<!-- Umfanekiso wesikrini awukho: ukungenisa okulindileyo kwesayithi enye kuluhlu -->

Ungayimisa inkqubo ngokucofa **Rhoxisa Ukungenisa**.

<!-- Umfanekiso wesikrini awukho: iqhosha elithi Rhoxisa Ukungenisa ukumisa inkqubo yokungenisa -->

Emva kokuba inkqubo igqityiwe, isayithi yakho iya kutshintshwa yisayithi osandula ukuyingenisa.
