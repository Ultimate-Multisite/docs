# Umthengisi weeDomeyini

**Multisite Ultimate: Umthengisi weeDomeyini** iguqula inethiwekhi yakho ye-WordPress Multisite ibe liqonga elinamandla lokuthengisa iidomeyini. Vumela abathengi babhalise iidomeyini zesiko ngqo ngexesha lokubhalisa okanye kwi-Dashboard yabo, ngokujonga ukufumaneka ngexesha lokwenyani kunye namaxabiso acacileyo.

### Kutheni Umthengisi weeDomeyini? {#why-domain-seller}

Ubhaliso lwedomeyini yenye yezongezo ezithengiswa kakhulu ezicelwa rhoqo kulo naliphi na iqonga le-WaaS (Website as a Service). Endaweni yokuthumela abathengi bakho kumbhalisi womntu wesithathu, unokunikela ngeedomeyini ngqo — ugcine ingeniso engakumbi kwinethiwekhi yakho ngelixa unika abathengi amava angenazithintelo.

### Iimpawu eziphambili {#key-features}

#### Inkxaso yabaBhalisi beeDomeyini abaninzi {#multiple-domain-registrar-support}
* **OpenSRS** — Ubhaliso olupheleleyo, uhlaziyo, nolawulo
* **Namecheap** — Udibaniso olupheleleyo ne-WhoisGuard kunye novavanyo lwe-sandbox
* **HostAfrica** — Ukukhangela idomeyini, amaxabiso, ubhaliso, ukudluliselwa, DNS, kunye nokhuseleko lwe-ID nge-Domains Reseller API
* **Openprovider** — Amaxabiso omthengisi kwakhona, ubhaliso, uhlaziyo, ukudluliselwa, DNS, ubumfihlo be-WHOIS, kunye nokungqamanisa kwe-TLD
* **Hostinger** — Ukukhangela nokubhalisa idomeyini kusetyenziswa i-token yodibaniso ekwabelwana ngayo ye-Hostinger
* **Uyilo olunokwandiswa** — Yakhiwe kwipateni ye-Integration Registry, nto leyo eyenza kube lula ukongeza ababoneleli abangakumbi
* **Uvavanyo loQhagamshelwano olwakhelwe ngaphakathi** — Qinisekisa iinkcukacha zomboneleli noqhagamshelwano phambi kokuba uye bukhoma

#### I-Wizard yoSeto ekhokelwayo {#guided-setup-wizard}
* **Ukuqalisa ngonqakrazo olunye** — Ikukhokela kuqwalaselo lomboneleli, ukungenisa ii-TLD, kunye nokudala imveliso yedomeyini
* **Ukungqamanisa okuzenzekelayo kwe-TLD** — Ingenisa zonke ii-TLD ezifumanekayo kunye namaxabiso evenkile enkulu kubaboneleli bakho abaqhagamshelweyo
* **Ukudala imveliso emiselweyo** — Idala imveliso yedomeyini ekulungeleyo ukusetyenziswa enezimiso ezisisiseko zenzuzo enengqiqo

#### Amaxabiso eDomeyini aguquguqukayo {#flexible-domain-pricing}
* **Iindlela ezintathu zokongeza inzuzo** — Ukongeza inzuzo ngepesenti, ukongeza inzuzo esisigxina, okanye ixabiso elisisigxina nge-TLD nganye
* **Iimveliso ezikhethekileyo ze-TLD** — Dala iimveliso zedomeyini ezahlukileyo kumaqela ahlukeneyo e-TLD (.com/.net, .io, iikhowudi zamazwe, njl.)
* **Amaxabiso entshayelelo** — Nikezela ngexabiso lonyaka wokuqala elithotyiweyo ukutsala ubhaliso olutsha
* **Amaxabiso exesha lokwenyani** — Amaxabiso aphilayo evenkile enkulu avela kubaboneleli ngokugcinwa kwe-cache ngobukrelekrele
* **Inkxaso yekhuphoni** — Ukuhambelana okupheleleyo neekhowudi zesaphulelo ze-Multisite Ultimate

#### Ukhuseleko loBumfihlo be-WHOIS {#whois-privacy-protection}
* **Uqwalaselo ngemveliso nganye** — Seta ubumfihlo be-WHOIS ku-Disabled, Always Included, okanye Customer Choice
* **Ukuzikhethela komthengi** — Xa kusetwe ku-Customer Choice, ibhokisi yokukhangela kwi-checkout ivumela abathengi bongeze ukhuseleko lobumfihlo
* **Amaxabiso anokuqwalaselwa** — Seta ixabiso lonyaka ngesongezo sobumfihlo, okanye usinikele simahla
* **Udibaniso nombhalisi** — Lwenza i-WhoisGuard (Namecheap) okanye ubumfihlo be-WHOIS (OpenSRS) ngokuzenzekelayo ngexesha lobhaliso

#### Amava e-Checkout adibeneyo {#unified-checkout-experience}
* **Iindlela ezintathu zedomeyini** — Abathengi bakhetha phakathi kwe-subdomain (simahla), ukubhalisa idomeyini entsha, okanye ukudibanisa idomeyini ekhoyo abasele benayo
* **Ukukhangela idomeyini ngexesha lokwenyani** — Ukujonga ukufumaneka okuxhaswa yi-AJAX ngaphandle kokulayisha iphepha kwakhona
* **Iingcebiso ezikrelekrele** — Iingcebiso ze-TLD ezizezinye xa idomeyini ekhethiweyo sele ithathiwe
* **Amaxabiso acacileyo** — Ukuboniswa okucacileyo kwexabiso lobhaliso, ixabiso lohlaziyo, kunye nentlawulo yokuzikhethela yobumfihlo be-WHOIS
* **Iinkalo zoqhagamshelwano lombhalisi** — Iqokelela ulwazi olufunekayo lombhalisi (igama, idilesi, ifowuni) ngaphakathi ngexesha le-checkout
* **I-URL yesayithi eyenziwa ngokuzenzekelayo** — Idilesi yesayithi itsalwa ngokuzenzekelayo kwidomeyini ekhethiweyo

#### Inkqubo yoHlaziyo oluZenzekelayo {#auto-renewal-system}
* **Uhlaziyo olubotshelelwe kubulungu** — Iidomeyini zihlaziywa ngokuzenzekelayo ngelixa ubulungu bomthengi busebenza
* **Ukucwangcisa okukrelekrele** — Imisebenzi yohlaziyo ifakwa emgceni ngokusekelwe kwimihla yokuphelelwa kweedomeyini
* **Ingqiqo yokuzama kwakhona** — Ukuzama kwakhona ngobukrelekrele xa iinzame zohlaziyo zisilela
* **Izaziso ze-imeyile** — Izilumkiso ezinokuqwalaselwa zohlaziyo oluzayo, uhlaziyo oluphumeleleyo, ukusilela, kunye nokuphelelwa

#### Ulawulo lwe-DNS lwabaThengi {#customer-dns-management}
* **DNS yokuzisebenzela** — Abathengi banokongeza, bahlele, kwaye bacime iirekhodi ze-DNS (A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, TXT) kwi-Dashboard yabo
* **Isimo sedomeyini** — Isimo sobhaliso sexesha lokwenyani, imihla yokuphelelwa, kunye nolwazi lwe-nameserver
* **Ukudityaniswa kwedomeyini okuzenzekelayo** — Iidomeyini ezibhalisiweyo okanye ezikhoyo zidityaniswa ngokuzenzekelayo nesayithi yomthengi

#### Izixhobo zoMlawuli {#admin-tools}
* **Ubhaliso lwedomeyini ngesandla** — Bhalisa iidomeyini egameni labathengi kwiphaneli yomlawuli
* **Ukubeka iliso kwiidomeyini** — Jonga zonke iidomeyini zabathengi, izimo, kunye nemihla yokuphelelwa kwiphepha elinye
* **Ukuloga okukhethekileyo kumboneleli** — Iitshaneli zelogi ezahlukileyo kumbhalisi ngamnye (umz., domain-seller-namecheap.log)
* **Iinkcukacha zobhaliso** — Jonga ulwazi lombhalisi kunye nedatha yobhaliso kwiphepha elingundoqo lokuhlela idomeyini

### Ababoneleli abaXhasiweyo {#supported-providers}

* **OpenSRS** — Udibaniso olupheleleyo nobhaliso, uhlaziyo, ulawulo lwe-DNS, kunye nobumfihlo be-WHOIS
* **Namecheap** — Udibaniso olupheleleyo nobhaliso, uhlaziyo, ukujonga ukufumaneka ngobuninzi, kunye ne-WhoisGuard yasimahla
* **HostAfrica** — Ubhaliso, uhlaziyo, ukudluliselwa, ulawulo lwe-DNS, ukufunyanwa kwekhowudi ye-EPP, i-registrar lock, kunye nokhuseleko lwe-ID
* **Openprovider** — Ubhaliso, uhlaziyo, ukudluliselwa, ulawulo lwe-DNS, ukufunyanwa kwekhowudi ye-EPP, i-registrar lock, ubumfihlo be-WHOIS, kunye nokungqamanisa kwe-TLD yomthengisi kwakhona
* **Hostinger** — Ubhaliso, ukukhangela idomeyini, uhlaziyo lwe-nameserver, i-registrar lock, kunye nobumfihlo be-WHOIS; uhlaziyo luphathwa yi-auto-renew ye-Hostinger
* **GoDaddy** — Ubhaliso kunye nohlaziyo
* **ResellerClub** — Ubhaliso, uhlaziyo, kunye nolawulo lwe-DNS
* **NameSilo** — Ubhaliso kunye nohlaziyo
* **Enom** — Ubhaliso, uhlaziyo, kunye nolawulo lwe-DNS

### Iinkcukacha zobuGcisa {#technical-details}

* **Ifuna:** PHP 7.4+, Multisite Ultimate 2.4.12+
* **Uyilo:** Ipateni ye-Integration Registry ene-interface ye-Domain_Selling_Capability
* **Autoloading:** I-autoloader ye-Jetpack enokuhambelana kweeklasi ze-PSR-4
* **Imigangatho:** Imigangatho yokubhala ikhowudi ye-WordPress, ukuchwetheza okungqongqo, iingxelo ezilungiselelweyo

## Ufakelo {#installation}

1. Qinisekisa ukuba **Multisite Ultimate v2.4.12+** ifakiwe kwaye yenziwe yasebenza kwinethiwekhi
2. Layisha i-plugin ku-`/wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite-domain-seller/`
3. Yenza i-plugin isebenze kwinethiwekhi ukusuka ku-**Plugins** kwi-WordPress Network Admin
4. I-**Setup Wizard** iqalisa ngokuzenzekelayo ekusebenzeni kokuqala — landela amanyathelo ukuze:
   * Qhagamshela umbhalisi wakho wedomeyini
   * Vavanya uqhagamshelwano
   * Ngenisa ii-TLD ezifumanekayo kunye namaxabiso evenkile enkulu
   * Dala imveliso yakho yokuqala yedomeyini enokongeza inzuzo kumaxabiso
5. Yongeza inkalo ye-**Ukukhetha iDomeyini** kwifomu yakho ye-checkout ku-**Ultimate Multisite > Checkout Forms**

## Imibuzo ebuzwa rhoqo {#frequently-asked-questions}

### Loluphi uhlobo lwe-Multisite Ultimate olufunekayo? {#what-version-of-multisite-ultimate-is-required}

Uhlobo 2.4.12 okanye oluphezulu. I-addon ayihambelani ne-WP Ultimo v1.x okanye iinguqulelo ze-Ultimate Multisite ezingaphantsi kwe-2.4.12.

### Ngabaphi ababoneleli beedomeyini abaxhasiweyo? {#which-domain-providers-are-supported}

Kuxhaswa ababhalisi abasithoba: **OpenSRS**, **Namecheap**, **HostAfrica**, **Openprovider**, **Hostinger**, **GoDaddy**, **ResellerClub**, **NameSilo**, kunye **Enom**. Amandla ayahluka ngokomboneleli kuba ii-API zababhalisi ziveza imisebenzi eyahlukileyo; bona isikhokelo soseto ukuze ufumane isishwankathelo somboneleli ngamnye. Uyilo lwe-Integration Registry lwe-addon lwenza kube lula ukongeza ababoneleli besiko — bona [isikhokelo somphuhlisi](/developer/integration-guide/custom-registrar).

### Ndinganikezela ngokhuseleko lobumfihlo be-WHOIS? {#can-i-offer-whois-privacy-protection}

Ewe. Imveliso nganye yedomeyini inesethingi ye-WHOIS Privacy eneendlela ezintathu: Disabled, Always Included, okanye Customer Choice. Xa kusetwe ku-Customer Choice, abathengi babona ibhokisi yokukhangela ngexesha le-checkout enexabiso (okanye "Simahla" kwi-WhoisGuard ye-Namecheap). Iflegi yobumfihlo idluliselwa ngokuzenzekelayo kumbhalisi ngexesha lobhaliso.

### Ndingaseta amaxabiso ahlukeneyo kwii-TLD ezahlukeneyo? {#can-i-set-different-pricing-for-different-tlds}

Ewe. Dala iimveliso zedomeyini ezahlukileyo kumaqela ahlukeneyo e-TLD. Imveliso nganye inokuba nohlobo lwayo lokongeza inzuzo (ipesenti, ukongeza inzuzo esisigxina, okanye ixabiso elisisigxina), ii-TLD ezixhasiweyo, kunye namaxabiso entshayelelo.

### Zeziphi iinketho ze-checkout ezibonwa ngabathengi? {#what-checkout-options-do-customers-see}

Inkalo ye-Ukukhetha iDomeyini inika abathengi iithebhu ezintathu: **Subdomain** (sebenzisa i-subdomain yasimahla kwinethiwekhi yakho), **Bhalisa iDomeyini eNtsha** (khangela uze ubhalise idomeyini entsha), kunye **iDomeyini eKhoyo** (dibanisa idomeyini abasele benayo). Ziindlela ozivumelayo kuphela eziza kuvela.

### Ngaba ixhasa uhlaziyo oluzenzekelayo? {#does-it-support-automatic-renewals}

Ewe. Iidomeyini zinokusetwa ukuba zihlaziywe ngokuzenzekelayo kwaye zibotshelelwe kwisimo sobulungu somthengi. Iintlawulo zohlaziyo ziqhutywa ngesango lentlawulo esele likhona lomthengi.

### Ngaba abathengi banokulawula iirekhodi zabo ze-DNS? {#can-customers-manage-their-dns-records}

Ewe. Abathengi bafumana ujongano lolawulo lwe-DNS kwi-Dashboard yabo ukuze bongeze, bahlele, kwaye bacime iirekhodi ze-A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, kunye ne-TXT. Ulawulo lwe-DNS luyafumaneka nge-OpenSRS, ResellerClub, Enom, HostAfrica, kunye ne-Openprovider. I-DNS ye-Hostinger yeedomeyini ezisingathiweyo iphathwa ludibaniso olungundoqo lwe-Hostinger lokudibanisa iidomeyini.

### Ingaba ikhona i-wizard yoseto? {#is-there-a-setup-wizard}

Ewe. I-wizard yoseto isebenza ngokuzenzekelayo ekusebenzeni kokuqala kwaye ikukhokela ekudibaniseni umbhalisi, ukungenisa ii-TLD, kunye nokudala imveliso yakho yokuqala yedomeyini. Unokuphinda uqhube i-wizard ukusuka kwiisethingi ze-addon.
