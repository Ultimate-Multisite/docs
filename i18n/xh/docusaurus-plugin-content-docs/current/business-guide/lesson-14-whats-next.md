# Isifundo 14: Yintoni Elandelayo

Wakhe iqonga lewebhusayithi le-niche ukusuka ekuqaleni. IFitSite iyasebenza, inceda ii-studio zokomelela, kwaye ivelisa ingeniso ephindaphindayo. Esi sifundo sokugqibela sijonga apho ungaya khona ukusuka apha.

## Apho Sishiyeke Khona {#where-we-left-off}

IFitSite lishishini eliqhubayo elinabathengi, imisebenzi, kunye nendlela yokukhula. Ngoku sicinga ngento elandelayo.

## Ukhetho 1: Ngena Nzulu Kwi-Niche Yakho {#option-1-go-deeper-in-your-niche}

Ngaphambi kokwandisa uye kwezinye ii-niche ezintsha, cinga ngokungena nzulu kwezokomelela:

### Ii-Sub-Niche {#sub-niches}

- **Ii-studio ze-yoga** -- iitemplate ezikhethekileyo ezineempawu zokucamngca, iiretreat, kunye neeworkshop
- **Ii-CrossFit boxes** -- ukulandelela i-WOD, iziphumo zokhuphiswano, iimpawu zoluntu
- **Izikolo zobugcisa bokulwa** -- amanqanaba eebhanti, ulwazi lweetumente, uluhlu lweeklasi
- **Ii-studio zomdaniso** -- iishedyuli zeerecital, iigalari zemiboniso, ulwazi lwezambatho

I-sub-niche nganye ingaba neetemplate zayo kwaye mhlawumbi nebrand yayo, zonke zisebenza kwinethiwekhi enye ye-Ultimate Multisite.

### Iimpawu zePremium {#premium-features}

Yakha iimpawu ii-studio zokomelela eziza kuhlawula ngakumbi ngazo:

- **Udibaniso lokubhukisha iiklasi** -- udibaniso olunzulu namaqonga okubhukisha adumileyo
- **I-portal yamalungu** -- indawo engenwa ngemvume yamalungu e-gym eneeshedyuli nezixhobo
- **Iitemplate zeblog yezokomelela** -- izakhiwo zomxholo ezakhiwe kwangaphambili zentengiso yomxholo wezokomelela
- **Udibaniso lwemidiya yoluntu** -- ukupapasha ngokuzenzekelayo iishedyuli zeeklasi nohlaziyo

### Ukwandiswa Ngokwejografi {#geographic-expansion}

Ukuba uqale kwindawo yakho, yandisa uye:

- Kwezinye izixeko kwilizwe lakho
- Kwamanye amazwe (sebenzisa [iimpawu zokulungelelanisa ulwimi](/user-guide/localization/translating) ze-Ultimate Multisite ukuxhasa iilwimi ezininzi)

## Ukhetho 2: Yandisa Uye Kwii-Niche Ezisondeleyo {#option-2-expand-to-adjacent-niches}

Inkqubo oyilandeleyo kwii-studio zokomelela isebenza kuyo nayiphi na i-niche. I-niche yakho yesibini ilula ngakumbi kuba sele unayo:

- Isiseko sobugcisa
- Amava okusebenza
- Isikhokelo esiqinisekisiweyo

### Ii-Niche Ezisondeleyo Kwezokomelela {#adjacent-niches-to-fitness}

- **Impilo-ntle kunye ne-spa** -- iingcali zokuthambisa, ii-day spa, amaziko empilo-ntle
- **Iiklabhu zemidlalo** -- amaqela emidlalo abangahlawulwayo, iiligi, iiklabhu zokuzonwabisa
- **Iingcali zempilo** -- ii-chiropractor, ii-physiotherapist, iingcali zesondlo

Ezi zabelana ngokudibana kwabaphulaphuli nezokomelela, ngoko intengiso yakho nobambiswano bakho banokudlulela kuzo.

### Ii-Niche Ezintsha Ngokupheleleyo {#completely-new-niches}

Sebenzisa isakhelo esivela kwiSifundo 2 ukuvavanya nayiphi na i-niche:

1. Ngaba bafuna iiwebhusayithi kodwa bayasokola ukufumana ezilungileyo?
2. Ngaba banako ukuhlawula umrhumo wenyanga?
3. Ngaba bayathetha omnye nomnye?

I-niche nganye entsha ifuna:

- Iitemplate ezintsha eziyilelwe elo shishini
- Izakhiwo zeeplan ezintsha ezibonisa iimfuno zelo shishini
- Intengiso entsha kunye namajelo okufumana abathengi
- Umxholo omtsha wokuqalisa kunye nolwazi lwenkxaso

Isiseko seqonga, ukuhlawulisa, kunye neenkqubo zokusebenza zihlala zinjalo.

## Ukhetho 3: Yakha Iqonga Lamaqonga {#option-3-build-a-platform-of-platforms}

Kwinqanaba elikhulu, ungasebenzisa iibrand ze-niche ezininzi kwinethiwekhi enye ye-Ultimate Multisite:

- `fitsite.com` -- ii-studio zokomelela
- `menusite.com` -- iiresityu
- `lawsite.com` -- iifemu zomthetho

Ibrand nganye ineetemplate zayo, iiplan zayo, kunye nentengiso yayo, kodwa zabelana ngesiseko, ukuhlawulisa, kunye neenkqubo zokusebenza. Apha kulapho uqoqosho lwe-Ultimate Multisite luba namandla khona -- iqonga elinye, amacandelo amaninzi.

### Multi-Network {#multi-network}

Ukwahlula ngokupheleleyo phakathi kwee-niche, cinga nge-[addon ye-Multi-Network](/addons/multinetwork) ekuvumela ukuba usebenzise iinethiwekhi ezininzi ze-WordPress kufakelo olunye.

## Ukhetho 4: Thengisa Ishishini {#option-4-sell-the-business}

Iqonga lewebhusayithi le-niche elinengeniso ephindaphindayo, ukushiya okuphantsi, kunye nendawo ecacileyo emarikeni liyiasethi exabisekileyo. Amashishini e-SaaS adla ngokuthengiswa ngexabiso eliphindwe ka-3 ukuya ku-5 kwingeniso yonyaka.

IFitSite enabathengi abangama-200 kwi-$89 ARPU:

- **MRR**: $17,800
- **ARR**: $213,600
- **Ixabiso lentengiso elinokubakho**: $640,000 - $1,068,000

Oku akusosiphumo esiqinisekisiweyo, kodwa sisiphumo esinokwenzeka ngokwenene kwiqonga le-niche eliqhutywa kakuhle.

## Oko Ukufundileyo {#what-you-have-learned}

Le khosi ikufundise inkqubo enokuphindwa:

1. **Khetha i-niche** eneemfuno ezicacileyo, amandla okuhlawula, kunye noluntu
2. **Qinisekisa** ngaphambi kokwakha
3. **Yakha iqonga elilungiselelweyo** elineetemplate, iiplan, kunye nebranding ezikhethekileyo kwi-niche
4. **Yenza amava angenazithintelo** ukusuka ekubhaliseni ukuya ekuqaliseni
5. **Beka ixabiso ngokwexabiso elinikezelwayo** ngamanqanaba acwangcisiweyo, izilingo, kunye nezaphulelo
6. **Yazisa ngendlela ecwangcisiweyo** ngokuqalisa okuthambileyo kunye noluhlu lokukhangela
7. **Fumana abathengi** ngamajelo akhethekileyo kwi-niche
8. **Qhuba imisebenzi** ngeendlela ezizinzileyo
9. **Khulisa** isiseko, ingeniso, kunye neqela njengoko ukhula

I-niche sisicwangciso. Iqonga sisixhobo. Ultimate Multisite yenza esi sixhobo sibe nokwenzeka. Ukuqonda kwakho i-niche kuyenza ibe nexabiso.
