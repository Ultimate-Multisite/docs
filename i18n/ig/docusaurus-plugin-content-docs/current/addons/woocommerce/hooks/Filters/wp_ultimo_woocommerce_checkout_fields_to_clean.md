# Filter: wp_ultimo_woocommerce_checkout_fields_to_clean

Na-enye ndị mmepe ohere ịza default WooCommerce billing address fields.

Ebe Ultimate Multisite nwere billing address fields, ọ nwere ike ịdị mma iwepụ fields ndabara WooCommerce na-egosi na checkout form. Na ndabara, a naghị eziga uru ọ bụla na ndepụta a, ya mere fields niile a haziri na WooCommerce ka egosipụta ga-edobe.

### Kemgbe

- 2.0.0
### Isi mmalite

A kọwara ya na [`inc/gateways/class-woocommerce-gateway.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite-woocommerce/blob/main/inc/gateways/class-woocommerce-gateway.php#L277) na ahịrị 277


## Nlaghachi
Ndepụta fields a ga-ewepụ. Uru dịnụ gụnyere billing_first_name, billing_last_name, billing_company, billing_country, billing_address_1, billing_address_2, billing_city, billing_state, billing_postcode, billing_email, na billing_phone.
