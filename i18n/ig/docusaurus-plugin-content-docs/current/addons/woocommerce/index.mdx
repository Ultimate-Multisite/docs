# WooCommerce Integration

WooCommerce Integration na-arụ ọrụ n'ụzọ yiri nnọọ ka Manual Gateway anyị si arụ ọrụ. Ọ na-enye ndị ọrụ gị nnukwu mgbanwe, na-enye ha ohere ịhọrọ ụdị ịkwụ ụgwọ ọ bụla dị na nrụnye WooCommerce gị. Ọ dịkwa mfe: ozugbo oge billing onye ọrụ kwụsịrị, a na-etinye subscription onye ọrụ n'ọnọdụ nkwụsị. N'otu oge ahụ, a ga-emepụta order na nrụnye WooCommerce nke saịtị isi nke network ahụ. Mgbe ahụ onye ọrụ na-enweta invoice na email ha, nwere njikọ iji mee ịkwụ ụgwọ. Ozugbo anabatara ịkwụ ụgwọ ahụ, a na-emeghachi subscription ahụ.

## Njirimara

- Njikọ dị mfe n'etiti Ultimate Multisite na WooCommerce
- Ọtụtụ ụzọ ịkwụ ụgwọ dị
- Billing na-emegharị onwe ya na-akpaghị aka site na iji bundled subscriptions core, yana nkwalite plugin WooCommerce Subscriptions ma ọ bụrụ na achọrọ
- Checkout totals gụnyere ụtụ isi Ultimate Multisite dị ka ahịrị ụgwọ WooCommerce dị iche, na-abụghị taxable ka customers nwee ike nyochaa ọnụọgụ ụtụ isi tupu ịkwụ ụgwọ

## Nduzi Ojiji

_**IHE NDỊ DỊ MKPA: Edemede a na-ezo aka na Ultimate Multisite ụdị 2.x.**_

_**NTỊ:** Ultimate Multisite: WooCommerce Integration chọrọ ka a mee ka WooCommerce rụọ ọrụ ma ọ dịkarịa ala na saịtị isi gị._

Anyị ghọtara na _Stripe_ na _PayPal_ adịghị na mba ụfọdụ nke na-egbochi ma ọ bụ na-eme ka ọ sie ike maka ndị ọrụ Ultimate Multisite iji plugin anyị nke ọma. Ya mere, anyị mepụtara **add-on iji jikọta _WooCommerce_**.

_WooCommerce_ bụ plugin eCommerce a ma ama nke ukwuu. Ndị developers gburugburu ụwa mepụtara add-ons iji jikọta payment gateways dị iche iche na ya. Anyị jiri ohere a gbasaa payment gateways ị nwere ike iji na sistemụ billing Ultimate Multisite.

### Ahịrị ụgwọ ụtụ isi checkout

Mgbe checkout Ultimate Multisite gụnyere ụtụ isi, njikọ ahụ na-agbakwunye ọnụọgụ ụtụ isi Ultimate Multisite ọ bụla na checkout WooCommerce dị ka ahịrị ụgwọ dị iche, na-abụghị taxable. Nke a na-eme ka mkpokọta ụtụ isi pụta ìhè tupu ịkwụ ụgwọ ma na-egbochi WooCommerce ịtụ ụtụ isi na ụgwọ ụtụ isi ahụ ọzọ.

## Otu esi ejikwa ịkwụ ụgwọ

WooCommerce Integration na-arụ ọrụ n'ụzọ yiri nnọọ ka **Manual Gateway** anyị si arụ ọrụ. Ihe ọghọm ya bụ na a ga-akwụrịrị ịkwụ ụgwọ ọ bụla n'aka na njedebe nke oge billing. Nke a bụ otú flow ahụ si arụ ọrụ:

  * Ozugbo billing cycle onye ọrụ kwụsịrị, a na-etinye membership ahụ **on hold**. N'otu oge ahụ, a ga-emepụta order na nrụnye _WooCommerce_ na saịtị isi nke network gị.

  * Mgbe ahụ ndị ọrụ na-enweta invoice na email ha, nwere njikọ iji mee ịkwụ ụgwọ (njikọ ahụ na-apụtakwa na billing history ha site na dashboard subsite ha). Njikọ ahụ na-ebuga onye ọrụ na _**WooCommerce**_ **checkout** , ebe ndị ọrụ ga-enwe ike ịhọrọ otu n'ime **gateway _WooCommerce_ dị** iji hazie ịkwụ ụgwọ ha.

  * Ozugbo anabatara ịkwụ ụgwọ ahụ, a na-eme ka membership ahụ **dị ọhụrụ** , a na-edekọ ịkwụ ụgwọ ahụ na Ultimate Multisite ma tọọ order ahụ ka ọ zuru ezu.

### Ụfọdụ Ihe Dị Mkpa Icheta:

  * Ọrụ ndabara nke Ultimate Multisite ka na-arụ ọrụ na ụdị njikọ a. Dịka ọmụmaatụ, **ịnye refunds** site na ihuenyo **Subscription Management** ga-ekwurịta na WooCommerce iji nye refund ahụ site na iji ụdị ịkwụ ụgwọ ahọpụtara.

  * Ultimate Multisite: WooCommerce Integration na-adabere na WP-Cron ka ọ rụọ ọrụ. Nke a na-arụ ọrụ nke ọma ọtụtụ oge, mana ma ọ bụrụ na saịtị ahụ nwere obere traffic, a naghị akpali cron ka ọ lelee scheduled events ma event ahụ efunahụ, nke nwere ike ịkpata igbu oge n'imezu ọrụ.

## Plugin WooCommerce Subscriptions

Ịmalite ịkwụ ụgwọ n’onwe gị kwa ọnwa adịghị mma maka ndị ọrụ ikpeazụ n’ihi na a na-atụ anya na ndebanye ga-emeghachi onwe ya akpaghị aka n’oge ịgba ụgwọ ọ bụla. Add-on WooCommerce Integration ugbu a jikọtara koodu ndebanye bụ isi, ya mere ngwaahịa na-emegharị mgbe niile na-eji ndebanye maka ịgba ụgwọ na-emegharị.

Ị **gaghị** azụta ma ọ bụ wụnye [WooCommerce Subscriptions plugin](https://woocommerce.com/products/woocommerce-subscriptions/) dị iche iji ree ngwaahịa na-emegharị site na njikọta a. Ọ bụrụ na a zụrụ plugin ahụ, wụnye ya, ma ọ dị na saịtị isi gị, Ultimate Multisite ga-eji ya, WooCommerce ga-egosikwa atụmatụ njikwa ndebanye ndị ọzọ. Mgbe ọ naghị adị, isi ndebanye ejikọtara ka na-elekọta ngwaahịa na-emegharị.

![Ntọala njikọta ndebanye WooCommerce](/img/addons/woocommerce-subscription-integration.png)

Nke ahụ bụkwa ya. Ndị ọrụ ikpeazụ gị agaghịzi achọ iji aka ha kwụọ ụgwọ na ngwụcha oge ịgba ụgwọ ha maka ngwaahịa na-emegharị.

Ewezuga iwu a na-emepụta na saịtị isi gị n'okpuru _WooCommerce_, ọ ga-emepụta ndebanye maka Account ọ bụla.

### Omume nkwụsị mgbe mmeghari dara

Na ndabara, memberships na-anọgide na-arụ ọrụ mgbe WooCommerce Subscriptions na-ahazi usoro nnwale ọzọ maka ịkwụ ụgwọ dara. Nke a na-echekwa windo amara nkịtị nke WooCommerce Subscriptions ka mmeghari eweghachitere wee nwee ike ime ka ndebanye na membership laghachi n'otu ọnọdụ na-enweghị igbochi ohere tupu oge eruo.

Ndị nchịkwa chọrọ njikwa ohere siri ike karị nwere ike ime ka **Suspend Memberships Immediately on Failed Renewals** rụọ ọrụ na ntọala WooCommerce Integration. Mgbe ntọala opt-in a rụrụ ọrụ, Ultimate Multisite na-akwụsị membership metụtara ya ozugbo ịkwụ ụgwọ mmeghari dara kama ichere ka njikwa nnwale ọzọ nke WooCommerce Subscriptions kwụsị.

Ndị mmepe nwere ike iji filter `wu_woo_suspend_on_payment_failure` gbanwee mkpebi ahụ. A na-emepụta peeji ntụaka hook site na isi mmalite add-on, ya mere nchịkọta a na-akpọ naanị aha filter maka iwu nkwụsị ahaziri iche.
