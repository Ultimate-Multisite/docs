# Email {#emails}

Ultimate Multisite: Emails addon na-enye ndị nwe network ohere ịmepụta ma jikwaa akaụntụ email ndị ahịa n'akụkụ saịtị na membership. Version 1.1.0 na-agbakwunye njikwa akaụntụ email nke ndị ahịa na-ahụ, webmail single sign-on, nkwado CyberPanel forwarder, na mmezi provisioning n'oge checkout.

## Njirimara Ndị Dị Mkpa {#key-features}

* **Widget akaụntụ email onye ahịa**: Ndị ahịa nwere ike ịmepụta ma jikwaa igbe ozi site na peeji Account ha mgbe ha nwere email membership na-arụ ọrụ.
* **Webmail SSO na magic links**: Ndị ahịa nwere ike imeghe webmail ozugbo site na peeji Account site na authentication otu-pịa mgbe provider na-akwado ya.
* **Mgbanwe okwuntughe**: Ndị ahịa na administrators nwere ike imelite okwuntughe igbe ozi site na widget njikwa email.
* **Provisioning ikike checkout**: Checkout na-emepụta email membership, jikọọ ya na domain saịtị onye ahịa, ma mee ka ngwaọrụ igbe ozi peeji Account dị.
* **CyberPanel forwarders**: Network ndị CyberPanel na-akwado nwere ike ịmepụta ma jikwaa email forwarders dịka akụkụ nke usoro ọrụ email.
* **Ntọala maara provider**: Peeji ntọala na-enyocha providers ka ọ bụrụ integrations ndị na-arụ ọrụ ma hazie, ka administrators hụ naanị nhọrọ ndị a pụrụ iji.

## Hazie Ngwaahịa Email {#configure-an-email-product}

1. Gaa **Network Admin > Ultimate Multisite > Products > Add Product**.
2. Tọọ **Product Type** ka ọ bụrụ **Email**.
3. Hazie ọnụahịa. Jiri ọnụahịa recurring akwụ ụgwọ maka plan a na-ere, ma ọ bụ tọọ ngwaahịa ahụ ka ọ bụrụ n'efu/ọnụahịa-efu ma ọ bụrụ na ọ ga-abụ plan email onyinye.

5. Họrọ **Email Provider** ahaziri.
6. Tọọ **Number of Accounts**, **Storage Quota (MB)**, oke alias, forwarders, na autoresponders dịka achọrọ.
7. Chekwaa ngwaahịa ahụ.

Naanị providers ndị akwadoro ma hazie kpamkpam na peeji **Integrations** na-apụta na selector provider. Ọ bụrụ na selector ahụ tọgbọ chakoo, buru ụzọ hazie ma nwalee provider.

## Webmail SSO na Nbanye Magic-Link {#webmail-sso-and-magic-link-login}

Webmail SSO na-enye ndị ahịa omume **Mepee webmail** ozugbo site na account page. Mgbe provider na-arụ ọrụ na-akwado ya, addon na-arịọ URL access dị mkpirikpi ma na-eduzi onye ahịa banye na webmail na-ajụghị ha ka ha detuo credentials mailbox.

Omume provider nwere ike ịdị iche:

* Providers nwere nkwado magic-link nwere ike ịmepụta njikọ nbanye nwere oge njedebe.
* Providers na-enweghị nkwado SSO ma ọ bụ magic-link ka kwesịrị igosi nkọwa mailbox na omume njikwa okwuntughe, mana enweghị bọtịnụ webmail otu-click.

Mee ka access webmail rụọ ọrụ naanị maka providers ebe ị hazirila credentials API achọrọ. Ọ bụrụ na omume ahụ adịghị maka provider, buru ụzọ kwenye na provider ahụ na-arụ ọrụ, ahaziri ya, ma na-akwado njikọ nbanye webmail.

## Ajụjụ A Na-ajụkarị {#frequently-asked-questions}

### Ndị ahịa nwere ike ịmepụta akaụntụ email nke ha? {#can-customers-create-their-own-email-accounts}

Ee. Mgbe onye ahịa nwere membership email dị ndụ maka domain ma product ahụ kwere ka enwekwuo Account, ndị ahịa nwere ike ịmepụta ma jikwaa mailbox nke ha site na ibe Account.

### Onye na-eweta email ọ bụla na-akwado webmail SSO? {#does-every-provider-support-webmail-sso}

Mba. Nkwado SSO na magic-link dabere na onye na-eweta email dị ndụ. Ndị na-eweta email na-enweghị API access-link ka nwere ike ịkwado imepụta mailbox na mgbanwe okwuntughe.

### Ndị administrators nwere ike ịmepụta akaụntụ email maka ndị ahịa? {#can-administrators-create-email-accounts-for-customers}

Ee. Ndị administrators nwere ike ịmepụta akaụntụ email site na interface admin, gụnyere ịtọ okwuntughe mbụ mgbe a na-emepụta Account ahụ. Maka njikwa self-service nke onye ahịa, hụkwa na onye ahịa nwere membership email dị ndụ maka domain ahụ.

### Enwere m ike inye akaụntụ email n'efu? {#can-i-give-an-email-account-away-for-free}

Ee. Mepụta product Email efu/ọnụahịa-efu ma tinye ya dị ka membership dị ndụ maka onye ahịa, ma ọ bụ hapụ onye ahịa ka o mechaa checkout na mbelata 100%. Mgbe membership email dị, onye ahịa ma ọ bụ administrator nwere ike ịmepụta mailbox ahụ n'ezie.

### Gịnị mere ikenye akaụntụ email ji ajụ maka nọmba order? {#why-does-assigning-an-email-account-ask-for-an-order-number}

### Forwarders hà bụ otu ihe dị ka mailboxes? {#are-forwarders-the-same-as-mailboxes}
