# VAT

Multisite Ultimate: VAT Europe

Na-agbakwunye atụmatụ na ngwá ọrụ ndị ọzọ na nhazi ụtụ isi ndabara nke Multisite Ultimate iji kwado VAT Europe nke ọma.
Na-agbakwunye ọnụego EU VAT na-akpaghị aka na ibe Tax Rates site na

## Ntinye {#installation}

1. Bulite 'ultimate-multisite-vat' na ndekọ '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Mee ka plugin rụọ ọrụ site na menu 'Plugins' na WordPress
3. Gaa na Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Taxes ma mee ka EU Vat rụọ ọrụ.
4. Pịa na Tax Rates wee Update EU VAT Rates na ala ma Save Tax Rates.
5. Emechara!
