# AffiliateWP {#affiliatewp}

Ultimate Multisite: Mmekọrịta AffiliateWP

Jiri AffiliateWP dị ike mee ka ntọala ndị ahịa nke Ultimo Network gị too!

## Nwụnye {#installation}

1. Bulite 'ultimate-multisite-git ' na ndekọ '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Mee ka mgbakwunye ahụ rụọ ọrụ site na menu 'Mgbakwunye' na WordPress
3. Soro Wizard nzọụkwụ n’otu n’otu iji hazie mgbakwunye ahụ

## Ntuziaka Ojiji {#usage-guide}

[AffiliateWP](https://affiliatewp.com/) aghọwo ngwọta a na-ejikarị eme ihe mgbe a na-ewu mmemme ndị mmekọ maka azụmahịa gị n’ịntanetị. Ultimate Multisite: AffiliateWP bụ akwa ga-eme ka ị nwee ike wuo otu ihe ahụ maka netwọkụ premium gị.

## Ibido {#getting-started}

Nke mbụ, ị **ga-enwerịrị** [**AffiliateWP**](https://affiliatewp.com/) **na-arụ ọrụ**! Ị gaghị enwe ike iji add-on anyị ma ọ bụrụ na ị nweghị **mgbakwunye na-emekọrịta na ya**. Ọ bụrụ na ị naghị ezube ikwe ka ndị ahịa gị jiri ya na saịtị ha kwa, AffiliateWP kwesịrị ịdị na-arụ ọrụ naanị na saịtị isi gị.

Ọzọkwa, ị ga-achọ ka **Ultimate Multisite** na **Ultimate Multisite: AffiliateWP** **rụọ ọrụ** **n’ofe netwọkụ niile**.

## Ịgbakwunye Ultimate Multisite dịka Mmekọrịta AffiliateWP {#adding-ultimate-multisite-as-an-affiliatewp-integration}

Na saịtị isi gị, gaa na Ntọala Affiliates, ma na taabụ Integrations, họrọ Ultimate Multisite. Nke a ga-agwa [AffiliateWP](https://affiliatewp.com/) ka ọ malite ịchọpụta nleta na mgbanwe sitere na usoro ndebanye aha Ultimate Multisite.

<!-- Nseta ihuenyo adịghị: taabụ ntọala AffiliateWP Integrations nwere igbe nhọrọ Ultimate Multisite -->

_Họrọ igbe Ultimate Multisite n’ala ma chekwaa_

Nke ahụ bụ ihe niile ọ na-ewe. A ga-achọpụta nleta sitere na njikọ referral, ma mgbe onye ọrụ ahụ mechara kwụọ ụgwọ mbụ ya, a ga-emepụta mgbanwe ahụ ma debe commission n’akụkụ maka onye mmekọ kpatara mgbanwe ahụ.

A ga-achọpụta naanị ụgwọ mbụ natara site n’aka onye ọrụ ọhụrụ ahụ e kere, n’agbanyeghị. Iji mee ka ịkwụ ụgwọ referral na-emegharị ugboro ugboro rụọ ọrụ, ị ga-achọ [AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals Add-on](https://affiliatewp.com/add-ons/pro/recurring-referrals/)

## Ime Ka Recurring Referrals Rụọ Ọrụ {#enabling-recurring-referrals}

Mgbe ị wụnyechara [AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals Add-on](https://affiliatewp.com/add-ons/pro/recurring-referrals/), gaa na taabụ Affiliates Settings Recurring Referrals, ma mee ka iji recurring referrals rụọ ọrụ.

<!-- Nseta ihuenyo adịghị: ntọala AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals nwere toggle Enable Recurring Referrals -->

_Dị mfe otú ahụ: Mee ka Recurring Referrals rụọ ọrụ, ihe niile adịla njikere!_

Ọzọkwa, nke ahụ bụ ihe niile ọ na-ewe!

Ugbu a, oge ọ bụla a natara ụgwọ ọhụrụ na account e zigara site na referral, onye mmekọ kpatara ya ga-enweta òkè nke ya n’akpaghị aka!
