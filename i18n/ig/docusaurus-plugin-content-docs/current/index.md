# Akwụkwọ nduzi Ultimate Multisite

Nnọọ na akwụkwọ nduzi Ultimate Multisite. Ultimate Multisite na-agbanwe WordPress Multisite ka ọ bụrụ ikpo okwu Weebụsaịtị dị ka Ọrụ (WaaS) zuru oke.

## Nkebi Akwụkwọ nduzi

### [Nduzi Onye ọrụ](/user-guide/getting-started/ultimate-multisite-101)

Ihe niile ịchọrọ iji hazie, dozie, ma jikwaa ikpo okwu WaaS gị. Ọ na-ekpuchi ntinye, ụzọ ịkwụ ụgwọ, njikọ domain, njikwa client, na ndị ọzọ.

### [Akwụkwọ nduzi Onye mmepe](/developer/hooks)

Ntụaka teknụzụ maka ndị mmepe na-ewu n’elu Ultimate Multisite. Gụnyere ntụaka hooks, akwụkwọ nduzi REST API, na nduzi mmepe mgbakwunye.

### [Mgbakwunye](/addons)

Akwụkwọ nduzi maka ngwa mgbakwunye akwụ ụgwọ na-agbatị Ultimate Multisite site na njirimara ndị ọzọ dịka njikọ WooCommerce, nyocha, ire domain, na ndị ọzọ.
