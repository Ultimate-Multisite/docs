# Websites Na Klien Ọ (v2)

A na m nwere ịzụrụ ọkụ na mmemme gị, ndị khách nwere ike ịnwale dashboard ha.

Naraa na akụkọ **Account**, onye nwere ike ịhụ ihe ndị dị mkpa maka ịkwado ha, membership-ah, domains, nkọwa plan ha na ihe ndị ọzọ, ha kuma nwere eziokwu. Naa ọ bụ, ha nwere akses na akụkọ **My Sites**, onye nwere ike ịhụ na iji gị na ịgwara mmemme ha. Ihe dị mkpa na ha ga-agba My Sites na dashboard ha.

![My Sites link in the customer dashboard](/img/account-page/my-sites-link.png)

Ha ga-agba mmemme ha onye ọ bụla, na-akpọ **Manage** button a n'ụlọ ha. Ma ha na-akpọ ya, ha ga-agba ha n'dashboard nke mmemme ahụ.

Ha na-agba mmemme ha ọ bụla bụ mmemme **primary** (nri-ime) maka plan ndị na-eme ka ha nwere mmemme ọzụ ahụ.

![My Sites page showing customer websites with Manage buttons](/img/account-page/my-sites-page.png)

Nke a bụ ihe niile nke interface ịgwara site:

![Site edit full interface](/img/admin/site-edit-full.png)

Ma ọ bụrụ na ha chọrọ ịgba mmemme primary ha, ha ga-agba akụkọ Account ma ha na-akpọ **Account** menu.

![Account menu link in customer dashboard](/img/account-page/account-menu.png)

Ha nwere ike ịhụ na iji gị na ịgwara mmemme ha:

![Customer edit interface](/img/admin/customer-edit.png)

Ọ bụrụ na ọ bụ, ha ga-agba **Change Default Site**.

![Change Default Site button on account page](/img/account-page/change-default-site.png)

Oge pop-up window ga-abụghị. Ịkpọ na drop-down list ahụ ma chọrọ mmemme ọ bụla ị chọrọ ka ọ bụrụ mmemme primary.

![Change Default Site dropdown selection](/img/account-page/change-default-site-dropdown.png)

Ọ bụ, kpọ ya **Change Default Site** ma ị na-agba!

![Ndemany na zműzítés a defált helyre](https://example.com/images/account-page/change-default-site-confirm.png)
