# Ọkụ na-eme Dashboard Ultimate Multisite

Mkpọrọ mịrị n'ụrụ ọrụ dị nwere, ma ọ bụrụ na ị ga-akwụsị anya n'ụzọ dị elu nke configuration na asụsụ ọrụ Ultimate Multisite, chọrọ ị chọpụta dashboard.

Miti mgbe ọtụtụ WordPress plugin ndị ọzọ, Ultimate Multisite na-agbanwe menu entry na oge ọkụ (network-level) na-enwe ahịrị anyị bụ **Ultimate Multisite** na-enwe ụfọdụ sub-menu anọ n'ime.

## Widgets Dashboard Network

Ultimate Multisite na-inject ụfọdụ widget dị mkpa n'ime dashboard oge ọkụ (network-level). Ị chọpụta dashboard a na-akwụsị **My Sites → Network Admin → Dashboard**. Widgets ndị a na-adị nwere ịnwale nkwado n'ime ịnwale maara na ihe ndị dị mkpa maka administrator network.

![Network Dashboard with Ultimate Multisite widgets](/img/admin/network-dashboard-full.png)

### Ịkpọ Ihe Ndị Ọzọ (First Steps)

![First Steps widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-first-steps.png)

Widget a na-enwe n'ụzọ dị ọhụrụ nke Ultimate Multisite ma na-enwe mma maka administrator network ịkpọ ihe ndị ọzọ dị ka ịkwado onye ịkwado (payment method) na ịchịkọta aka (test account).

### Ọzọ (Summary)

![Summary widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-summary.png)

Widget Summary na-kọwa ihe nwere n'ime onye ịkwado na ndụọrọ ha ọrụ (revenue) ọ bụ ọjọọ. MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) na-enwe mma maka ihe ndị dị nwere ike ịkwado ọtụtụ onye na onye ọrụ membership.

### Oge Agha (Activity Stream)

![Activity Stream widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-activity-stream.png)

Widget a na-kọwa ihe ndị na-eme n'ime Ultimate Multisite. Ihe ndị a na-enwe signups, cancellations, site creations, deletions, na ọrụ ndị ọzọ.

Biko ị chọpụta anyị n'ime Events section nke documentation ahụ maka mgbọrọ dị mkpa.

### Oge Agha (Right Now)

![Right Now widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-right-now.png)

Widget utiliti a nri ebe nkwado aka ọkụkọ dị n'ime mmekọrịta. Ọnụmọdụ ndị dị n'ụbọchị na-eme ihe dị n'ụbọchị anọ dị n'ime mmekọrịta. Ịgbagwa mma na akụkọ ahụ a na-eme ihe ọ bụrụ ịche ọkụkọ ọhụrụ ma ndị mmadụ.

### Nyinyere na Ndị Na-eme Ihe (News and Discussions)

![News and Discussions widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-news.png)

Widget a na-achie na nwere nyinyere Ultimate Multisite ahụ. Ịgbagwa mma ị chọpụta widget a maka ma ọ bụ ihe ndị dị n'ime update, ihe ndị dị mkpa nke security, na ihe ndị dị mkpa.

## Ultimate Multisite Dashboard

Ndeewo, dashboard-level na-eme ihe dị n'ime mmekọrịta na-eme ihe dị n'ime mmekọrịta ahụ, onye Ultimate Multisite dashboard (dị n'ime top-level menu nke Ultimate Multisite) na-eme ihe dị n'ime akụkọ ahụ.

![Ultimate Multisite Dashboard](/img/admin/um-dashboard-top.png)

### Ọrụ Ndị Ndu Ndu Na-eme Ihe (Monthly Recurring Revenue Growth)

Chart MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) na-eme ihe dị n'ime ụbwatị ọkụkọ anọ dị ka ọrụ ndị dị n'ime mmekọrịta ọ bụ ọzọ n'ụbọchị anọ dịka 12 mọdụ.

![MRR Growth chart](/img/admin/um-dashboard-mrr.png)

Ihe ọrụ ọhụrụ na-eji akụkọ biru, ọrụ ndị dị n'ime mmekọrịta na-eme ihe dị n'ime akụkọ akụkọ.

### Ịdoro Ndị Anọ (Date-range Support)

Ọ bụ eziokwu na-akọwa na ịdị mma nke ọrụ ndị a na-achie, statistics ga-anọ mma ma ọ bụ anọ dị ukwụ. Iredere date-range support na-eme ihe dị n'ime dashboard ahụ na-eme ka administrator mmekọrịta chụrụ na akụkọ na oge dị mkpa anọ dịka a.

![Date range selector and stats](/img/admin/um-dashboard-date-range.png)

Ịdoro date-range na-akọwa mọdur ihe ndị widget dị n'ime ahụ na-eme ka, onye ọrụ, ịzụ ahụ na akụkọ, ịzụ ahụ na form, na sites ndị a na-ejiwo n'ime mmekọrịta.

![Dashboard statistics widgets](/img/admin/um-dashboard-stats.png)

:::tip
Iji ka widget **Most Visited Sites** le gaa ihe, chọpụta visit counting na **Ultimate Multisite → Settings → Sites → Enable Visits Limitation and Counting**.
:::
