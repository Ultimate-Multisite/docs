# Overwàda Dashboard Overview {#dashboard-overview}

Ultimate Multisite na ọkụ **Dashboard** nwere ike ịrụ ọrụ n'ime menyu Ultimate Multisite, ma ọ bụrụ na ị chọrọ widget ndị dị n'ime Network Dashboard ahụ, ị nwere ike ịchukwu (add) widget-ahụ. Atụkọ a na-eji ịgbagba ihe ị ga ịhụ n'ebe obere ahụ.

## Network Dashboard {#the-network-dashboard}

Ultimate Multisite na-agbanwe widget ndị dị mkpa n'ime dashboard nke network ahụ, nke si nwere n'ebe **My Sites → Network Admin → Dashboard**.

![Network Dashboard with Ultimate Multisite widgets](/img/admin/network-dashboard-full.png)

Nke a bụ ihe ọ bụ na ma ọ bụ ihe widget ọ bụla na-agbanwe:

**First Steps:** Na-akọwa n'ime installation baru (fresh installation) na-ekwu okwu na ị chọrọ ka ịmata ihe ndị dị mkpa dị ka ịgbanwe cara-ọrụ payment method na ịchịkọta account uji.

![First Steps widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-first-steps.png)

**Summary:** Na-ekwu ihe nwere n'ime akụkọ (signups) na ndụi (revenue) nke ụbọchị ahụ. MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) na-agba ihe dị ka ọ bụ ihe ọrụ ọ bụla n'ime ndị mmadụ nwere membership ndị na-agbanwe ndụi.

![Summary widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-summary.png)

**Activity Stream:** Na-ekwu ihe ndị na-eme (events) n'ime Ultimate Multisite — akụkọ, gbagba, ịrụ site, ịgbagba site, na ihe ọ bụla dị ka ihe ndị ọzọ.

![Activity Stream widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-activity-stream.png)

**Right Now:** Na-akọwa ihe nwere n'ime mmadụ na sites n'ime network ahụ na-ekwu shortcut (shortcuts) ịrụ site baru ma ịrụ user.

![Right Now widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-right-now.png)

**News & Discussions:** Na-agbanwe na na-ekwu akụkọ Ultimate Multisite ndị a-azụ (latest news) na-akwụsị ihe nwere ike ịgba ọsọ, bugs mkpa, na fixes security.

![News widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-news.png)

## Ultimate Multisite Dashboard {#the-ultimate-multisite-dashboard}

Maka network dashboard na-ekwu ihe dị obere na network ahụ, maka Ultimate Multisite dashboard (n'ime top level menyu Ultimate Multisite) na-ekwu ihe nwere n'ime service ahụ.

![Ultimate Multisite Dashboard](/img/admin/um-dashboard-top.png)

**Crescimento de Receita Recorrente Mensal (Monthly Recurring Revenue Growth):** Nkebe ihe nwere ike ịle mmezi nke a na-akọwa akụkọ ahụ nke a na-akọwa 12 mọdụ kalendar. A na-akọwa ndụ ebe ọhụrụ (new revenue) na ajụjụ azụ (blue), ma anọchịrị (cancellations) na ajụjụ dị n'aka (red).

![MRR Growth chart](/img/admin/um-dashboard-mrr.png)

**Dịdo nke Mọdụ Nieme (Date-range Support):** Ọ bụ ihe ị chọrọ ka ịle data dashboard na mmezi akụkọ ahụ maka ị nwere ike ịhụ ihe ị chọrọ. Dịdo nke mmezi a na-arụ ọrụ na ihe ndị dị n'ime widgets a na-akọwa.

![Date range and dashboard stats](/img/admin/um-dashboard-date-range.png)

Ihe ndị dị n'ime widgets dashboard ahụ bụ:

- **Revenue (Receita)** — Ọtụtụ ebe receita maka mmezi ahụ na-akọwa MRR na ịle ihe ọrụ ọ bụla (per-product breakdowns).
- **New Memberships (Membreship Baru)** — Ọ bụ kaçere ndị nwere ike ịmụta baru a na-agbanwe n'ime mmezi a kacha ahụ.
- **Signups by Countries (Ịchọrọ na Ụwa)** — Akụkọ ihe mere na ọdị igwe nke ịzụ (geographic breakdown) nke ịzụ.
- **Signups by Forms (Ịchọrọ na Form)** — Ka ọ bụ kaçere ndị nwere ike ịzụ via each checkout form vs. ka ndị admin ji jiji (added by admins).
- **Most Visited Sites (Ihe Nwere Ike Na-akwụkwọ)** — Ihe ndị a na-akwụkwọ na ọkụkọ gị (your network) (ii na-agbanwe site visit counting na **Ultimate Multisite → Settings → Sites → Enable Visits Limitation and Counting**).

![Dashboard statistics](/img/admin/um-dashboard-stats.png)
