# Ọrụaka na Link-a Shareable (Link-a Bisa) {#managing-shareable-links-for-plans}

Ultimate Multisite na-eme ka ị chọrọ ịgba link-a plan direct na ndị khách của gị. Ka ị chọrọ ịdeewo ihe a, jupụ n'edee editi (edit page) anya nke plan tupu gị ma click button _Click to copy the Shareable Link_.

![Product edit page with shareable link button](/img/config/product-edit-full.png)

_Na edee editi Plan, ị ga-eme ka ị chọrọ create shareable links._

Bara ị chọpụta na ihe a na default billing frequency (nkwado nkwado mbụ) ahụ mee link. Oge a na-eme ka ọ bụrụ na nkwado nkwado mbụ gị bụ monthly, ọbụghị ya na gị link dị ka nke m ga-adị: [_**https://network.com/register/premium/**_](https://network.com/register/premium/)

Ọ bụrụ na ị chọrọ link-a shareable ahụ bụ default quarterly (nkwado nkwado ọtụtụ), ị ga-eme 3 na kpọtụrụ ya:

[_**https://network.com/register/premium/3**_](https://network.com/register/premium/3)

Ọ bụrụ na ị chọrọ a plan _premium_ bụ yearly (nkwado nkwado ọtụtụ), ị ga-eme 1 pụrụcha na akụkọ:

[_**https://network.com/register/premium/1/year**_](https://network.com/register/premium/1/year)

Bara ị chọpụta, ka ị ma ọ bụ link-a shareable ahụ bụ ihe dị ọnụ (default), ị ga-eme na-adịghị link-a ọzọ nke ọ bụla nkwado nkwado mbụ. Ị ga-chọpụta link-a nwere ike ịbụ site n'edee editi plan tupu gị.
