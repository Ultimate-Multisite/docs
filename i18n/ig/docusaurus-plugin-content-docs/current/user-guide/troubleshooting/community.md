# Naa gị jiri n'ụ Ultimate Multisite Community

Ultimate Multisite Community na-en ihe ọkụ, ọ bụrụ na ị chọrọ ịkpọ aka ndị ọzọ na Ultimate Multisite na-enweghị site n'ime, ị nwere ike ịgba n'ebe a. Ka ọ dị mma ka ị gidi: https://community.ultimatemultisite.com/home.

Na akụna welcome page ahụ, jikọ ya link “**creating your account here** ”.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Community welcome page with creating your account link -->

Ọ ga-arị ịgbanne gị n'akụna register page eto. Oge a bụ ị chọrọ ịri username, email na password. Kiri ị chọrọ Captcha ahụ ma jikọ button **Register**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Community register page with username, email, password fields and Captcha -->

Ọ bụrụ na ị đãghị register, oge a bụ ịgba profile gị. Na akụna a, ị nwere ike ịdepụta pụrụ ego gị, ri email gị, kọwa bio bi dị n'ụmụ, na ihe ndị ọzọ. Ka ọ dị mma ka ị nwere ike ịsị link social media gị ma kọwa izin (permissions) maka account gị.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Community profile creation page with photo upload and bio fields -->

Ọ bụrụ na ị na akụna “Introductions”, onye ị nwere ike ịkpọ aka gị ma ị chọrọ ịkenkene ihe bi dị n'ime ndị ọzọ. Ị nwere ike ịgbanwe post gị ma ị jikọ button **New Post**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Community Introductions channel with New Post button -->

Channels na Lounge section na “Dev Logs” channel bụrụ ihe niile nwere ike ịkpọ aka. Mana, ị ga-anọ site izin ya dị mma ka ị chọrọ ịgbanwe n'ime channels ndị ọzọ n'ime community ahụ.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Community channels showing Lounge section and Dev Logs channel -->

Ọ ga-anọ site a ga-anọ site gị ọ bụrụ na ị gwesị gị key license Ultimate Multisite gị ahụ. Ka ị chọrọ ịgbanwe, jikọ link channel private anyị ma jikọ button **Unlock**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Community private channel with Unlock button -->

Ọ ga-arị ịgbanwe n'ụlọ menya (unlock access page) anyị. Jere key lizen Ultimate Multisite tusi na bọọlụ Unlock Access, onye a bụ:

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Community unlock access page with license key input -->

Lizen gị ga-arị iji ihe ọmụmọdụ (validated), ma ị ga-anọchịrị n'ụlọ menya. Ọrụ dị ka ihe dị ka ụbọchị 24, karị ịkpọ link ahụ iji geri n'ụlọ menya ma ị chọrọ ọdị n'ime ụlọ ndị ọzọ mgbe anyị na-agbanwe ihe gị.
