# Ihe Ọmụmụ 9: Ịtọ Ọnụahịa Maka Uru {#lesson-9-pricing-for-profit}

Na Ihe Ọmụmụ 5 anyị tọrọ ọnụahịa mbụ maka plan FitSite. Ugbu a anyị na-eme ka usoro ịtọ ọnụahịa ka mma site na teknụzụ ndị na-abawanye ego, na-akwalite upgrade, ma na-ebelata churn.

## Ebe Anyị Kwụsịrị {#where-we-left-off}

FitSite enweelarị plan, template, checkout, branding, na onboarding n'ọnọdụ. Ọnụahịa mbụ bụ $49/$99/$199 kwa ọnwa. Ugbu a anyị na-eme ka ọnụahịa ahụ rụọ ọrụ nke ọma karịa.

## Ụkpụrụ Ịtọ Ọnụahịa Maka Platform Niche {#pricing-principles-for-niche-platforms}

### Tụọ Ọnụahịa Dabere na Uru, Ọ bụghị Ọnụ Mmefu {#price-on-value-not-cost}

Ọnụ mmefu hosting gị nwere ike ịbụ $5-$15 kwa saịtị onye ahịa. Nke ahụ apụtaghị na ọnụahịa gị kwesịrị ịbụ $20. Ị naghị ere hosting. Ị na-ere:

- Webụsaịtị fitness ọkachamara nke ga-efu $2,000-$5,000 iji wuo ya custom
- Mmezi na-aga n'ihu, mmelite, na nchebe ha agaghị echebara echiche
- Njirimara pụrụ iche maka niche nke builder nkịtị anaghị enye
- Ntụkwasị obi nke platform e wuru maka ụlọ ọrụ ha

Tụọ ọnụahịa dabere na uru ị na-enye, ọ bụghị ọnụ mmefu iji nye ya.

### Jikọọ Ọnụahịa na Nhọrọ Ndị Ọzọ {#anchor-to-alternatives}

Mgbe onye nwe ụlọ fitness na-enyocha FitSite, ha na-atụnyere ya na:

- **Ịkpọ web developer ọrụ**: $2,000-$5,000 n'ihu + $50-$100/ọnwa maka mmezi
- **Wix/Squarespace**: $16-$45/ọnwa mana enweghị njirimara pụrụ iche maka fitness, ha ga-ewu ya onwe ha
- **Ime ihe ọ bụla**: Ndị otu furu efu ndị na-enweghị ike ịchọta ha online

Na $49-$199/ọnwa, FitSite dị ọnụ ala karịa developer, nwere ikike karịa builder nkịtị, ma dị mma nke ukwuu karịa enweghị webụsaịtị.

## Itinye Ụdị Ọnụahịa Dị Iche Iche n'Ọrụ {#implementing-price-variations}

Ọnụahịa kwa afọ na-akwalite nkwa ma na-ebelata churn. Gaa na tab **Price Variations** nke plan ọ bụla ma tinye nhọrọ kwa afọ:

| Plan | Kwa ọnwa | Kwa afọ (kwa ọnwa) | Ngụkọta Kwa Afọ | Ego Echekwara |
|------|---------|-------------------|--------------|---------|
| Starter | $49/ọnwa | $39/ọnwa | $468/afọ | 20% belata |
| Growth | $99/ọnwa | $79/ọnwa | $948/afọ | 20% belata |
| Pro | $199/ọnwa | $159/ọnwa | $1,908/afọ | 20% belata |

Tinye field **Period Selection** na form checkout gị ka ndị ahịa nwee ike ịgbanwe n'etiti billing kwa ọnwa na kwa afọ. Lee [Form Checkout](/user-guide/configuration/checkout-forms#adding-a-period-selection-toggle).

### Ihe Mere Ọnụahịa Kwa Afọ Ji Arụ Ọrụ {#why-annual-pricing-works}

- **Maka gị**: Ego n'ihu, churn dị ala (ndị ahịa na-akwụ kwa afọ adịghị akagbu n'etiti oge ahụ ngwa ngwa)
- **Maka ha**: Ego echekwara n'ezie, amụma mmefu ego a pụrụ ịtụkwasị obi

## Ụgwọ Setup {#setup-fees}

Tụlee ụgwọ setup otu ugboro maka plan ndị gụnyere nhazi aka:

- **Starter**: Enweghị ụgwọ setup (wepụ ihe mgbochi ịbanye)
- **Growth**: $99 ụgwọ setup (na-ekpuchi nhazi custom domain na mkparịta ụka mbụ)
- **Pro**: $299 ụgwọ setup (na-ekpuchi setup ọtụtụ ọnọdụ na oku onboarding)

Hazie ụgwọ setup na ngalaba ọnụahịa nke plan ọ bụla. Lee [Ịmepụta Product Subscription Mbụ Gị](/user-guide/configuration/creating-your-first-subscription-product) maka nkọwa.

## Oge Trial {#trial-periods}

Inye trial na-ebelata nsogbu signup mana na-ebute ihe ize ndụ nke enweghị conversion. Maka FitSite:

- **trial efu ụbọchị 7** na plan Starter naanị
- Enweghị trial na Growth na Pro (ndị ahịa ndị a nwere nkwa karịa ma na-atụ anya ịkwụ ụgwọ)

Hazie trial na ngalaba ọnụahịa nke plan Starter site n'ịgbanye toggle **Offer Trial** ma tọọ ogologo oge ya ka ọ bụrụ ụbọchị 7.

### Ime Ka Trial Gbanwee {#making-trials-convert}

Trial na-arụ ọrụ naanị ma ọ bụrụ na onye ahịa ahụ hụrụ uru n'oge trial. Usoro onboarding gị (Ihe Ọmụmụ 8) dị mkpa ebe a -- e kwesịrị ịhazi oge email nnabata iji kpalite itinye aka n'ime windo ụbọchị 7 ahụ.

## Koodu Mbelata Ọnụahịa {#discount-codes}

Mepụta koodu mbelata ọnụahịa nwere atụmatụ maka ebumnuche kpọmkwem:

- **FITLAUNCH** -- 30% belata ọnwa 3 mbụ (maka mgbasa ozi mbido)
- **ANNUAL20** -- 20% belata plan kwa afọ (ma ọ bụrụ na ebelatabeghịrị ya)
- **REFERRAL** -- ọnwa 1 n'efu (maka referral ndị ahịa)

Gaa na **Ultimate Multisite > Discount Codes** iji mepụta ndị a. Lee [Ịmepụta Koodu Mbelata Ọnụahịa](/user-guide/configuration/creating-discount-codes) maka ntuziaka zuru ezu.

:::tip Do Not Discount by Default
Discounts should be strategic tools, not permanent fixtures. If everyone gets a discount, it is not a discount -- it is your real price. Use them for specific campaigns, referrals, and time-limited promotions.
:::

## Ijikwa Tax {#tax-handling}

Ọ bụrụ na ị na-arụ ọrụ n'obodo iwu chọrọ nchịkọta tax:

1. Hazie ọnụego tax na **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Taxes**
2. Tinye otu tax na plan gị
3. Gbaa mbọ hụ na invoice na-egosi tax nke ọma

Lee [Ijikwa Tax](/user-guide/payment-gateways/tax-handling) maka nhazi zuru ezu. Ọ bụrụ na ị na-ejere ndị ahịa mba ụwa ozi, tụlee [VAT addon](/addons/vat) maka nrube isi EU.

## Inyocha Ọnụahịa Gị {#reviewing-your-pricing}

Mgbe mbido gasịrị, laghachi na ọnụahịa kwa nkeji afọ. Lee:

- **Ọnụego conversion site na plan**: Ọ bụrụ na Starter na-convert nke ọma mana Growth anaghị eme ya, ọdịiche dị n'etiti ha nwere ike ịdị ukwuu
- **Ọnụego upgrade**: Ọ bụrụ na ndị ahịa ole na ole na-upgrade site na Starter gaa Growth, plan Growth nwere ike ọ gaghị enye uru mgbakwunye zuru ezu
- **Churn site na plan**: Ọ bụrụ na ndị ahịa kwa afọ na-churn obere, kwalite ọnụahịa kwa afọ nke ukwuu
- **Ọnụahịa competitor**: Gịnị ka nhọrọ ndị ọzọ na-ana? Ị nọ n'ọnọdụ ziri ezi?
