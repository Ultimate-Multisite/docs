#  איך  Úm tshawb (How to get help)

Kuv tshawb license key chu koj muaj, thiab hais txog ntawd yog tus kaus los ntawm peev xim rau peev xim los tsim Ultimate Multisite. Kuv yuav pab koj tsim kom zoo thiab tsim kom koj tshawb sau txhua yam uas koj tuaj yeem thiaj paub thaum koj tsim Ultimate Multisite.

Qhov kev tshawb sau no yog qhov uas muaj rau cov neeg uas muaj license, thiab kuv yuav hais txog qhov chaw los tshawb sau thiab qhov raws li yam uas koj yuav xav tau los tshawb sau kom peev xim los pab.

## Contact Support (Tshawb Sau Pab)

Yog koj txawm thiab tsis paub cov lus dang ntawm chaw help site, koj tuaj yeem mus los hais rau peev xim pab tshiab los ntawm kev klik icon speech bubble uas muaj hauv ib lub qij yuav tsis tas tam ntawm pe website lossis los hlub email txog support@ultimatemultisite.com tiam.

Thaum koj hais sau rau cov lus dang zoo, pls kaya kom koj pab tsim kev sib txawv ntawm koj teeb meem nrog kuv thiab muaj screenshot, error messages, lossis txhua yam uas yuav pab tau rau peev xim pab los paub thiab tsim kom koj teeb meem zoo heev. Koj tseem yuav tsum hais license key ntawm koj. Tsis muaj tsuas cov neeg uas muaj license active tab tom muaj kev pab.

## Community Site (Chaw Hlub Txawv)

Cov neeg Ultimate Multisite muaj [community site](https://community.ultimatemultisite.com/) yuav los hlub txawv rau txhua tus Ultimate Multisite users. Qhov no yog ib qho chaw uas cov neeg yuav tshawb sau, kom tsis txhob tsim kom tsis muaj txhua yam nrog nhau. Cov neeg muaj tag nrho yuav tsum hais license key Ultimate Multisite ntawm koj los ua rau cov chaw/pages txhais tau.
