# Casharka 14: Waxa Xiga {#lesson-14-what-comes-next}

Waxaad meel eber ah ka dhistay madal website oo niche ah. FitSite waa live, waxay u adeegaysaa studio-yada fitness-ka, waxayna dhalinaysaa dakhli soo noqnoqda. Casharkan ugu dambeeya wuxuu eegayaa halka laga sii socdo.

## Halkii Aan Ku Joognay {#where-we-left-off}

FitSite waa ganacsi socda oo leh macaamiil, hawlgallo, iyo jihayn koritaan. Hadda waxaan ka fikiraynaa waxa xiga.

## Ikhtiyaarka 1: Si Qoto Dheer Ugu Gal Niche-kaaga {#option-1-go-deeper-in-your-niche}

Kahor intaadan u ballaarin niche-yo cusub, tixgeli inaad si qoto dheer ugu sii gasho fitness-ka:

### Niche-yo Hoose {#sub-niches}

- **Studio-yada yoga** -- templates khaas ah oo leh astaamo meditation, retreat, iyo workshop
- **Goobaha CrossFit** -- la socodka WOD, natiijooyinka tartanka, astaamaha bulshada
- **Dugsiyada martial arts** -- darajooyinka suunka, macluumaadka tartanka, kala-sarraynta fasallada
- **Studio-yada qoob-ka-ciyaarka** -- jadwallada bandhigga, galleries-ka performance-ka, macluumaadka dharka

Niche kasta oo hoose wuxuu yeelan karaa templates u gaar ah iyo suurtagal ahaan brand u gaar ah, dhammaantoodna waxay ku shaqaynayaan isla shabakadda Ultimate Multisite.

### Astaamo Premium ah {#premium-features}

Dhis astaamo ay studio-yada fitness-ku lacag dheeraad ah ku bixin doonaan:

- **Isdhexgalka booking-ka fasalka** -- isdhexgal qoto dheer oo lala yeesho madallada booking-ka caanka ah
- **Portal-ka xubinta** -- aag logged-in ah oo loogu talagalay xubnaha gym-ka, oo leh jadwallo iyo kheyraad
- **Templates-ka blog-ka fitness-ka** -- qaab-dhismeedyo content oo hore loo dhisay oo loogu talagalay content marketing-ka fitness-ka
- **Isdhexgalka social media** -- si toos ah u posting-gareynta jadwallada fasalka iyo cusboonaysiinta

### Ballaarinta Juqraafiyeed {#geographic-expansion}

Haddii aad si maxalli ah ku bilowday, u ballaari:

- Magaalooyin kale oo dalkaaga ah
- Dalal kale (isticmaal [astaamaha turjumaadda](/user-guide/localization/translating) ee Ultimate Multisite si aad u taageerto luqado badan)

## Ikhtiyaarka 2: U Ballaari Niche-yo Kugu Dhow {#option-2-expand-to-adjacent-niches}

Habka aad u raacday studio-yada fitness-ka wuxuu u shaqeeyaa niche kasta. Niche-kaaga labaad wuu fudud yahay sababtoo ah hore ayaad u leedahay:

- Kaabayaasha farsamada
- Khibrad hawlgallo
- Playbook la caddeeyay

### Niche-yo U Dhow Fitness-ka {#adjacent-niches-to-fitness}

- **Wellness iyo spa** -- massage therapists, day spas, xarumaha wellness-ka
- **Naadiyada sports-ka** -- kooxaha sports-ka hiwaayadda ah, leagues, naadiyada madadaalada
- **Dhakhaatiirta caafimaadka** -- chiropractors, physiotherapists, nutritionists

Kuwani waxay la wadaagaan fitness-ka is-dulsaarka dhagaystayaasha, sidaas darteed marketing-kaaga iyo iskaashiyadaadu way sii socon karaan.

### Niche-yo Gebi Ahaan Cusub {#completely-new-niches}

Adeegso framework-ka Casharka 2 si aad u qiimeyso niche kasta:

1. Ma u baahan yihiin websites laakiin ma ku dhibtoonayaan inay helaan kuwo wanaagsan?
2. Ma awoodaan subscription bille ah?
3. Ma wada hadlaan?

Niche kasta oo cusub wuxuu u baahan yahay:

- Templates cusub oo loogu talagalay warshaddaas
- Qaab-dhismeedyo plan cusub oo ka tarjumaya baahiyaha warshaddaas
- Marketing cusub iyo channels helitaanka macaamiisha
- Content onboarding cusub iyo aqoonta support-ka

Kaabayaasha madasha, billing-ka, iyo hababka hawlgalladu sidii ayay ahaanayaan.

## Ikhtiyaarka 3: Dhis Madal ka Kooban Madallo {#option-3-build-a-platform-of-platforms}

Marka la gaaro scale, waxaad hal shabakad Ultimate Multisite ah ku maamuli kartaa brand-yo niche oo badan:

- `fitsite.com` -- studio-yada fitness-ka
- `menusite.com` -- maqaayadaha
- `lawsite.com` -- shirkadaha sharciga

Brand kasta wuxuu leeyahay templates, plans, iyo marketing u gaar ah, laakiin waxay wadaagaan kaabayaal, billing, iyo habab hawlgallo. Halkan ayay dhaqaalaha Ultimate Multisite ku noqdaan kuwo awood leh -- hal madal, verticals badan.

### Multi-Network {#multi-network}

Kala-sooc buuxa oo u dhexeeya niche-yada, tixgeli [addon-ka Multi-Network](/addons/multinetwork) kaas oo kuu oggolaanaya inaad hal installation ka maamusho shabakado WordPress oo badan.

## Ikhtiyaarka 4: Iibi Ganacsiga {#option-4-sell-the-business}

Madal website oo niche ah oo leh dakhli soo noqnoqda, churn hoose, iyo boos suuqa oo cad waa hanti qiimo leh. Ganacsiyada SaaS caadiyan waxay ku iibsamaan 3-5x dakhliga sannadlaha ah.

FitSite leh 200 macaamiil oo ah $89 ARPU:

- **MRR**: $17,800
- **ARR**: $213,600
- **Qiimaha iibka ee suurtagalka ah**: $640,000 - $1,068,000

Tani ma aha natiijo la dammaanad qaaday, laakiin waa mid macquul ah oo loogu talagalay madal niche oo si wanaagsan loo maamulo.

## Waxa Aad Baratay {#what-you-have-learned}

Koorsadani waxay ku bartay hab lagu celcelin karo:

1. **Dooro niche** leh baahiyo cad, awood ay ku bixiyaan lacag, iyo bulsho
2. **Xaqiiji** kahor intaadan dhisin
3. **Dhis madal la waafajiyay** oo leh templates, plans, iyo branding ku gaar ah niche-ka
4. **Abuur khibrad aan xannibaad lahayn** laga bilaabo signup ilaa onboarding
5. **Qiimee iyadoo lagu salaynayo qiimaha** adigoo adeegsanaya tiers istiraatiiji ah, trials, iyo discounts
6. **Si habaysan u launch-garee** adigoo leh soft launch iyo checklist
7. **Hel macaamiil** adigoo maraya channels ku gaar ah niche-ka
8. **Maamul hawlgallada** adigoo adeegsanaya routines waara
9. **Scale-garee** kaabayaasha, dakhliga, iyo kooxda markaad korayso

Niche-ku waa istiraatiijiyadda. Madashu waa qalabka. Ultimate Multisite ayaa qalabka suurto geliya. Fahamkaaga niche-ka ayaa ka dhiga mid qiimo leh.
