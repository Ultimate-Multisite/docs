# Casharka 8: Soo-dhaweynta Macmiilka {#lesson-8-customer-onboarding}

In macmiil la diiwaangeliyo waa kala bar shaqada oo keliya. Haddii ay galaan, dareemaan culays, oo aanay mar dambe soo noqon, waad wayday. Casharkani wuxuu naqshadaynayaa khibradda ka beddesha isdiiwaangelin cusub macmiil firfircoon oo ku xiran.

## Meeshii Aan Ku Joognay {#where-we-left-off}

FitSite si buuxda ayaa loo calaamadeeyay, waxaana jira socod checkout oo shaqaynaya. Hadda waxaan diiradda saaraynaa waxa dhaca ka dib marka milkiilaha studio jimicsi uu dhammeeyo isdiiwaangelinta oo markii ugu horreysay yimaado goobtiisa cusub.

## Sababta Soo-dhaweyntu Muhiim u Tahay {#why-onboarding-matters}

30-ka daqiiqo ee ugu horreeya ka dib isdiiwaangelinta ayaa go'aamiya haddii macmiilku sii joogo ama ka tago. Milkiilaha studio jimicsi oo:

- Gala oo arka goob horeba ugu eg website jimicsi → wuu joogayaa
- Si sax ah u yaqaan waxa xiga ee la sameeyo → wuu joogayaa
- Ku luma WordPress Dashboard guud → wuu baxayaa

Qaababkaaga niche-ka (Casharka 4) ayaa qabta qodobka koowaad. Casharkani wuxuu qabtaa kan labaad.

## Khibradda Galitaanka Koowaad {#the-first-login-experience}

### Welcome Dashboard Widget {#welcome-dashboard-widget}

Abuur Dashboard widget gaar ah oo salaama macaamiisha cusub kuna hago dejinta. Tani waa inay si muuqata u soo baxdaa marka ay markii ugu horreysay galaan.

**FitSite Quick Start:**

1. **Ku dar magaca studio-gaaga iyo astaantaada** -- Link u dir Customizer ama dejimaha Aqoonsiga Goobta
2. **Cusboonaysii jadwalka fasalladaada** -- Link si toos ah ugu dir tifaftiraha bogga Classes
3. **Ku dar tababarayaashaada** -- Link u dir tifaftiraha bogga Trainers
4. **Deji faahfaahinta xiriirkaaga** -- Link u dir tifaftiraha bogga Contact
5. **Horay u eeg goobtaada** -- Link u dir qaybta hore

Tallaabo kasta waxay si toos ah ugu xirmaysaa bogga ama dejinta khuseeya. Looma baahna in menu-yada laga dhex raadiyo.

### Fududee Dashboard-ka {#simplify-the-dashboard}

Macaamiisha cusub uma baahna inay arkaan shay kasta oo menu-ga WordPress ah. Tixgeli:

- Qarinta waxyaabaha menu-ga ee aan khusayn maamulka goob jimicsi (tusaale, Comments haddii aan la isticmaalin)
- Dib-u-habaynta menu-ga si waxyaabaha ugu badan ee la isticmaalo loo hormariyo
- Ku darista calaamado menu oo gaar ah oo macno u leh niche-ka ("Studio-gaaga" halkii laga isticmaali lahaa "Muuqaal")

Addon-ka [Plugin & Theme Manager](/addons/plugin-and-theme-manager) wuxuu kaa caawin karaa xakamaynta waxa macaamiishu arkaan.

## Taxanaha Email-ka Soo-dhaweynta {#welcome-email-sequence}

Hal email soo-dhaweyn ah kuma filna. Deji taxane macaamiisha ku haga toddobaadkooda koowaad:

### Email 1: Soo-dhaweyn (Isla markiiba ka dib isdiiwaangelinta) {#email-1-welcome-immediately-after-signup}

- Mawduuc: "Ku soo dhawoow FitSite -- website-ka studio-gaagu wuu shaqaynayaa"
- Nuxur: Link-ga galitaanka, tallaabooyinka quick start, link-ga ilaha caawinta
- Cod: Farxad leh, dhiirrigelin leh, oo si gaar ah ugu saabsan jimicsi

### Email 2: Guulo Degdeg ah (Maalinta 1) {#email-2-quick-wins-day-1}

- Mawduuc: "3 shay oo marka hore lagu sameeyo FitSite-kaaga"
- Nuxur: Ku dar astaantaada, cusboonaysii sawirka hero ee bogga hore, ku dar jadwalka fasalladaada
- Ku dar sawirro shaashad ah oo si sax ah u muujinaya meesha la gujiyo

### Email 3: Ka Dhig Mid Adiga kuu Gaar ah (Maalinta 3) {#email-3-make-it-yours-day-3}

- Mawduuc: "Ka dhig goobtaada jimicsi mid soo baxda"
- Nuxur: Habee midabbada, ku dar sawirrada tababarayaasha, qor sheekada studio-gaaga
- Link u dir tusaalooyin goobo jimicsi oo heer sare ah oo ku jira platform-ka

### Email 4: Si Toos ah u Daahfur (Maalinta 7) {#email-4-go-live-day-7}

- Mawduuc: "Diyaar ma u tahay inaad FitSite-kaaga la wadaagto adduunka?"
- Nuxur: Liis hubin ah waxa la xaqiijinayo ka hor wadaagista, sida loo xiro domain gaar ah (haddii aad ku jirto Growth/Pro), talooyin wadaagis bulsho

:::tip Email Automation
Isticmaal [Webhooks](/user-guide/integrations/webhooks) ama [Zapier](/user-guide/integrations/zapier) si aad email-ladan uga kiciso platform-kaaga suuqgeynta email-ka. Tani waxay ku siinaysaa xakameyn dheeraad ah oo ku saabsan waqtiga waxayna kuu oggolaanaysaa inaad la socoto ka-qaybgalka.
:::

## Ilaha Caawinta {#help-resources}

Abuur nuxur caawimo oo niche-gaar ah oo ka jawaaba su'aalaha milkiilayaasha studio-yada jimicsigu dhab ahaantii weydiiyaan:

### Maqaallada Saldhigga Aqoonta {#knowledge-base-articles}

- "Sida loo cusboonaysiiyo jadwalka fasalladaada"
- "Ku darista iyo tafatirka profiles-ka tababarayaasha"
- "Beddelidda astaanta iyo midabbada studio-gaaga"
- "Ku xiridda magaca domain-kaaga gaarka ah" (loogu talagalay macaamiisha Growth/Pro)
- "Ku darista widget booking goobtaada"

U qor kuwan isticmaaleyaasha aan farsamo-yaqaanno ahayn. Isticmaal sawirro shaashad ah. Ka fogow eray-bixinta WordPress.

### Socod-muuqaallo Video ah {#video-walkthroughs}

Duubisyo shaashad gaaban (2-3 daqiiqo) oo muujinaya:

- Galitaanka koowaad iyo jihaynta
- Tafatirka bogga hore
- Cusboonaysiinta jadwalka fasallada
- Ku darista tababare cusub

Kuwani uma baahna inay noqdaan wax-soo-saar si aad ah loo qurxiyay. Waxa muhiimka ah waa inay cad yihiin, caawinayaan, oo niche-gaar yihiin.

## Bogga Account {#the-account-page}

Ultimate Multisite waxaa ku jira [Bogga Account](/user-guide/client-management/account-page) oo macmiilku wajaho halkaas oo macaamiishu ku maamulaan subscription-kooda. Habee tan si ay:

- U muujiso qorshahooda FitSite ee hadda
- U soo bandhigto xulashooyin upgrade ah oo leh faa'iidooyin jimicsi-gaar ah
- U bixiso taariikhda billing-ka iyo soo-dejinta invoice-yada
- Link ugu dirto ilaha caawinta

## Cabbiridda Guusha Soo-dhaweynta {#measuring-onboarding-success}

La soco tilmaamayaashan si aad u ogaato haddii soo-dhaweyntaadu shaqaynayso:

- **Heerka hawlgelinta**: Boqolkiiba imisa ka mid ah isdiiwaangelinnadu dhab ahaantii habeeyaan goobtooda toddobaadka koowaad gudihiisa?
- **Galitaannada toddobaadka koowaad**: Immisa jeer ayuu macmiil cusub galaa toddobaadkiisa koowaad?
- **Tigidhada taageerada ee macaamiisha cusub**: Mug sare wuxuu ka dhigan yahay in soo-dhaweyntaadu leedahay goldaloolooyin
- **U-beddelka tijaabada ilaa lacag-bixinta**: Haddii aad bixiso tijaabooyin, boqolkiiba imisa ayaa u beddelma?

## Shabakadda FitSite Ilaa Hadda {#the-fitsite-network-so-far}

```
Shabakadda FitSite
├── WordPress Multisite (hab subdomain)
├── Ultimate Multisite (la habeeyay + la calaamadeeyay)
├── Domain-ka Platform-ka (fitsite.com + wildcard SSL)
├── Qaababka Goobta (Studio Essential, Gym Pro, Fitness Chain)
├── Badeecadaha (Starter, Growth, Pro + Order Bumps)
├── Socodka Checkout (niche-gaar ah, la tijaabiyay)
├── Calaamadeyn (galitaan, dashboard, emails, invoices, goob suuqgeyn)
├── Soo-dhaweynta Macmiilka
│   ├── Quick Start Dashboard widget
│   ├── Dashboard la fududeeyay oo loogu talagalay maamulka goob jimicsi
│   ├── Taxane soo-dhaweyn oo 4-email ah
│   ├── Saldhig aqoon oo niche-gaar ah
│   ├── Socod-muuqaallo video ah
│   └── Bog Account oo la habeeyay
└── Diyaar u ah istaraatiijiyadda qiimeynta (casharka xiga)
```

## Waxa Aan Ku Dhisnay Casharkan {#what-we-built-this-lesson}

- **Khibrad galitaan-koowaad oo la hago** oo leh Quick Start widget
- **Dashboard la fududeeyay** oo diiradda saaraya hawlaha maamulka goob jimicsi
- **Taxane email soo-dhaweyn ah** oo macaamiisha ku haga toddobaadkooda koowaad
- **Ilo caawimo oo niche-gaar ah** oo loo qoray milkiilayaasha studio-yada jimicsiga ee aan farsamo-yaqaanno ahayn
- **Cabbirro soo-dhaweyn** si loola socdo loona hagaajiyo khibradda
