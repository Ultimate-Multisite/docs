# Cashar 4: Dhisidda Templates Niche ah

Templates waa udub-dhexaadka qiimaha aad u soo bandhigto niche-kaaga. Milkiile studio fitness ah oo isdiiwaangeliya oo arka site horeba ugu eg website fitness -- leh jadwalada fasallada, profiles tababarayaal, iyo sawirrada saxda ah -- aad ayuu uga badan yahay inuu sii joogo marka loo eego mid arka bog madhan.

## Meeshii Aan Ku Harnay {#where-we-left-off}

Waxaan haysannaa network FitSite shaqaynaya oo Ultimate Multisite la rakibay lana habeeyay. Hadda waxaan dhisaynaa templates-ka ka dhigaya FitSite inuu u ekaado mid si gaar ah loogu dhisay ganacsiyada fitness-ka.

## Sababta Templates Niche Ah Muhiim U Yihiin {#why-niche-templates-matter}

Templates guud waxay macaamiishaada ku qasbaan inay qabtaan shaqada adag: inay ogaadaan bogagga ay u baahan yihiin, qaab-dhismeedka nuxurka ee shaqeeya, iyo sida looga dhigo inuu si sax ah ugu ekaado warshaddooda. Templates niche ah waxay meesha ka saaraan caqabaddaas.

Milkiile studio fitness ah oo isku diiwaangeliya FitSite waa inuu arkaa:

- Homepage u eg website studio fitness ah
- Bogag loogu talagalay fasallo, jadwalo, tababarayaal, iyo qiimeyn oo hore loo abuuray
- Sawirro iyo nuxur placeholder ah oo ku habboon warshaddooda
- Naqshad u muuqata mid xirfad leh oo la jaanqaadaysa summadda fitness-ka

Waxay buuxiyaan faahfaahintooda. Kama bilaabaan eber.

## Qorshaynta Templates-kaaga {#planning-your-templates}

Ka hor intaadan dhisin, go'aanso templates-ka aad bixinayso. FitSite ahaan, waxaan abuuri doonaa saddex:

### Template 1: Studio Essential {#template-1-studio-essential}

Loogu talagalay studios yaryar iyo tababarayaal shaqsiyeed.

- **Homepage** leh sawir hero ah, muujinta fasallada, iyo baaq ficil
- Bogga **About** oo leh sheekada studio-ga iyo hadafka
- Bogga **Classes** oo leh qaab jadwal
- Bogga **Trainers** oo leh kaararka profiles
- Bogga **Contact** oo leh khariidad goob iyo foom
- **Naqshad nadiif ah, casri ah** oo leh nidaam midabbo ku habboon fitness

### Template 2: Gym Pro {#template-2-gym-pro}

Loogu talagalay gyms dhisan oo leh adeegyo badan.

- Wax kasta oo ku jira Studio Essential, iyo sidoo kale:
- Bogga **Membership** oo leh miis qiimeyn
- Bogga **Gallery** oo loogu talagalay sawirrada xarunta
- Qaybta **Blog** oo loogu talagalay talooyin fitness iyo warar
- Qaybta **Testimonials** ee homepage
- Ikhtiyaarro **summadeyn aad u muuqata**

### Template 3: Fitness Chain {#template-3-fitness-chain}

Loogu talagalay hawlgallo leh goobo badan.

- Wax kasta oo ku jira Gym Pro, iyo sidoo kale:
- Bogga **Locations** oo leh liisaska xarumo badan
- Template bog-hoosaad **Franchise/location**
- **Summadeyn dhexe** oo leh faahfaahin gaar u ah goobta
- **Tusmada shaqaalaha** ee goobaha kala duwan

## Dhisidda Site Template ah {#building-a-template-site}

Gudaha Ultimate Multisite, template waa si fudud WordPress site loo habeeyay sida aad rabto sites-ka macaamiisha cusub inay u ekaadaan. Waa sidan sida loo abuuro mid:

### Tallaabo 1: Abuur Site-ka Template-ka {#step-1-create-the-template-site}

1. U gudub **Sites > Add New** gudaha admin-ka network-kaaga
2. Abuur site la yiraahdo `template-studio-essential`
3. Site-kan wuxuu noqdaa goobtaada shaqo

### Tallaabo 2: Rakib oo Habee Theme-ka {#step-2-install-and-configure-the-theme}

U wareeg dashboard-ka site-ka template-ka oo:

1. Dhaqaaji theme ku habboon ganacsiyada fitness-ka
2. Habee dejimaha theme-ka, midabbada, iyo typography
3. Dejiso header-ka iyo footer-ka leh navigation ku habboon fitness

:::tip Theme Selection
Choose a theme that is flexible enough to look good for fitness but not so complex that your customers cannot manage it. Themes like Astra, GeneratePress, or Kadence work well because they are lightweight, customizable, and well-supported.
:::

### Tallaabo 3: Abuur Bogagga {#step-3-create-the-pages}

Dhiso bog kasta adigoo leh:

- **Nuxur placeholder ah** oo si dabiici ah ugu akhrismaya fitness ("Ku soo dhowow [Magaca Studio]" ma aha "Lorem ipsum")
- **Sawirro placeholder ah** oo laga helo sites sawirro stock bilaash ah oo muujinaya hawlo fitness
- **Layouts shaqaynaya** iyadoo la adeegsanayo page builder ama block editor

Ka dhig nuxurka placeholder-ka mid tilmaam bixiya halka ay suurtogal tahay. Halkii aad ka qori lahayd qoraal buuxin guud ah, qor nuxur sida: "Tan ku beddel sharaxaad kooban oo ku saabsan studio-gaaga iyo waxa ka dhigaya mid gaar ah. Xus falsafaddaada tababarka, sannadaha khibradda, ama waxa clients filan karaan."

### Tallaabo 4: Habee Plugins {#step-4-configure-plugins}

Rakib oo dhaqaaji plugins ay studios fitness u baahan yihiin:

- Booking ama scheduling plugin (haddii ay ku habboon tahay heerka plan-kaaga)
- Contact form plugin
- SEO plugin (hore loogu habeeyay default-yo la xiriira fitness)

### Tallaabo 5: Calaamadee Sida Template {#step-5-mark-as-template}

1. U gudub **Ultimate Multisite > Sites**
2. Wax ka beddel site-ka template-ka
3. Daar toggle-ka **Site Template**

Ku celi habkan template kasta oo aad rabto inaad bixiso.

## Liiska Hubinta Tayada Template-ka {#template-quality-checklist}

Ka hor inta aan template la heli karin, xaqiiji:

- [ ] Dhammaan bogaggu si sax ah bay u furmaan oo waxay u muuqdaan xirfad
- [ ] Nuxurka placeholder-ku waa waxtar leh oo niche-gaar ah
- [ ] Sawirradu waa ku habboon yihiin oo si sax ah ayaa loo shatiyeeyay
- [ ] Jawaab-celinta mobile-ka waxay ka shaqaysaa dhammaan bogagga
- [ ] Navigation-ku waa macquul oo dhammaystiran
- [ ] Foomamka contact way shaqeeyaan
- [ ] Ma jiraan links jaban ama assets maqan
- [ ] Xawaaraha load-ka boggu waa la aqbali karaa
