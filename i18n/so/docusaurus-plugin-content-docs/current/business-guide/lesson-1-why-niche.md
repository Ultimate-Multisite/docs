# Casharka 1: Sababta Niche-ku u Guuleysto

Ka hor inta aynaan taaban wax software ah, waxaad u baahan tahay inaad fahanto sababta dhisidda wax loogu talagalay dhagaystayaal gaar ah ay u tahay go'aanka keliya ee ugu muhiimsan ee aad qaadan doonto.

## Dhibaatada Iibinta Website-yo Guud {#the-problem-with-selling-generic-websites}

Haddii aad bixiso "website-yo qof walba u ah," waxaad la tartamaysaa:

- Wix, Squarespace, iyo WordPress.com dhanka is-adeegga
- Kumannaan freelancer oo ku jira dhisidda custom
- Fiverr iyo Upwork dhanka qiimaha

Kuma guuleysan kartid qiime. Kuma guuleysan kartid features. Suuqa website-yada guud waa tartan hoos u dhacaya.

## Waxa Isbeddela Marka Aad Doorato Niche {#what-changes-when-you-pick-a-niche}

Marka aad dhisto platform si gaar ah loogu talagalay fitness studios (ama niche kasta), wax walba waxay u wareegaan dhankaaga:

### Waxaad Xallisaa Dhibaatooyin Dhab ah {#you-solve-real-problems}

Milkiilaha fitness studio ma rabo "website." Waxay rabaan:

- Jadwal fasallo oo si otomaatig ah u cusboonaysiiya
- Hab xubnuhu online ugu qabsan karaan sessions
- Profile-yada trainer-ka oo u muuqda xirfad
- Isdhexgal la leh nidaamkooda booking
- Site u eg inuu ka tirsan yahay brand fitness ah, ee aan ahayn template guud

Marka platform-kaagu xalliyo dhibaatooyinkan gaarka ah si diyaar ah, mar dambe ma iibinaysid website. Waxaad iibinaysaa xal.

### Waxaad Qaadi Kartaa Lacag Badan {#you-can-charge-more}

WordPress site guud wuxuu u iibsan karaa $500-$2,000 mashruuc hal mar ah ahaan. Platform niche ah oo xalliya dhibaatooyin industry-gaar ah wuxuu qaadi karaa $49-$199/bishii sida subscription soo noqnoqda, sababtoo ah qiimuhu waa mid socda oo gaar ah.

### Waxaad Noqotaa Khabiirka {#you-become-the-expert}

Marka aad diiradda saarto hal industry, waxaad barataa luqaddeeda, meelaha xanuunka leh, iyo qaababka wax iibsigeeda. Milkiilayaasha fitness studio way is la hadlaan. Marka midkood helo platform shaqeeya, waxay u sheegaan kuwa kale. Hadal ku faafta niche gudihiisa waa awood badan sababtoo ah bulshadu way isku dhowdahay.

### Suuq-geyntaadu Way Fududaanaysaa {#your-marketing-gets-easier}

Halkii aad ka oran lahayd "waxaan website-yo u dhisnaa ganacsiyada," waxaad oranaysaa "waxaan website-yo u dhisnaa fitness studios." Landing page-kaagu si toos ah ayuu ula hadlaa milkiilayaasha gym-ka. Templates-kaagu waxay u egyihiin fitness sites. Testimonials-kaagu waxay ka yimaadaan trainers iyo milkiilayaasha studio. Qayb kasta oo suuq-geyn ah si ka xoog badan ayay u dhacdaa sababtoo ah waa gaar.

## Fursadda FitSite {#the-fitsite-opportunity}

Aan ku eegno industry-ga fitness-ka muraayaddan:

### Cabbirka Suuqa {#market-size}

- Waxaa jira in ka badan 100,000 fitness facilities gudaha United States oo keliya
- Intooda badan waa ganacsiyo yaryar oo madax-bannaan loo leeyahay
- Badankoodu ama website ma laha, ama waxay leeyihiin website liita, ama website qaali ah oo aysan iyagu cusboonaysiin karin

### Xalalka Hadda Jira {#current-solutions}

- **Dhisayaal guud** (Wix, Squarespace): Ma laha features fitness-gaar ah, milkiilayaasha studio-na way ku dhibtoonayaan inay dhisaan waxa ay u baahan yihiin
- **Horumarin custom ah**: Qaali, hal-mar ah, adag in la dayactiro
- **Platforms industry-gaar ah**: Wax yar ayaa jira, badankooduna waxay ku xiran yihiin nidaamyada booking halkii ay bixin lahaayeen web presence dhammaystiran

### Farqiga {#the-gap}

Waxaa jira farqi cad oo u dhexeeya "dhise website guud" iyo "horumarin custom ah oo qaali ah" ee ganacsiyada fitness-ka. Platform siinaya milkiilayaasha studio website xirfad leh, fitness-gaar ah, oo leh features-ka ay u baahan yihiin -- qiime macquul u ah ganacsi yar -- ayaa buuxinaya farqigaas.

Tani waa fursadda FitSite bartilmaameedsan doonto.

## Qodobka Muhiimka ah {#key-takeaway}

Doorashada niche ma aha inaad naftaada xaddidayso. Waa inaad dadaalkaaga ku diiradda saarto meesha uu saamaynta ugu badan leeyahay. Platform niche ah oo u adeega 500 customers qiime $99/bishii ah waa ganacsi $600,000/sanadkii. Uma baahnid malaayiin customers ah. Waxaad u baahan tahay kuwa saxda ah.

## Waxa Aan Ku Dhisnay Casharkan {#what-we-built-this-lesson}

Wali shaqo farsamo ma jirto -- laakiin hadda waxaad haysataa aasaaska istiraatiijiga ah:

- **Sababta niche-ku u guuleysto**: gaar ahaanshuhu wuxuu ka adkaadaa guud ahaanshaha xagga qiimaha, pricing, suuq-geynta, iyo hadal-ku-faafka
- **Fikradda FitSite**: platform website ah oo si gaar ah loogu dhisay fitness studios
- **Fursadda suuqa**: suuq weyn oo la gaari karo, aan si ku filan loogu adeegin, farqi cad
