# Shaandheeye: wu_sso_enabled {#filter-wussoenabled}

Daar/dami awoodda single-sign-on ee ka dhexaysa domains kala duwan.

Shaandhee qiimahan si aad single-sign-on gabi ahaanba u damiso, ama beddelkeeda si shuruudaysan ugu daarto.

## Halbeegyada {#parameters}

| Magac | Nooc | Sharaxaad |
|------|------|-------------|
| $enabled | `bool` | SSO ma la daarayaa? True haddii la daarayo, false-ish haddii la daminayo. |

### Laga bilaabo {#since}

- 2.0.11
### Isha {#source}

Waxaa lagu qeexay [`inc/sso/class-sso.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite/blob/main/inc/sso/class-sso.php#L110) safka 110


## Soo celinta {#returns}
Haddii SSO la daaray iyo in kale.
