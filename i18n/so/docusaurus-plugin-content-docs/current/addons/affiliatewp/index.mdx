# AffiliateWP

Multisite Ultimate: Isdhexgalka AffiliateWP

Adeegso AffiliateWP awoodda leh si aad u kobciso saldhigga macaamiisha ee Shabakaddaada Ultimo!

## Rakibid

1. Soo geli 'ultimate-multisite-git ' galka '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Ka hawlgeli kordhiyaha adigoo adeegsanaya liiska 'Kordhiyeyaasha' ee WordPress
3. Raac Hagaha tallaabo-tallaabo ah si aad kordhiyaha u dejiso

## Hagaha Isticmaalka

[AffiliateWP](https://affiliatewp.com/) wuxuu noqday xalalka ugu badan ee la adeegsado marka loo dhisayo barnaamij la-hawlgalayaal ganacsigaaga khadka tooska ah. Ultimate Multisite: AffiliateWP waa buundada kuu suuragelin doonta inaad isla taas u dhisto shabakaddaada qiimaha leh.

## Bilaabid

Ugu horreyn, **waxaad u baahan tahay inaad haysato** [**AffiliateWP**](https://affiliatewp.com/) **oo firfircoon**! Ma awoodi doontid inaad isticmaasho ku-darkeenna haddii aadan haysan **kordhiyaha la isdhexgala**. Haddii aadan qorshayn inaad macaamiishaada u oggolaato inay sidoo kale ku isticmaalaan bogaggooda, AffiliateWP waa inuu firfircoon yahay oo keliya boggaaga ugu weyn.

Sidoo kale, waxaad u baahan doontaa in **Ultimate Multisite** iyo **Ultimate Multisite: AffiliateWP** labadaba **la hawlgeliyo** **shabakadda oo dhan**.

## Ku darista Ultimate Multisite sida Isdhexgal AffiliateWP

Boggaaga ugu weyn, tag Dejinta Affiliates, ka dibna tab-ka Isdhexgalka, calaamadee Ultimate Multisite. Tani waxay u sheegi doontaa [AffiliateWP](https://affiliatewp.com/) inuu bilaabo daba-galka booqashooyinka iyo beddelashooyinka ka imanaya socodka is-diiwaangelinta Ultimate Multisite.

<!-- Sawir-qaadis lama heli karo: tab-ka dejinta Isdhexgalka AffiliateWP oo leh sanduuqa calaamadeynta Ultimate Multisite -->

_Calaamadee sanduuqa Ultimate Multisite ee hoose oo kaydi_

Taasi waa inta badan waxa loo baahan yahay oo dhan. Booqashooyinka ka imanaya xiriiriyeyaasha tixraaca waa la daba-gali doonaa, marka isticmaalahaasi dambe sameeyo lacag-bixintiisa koowaad, beddelashada waa la abuuri doonaa waxaana dhinac loo dhigi doonaa guddiga la-hawlgalaha mas'uulka ka ah beddelashada.

Si kastaba ha ahaatee, kaliya lacag-bixinta koowaad ee laga helo isticmaalaha cusub ee la abuuray ayaa la daba-gali doonaa. Si aad u suurtageliso lacag-bixinnada tixraaca ee soo noqnoqda, waxaad u baahan doontaa [AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals Add-on](https://affiliatewp.com/add-ons/pro/recurring-referrals/)

## Suurtagelinta Tixraacyada Soo Noqnoqda

Kadib markaad rakibto [AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals Add-on](https://affiliatewp.com/add-ons/pro/recurring-referrals/), tag tab-ka Tixraacyada Soo Noqnoqda ee Dejinta Affiliates, oo suurtageli isticmaalka tixraacyada soo noqnoqda.

_Sida ugu fudud ee ay noqon karto: Suurtageli Tixraacyada Soo Noqnoqda, waadna diyaar tahay!_

Mar kale, taasi waa waxa oo dhan ee loo baahan yahay!

Hadda, mar kasta oo lacag-bixin cusub lagu helo Account la tixraacay, la-hawlgalaha mas'uulka ah si toos ah ayuu u heli doonaa qaybtiisa!
