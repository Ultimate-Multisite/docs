# Domain Seller

**Multisite Ultimate: Domain Seller** wuxuu shabakaddaada WordPress Multisite u beddelaa madal awood badan oo lagu iibiyo domain-yo. U oggolow macaamiisha inay si toos ah u diiwaangeliyaan domain-yo gaar ah inta lagu jiro is-diiwaangelinta ama Dashboard-kooda, iyadoo la hubinayo helitaanka waqtiga-dhabta ah iyo qiimeyn hufan.

### Maxaa loo doortaa Domain Seller?

Diiwaangelinta domain-ku waa mid ka mid ah iib-kordhisyada ugu badan ee laga codsado madal kasta oo WaaS (Website as a Service) ah. Halkii aad macaamiishaada u diri lahayd diiwaangeliye dhinac saddexaad ah, waxaad si toos ah u bixin kartaa domain-yo — adigoo dakhli badan ku haynaya shabakaddaada isla markaana macaamiisha siinaya khibrad aan carqalad lahayn.

### Astaamaha Muhiimka ah

#### Taageerada Diiwaangeliyeyaal Domain oo Badan
* **OpenSRS** — Diiwaangelin, cusboonaysiin, iyo maamul buuxa
* **Namecheap** — Isdhexgal dhammaystiran oo leh WhoisGuard iyo tijaabinta sandbox
* **HostAfrica** — Raadinta domain, qiimeyn, diiwaangelin, wareejin, DNS, iyo ilaalinta ID iyada oo loo marayo Domains Reseller API
* **Openprovider** — Qiimeynta reseller, diiwaangelin, cusboonaysiin, wareejinno, DNS, asturnaanta WHOIS, iyo isku-dubaridka TLD
* **Hostinger** — Raadinta iyo diiwaangelinta domain iyadoo la adeegsanayo token-ka isdhexgalka Hostinger ee la wadaago
* **Qaab-dhismeed La Ballaarin Karo** — Waxaa lagu dhisay qaabka Integration Registry, taasoo fududaynaysa in lagu daro bixiyeyaal dheeraad ah
* **Tijaabinta Xiriirka ee Ku Dhex-dhisan** — Xaqiiji aqoonsiyada bixiyaha iyo isku-xirnaanta ka hor inta aan la gelin live

#### Wizard Dejinta oo Hagaya
* **Soo-dhaweyn hal-gujin ah** — Wuxuu kugu hagayaa habaynta bixiyaha, soo-dejinta TLD, iyo abuurista product domain
* **Isku-dubarid TLD oo toos ah** — Wuxuu soo dejiyaa dhammaan TLD-yada la heli karo iyo qiimeynta wholesale ee bixiyeyaashaada ku xiran
* **Abuurista product-ka caadiga ah** — Wuxuu abuuraa product domain diyaar u ah isticmaalka oo leh dejimo markup macquul ah

#### Qiimeyn Domain oo Dabacsan
* **Saddex hab markup** — Markup boqolkiiba ah, markup go'an, ama qiime go'an TLD kasta
* **Product-yo u gaar ah TLD** — U samee product-yo domain oo kala duwan kooxaha TLD ee kala duwan (.com/.net, .io, koodhadhka dalalka, iwm.)
* **Qiimeyn hordhac ah** — Bixi qiime sannadka koowaad oo la dhimay si loo soo jiito diiwaangelinno cusub
* **Qiimeyn waqtiga-dhabta ah** — Qiimeynta wholesale ee live ka timaadda bixiyeyaasha iyadoo leh kaydin caqli badan
* **Taageerada coupon** — Waafaqid buuxda oo lala yeesho koodhadhka dhimista Multisite Ultimate

#### Ilaalinta Asturnaanta WHOIS
* **Habaynta product kasta** — U deji asturnaanta WHOIS Disabled, Always Included, ama Customer Choice
* **Macaamilku ha doorto** — Marka loo dejiyo Customer Choice, checkbox checkout ah ayaa u oggolaanaya macaamiisha inay ku daraan ilaalinta asturnaanta
* **Qiimeyn la habayn karo** — U deji qiime sannadkiiba add-on-ka asturnaanta, ama bilaash ku bixi
* **Isdhexgalka diiwaangeliyaha** — Si toos ah ayuu u hawlgeliyaa WhoisGuard (Namecheap) ama asturnaanta WHOIS (OpenSRS) waqtiga diiwaangelinta

#### Khibrad Checkout oo Midaysan
* **Saddex hab domain** — Macaamiishu waxay kala dooranayaan subdomain (bilaash), diiwaangelinta domain cusub, ama mapping domain jira oo ay hore u leeyihiin
* **Raadinta domain waqtiga-dhabta ah** — Hubinta helitaanka oo AJAX ku shaqaysa, iyada oo aan bogga dib loo rarin
* **Talooyin caqli badan** — Talooyin TLD oo beddel ah marka domain-ka la doorbiday la qaatay
* **Qiimeyn hufan** — Muujin cad oo ah qiimaha diiwaangelinta, qiimaha cusboonaysiinta, iyo khidmadda asturnaanta WHOIS ee ikhtiyaariga ah
* **Goobaha xiriirka registrant** — Wuxuu ururiyaa macluumaadka registrant ee loo baahan yahay (magac, cinwaan, telefoon) gudaha checkout
* **Site URL si toos ah loo abuuray** — Cinwaanka site-ka si toos ah ayaa looga soo saaraa domain-ka la doortay

#### Nidaamka Cusboonaysiinta Tooska ah
* **Cusboonaysiinno ku xiran membership** — Domain-yadu si toos ah ayay u cusboonaysmaan inta membership-ka macmiilku firfircoon yahay
* **Jadwalayn caqli badan** — Shaqooyinka cusboonaysiinta waxaa lagu daraa queue iyadoo lagu salaynayo taariikhaha dhicitaanka domain-ka
* **Logic dib-u-dayid** — Dib-u-dayid caqli badan oo loogu talagalay isku-dayada cusboonaysiinta ee fashilma
* **Ogeysiisyada email** — Digniino la habayn karo oo loogu talagalay cusboonaysiinno soo socda, cusboonaysiinno guulaystay, fashilaado, iyo dhicitaanno

#### Maamulka DNS ee Macmiilka
* **DNS is-adeeg ah** — Macaamiishu waxay ku dari karaan, wax ka beddeli karaan, oo tirtiri karaan DNS records (A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, TXT) iyagoo ka samaynaya Dashboard-kooda
* **Xaaladda domain** — Xaaladda diiwaangelinta waqtiga-dhabta ah, taariikhaha dhicitaanka, iyo macluumaadka nameserver
* **Mapping domain oo toos ah** — Domain-yada la diiwaangeliyey ama jira si toos ah ayaa loogu map-gareeyaa site-ka macmiilka

#### Qalabka Admin
* **Diiwaangelin domain oo gacanta ah** — U diiwaangeli domain-yo macaamiisha adigoo ka samaynaya admin panel
* **Kormeerka domain** — Hal bog ka eeg dhammaan domain-yada macaamiisha, xaaladahooda, iyo taariikhaha dhicitaanka
* **Logging u gaar ah bixiyaha** — Kanaallo log oo kala gaar ah diiwaangeliye kasta (tusaale, domain-seller-namecheap.log)
* **Faahfaahinta diiwaangelinta** — Ka eeg macluumaadka registrant iyo xogta diiwaangelinta bogga tafatirka domain-ka core

### Bixiyeyaasha La Taageero

* **OpenSRS** — Isdhexgal buuxa oo leh diiwaangelin, cusboonaysiin, maamulka DNS, iyo asturnaanta WHOIS
* **Namecheap** — Isdhexgal buuxa oo leh diiwaangelin, cusboonaysiin, hubinta helitaanka batch, iyo WhoisGuard bilaash ah
* **HostAfrica** — Diiwaangelin, cusboonaysiin, wareejinno, maamulka DNS, soo-qaadashada koodhka EPP, registrar lock, iyo ilaalinta ID
* **Openprovider** — Diiwaangelin, cusboonaysiin, wareejinno, maamulka DNS, soo-qaadashada koodhka EPP, registrar lock, asturnaanta WHOIS, iyo isku-dubaridka TLD ee reseller
* **Hostinger** — Diiwaangelin, domain lookup, cusboonaysiinta nameserver, registrar lock, iyo asturnaanta WHOIS; cusboonaysiinnada waxaa maamula auto-renew ee Hostinger
* **GoDaddy** — Diiwaangelin iyo cusboonaysiin
* **ResellerClub** — Diiwaangelin, cusboonaysiin, iyo maamulka DNS
* **NameSilo** — Diiwaangelin iyo cusboonaysiin
* **Enom** — Diiwaangelin, cusboonaysiin, iyo maamulka DNS

### Faahfaahinta Farsamo

* **Wuxuu u baahan yahay:** PHP 7.4+, Multisite Ultimate 2.4.12+
* **Qaab-dhismeed:** Qaabka Integration Registry oo leh interface-ka Domain_Selling_Capability
* **Autoloading:** Jetpack autoloader oo leh PSR-4 class mapping
* **Heerarka:** Heerarka koodh-qorista WordPress, strict typing, prepared statements

## Rakibid

1. Hubi in **Multisite Ultimate v2.4.12+** la rakibay oo si shabakad ah loo hawlgeliyay
2. Ku rar plugin-ka `/wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite-domain-seller/`
3. Si shabakad ah uga hawlgeli plugin-ka **Plugins** gudaha WordPress Network Admin
4. **Setup Wizard** si toos ah ayuu u bilaabmaa marka ugu horreysa ee la hawlgeliyo — raac tallaabooyinka si aad u:
   * U xidhid diiwaangeliyaha domain-kaaga
   * Tijaabiso isku xidhka
   * Soo dhoofiso TLD-yada la heli karo iyo qiimaha jumlada
   * U abuurto product-kaaga domain ee ugu horreeya oo leh markup qiime
5. Ku dar field-ka **Domain Selection** foomkaaga checkout gudaha **Ultimate Multisite > Checkout Forms**

## Su’aalaha Inta Badan La Isweydiiyo

### Noocee Multisite Ultimate ah ayaa loo baahan yahay?

Nooca 2.4.12 ama ka sarreeya. Addon-ku lama jaanqaadi karo WP Ultimo v1.x ama noocyada Ultimate Multisite ee ka hooseeya 2.4.12.

### Bixiyeyaasha domain-kee ayaa la taageeraa?

Sagaal diiwaangeliye ayaa la taageeraa: **OpenSRS**, **Namecheap**, **HostAfrica**, **Openprovider**, **Hostinger**, **GoDaddy**, **ResellerClub**, **NameSilo**, iyo **Enom**. Awooduhu way ku kala duwan yihiin bixiye kasta sababtoo ah API-yada diiwaangeliyeyaashu waxay soo bandhigaan hawlgallo kala duwan; eeg hagaha dejinta si aad u hesho dulmar bixiye-bixiye ah. Qaab-dhismeedka Integration Registry ee addon-ku wuxuu ka dhigayaa mid fudud in lagu daro bixiyeyaal gaar ah — eeg [hagaha horumariyaha](/developer/integration-guide/custom-registrar).

### Ma bixin karaa ilaalinta asturnaanta WHOIS?

Haa. Product kasta oo domain ah wuxuu leeyahay dejin WHOIS Privacy ah oo leh saddex hab: Disabled, Always Included, ama Customer Choice. Marka loo dejiyo Customer Choice, macaamiishu waxay arkaan sanduuq calaamadayn ah inta lagu jiro checkout-ka oo leh qiimaha (ama "Free" ee Namecheap's WhoisGuard). Calanka asturnaanta si toos ah ayaa loogu gudbiyaa diiwaangeliyaha wakhtiga diiwaangelinta.

### Ma dejin karaa qiimeyn kala duwan TLD-yo kala duwan?

Haa. U samee products domain oo kala duwan kooxaha TLD ee kala duwan. Product kasta wuxuu yeelan karaa nooca markup-kiisa (boqolkiiba, markup go’an, ama qiime go’an), TLD-yada la taageero, iyo qiime hordhac ah.

### Ikhtiyaarradee checkout ah ayay macaamiishu arkaan?

Field-ka Domain Selection wuxuu macaamiisha siiyaa saddex tab: **Subdomain** (ku isticmaal subdomain bilaash ah shabakaddaada), **Register New Domain** (raadi oo diiwaangeli domain cusub), iyo **Existing Domain** (khariidadee domain ay hore u leeyihiin). Kaliya hababka aad hawlgeliso ayaa soo muuqan doona.

### Ma taageertaa cusboonaysiin toos ah?

Haa. Domains waxaa loo dejin karaa inay si toos ah isu cusboonaysiiyaan waxayna ku xidhan yihiin xaaladda membership-ka macmiilka. Lacag-bixinnada cusboonaysiinta waxaa lagu farsameeyaa payment gateway-ga jira ee macmiilka.

### Macaamiishu ma maamuli karaan diiwaannadooda DNS?

Haa. Macaamiishu waxay dashboard-kooda ka helaan interface maareyn DNS ah si ay ugu daraan, u tafatiraan, una tirtiraan diiwaannada A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, iyo TXT. Maareynta DNS waxaa laga heli karaa OpenSRS, ResellerClub, Enom, HostAfrica, iyo Openprovider. Hostinger DNS ee domains-ka la martigeliyay waxaa maamula integration-ka domain-mapping ee Hostinger core.

### Ma jiraa setup wizard?

Haa. Setup wizard-ku si toos ah ayuu u shaqeeyaa marka ugu horreysa ee la hawlgeliyo wuxuuna ku hagaa ku xidhidda diiwaangeliye, soo dhoofinta TLD-yada, iyo abuurista product-kaaga domain ee ugu horreeya. Waxaad sidoo kale dib uga ordi kartaa wizard-ka dejimaha addon-ka.
