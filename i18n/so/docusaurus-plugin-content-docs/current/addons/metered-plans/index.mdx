# Qorshayaal la Cabbiray

Ultimate Multisite: plugin-ka Qorshayaal la Cabbiray wuxuu beddelaa sida aad macaamiishaada uga qaaddo lacagta adigoo soo bandhigaya awoodaha biil-bixinta la cabbiro ee dabacsan. Halkii laga isticmaali lahaa xadad adag oo isticmaalayaasha xanniba marka ay dhaafaan, awood u yeelo kharashyada isticmaalka dheeraadka ah oo si otomaatig ah macaamiisha looga qaado isticmaalka dheeraadka ah.

## Astaamaha Muhiimka ah {#key-features}

* **Biil-bixin la Cabbiray**: Lacag ka qaad macaamiisha iyadoo lagu salaynayo isticmaalka dhabta ah halkii laga raaci lahaa heerar go'an
* **Maaraynta Isticmaalka Dheeraadka ah**: Si otomaatig ah lacag uga qaad isticmaalka ka badan xadadka qorshaha
* **Raad-raaca Isticmaalka**: La soco isticmaalka khayraadka macaamiisha waqtiga-dhabta ah
* **Qiimeyn Dabacsan**: U deji sicirro gaar ah khayraad kala duwan (kaydinta, bandwidth, sites, iwm.)
* **Biil-bixinta AI Token**: Raad-raac oo lacag ka qaad token-nada gelinta iyo soo-saarka ee isticmaalka AI ee dhammaan subsite-yada macaamiisha
* **Qaansheegad Otomaatig ah**: Si otomaatig ah u soo saar qaansheegad marka xuduudaha isticmaalka la dhaafo
* **Warbixinnada Isticmaalka**: Falanqayn iyo warbixin faahfaahsan oo isticmaalka ah oo loogu talagalay macaamiisha iyo maamulayaasha

## Ku Habboon {#perfect-for}

* **Bixiyeyaasha Hosting**: Bixi qorshayaal la ballaarin karo oo la kora baahiyaha macaamiisha
* **SaaS Platforms**: Lacag ku qaad iyadoo lagu salaynayo isticmaalka dhabta ah ee khayraadka
* **Shabakadaha Wakaaladaha**: Qaabab qiimeyn dabacsan oo loogu talagalay mashaariicda client-yada
* **Reseller Hosting**: U suurtageli lammaanayaasha inay bixiyaan qiimeyn ku salaysan isticmaalka

## Sida ay u Shaqayso {#how-it-works}

1. Habee khayraadka la cabbirayo (booska disk-ga, bandwidth, tirada sites, iwm.)
2. Deji xadadka qorshaha aasaasiga ah iyo sicirrada isticmaalka dheeraadka ah
3. Nidaamku si otomaatig ah ayuu ula socdaa isticmaalka dhammaan shabakaddaada
4. Marka macaamiishu dhaafaan xadadka, kharashyada isticmaalka dheeraadka ah ayaa la xisaabiyaa
5. Qaansheegadyada si otomaatig ah ayaa loo soo saaraa iyadoo lagu salaynayo wareegga biil-bixinta
6. Macaamiishu waxay waqtiga-dhabta ah ku arki karaan isticmaalkooda iyo kharashyada soo socda

## Biil-bixinta AI Token {#ai-token-billing}

Nooca 1.1.0 wuxuu ku darayaa biil-bixinta AI token shabakadaha bixiya astaamaha AI iyagoo adeegsanaya WordPress AI Client SDK. Qorshayaal la Cabbiray wuxuu dhagaystaa codsiyada jiilka AI ee dhammaaday, wuxuu diiwaangeliyaa tirada token-nada gelinta iyo soo-saarka ee subsite-ka hadda, wuxuuna token-nadaas u beddelaa kharash AI oo biil lagu qaadi karo muddada biil-bixinta hadda ee macmiilka.

Isticmaal biil-bixinta AI token marka aad rabto inaad qalabka AI ku bixiso sites-ka macaamiisha adigoon qaadin kharashyada moodalka ee aan la saadaalin karin. Codsi kasta waxaa loo nisbeeyaa subsite-ka, macmiilka, membership-ka, bixiyaha, iyo moodalka si maamulayaashu u hubin karaan isticmaalka halka macaamiishuna arki karaan sida isticmaalka AI-goodu u saameeyo account-kooda.

### Habee Sicirrada Token-kiiba {#configure-per-token-rates}

Sicirrada AI waxaa loo habeeyaa moodal kasta iyadoo loo kala saarayo sicirrada gelinta iyo soo-saarka. Sicirrada waxaa loo xisaabiyaa 1 milyan token kasta, taas oo la mid ah bogagga qiimeynta caadiga ah ee bixiyeyaasha AI.

1. Maamulka shabakadda, fur **Multisite Ultimate → Settings → Metered Plans**.
2. U oggolow cabbiridda kharashka AI ee product-ka ama membership-ka ay tahay inuu lacag ka qaado isticmaalka AI.
3. Ku dar gelinno sicirka moodalka adigoo adeegsanaya aqoonsiyada moodalka ee uu soo celiyo connector-kaaga, sida `gpt-4o`, `gpt-4o-mini`, ama aqoonsi kale oo moodal OpenAI-la jaanqaadi kara.
4. Geli qiimayaal kala duwan oo **Sicirka Gelinta** iyo **Sicirka Soo-saarka** ah oo ku jira lacagta site-kaaga halkii 1 milyan token.
5. Deji saamiga kharashka AI ee macmiilka ku jira iyo hab-dhaqanka isticmaalka dheeraadka ah ee qorshaha.

Marka aqoonsi moodal oo sax ah aan la habayn, Qorshayaal la Cabbiray wuxuu ku waafajin karaa horgalaha moodalka. Tusaale ahaan, sicir loo habeeyay `claude-sonnet-4` wuxuu ku dabaqi karaa aqoonsiyada moodalka ee waqtiga lagu suntay ee ku bilaabma horgalahaas. Horumariyayaashu sidoo kale waxay hagaajin karaan jadwalka sicirka ugu dambeeya iyagoo adeegsanaya filter-ka [`wu_ai_token_rates`](./hooks/Filters/wu_ai_token_rates).

### AI Connectors la Taageero {#supported-ai-connectors}

Biil-bixinta AI token waxay la shaqaysaa connectors-ka soo saara dhacdooyinka jiilka WordPress AI Client SDK oo ku dara isticmaalka token-ka metadata-da jawaabta. Tan waxaa ka mid ah qoyska Ultimate Multisite AI connector ee halkan lagu diiwaangeliyay:

* [Ultimate AI Connector ee Endpoints La Jaanqaadi Kara](../ultimate-ai-connector-compatible-endpoints/) ee API-yada OpenAI-la jaanqaadi kara, oo ay ku jiraan OpenAI, Mistral, albaabbada Ollama-la jaanqaadi kara, iyo bixiyeyaasha kale ee soo bandhiga metadata isticmaalka token-ka ee la jaanqaadi kara
* [Ultimate AI Connector ee WebLLM](../ultimate-ai-connector-webllm/) ee isticmaalka WebLLM ee browser-native marka codsiyada lagu maro socodka dhacdada AI Client SDK
* AI provider connectors ay isticmaasho [Gratis AI Agent](../gratis-ai-agent/) marka bixiyaha la doortay uu soo sheego tirada token-nada prompt iyo completion iyada oo loo marayo SDK

Connectors gaar ah way ka qayb qaadan karaan iyagoo kicinaya dhacdooyinka caadiga ah ee `wp_ai_client_before_generate_result` iyo `wp_ai_client_after_generate_result` oo wata metadata moodalka, bixiyaha, iyo isticmaalka token-ka. Dejimaha connector-ka waxaa laga dhaqan geliyaa site-ka weyn ilaa subsites si sites-ka macaamiishu u isticmaalaan bixiyeyaasha iyo xadadka shabakaddu oggolaatay.

### Isticmaalka Account-ka Macmiilka {#customer-account-usage}

Macaamiishu waxay isticmaalka AI ku arkaan agagaarka account-kooda oo ay la socdaan khayraadkooda kale ee la cabbiray. Safka kharashka AI wuxuu muujinayaa wadarta muddada biil-bixinta hadda oo laga soo saaray token-nada prompt iyo completion ee la diiwaangeliyay, sicirrada moodalka ee la habeeyay, iyo saamiga kasta oo qorshaha ku jira.

Haddii macmiilku haysto hab lacag-bixin oo faylka ku jira, isticmaalka ka sarreeya saamiga ku jira waxaa loo tixgeliyaa isticmaal dheeraad ah oo biil lagu qaadi karo. Haddii qorshaha loo habeeyay joojin adag oo macmiilkuna aanu haysan hab lacag-bixin oo la heli karo, codsiyada AI waa la xannibi karaa marka saamiga la gaaro waxaana macmiilka lagu wargelinayaa inuu ku daro faahfaahinta lacag-bixinta ka hor inta uusan sii wadin.

## Khayraadka la Taageero {#supported-resources}

* Kaydinta disk-ga
* Bandwidth/Wareejinta xogta
* Tirada sites
* Tirada isticmaalayaasha
* Isticmaalka AI token iyo kharashka AI
* Noocyada khayraadka gaarka ah

## Shuruudaha {#requirements}

* Multisite Ultimate 2.0.0 ama ka sarreeya
* Rakibid WordPress Multisite
* PHP 7.4 ama ka sarreeya

## Rakibidda {#installation}

1. U rar faylasha plugin-ka `/wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite-metered-plans`
2. Ka hawlgeli plugin-ka shaashadda 'Plugins' ee WordPress
3. U gudub Multisite Ultimate → Settings → Metered Plans
4. Habee khayraadkaaga la cabbirayo iyo qiimeynta
5. U oggolow biil-bixinta la cabbiray products-ka ku jira tifaftiraha product-ka

## Su'aalaha Badanaa la Isweydiiyo {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Maxaa dhacaya marka macmiilku dhaafo xadkiisa? {#what-happens-when-a-customer-exceeds-their-limit}

Halkii laga xannibi lahaa gelitaanka, nidaamku wuxuu la socdaa xad-dhaafka wuxuuna ku daraa qaansheegaddooda xigta iyadoo lagu salaynayo qiimayaasha aad dejisay.

## Ma dejin karaa qiimayaal xad-dhaaf oo kala duwan alaabooyin kala duwan? {#can-i-set-different-overage-rates-for-different-products}

Haa, alaab kasta waxay yeelan kartaa khayraad la cabbiro iyo qiimeyn xad-dhaaf oo u gaar ah.

## Sidee macaamiisha loogu wargeliyaa isticmaalka? {#how-are-customers-notified-about-usage}

Macaamiishu waxay ka arki karaan isticmaalka hadda Dashboard-kooda waxayna heli karaan ogeysiisyo ikhtiyaari ah marka ay ku dhow yihiin ama dhaafayaan xuduudaha.

## Sidee loo qiimeeyaa isticmaalka token-ka AI? {#how-is-ai-token-usage-priced}

Isticmaalka token-ka AI waxaa laga qiimeeyaa heerarka gelinta iyo soo-saarka ee model-ka loo habeeyey halkii 1 milyan oo token. Qorshayaasha la cabbiro waxay ku dhuftaan tirada token-ka prompt iyo completion ee codsi kasta heerarkaas, waxay natiijada ku daraan kharashka AI ee muddada biil-bixinta hadda ee macaamilka, waxayna dabaqaan qoondada qorshaha ama xeerarka xad-dhaafka.

## Weli ma isticmaali karaa xuduudo adag? {#can-i-still-use-hard-limits}

Haa, biil-bixinta la cabbiro waa ikhtiyaari alaab kasta. Waxaad isku dari kartaa alaabooyin leh xuduudo adag oo dhaqameed iyo alaabooyin la cabbiro.
