# Multi-Network

Addon-ka Ultimate Multisite: Multinetwork wuxuu ku kordhiyaa madalkaaga Ultimate Multisite shaqaynta awoodda leh ee shabakado badan. Addon-kan wuxuu u oggolaanayaa macaamiishaada inay iibsadaan oo maamulaan shabakado WordPress multisite oo dhan, ma aha oo keliya bogag gaar ah.

## Astaamaha Muhiimka ah

* **Diyaarinta Shabakadda**: Si otomaatig ah ugu samee oo u habeey shabakado WordPress multisite oo dhammaystiran macaamiishaada
* **Qaabab Shabakad**: U oggolow macaamiisha inay daalacdaan, horay u eegaan, oo doortaan qaab shabakad oo hore loo dhisay ka hor isdiiwaangelinta
* **Maamulka Shabakadda**: Awood maamul oo buuxda oo loogu talagalay deegaanada shabakado badan
* **Shabakado Go'doonsan**: Shabakad kasta oo macmiil waxay si madax-bannaan ugu shaqaysaa iyadoo leh jadwalladeeda database iyo habaynteeda
* **SSO Shabakadaha Dhexdooda ah**: Sii macaamiisha marin aan kala go' lahayn oo ay ku galaan shabakad-hoosaadyadooda iyagoo adeegsanaya xaqiijinta magic-link
* **Qaab-dhismeed La Ballaarin Karo**: Si hufan u maaree shabakado badan iyadoo la adeegsanayo qoondayn khayraad oo la hagaajiyay
* **Isdhexgal**: Si aan kala go' lahayn ayuu ula midoobaa nidaamyada subscription iyo billing ee Ultimate Multisite

## Xaaladaha Isticmaalka

* **Martigelin White Label**: Sii macaamiisha shabakado multisite oo leh summaddooda gaarka ah
* **Xalalka Hay'adaha**: U oggolow hay'adaha inay ka maamulaan shabakado badan oo macaamiil hal madal
* **Hirgelinta Enterprise**: Taageer ururro waaweyn oo leh shuruudo shabakado badan oo adag
* **Madalaha Waxbarashada**: U samee shabakado kala duwan dugsiyo ama waaxyo kala duwan

## Shuruudaha

* Ultimate Multisite 2.0.0 ama ka sarreeya
* Rakibid WordPress Multisite
* PHP 7.4 ama ka sarreeya
* MySQL 5.6 ama ka sarreeya

## Rakibid

1. Ku shub faylasha plugin-ka galka `/wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite-multinetwork`
2. Ka hawlgeli plugin-ka shaashadda 'Plugins' ee WordPress
3. Ku habeey dejimaha shabakado badan Ultimate Multisite → Settings

## Qaababka Shabakadda

Nooca 1.3.0 wuxuu ku darayaa socod ogaansho iyo xulasho qaab oo loogu talagalay macaamiisha iibsanaya shabakado cusub. Isticmaal qaababka shabakadda marka aad rabto in shabakad kasta oo macmiil cusub ay ka bilaabato qaab-dhismeed, naqshad, maktabad nuxur, ama dejin u gaar ah qayb ganacsi oo la diyaariyay.

### Horay-u-eegaha qaabka

Horay-u-eegaha qaabka shabakadda wuxuu u oggolaanayaa macaamiisha inay daalacdaan qaababka la heli karo ka hor checkout. Qaab kasta waxaa lagu furi karaa guddi horay-u-eegis toos ah, si macaamiishu u barbar dhigaan qaabeynta iyo nuxurka ka hor inta aysan iibsan shabakad.

Isticmaal horay-u-eegaha si aad uga caawiso macaamiisha:

* Dib u eeg muuqaalka iyo qaab-dhismeedka qaab kasta oo shabakad oo la heli karo
* Isbarbar dhig qaabab badan adigoon ka bixin socodka iibsiga
* Dooro qaabka sida ugu fiican ugu habboon xaaladdooda isticmaalka ka hor isdiiwaangelinta

Ka dhig magacyada iyo sharraxaadaha qaababka kuwo macaamiishu si fudud u fahmi karaan. Horay-u-eeguhu wuxuu ugu faa'iido badan yahay marka macaamiishu fahmi karaan xaaladda isticmaalka ee loogu talagalay, sida shabakadaha hay'adaha, shabakadaha dugsiyada, shabakadaha franchise, ama xirmooyinka martigelinta white-label.

### Doortaha qaabka ee checkout

Doortaha qaabka waxaa lagu dari karaa socodka signup checkout sida field. Marka la habeeyo, macaamiishu waxay doortaan qaabka shabakadda ay rabaan inta lagu jiro checkout, qaabka la doortayna waxaa la adeegsadaa marka shabakaddooda cusub la diyaariyo.

Si aad doortaha ugu isticmaasho socod checkout:

1. Diyaari shabakadaha aad rabto inaad bixiso sidii qaabab.
2. Ka shaqaysii field-ka xulashada qaabka Multinetwork ee foomka signup ama checkout ee khuseeya.
3. Dooro qaababka ay tahay inay diyaar u noqdaan socodkaas checkout.
4. Daabac socodka checkout oo tijaabi signup si aad u xaqiijiso in qaabka la doortay lagu dabaqay shabakadda cusub.

Haddii socod checkout uu bixiyo hal product shabakad oo keliya, doortuhu weli wuxuu faa'iido u yeelan karaa sidii tallaabo hagis onboarding ah. Funnels-ka plan-yo badan, soo bandhig oo keliya qaababka la jaanqaadi kara product-ka ama plan-ka la doortay.

## Magic Link SSO Shabakadaha Dhexdooda ah

Magic link SSO shabakadaha dhexdooda ah wuxuu u oggolaanayaa macaamiisha inay u kala guuraan aagga Account-kooda koowaad iyo shabakad-hoosaadyada iyagoo aan mar kale gacanta ku sign in-gareyn. Marka macmiil raaco link marin shabakadaha dhexdooda ah, Ultimate Multisite wuxuu abuuraa magic link waqti xaddidan leh wuxuuna macmiilka ku xaqiijiyaa shabakadda loo socdo.

Tani waxay wanaajisaa khibradda macaamiisha maamula wax ka badan hal shabakad ama u baahan inay isla markiiba galaan shabakado qaab ah oo cusub loo cloned ka dib iibsiga.

Liiska hubinta admin:

* Xaqiiji in addon-ka Multinetwork uu firfircoon yahay guud ahaan rakibidda WordPress multisite.
* Hubi in macaamiishu haystaan marin shabakad oo sax ah ka hor inta aan loo soo bandhigin links shabakadaha dhexdooda ah.
* Si sax ah u habeey domains-ka shabakadda iyo domains-ka la mapped-gareeyay si magic links ay ugu xalliyaan shabakadda loo socdo ee loogu talagalay.
* Tijaabi dashboard-ka macmiilka iyo badhamada marin ee post-checkout kasta ka dib markaad beddesho dejimaha shabakadda, domain, ama checkout.

Magic links waa in loola dhaqmaa sida links login. Waxaa loogu talagalay marin macmiil oo aan kala go' lahayn mana aha in loo gudbiyo ama dib loogu isticmaalo meel ka baxsan session-ka loogu talagalay.

## Su'aalaha Inta Badan La Isweydiiyo

### Waa maxay shabakado badan?

Shabakado badan waa astaamo WordPress oo kuu oggolaanaya inaad ku socodsiiso shabakado multisite oo badan oo madax-bannaan hal rakibid WordPress.

### Sidee tani uga duwan tahay multisite-ka caadiga ah?

Multisite-ka caadiga ah wuxuu oggolaanayaa bogag badan oo ku jira hal shabakad. Shabakado badan wuxuu oggolaanayaa shabakado badan, mid kasta oo leh bogaggeeda, dhammaantoodna ku jira hal rakibid WordPress.

### Tani ma u baahan tahay habayn server oo gaar ah?

Haa, shabakado badan waxay u baahan tahay dejin iyo habayn server oo gaar ah. Fadlan la tasho dukumeentiga si aad u hesho tilmaamo dejin oo faahfaahsan.
