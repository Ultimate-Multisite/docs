# Dhoofiyaha Goobta

## Nuxurka

Waxaan fahamsanahay baahida loo qabo in la awoodo in goob-hoosaadyada lagu raro gudaha shabakaddaada loona wareejiyo rakibid goob keliya ah, iyo lidkeed, haddii macmiilkaagu go'aansado inuu u guuro website-kiisa. Ku-darka dhoofiyaha iyo soo dejiyaha goobta ayaa hawshan fududeeya. Qalabkani wuxuu dhoofiyaa oo soo dejiyaa goobo isagoo adeegsanaya faylal zip ah, kuwaas oo sidoo kale u shaqayn kara kayd ahaan.

## Astaamaha

* Dhoofin iyo Soo dejin Goobo

* Is-dhexgal sahlan in la isticmaalo

* Abuur kayd goob

## Dukumentiyada
**Sida Goob loo Dhoofiyo loona Soo dejiyo**

## Rakibid

1. Ku rar **ultimate-multisite-site-exporter** galka **/wp-content/plugins/**
2. Ka hawlgeli fidinta adigoo maraya menu-ga **Fidinnada** ee WordPress
3. Raac Wizard-ka tallaabo-tallaabo ah si aad fidinta u dejiso

## Hagaha Isticmaalka

Adigoo adeegsanaya Ultimate Multisite waxaad goob ka dhoofin kartaa shabakaddaada ama goob waxaad ku soo dejin kartaa rakibiddaada multisite ama goob-keliya.

Si aad hawsha u bilowdo, tag boggayaga ku-darrada oo rakib ku-darka Ultimate Multisite: Dhoofiyaha & Soo dejiyaha Goobta.![Bogga ku-darrada](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

![Bogga ku-darrada oo leh ikhtiyaarka rakibidda](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

Tag boggaaga Fidinnada oo guji si aad **Shabakadda uga Hawlgeliso** ku-darka aad hadda rakibtay.<!-- Sawir-qaadis lama heli karo: Bogga Fidinnada WP oo muujinaya badhanka Ka Hawlgelinta Shabakadda ee ku-darka Dhoofiyaha Goobta -->

Ikhtiyaarka **Dhoofin & Soo dejin** waxaa laga heli karaa menu-ga Ultimate Multisite, dhinaca bidix.

## Sida goob loo dhoofiyo

Si aad goob uga dhoofiso shabakaddaada, tag **Dhoofin & Soo dejin** ee menu-ga Ultimate Multisite, kadibna tab-ka **Dhoofinta Goobaha**, guji **Abuur Dhoofin Goob oo cusub**.

<!-- Sawir-qaadis lama heli karo: Tab-ka Dhoofinta Goobaha oo leh badhanka Abuur Dhoofin Goob oo cusub -->

Raadi goobta aad rabto inaad dhoofiso oo dooro walxaha aad rabto inaad ku darto faylka la dhoofiyay. Waxaad ku dari kartaa naqshado, fidinno, iyo faylal la geliyay. Haddii goobtaadu aad u weyn tahay, waxaad dooran kartaa in hawsha dhoofinta lagu socodsiiyo gadaal.<!-- Sawir-qaadis lama heli karo: Ikhtiyaarrada dhoofinta goobta oo leh raadinta goobta, sanduuqyada calaamadinta ku dar naqshado/fidinno/faylal la geliyay -->

Guji badhanka **Dhoofinta Goobta**. Goobtaada la dhoofiyay waxay ka muuqan doontaa liiska **Dhoofinnada Jira**. Guji badhanka **Soo dejiso ZIP** si aad faylka u soo dejiso. ZIP-yada dhoofinta waxaa ku jira `index.php` iskiis u bilaabmaya iyo `readme.txt`, sidaas darteed waxaa lagu rakibi karaa martigeliye cusub adigoon marka hore rakibin fidin soo-dejiye oo gaar ah.<!-- Sawir-qaadis lama heli karo: Liiska Dhoofinnada Jira oo leh badhanka Soo dejiso ZIP -->

## Sida loo dhoofiyo shabakadda oo dhan

Si aad dhammaan goob-hoosaadyada wadajir ugu dhoofiso, tag **Dhoofin & Soo dejin** ee menu-ga Ultimate Multisite oo isticmaal ikhtiyaarka **Dhoofinta Shabakadda** ee shaashadda dhoofinta. Kaydka la abuuro wuxuu ka kooban yahay dhammaan goob-hoosaadyada hal ZIP gudihiis, si loogu isticmaalo guuritaanka martigeliyaha, dib-u-dhisidda staging, ama kaydinta heer-shabakad.

Dhoofinnada shabakadda way weynaan karaan. Socodsii marka taraafikadu yar tahay oo xaqiiji in server-ku haysto kayd meel bannaan oo ku filan kaydka iyo wixii faylal la geliyay, fidinno, iyo naqshado ah ee lagu daray.

## Sida goob loo soo dejiyo

Si aad goob u soo dejiso, tag tab-ka **Soo dejinta Goob** oo rar faylka .zip ee goobta aad rabto inaad soo dejiso. Waxaad URL-ka u beddeli kartaa mid cusub ama URL-kii hore waad haysan kartaa adigoo booska bannaan ka tagaya. Sidoo kale, waxaad calaamadin kartaa sanduuqa si faylka .zip looga tirtiro rakibiddaada kadib soo dejinta. Kadib guji **Bilow Soo dejinta**.

<!-- Sawir-qaadis lama heli karo: Tab-ka Soo dejinta Goob oo leh goobta rarista zip iyo badhanka Bilow Soo dejinta -->

Hawshu waxay qaadan kartaa xoogaa waqti ah. Waxaad liiska ka hooseeya soo-dejiyaha ku arki doontaa soo dejin sugaysa.

<!-- Sawir-qaadis lama heli karo: Soo dejin sugaysa oo ku jirta liiska ka hooseeya soo-dejiyaha -->

Waxaad hawsha joojin kartaa adigoo gujinaya **Jooji Soo dejinta**.

<!-- Sawir-qaadis lama heli karo: Badhanka Jooji Soo dejinta si loo joojiyo hawsha soo dejinta -->

Kadib marka hawshu dhammaato, goobtaadu durba waxay ka heli doontaa shabakaddaada.

## Sida goob loogu soo dejiyo rakibid keliya

Si aad goob ugu soo dejiso rakibiddaada keliya, marka hore rakib oo hawlgeli soo-dejiyaha goobtaada. Waxaad xiriiriyaha lagu soo dejisanayo ka heli kartaa tab-ka dhoofiyaha.

<!-- Sawir-qaadis lama heli karo: Tab-ka dhoofiyaha oo leh xiriiriye lagu soo dejisto soo-dejiyaha goob-keliya -->

Waxaad soo-dejiyaha ka heli kartaa menu-ga **Qalabka**, dhinaca bidix, hoos-qaybta **Dhoofin & Soo dejin**.

<!-- Sawir-qaadis lama heli karo: Menu-ga Qalabka ee goob-keliya oo muujinaya submenu-ga Dhoofin iyo Soo dejin -->

Rar faylka .zip ee goobta aad rabto inaad soo dejiso. Waxaad URL-ka u beddeli kartaa mid cusub ama URL-kii hore waad haysan kartaa adigoo booska bannaan ka tagaya. Sidoo kale, waxaad calaamadin kartaa sanduuqa si faylka .zip looga tirtiro rakibiddaada kadib soo dejinta. Kadib guji **Bilow Soo dejinta**.

<!-- Sawir-qaadis lama heli karo: Foomka soo dejinta goob-keliya oo leh rarista zip iyo badhanka Bilow Soo dejinta -->

Hawshu waxay qaadan kartaa xoogaa waqti ah. Waxaad liiska ka hooseeya soo-dejiyaha ku arki doontaa soo dejin sugaysa.

<!-- Sawir-qaadis lama heli karo: Soo dejin sugaysa ee goob-keliya oo ku jirta liiska -->

Waxaad hawsha joojin kartaa adigoo gujinaya **Jooji Soo dejinta**.

<!-- Sawir-qaadis lama heli karo: Badhanka Jooji Soo dejinta si loo joojiyo hawsha soo dejinta -->

Kadib marka hawshu dhammaato, goobtaada waxaa lagu beddeli doonaa goobta aad hadda soo dejisay.
