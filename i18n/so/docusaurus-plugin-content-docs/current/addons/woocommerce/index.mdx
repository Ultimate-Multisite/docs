# Isdhexgalka WooCommerce

Isdhexgalka WooCommerce wuxuu u shaqeeyaa si aad ugu eg sida uu u shaqeeyo Marinkeena Gacanta. Wuxuu isticmaaleyaashaada siinayaa dabacsanaan badan, isagoo u oggolaanaya inay doortaan mid kasta oo ka mid ah foomamka lacag-bixinta ee laga heli karo rakibaaddaada WooCommerce. Waana fudud yahay: marka muddada biilinta isticmaaluhu dhammaato, rukhsadda isticmaalaha waa la hakiyayaa. Isla waqtigaas, dalab ayaa lagu abuuri doonaa rakibaadda WooCommerce ee goobta ugu weyn ee shabakadda. Kadib isticmaaluhu wuxuu email-kiisa ku helayaa qaansheegad, oo leh xiriiriye uu lacag-bixinta ku sameeyo. Marka lacag-bixinta la helo, rukhsadda waa la cusboonaysiiyaa.

## Astaamaha

- Isdhexgal fudud oo u dhexeeya Ultimate Multisite iyo WooCommerce
- Habab lacag-bixin oo dhowr ah ayaa la heli karaa
- Biilin soo noqnoqda oo otomaatig ah iyadoo la adeegsanayo xudunta rukhsadaha ee ku lifaaqan, oo leh hagaajinno ikhtiyaari ah oo ka yimaada kordhiyaha WooCommerce Subscriptions
- Wadarta checkout-ka waxaa ku jira canshuuraha Ultimate Multisite sida khadad khidmad WooCommerce oo gaar ah, aan canshuur lagu darin, si macaamiishu u eegaan qaddarrada canshuurta ka hor lacag-bixinta

## Dukumentiyo

**Dejinta Isdhexgalka WooCommerce**

**Ku Ballaarinta Ikhtiyaarradaada Lacag-bixinta Ultimate Multisite: Isdhexgalka WooCommerce**

## Rakibid

1. Ku rar 'ultimate-multisite-woocommerce' galka '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Ka hawlgeli kordhiyaha menu-ga 'Plugins' ee WordPress
3. Raac Wizard-ka tallaabo-tallaabo ah si aad kordhiyaha u dejiso

## Hagaha Isticmaalka

_**OGEYSIIS MUHIIM AH: Maqaalkani wuxuu tixraacayaa nooca Ultimate Multisite 2.x.**_

_**FIIRO GAAR AH:** Ultimate Multisite: Isdhexgalka WooCommerce wuxuu u baahan yahay in WooCommerce laga hawlgeliyo ugu yaraan goobtaada ugu weyn._

Waxaan fahamsanahay in _Stripe_ iyo _PayPal_ aan laga heli karin dalalka qaarkood, taas oo xaddidaysa ama caqabad ku noqota isticmaaleyaasha Ultimate Multisite inay si wax-ku-ool ah u isticmaalaan kordhiyeheenna. Sidaas darteed waxaan abuurnay **kordhiye lagu dhexgeliyo _WooCommerce_**.

_WooCommerce_ waa kordhiye eCommerce oo aad caan u ah. Horumariyeyaal adduunka oo dhan ah ayaa sameeyay kordhino lagu dhexgeliyo marinnada lacag-bixinta ee kala duwan. Waxaan ka faa’iidaysannay tan si aan u ballaarinno marinnada lacag-bixinta ee aad ku isticmaali karto nidaamka biilinta Ultimate Multisite.

## Dejintiisa

Marka hore, waxaad u baahan tahay inaad rakibto kordhiyaha Isdhexgalka WooCommerce kaas oo **bilaash u ah isticmaaleyaasha shatiga haysta**. Si aad u rakibto, guji Ultimate Multisite ee Dashboard-ka super admin-kaaga oo dooro **Kordhino**.

![Bogga kordhinnada oo muujinaya Isdhexgalka WooCommerce](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

Daaqad ayaa soo bixi doonta oo leh faahfaahinta kordhiyaha. Kaliya guji **Hadda Rakib** *.*

Waxaad habayn kartaa dejimo aasaasi ah sida magaca bandhigga ee ka muuqan doona front end-ka iyo tilmaamo ku hagaya isticmaaleyaashaada socodkooda checkout-ka. Kadib waxaad aadi kartaa **dejimaha _WooCommerce_ ee goobtaada ugu weyn** si aad u hawlgeliso oo u habayso marinka lacag-bixinta ee aad doorbidayso inaad isticmaasho.

### Khadadka khidmada canshuurta checkout-ka

Marka checkout Ultimate Multisite uu ku jiro canshuuro, isdhexgalku wuxuu qaddar kasta oo canshuur Ultimate Multisite ah ku daraa checkout-ka WooCommerce sida khad khidmad oo gaar ah, aan canshuur lagu darin. Tani waxay wadarta canshuurta ka dhigaysaa mid muuqata ka hor lacag-bixinta iyadoo laga hortagayo in WooCommerce mar kale canshuur ka qaado khidmada canshuurta.

## Sida loo maareeyo lacag-bixinta

Isdhexgalka WooCommerce wuxuu u shaqeeyaa si aad ugu eg sida uu u shaqeeyo **Marinkeena Gacanta**. Khasaaradu waa in lacag-bixin kasta gacanta lagu bixiyo dhammaadka muddada biilinta. Waa kan sida socodku u shaqeeyo:

  * Marka wareegga biilinta isticmaaluhu dhammaado, xubinnimada waa la **hakiyayaa**. Isla waqtigaas, dalab ayaa lagu abuuri doonaa rakibaadda _WooCommerce_ ee goobta ugu weyn ee shabakaddaada.

  * Kadib isticmaaleyaashu waxay email-kooda ku helaan qaansheegad, oo leh xiriiriye lagu sameeyo lacag-bixinta (xiriiriyuhu sidoo kale wuxuu ka muuqdaa taariikhdooda biilinta ee Dashboard-ka goob-hoosaadkooda). Xiriiriyahaas wuxuu isticmaalaha u wareejiyaa _**WooCommerce**_ **checkout** , halkaas oo isticmaaleyaashu awoodi doonaan inay doortaan mid ka mid ah **marinnada _WooCommerce_ ee la heli karo** si ay u farsameeyaan lacag-bixintooda.

  * Marka lacag-bixinta la helo, xubinnimada waa la **cusboonaysiiyaa** , lacag-bixinta waxaa lagu diiwaangeliyaa Ultimate Multisite, dalabkana waxaa loo dejinayaa inuu dhammaystiran yahay.

### Qoraallo Muhiim ah Qaarkood:

  * Shaqeynta caadiga ah ee Ultimate Multisite weli way la shaqeysaa qaabkan isdhexgalka. Tusaale ahaan, **soo saarista lacag-celinno** laga bilaabo shaashadda **Maareynta Rukhsadda** waxay la xiriiri doontaa WooCommerce si lacag-celinta loogu soo saaro iyadoo la adeegsanayo foomka lacag-bixinta ee la doortay.

  * Ultimate Multisite: Isdhexgalka WooCommerce wuxuu ku tiirsan yahay WP-Cron si uu u shaqeeyo. Tani aad bay u shaqeysaa inta badan, laakiin haddii goobtu leedahay booqasho yar markaas cron-ka lama kiciyo si uu u hubiyo dhacdooyinka jadwalaysan, dhacdaduna way seegantaa, taas oo sababi karta dib-u-dhacyo fulin.

## Kordhiyaha WooCommerce Subscriptions

In si gacanta loogu bilaabo lacag-bixin bil kasta kuma habboona isticmaalayaasha kama dambaysta ah, sababtoo ah subscriptions waxaa laga filayaa inay si otomaatig ah ugu soo noqnoqdaan wareeg kasta oo biil-bixin ah. WooCommerce Integration add-on hadda waxay ku dartay subscriptions code-ka asaasiga ah, sidaas darteed alaabaha soo noqnoqda mar walba waxay u isticmaalaan subscriptions biil-bixinta soo noqnoqda.

Uma baahnid **inaad** iibsato ama rakibto [WooCommerce Subscriptions plugin](https://woocommerce.com/products/woocommerce-subscriptions/) gaar ah si aad ugu iibiso alaabo soo noqnoqda adigoo adeegsanaya is-dhexgalkan. Haddii plugin-kaas la iibsado, la rakibo, oo laga heli karo goobtaada ugu weyn, Ultimate Multisite ayaa isticmaali doona, WooCommerce-na waxay soo bandhigi doontaa astaamo dheeraad ah oo maamulka subscription ah. Marka aan la heli karin, subscriptions core-ka lagu daray weli wuxuu maareeyaa alaabaha soo noqnoqda.

![Dejinnada is-dhexgalka WooCommerce subscription](/img/addons/woocommerce-subscription-integration.png)

Waana intaas. Isticmaalayaashaada kama dambaysta ahi mar dambe uma baahnaan doonaan inay gacanta ku sameeyaan lacag-bixin dhammaadka wareeggooda biil-bixinta ee alaabaha soo noqnoqda.

Marka laga reebo amar laga abuuro goobtaada ugu weyn hoosta _WooCommerce_, sidoo kale waxay u abuuri doontaa subscription akoon kasta.

### Hab-dhaqanka hakinta cusboonaysiinta fashilantay

Sida caadiga ah, memberships way sii firfircoonaanayaan inta WooCommerce Subscriptions ay farsamayneyso jadwalkeeda isku-dayga lacag-bixin fashilantay. Tani waxay ilaalisaa muddada caadiga ah ee saamaxaadda WooCommerce Subscriptions si cusboonaysiin dib loo soo celiyey ay subscription-ka iyo membership-ka dib ugu waafajiso iyada oo aan si degdeg ah loo carqaladayn gelitaanka.

Administrators doonaya xakameyn gelitaan oo adag waxay ka hawlgelin karaan **Si Degdeg ah u Haki Memberships-ka Marka Renewals Fashilmaan** dejinnada WooCommerce Integration. Marka dejintan opt-in la hawlgeliyo, Ultimate Multisite waxay hakisaa membership-ka la xiriira isla marka lacag-bixinta renewal ay fashilanto halkii ay sugi lahayd in maaraynta isku-dayga WooCommerce Subscriptions ay dhammaato.

Developers waxay ku beddeli karaan isla go’aanka filter-ka `wu_woo_suspend_on_payment_failure`. Bogagga tixraaca hook waxaa laga soo saaraa ilaha add-on-ka, sidaas darteed dulmarkan wuxuu kaliya tilmaamayaa magaca filter-ka ee siyaasadaha hakinta gaarka ah.
