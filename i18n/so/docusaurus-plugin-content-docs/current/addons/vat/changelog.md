# Diiwaanka Isbeddellada VAT

Nooca 1.0.7 - La sii daayay 2026-02-03

* Hagaajin: Lambarrada VAT ee Jarmalka (DE) oo ku fashilmayay xaqiijinta sababo la xiriira khaladaadka MS_MAX_CONCURRENT_REQ ee adeegga VIES SOAP. Waxaa laga beddelay SOAP API-gii hore loona wareegay EU VIES REST API-ga rasmiga ah si loo helo xaqiijin lagu kalsoonaan karo.
* Hagaajin: Lambarrada VAT hadda waa la aqbalaa marka adeegga VIES si ku-meelgaar ah uusan u jirin, halkii si khaldan loo diidi lahaa. Gelin log ah ayaa loo abuuraa dib-u-eegis dambe.
* Hagaajin: Goobta VAT/Tax ID hadda waxay u muuqataa dhammaan waddamada, ma aha oo keliya xubnaha EU. Tax ID-yada aan EU ahayn (tusaale lambarrada Swiss CHE) waxaa loo kaydiyaa soo-bandhigidda qaansheegadka iyada oo aan la marin xaqiijinta VIES.
* Hagaajin: Raadinta horgalaha VAT ee Greece (GR), Monaco (MC), iyo Isle of Man (IM) way jabanayd sababo la xiriira hab-raac raadinta array oo khaldan.
* Horumarin: Waxaa la saaray ku-tiirsanaanta horumarinta wp-cli-bundle ee aan loo baahnayn taas oo sababi kartay khaladaad daran marka lala isticmaalo addon-ka Site Exporter.
* Horumarin: Waxaa lagu daray xirmo tijaabooyin unit oo dhammaystiran (53 tijaabo).

Nooca 1.0.6 - La sii daayay 2026-01-25

* Hagaajin: Macaamilada gudaha ee B2B hadda si sax ah ayay u qaadaan VAT. Sida ku cad xeerarka EU VAT, reverse charge waxay khusaysaa oo keliya macaamilada B2B ee xuduudaha ka gudba, ma aha marka waddanka macmiilku la mid yahay waddanka shirkadda.

Nooca 1.0.5 - La sii daayay 2026-01-22

* Hagaajin: Addon-ka oo aan si habboon u soo shubanayn ama u shaqaynayn.
* Beddel: Ilaha xogta heerka VAT waxaa laga beddelay euvatrates.com loona wareejiyay kaydka ibericode/vat-rates si loo helo xog lagu kalsoonaan karo oo si firfircoon loo dayactiro.
* Hagaajin: Waxaa la saxay qalad qoraal ee qiimaha ikhtiyaarka super_reduced_rates.
* Horumarin: Waxaa lagu daray hubinno null ah oo loogu talagalay maaraynta koodhka waddanka si looga hortago khaladaad.
* Beddel: Waxaa la saaray faylalka turjumaadda ee la socda iyadoo lagu beddelay cusboonaysiinno otomaatig ah oo loo maro Traduttore.
