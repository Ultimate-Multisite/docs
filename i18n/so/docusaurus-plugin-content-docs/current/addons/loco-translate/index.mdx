# Loco Translate

Ultimate Multisite: Loco Translate Integration addon wuxuu keenayaa awoodaha turjumaad u gaar ah goob kasta shabakaddaada WordPress multisite. Haddii aad waligaa u baahatay in goobo kala duwan oo ku jira shabakaddaadu ay yeeshaan turjumaado kala duwan oo isla plugin ama theme ah, addon-kan ayaa ah xalka ugu habboon.

**Waxa Addon-kan Qabto**

Addon-kan wuxuu si habsami leh u mideeyaa Ultimate Multisite iyo plugin-ka caanka ah ee Loco Translate, isagoo u suuragelinaya subsite kasta oo ku jira shabakaddaada inuu hayo turjumaadihiisa gaarka ah. Haddii aad maamulayso madal gobollo badan, u adeegayso bulshooyin luqado kala duwan leh, ama si fudud aad ugu baahan tahay dabacsanaan turjumaad oo ka jirta shabakaddaada, addon-kan wuxuu ka dhigayaa mid sahlan.

**Ku Habboon:**

* Ganacsiyo gobollo badan leh oo leh baahiyo kala duwan oo maxalliyeyneed
* Hay'ado waxbarasho oo u adeegaya bulshooyin kala duwan
* Madallo SaaS ah oo bixiya taageero luqado badan
* Hay'ado web ah oo maamula goobo badan oo macaamiil
* Shabakad multisite kasta oo u baahan dabacsanaan turjumaad

## Rakibid {#installation}

1. Hubi in Ultimate Multisite la rakibay oo shabakad ahaan la hawlgeliyey
2. Rakib oo hawlgeli plugin-ka Loco Translate
3. Soo geli addon-kan galkaaga plugins
4. Shabakad ahaan u hawlgeli addon-ka Loco Translate Integration
5. Bilow abuurista turjumaado u gaar ah goob kasta adigoo adeegsanaya Loco Translate subsite kasta

Waa intaas! Isdhexgalka si toos ah ayuu u shaqeeyaa iyada oo aan loo baahnayn habayn dheeraad ah.

## Su'aalaha Inta Badan La Isweydiiyo {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Ma u baahanahay inaan wax habeeyo rakibidda ka dib? {#do-i-need-to-configure-anything-after-installation}

Maya! Isdhexgalku waa toos. Marka addon-ka iyo Loco Translate labaduba firfircoon yihiin, subsites-ku waxay isla markiiba bilaabi karaan abuurista turjumaadahooda gaarka ah.

## Maxaa dhacaya haddii subsite uusan abuurin turjumaado gaar ah? {#what-happens-if-a-subsite-doesnt-create-custom-translations}

Goobtu si toos ah ayay u isticmaali doontaa turjumaadaha shabakadda oo dhan sidii kayd beddel ah. Waxba ma jabayaan, wax walbana si habsami leh ayay u shaqeeyaan.

## Subsites-ku ma geli karaan turjumaadaha shabakadda oo dhan? {#can-subsites-access-network-wide-translations}

Maamulayaasha goobta ee caadiga ah waxay arkaan oo wax ka beddelaan oo keliya turjumaadaha goobtooda. Super admins waxay geli karaan turjumaadaha shabakadda oo dhan iyo kuwa u gaar ah goobta labadaba, iyadoo ku xiran goobta ay daawanayaan.

## Tani ma la shaqaysaa plugins iyo themes labadaba? {#does-this-work-with-both-plugins-and-themes}

Haa! Turjumaadaha u gaar ah goobta waxay u shaqeeyaan WordPress plugins iyo themes labadaba.

## Tani ma gaabin doontaa shabakaddayda multisite? {#will-this-slow-down-my-multisite-network}

Maya. Addon-ku wuxuu isticmaalaa rarista faylalka oo hufan, taas oo kaliya hubisa turjumaadaha u gaar ah goobta marka loo baahdo. Ma jiro saameyn waxqabad oo la dareemi karo.

## Ma koobiyn karaa turjumaadaha shabakadda goob gaar ah si ay u noqdaan meel laga bilaabo? {#can-i-copy-network-translations-to-a-specific-site-as-a-starting-point}

Haa! Marka aad wax ka beddelayso turjumaadaha Loco Translate, si fudud ayaad uga bilaabi kartaa turjumaadaha shabakadda oo aad ugu habayn kartaa goob kasta.

## Tani ma la shaqaysaa WooCommerce iyo plugins kale? {#does-this-work-with-woocommerce-and-other-plugins}

Hubaal! Plugin ama theme kasta oo isticmaala shaqooyinka turjumaadda caadiga ah ee WordPress wuxuu la shaqayn doonaa turjumaadaha u gaar ah goobta.
