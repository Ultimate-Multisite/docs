# Horumarinta qorshe (v2)

_**Fadlan oo muhiim ah: Maqaalkan wuxuu la xiriira Ultimate Multisite version 2.x.**_

Horumarinta ama beddelista qorshaha ama subscription-ka waa wax caadi ah oo macaamiisha ay samayn karaan haddii ay haystaan miisaan kooban ama ay go'aansadeen inay u baahanin ma laha wax badan oo loo isticmaalo in ay ku tiirsadaan subsite-kooda.

## Sida loo horumariyo qorshe (downgrade) {#how-to-downgrade-a-plan}

Macmaaliyadu waxay horumari karaan qorshaha iyag markii ay galayaan dashboard-ka admin-ka subsite-kooda oo ay riixaan **Change** (Beddel) hoos ka dhaca bogga account-ka.

![Bogga account-ka oo leh Kaarka Xubnahaaga iyo badhanka Change](/img/account-page/membership-change-button.png)

Marka la riixo badhanka **Change**, isticmaaluhu/macmaaliyadu wuxuu u soconayaa bogga checkout-ka (bixinta) halkaas oo ay iska dooran karaan qorshaha aad rabto inaad beddesho subscription-kaaga.

![Bogga doorashada qorshaha horumarinta macmiilka](/img/account-page/downgrade-picker.png)

In tusaale ahaan, waxaan horumari doonaa qorshaha oo laga qaadayo **Premium** (Waxqabadka sare leh) ilaa **Free** (Bilaash ah).

Si aad u sii socoto, isticmaaluhu wuxuu kaliya waa inuu riixdo badhanka **Complete Checkout** (Qorista bixinta dhammaystiran). Ka dib, waxa uu ku soo laabaysaa bogga account-ka oo muujinaya farriin ku saabsan isbeddelka la filayo ee xubnaha. Isbeddelladaas waxay si toos ah u shaqayn doonaan **dhismaha bixinta (billing cycle)** cusub ee macmiilka.

![Bogga account-ka oo muujinaya bandarwadda isbeddelka la filayo ee xubnaha](/img/account-page/pending-change.png)

### Maxaa dhaca marka isticmaaluhu horumariyo qorshahiisa? {#what-happens-when-a-user-downgrades-their-plan}

Waa muhiim in la ogaado in horumarinta qorshaha ays wax ka beddelin qaabka hore ee ku jira subsite-kooda isticmaalaha.

Ma beddelin si toos ah template-ka (qaabka bogga) sababtoo ah isbeddelka template-ka wuxuu si buuxda u saarayaa oo dib u hagaaji doonaa subsite-kooda. Tani waa in lagu ka fogaado luminta macluumaadka aan loo baahnayn. Sidaas darteed, meelka disk-ka (disk space), themes-yada (themes), plugins-yada (plugins) iwm. waxay ku sii ahaadaan sida ay u yihiin haddii la isbeddelin posts-ka (maqaallada).

Waxaan fahannaa in waligood arrimaha ugu muhiimsan ee aad ka walaacaysid ay yihiin xaddidaadda iyo qodobbada (quotas) aad dejisay qorshaha kasta, laakiin waxaan u baahanahay in aan tixgelino dhibaatada uu u geysto subsite-ka isticmaalaha haddii aan fuliyo ama beddelno wax kasta oo ka mid ah dejimaday.

Waxaa jira fursado saddex ah oo aad dooran karto marka maqaalka (posts) uu ka badan yahay xaddidaadda ee qorshaha la dejiyay: **Soo ilaali maqaallada sida ay yihiin** *,* **U wareejinta maqaallada inta lagu jiro weydiinta (trash)** *,* ama **U wareejinta maqaallada inta lagu jiro hal-abuurka (draft)** *.* Waxaad dejin kartaa kanas iyadoo la isticmaalayo dejimaha Ultimate Multisite.

![Network Admin Settings Sites page showing post-limit-exceeded behaviour options](/img/account-page/settings-sites-post-limit.png)

### Maxaa dhaca lacag bixinta (payment) {#what-happens-to-the-payment}

In nooc 2.0, ma u baahanin in la hagaajiyo waxyaabaha lacag bixinta sida qaybinta lacagta (proration).

Tani waxay sabab u ah in nidaamku ka sugnaado aabuurka jira **inuu dhammaystiro wareegga lacagtiisa ka hor inta aan la saixin qorshaha/xubnaha cusub**. Lacag bixinta cusub ee xubnaha cusub ayaa si toos ah loo adeegsadaa oo lagu soo celiyo wareegga lacagta xigta.
