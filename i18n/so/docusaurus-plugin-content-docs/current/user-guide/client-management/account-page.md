# Bogga-ga Macaalka Macaamiishaada (v2) {#your-clients-account-page-v2}

_**Fadlan ogaow: Maqaalkan wuxuu la xiriira Ultimate Multisite nooca 2.x.**_

Marka macaamiishu ay ku biiraan qorshe (plan) oo shirkaddaada ka mid ah, waxay helayaan fursad inay adeegsadaan website-ka iyo dashboard-kiisa oo leh macluumaad muhiim ah oo ku saabsan lacagta bixinta, xubnaha, domain-yada, xaddidda qorshaha, iwm...

In shaqadaas (tutorial) aanu kuu haggo bogga macaamiisha iyo waxaad arki doontaa waxa ay macaamiishu ka arki karaan oo ka samayn karaan gudaha boggagaas.

## Bogga Macaamiisha (The Account Page) {#the-account-page}

Bogga macaamiisha waxaa la heli karaa iyadoo la riixayo **Account** (Macaan) gudaha dashboard-ka macaamiishaada.

![Menu-ga Macaamiisha ee dashboard-ka macaamiisha](/img/account-page/account-menu.png)

Marka ay shaqaynayaan shirkadaha madaxbannaan (sovereign tenant networks), Ultimate Multisite v2.13.0 waxay ilaalinaysaa waayo-aragnimada maamulka macaamiisha ee bogga koowaad. Haddii macaamiishu ka bilaabaan account, checkout, billing, invoice, site-management, template-switching, ama domain-mapping oo ay ka samaynayaan shirkadda madaxbannaan, hawlahaas waxay dib u socdaan dashboard-ka boggaga koowaad si xogta lacagta iyo xubnaha ee shirkaddaada ay sii ahaadaan kuwa awoodda leh.

Marka macaamiishu ka soo galaan shirkad madaxbannaan, dashboard-ka boggaga koowaad wuxuu noqon karaa inuu ku darto xiriir laabta (return link) oo dib u socda goobta shirkadda madaxbannaan. Xiriir laabtaas waxaa lagu muujiyaa haddii Ultimate Multisite uu aqoonsan karo meesha la laabto inay tahay mid ka mid ah website-ka macaamiisha, taasoo ka hor istaagaysa dib u socodka aan loo baahnayn iyadoo la ilaalinayo habka shirkadda madaxbannaan.

![Aragtida bogga macaamiisha](/img/account-page/overview.png)

Marka ay riixaan, waxay arki doonaan aragti guud oo ku saabsan xubnaha (membership), ciwaanka lacagta bixinta (billing address), invoice-yada, domain-yada, xaddidda website-ka, sidoo kale waxay awood u lahayd inay bedelaan **Site Template** (Template-ka Website-ka) (haddii uu shirkaddaada u oggolaado).

Waxay sidoo kale samayn karaan isbeddel xubnaha oo ay ka guurayaan qorshe kale, ama waxay iibsadaan package ama adeeg kale oo aad bixisay. Aan si gaar ah uga fiirino qayb kasta.

### Aragtidaadaaga oo ku saabsan Xubnahaaga: {#your-membership-overview}

Waxaa block-ka ugu horreeya hoos ka dhaca magaca website-ka macaamiishaada waxaa lagu soo bandhigayaa aragti guud oo ku saabsan qorshaha iyo adeegyada/package-yada ay iibsadeen. Block-kaas wuxuu sidoo kale muujinayaa lambarka xubnaha, lacagta hore ee la bixiyay, inta uu qorshaha iyo adeegga/package-ka qiimuhu yahay, iyo inta ay la soo diray (billed) xubnahaas. Waxay sidoo kale arki karaan haddii xubnahaagu yahay **Active** (Socda), **Expired** (Waqti ka gudbay) ama **Canceled** (La joojiyay).

![Membership overview showing plan, amount, and billing details](/img/account-page/membership-card.png)

Hoos ka dambeeya block-kaas, macaamiishaadu waxay arki karaan block-yada **About This Site** (Waa maxay Website-kan?) iyo **Site Limits** (Xaddidda Website-ka). Block-yadan waxay muujinayaan dhammaan xaddidda ay ku jiraan qorshahaaga: meelka diskka, maqaallada (posts), bogagga (pages), booqashooyinka (visits), iwm. Xaddiddaas waxaa lagu hagaajin karaa bogga qorshaha kasta oo ku yaalla **Ultimate Multisite > Products**.

![About This Site and Site Limits blocks showing plan limitations](/img/account-page/site-limits.png)

Dhanka midig ee **Your Membership** (Xubnahaaga), macaamiishaadu waxay ku riix karaan **Change** (Beddel). Tani waxay si cad u muujinaysaa dhammaan qorshaha iyo package-yada/adeegyada la heli karo. Haddii ay dooranayaan qorshe kale, xaddiddaas ayaa ka dhacaysa halkii xaddidda hadda jirta ee xubnahaaga - ma ahayn in la hoos u dejiyo (downgrading) ama la kor u qaado (upgrading).

Hadda, haddii macaamiishaadu dooranayaan inay iibsadaan package-yo ama adeegyo ku saabsan xubnaha hadda jira - sida meel diskka badan ama booqashooyin dheeraad ah - xubnaha hadda jirka ma la beddelin, kaliya package-yada cusub ayaa lagu darayaa.

Fadlan ogaow in codadka kuwan (coupon codes) aan la dari karin bogga beddelista xubnaha. Haddii macaamiishu ay isticmaaleen cod adiga ah marka ay iibsadeen xubnaha hore, koodkaas wuxuu sidoo kale shaqeyn doonaa xubnaha cusub.

### Cusboonaysiinta Cinwaanka Lacagta (Billing Address): {#updating-the-billing-address}

د صفحteenka xogga, macaamihiintu waxay sidoo kale wax ka beddeli karaan qogaaylka bixinta (billing address). Waxay kaliya baahan yihiin inay ku riixaan **Update** oo ku taal _Billing Address._

![Billing Address section with Update button](/img/account-page/billing-address.png)

Fadlan, daaqad cusub ayaa soo muuqan doonta macaamiishaada. Waxa kaliya ay u baahan yihiin inay buuxiyaan qogaaylka cusub oo ay riixaan **Save Changes**.

![Update billing address form](/img/account-page/billing-address-form.png)

### Beddelidda Qoraalka Website-ga (Changing the Site Template): {#changing-the-site-template}

Si aad u oggolaato macaamiishaada inay beddelaan qoraallada website-yada (site templates), waxaad u baahan tahay inaad tagto **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Sites** oo aad furto doorashada **Allow Template Switching**.

Sidoo kale, **Ultimate Multisite > Products** ku socda, dooro qorshayaashaada (plans) oo tag tab-ka **Site Templates**. Hubi in doorashada **Allow Site Templates** ay furan tahay, iyo in **Site Template Selection Mode**-ka, doorashada **Choose Available Site Templates** ay la doortay.

![Product site templates tab with template selection mode](/img/config/product-site-templates.png)

Waxaad arki kartaa dhammaan qoraallada website-yada ee la heli karo oo ku jira website-kaaga. Doorasho kuwa aad rabto inaad u samayso bilaash, iyo kuwa aan macaamiishaada ku siin karin haddii ay ku jiraan qorshahaas. Xusuusnow in doorashadan ay saamaynta leeyihiin foomka lacagta (checkout form), sidaas darteed qoraalka la doorto **Not Available** ma soo muuqdo bogga diiwaangelinta ee qorshahan.

Hadda macaamiishaada waxay ku riix karaan **Change Site Template** gudaha bogga xogta (account page) ee ay ka helayaan.

![Change Site Template button on account page](/img/account-page/change-template-button.png)

Ultimate Multisite 2.10.0 wuxuu soo bandhigayaa dashboard cusub oo beddelidda qoraalka website-ga (template switch panel). Dashboard-ka ayaa bilaabmaya **current-template card** (kaarka qoraalka hadda jira) si macaamiishu ay arkaan qoraalka uu socdo ka hor inta aysii dooran qoraal cusub.

مخطط دائم oo la bilaab ah oo ku jira qoraalka webka ayaa weli muuqda inta ay macaamiishu qiimeynayaan doorbada ay doonayaan. Tani waxay ka caawisaa inay is baranayaan qaababka (templates) loo oggolaado bixinta iyagoo aan lumina waxa hadda la doortay.

![Liiska qaababka website-ka la heli karo ee qorshaha](/img/config/site-templates-list.png)

Marka ay dooranayaan kan ay rabto inay beddelaan, waxaa loo weydiin doonaa in ay xaqiijiyaan isbeddelkaas.

![Dialogga xaqiijinta badhanka qaabka website-ka](/img/account-page/template-switch-confirm.png)

Marka ay dib u socdaan (toggle on) xaqiijinta oo ay gujiyaan **Process Switch**, qaabka website-ka cusub ayaa loo isticmaali doonaa website-ka macaamiishaada.

Macaamiishu sidoo kale waxay isticmaali karaan **Reset current template** laga helo panelkan haddii ay u baahan yihiin inay dib u soo celiyaan qaabka hore ee la siiyay. Sida isbeddelka qaabka website-ka, dib u socodka qaabka wuxuu ka dhigi karaa waxyaabaha ku jira website-ka oo laga saari karaa, sidaas darteed macaamiishu waa inay xaqiijiyaan haddii ay fahmaan habka la dib u soo celinayo.

### Bixinta Domain-yada Gaarka ah (Adding Custom Domains): {#adding-custom-domains}

Macaamiishaadu waxay sidoo kale leeyihiin fursad ay ku dari karaan domain gaar ah qorshahaas bogga xogta (account page). Si macaamiishaadu ay u isticmaali karaan domain-yada gaarka ah, tag **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Domain Mapping**.

Dib u socodka (toggle on) dooro **Enable Domain Mapping**. Tani waxay u oggolaanaysaa macaamiishaada inay isticmaali karaan domain-yada gaarka ah heer koronto (network level).

Ha ilaawin in aad sidoo kale hubiso haddii domain mapping-ka uu la socdo qorshaha bixinta - sababtoo ah waxaad xaddidaa qorshaha si aan macaamiishaadu u isticmaali karaan domain-yada gaarka ah.

Tag **Ultimate Multisite > Products**. Dooro qorshaha aad dooratay oo tag tabka **Custom Domains**. Dib u socodka (toggle on) dooro **Allow Custom Domains**.

![Tabka Custom Domains iyo badhanka Allow Custom Domains](/img/config/product-custom-domains.png)

Tani waxay u oggolaan doonaa dhammaan macaamiisha ku takhasusay qorshaha gaarka ah inay isticmaala domain-yo gaarka ah (custom domains). Hadda, bogga Account-ka, macaamiishaadu waxay ku dari karaan domain gaar ah iyagoo riixaya **Add Domain**.

![Add Domain button on the account page](/img/account-page/add-domain-button.png)

Fadlan, daaqadda ugu horreysa ee soo baxda waxay siin doontaa macaamiishaada farriin tilmaamaya sida ay u cusboonaysiin karaan DNS records-ka si domain gaarka ah (custom domain) uu shaqeeyo shabakadahaaga.

![DNS instructions shown when adding a custom domain](/img/account-page/add-domain-dns.png)

Farriintan waxaa la wax ka beddeli karaa (adiga) **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Domain Mapping > Add New Domain Instructions**.

![Add New Domain Instructions setting in Domain Mapping](/img/config/settings-domain-mapping.png)

Halkan waa aragti dhammaystiran oo ku saabsan bogga domain mapping settings:

![Domain mapping settings full page](/img/config/settings-domain-mapping-full.png)

Kadib marka la riixo **Next Step**, macaamiishaadu waxay dari karaan magaca domain gaarka ah (custom domain name) ee ay doonayaan oo ay dooranayaan haddii domain gaarka ahani uu yahay midka ugu muhiimsan (primary one). Fadlan ogaow in macaamiishaadu ay isticmaali karaan domain-yo gaar ah ka badan hal website-keeda, sidaas darteed waxay dooran karaan kan u noqon doono midka ugu muhiimsan.

![Custom domain name entry with primary domain option](/img/account-page/add-domain-primary.png)

Kadib marka la riixo **Add Domain**, domain-ka waxaa lagu dari doonaa account-ka macaamiishaada. Waxa kaliya ay u baahan yihiin inay beddelaan DNS records-ka domain gaarka ah ee domain registrar-kooda.

### Beddelidda Password-ka: {#changing-password}

Inta badan dashboard-ka account-ka, macaamiishaadu sidoo kale waxay beddeli karaan password-kooda iyagoo riixaya **Change Password**.

![Change Password button on account page](/img/account-page/change-password-button.png)

Tani wuxuu muujin doona daaqad cusub oo macaamiishaadu waxay u baahan yihiin inay buuxiyaaso sirta hore (current password) ka dibna buuxiyaan sirta cusub ee ay rabto inay isticmaalaan.

![Change password form with current and new password fields](/img/account-page/change-password-form.png)

### Meel Khatar: {#danger-zone}

Waxaan leenahay laba dooro oo muujinaya qaybta **Danger Zone** (Meel Khatar): **Delete Site** (Saarsid Website-ka) iyo **Delete Account** (Saarsid Akawntka). Labadaas waxay ku jiraan Meel Khatar sababtoo ah ficilladan ma laha dib u samayn karo. Haddii macaamiishaadu saarsadaan website-kooda ama akawntkooda, ma heli karaan dib u soo celin.

![Danger Zone with Delete Site and Delete Account options](/img/account-page/danger-zone.png)

Haddii macaamiishaadu ka riixaan mid ka mid ah labada dooroasani, waxaa lagu muujin doonaa daaqad ay u baahan yihiin inay dib u shidan (toggle on) doorashada saarsid website-ka ama akawntka, waxayna la ogeyn doonaan in ficilkan aan la dib u samayn karin.

![Delete Site confirmation dialog](/img/account-page/delete-site-confirm.png)

![Delete Account confirmation dialog](/img/account-page/delete-account-confirm.png)

Haddii ay saarsadaan website-kooda, akawntkooda iyo xubnaha (membership) waxay weli ku sii ahaadaan sidaas oo aan la beddelin. Waxay kaliya lumiyaan dhammaan waxyaabaha ku jira website-kooda. Haddii ay saarsadaan akawntkooda, dhammaan website-yada, xubnaha iyo macluumaadka la xiriira akawntkan ayaa looga lumin doonaa.
