# Wax-ka Bixinta Qorshaha (v2)

_**Fadlan oogu xusuusnow: Maqaalkan wuxuu la xiriira Ultimate Multisite nooca 2.x.**_

Macacaaga waxay awood u leeyihiin inay hor mar walba ka badbaadaan qorshahooda. Waxay ama horumar kale qaadan karaan ama ay iibsadaan adeegyo ama kooxro kale oo aad shabakadahaaga ku bixisid.

In casharkaani, waxaan diiradda saari doonaa sida ay u horumarin karaan qorshahaas iyo waxa ka dhasha habka horumarinta.

Si ay u horumaraan qorshahooda, macacaagu waa inuu u furaya dashboard-kiisa oo uu aado bogga **Account**.

![Dashboard-ka subsite-ka macaamiisha oo leh xiriirka Account menu la arki karo](/img/account-page/account-menu.png)

Bogga Account-ka, waxaa lagu muujin doonaa xubsihii hadda jira iyo qorshaha la xiriira. Si loo horumaro qorsho kale, waa inuu riixaa **Change** (Beddel) ee qaybta **Your Membership** (Xubshadaada) oo ku taal qogaa-dhigga sare.

![Bogga Account Your Membership card oo leh badhka Change](/img/account-page/membership-change-button.png)

Waxay dib u la wareegi doonaan foomka lacagta (checkout form) halkaas oo lagu soo bandhigayo dhammaan qorshaha la heli karo.

Waxay sidoo kale awood u leeyihiin inay arkaan **services and packages available for their current plan** (adeegyo iyo kooxro la heli karo qorshaha hadda jira), haddii ay si gaar ah u doonayaan inay iibsadaan adeeg ama kooxro gaar ah (sida booqasho aan la xaddidan ama meel kaydka diskka ee tusaalaha halkan), halkii ay ka horumarin qorshaha.

![Upgrade picker oo muujinaya qorshaha iyo kooxrooyinka la heli karo dhinaca macaamiisha](/img/account-page/upgrade-picker.png)

Marka ay dooranayaan badeecadda ay doonaan inay iibsadaan, waxay arki doonaan inta ay u baahan yihiin inay bixiya hadda - iyadoo aan la isku darayn wax lacagta hore oo la haysan - iyo inta ay la rabo in lagu soo celiyo maalinta lacagta xogta (billing date) xigta.

Inta badan, haddii badeecaddu tahay qorsho kale oo lacagta la bixiyo inta u dhaxaysa kharashka xubshada iyo lacagta la bixinayo, waxay helayaan lacag la isku celin (credit) ee lacagta la bixiyay qorshaha hore.

![Upgrade payment summary showing credit applied and next billing amount](/img/account-page/upgrade-summary.png)

Haddii ay iska doorbixin (plan ama package) u doonaysaan, haddii aan waxba ka beddelin qiimaha hadda socda, waxay arki doonaan farriin sharaxaysa arrintaas.

![Fariinta marka plan la doonayay aysub beddelin ciyaarta](/img/account-page/upgrade-no-change.png)

Marka aad dhammaystirto lacag bixinta (checkout), alaabta cusub ayaa lagu darayaa xogta macaamiishaada, dhammaan xaddidda ama astaamaha alaabta cusub waxay si degdeg ah ugu lahaan doonaan: booqasho, meelka diskiga, maqaallada, iwm...

## Waddooyinka U-beddelidda (Upgrade and Downgrade Paths)

Waxaad ka heli doontaa tabka **Up & Downgrades** oo ku yaalla qalab kasta. Doorashada ugu horreysa ee tabkan waa meel la yiraahdo **Plan Group**.

**Plan groups** waa waxa kuu oggolaanaya inaad Ultimate Multisite u sheegto in qaar ka mid ah plans-ka ay ku tirsan "qoys" isku mid ah, sidaas darteed si loo isticmaalo si loogu dhiso doorashada u-beddelidda (upgrade/downgrade path).

![Tabka wax ka beddelka qalabka Up and Downgrades oo leh meesha Plan Group](/img/config/product-upgrades-plan-group.png)

Tusaale ahaan, aad leedahay **Free plan**, **Basic Plan** iyo **Premium Plan**. Waxaad rabtaa in isticmaalayaasha ku jira **Free Plan** ay kaliya u qaadan karaan **Premium Plan** oo aad uma rabto inay arkaan "Basic Plan" oo ah doorasho u-beddelid. Waxa kaliya ee aad u baahan tahay waa inaad si isku mid ah u magacdo plan group-ka labada (Free iyo Premium plans) sida ay muujinayaan sawirrada hoose.

![Bogga wax ka beddelka Free Plan oo la siiyay High End plan group](/img/config/product-upgrades-free.png)

![Bogga wax ka beddelka Premium Plan oo la siiyay High End plan group](/img/config/product-upgrades-premium.png)

Waxaad u baahan tahay inaad Uloomaha Weyn (Ultimate Multisite) u sheegto in ay jirto "qoys" oo qorsheyaal ah oo ku jira shabakadda la yiraahdo **High End**. Marka aad bixinayso korodhka ama hoos u dhista, kaliya qorshayaasha ka tirsaya qoyskaas ayaa loo soo bandhigi doonaa doorashada isticmaalaha.
