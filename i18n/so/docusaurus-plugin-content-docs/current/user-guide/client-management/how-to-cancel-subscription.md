# Sideeysanayaa (Subscription) sidaadaba?

Haddii aad leedahay Ultimate Multisite license, waxaad dib u bilaabidda (renewal) si aad u rabto inta aad rabto. Si kastaba ha ahaatee, raac tallaabooyinkan:

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Account page email with access link -->

Isticmaal email-ka aad bixisada ku bixisay iyo sirtaada.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Login page with email and password fields -->

Tani waa bogga hore ee Akshigaaga (Account Page):

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Account page initial screen showing subscription details -->

Si aad dib u bilaabidda (cancel) siida, menu-ga ah ee beelaha (left side), tag _Renewing & Billing._

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Renewing & Billing page with cancel option -->

Riix sawirka (arrow)-ga dhanka midig ee bogga si la furay qayb dhinac. Markaas, waa inaad doorataa ikhtiyaarka ah ee dib u bilaabidda (cancel) **auto-renew**.

Nidaamku wuxuu kuu soo bandhigi doonaa fariin xaqiijin ah.

Haddii aad hubiso inaad rabto inaad dib u bilaabiddo, si fudud riix badhanka _**Cancel Renewals**_. Ka dib ficilkan, waxaa lagu weydiin doonaa inaan jawaabto su'aal kooban.

Waxaad dhammaystirtay! Dib u bilaabiddaada (subscription) ma dib u bilaaban doonin si toos ah.

**Waxaad haysataa keyd shaqaynaysa ilaa ay dib u bilaabiddaada (subscription) dhacdo**. Haddii aad rabto inaad dib u bilaabiso, waxaad u baahan doontaa inaad si gacanta ah u samayso.
