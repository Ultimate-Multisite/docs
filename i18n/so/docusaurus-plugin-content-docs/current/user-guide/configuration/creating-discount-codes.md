# Abuuristaan Koodyada Discount (v2)

_**Fadlan oogu ogow: Maqaalkan wuxuu la xiriira Ultimate Multisite nooca 2.x.**_

Ultimate Multisite-kaaga, waxaad samayn kartaa koodyada discount si aad u siiso macaamiisha rabitaanka (subscriptions) kuuma bixiso qiimo yar oo kala duwan. Waxayna fududahay in la sameeyo!

## Abuurista iyo Wax Ka Bedelka Koodyada Discount {#creating-and-editing-discount-codes}

Si aad samayso ama aad wax ka beddesho kood discount, u tag **Ultimate Multisite > Discount Codes**.

![Liiska koodyada discount — xaaladda bannaan intaadan jirin koodbo](/img/config/discount-codes-empty.png)

Waxaad heli doontaa liis koodyada discount-ka aad horey u samaysay.

Waxaad ku riix kartaa **Add Discount Code** si aad u samayso coupon cusub ama waxaad dib u beddeli kartaa kuwa jira adigoo ku wareegaya (hover) oo riixaya **Edit**.

![Liiska koodyada discount oo leh hawlaha wareegga muujinaya Edit iyo Delete links](/img/config/discount-codes-list-hover.png)

![Badhanka Add Discount Code ee madaxa bogga](/img/config/discount-codes-add-button.png)

Waxaad la wareegi doontaa bogga halkaas oo aad samaynayso ama wax ka beddesho koodka coupon-kaaga. In tusaale ahaan, halkan waxaan samayn doonaa mid cusub.

![Bogga wax ka beddelka koodka discount oo muujinaya dhammaan qaybaha](/img/config/discount-code-edit.png)

Aan eegno dejinta la heli karo halkan:

**Enter Discount Code (Gal dhig Koodka Discount):** Tani waa magaca koodka discount-kaaga. Ma aha koodka macaamiisha u baahan inay isticmaalaan foomka checkout-ka.

**Description (Sharaxaad):** Halkan, waxaad si kooban u sharxi kartaa wuxuu yahay coupon-kan.

![Magaca iyo qaybta Sharaxaadda koodka discount ee sare ee bogga wax ka beddelka](/img/config/discount-code-description.png)

Waxaad sidoo kale arki kartaa koodka discount-ka oo ah mid firfircoon (active) ama aan firfirayn (inactive):

![Xaaladda firfircoonida koodka discount](/img/config/discount-code-active.png)

**Coupon code (Koodka Coupon-ka):** Halkan ayaad qeexaysaa koodka macaamiishu u baahan inay ku galayaan inta ay ku jiraan foomka checkout-ka.

![Coupon code field where customers enter the code at checkout](/img/config/discount-code-coupon-field.png)

**Discount:** Halkan, waxaad qoraa **boqolkiisa** ama **miqden cayiman** oo lacag ah ee koodka ruxiddaada (discount code).

![Discount amount setting with percentage or fixed-amount dropdown](/img/config/discount-code-amount.png)

**Apply to renewals:** Haddii fursadan la xirmo (toggle) la istaago, koodkan ruxidda ah wuxuu kuugu isticmaali doonaa **bixinta ugu horreysa**. Bixinnada kale ma heli doonaan wax ruxid ah. Haddii fursadan la istaago, koodka ruxidda ah wuxuu shaqeyn doonaa dhammaan bixinnada mustaqbalka ah.

**Discount setup fee:** Haddii fursadan la xirmo la istaago, koodka coupon-ku **ma siinayo wax ruxid ah oo ku saabsan kharashka aasaasiga (setup fee)** ee order-ka. Haddii fursadan la istaago, waxaad qeexi kartaa boqolkiisa ama miqden cayiman ee koodkan coupon-ku siinayo kharashka aasaasiga qorsheeda (plans).

![Apply to renewals and setup fee discount toggle options](/img/config/discount-code-renewals.png)

**Active:** Si gacanta ah u fur ama u xir koodka coupon-kan.

![Active toggle to manually enable or disable the discount code](/img/config/discount-code-active.png)

Dhexda **Advanced Options**, waxaan leenahay qorsoshada soo socota:

![Discount code advanced options](/img/config/discount-code-advanced.png)

**Limit uses:**

  * **Uses (Isticmaalka):** Halkan, waxaad arki kartaa inta jeer koodka ruxidda ah la isticmaalay.

  * **Max uses (Inta ugu badan ee isticmaali karo):** Tani waxay xaddidan doontaa inta jeer ay isticmaalayaal koodka ruxidda ah ku isticmaali karaan. Tusaale ahaan, haddii aad halkan qorto 10, coupon-ku wuxuu isticmaali karaa kaliya 10 jeer. Marka la gaaro xaddidan, koodka ruxidda ah ma isticmaali karo mar kale.

![Limit uses setting with current uses count and max uses field](/img/config/discount-code-limit-uses.png)

**taariikhda iyo taariikhda dhammaadka:** Halkan waxaad heli doontaa doorashada inaad ku dari lahayd taariikh bilaab ah iyo/ama taariikh dhammaadka coupon-kaaga.

![Goobaha taariikhda bilaabka iyo dhammaadka si loo qorsheeyo koodka xisaabta khasiyadda](/img/config/discount-code-dates.png)

**Xaddidada alaabta:** Haddii aad fuliso **Select products** (Dooro alaabta) oo aad ka furto, dhammaan alaabtaada ayaa kuu soo bandhigaya. Waxaad heli doontaa doorashada inaad si gacanta u xirto (iyadoo la fulinayo in la furto ama la xiro) nooca alaabta ee qaadi kara koodka coupon-kan. Alaabta laga xiro halkan ma muuqani doono wax isbeddel ah haddii macaamiishaadu isku dayaan inay isticmaalaan koodka coupon-kan.

![Qaybta Xaddidada alaabta iyadoo la isticmaalayo badhaska toggle ee qof kasta oo alaab ah](/img/config/discount-code-limit-products.png)

Marka aad dejisay dhammaan doorashooyinkan, riix **Save Discount Code** (Kaydi Koodka Xisaabta) si aad u kaydiso coupon-kaaga, waad dhammaystiraysaa!

![Badhanka Kaydinta Koodka Xisaabta oo ku yaalla hoos-gaalka bogga wax ka beddelka](/img/config/discount-code-save.png)

Coupon-ku hadda wuxuu ku jiraa liiskaaga, laga bilaabo xilligaas ayaad riixin kartaa inaad **edit** (wax ka beddelo) ama aad **delete** (saxiiso) koodka.

![Soo-bandhigga sariirta koodka xisaabta ee liiska iyadoo la isticmaalayo hawlaha hover ee Edit iyo Delete](/img/config/discount-codes-list-hover.png)

### Isticmaalka URL Parameters: {#using-url-parameters}

Haddii aad rabto inaad u qaabaysato miisaska qiimahaaga ama aad dhisto bogka koodka coupon-kaaga qurux badan ee website-kaaga oo aad rabto inaad si toos ah ugu adeegsato koodka xisaabtaada foomka checkout-kaaga, waxaad sidaas samayn kartaa iyadoo la isticmaalayo URL parameters.

Marka hore, waa inaad heshaa xiriirka la wadaagta (shareable link) ee qorsheyntaada. Si aad u samayso, tag **Ultimate Multisite > Products** (Ultimate Multisite > Alaabta) oo dooro qorshe.

Riix badhanka **Click to Copy Shareable Link** (Riix si aad u nuquliso Xiriirka La Wadaagta). Tani waxay kuu soo bandhigi doontaa xiriirka la wadaagta ee qorshegaas gaarka ah. In kasta oo aan tusaale ahaan, xiriirka la wadaagta la siiyay yahay waa [_**mynetworkdomain.com/register/premium/**_](http://mynetworkdomain.com/register/premium/)_.

![Bogga alaabta oo leh badhanka xiriirka la wadaagta](/img/config/products-list.png)

Si aad u isticmaasho koodka rabitaankaaga (discount code) ee qorshega gaarka ah, kaliya ku dar parameter-ka **?discount_code=XXX** URL-ka. Halkii **XXX** waa koodka rabitaankaaga (coupon code).

In tusaalahenna hadda, waxaan ku isticmaalaynaa koodka rabitaanka **50OFF** ee alaabtan gaarka ah.

URL-ka qorshega gaarka ah iyo marka la isticmaalo koodka rabitaanka 50OFF wuxuu u ekaan doona sida: [_**mynetworkdomain.com/register/premium/**_](http://mynetworkdomain.com/register/premium/) _**?discount_code=50OFF**_.
