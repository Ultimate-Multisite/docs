# Fahamka Dashboard-ga Ultimate Multisite

Ka hor inta aan si fiican u baranno doorooyin qaab-dhismeedka ah iyo sida shaqada Ugu Dambaysa (Ultimate Multisite), hadda aan eegno dashboard-ka.

Sida plugin-yada WordPress ee badan, Ultimate Multisite wuxuu abuuraa menu-ga heerka shirkadda oo la yiraahdo **Ultimate Multisite** oo leh qaar ka mid ah qaybaha sub-menu hoos ku jira.

## Widgets-ka Dashboard-ga Shirkadda (Network Dashboard)

Ultimate Multisite wuxuu ku darsadaa widgets yar oo faa'iido leh dashboard-ka heerka shirkadda. Dashboard-kan waxaa la heli karaa iyadoo la tagayo **My Sites → Network Admin → Dashboard**. Widgets-kaas waxaa loo qaabeeyay si ay fududaan u helaan macluumaadka iyo hawlaha caadiga ah ee maamulaha shirkadda (network administrator).

![Network Dashboard with Ultimate Multisite widgets](/img/admin/network-dashboard-full.png)

### Tallaabooyinka Koowaad

![First Steps widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-first-steps.png)

Widget-kan wuxuu soo muuqdaa marka la cusboonaysiiyo Ultimate Multisite, wuxuuna maamulaha shirkadda (network administrator) u xusuusyaa inuu dhammaystiro hawlo kale sida dejinta hab lacag bixinta iyo abuurista akoon lagu baadhayo.

### Soo koobid (Summary)

![Summary widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-summary.png)

Widget-ka Soo koobid wuxuu soo sheegayaa tirada dadka ku biiray iyo dakhliga ay helaan maalintaas. MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) wuxuu muujinayaa dhasha guud oo ku aaddan macaamiisha leh miisaaniyado wax soo saaraya lacag.

### Qaybta Hawlaha (Activity Stream)

![Activity Stream widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-activity-stream.png)

Widget-kan wuxuu soo sheegayaa dhacdooyinka ka jira Ultimate Multisite. Dhacdooyinka waxaa ka mid ah biirista, dib u laabashada (cancellations), abuurista iyo nisbitaalka (site creations), nisbitaalka la saarista (deletions), iyo hawlo kale.

Fadlan istaag qaybta Events ee dukumentiga si aad faahfaahin dheeraad ah u hesho.

### Hadda

![Right Now widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-right-now.png)

Widgetkanani ah ayaa soo bandhigaya soo koob gaaban oo ku saabsan macaamiisha iyo meelaha (sites) ee shabakadaha. Qorsheyn fudud oo sare lagu heli karo wuxuu kuu oggolaanaya inaad hal mar la isticmaasho inaad samayso meelo cusub ama macaamiil cusub.

### Wararrada iyo Is-diiwaangelinta (News and Discussions)

![Wararrada iyo Is-diiwaangelinta widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-news.png)

Widgetkan wuxuu soo qaataa oo wada soo bandhigaya wararrada ugu dambeeyay ee Ultimate Multisite. Marka aad diiradda saarto widgetkan, waxaad heli doontaa macluumaad ku saabsan cusboonaysiinta, qaladka muhiimka ah (critical bugs), iyo hagaajinta amniga.

## Dashboard-ka Ultimate Multisite

Halkaas oo dashboard-ka heer shabakadda uu soo bandhigayo macluumaad guud oo ku saabsan shabakadda, dashboard-ka Ultimate Multisite (oo ku yaalla heerka sare ee menu-ga Ultimate Multisite) wuxuu soo bandhigayaa macluumaadka adeegga.

![Dashboard-ka Ultimate Multisite](/img/admin/um-dashboard-top.png)

### Korodhka Dakhliga Mar walba La Soo Gelaya (Monthly Recurring Revenue Growth)

Qaabka MRR (Dakhliga Mar walba La Soo Gelaya) wuxuu soo bandhigayaa qayb kooban oo bil go'an ah oo ku saabsan dakhliga muddo 12-bilood ah.

![Qaabka Korodhka MRR](/img/admin/um-dashboard-mrr.png)

Dakhliga cusub waxaa la raacaya mid buluug ah, halka dib u dhicinta (cancellations) lagu muujiyo mid cas ah.

### Taageerada Qaabka Taryahadda (Date-range Support)

Waxaa laga yaabaa in tirada macaamiisha iyo hawlaha ay ka dhashaan inay si badan yihiin oo macluumaadka ay u noqdaan kuwo aad u badan. Taageerada qaabka taariikhda ee ku jirta dashboard-ka waxay u oggolaanaysaa maamulaha shabakadda inuu diiradda saaro kaliya macluumaadka iyo mudada taariikhda loo baahan yahay.

![Doorka Qaabka Taariikhda iyo Tallaabada](/img/admin/um-dashboard-date-range.png)

Qaabka taariikhda wuxuu maamulaa mudada macluumaadka ay widget-yada hoose si toos ah u soo bandhigayaan, oo ay ku jiraan dakhliga, xilliyada cusub ee socda (new memberships), isdiiwaangelinta iyadoo loo marayo wadden, isdiiwaangelinta iyadoo loo marayo foomamka, iyo meelaha ugu badan ee la booqanayo.

![Widget-yada Tallaabada Dashboard-ka](/img/admin/um-dashboard-stats.png)

:::tip
Si aad u shaqeeyso widget-ka **Meelaha Ugu Badan ee La Booqanayo (Most Visited Sites)**, hubi in la fuliyo tirinta booqashada (visit counting) iyadoo la isticmaalayo: **Ultimate Multisite → Settings → Sites → Enable Visits Limitation and Counting**.
:::
