# Aragtida ee Dashboard-ka {#dashboard-overview}

Ultimate Multisite wuxuu leeyahay **Dashboard** gaar ah oo ku jira menu-ga Ultimate Multisite, sidoo kale wuxuu ku darayaa widgets (abuurinro) Network Dashboard-ka. Maqaalkan wuxuu ka hadlayaa waxa aad arki doontid labada meelbaas.

## Network Dashboard-ka {#the-network-dashboard}

Ultimate Multisite wuxuu ku darsadaa widgets muhiim ah ee dashboard-ka heerka shirkadda (network-level) oo laga helayo **My Sites → Network Admin → Dashboard**.

![Network Dashboard with Ultimate Multisite widgets](/img/admin/network-dashboard-full.png)

Waa kan waxa widget kasta uu bixiyo:

**First Steps:** Wuxuu soo muuqdaa marka la cusboonaysiiyo (fresh installation) wuxuuna ku xusuusyaa inaad dhammaystirto hawlaha aasaasiga ah sida habka lacagta lagu qaado (configuring a payment method) iyo abuurista akoon test ah.

![First Steps widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-first-steps.png)

**Summary:** Wuxuu soo sheegayaa tirada codsiyada la helay (signups) iyo dakhliga maalintaas. MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) wuxuu muujinayaa dhasha guud oo ku aaddan macaamiisha leh booska lacagta ka soo saara.

![Summary widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-summary.png)

**Activity Stream:** Wuxuu soo sheegayaa dhacdooyinka ka jira Ultimate Multisite — codsiyada la helay, dibadda la qaaday (cancellations), abuurista goobaha (site creations), caddeynta (deletions), iyo hawlo kale.

![Activity Stream widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-activity-stream.png)

**Right Now:** Wuxuu soo bandhigayaa soo koobid gaaban oo ku saabsan macaamiisha iyo goobaha ee network-ka, wuxuuna bixiyaa fariimo toos ah (shortcuts) oo aad ku samayn karto abuurista goobo cusub ama isticmaalayaal cusub.

![Right Now widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-right-now.png)

**News & Discussions:** Wuxuu soo qaataa waraaqaha ugu dambeeya ee Ultimate Multisite oo ay ku jiraan laabasho (updates), qaladkarka muhiimka ah (critical bugs), iyo hagaajinta amniga (security fixes).

![News widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-news.png)

## Dashboard-ka Ultimate Multisite {#the-ultimate-multisite-dashboard}

In kasta oo dashboard-ka network-ka uu soo bandhigayo macluumaad guud oo ku saabsan network-ka, dashboard-ka Ultimate Multisite (oo ku yaalla heerka ugu sarreeya ee menu-ga Ultimate Multisite) wuxuu soo bandhigayaa macluumaadka ku saabsan adeegga.

![Ultimate Multisite Dashboard](/img/admin/um-dashboard-top.png)

**Kordha Cashka ee Tirada Bilowga ah:** Waxay muujinaysaa qayb kasta oo bilbax ah oo ka mid ah dakhliga muddo 12 bilood ah. Dakhliga cusub waxaa lagu soo bandhigaa miduu yahay buluug, halka dib-u-dhacashada (cancellations) ay muujinayaan midas oo cas yahay.

![MRR Growth chart](/img/admin/um-dashboard-mrr.png)

**Taageerada Xaddidaadda Taariikhda:** Waxaad filter-ka samayn kartaa xogta dashboard-ka iyadoo la isticmaalayo muddo taariikhi ah si aad diiradda u saarto macluumaadka aad u baahan tahay. Muddo taariikhda ayaa hubinaysa waxa lagu soo bandhigo widgets-ka hoose.

![Date range and dashboard stats](/img/admin/um-dashboard-date-range.png)

Widgets-yada dashboard-ku waxaa ka mid ah:

- **Dakhliga (Revenue)** — Dakhliga guud ee mudadaas oo leh MRR iyo qaybta dhasha qof kasta.
- **Xilliyaha Cusub (New Memberships)** — Inta badan oo xilliyo cusub la abuuro muddo la dooratay.
- **Dhashooyinka Ku Qaran Wadamada (Signups by Countries)** — Kala saarista miyiga ee dhashooyinka.
- **Dhashooyinka Ku Qaran Qaababka (Signups by Forms)** — Inta badan oo macaamiisha ku dhashay qaabka checkout-ka kasta marka la barbardhigo kuwa ay maamulayaashu ku daray.
- **Goobaha U Adeegsan Ee Badan (Most Visited Sites)** — Goobaha ugu badan ee aad isticmaasho shirkaddaada (waxa uu u baahan yahay inaad furto 'Ultimate Multisite → Settings → Sites → Enable Visits Limitation and Counting' ka hor).

![Dashboard statistics](/img/admin/um-dashboard-stats.png)
