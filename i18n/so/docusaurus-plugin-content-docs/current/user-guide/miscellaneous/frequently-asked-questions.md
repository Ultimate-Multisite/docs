# Su'aalada Caadiga ah (Frequently Asked Questions)

Halkan waxaa ku jira su'aalo caadi ah oo inta badan soo baxda marka la codsado taageero.

  * **Maxay tahay Ultimate Multisite?**

Ultimate Multisite waa plugin-ka WordPress, kaas oo loogu talagalay install-yada WordPress multisite, kaas oo ka beddelaya install-kaaga WordPress si uu u noqdo shabakad heer sare ah oo bogag ah – sida [WordPress.com](https://WordPress.com) – taasoo u oggolaanaysa macaamiisha inay bixiyaan meel ay ku abuuraan bogag iyadoo la isticmaalayo lacag bil-bil, seksi, ama sanad (waxaad sidoo kale samayn kartaa qorsheada Free).

  * **Ma shaqeyn doonto Ultimate Multisite haddii aanu multisite lahayn?**

Maya. Ultimate Multisite waxay u baahan tahay WordPress multisite, subdirectory, ama subdomain.

  * **Maxay yihiin shuruudaha Ultimate Multisite?**

Ultimate Multisite waxay u baahan tahay:

**Nooca WordPress (WordPress Version)** : v5.3+ (La taloobay: Nooca ugu dambeeya ee lahayd)
**PHP Version** : 7.4.x (Taageerada > 8.0 ayaa soo socota)
**MySQL Version** : v5+ (La taloobay: Nooca 5.6, haddii aanad hayn 8.0 ee server-kaaga hosting-ka ah)

_Multisite (subdomain ama subdirectory) sidoo kale waa in la fuliyo._ [Sida loo install gareeyo WordPress Multisite](/user-guide/getting-started/how-to-install-wordpress-multisite).

  * **Ma shaqeyn doonto Ultimate Multisite multisite-ka leh subdirectory?**

Haa. Ultimate Multisite waxay taageertaa labada nooc ee install-yada subdirectory iyo kuwa kale.

  * **Ma shaqeyn doonto Ultimate Multisite multisite-ka leh subdomain?**

Haa. Ultimate Multisite waxay taageertaa labada nooc ee install-yada subdirectory iyo kuwa subdomain.

  * **Ma isticmaali karaa Ultimate Multisite si aad u xaddid meel ay isticmaalayaa Isticmaalayaasha (Users) Plugins iyo Themes?**

Haa. Adiga oo isticmaalaya Ultimate Multisite, waxaad dooran kartaa nooca plugins iyo themes-ka ee la heli karo qorshe kasta oo ku jira shabakaddaada.

  * **Ma isticmaali karaa Ultimate Multisite si aad u xaddid tirada posts-ka (maqallada) Isticmaalayaashu samayn karaan boggiisa?**

х우. With Ultimate Multisite waxaad samayn kartaa post quotas (liita qoraallo) oo aad ku leedahay **tusaale kasta oo nooc ka ah post-ka** ee ku jira shirkaddaada. Waxaad sidoo kale xaddidan kartaa tirada isticmaalayaasha admin-ka u soo martiqa, **miisa barnaamijka (disk space)**, iyo waxyaabo badan oo kale.

  * **Ma taageera Ultimate Multisite Site Templates?**

Haa. With Ultimate Multisite waxaad samayn kartaa inta aad rabto Site Templates. Site Templates ma aha themes; waa goobaha caadiga ah ee ku jira shirkaddaada. Waxaad fulin kartaa plugins, themes, iyo inaad u beddesho (customize) Site Template-ka sida aad rabto. Haddii isticmaaluhu dooro template-kan, website-kiisa wuxuu noqon doonaa nuqul ka mid ah template-kaas, oo dhammaan hagaajintaasi ay ku jiraan.

Waxaad sidoo kale qiyaas (set) template gaar ah oo aad u siiso qorshe (plan). Markaas, isticmaalayaasha kasta oo ku biirta qorshahan wuxuu heli doonaa website-kiisa nuqul ka mid ah Site Template-kaas.

  * **Ma la shaqeeyaa Ultimate Multisite WooCommerce?**

Haa!

Haddii aad rabto isticmaalayaasha shirkaddaada inay helaan WooCommerce, oo u oggolaanid inay diyaariyaan dukumeentiyada (stores), taas waa suurtagal adigoo isticmaalaya Ultimate Multisite. Waxaad xaddidan kartaa tirada alaabaha (products) ee macaamiishaadu ay samayn karaan qorshe kasta oo lahayd.

Waxyaabaha ku saabsan lacag bixinta (subscription payments) ee Ultimate Multisite, waxaad isticmaali kartaa [Ultimate Multisite: WooCommerce Integration Add-on](https://ultimatemultisite.com/addons/wp-ultimo-woocommerce/) si aad u oggolaato macaamiishaada Ultimate Multisite inay lacag bixiyaan iyagoo isticmaalaya gateway-yada WooCommerce ee website-kaaga ugu weyn. Sidaas, waxaad ku lahayd kartaa laabta weyn ee gateways-ka lacag bixinta ee loo sameeyay WooCommerce oo aad u isticmaali karto si aad u billowdo macaamiishaada Ultimate Multisite.

  * **Ma jiraa hab lagu guurayo (migrate) subscription-yada ka imanaya WPMUDEV Pro Sites?**

Haa, Ultimate Multisite hadda waxay hadda kuu soo bandhigaysaa [qalab caawin ah oo kaa caawin doona in aad ka guurto wareejinta](https://ultimatemultisite.com/addons/wp-ultimo-pro-sites-migrator/) shabakadkaaga Pro Sites.

  * **Maxay tahay sababta tab-ka “Allowed Plugins” (Plugins la oggolaanshaha) uu faa'iidada Edit Page-ka miisaanka (Plan Edit Page) u muuqdo boos boşon?**

Ultimate Multisite wuxuu kuu ogolaanayaa inaad oggolaato ama ka hor istaagto isticmaalka plugins-ka aan ku shaqayn shabakaddaada. Marka plugin-ku la shaqeeyo shabkadda, si dabiici ah, wuxuu si kaliya u soo muuqdaa dhammaan meelaha (sites) ee shabakadkaaga.

Haddii aadan arag wax plugins ah oo ku jira tab-ka “Allowed Plugins”, hubi in plugin-ka aad rabto uu yahay mid la shaqeeya shabkadda ama aan.

  * **Ma isticmaali karaa qaabka qiimaha (Pricing Table design) ee gaarka ah?**

Haa. Lammaanka 1.4.0 ka dib, Ultimate Multisite wuxuu taageeraa in la beddelo qaababka template-ka (template overrides).

  * **Ma shaqeeyaa Ultimate Multisite WP Engine-ga?**

Haa. Ultimate Multisite mar walba waxaa taageeray WP Engine, laakiin si loo hubiyo in isbeddellada domain-ka u shaqeeyaan, waxay u baahanayd tallaabooyin dheeri ah oo ay samaynayso admin-ka (in lagu daro domain-ka dashboard-ka WP Engine). **Tani ma aha arrin hadda jirta markii la gaaro version 1.6.0.** Ultimate Multisite si toos ah ayaa ula shaqeeya API-ga WP Engine, waxaana domain-yada si toos ah ayaa loo daraya dashboard-ka ka dib marka la qabto (mapping).

  * **Maxay tahay bixiyaha hosting-ka aad talinayso?**

Ultimate Multisite wuxuu shaqeyn karaa dhammaan bixiyaha hosting-ka. Waxa kaliya ee kala duwanaanshaha waa in qaar ka mid ah shirkadaha hosting-ka sida Closte, Serverpilot, WPMUDEV, WP Engine, Cloudways, Gridpane iyo [Runcloud.io](http://Runcloud.io) waxay leeyihiin isku xidhidho (integration) dhow oo ku jira awoodda domain mapping-ka si ay si toos ah u shaqeeyaan. Inkasta oo uu yahay bixiyaha kale, waxaa laga yaabaa inaad u baahan tahay inaad si gacanta ah u samayso domain mapping-ka.
