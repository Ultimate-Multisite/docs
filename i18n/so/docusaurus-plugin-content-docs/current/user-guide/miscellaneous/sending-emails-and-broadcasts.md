# Email-yada iyo Broadcasts (v2)

_**Fadlan ogaansho MUHIIM: Maqaalkan wuxuu la xiriira Ultimate Multisite version 2.x.**_

Ultimate Multisite waxaa ku jira hab ay kuu oggolaan doono inaad la sheegso macaamiisha adiga oo u diraya email gaar ah ama koox oo dadka loo dirayo, sidoo kale in aad soo bandhigto warar (notices) dashboard-ka admin-ka ahaa si aad u bixiso warka.

## Ku dar warar admin-ka ah dashboard-ka macaamiishaada iyadoo la isticmaalayo Broadcasts

Adiga oo isticmaalaya habka broadcast-ka ee Ultimate Multisite, waxaad ku dari kartaa **warar admin-ka (admin notices)** dashboard-ka admin-ka subsite-kaaga.

Tani waxay aad u caawisaa haddii aad u baahan tahay inaad bixiso warka sida hagaajinta nidaamka (system maintenance) ama aad soo bandhigto badeecooyin cusub ama adeegyo cusub macaamiishaada jira. Tani waa sida ay warar admin-ka ah ugu muuqda dashboard-ka macmiilkaaga.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Admin notice broadcast shown on a customer's subsite dashboard -->

Si aad u bilaaw warar admin-ka ah, tag dashboard-ka admin-ka shirkaddaada (network admin dashboard) oo hoos ka socda menu-ga **Ultimate Multisite**, waxaad ka heli doontaa doorashada **Broadcasts**.

![Broadcasts list page in Ultimate Multisite admin](/img/admin/broadcasts-list.png)

Waxaad sidoo kale wax ka beddeli kartaa broadcast-ka jira:

![Broadcast edit interface](/img/admin/broadcast-edit.png)

Laga bilaabo boggan, riix badhka **Add Broadcast** ee sare.

Tani waxay soo bandhigi doontaa meel la yiraahdo Add broadcast modal window (fududka ku dhex jira) halkaas oo aad dooran karto nooca broadcast-ka aad rabto inaad dirto.

Tag, dooro **Message** ka dibna riix badhka **Next Step**.

![Add broadcast modal with the Message option selected](/img/admin/broadcast-add-message.png)

Fududka xigta wuxuu ku weydiin doonaa macaamiisha aad u rabo inaad la gaarto, ama **Target customer** (macmiilka la gaar ah) ama **Target product** (badeynta la gaar ah). Xusuusnow in aad dooran karto in ka badan hal isticmaalye (user) ama in ka badan hal badeeco.

Бараатaad raadinayso isticmaalayaal ama alaab, waxaad u baahan tahay inaad bilaawdo inaad qorto ereyga muhiimka ah gudaha meeshaas.

Dhinaca qaybta **Message type** (Nooca Fariinta), waxaad dooran kartaa midabka ogeysiga. Tani waxay xoojinaysaa sida uu muhiim u yahay fariintaada.

Markaas, waxaad ku riixidda kartaa **Next Step** (Tallaabta Xigta).

![Target customers, target product and message type fields for a Message broadcast](/img/admin/broadcast-message-targets.png)

Fariinta xigta waa meesha aad ka bilaabi karto inaad qorto fariintaada iyadoo aad ku dhex geli doonto mawduuca (subject) iyo waxa aad rabto inaad u dirto dadka.

![Broadcast message subject and content editor on the compose step](/img/admin/broadcast-edit.png)

Marka aad samaysato fariintaada, waxaad riixidda kartaa badhanka **Send** (Dir).

Waa kan dhammaadkii. Ogeysiga admin-ka wuu si degdeg ah u soo muuqanayaa dashboard-ka isticmaalahaaga.

## U dir email-yo macaamiishaada

Adigoo adeegsanaya astaanta broadcast-ka Ultimate Multisite, waxaad u dir kartaa email u isticmaalayaashaada. Waxaad leedahay dooro inaad email-ka u dirto isticmaalayaal gaar ah ama aad u taagto koox isticmaalayaal oo gaar ah iyadoo la tixgelinayo alaabta ama qorshaha ay ku jiraan.

Si aad u bilaawdo broadcast-ka email, tag dashboard-ka admin-ka shirkaddaada (network admin dashboard) oo hoos ka dhanka menu-ga Ultimate Multisite, waxaad ka heli doontaa doorashada Broadcast.

![Broadcasts list page used as the starting point for an email broadcast](/img/admin/broadcasts-list.png)

Maga soo bilaab boggan, riix badhanka **Add broadcast** (Ku dar broadcast) ee sare.

Tani waxay soo bandhigi doontaa modal-ka Add broadcast halkaas oo aad dooran karto nooca broadcast-ka aad rabto inaad u dirto. Si diirista ugu horreysa, dooro **Email** ka dibna riix badhanka **Next Step**.

![Add broadcast modal with the Email option selected](/img/admin/broadcast-add-email.png)

Fariinta xigta waxay weydiin doontaa isticmaalayaashaada **Target customer** (Macmaalka la taagay) ama **Target product** (Alaabta la taagay). Xusuusnow in aad dooran karto ka badan hal isticmaalye ama ka badan hal alaab.

باشگاهي ama aad u raadin macmiilka ama alaabta aad rabto inaad raadiso, waa inaad bilaawdaa inaad qorto ereyga (keyword) gudaha meeshaas.

Marka aad doorato dadka aad la hadlayso (target audience), waxaad ku riixaysaa **Next Step** (Tallaabta xigta).

![Dooro dadka la rabo iyo alaabta la rabo si loo dirayo email broadcast](/img/admin/broadcast-email-targets.png)

Fadlan qaybta xigta waa meesha aad ka bilaabi karto samaynta email-kaaga adiga oo qoraya mawduuca (subject) iyo waxa aad rabto inaad u soo dirto dadkaas.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Waxay ku tahay hagaajinta mawduuca iyo qoraalka email-kaada ee qaybta samaynta -->

Kadib markaad samayso fariintaada, waxaad riixaysaa badhanka **Send** (Dir).

Waa sidaas ay fududahay tahay inaad u dirto email dadka aad la hadlayso adiga oo isticmaalaya astaanta broadcast-ka.

## Email-yada nidaamka (System emails)

Email-yada nidaamka ee Ultimate Multisite waa **ooyin macmiil ah** (automatic notifications) ay bixiyo nidaamku ka dib markii la sameeyay waxyaabo gaar ah sida isdiiwaangelinta, lacag bixinta, xiritaanka domain-ka, iwm. Email-yadan waxaa lagu hagaajin karaa ama la beddeli karaa dejimaha Ultimate Multisite. Waxaa sidoo kale jira hab uu kuu oggolaanayo inaad dib u dejisid oo aad ka soo geliso dejimaha hore ee Ultimate Multisite kale.

### Dib u dejinta iyo soo gelitaanka (Resetting & Importing)

Noocyada cusub ee Ultimate Multisite, iyo add-ons-yadaas, waxay mararka qaarkood samayn karaan email-yo cusub oo ay soo diriayaan.

Si loo ka horjoobiyo isku dhacnaanta iyo dhibaatooyinka kale, **aanu ku darin email-template-yada cusub sida Email-yada Nidaamka si toos ah** (automatically) install-kaaga, haddii ay muhiim u yihiin shaqaynta saxda ah oo ah muuqaalka gaarka ah.

Si kastaba ha ahaatee, super admins iyo agents-ku waxay soo gelin karaan email-yada cusub ee la samaynayay iyagoo isticmaalaya tool-ka importer-ka (importer tool). Habkani wuxuu abuuri doonaa email nidaam ah oo cusub oo leh waxyaabaha iyo dejinta template-ka email-ka cusub, taasoo u oggolaanaysa super admin-ka inuu sameeyo wax kasta oo uu rabto ama uu ku ilaaliyo sida ay yihiin.

#### Sida loo soo geliyo email-yada nidaamka (How to import system emails)

Intaada, tag ciyaarta aad ka qaadi doontaa **Emails** tab-ka ee Ultimate Multisite Settings-ka.

![Emails tab in Ultimate Multisite settings showing the System Emails section](/img/config/settings-emails-tab.png)

Markaas, dhinaca hore (sidebar), riix badhanka **Customize System Emails**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Customize System Emails button on the System Emails sidebar panel -->

Adiga oo ku jirta bogga System Emails, waxaad arki doontaa badhanka **Reset & Import** ee sare. Marka aad riixdo badhankan, wuxuu furayaa dalka import iyo reset modal window-ka.

![Reset or Import action button on the System Emails admin page](/img/admin/system-emails-reset-import.png)

Markaas, waxaad dib u beddeli kartaa (toggle) doorarka Import Emails si aad aragto noocyada emails ee laga soo diri karo.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Reset and Import modal with the Import Emails options expanded -->

#### Dib u dejinta System Emails

Mararka kale, waxaad ogaan kartaa in isbeddellada aad samaysay qoraalka email gaar ah aysan kuu shaqaynayn oo aad rabto inaad dib ugu dejiso **halista (default state)**-kiisa.

Xilliyadaas, waxaad leedahay laba dooro: si fudud waxaad ka saari kartaa emailka system-ka oo aad dib u soo darsan kartaa (adiga oo isticmaalaya tilmaamaha korantii), taasoo nadiifin doonta metrikada diyaarinta iyo waxyaabaha kale, taasoo ka dhigaysa habkan ugu yar la talinaya.

Ama waxaad isticmaali kartaa **Reset & Import tool** si aad dib u dejiso qoraalka emailkaas.

Si aad dib u dejisid qoraalka emailka, waxaad raaci kartaa talaabooyinka korantii ilaa aad gaarto Reset & Import tool-ka, ka dibna, dib u beddel **Reset** option-ka oo dooro emails-ka aad rabto inaad dib ugu dejiso macluumaadka halista (default content).
