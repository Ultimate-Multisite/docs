# Dirista Emails iyo Broadcasts (v2)

_**QORAAL MUHIIM AH: Maqaalkani wuxuu khuseeyaa Ultimate Multisite nooca 2.x.**_

Ultimate Multisite wuxuu la yimaadaa sifo kuu oggolaanaysa inaad la xiriirto macaamiishaada adigoo email u diraya isticmaale la beegsaday ama koox isticmaaleyaal ah, sidoo kalena ogeysiisyo ugu diraya admin dashboard-kooda si loogu faafiyo ogeysiisyo

## Ku dar ogeysiisyada admin dashboard-ka macaamiishaada adigoo adeegsanaya Broadcasts {#add-admin-notices-to-your-customers-dashboard-with-broadcasts}

Adigoo adeegsanaya sifooyinka broadcast ee Ultimate Multisite, waxaad ku dari kartaa **ogeysiisyada admin** subsite admin dashboard-ka isticmaalahaaga.

Tani aad bay waxtar u leedahay haddii aad u baahan tahay inaad sameyso ogeysiis sida dayactirka nidaamka ama bixinta alaabooyin ama adeegyo cusub isticmaaleyaashaada hadda jira. Sidan ayuu ogeysiiska admin uga muuqan doonaa dashboard-ka isticmaalahaaga.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Ogeysiis admin broadcast ah oo lagu muujiyey subsite dashboard-ka macmiil -->

Si aad u bilowdo ogeysiis admin, tag network admin dashboard-kaaga, hoosta menu-ga **Ultimate Multisite**, waxaad ka heli doontaa ikhtiyaarka **Broadcasts**.

![Bogga liiska Broadcasts ee admin-ka Ultimate Multisite](/img/admin/broadcasts-list.png)

Waxaad sidoo kale wax ka beddeli kartaa broadcasts jira:

![Is-dhexgalka tafatirka broadcast](/img/admin/broadcast-edit.png)

Boggan, guji badhanka **Add Broadcast** ee kore.

Tani waxay soo bandhigi doontaa daaqadda modal ee Add broadcast halkaas oo aad ka dooran karto nooca broadcast ee aad rabto inaad dirto.

Sii wad oo dooro **Message** kadibna guji badhanka **Next Step**.

![Modal Add broadcast oo ikhtiyaarka Message la doortay](/img/admin/broadcast-add-message.png)

Daaqadda xigta waxay ku weydiin doontaa **Target customer** ama **Target product**. Ogow inaad dooran karto in ka badan hal isticmaale ama in ka badan hal badeeco.

Si aad u raadiso account isticmaale ama badeeco, waxaad u baahan tahay inaad bilowdo qorista erayga muhiimka ah gudaha field-ka.

Hoosta field-ka **Message type**, waxaad dooran kartaa midabka ogeysiiska. Tani waxay xoogga saari doontaa degdegga farriintaada.

Kadib waxaad gujin kartaa **Next Step**.

![Fields-ka target customers, target product iyo message type ee broadcast Message ah](/img/admin/broadcast-message-targets.png)

Daaqadda xigta waa meesha aad ka bilaabi karto diyaarinta farriintaada adigoo gelinaya cinwaanka iyo nuxurka/farriinta aad rabto inaad u broadcast-gareyso isticmaaleyaasha.

![Cinwaanka farriinta broadcast iyo tafatiraha nuxurka ee tallaabada diyaarinta](/img/admin/broadcast-edit.png)

Kadib markaad abuurto farriintaada, waxaad markaas riixi kartaa badhanka **Send**.

Waana intaas. Ogeysiiska admin waa inuu isla markiiba ka muuqdaa dashboard-ka isticmaalahaaga.

## U dir emails macaamiishaada {#send-emails-to-your-customers}

Adigoo adeegsanaya sifooyinka broadcast ee Ultimate Multisite, waxaad email u diri kartaa isticmaaleyaashaada. Waxaad leedahay ikhtiyaar aad email-ka ugu diri karto oo keliya isticmaaleyaal gaar ah ama aad ku beegsato koox isticmaaleyaal gaar ah iyadoo lagu saleynayo badeecada ama qorshaha ay ku subscribed yihiin.

Si aad u bilowdo email broadcast, tag network admin dashboard-kaaga, hoosta menu-ga Ultimate Multisite, waxaad ka heli doontaa ikhtiyaarka Broadcast.

![Bogga liiska Broadcasts oo loo adeegsaday barta bilowga ee email broadcast](/img/admin/broadcasts-list.png)

Boggan, guji badhanka **Add broadcast** ee kore.

Tani waxay soo bandhigi doontaa daaqadda modal ee Add broadcast halkaas oo aad ka dooran karto nooca broadcast ee aad rabto inaad dirto. Sii wad oo dooro **Email** kadibna guji badhanka **Next Step**.

![Modal Add broadcast oo ikhtiyaarka Email la doortay](/img/admin/broadcast-add-email.png)

Daaqadda xigta waxay ku weydiin doontaa **Target customer** ama **Target produc** t. Ogow inaad dooran karto in ka badan hal isticmaale ama in ka badan hal badeeco.

Si aad u raadiso account isticmaale ama badeeco, waxaad u baahan tahay inaad bilowdo qorista erayga muhiimka ah gudaha field-ka.

Marka dhagaystayaashaada la beegsanayo la doorto, waxaad gujin kartaa **Next Step**.

![Xulashada target customers iyo target product ee broadcast Email ah](/img/admin/broadcast-email-targets.png)

Daaqadda xigta waa meesha aad ka bilaabi karto diyaarinta email-kaaga adigoo gelinaya cinwaanka iyo nuxurka/farriinta aad rabto inaad u dirto isticmaaleyaasha.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Tafatiraha cinwaanka iyo nuxurka email broadcast ee tallaabada diyaarinta -->

Kadib markaad abuurto farriintaada, waxaad riixi kartaa badhanka **Send**.

Waana sida ay u fududahay in email loo diro end-users-kaaga adigoo adeegsanaya sifooyinka broadcast.

## Emails-ka nidaamka {#system-emails}

Emails-ka nidaamka ee Ultimate Multisite waa **ogeysiisyada otomaatigga ah** ee uu nidaamku diro kadib ficillo gaar ah sida diiwaangelin, lacag-bixin, domain mapping, iwm. Emails-kan waxaa laga tafatiri karaa ama laga beddeli karaa settings-ka Ultimate Multisite. Waxa kale oo uu la yimaadaa sifo kuu oggolaanaysa inaad reset-gareyso oo aad ka soo dejiso settings jira rakibid kale oo Ultimate Multisite ah.

### Reset-gareynta & Soo dejinta {#resetting--importing}

Noocyada cusub ee Ultimate Multisite, iyo sidoo kale add-ons, waxay diiwaangelin karaan oo diiwaangelin doonaan emails cusub mararka qaarkood.

Si looga hortago iskahorimaadyo iyo arrimo kale, **si otomaatig ah uguma dari doonno templates-ka email-ka cusub sida System Emails rakibiddaada** , haddii aysan muhiim u ahayn shaqeynta saxda ah ee sifo gaar ah.

Si kastaba ha ahaatee, super admins iyo agents waxay soo dejin karaan emails-kan cusub ee la diiwaangeliyey iyagoo adeegsanaya qalabka importer-ka. Habkaas wuxuu abuuri doonaa email nidaam cusub oo leh nuxurka iyo qaabeynta template-ka email-ka cusub, taasoo u oggolaanaysa super admin inuu sameeyo wax ka beddel kasta oo uu rabo ama uu u daayo sida ay yihiin.

#### Sida loo soo dejiyo emails-ka nidaamka {#how-to-import-system-emails}

Tag bogga Settings ee Ultimate Multisite oo u gudub tab-ka **Emails**.

![Tab-ka Emails ee settings-ka Ultimate Multisite oo muujinaya qaybta System Emails](/img/config/settings-emails-tab.png)

Kadib, sidebar-ka, guji badhanka **Customize System Emails**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Badhanka Customize System Emails ee panel-ka sidebar ee System Emails -->

Bogga System Emails, waxaad dusha ka arki doontaa badhanka ficilka **Reset & Import**. Gujinta badhankaas waa inay furtaa daaqadda modal ee soo dejinta iyo reset-ka.

![Badhanka ficilka Reset ama Import ee bogga admin System Emails](/img/admin/system-emails-reset-import.png)

Kadib, waxaad rogi kartaa ikhtiyaarrada Import Emails si aad u aragto emails-ka nidaamka ee diyaar u ah in la soo dejiyo.

<!-- Sawir-qaadis lama heli karo: Modal-ka Reset and Import oo leh xulashooyinka Import Emails oo la ballaariyey -->

#### Dib-u-dejinta Email-lada Nidaamka {#reseting-system-emails}

Mararka qaarkood, waxaad ogaan doontaa in isbeddelladii aad ku samaysay template email gaar ah aysan mar dambe kuu shaqaynayn, oo aad rabto inaad dib ugu dejiso **xaaladdiisa caadiga ah**.

Xaaladahaas, waxaad haysataa laba ikhtiyaar: si fudud ayaad u tirtiri kartaa email-ka nidaamka oo aad dib ugu soo gelin kartaa (adigoo adeegsanaya tilmaamaha kore) - taas oo tirtiri doonta cabbirrada dirista iyo waxyaabo kale, taas oo ka dhigaysa habkan midka ugu yar ee la doorbido.

Ama waxaad isticmaali kartaa **qalabka Reset & Import** si aad dib ugu dejiso template-ka email-kaas.

Si aad dib ugu dejiso template email, waxaad raaci kartaa tallaabooyinka kore ilaa aad ka gaarto qalabka Reset & Import, ka dibna, daar xulashada **Reset** oo dooro email-lada aad rabto inaad dib ugu celiso nuxurkooda caadiga ah.
