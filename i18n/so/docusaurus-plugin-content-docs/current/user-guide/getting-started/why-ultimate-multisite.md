# Maxaybaha Ujeedka Ultimate Multisite

Haddii aad gaartay maqaalkan, waxaad u badan tahay inaad maqashay in plugin-keenna uu yahay xalka ah inuu hawsha ganacsigaaga la qabto. Waxaan rajaynaynaa inaad hesho jawaabaha aad u baahan tahay dhammaadka qoraalka.

## Waa maxay Ultimate Multisite? {#what-is-ultimate-multisite}

Ultimate Multisite waa plugin WordPress Multisite oo kuu oggolaanaya inaad siinaysid macaamiisha WaaS (Website as a Service). Waxaad lahayd plugin-keenna, waxaad leedahay heerarka guud ee subscription ah (subscription tiers) oo aad ka qaadi karto lacag isdiiwaangelinta (recurring fee) macaamiisha si ay isticmaalaan meel loo hosteyay shabakadaha Multisite-kaaga.

Ultimate Multisite wuxuu kuu oggolaanaya inaad hesho lacagtaada iyadoo la adeegsanayo albaabo kala duwan (gateways) sida WooCommerce ama Stripe, aad samayn karto templates oo website ah si ay nololkaagu u fududaato marka macmiil cusub uu ku biiro shabakadahaaga, aad qorsheyn karto domain-yada (map domains), iyo inaad u beddesho foomamka diiwaangelinta (registration forms) si aad u abuurtid waayo-aragnimo gaar ah oo macmiilayaashaada la mid ah.

Waxaan sidoo kale ku sii socon karaa hababka ay leedahay add-ons iyo webhooks.

## Waxyaabo kale {#other-features}

Ultimate Multisite wuxuu si kaleugu bixiyaa:

  * Isdhexgalka Domain Mapping (Domain Mapping integration) lahayd adeegyada hosting-ka WordPress ee la maamula: Ultimate Multisite si toos ah ayaa u dari doona domain-yada la qorsheeyay (mapped domains) dashboard-yada admin-ka WP Engine, Cloudways, cPanel, RunCloud, Closte, iyo kuwa kale marka domain cusub la qorsheeyo, sidaasina uma baahnid inaad si gacanta ah u samayso. Waxaan sidoo kale mararka qaarkood kuu bilownaa soo celinta shahaadada SSL (SSL certificate fetching).

  * Webhooks: Ultimate Multisite wuxuu leeyahay nidaam Webhook oo xooggan oo kuu oggolaanaya inaad isdhegiso shabakadahaaga (network) iyadoo la adeegsanayo adeegyo badan. Waxaan si toos ah ugu bixinaa isdhexgalka Zapier, taasoo u furaysa shabakadahaaga 1000+ app iyo adeeg oo ku jira platform-ka ay leeyihiin.

صفحات ادمن قويات لتساعد عملائك باستخدام مُنشئ الصفحات المفضل لديك: Ultimate Multisite waxaa jira add-on la fudud oo la yiraahdo Admin Pages Creator, kaas oo kuu oggolaanaya inaad ku dari karto Page-y Admin oo si buuxda loo habeeyay dashboard-ka macaamiishaaga adigoo isticmaalaya templates sida Elementor, Beaver Builder, iyo Brizy.

* Labo tema (Theme) oo Admin ah oo kala duwan si aad u habeeyso muuqaalka dashboard-ka macaamiishaada: Check Material WP iyo PRO.

Waxyaabo ka horumaray:

* Taageerada Template Overrides (Badbaadinta qaab-dhismeedka): Iyadoo la isticmaalayo Ultimate Multisite, waxaad beddeli kartaa templates HTML-ka caadiga ah ee habka isdiiwaangelinta (signup flow) kuwaaga cusub adiga oo aan walaacayn in aad wax ka beddesho faylasha core-ka plugin-ka.

* Search and Replace API: Waxaad sidoo kale ku qori kartaa templates-ka website-ka meelaha la isku dayay sida {{site_name}} (placeholder) adigoo isticmaalaya Search and Replace API si aad u buuxiso placeholder-yadaas macluumaadka ay bixiyaan macaamiishaaga isla markaaba ka dib markii ay isdiiwaangashada qabtaan, si toos ah.

## Isticmaalka (Use cases) {#use-cases}

Hadda waa waqtiga inaad aragto qaar ka mid ah isticmaalida suurtagalka ah ee Ultimate Multisite.

### Tallaabada 1: Agent (Agency) {#case-1-an-agency}

Guud ahaan, xirfadaha asaasiga ah ee agent-ka waxay ku jiraan naqshadaynta website-yada iyadoo la raacayo arrimaha sida hosting ama marketing oo loo qor yahay adeegyo dheeri ah.

Waxaa Ultimate Multisite si weyn u soo bandhigaya qiimo badan (value proposition) marka la eego awoodda uu leeyahay inuu host-kaaro oo maamulo website-yada badan hal platform ah. Waxay ka sii muhiim tahay agent-ka kuwaas oo qaab dhismeedkooda si cad u habeeyay themes gaar ah sida GeneratePress, Astra, OceanWP, ama kuwa kale, waxay isticmaali karaan awoodda Ultimate Multisite si ay si toos ah u fuliyaan activation-ka theme-yadaas website-ka cusub.

Xakamaynta adag ee plugins iyo themes waxaa la ilaaliyaa iyadoo la adeegsanayo settings-ka Ultimate Multisite, taasoo u oggolaanaysa in plugins iyo themes la iibiyo ama la qabto xilli la isticmaalayo (instantiated) website cusub.

Agencies-yadu waxay heshiis wanaagsan ka helayaan Ultimate Multisite oo u oggolaanaya inay galaan waxa ay ugu fiican yihiin - naqshadaynta website-yo aan la barbardhigi karin.

### Ardhka 2: Bixiyaha Gaarka Ah (Niche Provider) {#case-2-niche-provider}

Waxaa jira hadal qadiim ah oo leh, "Waxaad samee hal shay oo si fiican u samayso." Kuwa gaarka ah ee aqoonta badan, tani waxay ka dhigan tahay in la abuuro alaab ama adeeg ku salaysan fikrad muhiim ah.

Haddii aad diiradda saarto horumarinta shabakad gaar ah oo websites ah, waxaa laga yaabaa inaad rabto inaad bixiso adeegyo ku salaysan qaab wadajir ah iyo platform. Waa tan marka qoraallada (site templates) ay caawin karaan!

Mid ka mid ah astaamaha cusub ee Ultimate Multisite-ka, website template waa meel ay theme-ku lagu soo saaro oo la hawlgeliyo, plugins-yada loo baahan lagu soo dejiyo oo la hawlgeliyo, iyo qoraallo ama bogag tusaale ah la abuuro. Marka macmiil cusub uu sameeyo website cusub oo ku salaysan template-ka, waxyaabaha iyo settings-ka template-ka waxaa la koobaya (copied) website-ka cusub ee la samaystay.

Haddii aad tahay bixiyaha websites iyo adeegyada gaarka ah, tani waxay si aan la barbardhigi karin u siisaa awood weyn oo ah in la abuuro website degdeg ah oo diyaar u yahay iyadoo lagu darayo plugins iyo naqshado gaar ah. Macmiilku wuxuu kaliya u baahan yahay inuu bixiyo wax yar oo macquul ah si loo dhammaystiro adeeggaas.

### Ardhka 3: Web Hosting-ga WordPress {#case-3-wordpress-web-hosting}

Waxaa jira habab badan oo lagu hosting gareeyo websites-ka WordPress, laakiin si fudud ma aha in la siiyo meel internet (web space) macmiilka oo leh nooc hore loo soo saaray WordPress. Sababta ay sidaas tahay waa in go'aanno iyo tixgelinro badan ay isugu yimaadaan si loo bixiyo adeeg muhiim ah.

Ultimate Multisite waxay kuugu guulaysataa qaybtaas iyadoo bixisa xal dhammaystiran oo la samayn karo (turnkey solution) ee hosting-ga websites-ka WordPress. Xalka waxaa ka mid ah hababka aasaasiga ah ee si loo bixiyo adeegyada subscription-ka, ururinta lacagta, foomamka checkout-ka, voucher-yada ruxa (discount vouchers), iyo waraaqaha macmiilka.

Shaqada muhiimka ah ee loo baahan yahay in si sax ah loogu dhisayo, u hagaajinayo, oo la ilaaliyo WordPress Multisite, Ultimate Multisite wuxuu fududayaa ilaa maamulayaasha shirkadaha (network administrators) ay kaliya ka tixgeliyaan dhinacyada la xiriira adeegga ama booska ay leeyihiin sida heerarka alaabta (product tiers), qiimaha, iyo bixinta adeegyada.

Waxaa jira developer-ka (developers) kuwaas oo raba inay la shaqeeyaan Ultimate Multisite, waxaana xalkaasi wuxuu si dhammaystiran u bixiya RESTful API iyo Webhooks si loo soo dirto ogeysiisyo ku saabsan dhacdooyinka.

Iyada oo aan la isku dayin inta badan plugin-yada iyo lisansiyadaha dibadda ah, Ultimate Multisite wuxuu bixiyaa xalka leh astaamo badan oo la barbardhiga Wix, Squarespace, [WordPress.com](https://WordPress.com), iyo kuwa kale.
