# sida Maxayso Bulshada Ultimate Multisite

Ultimate Multisite Community waa meel aad la kulmi karto isticmaalayaasha kale ee Ultimate Multisite oo aad wadaagto talooyin, waayo-aragnimo iyo waxyaabo kale. Si aad u gaarto Ultimate Multisite Community, tag https://community.ultimatemultisite.com/home.

Marka aad galato bogga soo dhaweynta (welcome page), tag xiriirka "**creating your account here**".

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Community welcome page with creating your account link -->

Waxaa lagaa dirayaa bogga diiwaangelinta (register page) ee aan lahayn. Hadda waxaad u baahan tahay inaad galiso magac isticmaalayaal (username), email-kaaga iyo sirta (password). Samee Captcha ka dibna riix badhanka **Register**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Community register page with username, email, password fields and Captcha -->

Marka aad diiwaangelisay, wuxuu la'aami doonaa inaad samayso profile-kaaga. Boggan waxaad ku soo gali kartaa sawirkaaga, magacaaga oo dhan, qoraal kooban (bio) iyo waxyaabo kale. Sidoo kale, ku dar xiriirrada bulshadaada (social media links) oo dejiso uundinkii (permissions) account-kaaga.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Community profile creation page with photo upload and bio fields -->

Hadda waxaad ku jirtaa channel-ka “Introductions”, halkaas oo aad isku soo bandhigayso naftaada oo aad wax yar ka ogaati karto xubnaha kale. Waxaad samayn kartaa post-kaaga adigoo riixaya badhanka **New Post**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Community Introductions channel with New Post button -->

Channel-yada ku jira qaybta Lounge iyo channel-ka “Dev Logs” waxay u furan yihiin dhammaan dadka. Si kastaba ha ahaatee, waxaad u baahan doontid oggolaansho gaar ah si aad u heli karto channel-yada kale ee bulshada.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Community channels showing Lounge section and Dev Logs channel -->

Oggolaanshahaas waxaa la siin doonaa marka aad noo bixiso furaha isticmaalka (license key) Ultimate Multisite-kaaga oo shaqaynaya. Si aad u samayso, riix xiriirka channel-ka gaarka ah ee aad rabto ka dibna riix badhanka **Unlock**.

<!-- Sawirka ma lahayn: Channel gaar ah oo ay ka mid yihiin bulshada oo leh badhanka Unlock -->

<!-- Sawirka ma lahayn: Bogga furista bulshada oo leh gelitaanka furaha lisen-ka -->

Lisen-kaaga waxaa lagu xaqiijin doonaa waxaadna heli kartaa channel-yada gaarka ah. Habkan wuxuu qaadan karaa ilaa 24 saacadood, sidaas darteed riix xiriirka si aad ugu noqoto bulshada oo aad uga faa'iidaysato meelaha kale inta aanu samaynno codsigaaga.
