# Tuhinga Ultimate Multisite

Nau mai ki ngā tuhinga Ultimate Multisite. Ka huri a Ultimate Multisite i te WordPress Multisite hei tūāpapa Website as a Service (WaaS) katoa.

## Wāhanga Tuhinga

### [Aratohu Kaiwhakamahi](/user-guide/getting-started/ultimate-multisite-101)

Ngā mea katoa e hiahiatia ana e koe hei whakarite, hei whirihora, hei whakahaere i tō tūāpapa WaaS. Ka kapi te tāutanga, ngā kūaha utu, te mahere rohe, te whakahaere kiritaki, me ētahi atu.

### [Tuhinga Kaiwhakawhanake](/developer/hooks)

Tohutoro hangarau mō ngā kaiwhakawhanake e hanga ana i runga i Ultimate Multisite. Kei roto ko te tohutoro hooks, te tuhinga REST API, me ngā aratohu whakawhanake addon.

### [Addons](/addons)

Tuhinga mō ngā plugin addon utu e whakawhānui ana i Ultimate Multisite ki ngā āhuatanga tāpiri pēnei i te whakaurunga WooCommerce, ngā tātaritanga, te hoko rohe, me ētahi atu.
