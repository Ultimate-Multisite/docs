# Filter: wu_\{$this->model\}_meta_pre_save {#filter-wuthis-modelmetapresave}

Ka tātari i te meta raraunga i mua i tōna raupapatanga kia penapenatia ki te pātengi raraunga.

## Ngā tawhā {#parameters}

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $meta | `array` | Te meta raraunga ka penapenatia, kāore i raupapatia kua raupapatia. |
| $data_unserialized | `array` | Ngā raraunga ahanoa ka penapenatia. |
| $this | `\Base_Model` | Te tauira ahanoa. |
