# Tātari: wu_independent_billing_cycle_product_types {#filter-wuindependentbillingcycleproducttypes}

Tātarihia ngā momo hua e whai ana i ngā huringa nama motuhake.

Ko ngā hua whai huringa nama motuhake kāore e herea kia ōrite ki te wā nama o te mahere i roto i te kāta. He whaihua tēnei mō ngā hua pēnei i ngā rēhitatanga rohe e nama ā-tau tonu ana ahakoa he ā-marama, he ā-tau rānei te mahere.

## Ngā tawhā {#parameters}

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $types | `array` | Huānga o ngā momo hua whai huringa nama motuhake. |

- 2.5.0
