# Tātari: wu_minimum_password_length

Tātaria te roa kupuhipa iti rawa.

Ka whakatinanahia anake ina he true te wu_enforce_password_rules.

## Tawhā

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $min_length | `int` | Te roa kupuhipa iti rawa. Taunoa 12 (e ōrite ana ki Defender Pro). |
| $defender_active | `bool` | Mēnā kei te hohe a Defender Pro Strong Password. |
