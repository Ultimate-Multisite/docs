# Tātari: wu_get_site_domain_and_path

Whakaaetia ngā kaiwhakawhanake kia raweke i ngā takirua domain/path.

Ka whaihua tēnei mō ngā āhuatanga maha, pērā i te whakatinana i tētahi momo otinga staging, ngā tūmau rerekē, aha atu.

## Ngā tawhā {#parameters}

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $d | `object` | Te ahanoa o nāianei e mau ana i ngā kī domain me path. |
| $path_or_subdomain | `string` | Te path/subdomain taketake i tukuna ki te taumahi. |

### Mai {#since}

- 2.0.0
### Pūtake {#source}

Kua tautuhia i [`inc/functions/site.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite/blob/main/inc/functions/site.php#L235) i te rārangi 235


## Ngā whakahokinga {#returns}
He ahanoa e mau ana i ngā kī domain me path.
