# Tātari: wu_post_count_statuses

Tukua ngā kaiwhakawhanake plugin kia huri ko tēhea tūnga whakairinga me tatau. Mā te taunoa, ka tatauria ngā whakairinga kua whakaputaina me ngā whakairinga tūmataiti

## Ngā Tawhā {#parameters}

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $post_status | `array` | Te rārangi o ngā tūnga whakairinga |
| $post_type | `string` | Te slug momo whakairinga |

### Mai i {#since}

- 1.9.1
### Pūtake {#source}

Kua tautuhia ki [`inc/limitations/class-limit-post-types.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite/blob/main/inc/limitations/class-limit-post-types.php#L119) i te rārangi 119


## Ngā Whakahokinga {#returns}
He array hou o te tūnga whakairinga
