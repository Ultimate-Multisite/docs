# Tātari: wu_should_create_domain_record_for_site

Ka tātari mēnā me waihanga e Ultimate Multisite tētahi pūkete rohe mō tētahi pae kātahi anō ka waihangatia.

Whakamahia tēnei tātari hei pehi, hei whakaroa rānei i te waihangatanga aunoa o te pūkete rohe mō ngā pae e whakamahi ana i tētahi rohe pūtake puka-checkout tiritahi, i tētahi kaihautū ā-roto, i tētahi rohe rānei ka whakahaerehia motuhaketia e tētahi atu whakaurunga.

## Ngā Tawhā

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $create | `bool` | Mēnā me waihanga te pūkete rohe. |
| $site | `WP_Site` | Te ahanoa pae kātahi anō ka waihangatia. |

### Mai

- 2.13.0

### Pūtake

Kua tautuhia ki `inc/functions/domain.php`.


## Ngā Whakahokinga

Uara Boolean e tohu ana mēnā me waihanga te pūkete rohe.
