# Tātari: wu_enforce_password_rules

Tātari mēnā ka whakakaha i ngā ture kupuhipa tāpiri.

Ina pono, ka whakakaha i te roa mōkito me ngā whakaritenga pūāhua. Ka whakahohe aunoatia mō te tautuhinga "Super Strong", ina hohe rānei te āhuatanga Strong Password a Defender Pro.

## Ngā Tawhā {#parameters}

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $enforce_rules | `bool` | Mēnā ka whakakaha i ngā ture tāpiri. |
| $strength_setting | `string` | Te uara tautuhinga a te kaiwhakahaere. |
| $defender_active | `bool` | Mēnā kei te hohe a Defender Pro Strong Password. |

### Mai i {#since}

- 2.4.0
