# Tātari: wu_country_get_states {#filter-wucountrygetstates}

Whakahoki ana i te rārangi o ngā state mō tēnei whenua.

## Ngā tawhā {#parameters}

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $states | `array` | Rārangi o ngā state i te hōputu XX =&gt; Name. |
| $country_code | `string` | Waehere ISO pū-rua mō te whenua. |
| $current_country | `\WP_Ultimo\Country\Country` | Tauira o te karaehe o nāianei. |

### Mai i {#since}

- 2.0.11
### Pūtake {#source}

Kua tautuhia ki [`inc/country/class-country.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite/blob/main/inc/country/class-country.php#L86) i te rārangi 86


## Ngā whakahokinga {#returns}
Te rārangi kua tātaria o ngā state.
