# Tātari: wu_country_get_cities {#filter-wucountrygetcities}

Ka whakahoki i te rārangi tāone mō tētahi state i tētahi whenua.

## Tawhā {#parameters}

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $cities | `array` | Rārangi o ngā ingoa tāone o te state. Kāore he kī e noho ana. |
| $country_code | `string` | Waehere ISO pū-rua mō te whenua. |
| $state_code | `string` | Waehere ISO pū-rua mō te state. |
| $current_country | `\WP_Ultimo\Country\Country` | Instance o te class o nāianei. |

### Mai i {#since}

- 2.0.11
### Pūtake {#source}

Kua tautuhia ki [`inc/country/class-country.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite/blob/main/inc/country/class-country.php#L146) i te rārangi 146


## Ngā whakahokinga {#returns}
Te rārangi state kua tātarihia.
