# Tātari: wu_magic_link_enforce_ip {#filter-wumagiclinkenforceip}

Tātari mēnā ka whakaūngia te manatoko wāhitau IP.

Tautuhia ki false kia āhei ai ngā tokens te mahi mai i ngā whatunga rerekē. Ka whakaiti tēnei i te haumarutanga, engari ka whakapiki i te whakamahinga (hei tauira, mō ngā kaiwhakamahi pūkoro e whakawhiti ana i ngā whatunga).

## Ngā tawhā {#parameters}

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $enforce | `bool` | Mēnā ka whakaūngia te ōritenga wāhitau IP. |

### Mai i {#since}

- 2.0.0

Kua tautuhia ki [`inc/sso/class-magic-link.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite/blob/main/inc/sso/class-magic-link.php#L422) i te rārangi 422
