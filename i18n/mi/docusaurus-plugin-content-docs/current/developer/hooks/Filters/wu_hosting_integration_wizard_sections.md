# Tātari: wu_hosting_integration_wizard_sections

Ka tātari i ngā wāhanga wizard mō te tatūnga whakaurunga hautū.

Ka āhei ngā addon ki te tāpiri, tango, whakarerekē rānei i ngā wāhanga wizard.

## Ngā Tawhā

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $sections | `array` | Ngā wāhanga wizard. |
| $integration | `\WP_Ultimo\Integrations\Host_Providers\Base_Host_Provider\|\WP_Ultimo\Integrations\Integration` | Te whakaurunga e whirihorahia ana. |
| $page | `\Hosting_Integration_Wizard_Admin_Page` | Te instance whārangi wizard. |
