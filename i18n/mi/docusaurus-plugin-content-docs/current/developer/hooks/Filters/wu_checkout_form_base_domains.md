# Tātari: wu_checkout_form_base_domains {#filter-wucheckoutformbasedomains}

Ka tātari i ngā rohe pūtake puka utu tiritahi kāore e tika kia noho hei pūkete rohe kua maheretia.

Whakamahia tēnei tātari ina whakarato tētahi tuituinga i ētahi atu rohe pūtake mō ngā āpure **URL Pae** o te puka utu. Ko ngā rohe ka whakahokia e tēnei tātari ka whakaarotia hei kaihautū rēhitatanga tiritahi, kaua ko ngā rohe ritenga mō ia pae.

## Ngā tawhā {#parameters}

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $domains | `array` | Ngā rohe pūtake tiritahi kua kohia mai i te whirihoranga puka utu. |

### Mai i {#since}

- 2.13.0

### Pūtake {#source}

Kua tautuhia ki `inc/functions/domain.php`.


## Ngā whakahokinga {#returns}

He huānga o ngā rohe pūtake puka utu kua whakaritea.
