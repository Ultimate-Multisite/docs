# Tātari: wp_ultimo_render_vars {#filter-wpultimorendervars}

Whakaaetia ngā kaiwhakawhanake monomai kia tāpiri taurangi anō ki te horopaki tāhuahua puta noa.

## Ngā Tawhā {#parameters}

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $args | `array` | Huānga kei roto ngā taurangi i tukuna e te karanga tāhuahua. |
| $view | `string` | Ingoa o te tirohanga hei tāhuahua. |
| $default_view | `string` | Ingoa o te fallback_view |

### Mai i {#since}

- 2.0.0
