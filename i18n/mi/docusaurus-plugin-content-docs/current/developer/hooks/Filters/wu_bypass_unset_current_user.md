# Tātari: wu_bypass_unset_current_user

Tukua ngā kaiwhakawhanake kia hipa i te waehere unset current user.

Mā te whakahoki i tētahi mea kē atu i te null ka hipa i te unset o te kaiwhakamahi o nāianei kua takiuru. Ka whai hua tēnei i ētahi horopaki, hei tauira, ina whakahaere ana i ngā pae-iti e whakamahia ana hei paewhiri kaiwhakahaere.

## Tawhā

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $null_or_bypass | `mixed` | Null kia haere tonu, ko tētahi atu mea hei hipa i tēnei. |
| $current_user | `false\|\WP_User` | Te ahanoa kaiwhakamahi o nāianei. |
