# Tātari: wu_magic_link_enforce_user_agent

Tātari mēnā ka whakaūngia te manatoko user agent.

Tautuhia ki te false kia āhei ai ngā tohu ki te mahi puta noa i ngā pūtirotiro/pūrere rerekē. Ka whakaiti tēnei i te haumarutanga, engari ka whakapiki i te ngāwari o te whakamahi.

## Ngā Tawhā {#parameters}

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $enforce | `bool` | Mēnā ka whakaūngia te ōrite o te user agent. |
