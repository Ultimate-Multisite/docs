# Mahi: wu_page_added

Whakaaetia ngā kaiwhakawhanake mono-mai kia whakahaere i ētahi atu mea ina rēhitatia ngā whārangi.

He rerekē i te wu_page_load, e whakahaerehia ana anake ina tirohia tētahi whārangi motuhake, ka whakahaerehia tēnei hook i te rēhitatanga mō ia whārangi kaiwhakahaere e tāpirihia ana mā te waehere Ultimate Multisite.

## Tawhā {#parameters}

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $page_id | `string` | Te ID o tēnei whārangi. |
| $page_hook | `string` | Te ingoa hook o tēnei whārangi. |

- 2.0.0
