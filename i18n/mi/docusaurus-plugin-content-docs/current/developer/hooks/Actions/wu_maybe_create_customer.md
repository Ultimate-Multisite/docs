# Mahi: wu_maybe_create_customer {#action-wumaybecreatecustomer}

Tukua ngā kaiwhakawhanake mono-mai kia mahi i ētahi atu mahi ina tāpiritia te kiritaki.

Anei te wāhi e tāpiri ai mātou i ngā hooks mō te tāpiri i te customer-&gt;user ki te pae matua hoki, hei tauira.

## Tawhā {#parameters}

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $customer | `\Customer` | Te kiritaki i hangaia pea. |
| $checkout | `\Checkout` | Te akomanga utu-whakamutunga o nāianei. |
