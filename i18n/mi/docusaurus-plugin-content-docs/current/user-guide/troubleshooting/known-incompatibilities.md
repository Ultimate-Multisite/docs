# Whakarongo Incompatibilities me Pākehā Plugin {#known-incompatibilities-with-other-plugins}

Iha i roto i te whakatairanga me te whakamahi Ultimate Multisite, he mea e taea ana akorua ki te pēnei i ngā pētranga (plugins) kia whai i ngā tino pai i roto i te mahi.

Kami e hoki atu ki te whakaheke i tēnei list akorua me harakeke i roto i harakeke hou, engari ko te mea nui, ko ko te mea kei te pēnei ana i te list, he mea e taea ai ana koe te whai i te deaktive (deactivate) i ahau i te mea he tino pai te kainga Ultimate Multisite Network.

He tēnei list me te mea kua whakaritea i te mea e taea ai akorua ko te whakamahi i āhuatanga hāpai i ngā mea kei te pēnei ana:

**Kei te whai i nekehanga WPMU DEV:** He tino pai ahau i ngā pētranga **noa** e whai i te WPMU DEV, he mea e pēnei ana ia e taea ai te whawhai i te flow o te registration o Ultimate Multisite i te whakamutunga (he mea e taea ai ki te mea kia whakatō akounta). He tēnei list me ngā pētranga kei te whai i ngā mea e whakahou ana i tēnei mea:

  * Signup Language: _He tino pai ahau i Ultimate Multisite, he mea e pēnei ana ia e hihau (add-on) e tino pai ahau, he whai te whakamahi ingoa Ultimate Multisite: Language Switcher._

  * Set Blog Description on Blog Creation: _He tino pai ahau i Ultimate Multisite, ko te mea kei te pēnei ana ia, ko tautoko ai te whakamahi plugin e hāpai. He mea e pēnei ana ia, ko tautoko ai te whakamahi plugin e hāpai._

  * New Blog Templates: _He tino pai ahau i Ultimate Multisite, ko te mea kei te pēnei ana ia, ko tautoko ai te whakamahi plugin e hāpai._

  * Set Password on Multisite Blog Creation: _He tino pai ahau i Ultimate Multisite, ko te mea kei te pēnei ana ia, ko tautoko ai te whakamahi plugin e hāpai._

  * Signup TOS: _He tino pai ahau i Ultimate Multisite, ko te mea kei te pēnei ana ia, ko tautoko ai te whakamahi plugin e hāpai._

  * Anti-Splog: Engari he tino pai ahau i Ultimate Multisite.

**Aha** : He tino pai ahau i ngā pētranga WPMU Dev katoa kia whai i te mahi i runga i te Ultimate Multisite. E ngā whakamāhi, ko e taea ai akorua te whakamahi i _Smush_, _Forminator_, _Defender_, me ngā mea kei a.
