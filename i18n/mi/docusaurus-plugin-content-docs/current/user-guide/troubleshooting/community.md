# He aha te wahi ki te whai i te Ultimate Multisite Community {#how-to-join-ultimate-multisite-community}

He mea hekeheki te Ultimate Multisite Community, he wā i taea ai koe te whai i ngā uaua me ngā koutou katoa o Ultimate Multisite, ka whakatau i ngā whakaaro, ngā wero, me ngā mea katoa. Hei whai i te Ultimate Multisite Community, pēnei i te https://community.ultimatemultisite.com/home.

I te manawa whai ake (welcome page), whai i te link " **tuku ahau akau i tēnei** ".

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Manawa whai ake o te Community me te link tuku ahau akau -->

Ka whai koe ki te puta mai i te puta mai o te whakatere ahau. I tēnei wā, ka taea e koe te teke i te username, email, me password. Whakawhana (Captcha) me whakatipu i te puta mai o te **Register** button.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Manawa Register o te Community me ngā feteri username, email, password me Captcha -->

Ita kua whakatere koe, he wā ki te whakamaoritanga o tō profile. I tēnei manawa, ka taea e koe te iawhia i tō pōhakaheke (photo), te whero tō ingoa katoa, te kōrero iti mārie me ngā mea katoa. He tino pai hoki te whero ngā link o ngā social media mo tō account me te whakarite i ngā whakataunga mō tō account.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Manawa whakamaoritanga o tō profile o te Community me ngā feteri iawhia pōhakaheke me kōrero -->

I tēnei wā, ka puta koe ki te channel "Introductions", i tēnei wā ka taea e koe te whai i te whakamāoritanga i tō pūrongo me te māngere i ngā anggota katoa. Ka taea e koe te whakamaoritanga o tō pūrongo i te whakapichu i te button **New Post**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Channel Introductions o te Community me te button New Post -->

Ngā channel o te Lounge section me te channel "Dev Logs" he tino pai mō koutou katoa. He tino nui te whakataunga wairua (special permission) ki te whai i ngā channel hou i te community.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Channel o te Community e tino i te lounge section me te channel Dev Logs -->

Ka puta tēnei whakataunga i te wā ka puta koe i tō ana key Ultimate Multisite active. Hei te whakamahi i tēnei, whai i ngā link o te channel hou me te whakatipu i te button **Unlock**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Community private channel with Unlock button -->

Ka whaiakahi koe ki te puta mai o te wāwharawhai (unlock access) mātou. Aroha i te key lisensi o aua Ultimate Multisite me te klikki i te nútua Unlock Access:

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Community unlock access page with license key input -->

Ko tō lisensi e whakamārama ana, ā, ka taea e koe te whai ake i ngā kanero (private channels). Ko tēnei mahi he pēnei i ka taea te whakawhanake i 24 ora, ko tēnā, klikki i te link ki te whawhai i te wāhi i te whānau me te whai i ngā wāhi kia kaha koe i roto i te whakahaere i tēnei mahi.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Community license validation confirmation page -->
