# He aha te waiata (How to get help)

He tino ana koe ki tō key License, ā, he whai ake i tēnei he whaiako ki te timata mātou kia tū he tino pai i roto i te wā e ka puta koe i te whakauru Ultimate Multisite.

Ko tēnei pūrongo e whakatakoto ngā oneto whai tika mō te whaihohonotanga (support channels) mō ngā oneto hou, ā, ko te mea nui he waiata/māhi whakahou (info) kua mau koe kia whai hoahoa ki te timata mātou.

## Wāhi Whakarongo (Help Site)

Ko taea e koe te whai i ngā meka (articles) me ngā mōhaka whakatika (tutorials) mai i tō wāhi whakarongo, ko e huna ana i te link i runga i runga i.

[)

He tino whakamārama mātou ki ngā mea nui o Ultimate Multisite ā-ātua. He ngā meka "Getting Started" (Whakatū Tō Whakatika) mo te wāhi whakarongo, ā, he ngā waiata mō ngā āhuatanga whakahou (answers to common questions) ka mau ana ra i te wāhi hoki, e tēnei ahau, he pēnei i te mea kāore koe i tahu i te whakatū add-ons, he pēnei i te mea kāore koe i tahu i te whakatō payment gateways.

## Whakawhanaungatanga me te Whakarongo (Contact Support)

Ko ahau e pēnei i ka puta koe i te wāhi whakarongo mā te whakamahi i te icon o te speech bubble (whakaaro) e huna ana i te tangata whakatika (lower right side) o tō website, ATAU he ngā email tika ki support@ultimatemultisite.com.

I roto i te wā e ka whai hoahoa ki te timata mātou mō ngā mea whakamārama (technical issues), he tino nui ana te whakatū i te whakataki mā te whakatakoto whakamārama i tō mea, me te screenshot, ngā error messages, ahau ngā waiata/māhi whakahou (information) katoa e pēnei i te mea ka taea ai ki ngā tangata whakatika mōna te whakaaro ātaahua me te whakaaro i tō mea. He tino nui hoki te whai ake i tō key License. Ko ngā oneto hou (active license holders) tekau ana ki te whai hoahoa.

## Wāhi Whakawhanaungatanga (Community Site)

Ko tō [community site](https://community.ultimatemultisite.com/) he wāhi mō ngā Ultimate Multisite users kia whai hoahoa me ngā rātou. He pēnei i te hāmonga mō te waiata/māhi whakahou (information system) ko ngā anggota e taea te post, comment ki ngā whakataki, give advice, collaborate, he tino nui ana te mea he ka mau koe i tō key Ultimate Multisite kia whakawhiti i ngā wāhi/pages whai rāhui.
