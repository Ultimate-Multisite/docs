# Whakaritenga Pākehā (Manual Payments) (v2)

_**NOTE WHAKANGA: Ko tēnei whakamārama e tino whai ana ki Ultimate Multisite version 2.x.**_

Ko te pākehā (manual payments) he wahi mā koe kia offeri ngā oneto pākehā i runga i a koe, ko te Stripe pe ka PayPal ā, he pēnei i ahau e hiahia ana mō ngā mea e taea mo ngā user. Hehe, he rite ki te wire transfer (wire) heine he bank transfer heine he pai katoa mā ngā user o koe i te wāhi o koe.

Kia pakihi i te waiata manu (manual payment), whai ki te menu **Payments** i te bar kiriwi (left bar). I tēnei wā, ka taea e koe te whakaahua katoa o ngā waiata (payments) o tō wāhi me ngā whakamārama oha, ā, ngā **status** (status) o ngā waiata hāpai. Ka mau te waiata manu i te status **Pending** (hanga/e whai) i roto i ngā waiata katoa i ko atu, ko koe ka whakawhiti i tōna status manu.

![Payments list showing pending manual payment](/img/admin/payments-list.png)

Whakarite ki te puta mai i te puta mai o te waiata (payment page) i runga i te **reference code** (kode o whakamārama). I tēnei puta mai, he ko atu katoa ngā whakamārama o te waiata hāpai, engari e hoki ana i te reference ID, ngā aua (products), ngā wā (timestamps) me ngā mea katoa.

![Payment details page showing reference code, products, and totals](/img/admin/payment-edit.png)

I te whāinga (right column), ka taea e koe te whak whakarē i te status o te waiata hāpai. Ka whakawhiti ai i a ia ki te **Completed** (whakamārama) me te whakamārama i te option **Activate Membership** (whakawhanaungatanga o te membership), ka whakatika i tō wāhi o te mea o te koutou me ka whakahirahira ngā membership o tō koutou.

![Payment edit page with Status set to Completed and Activate Membership toggle](/img/admin/payment-activate-membership.png)
