# Whakawhanaungatanga me Ultimate Multisite

He whakawhanake mai i Pākehā, engari ko te plugin he tino whai whakamaumahara (translatable) ahau, ā, e tino mōhio mātou ana ki te whai whakaaro i ngā whakataunga o te whakawhanaungatanga.

## Whakawhanaungatanga Plugin Core {#core-plugin-translations}

Ko te core Ultimate Multisite plugin he whakawhanake mai i te **WordPress.org translation platform** ahau:

**[translate.wordpress.org/projects/wp-plugins/ultimate-multisite/](https://translate.wordpress.org/projects/wp-plugins/ultimate-multisite/)**

He tēnei systeme whakawhanatanga o te whānau WordPress. I roto i te wā e hōtuitia ngā whakawhanatanga ki te huihui tika, ka whakatika ai ana ngā ia ki ngā mea katoa o Ultimate Multisite mō ngā wāhi katoa.

**He tino pai ahau ki te tino whai whakaaro!** Ko koe he reo he hoki i te reo Pākehā? He tino pai ahau ki te whai whakaaro i te whakawhanatanga. Ko te whakatau i ngā stringa (strings) a tōku reo, he tino mārama ai. Hei whakatū atu:

1. Rongo ki [translate.wordpress.org](https://translate.wordpress.org/projects/wp-plugins/ultimate-multisite/)
2. Whakarite i tō reo
3. Whakawhiti i "Contribute Translation" (Whakawhanaungatanga Whakawhanatanga)
4. Ko koe e pai ana akorā i he account WordPress.org

Ko te tino pai ahau ki te whakahaere he editor o whakawhanatanga mō tō reo (he taea ai i ngā whakawhanatanga), he whai whakaaro atu qua i [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite/discussions).

## Whakawhanaungatanga Addon {#addon-translations}

Ko ngā addon plugins e whakawhanake ana i roto i te wāhi:

**[translate.ultimatemultisite.com](https://translate.ultimatemultisite.com/)**

Ngā whakawhanatanga addon he tino whakamahinga AI mō te whakatū atu. Ko e whai whakaaro ana ahau ki ngā reo katoa, engari he taea te hiahia i ngā whakahau (phrasing) kei te pēnei, ā, ka whai whakaaro mātou ki ngā whakatika o ngā whakatūmanaki (human translators) kia tika me tino pai.

## Whakawhanatanga Whenua {#local-translations}

### Whakawhanainga i Poedit {#using-poedit}

Kia kaha ki te whakamahi te desktop application **[Poedit](https://poedit.net)** ki te whakamaoritanga o te file `.pot`:

1. Download Poedit mo [poedit.net](https://poedit.net)
2. Open te file `.pot` mai i `wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite/lang/`
3. Whakamaoritia ngā strings (ngā kupu) i te interface o Poedit
4. Save -- Ka whakamaoritia e Poedit i ngā file `.po` me `.mo`
5. Whakarite ngā file output i te folder `lang`

### Whakawhanainga i Loco Translate {#using-loco-translate}

He pikinini WordPress **[Loco Translate](https://wordpress.org/plugins/loco-translate/)** e hiahia ana ki te whakamaoritanga, e hiahia ana ki te whakatō i ngā whakamaoritanga i te admin panel anō, me te whakamahi i ngā taonga desktop. E tino pai ai ia i te whakamahi i te pikinini ko e whakatō ana me te whakatū i ngā file translation mo koe.
