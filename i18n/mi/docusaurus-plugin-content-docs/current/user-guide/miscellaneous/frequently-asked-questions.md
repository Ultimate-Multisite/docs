# Pātai Whakamahi (Frequently Asked Questions)

He pēpi o ngā **Pātai Whakamahi** (Frequently Asked Questions) e whāinga ki te tino whakamahinga i roto i ngā whai whakaaro me ngā whai whakaaro e pēnei i tōna whakatau.

  * **He aha te Ultimate Multisite?**

Ko Ultimate Multisite he plugin o WordPress, tino āhua ana ia mō ngā installa multisite o WordPress, ka whakawhiti i tō installa WordPress ki te whare whenua premium o ngā wāhi – pēnei i [WordPress.com](https://WordPress.com) – e hiahia ana ki te whaiaro (clients) kia whakatō i ngā wāhi mā ngā fees o mōna whaea, mōna wā, mōna takiwā (you can also create Free plans).

  * **Māora akorua ko e hiahia ana Ultimate Multisite ki te whakawhiti i te Multisite?**

Kāo. He tino āhua ana ko e hiahia ana Ultimate Multisite ki te WordPress multisite, subdirectory, hena subdomain.

  * **He aha ngā āhuatanga e hiahia ana Ultimate Multisite?**

He tino āhua ana ko e hiahia ana Ultimate Multisite:

**WordPress Version** : v5.3+ (Recommended: Last Stable Version)
**PHP Version** : 7.4.x (Support to > 8.0 coming soon)
**MySQL Version** : v5+ (Recommended: Version 5.6, in case you don't have 8.0 available on your hosting provider)

_He multisite (subdomain or subdirectory) hiahia ana ki te whakawhiti._ [How to Install WordPress Multisite](/user-guide/getting-started/how-to-install-wordpress-multisite).

  * **Māora akorua ko e hiahia ana Ultimate Multisite ki te Multisite me Subdirectories?**

Āe. He tino āhua ana ko e whaiho i ngā installa subdirectory me ngā installa subdirectory.

  * **Māora akorua ko e hiahia ana Ultimate Multisite ki te Multisite me Subdomains?**

Āe. He tino āhua ana ko e whaiho i ngā installa subdirectory me ngā installa subdomain.

  * **Ko ahau hei whakamahi Ultimate Multisite mō te whakatau i ngā Plugins me Themes e taea ai ngā user?**

Āe. Me Ultimate Multisite, ka taea e koe te whiri i ngā plugins me themes e hiahia ana ki te every plan o tō network.

  * **Ko ahau hei whakamahi Ultimate Multisite mō te whakatau i te whakapapa (number of posts) he taea ai te user i tō wāhi?**

He. Mea te Ultimate Multisite, e taea ai koe te whakarite quota pō (post quotas) mō **every available post type** i runga i tō whānau (network). E taea ai kō koe te whakatau te whakapapa o ngā au (users) kua taea te fesoaso atu, te **whakahaere o disk space**, me ngā mea katoa.

  * **E tino tika ana ko e puta mai Ultimate Multisite mō Site Templates?**

He. Me Ultimate Multisite, e taea ai koe te whakamaoritanga (create) so many Site Templates ka tino pai ahau. Iha Site Templates he nui ake i themes; he site pūrere (normal sites) i runga i tō whānau. E taea ai koe te ativa plugins, themes, me te whakarite akorua (customize) tēnei Site Template i te mea e tino tika ana. Ko te mea, ko inā he whai ake te au, ko te site o ia i whakamaoritanga i te template hāpai i te whakamahi, ko e puta mai he copy o tēnei template, me ngā akorua katoa i roto i te pūrere.

E taea ai koe te whakarite template-tahi mō he plan. I te mea he tino tika ana, ko ia au kua whai ake i te plan hāpai i te site o ia e puta mai he duplicate o tēnei Site Template.

  * **E tino tika ana ko e whai akorua (integrate) Ultimate Multisite ki WooCommerce?**

He!

Ko te mea, ko inā koe e tino tika ana ko ngā au o tō whānau kua taea te whakamahi i WooCommerce mō rātou, he taea ahau i te whakamahi Ultimate Multisite. E taea ai kō koe te whakatau te whakapapa o ngā puta (products) kua taea te whakamaoritanga e ngā wāhi plan (plan tier).

I te mea e hoa ana ki ngā whakaingo paimā (subscription payments) mō Ultimate Multisite, he taea ahau te whakamahi i te [Ultimate Multisite: WooCommerce Integration Add-on](https://ultimatemultisite.com/addons/wp-ultimo-woocommerce/) kia whai ake i ngā au o tō Ultimate Multisite kua taea te whakamahi i mga gateway o WooCommerce mō tō site pūrere (main site). I te mea he tino tika ana, ko e taea ai koe te whai ake i te library nui o mga payment gateways kua whakawhanake mo WooCommerce me te whakamahinga i ahau ki te whakatau ngā au o tō Ultimate Multisite.

  * **He wahi ana ko te whakaputa (migrate) ngā au mō WPMUDEV Pro Sites?**

He Ultimate Multisite ora tino āhuatanga ki a koe, he [taonga mātou hei i roto i te whakawhanake] (https://ultimatemultisite.com/addons/wp-ultimo-pro-sites-migrator/) i tō whānau Pro Sites network.

  * **Nga "Allowed Plugins" tab e pōtiki ana i tō Edit Page o te Plan?**

He Ultimate Multisite hei tino whakaae akorua ki ngā plugin e āhuatanga (network-activated) ana i tō network, he whakawhakarite akorua ki ngā plugin e āhuatanga ana. I roto i te mea he āhuatanga ana te plugin, ka whai ake ai i a ia noa i roto i every site o tō network.

Ko ko te mea, ko koe kia whakaaro akorua mō te plugin e tika ana, me tino tikanga ki te pūrongo, he āhuatanga ana au te plugin ahau, me te āhuatanga ana au ahau.

  * **Ko taea ai ahau te whakamahi i tō Whakawhiti Pākehā (Pricing Table) design?**

He. Ko ko e 1.4.0, e whai akorua ki ngā template overrides he Ultimate Multisite.

  * **Mā te Ultimate Multisite pēnei ana i WP Engine?**

He. He āhuatanga ana au te Ultimate Multisite noa i WP Engine, engari ko te whakamahi o te domain mapping kia pēnei ana, ka tino whai ake ai ngā mahi manu manu (manual extra steps) e tō admin (whakawhiti te domain ki te panel o WP Engine). **He mea he tino āhuatanga ana au i te 1.6.0.** He Ultimate Multisite hei whai akorua tika ki te WP Engine API, me ngā domains ka pēnei ana ki te panel automatically i roto i te mapping.

  * **Ko wai tō whakaaro mō te hosting provider?**

He Ultimate Multisite hei tino pai ana ki ngā hosting provider ahau. Ko te mea tino āhuatanga ana ko he nekehanga akorua ana i ngā compani hosting ahau, he pēnei i Closte, Serverpilot, WPMUDEV, WP Engine, Cloudways, Gridpane me [Runcloud.io](http://Runcloud.io) mō te domain mapping capabilities, engari ka tino pai ana ia noa i roto i te whakawhanake (work out of the box). Ko mō ngā hosting ahau, he mea e taea ana koe kia whakatika akorua ko te domain mapping manu manu.
