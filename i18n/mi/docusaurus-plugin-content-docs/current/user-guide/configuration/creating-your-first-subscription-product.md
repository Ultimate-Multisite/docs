# Whakawhanaungatanga Pākehā (v2)

_**AHA! He tino mō te whakamahi Ultimate Multisite v2.x ko tēnei article. Ko te mea,**_ **_**he tino mō te whiti i tēnei article**_**.

Kia kaha ki te whakawhanaunga o tō wāhi (network) ā, ka pūrongo i tō mahi ki ngā mea e taea e ngā mea e tika ana. He tino pai ahau ki te whakaatu i tō mahi ki ngā mea e tika ana. Ka waiho mātou i te whakatika i te mea katoa e toa i te wāhi (products).

## Tau utu o te Pākehā (Product Type)

Me Ultimate Multisite, ka taea e koe te ofiri tau iki (subscription) mō ngā mea toru ki tō wāhi: **plans** me **add-ons (Order Bump)**. Ka taea te whakahoki i ngā add-ons ki te tauira rua: **packages** me **services**. Ka whakaatu mātou i ngā whakamārama o ngā rātou whakamahinga me ngā mea e tika ana i roto i te ao.

  * **Plans** : Ko te puku (fundamental) o Ultimate Multisite ko te plan. He tino pai ki tō wāhi he taea ai i tō wāhi te whai i te membership, engari ka puta koe i te plan. Ka whai whakaaro ahau ki ngā wāhi e toa i tō puku (plan) me ngā whakamārama e toa i tō wāhi e toa i tō puku (plan).

  * **Packages** : Ngā add-ons e tino pai ana ki te mahi o ngā plans o Ultimate Multisite. Ka puta he whakaaro ahau i ngā whakamārama me ngā mea hou, plugins, ko theme ki te plan rangatira e toa i tō wāhi kua oti. Ko te pēpi (basic) plan he taea ai 1,000 wāhi e tika ana i te rā, engari ka puta koe i te package e whaora i tēnei wāhi ki te 10,000.

  * **Services:** Ngā add-ons e kāore e paku i ngā mahi o Ultimate Multisite. He mahi (tasks) e toa i tō wāhi mo tō wāhi e tika ana i roto i te mea kua oti koe i te plan e toa i tō wāhi. Ko te pēpi, he taea ai i tō wāhi te whai i te membership, engari ka puta koe i te service e pai i tēnei design o te wāhi.

## Whakawātea ngā Products (Managing Products)

Muitia irau i te **Products** tab i Ultimate Multisite **(Ultimate Multisite > Products)** he taea te whakatira ki ngā plānē (plans) i te ao o te hosting mōhio.

I roto i te Ultimate Multisite, tino whai whakaaro ana te Products tab i te whakamahi me ngā pōtiki (limitations) e puta mai ki te aua product ahau ahau. Ko ngā pōtiki ei whakatika ana i te whakataki o te whakatohu product ahau ahau, te whero, ngā kāzi, me ngā whakatau.

Ko tēnei wiki e whakamana ana i tō whakamārama mō tēnei pānuku nui o Ultimate Multisite.

![Products list page](/img/config/products-list.png)

## Whāinga Products (Adding Products)

Mā te **Ultimate Multisite > Products > Add Product** he te whakataki ki te whakamahi i te plan, package, ahau service ahau e whakaaro ana i te whakamahinga o item hou.

![Add Product button](/img/config/product-add-button.png)

Ko te interface he whero tūāhi rua. I te tangata tangata (left), he ngā tabs mō te tino whakatohu product ahau, me te tangata tangata (right), he ngā pōtiki mō te whakatau i te whero base price o te product, te aua state active, me te image o te product.

![Product edit page overview](/img/config/product-edit-full.png)

### Whakataki (Description)

Ko te information base product ahau e taea te whakaaro ana mō te whero name me te description. Ko ngā identifiers ei puta mai i te wā e pēnei i te plan me te pricing selection, invoices, upgrades, me ngā mea katoa e pēnei.

![Product description section](/img/config/product-description.png)

### Ngā Mahi o Pricing (Pricing Type)

Ko Ultimate Multisite e puta mai i ngā mahi pricing tino whero. Ko te option **paid** e pōtiki ai te network administrator mō te information mō te price o te product me te aua billing frequency.

### Pricing (Pricing)

Ko te component o price e whakaaro ana i te base product price ahau me te interval o te billing.

![Pricing and save section](/img/config/product-pricing-save.png)

kia whakamāhi, he mea tino nui ki te whakaahuatanga o te whakaingoa o te whaiaro (price). i roto i te whakamāhi, he pēpi o te whakaingoa o te $29.99 me te whakaritenga o 1 mōtuhake, ka whakawhanaunga ai i te $29.99 i roto i te mea e whaiaro ana i tona mōtuhake ko i roto i te mōtuhake o mōtuhake. ā, he pēpi o te whakaingoa o $89.97 me te whakaritenga o 3 mōtuhake, ka whakawhanaunga ai i te whakahaupūā tuina i tona mōtuhake ko i roto i te mōtuhake tuina.

### Whakamāhi Tuina (Billing Cycles)

Ko te whakamāhi tuina e whaiaro ana i te āhuatanga o te whakahaupūā o te mōtuhake whakahaupūā tuina, ā, he tino tika te whakaaro i tēnei i runga i ngā te pakihi poari (contracts) me ngā wā tuina.

![Pricing and save section](/img/config/product-pricing-save.png)

E roto i tēnei, he pēpi o te whakahaupūā o te whaiaro $29.99 me te mōtuhake o 1 mōtuhake me 12 whakahaupūā tuina, ka whakawhanaunga ai i te $29.99 ko i roto i te mōtuhake o mōtuhake mo te puku i te mea e whaiaro ana i tēnei i te mōtuhake 12 mōtuhake. i roto i ngā kupu, ka whakatutaku i tēnei whakaritenga he wā tuina o $29.99 ko i roto i te mōtuhake mo mōtuhake mo 12 mōtuhake ā, ā, ka whakahoki i te whakahaupūā.

Ko te **Customer Role** e whakatū ana i te roa (role) e whai ake ai ko te mea e whakatū ana i te wā ko ka whakahou i te site. Ko te roa default o Ultimate Multisite e taea te whakatū i **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Login & Registration**.

Mō te whero i ngā pēri me te hoahoa i ngā pēri o te pēri e whakahirahira, he tino pai tēnei mō te whakamahi i te whaiaro (documentation) i **Ultimate Multisite: Settings**.

I tōna wā, i tōna wā, ko te rāote tax e whakatau ana i **Ultimate Multisite > Settings > Manage Tax Rates** he tino tika ki te whiriwhiri i te rāote mo te puta mai o te mea.

#### Site Templates

I te tino, ko site templates he website WordPress paku katoa kua oti te clone ki te wāpehi o te customer i tōna whakataunga (subscription).

![Site Templates tab](/img/config/product-site-templates.png)

Ko te administrator o te network he whakamaoritanga me whakamahi i te template site he website WordPress pēnei i te website regular, me ngā themes, plugins, me te mōhaka (content) kua oti te whakatau me whakamahinga. Ko te template site e clone ana i te mea katoa ki te customer.

Ko tēnei tab e hiahia ai te administrator o te network kia whakatau i te pēhea te mihi o ngā site templates i runga i te puta mai o te whakataunga hou. Mo te whakamahi i ngā site templates mo tēnei plan, kia whakatau i te **Allow Site Templates** toggle ki te active (whakaahua) state.

I tōna wā, ko te mea e whai i te **Allow Site Templates** he whakawhiti, ko te customer ei taea te whiriwhiri ngā templates i roto i te plan ahakoa he pēnei i he checkout form, shareable link, he URL parameter he i taea ai te whakatau i ngā templates. Ko Ultimate Multisite e whakatau tēnei pōtanga i roto i te chain o whakamāhi (fallback chain) mo ngā entry points e kaha ana: ko te settings o te plan e whakawhiti atu, ā, kua oti te whakatau i ngā settings o te checkout-form template, ā, kua oti te whakatau i ngā templates e whiriwhia mō aua URL. Ko tēnei e whakatika ai ngā pōtanga o te plan ki te tika me te whakatika ana i ngā templates ngaahi mea e hāpai ahau i te mea he āhua.

Ko te **site template selection mode** ko e whakatau i te mihi o ngā site templates i runga i te whakataunga (subscription) process.

Te **D** **efault** setting e whaihi i ngā mahi i te fōme checkout. Ko te mea, ko te administrator o te wāhi network i tino whakatau he apa selection step i te tikanga checkout me te apa hoki i whakaataki i templates, ko tēnei setting ei whaiaro i ngā ara whakatau e whakahou mai i te apa checkout.

Ko te whakatau **A** **ssign Site Template** e whakatō ana te whakatau o te template e whaiaro. Ko tēnei, e whakatū i te whakatau selection step i te tikanga checkout katoa.

I te whakamutunga, ko **C** **hoose Available Site Templates** e whakahou mai ngā templates e whakahau i te apa checkout me ngā templates e whaiaro i tēnei setting. Ko te mea, he taea ai te whakatau template e whaiaro ana hei tino whakatutuki i te mea ko te mea e whaiaro ana koe ki te whakatau.

I te whakamutunga, ko te mea, ko te administrator o te wāhi network e tino whakaatu ana he te selection o templates e tū i ngā apa checkout steps, ko te setting ' _default_ ' tekau mai. He pēnei, hei whakatū i te whakatau selection template me te whakahaere i te whakatau ki ngā plan settings, ko ngā option ' _assign new template_ ' he tino pai, ko ' _choose available site templates_ ' hoki.

#### Sites

Ko te tab **Sites** he pēterero o te mahi whakatutuki (limitations functionality) o Ultimate Multisite.

![Sites tab](/img/config/product-sites.png)

Ko tēnei setting e tino whakatau ana te whakapapa maximum number of sites (whakamārama: ahau e tino whakahau i te whakanui o te pūtaiao) he mea e taea te whakamaoritia e te mea e whaiaro ana koe ki te membership.

Ko te mea, ko te mea e tika ana hei whakatau i te limitation, kia whakatō i te toggle **limit sites** ki te akorua (active state) me te tino whakahau i te maximum number of sites i te field **site allowance**.

#### Visits

Ko te tab **Visits** he pēterero mō te system o whakamārama (limitations system) o Ultimate Multisite. Ko tēnei setting e tino whakatū ana te accounting me te throttling subsequent unique visitors ki te site o te mea e whaiaro ana koe.

![Visits tab](/img/config/product-visits.png)

Mauri whakamahi i tēnei whakaritenga he pēke marketing. He aha te mea e taea ai ngā administrator o te network ki te whakamahi i tēnei whakaritenga mō te whaiaro i ngā wāhi katoa, he mea hei whakamōhio atu ki ngā mea wāhi (customers) kia whai whakaaro ki te whakawhanake plan hou i roto i te wā e hakoa ngā wāhi whakahau. He aha te mea e taea ai tēnei whakaritenga mō te administrator o te network, he mea hei whakatika me te whakatika i te wāhi nui o te traffic ki ngā wāhi (sites) kia whaiaro ngā rātou manawa o te systema.

Mō te whakamahi i tēnei āhuatanga, whakatika te **limit unique visits** toggle ki te wāhi active (whakaahua) me tino whakatau te whakapapa mō te hāpai nui o ngā manawa whakahau i te **unique visits quota** field.

I raro i te limit ko e whaiaro Ultimate Multisite ka whawhai atu ki te hoatu i te wāhi o te mea wāhi (customer's site) i tētahi waiata, i runga i te whakaatu i he pēke mō te hāpai nui.

#### Users

He pēke 'Users' o Ultimate Multisite e hakoa ngā wāhi whakahau ki te administrator o te network kia whakatika ngā wāhi mō te whakaputa me te whaiaro i ngā manawa (roles) he taea ai.

![Users tab](/img/config/product-users.png)

Mō te whakamahi i tēnei āhuatanga whakahau, whakatika te **limit user** toggle ki te wāhi active e piko i a ia ki te whero.

I raro i te roa mō ngā manawa (roles) he taea ai te whakahau, whakatika te toggle i runga i tēnei me te tino whakatau te limit nui hoki i te field ingoa.

#### Post Types

He pēke 'Post Types' e hakoa ngā wāhi whakahau ki te administrator o te network kia whakatika ngā wāhi mō te post types katoa he nui ake i WordPress.

![Post Types tab](/img/config/product-post-types.png)

I te whakawhanake o WordPress, ngā posts me ngā post types he pēke nui o te mahi nui o ia, ko he mea hei whakatika i te Ultimate Multisite's limitations system mo te administrator o te network kia taea ai te whakatama me te whakatika i ngā limit.

Mō te whakamahi i tēnei subsystem whakahau, whakatika te **limit post types** toggle ki te wāhi active e piko i a ia ki te whero.

He aha, mō taku whakamārama: Mo te ao Māori, he tino tika tēnei whakamārama.

#### Whakawātea Whainga (Disk Space)

Ko te **Disk Space** tab e hiahia ana i ngā administrator o te wānanga (network administrators) kia whakatū i te wāhi e pēnei i te whaiaro o ngā mea e whakamahinehia e ngā mea mō te whānau.

![Disk Space tab](/img/config/product-disk-space.png)

E tōtōtō, i WordPress multisite, ko ngā core files (ngā file ana o te wānanga) e whare i roto i ngā wāhi katoa o te wānanga, ā, ko ngā directory (wāhi) whakahouake kia whaiaro mō ngā media files me ngā uploads. Ko ko ngā whakaritenga me ngā whakawātea (limitations) he pēnei i te mea e whakatū ana i tēnei.

Kia whero i te whakamārama o te disk usage limitation, kia whero i te **limit disk size per site** toggle ki te wāhi e tika ana.

Engari, whakatō i te mahinga mō te whakatū ake (maximum upper limit) i megabytes i te field **disk space allowance**.

#### Domain Whaiaro (Custom Domain)

E ngā whakamārama, i te toggling tēnei option, e taea ai koe te whero i ngā domain whaiaro (custom domains) mō tēnei plan anara.

![Custom Domains tab](/img/config/product-custom-domains.png)

#### Themes (Ngā Whānau)

Ko te **Themes** tab i roto i ngā option o te puku (product options) e hiahia ana i ngā administrator o te wānanga kia whero i ngā themes mō ngā mea e taea ai ngā customer mō te whirihi, ā, he tino tika, hei whakatū i te state (whakahaere) o te theme.

![Themes tab](/img/config/product-themes.png)

_**He whakamārama: Ko mō te themes kia taea ai ngā customer mō te whirihi, ko me te network enabled (whakaae) e ngā administrator o te wānanga.**_

![Network themes page](/img/config/product-themes-network-enabled.png)

Ko te option **visibility** e tino whakamārama ana i te mea he visible (hei tika) au te theme ki te customer ahiahi i te viewing tab **Appearance > Themes** o te wāhi rā i te wānanga. Kia whero tēnei option ki **Hidden**, ko te theme e whakatū mai i te hiahia me te whakatū i te ability (whakahaere) kia whirihi ana i a ia.

![Themes tab](/img/config/product-themes.png)

Ko te selection **behavior** e tino whakamārama ana i te mea he tika au te state o te theme i te wāhanga e whakatū mai i te site o te customer.

I roto i te au **A vailable**, ka taea e te theme whakamahi atu he tino pai ki te mea mo te mea e whakapāoa ana (self-activation) e te mea mua. I roto i te au **Not Available** (Kua nei nei), ka whakatū atu i te mea mai i te mea mua i te mea e taea ai te whakaatu i te theme. I te awhi, ko te rito **Force Activate** ka whakatō i te pēpi me te whakamahi i te theme, ā, ka whai ake ana i te theme he mea nui (default) i roto i te whakamaoritanga o te wāhi.

#### Plugins

Mā te au Themes, ka taea e Ultimate Multisite ki te administrator o te wāhi te tōtō atu i te whakaaturanga o ngā plugins ki ngā mea mua ahau me te mea e pēpi ana i te whakatō i te wāhi hou.

![Plugins tab](/img/config/product-plugins.png)

Ko te **visibility** drop-down ka tino whakamahi ai i te plugin ki te tika katoa he taea ai ia te whakaatu ana (visible) au te whakaatu ana (hidden) ki te mea mua i te mea e pēpi ana i te wāhi ahau, i roto i te menu option o Plugins.

Ka taea te whakatō atu i te ao administrator o te wāhi i te ao o ngā plugins ma te whakamahi i ngā options i te drop-down o behavior (whakaritenga).

![Plugins tab](/img/config/product-plugins.png)

Ko te rito **Default** ka whai ana ki te au o te plugin e whakatō ana i te template o te wāhi e pēpi ana i te mea mua ahau. I roto i te mea, ko ngā plugins e whakamahinga ana i roto i te template e whakamahia ana ka mau tonu he mea nui (activated) i roto i te site e whakatō ana i te customer i te cloning o te template ki te site o te customer.

Ko te **Force Activate** ka whakatō i te plugin i te au active i runga i te whakamaoritanga o te wāhi, ā, i te awhi, ko te **Force Inactivate** ka whakatū atu i te plugin i te au deactivated i runga i te whakamaoritanga o te wāhi. I ngā wāori e hoki ana ki ngā mea he tēnei, ka taea ai te whakarổiake manuahi (manually) te au o te plugin e te customer i roto i te menu Plugins o te WordPress o te taumaha.

Te whakaritea i te **Force Activate & Lock** setting pēnei i te whakatika, engari ka whakatika i te mea he tino whaiora ana (locked), kia whakamaoritia te tētahi plugin e noho ana i te wā. Ko te setting o Force Activate and Lock ka whakatika i te plugin ki te wā pēnei i te active, ā, ka whakatika i te kōrero o te mea he tino whaiora ana (locked) kia whakamaoritia i te customer. Pēnei i te **Force Inactivate & Lock** setting, ka whakatika i te plugin ki te wā pēnei i te inactive, ā, ka whakatika i te kōrero o te user kia whakamaoritia i te plugin.

He mea nui ana ki te administrator o te wāwhara (network administrator) na te whakaaro i te Force Activate & Lock me te Force Inactivate & Lock settings i runga i te site templates, he mea e taea te whakatika i te plugin, ā, ka taea te whakawhanake i te plugin state i roto i ngā templates hei pēnei i ko te setting e whai ake nei.

#### Whakamutunga o te Whakawhiti (Reset Limitations)

Ka whakatika i te **Reset Limitations** tab i te whakatika katoa i ngā mea he tino whaiora ana (custom limits) e whakahou ana i te taonga. Hei whakamoteutake i te whakamaoritanga, whakatika i te button o **reset limitations**.

![Reset Limitations tab](/img/config/product-reset-limitations.png)

Hei tiaki i te mahi, whakatika i te toggle o **confirm reset** ki te wā active (whakaahua) i te tupu, ā, whakatika i te button o **reset limitations**.

![Reset Limitations tab](/img/config/product-reset-limitations.png)
