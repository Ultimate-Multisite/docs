# Whakawhanaungatanga me Link Shareable mō Rapa {#managing-shareable-links-for-plans}

Mahere Ultimate Multisite e tino whai ake ana i koe ki te share plan links akorua ki ngā wakamahi ahau. Ko te whakamahi i tēnei, pēnei i te, go te mana i ngā puta mai o ngā mahere ahau, he rātou i te whakarite i te _Click to copy the Shareable Link_ button.

![Product edit page with shareable link button](/img/config/product-edit-full.png)

_I te Page Edit Plan, ka mau koe i te button mō te whakatō link shareable._

Kia tino mārama ana ahau, he whakamahi tēnei o te whakahere (billing frequency) pūrere default ki te whakatō te link. He reira, ko ahakoa te pūrere billing default ahau mahara, i roto i te whakamutunga, ka hoatu koe i link pēnei i: [_**https://network.com/register/premium/**_](https://network.com/register/premium/)

Ko te mea e tino tika ana he tino whakatō (default) ki te quarterly ahau, ka taea e koe te whakarite i 3 i te whakamutunga.

[_**https://network.com/register/premium/3**_](https://network.com/register/premium/3)

Kia tino whai ake ana i te mahere _premium_ mō te pūrere yearly, ka taea e koe te whakarite i 1 me te kupu:

[_**https://network.com/register/premium/1/year**_](https://network.com/register/premium/1/year)

Kia tino whakamārama ana ahau, he mea nui ona i roto i te link ake mō pūrere billing whakahara (billing frequencies) ā-au. Me share link tika mō kuitiriki o ngā pūrere billing ahau.

I raro i te link e share koe ki ngā wakamahi ahau, ka whakawhiti ahau ngātau ahau ki te whakamutunga tīmatanga o tō flow o te signup.
