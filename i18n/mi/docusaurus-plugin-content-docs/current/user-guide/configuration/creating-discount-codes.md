# Whakawhanaungahau Kōrero (v2)

_**TŪTOHUTU MAHU: Ko tēnei kōrero e tino whakamahia mō Ultimate Multisite v2.x.**_

Me te Ultimate Multisite, he taea ahau i roto i whakawhanaunga kōrero (discount codes) ki te whai whakaaro mā te whero i ngā client o koe mō ngā subscription o rātou. He ātaahua te whakamahi i ahau!

## Whakawhanaungahau me Whakawhanaungahau Kōrero {#creating-and-editing-discount-codes}

Mō te whakawhanaunga kōrero (discount code) ana, pēnei i te whakahou ana, he wahi e taea te pahi: **Ultimate Multisite > Discount Codes**.

![List o discount codes — state when no codes exist](/img/config/discount-codes-empty.png)

I tēnei wā, ka hoahoa koe i rāngi o ngā discount codes kua whakahou koe.

Ka taea e koe te whakatipu i **Add Discount Code** (Whakawhanaungahau Kōrero) ki te whakawhanaunga kōrero hou, he tino pai ahau ana, ka taea hoki i roto i te whakamārama i ngā kua whakahou koe.

![List o discount codes me āhuatanga hover e tino mō Edit me Delete](/img/config/discount-codes-list-hover.png)

![Button Add Discount Code i te header o te puta noa](/img/config/discount-codes-add-button.png)

Ka whai koe ki te puta noa i te wā e ka whakawhanaunga kōrero (coupon code) ana, he pēnei ahau i whakamahia ana. I tēnei whakamārama, ka whakawhanaunga kōrero hou mātou.

![Whakaahua o puta noa whakahou discount code me ngā whero katoa te hoki](/img/config/discount-code-edit.png)

Kia whakaaro ahau i ngā whakaritenga e taea ana i tēnei wā:

**Enter Discount Code (Whakarite Kōrero):** He tino whakamārama ahau, he ingoa anō o tō discount code. He kore ai te kōrero kua roa i ngā mea kua roa i ngā customer e pēnei ana ki te whakamahi i te checkout form.

**Description (Whakawhanaungahau):** I tēnei wā, ka taea e koe te whakatipu i te whakamārama iti ahau mō te coupon ko.

![Ngā ingoa o discount code me ngā whakawhanaungahau i te whero o te puta noa](/img/config/discount-code-description.png)

Ka taea hoki ahau te whakahti i te discount code he active (whakaahua) ana he inactive (kua whakahou) ana:

![Status o discount code active](/img/config/discount-code-active.png)

**Coupon code (Kōrero Kōrero):** He tēnei wā, he pēnei ahau i tino whakatau ahau te kōrero kua roa i ngā customer e pēnei ana ki te whakire i te checkout.

![Coupon code field where customers enter the code at checkout](/img/config/discount-code-coupon-field.png)

**Koha (Discount):** Ihi, koe i taea te tōna **pērehenga** (percentage) ka **whakamahara** (fixed amount) o te mōhaka mo tō kōkōrero (discount code).

![Discount amount setting with percentage or fixed-amount dropdown](/img/config/discount-code-amount.png)

**Whakarite i whakamahinga i te whakatere (Apply to renewals):** Ko te mea, ko te option (toggle) he tino whakahou (off) ana, ko tēnei kōkōrero (discount code) e whakamahau ana i te **whakamahara wāhi tūpuna (first payment)** anake. Ngā whakamahi pai katoa kia noa. Ko te mea, ko te option he tino whakahou (on), ko te kōkōrero (discount code) e tika ana mō ngā whakamahinga katoa i roto i te wā.

**Whakarite pērehenga o te whakatū (Setup fee discount):** Ko te mea, ko te option he tino whakahou (off), ko te kōkōrero (coupon code) **e whakawero ana i te pērehenga mo te whakatū (setup fee)** o te rāpanake. Ko te mea, ko te option he tino whakahou (on), koe e taea ai te whakarite te pērehenga (percentage ka fixed amount) ki te whakamahi i tēnei kōkōrero (coupon code) mo te pērehenga o ngā rāpanake.

![Apply to renewals and setup fee discount toggle options](/img/config/discount-code-renewals.png)

**Tūmatau (Active):** Ko te whakamahi manuia ana koe ki te whakahou ana ko te kōkōrero (coupon code) he tino whakahou (active) ka taea te whakawero ana au i te tino whakahou ana ko te kōkōrero (discount code).

![Active toggle to manually enable or disable the discount code](/img/config/discount-code-active.png)

I roto i **Advanced Options**, he mea katoa tēnei whakaritenga:

![Discount code advanced options](/img/config/discount-code-advanced.png)

**Whakarite whakamahi (Limit uses):**

  * **Whakamahinga (Uses):** Ihi, koe e taea te whakaahua i te whakapau o ngā whakamahinga mo tēnei kōkōrero (discount code).

  * **Māori Whakamahinga (Max uses):** Ko te mea, ko te mea he whakatutuku ana i te pērehenga o ngā wā e taea ai ngā tauira (users) whakamahi tēnei kōkōrero (discount code). Ko te mea, ko te mea he puta mai i te 10 ki te hihiko ana, ko te kōkōrero (coupon) e tika ana whakamahinga i te 10 wā anake. I roto i te whakatutuku tēnei, ka taea te whakamahi anō ko te kōkōrero (discount code).

![Limit uses setting with current uses count and max uses field](/img/config/discount-code-limit-uses.png)

**Ngā rawa i te whakatūatanga me te whakamutunga:** I tēnei wā, e hiahia koe ki te āwhina i te coupon kia mārama i te ra raka (start date) me te ra whakamutunga (expiration date).

![Start and expiration date fields for scheduling the discount code](/img/config/discount-code-dates.png)

**Whakawātea mga aua:** Ko te mea, ko te toggle **Select products** e whakatika ana i te mea katoa o waeaau ahau ki a koe. I roto i tēnei wā, e hiahia koe ki te whakatō (toggle on or off) manuahi he pēnei i te aua aua hei whakatō (toggle on or off) he aua aua he taea ai i te aua aua kia whai i te coupon code he taea ana. I ngā aua aua e whakatō ki te wāeaau, ka noho rānei i te wāeaau, ka noho rānei i te wāeaau, ka noho rānei i te wāeaau, ka noho rānei i te wāeaau. I ngā aua aua e whakatō ki te wāeaau, ka noho rānei i te wāeaau, ka noho rānei i te wāeaau, ka noho rānei i te wāeaau.

![Limit products section with per-product toggle switches](/img/config/discount-code-limit-products.png)

I whakatō ai katoa ngā aua aua he tino pai, whakatipu i **Save Discount Code** ki te whakatō i tō coupon me ka whakatou!

![Save Discount Code button at the bottom of the edit page](/img/config/discount-code-save.png)

Ko te coupon e hoki ana i tō list me, i tēnei wā, ka taea e koe ki te whakatō aua he tino pai (edit or delete).

![Discount code row in the list with Edit and Delete hover actions](/img/config/discount-codes-list-hover.png)
