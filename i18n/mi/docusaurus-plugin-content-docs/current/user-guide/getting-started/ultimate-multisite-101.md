# Ultimate Multisite 101

Ultimate Multisite he i te WordPress Multisite plugin, e tino whaiaroa ana ki ahau hei tino whakamahi ai ngā WaaS (Website as a Service) me Websites mō ngā mea wahi. I roto i te whakawhanake mātou ko te pūrere kua tino pai, he mea nui te mōhaka tūnga e tātou kua whakarite ake mō. I roto i te whakatika ki te whakamārama ahau i te waiata ko Ultimate Multisite ka tino whakamohahau ai i tō mahi me ngā mea wahi, he mea nui te mōhaka tūnga e tātou kua whakarite ake mō.

## Te WordPress Multisite

Ko teitei mātou katoa mō te installation o WordPress kua mau ana. Ko koe ka tino whakatō i te i roto i te control panel o tō hosting provider, he tino kaha, ka tino whakatō i te web server me te database hou, ka tino whakatō i ngā core files me te whakaatu i te whakataunga.

He pēnei i teitei mō ngā website WordPress ake i te ao katoa, engari i te hiahia o te agency he tino whakamārama ai te volume (whakaritenga) mō tētahi wakamahi hosting.

Ko i roto i te whakatō i te wahi WordPress paku, ko i roto i te automated control panel ka tino whakahou ake ngā mea e hoki mai ki te 2010 i te whakatū o WordPress 3.0. Ko i tēnei wā, he pēnei i teitei mō te whakamārama ahau i te waiata ko Ultimate Multisite ka tino whakamohahau ai i tō mahi me ngā mea wahi, he mea nui te mōhaka tūnga e tātou kua whakarite ake mō.

I te tino, he pēnei i teitei mō te whakamārama ahau i te waiata ko Ultimate Multisite ka tino whakamohahau ai i tō mahi me ngā mea wahi.

### Te Whānau (The Network)

I roto i WordPress, he whānau multisite ahau te mea e pēnei ana, ko he tino whānui o ngā sub-sites (sub-sites) kua kaha ki te whakamaoritia mai i te dashboard paku. Engari he pēnei te kaha atu ki te whaiaro i te whakamahi o te multisite network e rite ki te whaiaro i te whakaaturanga.

He pēnei ngā whakaaro tino āriki (distinct differences) he mea nui kohekoheko i antara he multisite network me he wahi WordPress e pēnei ana, kia kaha atu tātou whakamārama i tēnei mea.

Mauri i DNS, te whakamahi o _subdirectories_ he te pōtanga mārama. Ae whai ake ana ngā wāhi o te wāhi whene (network sites) ki te whānui o te wāhi pai, ko e hiahia ana koe ki te whakatipu i te whiriwhiri domain name tika mō te domain ana. Ko mō _subdomains_, he pōtanga mōhiohia mō, e hiahia ana i te whiriwhiri CNAME separate mō every network site, apo he wildcard (*) entry nei i ngā DNS records.

He wāhi anō e whakamohua ana ko o SSL me te whakamahi me te pēnitanga o mga sertifika SSL. I te configuration o _subdirectory_, he certificate domain tika ake he tino whai tikanga, ko te wāhi whene ahau ngā wāhi pai o te domain name ana. Ko te certificate mō domain.com e hiahia ana i te provide SSL mō https://domain.com/site1, https://domain.com/site2 me ngā mea katoa.

I te configuration o _subdomain_, he whiriwhiri wildcard SSL certificate he tino whai tikanga. Ko te certificate SSL pēnitanga ahau mō te domain ana me ngā _subdomains_ ana. Ko te certificate SSL wildcard ahau e provide encryption mō https://site1.domain.com, https://site2.domain.com me https://domain.com anara.

Engari he whiriwhiri hou katoa, ko tino pōtanga i te wāhi me te whakamahi ana, ā, e hiahia ana i te configuration ahau anō me te whakaaro mō te tika o te mea.

#### Plugins me Themes

He tino takawa i te WordPress, he tino takawa ana ahau, ko mō te mea o te mea whai whenua (customer). I te installation o WordPress e tōtōtō, ko ahau administrator o te wāhi, ko he plugin pēru he tino whakamahinga apo he pēru i te whakatipu i te installation ana. Engari, he admin o te wāhi e installing he plugin pēru he tino whakamahinga i te installation multisite e tino whai whenua mō every site ana i te network.

Mā te mea he mea nui ki te whaiaro i tēnei: i roto i WordPress Multisite, ka whakatika e te system noa ko te mea e tino nui ana ia (site administrator) noa i te kaha o te site administrators ki te whakauru plugin me theme. I roto i te wā, ka whakawhiti i tēnei kaha ki te tika hou e pakihi (network administrator) he tino whaiako 'super admin'. Ko tēnei tika nui ahau e taea ai ia kia arahi ko mō te administrators o ngā site o te network kia whakaae ana me te whakatū menu plugin i te dashboard o rā, ā, ko te mea e hoki ana ki te whakamahi aua manawaratanga he ka puta mai i te _activating_ (whakawhanaungatanga) me te _deactivating_ (whakamutunga) o ngā plugin.

I tēnei wā, ko te pakihi administrator ahau e tino whaiako ana ki te whakauru plugin me theme ki te network, ā, ka whakatika i ngā manawaratanga kia taea ai ngā administrators o ngā site o te network te whakamahi aua plugin me theme. Ka nui te mea he mea nui: ko ngā site administrators hei pakihi e kore ana i te mea he tino nui ki te whakauru plugin me theme, ka taea hoki te whaiaro i ngā plugin me theme nga ana i te site o rā.

#### Users and Administrators (Ngā Hua me ngā Pakihi)

I WordPress Multisite, ko ngā site katoa o te network e whare i te database tōna, ā, he tino whare i ngā users, roles, me ngā capabilities. Ko te mea mō te whakaaro nui ki tēnei, ko ko ngā users katoa he ngā mema o te network ahau, ka tino pakihi i te site-pakihi tika.

I runga i tēnei whakaaro, he mea nui ana ona kia kore e whaiaro i te whakamahi user (user) ki te whakauru i ngā users, ā, i tēnei wā, ka whakatika e WordPress Multisite ko te kaha o te site administrators ki te whakauru i ngā users, ā, ka whakatika i tēnei tika ki te pakihi administrator o te network. I tēnei wā, he taea ana koe mō te pakihi administrator o te network kia whakatika i ngā manawaratanga e puta mai ki te site administrator kia taea ai ia te whakauru i ngā user accounts mo te site o rā.

Kia whakatika anō tēnei mea, he tino nui ana ko me ngā user accounts he hoki ana ki te site ahau, engari e tika ana ia ki te network ahau, ā, i tēnei wā, he mea nui ona kia kore i roto i te network. He pēnei ngā wā i taea te whakatika i ngā usernames i te whakamahi i tēnei wāhi ahau i te whakamaoritanga ahau.

Māori, he mea e noho ana i te ao o te whakamahi, engari he mea pēnei tēnei te whakatō i te whakamahi wakamāhi me te whakamaoritanga (user registration and authentication) katoa i te wāhi, he mea tino whakahaupūake ki ngā kaiwhakaahua e whai i WordPress installation-sere.

#### Media

Iroha i te wāhi e whare whene (network sites) e whare pēnei ana i database tahi i WordPress Multisite, ka whakatō ngā rāhui (paths) ā-filesystem e whero mō ngā file media.

Ko te wāhi standard o WordPress (wp-content/uploads) ka noho; engari ka whakawātea i tō rāhui i te whakaahua i te ID tahi o te whene. I roto i te mea, ka purine ngā file media mō te whene pēnei i wp-contents/uploads/site/[id].

#### Permalinks

I whakatakita mātou i te wāhi e tino kaha o _subdomain_ i te whakamahi i _subdirectory_, me te mea he pēnei tēnei: ngā rāhui (paths).

I te configuration _subdirectory_, ka taea te whai ake, ko te wāhi nui (te site tūiti i whakawhanakea i te wāhi e whakatō te whene) me ngā subsites o te whene ka whai i te rāhui tēnei mai i te wāhi o te domain. He mea e taea ana ki te whakahaupūake nui ake.

Mō ngā posts, ka puta he rāhui /blog/ mō te site nui kia whakatika i te whakahaupūake me ngā sites o te whene. Ko te mea e tēnei, ko ngā permalink (permalinks) pēnei i ‘Post name’ ka puta i te wāhi domain.name/blog/post-name/.

#### Static Pages

I roto i te whakaritenga _subdirectory_ (sub-directory), he mea e taea te whakahau i ngā ingoa, ā, he tino nui tēnei mō ngā pages static (static pages) ahau te wāhi nui o te site ana me ngā site network. Ka whai ake ngā site network ngā samea (path) puta mai i te site ana.

Kia whakaheke i tēnei mea, e hihia WordPress he mea hei whakawhiti i ngā ingoa site-tahi (site names) ahau i te mea he pai ona te whakatika i ngā ingoa o te site tūpuna. I roto i te awhi, ka taea e te administrator o te network te whakireke i ngā root paths o ngā pages o te site ana.

I te whakaritenga _subdomain_ (sub-domain), he mea e whakaheke ai te mea e taea te whakahau i ngā ingoa site ahau, ko te _subdomain_ ahau he tino pai ana ki te site network me ā-āhi ana i te site ana. He tino whai ake i te site ana.

### Domain me SSL

He pātaki atu ki te whakamārama mō te installation o WordPress Multisite, he mea tino whakamārama ana i te onua o WordPress.com – ko he tino whakatutuki ahau i te multisite acai me te whakaatu i ngā kaha nui o ia hei whakawātea me te whakatū ki te whaiaro tō mahi.

I te wā, i te internet modern, he tino whakamārama ana i te whakamahi o SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) āwhina ahau, ā, ko ngā network administrators o WordPress Multisite e hiahia atu ki ngā whakataukī mō tēnei.

I te configuration o _subdomain_, he tino whakamārama ana i te site katoa i runga i te root domain name. Ko i tēnei wā, he whakawātea ahau i te site pēne ‘site1’ hei ‘site1.domain.com’. Hei whakamahi i wildcard SSL certificate, he tino whakatutuki ahau i tēnei whakataukī me te whaiaro i ngā kaha o encryption SSL mō te network.

Inaheke, he kitea i te WordPress Multisite he mea nui, he tino whaiho ana ki te whakamahi o domain mapping function, he tino whaiaro ana i ngā site network ki ngā domain custom (custom domain names) ka taea te whakawhiti atu ki ngā domain names ka kaha ake i te root domain o te network.

Mō ngā administrator o te network, he pēke whakahou (additional layer of complexity) e tino nui ana i te whakamahi o te configuration o domain names, ā, mō te whakawhanainga me te whaiaro o SSL certificates.

I tēnei mea, ahakoa he whānau WordPress Multisite ka puta mai he onua mō te whakamahi o [www.anotherdomain.com](http://www.anotherdomain.com) ki te mapping ki 'site1', ko te administrator o te network e pēke ana i te whakatau o ngā DNS entries me te whakamahi o SSL certificates ki te ao.

## Ultimate Multisite

Me tino whakatika ahau i te whakamārama, he tino whakahou (ultimate arsenal) he hoki mai mō te whaiaro o Websites as a Service (WaaS). Kei te whakaaro i Wix.com, Squarespace, WordPress.com me te whakataki ki te mua o te tino whonua o koe.

I te pito o te whakamahi Ultimate Multisite, he fa'aoritanga ana i te WordPress Multisite, engari ka tino whakahou ana i te kaha, he whakatau ana i ngā pēke e tino nui ana ki te administrator o te network me te whaiaro ana i ngā kaha ka taea te whakamahi i ngā mea katoa.

I ngā seks mō muri, ka kite ahau i ngā use cases (ngā wāwha whaiutu) whakahou me ngā whakaaro e tino nui ki te whakamohoho i oi ngā use cases ei whaiutu.

### Use Cases

#### Case 1: He Agency

E tōtōtō, he tino mōhiotanga o te agency kei te whakatō i ngā website me ngā mea ka puta ake i te hosting (hosting) me te marketing ka whakahou ana i ngā mea e tika ana.

Māori agencies, te Ultimate Multisite e whai ake ana i te whakataunga mō te whakahaupū (value proposition) tino pai i te kaha o ia ki te hoakoa me te whakamaumahara i ngā waka wāhi (websites) maha i te wāhi pūriri (single platform). He tino pai hoki mō ngā agency e whakatō i ngā mātakitaki (designs) mō theme-tanga taketake, ahau e roto i GeneratePress, Astra, OceanWP, ahau e taea ai ki te whakamahi ngā kaha o Ultimate Multisite kia whakamaumahara atu i ngā theme-tanga hāpai ana mō every new site.

Eke ana hoki me te whānui o ngā whakataunga (deals) mō ngā plugin maha mō agency pricing, te whakamahi o Ultimate Multisite e hiahia ana ki te whakawhiti i ngā whakataunga eke ana hei hoakoa platforma whakahou (common platform) noa me ko ahau e taea ai i ngā plugin tika atu, te whaiaro atu, me te whakamahi.

He tino pai hoki te whaiaro i te configuration, ā, he tino pai hoki te whakamahi o Ultimate Multisite ki te whakahaere i te domain mapping me SSL certificates mō ngā whaiwhakaritanga (integrations) noa i whānui mō mga penyatoru hoakoa hosting ahau e whai ake ana ahau i Cloudflare me cPanel.

Ko i roto i te whakamahi i tēnei penyatoru, ahau e taea ai ngā mahi mō te management o domain me SSL certificates he tino paha (trivial).

Ngā agency e hiahia ki te whakatutaku tino pai i te whakawhitanga o ngā waka wāhi e āwhina ana i te āhua e tino pai ake, ahau e taea ai atu te whakamaumahara i ngā waka wāhi me ngā customer me ngā plans mō ngā agency e whakatutaku (streamlined interface) o Ultimate Multisite.

#### Case 2: Pūmanawa Whānui (Niche Provider)

He kupu purini he ahua, "taku te pēke me aku te whakamahi." Mō ngā mātanga katoa, he te mea e whai ake ana ko te whakatō i āhuatanga tahi ki te whakaaro pūrākau tahi.

Mā te mea koe he tino pai i te golf e whakatō waka ahau ki ngā club, he taea ai koe he tino pai i te esports gamer e whakatō waka ahau ki ngā clan. Heheke ana he heke ahau i te hoki (booking) service ki ngā restaurant?

Mō ngā mea nui katoa, e hiahia koe ki te hoatu i ngā mahi based ki te whare pūtahe (framework) me te platforma tahi. He taea ai ko he tino pai i te whakatō plugin mō te whakamahi i te mahi e tika ana, he taea hoki te mea e whai ake ana ko ngā best practices o te whānau e hiahia ki te āhuatanga tahi mō te whero (design).

He pūmanawa hou o Ultimate Multisite he whakamahi i template sites. He template site he heke ahau i te theme, he whakatō i ngā plugins e tika ana me te whakatō i ngā post/page rātou, me te whakamārama i ngā mea tino nui. I roto i te wā ka tino pai akorero (copy) i te mōhio me ngā setting o te template ki te site hou e whakatō ahau based ki te template.

Mō he provider o niche sites me services, he pūmanawa tino kaha ana i te mea e taea ai koe te tino whakamahi i te site e hiahia ahau i te wā, me ngā custom plugins me te design. He hiahia i te whaiaro i te mea tino piko (minimal input) ki te whakatō i te service.

Kua ngā tino āhua e tika ana, he taea te whakamahi te _subdirectory_ (subdirectory) ahau, heitahe (subdomain). I tēnei wā, ko te whiriwhiri i te arki-te-kaha (architecture choices) e hoki mai ki te whiriwhiri certificate SSL simple mō _subdirectories_ ahau, hetahe wildcard SSL certificate mō _subdomains_.

#### Case 3: Hosting Web WordPress

He tino pai ngā puta noa o te rātou i whakauru (host) mga waka WordPress, engari hānei he tino paha ana te whakamahi i te wero ki te whai whakaaro ki te hoatu wāhi web ki te mea e pēnei i heke WordPress pre-installed. Ko ko te mea i te tino āhua ana, he tino pātai me whakaaro ake kia whai i te mahi nui mō te hoatu whakamana.
