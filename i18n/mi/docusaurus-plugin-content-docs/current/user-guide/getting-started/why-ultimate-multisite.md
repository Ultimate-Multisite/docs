### Case 2: Pūtaiao Whānui (Niche Provider) {#case-1-an-agency}

He kupu purini ahau i te whaiaro, "Whakawhanaungatanga i tētahi mea me te whakamahi i te pai." Mo ngā mātanga katoa, he tino āhua ana ko te whero i te mea e whakatō atu i te mea tino pēke.

Ko i roto i te whakawhanake wāhi nui o ngā site (niche network of sites) koe, he tino tika ana ko e hiahia koe ki te whakarato i ngā mahi based ahau i te whare pūtahe me te platforma mā te katoa. He tēnei te wā nō te template sites kia mōhio atu!

He pito whakamua o Ultimate Multisite, he template site he heke ana ko te theme e whaiiroa koe, ngā plugins tino pai e whaiiroa koe, me ngā posts ahau i te whakatū. I roto i te wā ka tino whakahoki ake i te mōhaka me ngā setting o te template ki te site hou kua oti.

Mo te pūtaiao whānui o ngā site me ngā mahi, he tino manawa ana tēnei i te mea e taea ai koe te whakawhanaunga i te site kua hono atu ki te pai anō me ngā plugins me te design mo te koutou. Ko te koutou he hiahia ki te whaiaro i ngā mea tino pēke kia whakatā i te mahi.

### Case 3: WordPress Web Hosting {#case-2-niche-provider}

He tino mōhaka ahau i te wāhanga o te hosting site WordPress, engari haere mai, he tino takawa tika ana ko e whakarato ai wāhi web ki te koutou me te version o WordPress kua oti koe. He tino pēke ahau i te mea he tino whakahoki ake ngā pātai me ngā whakaaro katoa kia whaiaro i te mahi tino pai.

He tino manawa ana tēnei i te wāhanga o te hosting site WordPress, engari haere mai, he tino takawa tika ana ko e whakarato ai wāhi web ki te koutou me te version o WordPress kua oti koe. He tino pēke ahau i te mea he tino whakahoki ake ngā pātai me ngā whakaaro katoa kia whaiaro i te mahi tino pai. Ko Ultimate Multisite he tino manawa ana i tēnei wāhanga e ngā whakamahi mō te hosting site WordPress. He hoki i te solution kua oti ki te whakarato i ngā core mechanisms mo te subscription services, payment collection, checkout forms, discount vouchers, me ngā customer communications.

Te mahi nui e pēnei i te whaiaro ki te whakauru, te whakarite, me te whakamahi i WordPress Multisite, he tino ātaahua ana ahau ki Ultimate Multisite. Ko te mea e hoki ana ki te administrator o te wāhi (network administrator) he whakaaro atu ki ngā mea e hāngai ana ki te mahi tō whānau ko, engari he tino pai ai i te whakamahi i āhua mahi pēnei i te rārangi aua (product tiers), te whakaingoa (pricing), me ngā ofa mahi.

Mō ngā developer-era e te pēhea te whakawhanaunga ki Ultimate Multisite, ko te whānau ahua ana he API RESTful mō te whaiaro, me Webhooks mō te hua o ngā pūrongo (event notification).

E hoa, i roto i te whakamahi i plugin a hinengaro nui me license, ka tino pai te hoki e Ultimate Multisite. Ko he whakaaro mā te whero ahau ki te whakahua ana i he whakataunga mō Wix, Squarespace, [WordPress.com](https://WordPress.com), me ngā mea katoa.
