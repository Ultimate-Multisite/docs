# Konsepurua

Mō te whakamārama mō he wāhi hou i WordPress Multisite me tētahi kore i pēhea e pēhea ana te whakamahinga i Ultimate Multisite, he tino pai. He mea nui te akorua ki te mārama i te platforma me te pēhea ia mahi katoa.

I tēnei article, ka tino whakamārama tātou i ngā koncepurua nui o WordPress. Kei te nekehanga mō ngā wāhi hou, he nekehanga mō ngā whānau, me nekehanga mō ngā katoa.

## WordPress Multisite {#wordpress-multisite}

He **Multisite** o WordPress he mea e pēnei i te whakamahi i tētahi installation o WordPress ki te whakauru me te whakamainga whānau o wāhi website (websites) mō tētahi dashboard o WordPress. Kei te taea te whakamainga katoa, ā, he mea nui te whai whakaaro ki te whakapapa o ngā wāhi, ngā āhuatanga, ngā theme, me ngā roa o ngā user roles. He tino pai te whakamainga i ngā roa he nui ake, he nui ake hoki.

## Whānau (Network) {#network}

I te reo o WordPress, he whānau multisite he heke i ngā **subsites** (wāhi website whānui) e taea ai te whakamainga ana i te wāhi tahi mōna. Engari he pēnei te whakamahinga i roto i ngā whipara hosting, engari he pēnei te mea, he tino pai te whakaaro ki ngā directive (whakamahi) hou i te file **wp-config.php** kia mō WordPress kia whai whakaaro ana i tēnei mode tika.

He pēnei te whakamārama i ngā whakamārama nui i roto i te whānau multisite me heke i te installation o WordPress anake, he tino pai te whakamainga i tēnei mea.

## Database (Pūtaiao Data) {#database}

He **database** he tautoko, he hara, he pūtahe o ngā data. I te reo o te computing, he database he software e whakamahia ki te whakatakino me te whakamainga i ngā data. He whakaaro ahau i te cabinet o file (file cabinet) he koheke koe i ngā data i ngā whero (sections) kua oti.

He Multisite o WordPress he whai anake database he heke i subsite, me kete table (tables) mōna, me te blog id i te prefix, ko i roto i te wā koe e installa he network installation, ka hei pēnei ngā tables:

_wp_1_options_ \- tēhara o ngā table options mō te subsite whiti i tētahi

_wp_2_options_ \- tēhara o ngā table options mō te subsite whiti i tētahi

## Pāho Whakaahinga (Hosting provider) {#hosting-provider}

He pānui whakaahinga he mea he whānau e hoki i te whakamahi ai i ngā wāhi o te Wāhi Whānui (World Wide Web) mō ngā kōrero o ngā whakahaere me ngā tangata. Ngā mahi āūnga (services) e wherootanga e ngā pānui whakaahinga web e hoki i te whakamahi ai, engari e taea te whakatika ana, he whai ake ana: te whai whakaaro ki te tapu o te waka web (website design), te wāhi hoako (storage space) mo te host, me te konektanga ki te Internet.

Subsite he tautoko (subsite) he tūmāhi e whakatō i roto i te whene whakawhiti (Multisite network). He taea ai ia he **subdomain** he **subdirectory** i runga i te mea kua whakatikaa te whakamahi o koe o WordPress Multisite.

## Super Admin {#subdomain}

He Super Admin o WordPress he tūmāhi wāhi e huto katoa ngā subsites i roto i whene whakawhiti (Multisite network). Mo ngā mātua o Multisite, he te **au tino whaiora** (highest level of access) he taea ai koe ki te whakarato ki te installation WordPress o koe.

## Plugin {#subdirectory}

I te pēpi, he plugin he tūmāhi kōrero (set of code) e puta mai i te mahi whero (extra functionality) ki wāhi i ngā site o koe. He taea ai ia te hihiko pēnei i te whakawhiti i te logo o te login, he taea ai ia te hihiko pēnei i te whaiaro e-commerce. _Woocommerce and Contact Form_ he ngā whakamāhi o plugin.

I WordPress Multisite, he taea ana i ngā plugin te whakauru anara (installed) anara admin dashboard e Super Admin. He taea ai i ngā Subsite Admins te whakatika (activate) me te whakatū (deactivate) ngā plugin i roto i ngā subsites o rā.

## Themes {#subsite}

He theme o WordPress he tūmāhi o ngā anara (_graphics, style sheets, and code_) e whakatau te mōhio o te site katoa. He puta mai i ngā whakamahi pēnei i te whakawhiti i te font, te hā o te page, ngā rangi, me ngā mea katoa o te front-end styling.

Kore pēnei ki plugin, he taea ana i ngā theme i WordPress Multisite te whakauru e Super Admin anara he taea ai te whakatika (activate) i te level o subsite e ngā Subsite admins.

## Site Template {#super-admin}

He **Site Template** he tūmāhi site pūrere (boilerplate site) he taea ai te whakamahi he pēpi i te whakawhiti i ngā site hou i roto i whene o koe.

Ko te mea he, he taea ai koe te whakatō he base site, te whakarite ngā plugin haere, te whakarite theme e active, me te whakatika i ia anara ki te mea kua tino whaiora (customized) mo te mea. Kei te mea, i roto i te wā kua e whakatō te customer pēpi hou, he taea ai ana ia te pūtake o site o koe he hoki (copy) o tō base site me ngā whakamahi katoa me ngā anara ko ka puta mai.

## Domain Mapping {#plugin}

**Domain mapping** me WordPress he mea he wahi i te whai ake ana i ngā aua ki te host tika, mō te ara o te waka website. I WordPress Multisite, ka whakamaoritia ngā subsite (sites pēri) mā te whakamahi i subdirectory pei te 'joesbikeshop' he te subdomain. He aha te mahi a domain mapping? E whai ana ki te whaiaro ai i ngā user o ngā subsite ki te whakamahi i te top-level domain he pēhi i te wāhi, ahau [**joesbikeshop.com**](http://joesbikeshop.com) kia kitea te ara o te waka i te mea he tino whaiaro.

## SSL {#themes}

SSL he whakamārama mō **Secure Sockets Layer**. He certificate digital e whakatau ana i te whakahui o te identity o te website me te tino whakatika konektanga (connection) i te mea he whakawhakarite (encrypted). I te ao hoki, he whakamahinga mā te standard technology mō te whakamahi ai te konektanga internet i te mea ātaahua me te whakamarama ai ngā aua sensitive data e pēnei i te whaiaro e pēnei i te whakawhiti i te wāhi o te two system, kia whakatika i ngā kaimahi ki te whakapāke (reading) me te whakarōpūke (modifying) i ngā aua information, ā, he whakaheke ai i ngā kaimahi e pēnei i ngā personal details. He tino nui te SSL i ngā modern browser, mōtake ana i te whakatika i te website me te whakamahi i a ia.

## Media {#site-template}

Media ahau ngā images (pēpi), audio (aute), video, me ngā aua files e pēnei i te website.

I ngā network sites, ka whare i te database he whetū-tahi i WordPress Multisite, ā, ka whai i ngā path separate ki te filesystem mō ngā media files.

He kaha te location o WordPress (wp-content/uploads); engari e whakawhiti ai i tō path kia whakaatu i te unique ID o te network site. I te mea he te mea he tino pēri, ka kitea ngā media files mō te network site hei wp-contents/uploads/site/[id].

## Permalinks {#domain-mapping}

Permalinks ahau ngā URL permanant (whaiaro) o tō individual blog post pei i tō page i runga i tō site. E whakawhiti ai i ngā permalink hoki hei **pretty links**. I te default, ka whai i ngā URL o WordPress he query string format e pēnei i:

[**http://www.example.com/registration**](http://www.example.com/registration)

## Ultimate Multisite {#ssl}

Ultimate Multisite he kitea i WordPress plugin, tino whakamua mo install WordPress Multisite, e whakawhiti i tō install WordPress ki te whānau premium o ngā wāhi – ehia [WordPress.com] – e hiahia ana ki te whaiaro nga klien hei whakawhitanga wāhana via fees mō tau, mā tau, he rā, (ae e taea ai koe te whakawhitanga Free plans).

## Checkout Form {#media}

Checkout Form he whakamārama pūrere rāke i tahi he pūrere rāke mō te whaiaro, membership, me user accounts i roto i te Ultimate Multisite registration. E hihia ana i ngā fields ā-ahau me ngā forms o te pai e taea ai i te wāhi e whakamahia e te user i tākaro i te whakawhitanga.

## Webhook {#permalinks}

Webhook (ae e whai mai ana ki te callback web he HTTP push API) he wahi he pēnei i te app hei whakamōhiho ai ngā application hou me ngā mōhiamo i te real-time. He webhook e whai data ki ngā application hou i te wā e hoki, e hiahia ana koe ki te data i runga i te wā.

**Ultimate Multisite webhooks** e puta mai i ngā whakataunga nui, e hiahia ana i ngā network admins hei whakauru i ngā mea katoa e tino pai me tino whakamahi, he tino pai ahau i te whakamahi i ngā services pēnei i _Zapier_ me _IFTTT_.

## Events {#ultimate-multisite}

Event he mahi e puta i runga i te whakataunga o te user pei o te click o te mouse. Ko Ultimate Multisite e whakatakino i te rekamati mō onua katoa i roto i tō network. E whakatakino ana i ngā mahi ā-ahau e puta i roto i tō multisite, ehia ngā whakawhiti i ngā plan.
