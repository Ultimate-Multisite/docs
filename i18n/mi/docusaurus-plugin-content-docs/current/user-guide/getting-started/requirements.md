# Mga Tūnga o Ultimate Multisite {#ultimate-multisite-requirements}

Kua te mea whakamua e koe i te whaina Ultimate Multisite he mea nui, ko te mea kua e whakaaro i te tūnga (requirements) ki te whakauru i tō plugin. Ahia, he tino āhua pēnei te tūnga mō te whakamahi Ultimate Multisite: he **domain name**, he **hosting provider** māhinaki, me heke WordPress **Multisite**. He mea katoa e tika ana!

## Domain Name {#domain-name}

Kia kaha ki te korero atu i te domain name.

I roto i te whai wāhi i te domain name, kia whakaaro i tō niche (te mea kua e tino whakamahi) me te mea kua e whakawhiti i runga i Ultimate Multisite: He pēhea koe e te whakawhitanga? He whakawhiti agency? Whakaue i ngā website ki te pēke? Āwhi he WordPress web hosting? He mea katoa e tika ana.

Ko tō plugin e whai whakaaro ki ngā wāhi o te subscription, te whakatapu i ngā raupa (payment collection), ngā checkout forms, ngā voucher discount me ngā korero ki te mea whai mua (customer communications). He tino mahi nui ki te whakauru, te whakarite me te whakamahi ana i WordPress Multisite, he e whai tika ana ki Ultimate Multisite.

He niche tuataua kua e whai i tō plugin he kaha ki te whakamaoritia ko e whakatere ahau ngā website ko mea he tino whakatau mai i tō plan o te mahi! Kia tiaki i te domain name kua e huna i tō plan o te mahi.

## Hosting Provider {#hosting-provider}

I te wā kua e pēnei te domain name kua e whakaatu i tō niche me tō mahi, ka taea e koe te whai i te company hosting (web hosting) māhinaki ki te host i tō network me Ultimate Multisite. He tino optiona mō te pako. He ngā hosting provider mō te pako, he mea e tika ana ahau i te mea kua e whakaaro mai i tō mahi.

Ka pai, e hoa! Te Ultimate Multisite e pēnei ana ki te mahi i ngā whipiti hosting katoa. Te whakaaro nui kei te nekehanga ana i roto i te whaiaro mapping o domain (domain mapping). Engari he tino whakamārama: i roto i ngā whipiti hosting taketake, he tino whaiaro atu te whaiaro mapping o domain. Mo ngā whipiti hou, he mea nui ki te whakaputa i te custom domains manu manu (manually map) mo ngā user. He tino pai tēnei te whaiaro ana i he whipiti hosting e tino whaiaro ana i AutoSSL. I roto i _Closte_, _Runcloud_ me _cPanel_, he tino whaiaro atu ai.

I roto i te whakatipu o te server specifications, he kore formula tino tika. He rite ki te mea e taea ana koe, engari e rite ana ki te feti o ngā āhuatanga me ngā mahi e ka whero i te wāhi mo ngā user. He tino pai te whaiaro hosting (shared hosting) mō te whakatupu i roto i te mihi, engari i te wā e piki te network mo te user, he tino kite ana kei te hiahia koe ki te VPS (dedicated VPS).

## WordPress Multisite {#wordpress-multisite}

I te wā, he tino pai tēnei te korero i te reo mō te installation o WordPress Multisite.

He tino whaiaro atu te Multisite o WordPress, e pēnei ana ana ki te tino whakamahi: e taea ai koe te hako i te installation paku, he network o ngā sites. He āhuatanga tino whaiaro atu, engari he kore ana i te whakatika (not active) mo te whakatō (default). He tino nui te whakaputa i tēnei āhuatanga i roto i te WordPress installatio me te control panel o hosting web.

He **article tino whaiaro** kei a tātou e whaiaro ana i ngā apa manu manu (step by step) he mea kua whakarite mo te whakamahi i te āhuatanga Multisite mo te installation WordPress mo koe.

I raro i te Multisite mo WordPress, he wā ki te whakatō te Ultimate Multisite. Mo te whakatō i te Ultimate Multisite i te tika, he tino nui kei a tātou:

  * WordPress: v5.3+ (Te mea tino whaiaro atu: Te version stable ake)

  * PHP: 7.4.x (He whakamua ki > 8.0 e pēnei ana)

  * MySQL: v5+ (Te mea tino whaiaro atu: 5.6, i case he kaha koe ki te 8.0 mo ngā whipiti hosting o koe)

He mea nui ki te whakamārama i ngā āhuatanga o te software e pēnei ana:

He tino āhuatanga software e tika ana mō Ultimate Multisite.

Kia tino whakaaro i te mea he taea te mahi ai te Ultimate Multisite me PHP 8.0, engari e tino whai te whakamārama mātou ki te whakamahi PHP 7.4.x.

Kia tino whai te whakamārama mātou ki te whakamahi i a ia i ngā **main domains** (domain ana), ā, he mea nui ona i roto i te subdomains (subdomains) me subdirectories (subdirectories). He taea te mahi i ngā subdomain/subdirectory, engari e taea te tino whai te whakaatu i ngā pētrā (errors) i tēnei wā.

Mō whakamārama mō te whakatau ā-tahi ki te Whakaritenga Ultimate Multisite, **whakawātea i tēnei link**.
