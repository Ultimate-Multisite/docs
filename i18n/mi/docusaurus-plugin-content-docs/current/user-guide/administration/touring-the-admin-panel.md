# Whakawhanaungatanga ki te Panel Admin

He aimahi nui o Ultimate Multisite he tika ana me tino whakamārama, engari mō tīnaki he mahi tino kaha, e taea ai ia te tino whaiaro. Kia kaha atu i ngā manawa admin kia tū mai i roto i te wā.

## Dashboard (Pango)

Te **Dashboard** o Ultimate Multisite e whakaatu i pūrongo me analytics mō te mea katoa, ahau e hoki ana ki te pētea, mahi o ngā site, whiriwhiri o ngā mema, nga whakatūpato o ngā manawa, me ngā tohu wāhi.

![Network Dashboard full page](/img/admin/network-dashboard-full-page.png)

## Checkout Forms (Ngā Pānga Whakawhanaungatanga)

**Checkout Forms** e hiahia atu ki ahau he tino whiriwhiri mō te whakamahi i ngā manawa whakaingo (registration pages) kua whakarite. E taea ai koe te whakaiti i ngā fields, te whiriwhiri i class me script mō te tika, me te whakatū i rāhui based ki te wāhi o te visitor ahau e hoki ana nei.

![Checkout Forms list](/img/admin/checkout-forms-list.png)

## Products (Ngā Puku)

Te whero **Products** he te wā kua e whakatō koe i ngā plānge, package me ngā mahi mō tō whenua. Whakawāhi i ngā hei, ngā wā o te pētea, me ngā rawhatanga & quota mō te product.

![Products list](/img/admin/products-list.png)

## Memberships (Ngā Mema)

Te whero **Memberships** e whakaatu ai i ngā subscription katoa i tō whenua. Titiro me whakarere i ngā korero o te subscription ahau, ahau e hoki ana ki ngā plānge, products, ngā wā o te pētea, me ngā timestamps.

![Memberships list](/img/admin/memberships-list.png)

## Payments (Ngā Whakawhanaungatanga Pētea)

Whakarongo: Te puta mai i te **Payments** page he whakaahuatanga wakamārama o te iti ake o te whakamahi pai o te whakataunga (payment history) mo te whenua katoa o koe, me whakamohahau mō ngā whakawhiti (transactions) mō ngā aua, me te whakamōhaka i ngā aua aua me ngā aua.

![Payments list](/img/admin/payments-list.png)

## Customers

Ko te **Customers** page e whakaatu ai katoa ngā onua (members) e pēnitanga ana i tō whenua, me whakamohahau mō ngā aua aua mā te whakamahi ingoa, email address, te wā o te whakamoti (last login), me te link "switch to" mō te whakamāhi ki te site o te whānau (subsite) o rā.

![Customers list](/img/admin/customers-list.png)

## Sites

Ko te **Sites** page e whakaatu ai katoa ngā subsites i tō whenua, me whakamohahau mō ngā template o te site me ngā sites e tino whai ake ana i ngā onua (customer-owned sites). Kia kaha ki te whakahaere i ngā domain mapu, ngā pango (limits & quotas), ngā plugin me ngā theme, me ngā thumbnail o te site.

![Sites list](/img/admin/sites-list.png)

## Domains

Ko te **Domains** page e tino whai ake ana ki ngā domain whakahou (custom domains) e mapu ana ki ngā subsites. Mā te tika o koe he super admin, ka taea e koe te whānui i te whakamahi aua ingoa domain whakahou me te whakaahua i ngā DNS records me ngā logs.

![Domains list](/img/admin/domains-list.png)

## Discount Codes

Ko te **Discount Codes** e tino pai ana ki a koe mō te whakamaoritanga (coupon codes) hei whakamōhaka aua discount mō ngā end-users o koe. Whakawhiti i te wā o te whakataunga me te whai ake ana i ngā onua (users) me ngā hui (groups) tika.

![Discount Codes list](/img/admin/discount-codes-list.png)

## Broadcasts

Ko te **Broadcasts** he taonga mō te whakamohaka o ngā whakatau (announcements) me te whakawhanaungatanga pērā i tinana (private messages) ki ngā users o tō subsites. Whakawhiti i ngā hui user tika based on te plan me te product e rite ana ki ahau.

![Broadcasts list](/img/admin/broadcasts-list.png)

## Settings

Ko te **Settings** page he wāhi i tēnei i whakatika i Ultimate Multisite — ngā whakaritenga o te pēnitanga (registration settings), ngā payments, API me ngā webhooks, ngā mapu o ngā domain, me ngā whakawhanake (integrations) e rite ana.

Kete lista o whakaritenga AI connector, i roto i te whakatutuki ana i mga provider OAuth e tino tika: Anthropic Max, OpenAI ChatGPT/Codex, me Google AI Pro. Ka taea e card provider ko e whakamahi atu hei koneki i aka, ki te whakaνi aka e pēnei, ki te whakatū aka noa (manual OAuth fallback) ko te mea e whai ake ana i te browser redirect e tino whakawātea te sandboxed environment. Ka whakatino ngā option setup o Cursor Pro mai i te admin panel.

Ka whakatino ngā aka ChatGPT/Codex ki te whakamahi i mga taonga (tool use) e whakahou ana i te mahi, ko te mea e pēnei i te whaiaro, ko ka taea ai i ngā workflow o te admin e whakamahi i te Codex tool behavior i ara i te koneki i te aka OpenAI.

## Events

Ka whakatutuki ngā **Events** page i te rekording katoa o ngā events me logs i roto i tō network. Ka whakatutuki ana i ngā mahi pēnei i ngā whakawirera plan, ngā whakamāhi (signups), me ngā whakawhanake hou — tino mō te whakaora i tō multisite.

![Events list](/img/admin/events-list.png)

## Webhooks

Ka whaiaro ai ngā **Webhooks** ki te whero data ki te ao whakamahi. Tino mō te whakawhanake data mai i Ultimate Multisite ki mga platform pēnei i Zapier.

![Webhooks list](/img/admin/webhooks-list.png)
