# Kia Whakawāhi Kōrero (Managing Customers)

Ko te puta mai o te puta mai o te **Customers** he whakaatu i te litanga o ngā anggota e whai whakamaoritanga ana i tō whenua. Ko tēnei puta mai e whakaatu i whakamohana mārama o te mea, email address, wā o te whakamoti i runga i te wāhi (last login), me te link **whakaahua ki** (switch to) e kitea i ahau he tino administrator o te subsite o te mea.

![Customers list page](/img/admin/customers-list.png)

Whakawātea **Manage** i runga i te card o te ao whānui (customer's card) ki te kite i whakamohana mārama i te mea e hoki ana i tō korero.

I te puta mai o te whakamohana mārama o te ao whānui, ko te **At a Glance** e whakaatu i te ID o te ao whānui, wā o te whakamoti i runga i te wāhi (last login time), me te whakatupu whānui e whai ake ana o tēnei ao whānui.

Ko te **Customer Options** e tino pai ana ki a koe ki te whakatō i tēnei ao whānui he **VIP** (he taganga kia paha ia mō te kite). Ko te tab **Billing Info** e whakaatu i whakamohana mārama o te whakatupu, ahau, wāhi, me ngā wāhi.

Ko te **Fields** e whakaatu i te username o te ao whānui, email, me e tino pai ana ki a koe ki te whakawhiti te status o te **Email Verification**.

I te pō (below) o te Customer Options block, ka mau koe i aua whā bloku e pai ai:

- **Memberships** — Ngā subscription hou e hoki ana i tēnei ao whānui
- **Payments** — Te iti o te whakamahinga o te ao whānui (customer's payment history)
- **Sites** — Ngā wāhi e tino whai ake ai tēnei ao whānui
- **Events** — Ngā pūrākau e pai ana ki tēnei ao whānui (email verification, billing changes, etc.)

Whakawātea te icon o te arrow (→) i runga i te mea nui ki te hoki ki te puta mai o tō korero. Ko te tino whakamahi, ko te whakapā i te arrow i runga i te wāhi e whai koe ki te puta mai o tēnei puta mai o tō korero.

Ko te pēpi (the same) e pai ana ki ngā memberships me ngā payments — ko ata katoa e whai ana ki ngā puta mai o tō korero e whai koe ki te whakawhiti te status o te membership, te ao whānui e whai ake, ngā aua mea, ngā whakaritenga o te gateway o te whakamahi, me te whakaoti i ngā refund.

I te whero (on the right side) o te puta mai o tō korero, ka mau koe i:

- **Mata whakaroa me whānui (Last Login & IPs)** — Wākea mā te whai i te wā pēnei, whāea ip address, me koutou i te wāhi.
- **Wāpuna (Timestamps)** — I te wā o te whakawhanaungatanga o te aka o te mea, me te wā o te whakamutunga ki te whakarere.
- **Whakamutai (Delete)** — He rātou optiona ki te whakatū i te aka o te mea i te wāhi pēnei.

:::warning
He tino whakamutai te whakamahi i te whakamutai i te aka o te mea, ā, ka taea te whakahou i te mea i roto i te wā.
:::
