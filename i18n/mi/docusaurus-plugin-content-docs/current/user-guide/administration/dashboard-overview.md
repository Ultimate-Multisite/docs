# Orakau o te Dashboard

He **Dashboard** whakamahinga ana i te Ultimate Multisite di te menue o Ultimate Multisite, ā, e hoki i widget's ki te Network Dashboard. Ka whakataki tēnei tikanga i te mea e kitea i onua-onua iwi-iwi (both places).

## Te Network Dashboard {#the-network-dashboard}

E whāwhā ana te Ultimate Multisite me ngā widget's tino pai ki te dashboard o te network, e hoki i **My Sites → Network Admin → Dashboard**.

![Network Dashboard with Ultimate Multisite widgets](/img/admin/network-dashboard-full.png)

He aha te mea e whaihihia e koe i roto i roto i cada widget:

**First Steps:** E kitea i runga i te whakamahinga hou (fresh installation) ā, e tino whakamārama ana ki a koe mō ngā mahi whakamutunga e pēnei i te whakarite i te wahi whakaora (configuring a payment method) me te whero akounta whakamārama (creating a test account).

![First Steps widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-first-steps.png)

**Summary:** E hoki ana i te whakapapa o ngā whakamārama (signups) me te pūtake (revenue) mō te rā. Ko te MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) e hoki ana i te whakatika o te total katoa mo ngā mea whai utu i ngā mātua (customers) e hiahia ana i ngā membership e whai utu.

![Summary widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-summary.png)

**Activity Stream:** E hoki ana i ngā mahi katoa ki te Ultimate Multisite — ngā whakamārama, ngā whero (cancellations), ngā wahi hou, ngā whero (deletions), me ngā mahi pēnei.

![Activity Stream widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-activity-stream.png)

**Right Now:** E whakaatu ana te whakatūwha (short summary) o ngā aua (users) me ngā wahi i te network, me ngā tikanga whakamua ki te whero akounta hou (create new sites) ā me ngā aua.

![Right Now widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-right-now.png)

**News & Discussions:** E whaiaro ana me e whakaatu ana i ngā pūrongo hou o Ultimate Multisite, e hoki ana i ngā whakamutunga (updates), ngā pētranga nui (critical bugs), ā, ngā whakamaoritanga (security fixes).

![News widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-news.png)

## Te Dashboard o te Ultimate Multisite {#the-ultimate-multisite-dashboard}

I roto i roto i te network dashboard, e whakaatu ana te holistic information mō te network, ko te ultimate multisite dashboard (i te pū-uri o te menue o Ultimate Multisite) e whakaatu ana i ngā whakamohana mō te service.

![Ultimate Multisite Dashboard](/img/admin/um-dashboard-top.png)

**Pātai o Pūtea Whakaora Māhaki (Monthly Recurring Revenue Growth):** E whakaatu i te whakamahinga o pūtea mō mātou, e whakatika ana i te rā o 12 maea o te kalendera. Ka whakaataki te pūtea hou i te rangi paku (blue) i roto i te rangi, ā, ka whakaataki ngā whakatūnga katoa (cancellations) i te rangi paku (red).

![MRR Growth chart](/img/admin/um-dashboard-mrr.png)

**Whakawhiti o Rētaki Rāhui (Date-range Support):** Whakarite i o te data o te dashboard e ngā rawa o te rā, kia whakatika i te mea e tika ana ki ahau. Ka whakatika ai te whaikōrero o te rā (date range) ko te mea e whakaataki ana i te mea e hoki ana i roto i ngā widgets i runga ake.

![Date range and dashboard stats](/img/admin/um-dashboard-date-range.png)

Ka uaua i te widgets o te dashboard:

- **Pūtea (Revenue)** — Pūtea katoa mō te rā, me te whakatika o te MRR me pūtea mō au eke ana i roto i au.
- **Whakawhanaungatanga Hou (New Memberships)** — Kaau ngā whakahou hou o ngā mihi i te rangi e whakatika ana i te rā e whakatika ana.
- **Whakawhanaungatanga Ora (Signups by Countries)** — Whakarite i te wāhi o te ao mō ngā whakawhanaungatanga.
- **Whakawhanaungatanga Ora E Tōpohu (Signups by Forms)** — Kaau ngā whānau o ngā mea e whai ake ana i te checkout form, me te whakamahi o ngā admin.
- **Ngā Wāhi Whakatika Whakamau Manuia (Most Visited Sites)** — Ngā wāhi whakatika katoa i tō whānau (network) (e pōtanga ai te whakaatu o te hitihite i **Ultimate Multisite → Settings → Sites → Enable Visits Limitation and Counting**).

![Dashboard statistics](/img/admin/um-dashboard-stats.png)
