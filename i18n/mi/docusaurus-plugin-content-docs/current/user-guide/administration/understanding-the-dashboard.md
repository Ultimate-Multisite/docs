# Whakarongo i te Ultimate Multisite Dashboard

He aha mō mātou, i te whakawhanake o ngā optiona whakamārama mō te whakataunga me ngā mahi o te Ultimate Multisite, kia whakatika atu ki te dashboard.

Mā te pai o plugin WordPress, ka whakamaoritanga te Ultimate Multisite he menu entry o te whenua (network-level) ngaahi wāhi, e tino whakamārama ana i **Ultimate Multisite**, me ngā wāhi whai mana (sub-menu entries) mōna.

## Widgets o te Dashboard o te Whenua (Network Dashboard Widgets) {#network-dashboard-widgets}

Ka whakawhiti i widget mō te mahi katoa ki te dashboard o te whenua. Ka mau koe i tēnei dashboard i te whakamahi: **My Sites → Network Admin → Dashboard**. Ko ngā widgets he whai whakaaro nui, e tino whakamōhaka ana i te whakamohanga mō te administrator o te whenua (network administrator) ki te whai i mōhio me ngā mahi whānui.

![Network Dashboard with Ultimate Multisite widgets](/img/admin/network-dashboard-full.png)

### Ngā Wāhanga Whakatika Tū (First Steps) {#first-steps}

![First Steps widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-first-steps.png)

Ka pōwhiri i tēnei widget i te whakamahinga hou o Ultimate Multisite, ā, ka whai whakaaro ki te administrator o te whenua kia whakatā i ngā mahi whakahou, e ngaro ai te whakamahi mō te whakamahi pai, jastai ahau e whakawhiti i heke.

### Whakamārama (Summary) {#summary}

![Summary widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-summary.png)

Ka mau i te Summary widget i te whakatutuki o ngā wāhi whakahou me te pūtaiao o te hoki (revenue) mō te rā. Ka tino whakamārama ai te MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) te whakatika o te total katoa mō ngā mea e hoki i te pūtaiao me ngā membership.

### Manaakitanga Mahi (Activity Stream) {#activity-stream}

![Activity Stream widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-activity-stream.png)

Ka mau i tēnei widget i ngā mahi katoa o Ultimate Multisite. Ngā mahi he whakahou, te whakatū wāhi hou, te whakatū wāhi ki te whakatū (deletions), me ngā mahi pēnei i te mea katoa.

Mā te whakamahi i te Events section o te documentation, ka mōhaka atu koe ki whakamārama mō ngā mahi katoa i tino pai.

### I te Wā (Right Now) {#right-now}

![Right Now widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-right-now.png)

Kua whaiako tēnei widget mā te whakaatu whakamārama paku mō ngā aua aua me ngā wāhi i te whānau. He kuruitu tikanga (shortcuts) i te upoko e tino ātaahua ana ki te whaiaro atu ki te whakamaoritanga aua aua rā i te whakaputa aua aua hou.

### Tinana me Whakarongo {#news-and-discussions}

![Tinana me Whakarongo widget](/img/admin/network-dashboard-news.png)

Ko tēnei widget e whaikōrero ana me te whakaatu i ngā tinana hou o Ultimate Multisite. Kia mōhiamo i tēnei widget mō whakamohakohia i ngā whakamaoritanga, ngā pakihi nui (critical bugs), me ngā whakahaere tangata (security fixes).

## Ultimate Multisite Dashboard {#ultimate-multisite-dashboard}

I te where o te tinana whānau (network-level dashboard) e whakaatu ana i ngā mōhio katoa mō te whānau, ko te Ultimate Multisite dashboard (i te upoko o te menu o Ultimate Multisite) e whakaatu i ngā mōhio mō te mahi.

![Ultimate Multisite Dashboard](/img/admin/um-dashboard-top.png)

### Whakaora Pūtahe Mō Rēmi Māhunga (Monthly Recurring Revenue Growth) {#monthly-recurring-revenue-growth}

Ko te pūrere MRR e whakaatu i te whakatika o ngā rēmi māhunga (Monthly Recurring Revenue) i roto i te tino mara wā 12 mō te ora.

![MRR Growth chart](/img/admin/um-dashboard-mrr.png)

Ko ngā rēmi hou e whaiako ana i te kōkiri (blue), ko ngā whakawhanaungatanga (cancellations) e whakaatu ana i te rangi (red).

### Whakahaere o Ngā Mara Wā (Date-range Support) {#date-range-support}

I roto i te whakatutuki o ngā mea, he taea te whakakotahitanga mō ngā aua aua me te mahi. Ko te whakatutuki o ngā mara wā (date-range support) e whaiaro ana i te dashboard e hoki i te administrator o te whānau ki te fokus mō ngā mōhio me te mara wā pēnei i te rēmi, ngā aua hou, ngā whakamāhi i roto i ngā wā, ngā whakamāhi i runga i te fōrm, me ngā wāhi e whai anga hoki.

![Date range selector and stats](/img/admin/um-dashboard-date-range.png)

Ko te mara wā e whakatika i te mara wā o ngā mōhio e whakaatuotino e tōna upoko, ā, he whai ana ki ngā widget hou i runga i tēnei, ā, ko te rēmi, ngā aua hou, ngā whakamāhi i roto i ngā wā, ngā whakamāhi i runga i te fōrm, me ngā wāhi e whai anga hoki.

![Dashboard statistics widgets](/img/admin/um-dashboard-stats.png)

:::tip
Mōinei, kia whakatika i te widget **Most Visited Sites**, whakatika i te whakamahi o te counting o ngā pēri (visit counting) i **Ultimate Multisite → Settings → Sites → Enable Visits Limitation and Counting**.
:::
