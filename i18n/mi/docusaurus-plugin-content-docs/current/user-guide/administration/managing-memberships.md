# Kaha Whakamana (Managing Memberships)

I roto i Ultimate Multisite, e taea ai koe te whakamaoritanga me te whakamaoritanga katoa o ngā subscriptions o tō wāhi. Kia whakaoti me whakarere i ngā whakamārama o te subscription, ahakoaho ana ki ngā plān, ngā puta mai, te whāinga o ngā wāhi, te whāinga o te whaiwhana, te whakatūatanga o te rā, te whakatupu o te rā, te whakatūatanga o te rā, te whakahoki o te rā, me ngā wā o te whakatūatanga.

Kia pēnei i te **Memberships** tab i te menu o Ultimate Multisite, kia whai atu ki tēnei puta mai.

![Memberships list page](/img/admin/memberships-list.png)

I te puta mai o Memberships, e taea ai koe te whakaahua o ngā mea katoa o ngā mea whaiina me ngā puta mai e whakahou ana i ngā aka o ngā mea. E taea ai koe te whiriwhiri ngā memberships e runga i te status: active (e tino pai), pending (e pēnei), on hold (e whakatū), expired (e whakatū), or cancelled (e whakatū).

Whakatū i te membership ki te whakahou ngā details o te membership — billing info (mō te whaiwhana), site or visit limits (ngā rawa o te wāhi ahau me te pēnei).

Click on a membership to access the page where you can edit the membership details — billing info, site or visit limits, and more.

![Membership edit interface](/img/admin/membership-edit.png)

He aha tēnei whakaahua katoa o te puta mai o te membership:

![Membership edit full interface](/img/admin/membership-edit-full.png)

E taea ai koe te whānui i ngā memberships hou e whakahou ana i ngā users e pēnei i ka whakatū ana i tō wāhi. Click **Add Membership** and configure the details.

![Add Membership modal](/img/admin/membership-add-modal.png)

He aha, kia whakatū te membership. Ko te membership hou e whakahou ana i te list o ngā memberships.
