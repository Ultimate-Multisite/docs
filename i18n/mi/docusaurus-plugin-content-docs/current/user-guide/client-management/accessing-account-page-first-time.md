# He aha ahau e taea ai te whai ake i te manawa ahau ki te whai i te manawa ahau mō te wārie?

Kia ora ki Ultimate Multisite!

Whai i te link mo te manawa ahau i pēnei i te email kua pēnei i ka whiti i tō mua i ka whiti i tō plugin:

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Account page email with access link -->

Rauhia te email kua roa koe i te whaina me te password kua roa koe i te email.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Login page with email and password fields -->

Kia ora, ko tēnei te wārie taiao ahau ki te whakamahi i tēnei manawa ahau mō tēnei account, ka e whakapainga ana koe ki te whakahaupau i tō password. Whakaahua i i te whakaaahi ahau me te klik i te button _Save_.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Change password form with Save button -->

I tō manawa ahau, ka taea e koe te whakamaoritanga katoa o tō account me ngā mea katoa he pēnei i te whai mai i te whaina, te whakahou, te whakatika, me ngā mea nui ake.
