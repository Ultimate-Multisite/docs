# Whakamutunga i Heata (v2)

_**NOTE WHAKANGA: Ko tēnei whakamārama he tino mō Ultimate Multisite v2.x.**_

He tino whakahou ahau ki ai ngā mea o teina. E taea ana i ngā mea o teina te whakawhiti ki heata hou, ka taea hoki te whaina ngā wāhi katoa o te aoae e koe e hoatu.

I tēnei tutorial, ka whakatika ahau i te mea he pēnei te whakawhiti i teata me ko te mea e titiro ana i te mea e tū mai i ara i te whakamutunga o te whakawhiti.

Kia whakamutunga i teata, me ngā mea o teina te whai i te dashboard o teina me te pahi ki te puta mai i te **Account** page.

![Dashboard o te subsite o teina me te link menue Account e titiro ana](/img/account-page/account-menu.png)

I te page Account, ka whakaatu atu i ngā mea e koe e hoki ana ahau i te whānau o koe me te heata e pēnei ana ki ai. Kia whakawhiti ki heata hou, me ngā mea e koe te whakatika **Change** i te kura-kura (top-right corner) o te taiao **Your Membership**.

![Page Account Card Your Membership card me button Change](/img/account-page/membership-change-button.png)

Ka whakawhiti atu ahau ki te fōmai whai whakaaro (checkout form) i tēnei pūrere, ko ka whakaatu i ngā heata katoa e taea.

Ka taea hoki i a ngā mea o teina te whakahti i te **services and packages available for their current plan** , i roto i ngā wāhi heke ahau (in case they just want to purchase a specific service or package (like unlimited visits or disk space in our example here)), me nā te whakawhiti i teata.

![Upgrade picker e whakaatu i ngā heata me ngā packages e taea o teina](/img/account-page/upgrade-picker.png)

I taiao ahau i te aua mea e tika ana ki te whaina, ko ka whakaatu atu i a ngā mea e koe e hiahia te whai mai i tēnei wā - me whakawhiti i te credit e pai ana i te whakahaupū (excluding any existing credit) me te mea e koe e whakawhiti i teata.

I roto i te awhi, ko, inā he heata hou te aua mea me ka whai ake te whakamahi o te pai, ka pēnei ahau i te whai ake: ka whai i te whakahaupū (membership charge) e pai ana i te wā e kawhai atu i te aua heata hou.

![Upgrade payment summary showing credit applied and next billing amount](/img/account-page/upgrade-summary.png)

Ka whai whakaaro ki te plan (plan) takea koe, he mea e noho ana i te subscription o koe, ka e hiahia koe ki whakamārama i tēnei mea.

![Notice when the selected plan does not change the subscription](/img/account-page/upgrade-no-change.png)

Iha i te checkout (checkout) ko, ka puta ake i te whai whakaaro hou (new product(s)) ki ngā account o ngā mea e pēnei ana, ā, ka puta ake katoa ngā limit (limits) me ngā āhuatanga (features) o ngā mea hou ko te wakamutunga: visits, disk space, posts, etc...

## Ngā Wāhanga Whakamutunga me Whakawhanaungatanga (Upgrade and Downgrade Paths)

I roto i roto i ngā mea e pēnei ana, ka puta ake i te tab **Up & Downgrades**. Ko te oneto (option) tūroa i tēnei tab he ingoa ingoa **Plan Group**.

**Plan groups** ko te mea e hiahia koe ki whakamōhiho i Ultimate Multisite kia mōhio ana i ngā plan (plans) pēnei i ngā "whānau" (family) ata, ā, hei tauira, me whakamahinga ahau i ngā plan-group (plan group) ei te whaiaro ki te whakatō i ngā oneto o te upgrade/downgrade.

![Product edit Up and Downgrades tab with Plan Group field](/img/config/product-upgrades-plan-group.png)

Moe, he pēnei: he **Free plan**, he **Basic Plan** me he **Premium Plan** e pēnei ana. He hiahia koe i ngā mea e whaiaro ki te whakamārama i te mea he tino pai (upgrade) anō ki te **Premium Plan** nengai, ā, he tino kite ahau i te "Basic Plan" ko oneto upgrade. Ko te mea katoa e hiahia koe he te whakatō ingoa plan group (plan group name) pēnei i ngā screenshot o muri i tēnei:

![Free Plan product page with High End plan group assigned](/img/config/product-upgrades-free.png)

![Premium Plan product page with High End plan group assigned](/img/config/product-upgrades-premium.png)

He mea e taea ana ki te whakamahi ahau i tēnei: kia mō Ultimate Multisite, he tino whānau o ngā plānge (plans) i te wāhi o te wānanga (network), he pēnei i **High End**. I roto i te wā e ka whero ai koe i te whakamārama (upgrades) ana pe ka whero ai i te whero (downgrades), kia whakaatu anō i ngā plānge o te whānau tona (same family) anake mō te wānanga.
