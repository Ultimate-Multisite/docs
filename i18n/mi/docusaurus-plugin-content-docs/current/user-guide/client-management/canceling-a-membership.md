# Whakamutunga Membershipu (v2)

_**NOTE WHAKANGA: Ko tēnei tikanga he tino whakamahinga mō Ultimate Multisite v2.x.**_

He tino ātaheke te whakamutunga o membershipu i runga i te wāhi o ngā mea whānau.

Kia te wahi, ko te mea e te user te pito atu ki te **Account** page.

I tēnei wāhi, me rongo i te **Danger Zone**, ā, kliki ki **Delete Account**.

![Account page Danger Zone with Delete Account button](/img/account-page/danger-zone.png)

He module e whakamohua ana akua he tino whakahaupū. Me whakatika (toggle) te option **Confirm Account Deletion** i runga i te ongoa, ā, kliki ki **Delete Account**.

![Confirm Account Deletion dialog](/img/account-page/delete-account-confirm.png)

Ka whai te mea he whakawātea (log out) i te wāhi ahau, ka whakamutunga tō membershipu i runga i ngā wāhi ahau, ā, ka whakatika (delete) tō wāhi i runga i ngā wāhi ahau.

![Logged out after account deletion](/img/account-page/logged-out-after-delete.png)

![Membership canceled confirmation](/img/account-page/membership-canceled.png)

**Tūmanako nui:** Aroha, ka whakatika tēnei mahi i te mea ka taea e te mea whānau he tino mōhaka (more than one membership) ahau, ā, he tino mōhaka (none membership) ana i pēnei.

![Customer still exists in the network after cancellation](/img/admin/customer-after-cancel.png)

![Customer with no active memberships](/img/admin/memberships-customer-empty.png)
