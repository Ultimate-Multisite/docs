# Ngā Waka o Awa i Tō Rere (v2)

Iha i roto i te whakatere i tō wāhi, ka taea e ngā mea o te whenua (customers) te whai i te dashboard o rā.

Anu i te **Account** page, i tēnei i ko ahau e taea ai mōna te whakaahua mōna i ngā waiata, ngā mihi, ngā domain, ngā pōtanga o te plan me ngā mea katoa, he tino whai ake ana ki te **My Sites** page, i tēnei i ko ahau e taea ai mōna te whakaahua me te whakamaoritanga i ngā waka katoa. He tino tika anō, ka paku i runga i My Sites i tō dashboard.

![Link My Sites i te dashboard o te mea o te whenua](/img/account-page/my-sites-link.png)

Ka e whakaatu ana atu i ngā waka katoa, me **Manage** button i runga i roto i ko tōna whakatutuki i taro. Ko ka paku i iho i ahau i te **dashboard o tēnei waka-i-tara**.

Ka taea ai mōna te whakaahua i wai i ngā waka katoa, me te button Manage i runga i roto i ko tōna whakatutuki i taro. Ko ka paku i iho i ahau i te **dashboard o tēnei waka-i-tara**.

Ka taea ai mōna te whakaahua i wai i ngā waka katoa, me te button Manage i runga i roto i ko tōna whakatutuki i taro. Ka paku i iho i ahau i te **dashboard o tēnei waka-i-tara**.

Ka taea ai mōna te whakaahua i wai i ngā waka katoa, me te button Manage i runga i roto i ko tōna whakatutuki i taro. Ka paku i iho i ahau i te **dashboard o tēnei waka-i-tara**.

He pūmanawa mō te whakaahua edit interface:

![Site edit full interface](/img/admin/site-edit-full.png)

Ko ka paku i iho i ahau i te **Account** menu i te whakatere i tō wāhi.

![Link Account menu i te dashboard o te mea o te whenua](/img/account-page/account-menu.png)

Ka taea ai mōna te whakaahua me te whakamaoritanga i ngā whakamahi o te mea o te whenua:

![Customer edit interface](/img/admin/customer-edit.png)

Ka ka paku i iho i ahau i te **Change Default Site**.

![Button Change Default Site i te page Account](/img/account-page/change-default-site.png)

Ka e pōutu i tētahi window. Ka paku i iho i tēnei list drop-down me te whai i te waka i te tino primary website i te whakaputa.

![Selection dropdown Change Default Site](/img/account-page/change-default-site-dropdown.png)

Ka ka paku i iho i ahau i te **Change Default Site** anō, ko he tino whakamutunga!

![Kia pūrere i te whakawhiti te wāhi o te wāhi whaiako](https://example.com/images/account-page/change-default-site-confirm.png)
