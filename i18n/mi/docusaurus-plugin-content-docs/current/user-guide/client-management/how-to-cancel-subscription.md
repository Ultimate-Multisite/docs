# He aha ahau e taea ai te whakatau i te whakataunga (subscription) ahau? {#how-can-i-cancel-my-subscription}

Kia ora! Ko koe he tino multisite license (Ultimate Multisite license), ka taea e koe te whakatau i te whakataunga o ia i ngā wakamārama (renewal) i te wakamārama (time) kua e hiahia. Ka whai i ngā mahi i runga i te whakatipu (steps) ki muri:

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Account page email with access link -->

Rauatanga te email kua whai i ai mua i te whaiwhero (purchase) me te whakairo (password).

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Login page with email and password fields -->

He tēnei te whakatipu (initial screen) o te Page Account ahau:

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Account page initial screen showing subscription details -->

Kia whakatau i te whakataunga, i te menu i te tangata (left), pahi ki _Renewing & Billing._

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Renewing & Billing page with cancel option -->

Whakawā (Click) i te arrow i te tangata (right) kia pātaki i te window ahau. Kei tēnei wakamārama, ka taea e koe te whiri i te option (option) kua whakatau i te auto-renew.

Ka whakaatu te system i he pūrākau whakamārama (confirmation message).

Kia tino mārama ana koe kia hiahia koe ki te whakatau i te whakataunga, whakawā i te button _**Cancel Renewals**_. I tēnei mahi, ka whai i a koe ki te whakahaupū (survey) kua tīmata.

Whakahaere! Ka wona te whakataunga ahau i te auto-renew.

**Ka e hiahia koe i he key (key) mō te wakamārama o tō whakataunga, ka taea e koe te whakamahi i ai i te wakamārama (subscription) i te wakamārama (expires).** I ngā wakamārama (case) kua e hiahia koe ki te whakahaupū i tō whakataunga ahau, ka taea e koe te whakamahi i ai manuahi (manually).
