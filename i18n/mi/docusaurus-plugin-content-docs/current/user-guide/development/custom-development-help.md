# He aha te whakamahi i a Codeable mō āhuatanga whakawhanake tino pai? {#how-to-get-custom-development-help-from-codeable}

Māroaho, he tino kitea ki te wakamana ahau ki te whai i ngā kaha whakawhanake tino pai kia tika ai ngā mihi o tātou. Ko te mea e tino kitea ana, he tino pai tō [Codeable](https://codeable.io/partners/wpultimo/?ref=7wHwE) mō atu, he wāhi i taea ai koe te whai i whānau whānau whai mana me ngā whai whakawhanake e tino whai hoatu i te manawa o Ultimate Multisite.

He [Codeable](https://codeable.io/partners/wpultimo/?ref=7wHwE) he tūnga e whakatō ai ngā mea mō te whānau me ngā whai whakawhanake WordPress e tino whai mana, e taea te mahi i ngā pakihi o tika te nui. He mea tino whakamahi ahau i te whakatau i ngā mea, kia puta he whai manawa ki te whai whakawhanake e tino whai hoatu i te mahi e tino kitea ana mo koe.

### He aha te whakamahi i a Codeable mō te whakatō pakihi? {#how-to-create-a-project-in-codeable}

Kia kaha, pono ki [Codeable](https://codeable.io/partners/wpultimo/?ref=7wHwE) me te whakatipu i te **Start a Project** (Whakatō Pakihi).

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Codeable homepage with Start a Project button -->

I tēnei wā, kia puta te pakihi, whakatipu ki te kiri i te **Start a Project**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Codeable project submission page with Start a Project option -->

I "I need help with" (He mea e tino kitea ana), chono i **Customizing** (Whakawhanake Whakamahi).

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Codeable project form with Customizing selected -->

I "My" (Ko ahau), chono i **Plugin** (Plugin).

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Codeable project form with Plugin selected -->

Kia puta te **Continue** (Whakarite) button.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Codeable project form Continue button -->

I tēnei wā, he kitea ana ki te whakatakino i tō pakihi me te whakamahi te onua tino pai e taea ai koe. Kia kaha ki te mau i ngā mōhio ahau e tino nui!

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Codeable project description form -->

Whakarawhina i ngā mea katoa kia tū he whai whakaaro mō te whakaingo.

Whakarawhina i ngā āhuatanga berikut ki te tino mārama ai i te whakatau (pricing).

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Codeable project pricing questions -->

Me whai ake, ko koe heke i te [Codeable](https://codeable.io/partners/wpultimo/?ref=7wHwE) account? Whakawhana hou i tōna me whakamārama i te **Publish My Project** button.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: Codeable account creation and Publish My Project button -->

I roto i te wā kua whai koe ki te whakahihiko i tō [Codeable](https://codeable.io/partners/wpultimo/?ref=7wHwE) project, ka whai koe ki ngā developer (whakaahua) e tino pai ana ki te mahi e toa i te mea he tino āhua o te mahi e tika ana mō tō projectu.

Ka tū i tēnei whakataunga ki te tino pai i te mahi e tika ana mō tō mahi, he heke i te WordPress me Ultimate Multisite!

Ko te hāpai i te Codeable minimum rate he $70–$120 USD ko ora. Ka pēnei te whakaawe e whakahutai ana ki te āhuatanga o te mahi, te tino pai o te mahi, me te wakamārama o te mahi.

I roto i te wā kua pahi koe i tō whakaawe, ka taea e koe te whaikōrero i te developer e tika ana mō tō whatu me te whakatō i tō projectu together!
