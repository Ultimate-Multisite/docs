# Whakawāhi me Filter o Ultimate Multisite {#ultimate-multisite-hooks-and-filters}

He whakamahinga nui o Ultimate Multisite he tino nui te whānau o ngā funksiyana me ngā pūtaiao database (database queries). He hook me filter ana ahau ki te tino whakaaahi atu ki te whakawhiti, te whakarere, akorua kōrero hou ki ngā funksiyana eke ana.

He whakataki ahau i roto i te list o ngā hook me filters mō te whakamahi o ngā mātanga (advanced users) kia tino whai whakaaro atu ki te whakawhiti i te wāhi (network). Kei te taea e koe te whai ake i te list mai i Github i te whakapā (link) i runga i ngā mea i tēnei:

https://github.com/next-press/wp-ultimo-hooks-and-filters/blob/main/wp-ultimo.md
