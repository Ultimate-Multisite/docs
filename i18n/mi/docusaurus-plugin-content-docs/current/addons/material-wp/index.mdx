# Material WP {#material-wp}

Kua ōrite tonu te atanga kaiwhakahaere WordPress mō te wā roa. Engari kua whakaaro rānei koe ka pēhea te āhua o tētahi putanga Material Design o te atanga kaiwhakahaere? 

Ko Material WP he kaupapa kaiwhakahaere i waihangatia hei hoatu i taua āhua me taua rongo ki a koe, e āwhina ana i a koe ki te whakarato ki ō kiritaki i tētahi Dashboard kua nui ake te whakarite.

## Āhuatanga {#features}

- Tautoko CSS ritenga
- Ngā tautuhinga whakakāhua māmā
- Whakaraupapa anō i te tahua
- Kawemai/kaweake māmā

## Tuhinga āwhina {#documentation}

**Te whakarite i tō Paewhiri Kaiwhakahaere ki Material WP**

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Tukuake 'material-wp' ki te whaiaronga '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Whakahohe i te monomai mā te tahua 'Monomai' i WordPress
