# Rangitaki Panonitanga mō te WooCommerce Integration

Putanga 2.2.0 - I tukuna i te 2026-07-01
* Hou: Ka puta ināianei ngā rahinga tāke Ultimate Multisite hei rārangi utu WooCommerce motuhake i te checkout, kia mārama ake ai ngā tapeke tāke i mua i te utunga.
* Hou: Kua tāpirihia he tautuhinga kōwhiri-mai "Whakatārewatia Tonutia ngā Mema ina Rahua ngā Whakahoutanga" me te tātari `wu_woo_suspend_on_payment_failure` mō ngā pae e hiahia ana ki te whakatārewatanga ina rahua te whakahoutanga i te wā matapihi ngana-anō o WooCommerce Subscriptions.
* Whakatika: I whakakotahitia ngā tūnga WooCommerce Subscription ka noho hē pea te tukutahi ki ngā mematanga Ultimate Multisite i muri i ngā whakahoutanga i rahua, i ora ake rānei.
* Whakatika: Kua tāpirihia te moni o te toa WooCommerce ki te rārangi moni Ultimate Multisite i te wā i ngaro ai.
* Whakatika: I tiakina ngā taipitopito nama a te kiritaki i te aronga anō i ngā kaiohauru ki te checkout WooCommerce.
* Kua whakapai ake: Kua tāpirihia te hototahitanga ki Jetpack Autoloader 5.
* Kua whakapai ake: Kua whakapaingia te hanga mōkī tuku kia karo ai ngā kōnae zip GitHub me ngā mākete i ngā kōpaki whakarite kōhanga me ngā kōnae whanaketanga.
