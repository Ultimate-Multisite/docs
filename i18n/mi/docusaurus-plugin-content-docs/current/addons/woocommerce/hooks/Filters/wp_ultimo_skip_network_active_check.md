# Tātari: wp_ultimo_skip_network_active_check {#filter-wpultimoskipnetworkactivecheck}

Tukuna ngā kaiwhakawhanake kia whakakotiti wawe i te tirotiro whakahohe whatunga.

He whaihua tēnei ina whakamahi i ngā whakaritenga e hāngai ana ki composer me ētahi atu whakaritenga ritenga, pērā i Bedrock hei tauira, i reira ko te whakamahi i ngā plugin hei mu-plugins te tikanga.

## Ngā Tawhā {#parameters}

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $skip_network_activation_check | `bool` | Mēnā me tīpoka te tirotiro, kāore rānei; ko te taunoa he false. |

### Mai i {#since}

- 2.0.0
### Pūtake {#source}

Kua tautuhia i [`inc/class-requirements.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite-woocommerce/blob/main/inc/class-requirements.php#L272) i te rārangi 272


## Ngā Whakahokinga {#returns}
true mēnā e hiahia ana koe ki te tīpoka i te tirotiro, false ki te kore.
