# Tātari: wp_ultimo_woocommerce_checkout_fields_to_clean {#filter-wpultimowoocommercecheckoutfieldstoclean}

Ka tuku i ngā kaiwhakawhanake kia tātari i ngā āpure wāhitau nama taunoa a WooCommerce.

I te mea he āpure wāhitau nama tā Ultimate Multisite, tērā pea he tika kia tango i ngā āpure taunoa e whakaaturia ana e WooCommerce ki te puka checkout. Hei taunoa, kāore he uara e tukuna ki tēnei rārangi, nō reira ka puritia ngā āpure katoa kua whirihorahia ki WooCommerce kia whakaaturia.

### Mai i {#since}

- 2.0.0
