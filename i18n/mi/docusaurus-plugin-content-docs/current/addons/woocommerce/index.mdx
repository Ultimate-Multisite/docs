# WooCommerce Whakaurunga {#woocommerce-integration}

He rite rawa te mahi a te WooCommerce Whakaurunga ki te āhua o te mahi a tā mātou Tomokanga Ā-ringa. He nui rawa te ngāwari ka tukuna ki ō kaiwhakamahi, e āhei ai rātou ki te kōwhiri i tētahi o ngā puka utu e wātea ana i tō tāutanga WooCommerce. Ā, he māmā: ka mutu ana te wā nama a te kaiwhakamahi, ka whakatārewatia te ohaurunga kaiwhakamahi. I taua wā anō, ka waihangatia he ota ki te tāutanga WooCommerce o te pae matua o te whatunga. Kātahi ka whiwhi te kaiwhakamahi i tētahi nama ā-īmēra, me tētahi hononga hei whakahaere i te utu. Ina tae mai te utu, ka whakahoungia te ohaurunga.

## Ngā Āhuatanga {#features}

- He whakaurunga māmā i waenga i Ultimate Multisite me WooCommerce
- He maha ngā tikanga utu e wātea ana
- Te nama auau aunoa mā te pūtake ohaurunga kua whakaurua, me ngā whakapainga kōwhiringa o te monomai WooCommerce Subscriptions
- Kei roto i ngā tapeke tukanga utu ngā tāke Ultimate Multisite hei rārangi utu WooCommerce motuhake, kāore e tāketia, kia taea ai e ngā kiritaki te arotake i ngā rahinga tāke i mua i te utu

## Tuhinga Āwhina {#documentation}

**Te Whakarite i te WooCommerce Whakaurunga**

**Te Whakawhānui i ō Kōwhiringa Utu mā Ultimate Multisite: WooCommerce Whakaurunga**

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Tukuatu 'ultimate-multisite-woocommerce' ki te whaiaronga '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Whakahohe i te monomai mā te tahua 'Plugins' i WordPress
3. Whāia te Wizard ā-taahiraa hei whakarite i te monomai

## Aratohu Whakamahi {#usage-guide}

_**TUHIPOKA NUI: E kōrero ana tēnei tuhinga mō Ultimate Multisite putanga 2.x.**_

_**KIA TŪPATO:** Me whakahohe a Ultimate Multisite: WooCommerce Whakaurunga i a WooCommerce i te iti rawa ki tō pae matua._

E mārama ana mātou kāore a _Stripe_ me _PayPal_ e wātea ana i ētahi whenua, ā, ka whakawhāiti, ka aukati rānei i ngā kaiwhakamahi Ultimate Multisite kia whakamahi whai hua i tā mātou monomai. Nō reira i waihanga mātou i tētahi **tāpiritanga hei whakauru i _WooCommerce_**.

He monomai eTauhokohoko tino rongonui a _WooCommerce_. I waihanga ngā kaiwhakawhanake huri noa i te ao i ngā tāpiritanga hei whakauru i ngā tomokanga utu rerekē ki reira. I whakamahi mātou i tēnei hei whakawhānui i ngā tomokanga utu ka taea e koe te whakamahi i te pūnaha nama Ultimate Multisite.

## Te Whakarite {#setting-it-up}

Tuatahi, me tāuta e koe te tāpiritanga WooCommerce Whakaurunga, he **koreutu mō ngā kaiwhakamahi whai raihana**. Hei tāuta, pāwhiria a Ultimate Multisite ki tō papatohu kaiwhakahaere matua, ā, tīpakohia **Ngā Tāpiritanga**.

![Whārangi tāpiritanga](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

I konei ka kitea e koe ngā tāpiritanga Ultimate Multisite katoa. Pāwhiria te tāpiritanga **Ultimate Multisite: WooCommerce Whakaurunga**.

![Whārangi tāpiritanga e whakaatu ana i te WooCommerce Whakaurunga](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

Ka puta ake he matapihi me ngā taipitopito o te tāpiritanga. Pāwhiri noa ki **Tāuta Ināianei** *.*

Kātahi ka taea e koe te haere ki ngā tautuhinga **_WooCommerce_ o tō Pae matua** hei whakahohe, hei whirihora hoki i te tomokanga utu e hiahia ana koe ki te whakamahi.

### Ngā rārangi utu tāke o te tukanga utu {#checkout-tax-fee-lines}

Ina whai tāke tētahi tukanga utu Ultimate Multisite, ka tāpirihia e te whakaurunga ia rahinga tāke Ultimate Multisite ki te tukanga utu WooCommerce hei rārangi utu motuhake, kāore e tāketia. Mā konei ka kitea tonutia te tapeke tāke i mua i te utu, ā, ka ārai i a WooCommerce kia kaua e tāke anō i te utu tāke.

## Me pēhea te whakahaere i te utu {#how-to-manage-the-payment}

He rite rawa te mahi a te WooCommerce Whakaurunga ki te āhua o te mahi a tā mātou **Tomokanga Ā-ringa**. Ko te ngoikoretanga, me utu ā-ringa ia utu i te mutunga o te wā nama. Anei te āhua o te rerenga:

  * Kia mutu te huringa nama a te kaiwhakamahi, ka whakatārewatia te mematanga. I taua wā anō, ka waihangatia he ota ki te tāutanga _WooCommerce_ i runga i te pae matua o tō whatunga.

  * Kātahi ka whiwhi ngā kaiwhakamahi i tētahi nama i tā rātou īmēra, me tētahi hononga hei whakahaere i te utu (ka puta hoki te hononga ki tō rātou hītori nama mai i te papatohu o tō rātou pae-iti). Ka tuku anō taua hononga i te kaiwhakamahi ki te **tukanga utu** _**WooCommerce**_ , ā, ka taea e ngā kaiwhakamahi te tīpako i tētahi o ngā **tomokanga _WooCommerce_ e wātea ana** hei tukatuka i tā rātou utu.

  * Ina tae mai te utu, ka **whakahoungia** te mematanga, ka rangitakina te utu ki Ultimate Multisite, ā, ka whakaritea te ota kia oti.

### Ētahi Tuhipoka Nui: {#some-important-notes}

  * Kei te mahi tonu te taumahinga taunoa o Ultimate Multisite me tēnei momo whakaurunga. Hei tauira, ka **tuku moni whakahoki** mai i te mata **Whakahaere Ohaurunga**, ka whakawhiti kōrero ki WooCommerce hei tuku i te moni whakahoki mā te puka utu kua tīpakohia.

  * Ka whakawhirinaki a Ultimate Multisite: WooCommerce Whakaurunga ki WP-Cron kia mahi. He tino pai tēnei i te nuinga o te wā, engari mēnā he iti te hokohoko o te pae, kāore te cron e akiakina kia tirohia ngā takahanga kua whakaritea, ā, ka mahue te takahanga, ka puta pea he whakaroanga whakatinana.

## Monomai WooCommerce Subscriptions {#woocommerce-subscriptions-plugin}

Ko te tīmata ā-ringa i tētahi utu ia marama ehara i te mea pai mō ngā kaiwhakamahi whakamutunga, nā te mea e tūmanakohia ana kia auau aunoa ngā ohaurunga i ia huringa nama. Inaianei kua whakaurua e te tāpiri-atu WooCommerce Integration te waehere matua o ngā ohaurunga, nō reira ka whakamahi tonu ngā hua auau i ngā ohaurunga mō te nama auau.

Kāore koe e hiahia ki te hoko, ki te tāuta rānei i te [plugin WooCommerce Subscriptions](https://woocommerce.com/products/woocommerce-subscriptions/) motuhake hei hoko hua auau mā tēnei tuituitanga. Mēnā kua hokona, kua tāutahia, ā, e wātea ana taua plugin ki tō pae matua, ka whakamahia e Ultimate Multisite, ā, ka whakaatu a WooCommerce i ētahi āhuatanga whakahaere ohaurunga tāpiri. Ki te kore e wātea, ka whakahaere tonu te matua ohaurunga kua whakaurua i ngā hua auau.

![Ngā tautuhinga tuituitanga ohaurunga WooCommerce](/img/addons/woocommerce-subscription-integration.png)

Ā, koirā noa iho. Kāore āu kaiwhakamahi whakamutunga e hiahia anō ki te utu ā-ringa i te mutunga o tā rātou huringa nama mō ngā hua auau.

I tua atu i te waihangatanga o tētahi ota ki tō pae matua i raro i _WooCommerce_, ka waihanga hoki i tētahi ohaurunga mō ia pūkete.

### Te whanonga whakatārewa mō te whakahounga rahua {#failed-renewal-suspension-behavior}

Mā te taunoa, ka noho hohe tonu ngā mematanga i a WooCommerce Subscriptions e tukatuka ana i tana hōtaka ngana-anō mō te utu rahua. Ka pupuri tēnei i te matapihi aroha noa noa o WooCommerce Subscriptions, kia taea ai e tētahi whakahounga kua ora ake te whakahoki i te ohaurunga me te mematanga kia tukutahi, me te kore e haukoti wawe i te urunga.

Ka taea e ngā kaiwhakahaere e hiahia ana ki te mana urunga pakari ake te whakahohe i **Whakatārewatia Tonutia ngā Mematanga i ngā Whakahounga Rahua** i ngā tautuhinga WooCommerce Integration. Ina whakahohea tēnei tautuhinga kōwhiri-mai, ka whakatārewatia e Ultimate Multisite te mematanga e hāngai ana i te wā tonu ka rahua tētahi utu whakahounga, kaua ko te tatari kia mutu te whakahaere ngana-anō a WooCommerce Subscriptions.

Ka taea e ngā kaiwhakawhanake te whakakapi i taua whakatau anō mā te tātari `wu_woo_suspend_on_payment_failure`. Ka hangaia ngā whārangi tohutoro hook mai i te pūtake o te tāpiri-atu, nō reira ka whakahua noa tēnei tirohanga whānui i te ingoa tātari mō ngā kaupapa here whakatārewa ritenga.
