# Rārangi Huringa Tīkiti Tautoko {#support-tickets-changelog}

### 1.0.4 - 2026-05-05 {#104---2026-05-05}
* Kua whakapai ake: I tangohia te vendor/ directory i te whai a Git (kua hipokina kē e .gitignore), hei whakaiti i te rahi o te repository
* Kua whakapai ake: Kua whakamātauria tae atu ki WordPress 7.0

### 1.0.3 - 2026-05-01 {#103---2026-05-01}
* Whakatika: Whakaae ki ngā uara null i ngā nullable ticket model setters
* Whakatika: Rokiroki i ngā tīkiti katoa ki ngā ripanga ā-whatunga whānui o te ao mō te tautoko multisite tika
* Whakatika: Huna i ngā āpure kaimahi, ā, whakatika i te redirect whārangi-pātea i te puka tīkiti-hou a te kiritaki
* Whakatika: Whakakapi i ngā karanga add_meta() kāore i tautuhia ki te update_meta() tika mō te rokiroki metadata
* Whakatika: Whakakapi i te arowhai āheinga kāore i rēhitatia ki te wu_view_all_support_tickets tika
* Whakatika: Tāpiri i ngā AJAX handlers e ngaro ana mō te tūnga tīkiti, te tautapatanga, me ngā mahi quick-edit
* Whakatika: Whakatika i te ingoa tikanga mō te kitenga whakautu kaimahi i ngā tirohanga tīkiti
* Whakatika: Whakakotahi i ngā reply handlers tārua, ā, whakahāngai i ngā ingoa mahi nonce
* Whakatika: Tāpiri i te tirohanga frontend e ngaro ana mō te shortcode [wu_submit_ticket]
* Whakatika: Tāpiri i te tīwae user_id e ngaro ana, ā, whakatika i te tikanga Support_Ticket::get_user_id()
* Whakatika: Tango i te tohu kōrero-rua tāpiri i te tūtohu kōwhiringa tātari matua
* Whakatika: Tāpiri i te papa whakahaere tīkiti network admin mō ngā super admins
* Kua whakapai ake: Whakakotahi i te admin CSS ki tētahi stylesheet ā-waho kotahi
* Kua whakapai ake: Tango i te Settings submenu i te tahua admin o te pae-iti
* Kua whakapai ake: Utaina ā-herenga ngā frontend assets anake ki ngā whārangi tīkiti tautoko
* Kua whakapai ake: Pekehia te plugin autoloader ina kua utaina kē e te Bedrock root autoloader ngā dependencies

* Kua tāpirihia: Hononga tīkiti aunoa ki te kaiwhakamahi o nāianei mō ngā tukunga-whaiaro a te kiritaki
* Kua tāpirihia: Whakarei haumarutanga hei ārai i ngā kiritaki ki te whakakapi i te rangatiratanga tīkiti
* Kua tāpirihia: Ngā mahi āwhina e ngaro ana (wu_format_date, wu_user_can_view_ticket, aha atu)
* Kua tāpirihia: Tikiake me te whakahaere āpitihanga kōnae tika

* Kua whakatikahia: Kei roto ināianei i te puka whakautu tīkiti te ID tīkiti e hiahiatia ana
