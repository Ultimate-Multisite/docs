# Ngā Tīkiti Tautoko

Ko **Ngā Tīkiti Tautoko** he tāpiritanga kaha mō Ultimate Multisite e tāpiri ana i tētahi pūnaha tīkiti tautoko whānui ki tō whatunga WordPress. He tino pai mō ngā tūāpapa SaaS, ngā kaiwhakarato WaaS (paetukutuku hei ratonga), me ngā whatunga multisite e hiahia ana ki te whakarato tautoko kiritaki i ngā taumata whatunga me ngā pae takitahi hoki.

## He aha i Kōwhiri ai i Ngā Tīkiti Tautoko?

* **Tautoko Taumata-Maha**: Ka whakahaere ngā kaiwhakahaere whatunga i ngā tīkiti puta noa i te whatunga, ā, ka whakahaere ngā rangatira pae i tā rātou ake tautoko kiritaki
* **Rerengamahi Ngaio**: He whakahaere huringa-oranga tīkiti katoa me ngā taumata matua, ngā tūnga, me ngā tautapanga
* **Wheako Kiritaki**: He atanga ātaahua, urupare hoki e māmā ai te kimi āwhina mā ngā kiritaki
* **Haumaru, Whakapono hoki**: He mea hanga ki ngā tikanga pai rawa mō te haumarutanga WordPress, ā, kua arotauhia te mahinga pātengi raraunga
* **Whakaurunga Tuitui**: Ka mahi tino pai me te whakahaere kiritaki me te whakahaere pae a Ultimate Multisite

## Ngā Āhuatanga Matua

**Whakahaere Tīkiti Katoa**

* He pūnaha tau tīkiti ahurei mō te tohutoro māmā
* Ngā taumata matua ka taea te whirihora (Iti, Pūnoa, Teitei, Tino Hirahira)
* He rerengamahi tūnga whānui (Tuwhera, Kei te Mahia, E Tatari ana, Kua Whakatauria, Kua Katia)
* Te whakarōpū tīkiti (Whānui, Hangarau, Nama, Tono Āhuatanga, Pūrongo Hapa)
* Tautapahia ngā tīkiti ki ngā kaiāwhina tautoko me te tautapa-aunoa hei kōwhiringa

**Ngā Kōrerorero Rārangi-Whakautu**

* He rārangi kōrerorero katoa mō ngā whakautu tīkiti
* He kāhua motuhake mō ngā whakautu a ngā kaimahi me ngā kiritaki
* Ngā tuhipoka tūmataiti a ngā kaimahi kāore e kitea e ngā kiritaki
* He atanga wā-tūturu e whakakahaia ana e AJAX
* He ētita kuputuhi hou me ngā kōwhiringa whakahōputu

**Ngā Āpitihanga Kōnae**

* Tukuake kōnae haumaru me te tautoko tō-me-whakataka
* Ngā here momo kōnae me te rahi kōnae ka taea te whirihora
* Haumarutanga matatau me te kitenga kōnae kino
* Ngā tikiake kua tiakina me ngā mana uru

**Ngā Whakamōhiotanga Atamai**

* Ngā whakamōhiotanga īmēra aunoa mō ngā tīkiti hou, ngā whakautu, me ngā panonitanga tūnga
* Ngā tātauira īmēra ka taea te whakarite
* Ngā whakamōhiotanga ā-tūranga mō ngā kaimahi me ngā kiritaki
* Ngā taurangi tātauira hihiri mō ngā karere kua whakawhaiarotia

**Atanga Kaiwhakahaere Kaha**

* He rārangi tīkiti matatau me te kōmaka me te tātari
* Whakatika tere mō te tūnga, te taumata matua, me ngā tautapanga
* Ngā mahi ā-puranga mō te whakahaere tōtika
* Dashboard tatauranga me ngā inenga mahinga
* He paewhiri tautuhinga whānui

**Kūaha Kiritaki Ātaahua**

* He atanga mā, urupare hoki i runga i ngā pūrere katoa
* Hoahoa pūkoro-tuatahi
* Rapu me te tātari matatau
* He ētita hou me te paeutauta whakahōputu
* Ngā puka tuku tīkiti māmā te whakamahi

**Whakaurunga Hangore**

* Whakamahia ngā shortcode, ngā huānga UI rānei a Ultimate Multisite
* Whakaaturia ngā puka tīkiti me ngā rārangi ki hea rānei i tō pae
* Ka mahi tuitui me te hanganga whatunga multisite
* Ka taea te whakawhānui mā ngā hook me ngā filter

## Tino Pai Mō

* Ngā tūāpapa SaaS e hiahia ana ki te tautoko kiritaki
* Ngā kaiwhakarato WaaS e whakahaere ana i ngā pae kiritaki maha
* Ngā pae mematanga me te tautoko ā-taumata
* Ngā kaiwhakahaere whatunga e whakahaere ana i ngā waitohu maha
* Ngā rangatira pae e whakarato ana i te ratonga kiritaki

## Tāutanga

1. Me whakarite kua tāutatia, kua whakahohehia hoki te monomai Ultimate Multisite ki tō whatunga WordPress
2. Tukuakehia ngā kōnae tāpiritanga ki tō whaiaronga `/wp-content/plugins/`
3. Whakahohe ā-whatungatia te monomai mā te tahua 'Ngā Monomai' i WordPress
4. Whakatere ki Kaiwhakahaere Whatunga > Ngā Tīkiti Tautoko hei whirihora i ō tautuhinga

## Ngā Pātai Auau

## Me whai Ultimate Multisite tēnei?

Āe, me tāuta, me hohe hoki a Ultimate Multisite mō tēnei tāpiritanga. Ka tuitui hōhonu ki ngā āhuatanga whakahaere kiritaki me te whakahaere pae a Ultimate Multisite.

## Ka taea e ngā rangatira pae te whakahaere i tā rātou ake tautoko kiritaki?

Āe rawa! Ka taea e ngā rangatira pae te whakamahi i te pūnaha tīkiti hei whakarato tautoko ki ā rātou ake kiritaki, ā, ka taea e ngā kaiwhakahaere whatunga te whakahaere i ngā tīkiti tautoko puta noa i te whatunga.

## Me pēhea ngā kiritaki e tuku ai i ngā tīkiti?

Ka taea e koe te tāpiri puka tuku tīkiti ki hea rānei i tō pae mā te whakamahi i ngā shortcode, i ngā huānga UI rānei a Ultimate Multisite. Ka whakakī noa ngā kiritaki i te puka, ā, ka tuku i tā rātou tono.

## Ka taea e au te whakarite i ngā tūnga me ngā taumata matua o ngā tīkiti?

Āe! Ka taea e koe te whakarite katoa i ngā taumata matua, ngā tūnga, me ngā momo tīkiti i ngā tautuhinga kaiwhakahaere whatunga kia hāngai ki tō rerengamahi tautoko.

## E tautoko ana i ngā āpitihanga kōnae?

Āe, ka taea e ngā kiritaki me ngā kaimahi te āpiti kōnae ki ngā tīkiti me ngā whakautu. Kei a koe te mana katoa ki ngā momo kōnae e whakaaetia ana me ngā tepe rahi mō te haumarutanga.

## Me pēhea te whakahaere i ngā whakamōhiotanga?

Ka tuku aunoatia e te pūnaha ngā whakamōhiotanga īmēra mō ngā tīkiti hou, ngā whakautu, me ngā panonitanga tūnga. Ka taea e koe te whakarite i ngā tātauira īmēra katoa i ngā tautuhinga.

## Ka taea e au te tautapa tīkiti ki ngā kaiāwhina tautoko motuhake?

Āe, ka taea e koe te tautapa ā-ringa i ngā tīkiti ki ngā kaiāwhina, te whakahohe rānei i te tautapa-aunoa kia tohaina aunoatia ngā tīkiti.

## He tuhipoka tūmataiti anō mō ngā kaimahi?

Āe, ka taea e ngā mema kaimahi te tāpiri whakautu tūmataiti ka kitea anake e ētahi atu mema kaimahi, kāore e kitea e ngā kiritaki.

## He pai mō te pūkoro?

Āe rawa! Kua hangaia te atanga katoa ki te huarahi pūkoro-tuatahi, ā, ka mahi ātaahua i runga i ngā pūrere katoa.

## Ka taea e au te whakaatu i ngā rārangi tīkiti me ngā puka mā te whakamahi i ngā shortcode?

Āe! Ngā shortcode e wātea ana:
* `[wu_support_tickets]` - Whakaatu rārangi tīkiti kiritaki
* `[wu_support_ticket_form]` - Whakaatu puka tuku tīkiti hou
* `[wu_support_ticket_view ticket_id="123"]` - Whakaatu tīkiti motuhake

## He aha ngā momo kōnae ka taea te tukuake?

Ka taea e koe te whirihora i ngā momo kōnae e whakaaetia ana i ngā tautuhinga. Kei roto i te pūnaha he matawai haumarutanga matatau hei tiaki i a koe i ngā kōnae kino.

## Ka taea e au te kaweake raraunga tīkiti?

Āe, kei roto i te tāpiritanga ngā āheinga kaweake mō ngā raraunga tīkiti me ngā tatauranga.
