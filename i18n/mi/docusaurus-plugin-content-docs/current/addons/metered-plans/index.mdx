# Ngā Mahere Ine

Ka panoni te tāpiri Ultimate Multisite: Ngā Mahere Ine i tō āhua nama i ō kiritaki mā te whakauru i ngā āheinga nama ā-ine ngāwari. Kāore i te whakamahi i ngā tepenga mārō e aukati ana i ngā kaiwhakamahi ina hipa, ka taea ngā utu hipanga e nama aunoa ana i ngā kiritaki mō te whakamahinga tāpiri.

## Ngā Āhuatanga Matua {#key-features}

* **Nama Ā-Ine**: Utua ngā kiritaki i runga i te whakamahinga tūturu, kaua i ngā taumata pūmau
* **Whakahaere Hipanga**: Nama aunoa mō te whakamahinga e hipa ana i ngā tepenga mahere
* **Aroturuki Whakamahinga**: Aroturuki i te pau rauemi a te kiritaki i te wā-tūturu
* **Utu Ngāwari**: Tautuhia ngā reiti ritenga mō ngā rauemi rerekē (rokiroki, hōkaiipurangi, pae, aha atu)
* **Nama Token AI**: Aroturuki, nama hoki i ngā token tāuru me ngā token tāputa mō te whakamahinga AI puta noa i ngā pae-iti a te kiritaki
* **Nama Aunoa**: Waihanga nama aunoa ina hipa ngā paepae whakamahinga
* **Pūrongo Whakamahinga**: Ngā tātaritanga whakamahinga taipitopito me te pūrongo mā ngā kiritaki me ngā kaiwhakahaere

## He Tino Pai Mō {#perfect-for}

* **Ngā Kaiwhakarato Manaaki**: Tukuna ngā mahere whakawhānui ka tipu tahi me ngā hiahia o te kiritaki
* **Ngā Pae SaaS**: Nama i runga i te pau rauemi tūturu
* **Ngā Whatunga Pokapū**: Ngā tauira utu ngāwari mō ngā kaupapa kiritaki
* **Manaaki Kaihoko Anō**: Whakaahei i ngā hoa kia tuku utu e hāngai ana ki te whakamahinga

## Me Pēhea Tana Mahi {#how-it-works}

1. Whirihorahia ngā rauemi ā-ine (mokowā kōpae, hōkaiipurangi, maha o ngā pae, aha atu)
2. Tautuhia ngā tepenga mahere taketake me ngā reiti hipanga
3. Ka aroturuki aunoa te pūnaha i te whakamahinga puta noa i tō whatunga
4. Ina hipa ngā kiritaki i ngā tepenga, ka tātaihia ngā utu hipanga
5. Ka waihangatia aunoatia ngā nama i runga i te huringa nama
6. Ka taea e ngā kiritaki te tiro i tō rātou whakamahinga me ngā utu e haere ake ana i te wā-tūturu

## Nama Token AI {#ai-token-billing}

Ka tāpiri te putanga 1.1.0 i te nama token AI mō ngā whatunga e tuku ana i ngā āhuatanga AI mā te WordPress AI Client SDK. Ka whakarongo a Ngā Mahere Ine ki ngā tono whakaputa AI kua oti, ka tuhi i ngā tatauranga token tāuru me te token tāputa mō te pae-iti o nāianei, ā, ka huri i aua token hei whakapau AI ka taea te nama mō te wā nama o nāianei a te kiritaki.

Whakamahia te nama token AI ina hiahia koe ki te tuku taputapu AI ki ngā pae kiritaki me te kore e horomia ngā utu tauira kāore e matapaetia. Ka tautapahia ia tono ki te pae-iti, kiritaki, mematanga, kaiwhakarato, me te tauira kia taea e ngā kaiwhakahaere te arotake i te whakamahinga, ā, ka kite ngā kiritaki i te pānga o tō rātou pau AI ki tō rātou pūkete.

### Whirihorahia ngā Reiti Ia-Token {#configure-per-token-rates}

Ka whirihorahia ngā reiti AI ia tauira hei reiti tāuru me te reiti tāputa motuhake. Ka tātaihia ngā reiti ia 1 miriona token, e ōrite ana ki ngā whārangi utu noa a ngā kaiwhakarato AI.

1. I te kaiwhakahaere whatunga, whakatuwheratia **Ultimate Multisite → Settings → Ngā Mahere Ine**.
2. Whakahohea te ine whakapau AI mō te hua, mematanga rānei me nama i te whakamahinga AI.
3. Tāpirihia ngā tāurunga reiti tauira mā te whakamahi i ngā ID tauira ka whakahokia e tō tūhono, pērā i `gpt-4o`, `gpt-4o-mini`, tētahi atu tautuhi tauira hototahi-OpenAI rānei.
4. Tāuruhia ngā uara **Reiti Tāuru** me te **Reiti Tāputa** motuhake ki te moni o tō pae ia 1 miriona token.
5. Tautuhia te tahua whakapau AI kua whakaurua mō te kiritaki me te whanonga hipanga mō te mahere.

Ina kāore tētahi ID tauira tōtika i whirihorahia, ka taea e Ngā Mahere Ine te taurite mā te kuhimua tauira. Hei tauira, ka taea tētahi reiti kua whirihorahia mō `claude-sonnet-4` te hāngai ki ngā ID tauira whai-wātohu e tīmata ana ki taua kuhimua. Ka taea hoki e ngā kaiwhakawhanake te whakatika i te ripanga reiti whakamutunga mā te tātari [`wu_ai_token_rates`](./hooks/Filters/wu_ai_token_rates).

### Ngā Tūhono AI e Tautokona Ana {#supported-ai-connectors}

Ka mahi te nama token AI me ngā tūhono e whakaputa ana i ngā takahanga whakaputa WordPress AI Client SDK, ā, e whakauru ana i te whakamahinga token ki ngā metadata whakautu. Kei roto tēnei ko te whānau tūhono AI a Ultimate Multisite kua tuhia ki konei:

* [Ultimate AI Connector mō ngā Pito-mutunga Hototahi](../ultimate-ai-connector-compatible-endpoints/) mō ngā API hototahi-OpenAI, tae atu ki OpenAI, Mistral, ngā kūaha hototahi-Ollama, me ētahi atu kaiwhakarato e whakaatu ana i ngā metadata whakamahinga token hototahi
* [Ultimate AI Connector mō WebLLM](../ultimate-ai-connector-webllm/) mō te whakamahinga WebLLM taketake-pūtirotiro ina tukuna ngā tono mā te rerenga takahanga AI Client SDK
* Ngā tūhono kaiwhakarato AI e whakamahia ana e [Gratis AI Agent](../gratis-ai-agent/) ina pūrongo te kaiwhakarato kua tīpakohia i ngā tatauranga token akiaki me te token whakaoti mā te SDK

Ka taea e ngā tūhono ritenga te whai wāhi mā te whakakā i ngā takahanga paerewa `wp_ai_client_before_generate_result` me `wp_ai_client_after_generate_result` me ngā metadata tauira, kaiwhakarato, me te whakamahinga token. Ka whakatinanahia ngā tautuhinga tūhono mai i te pae matua ki ngā pae-iti kia whakamahia e ngā pae kiritaki ngā kaiwhakarato me ngā tepenga kua whakaaetia e te whatunga.

### Whakamahinga Account Kiritaki {#customer-account-usage}

Ka kite ngā kiritaki i te whakamahinga AI i te taha o ērā atu rauemi ā-ine i tō rātou wāhi Account. Ka whakaatu te rārangi whakapau AI i te tapeke o te wā nama o nāianei i takea mai i ngā token akiaki me ngā token whakaoti kua tuhia, ngā reiti tauira kua whirihorahia, me tētahi tahua kua whakaurua ki te mahere.

Mēnā he tikanga utu tō tētahi kiritaki kei te kōnae, ka whakatauria te whakamahinga i runga ake i te tahua kua whakaurua hei hipanga ka taea te nama. Mēnā kua whirihorahia te mahere mō tētahi tapahanga mārō, ā, kāore he tikanga utu e wātea ana ki te kiritaki, ka taea te aukati i ngā tono AI ina tae ki te tahua, ā, ka tonoa te kiritaki kia tāpiri taipitopito utu i mua i te haere tonu.

## Ngā Rauemi e Tautokona Ana {#supported-resources}

* Rokiroki kōpae
* Hōkaiipurangi/Whakawhiti raraunga
* Maha o ngā pae
* Maha o ngā kaiwhakamahi
* Whakamahinga token AI me te whakapau AI
* Ngā momo rauemi ritenga

## Ngā Whakaritenga {#requirements}

* Multisite Ultimate 2.0.0, teitei ake rānei
* Tāutanga WordPress Multisite
* PHP 7.4, teitei ake rānei

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Tukuake ngā kōnae plugin ki `/wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite-metered-plans`
2. Whakahohea te plugin mā te mata 'Plugins' i WordPress
3. Whakatere ki Multisite Ultimate → Settings → Ngā Mahere Ine
4. Whirihorahia ō rauemi ā-ine me te utu
5. Whakahohea te nama ā-ine mō ngā hua i te ētita hua

## Ngā Pātai Auau {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Ka aha ina hipa tētahi kiritaki i tōna tepenga? {#what-happens-when-a-customer-exceeds-their-limit}

Engari i te aukati urunga, ka aroturuki te pūnaha i te nui-tūwhena, ā, ka tāpiri atu ki tā rātou nama panuku i runga i ngā reiti kua whirihoratia e koe.

## Ka taea e au te tautuhi reiti nui-tūwhena rerekē mō ngā hua rerekē? {#can-i-set-different-overage-rates-for-different-products}

Āe, ka taea e ia hua ōna ake rauemi ine me ōna ake utu nui-tūwhena.

## Me pēhea te whakamōhio i ngā kiritaki mō te whakamahinga? {#how-are-customers-notified-about-usage}

Ka taea e ngā kiritaki te tiro i tā rātou whakamahinga o nāianei i roto i tā rātou Dashboard, ā, ka whiwhi whakamōhiotanga kōwhiringa ina tata ki ngā rohe, ina hipa rānei i aua rohe.

## Me pēhea te whakatau utu mō te whakamahinga token AI? {#how-is-ai-token-usage-priced}

Ka whakatau utu te whakamahinga token AI mai i ngā reiti tāuru me ngā reiti putanga kua whirihoratia mō te tauira, ia 1 miriona token. Ka whakarea e ngā Mahere Ine ngā tatauranga token akiaki me te token whakatutuki o ia tono ki aua reiti, ka tāpiri i te hua ki te whakapau AI o te wā-nama o nāianei a te kiritaki, ā, ka whakamahi i te tukunga, i ngā ture nui-tūwhena rānei o te mahere.

## Ka taea tonu e au te whakamahi rohe mārō? {#can-i-still-use-hard-limits}

Āe, he kōwhiringa te nama ine mō ia hua. Ka taea e koe te whakaranu i ngā hua rohe mārō tuku iho ki ngā hua ine.
