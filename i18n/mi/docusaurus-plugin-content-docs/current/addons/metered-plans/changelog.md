# Rangitaki Panonitanga mō ngā Mahere Ine

Putanga 1.1.0 - I tukuna i te 2026-05-05
- Hou: nama tohu AI mō ngā pae-iti multisite — aroturuki, nama hoki i te whakamahinga tohu AI puta noa i ngā pae kiritaki me ngā reiti ia-tohu ka taea te whirihora
- Hou: kua hanga anō te whakaū tūhono me te kitenga rohenga hihiri me te tuhi-tika, kia whakaritea ai te tika wā-tūturu puta noa i ngā tūhono katoa
- Whakatika: kua whakahāngai tika ināianei ngā whakahoutanga ripanga pātengi raraunga ki ngā tautuhinga aronuinga BerlinDB, e ārai ana i ngā rahunga whakahou i ngā tāutanga hou
- Whakatika: kua hurihia ngā callback whakahou pātengi raraunga ki te hōputu tika, e whakatika ana i ngā rahunga whakahou wahangū
- Whakatika: kua whakaaetia ināianei ngā uara hautanga ki te āpure tāuru AI Usage Overage Markup
- Whakatika: kua whakatauria ngā hapa fatal me ngā take tīmatanga-rua i te utanga o te monomai
- Kua whakapai ake: kua tāpirihia te hōtuhi npm check-env kia whirihora-aunoa ai ngā taiao kaiwhakawhanake i te whakahaerenga tuatahi
