# Ngā Āpiha Tautoko {#support-agents}

Ehara te whakahaere i tētahi whatunga katoa māu anake i te mahi ngāwari. I a tō whatunga e tīmata ana ki te tipu, ka tīmata koe ki te kite i te piki haere o te tono tautoko. He whakaaro pai kia whakauru mai i ētahi tāngata hei āwhina i a koe ki te whakamāmā i tō kawenga mahi. Heoi anō, he tūraru te tuku urunga/whakaaetanga katoa, ā, tērā pea ka whakaraerae i tō whatunga. Ko reira uru mai ai tēnei tāpiritanga Āpiha Tautoko.

Mā tēnei tāpiritanga koe e āhei ai ki te waihanga i tētahi momo kaiwhakamahi WordPress hou e kīia ana ko te Āpiha Tautoko, ā, ka hoatu ki a koe te mana katoa ki runga i ngā whakaaetanga kei taua kaiwhakamahi. He māmā te tuku whakaaetanga, pērā i te whakakā i tētahi pātene takahuri, ā, me whakaweto noa mēnā ka whakatau koe ki te tango atu.

## Ngā Āhuatanga {#features}

- Rārangi tūemi o ngā whakaaetanga WordPress me Ultimate Multisite
- Whakahohe, monokia hoki te urunga me te ngāwari
- Waihangatia he maha mutunga kore o ngā pūkete Āpiha

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Tukuake 'ultimate-multisite-support-agents' ki te whaiaronga '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Whakahohe i te mono mā te tahua 'Ngā Mono' i WordPress
3. Whāia te Wizard ā-taahiraa ki te whakarite i te mono
