# Site Exporter {#site-exporter}

## Ihirangi {#content}

E mārama ana mātou ki te hiahia kia taea te nuku i ngā pae-iti i roto i tō whatunga ki tētahi tāutanga pae kotahi, ā, me te hoki anō, mēnā ka whakatau tō kiritaki ki te neke i tōna paetukutuku. Mā te tāpiritanga kaweake me te kawemai pae e māmā ake ai tēnei mahi. Ka kaweake, ka kawemai hoki tēnei taputapu i ngā pae mā ngā kōnae zip, ā, ka taea hoki ēnei te noho hei tārua haumaru.

## Āhuatanga {#features}

* Kaweake me te Kawemai Pae

* Atanga ngāwari te whakamahi

* Waihanga tārua haumaru pae

## Tuhinga āwhina {#documentation}
**Me pēhea te Kaweake me te Kawemai i tētahi Pae**

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Tukuna ake **ultimate-multisite-site-exporter** ki te whaiaronga **/wp-content/plugins/**
2. Whakahohehia te monomai mā te tahua **Monomai** i WordPress
3. Whāia te Ruānuku, taahiraa i ia taahiraa, hei whakarite i te monomai

## Aratohu Whakamahi {#usage-guide}

Mā Ultimate Multisite ka taea e koe te kaweake i tētahi pae mai i tō whatunga, te kawemai rānei i tētahi pae ki tō tāutanga pae-maha, pae kotahi rānei.

Hei tīmata i te tukanga, haere ki tā mātou whārangi tāpiritanga, ā, tāutahia te tāpiritanga Ultimate Multisite: Site Exporter & Importer.![Whārangi tāpiritanga](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

![Whārangi tāpiritanga me te kōwhiringa tāuta](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

Haere ki tō whārangi Monomai, ā, pāwhiritia kia **Whakahohe Whatunga** i te tāpiritanga kātahi anō koe ka tāuta.<!-- Kāore te whakaahuamata i te wātea: whārangi Monomai WP e whakaatu ana i te pātene Whakahohe Whatunga mō te tāpiritanga Site Exporter -->

Ka kitea te kōwhiringa **Kaweake & Kawemai** i te tahua Ultimate Multisite, kei te taha mauī.

## Me pēhea te kaweake i tētahi pae {#how-to-export-a-site}

Hei kaweake i tētahi pae mai i tō whatunga, haere ki **Kaweake & Kawemai** i te tahua Ultimate Multisite, ā, i te ripa **Kaweake Pae**, pāwhiritia kia **Hangaia he Kaweake Pae hou**.

<!-- Kāore te whakaahuamata i te wātea: ripa Kaweake Pae me te pātene Hangaia he Kaweake Pae hou -->

Rapua te pae e hiahia ana koe ki te kaweake, ā, tīpakohia ngā tūemi e hiahia ana koe kia whakaurua ki te kōnae kua kaweakea. Ka taea e koe te whakauru kaupapa, monomai, me ngā tukunga ake. Mēnā he nui rawa tō pae, ka taea e koe te kōwhiri kia whakahaerehia te tukanga kaweake i muri.<!-- Kāore te whakaahuamata i te wātea: kōwhiringa kaweake pae me te rapu pae, ngā pouakataki whakauru kaupapa/monomai/tukunga ake -->

Pāwhiritia te pātene **Kaweake Pae**. Ka wātea tō pae kua kaweakea i te rārangi **Ngā Kaweake o Nāianei**. Pāwhiritia te pātene **Tikiake ZIP** hei tikiake i te kōnae. Kei roto i ngā ZIP kaweake tētahi `index.php` whakaoho-whaiaro me `readme.txt`, nō reira ka taea te tāuta ki tētahi kaiwhakahaere hou me te kore e tāuta tuatahi i tētahi monomai kawemai motuhake.<!-- Kāore te whakaahuamata i te wātea: rārangi Ngā Kaweake o Nāianei me te pātene Tikiake ZIP -->

## Me pēhea te kaweake i te whatunga katoa {#how-to-export-the-whole-network}

Hei kaweake ngātahi i ngā pae-iti katoa, haere ki **Kaweake & Kawemai** i te tahua Ultimate Multisite, ā, whakamahia te kōwhiringa **Kaweake Whatunga** i te mata kaweake. Kei roto i te pūranga i hangaia ngā pae-iti katoa i tētahi ZIP kotahi mō ngā nekehanga kaiwhakahaere, ngā hanga anō atamira, ngā tārua haumaru taumata-whatunga rānei.

Ka nui pea ngā kaweake whatunga. Whakahaerehia i ngā wā he iti te waka, ā, whakaūngia he nui te rokiroki a te tūmau mō te pūranga me ngā tukunga ake, monomai, me ngā kaupapa ka whakaurua.

## Me pēhea te kawemai i tētahi pae {#how-to-import-a-site}

Hei kawemai i tētahi pae, haere ki te ripa **Kawemai i tētahi Pae**, ā, tukuna ake te kōnae .zip o te pae e hiahia ana koe ki te kawemai. Ka taea e koe te whakarerekē i te URL ki tētahi mea hou, te pupuri rānei i te URL tawhito mā te waiho kia wātea te āputa. Waihoki, ka taea e koe te tohu i te pouaka hei muku i te kōnae .zip i tō tāutanga i muri i te kawemai. Kātahi ka pāwhiri kia **Tīmata Kawemai**.

<!-- Kāore te whakaahuamata i te wātea: ripa Kawemai i tētahi Pae me te āpure tukuake zip me te pātene Tīmata Kawemai -->

Ka roa paku pea te tukanga. Ka kite koe i tētahi kawemai e tārewa ana i te rārangi i raro iho i te kaiwhakawemai.

<!-- Kāore te whakaahuamata i te wātea: kawemai e tārewa ana i te rārangi i raro iho i te kaiwhakawemai -->

Ka taea e koe te whakamutu i te tukanga mā te pāwhiri **Whakakore Kawemai**.

<!-- Kāore te whakaahuamata i te wātea: pātene Whakakore Kawemai hei whakamutu i te tukanga kawemai -->

Ina oti te tukanga, kua wātea kē tō pae i tō whatunga.

## Me pēhea te kawemai i tētahi pae ki tētahi tāutanga kotahi {#how-to-import-a-site-on-a-single-install}

Hei kawemai i tētahi pae ki tō tāutanga kotahi, tuatahi tāutahia, whakahohehia hoki te kaiwhakawemai ki tō pae. Ka kitea e koe te hono hei tikiake i a ia i te ripa kaiwhakakaweake.

<!-- Kāore te whakaahuamata i te wātea: ripa kaiwhakakaweake me te hono hei tikiake i te kaiwhakawemai pae-kotahi -->

Ka kitea e koe te kaiwhakawemai i te tahua **Taputapu**, kei te taha mauī, i te tūemi-iti **Kaweake & Kawemai**.

<!-- Kāore te whakaahuamata i te wātea: tahua Taputapu pae-kotahi e whakaatu ana i te tahua-iti Kaweake me te Kawemai -->

Tukuna ake te kōnae .zip o te pae e hiahia ana koe ki te kawemai. Ka taea e koe te whakarerekē i te URL ki tētahi mea hou, te pupuri rānei i te URL tawhito mā te waiho kia wātea te āputa. Waihoki, ka taea e koe te tohu i te pouaka hei muku i te kōnae .zip i tō tāutanga i muri i te kawemai. Kātahi ka pāwhiri kia **Tīmata Kawemai**.

<!-- Kāore te whakaahuamata i te wātea: puka kawemai pae-kotahi me te tukuake zip me te pātene Tīmata Kawemai -->

Ka roa paku pea te tukanga. Ka kite koe i tētahi kawemai e tārewa ana i te rārangi i raro iho i te kaiwhakawemai.

<!-- Kāore te whakaahuamata i te wātea: kawemai pae-kotahi e tārewa ana i te rārangi -->

Ka taea e koe te whakamutu i te tukanga mā te pāwhiri **Whakakore Kawemai**.

<!-- Kāore te whakaahuamata i te wātea: pātene Whakakore Kawemai hei whakamutu i te tukanga kawemai -->

I muri i te otinga o te tukanga, ka whakakapia tō pae e te pae kātahi anō koe ka kawemai.
