# Mahi: wu_page_added

Tukua ngā kaiwhakawhanake monomai kia whakahaere i ētahi atu mea ina rēhitatia ngā whārangi.

He rerekē i te wu_page_load, e rere ana anake ina tirohia tētahi whārangi motuhake; ka rere tēnei hook i te rēhitatanga mō ia whārangi whakahaere e tāpirihia ana mā te whakamahi i te waehere WP Ultimo.

## Ngā tawhā {#parameters}

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $page_id | `string\|\WP_Ultimo_Site_Exporter\Admin_Pages\Base_Admin_Page` | Te ID o tēnei whārangi. |
| $page_hook | `string\|\WP_Ultimo_Site_Exporter\Admin_Pages\Base_Admin_Page` | Te hook i hangaia mō tēnei whārangi. |
