# Tukutahi Ihirangi

Multisite Ultimate: Tukutahi Ihirangi

Mā tēnei tāpiritanga mō Multisite Ultimate ka āhei ngā kaiwhakahaere whatunga ki te tukutahi ihirangi mai i tētahi pae ki ētahi atu pae i te whatunga. Ka tautoko i ngā momo whakairinga katoa, tae atu ki ngā whakairinga, ngā whārangi, ngā momo whakairinga ritenga, me ngā tauira Elementor. Kei roto i te tāpiritanga tētahi atanga ruānuku hikoinga-maha mō te tīpako ihirangi me ngā pae ūnga, me te tukatuka ā-pūhui me ngā urupare ahunga whakamua wā-tūturu.

## Ngā Āhuatanga {#features}

* Tīpakonga Ihirangi: He tukutuku kāri ā-ataata me ngā karakōnui me te tātari momo whakairinga
* Ngā Momo Whakairinga Katoa: Tukutahi i ngā whakairinga, ngā whārangi, ngā hua, ngā tauira Elementor, me tētahi momo whakairinga ritenga
* Tīpakonga Pae: Tīpakohia ngā pae hei tukutahi ihirangi ki reira, me te aukati i te pae pūtake me te tīpako-a-mua i te pae tamaiti
* Tukatuka ā-Pūhui: Ka tukutahi ki ngā pae 10 i te wā kotahi me ngā whakahōu ahunga whakamua
* Tukutahi Pāpāho: Ka tārua, ka mahere anō aunoa i ngā kōnae pāpāho (ngā atahanga, ngā ataata) mai i te ihirangi whakairinga ki ngā pae ūnga
* Tukutahi Tākeherenga: Ka pupuri i ngā kāwai, ngā tūtohu, me ngā tākeherenga ritenga
* Tautoko Elementor: He tautoko katoa mō ngā tauira Elementor me te hanga anō i te CSS me te mahere anō i ngā ID tauira
* Whakatau Papā: He kōwhiringa ki te whakakapi i te ihirangi onāianei, ki te hipa rānei
* Whakahaere Keteroki: Ka whakawātea aunoa i te keteroki Elementor ki ngā pae ūnga i muri i te tukutahi

## Ngā Whakaritenga {#requirements}

* WordPress 5.3, teitei ake rānei
* PHP 7.4, teitei ake rānei
* Multisite Ultimate plugin (kua whakahohea)
* Elementor plugin (kōwhiringa, e hiahiatia ana mō te tukutahi tauira Elementor)

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Tukuatu i te 'ultimate-multisite-content-sync' ki te whaiaronga '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Whakahohea te plugin mā te tahua 'Ngā Plugin' i tō WordPress Kaiwhakahaere Whatunga
3. Whirihorahia ngā tautuhinga ki Ultimate Multisite → Ngā Tautuhinga → Tukutahi Ihirangi
4. Whakatere ki Ultimate Multisite → Tukutahi Ihirangi kia tīmata ai te tukutahi
