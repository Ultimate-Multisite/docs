# Rangitaki Panonitanga Tukutahi Ihirangi {#content-sync-changelog}

### Putanga 1.1.0 - I tukuna i te 2025-12-31 {#version-110---released-on-2025-12-31}
* Hou: Tautoko mō te tukutahi i ngā momo whakairinga katoa (ngā whakairinga, ngā whārangi, ngā momo whakairinga ritenga)
* Hou: Tātari momo whakairinga i te taahiraa kōwhiri ihirangi
* Hou: Taahiraa kōwhiri pae pūtake - tukutahi mai i tētahi pae ahakoa ko tēhea, kaua ko te pae matua anake
* Hou: Kōwhiringa tōmua pae tamaiti mō ngā pae tātauira
* Hou: Tangohanga pāpāho mai i post_content mō ngā momo whakairinga katoa
* Hou: Tukutahi whakarōpūtanga (ngā kāwai, ngā tūtohu, ngā whakarōpūtanga ritenga)
* Kua panoni: Kua whakaingoatia anō mai i "Elementor Template Sync" ki "Tukutahi Ihirangi"
* Kua panoni: Kua whakaingoatia anō te taahiraa "Kōwhiri Tātauira" ki "Kōwhiri Raraunga"
* Kua whakapai ake: Ka rere anake te waehere motuhake ki Elementor mō te momo whakairinga elementor_library ināianei

### Putanga 1.0.0 - I tukuna i te 2025-12-19 {#version-100---released-on-2025-12-19}
* Tukunga tuatahi
* Ruānuku taahiraa-maha mō te kōwhiri tātauira me te pae
* Tukatuka ā-rōpū me ngā urupare ahunga wā-tūturu
* Tukutahi pāpāho katoa me te mahere anō ID aunoa
* Kōmitimiti tautuhinga ki Ultimate Multisite
* Tautoko whakakore pae mā ngā tautuhinga whatunga
* Whakawātea keteroki Elementor i muri i te tukutahi
* Tiaki whakarōpūtanga tātauira
