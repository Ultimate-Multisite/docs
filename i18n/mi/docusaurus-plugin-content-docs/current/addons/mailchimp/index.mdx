# Mailchimp

Multisite Ultimate: Tuitui Mailchimp

Tuituia tō whatunga Multisite Ultimate ki tō Mailchimp. Ka taea e koe te hono i ētahi mahere ki ngā Rārangi Mailchimp, ā, te tāpiri aunoa i ngā kaiohauru ki aua rārangi i te rēhitatanga.

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Tukuake 'ultimate-multisite-mailchimp' ki te whaiaronga '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Whakahohe i te mono mā te tahua 'Ngā Mono' i tō WordPress Whakahaere Whatunga

## Aratohu Whakamahi {#usage-guide}

He tino māmā te tukutahi i ngā īmēra a ngā kaiohauru ki ō rārangi MailChimp mā tā mātou tāpiri-ake Ultimate Multisite: Tuitui MailChimp. Ko te whāinga o tēnei akoranga he āwhina i a koe kia whakaritea katoatia ngā mea!

## Te tiki i tētahi kī API {#getting-an-api-key}

I mua i te tīmata ki te kuhu ki ngā tautuhinga rerekē e wātea ana, me whai tātou i tētahi kī API MailChimp. Mā tēnei e āhei ai a Ultimate Multisite ki te kōrero ki te API MailChimp hei tiki rārangi, rōpū, me te tāpiri wāhitau īmēra ki aua rārangi me aua rōpū ina rēhita ngā kaiwhakamahi.

Whaia tēnei akoranga kia whiwhi koe i tō ake kī API MailChimp: [Mō ngā Kī API | MailChimp](https://kb.mailchimp.com/integrations/api-integrations/about-api-keys)

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: MailChimp API key settings in Ultimate Multisite Add-on Settings -->

_Tāpirihia tō kī API MailChimp ki_ _**Ngā Tautuhinga Ultimate Multisite - > Ngā Tautuhinga Tāpiri-ake -> MailChimp**_

## Aratau Tuitui {#integration-mode}

E rua ngā “aratau tuitui” ka tukuna e te tāpiri-ake Tuitui MailChimp, arā, e rua ngā huarahi rerekē e taea ai e koe te whakamahi i te tāpiri-ake hei tukutahi i ngā wāhitau īmēra.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: MailChimp integration mode selection with Multiple Lists and Single List options -->

_E rua ngā aratau tuitui mō te ngāwari ake!_

### Aratau 1: Ngā Rārangi Maha {#mode-1-multiple-lists}

Ka whakamahia e te aratau taunoa ngā **Rārangi Maha**. Mā tēnei aratau e āhei ai koe ki te tīpako i ngā rārangi MailChimp maha mō ia mahere. Koinei te kōwhiringa pai rawa mēnā ka wehewehea e koe ō kaiwhakamahi ki ngā rārangi rerekē.

Hei tauira, me kī e 3 ō rārangi rerekē i tō pūkete MailChimp: **Pānui Kōrero** , **Mahere A** , me **Mahere B**. E hiahia ana koe kia whakaurua ngā kaiwhakamahi katoa, ahakoa tō rātou mahere, ki te rārangi Pānui Kōrero hoki, kia kore ai rātou e mahue i ō whakahōunga.

Hei whakatutuki i tērā, ka taea e koe te whakamahi i te aratau Rārangi Maha hei tāpiri i ngā rārangi e rua, **Pānui Kōrero** me **Mahere A**, ki ngā Tautuhinga MailChimp Mahere A, ā, **Pānui Kōrero** me **Mahere B** ki ngā Tautuhinga MailChimp Mahere B.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: MailChimp default lists selection for new accounts and canceled accounts -->

_Tīpakohia ngā rārangi taunoa mō ngā pūkete hōu me ngā pūkete kua whakakorea_

### Aratau 2: Rārangi Kotahi + Ngā Rōpū Maha {#mode-2-single-list--multiple-groups}

Ka whakaaetia koe e MailChimp kia wehewehe i tō rārangi ki ngā rōpū kaiwhakamahi iti ake e kīia ana ko **Ngā Rōpū**. Mā tēnei ka nui ake tō mana whakahaere i te mea ka taea e koe te tuku kaupapa whakatairanga ki tētahi wāhanga motuhake o ō kaiohauru me te kore e waihanga tārua maha o taua kaupapa whakatairanga anō i MailChimp (he mea ka uaua ai te tātari i ngā inenga o taua kaupapa whakatairanga ā muri ake).

Hei tauira, ka taea e koe tētahi rārangi kotahi e kīia ana ko **Ōku Kaiohauru Whatunga,** ā, kei roto he rōpū mō **Mahere A** me **Mahere B**.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: MailChimp single list and groups selection for default lists -->

_Tīpakohia ngā rārangi me ngā rōpū taunoa, mēnā e whakamahi ana i te aratau tuitui “rārangi kotahi + ngā rōpū maha”_

## Ngā Rārangi me ngā Rōpū Taunoa {#default-lists-and-groups}

Whai muri i tō tīpako ko tēhea aratau tuitui te mea whai take ake mō tō horopaki whakamahinga ake, kua tae ki te wā ki te tīpako i ngā rārangi taunoa (me ngā rōpū, i runga i te aratau). Ka whakamahia te rārangi taunoa ina kāore he Rārangi motuhake kua tīpakohia mō tētahi mahere kua tohua.

Ka taea hoki e koe te tīpako i te rārangi taunoa mō ngā pūkete kua whakakorea. Ina whakamutua e tētahi kaiwhakamahi tana ohaurunga, ā, ka tango i tana pūkete i tō whatunga, ka nukuhia tana wāhitau īmēra ki te rārangi kua whakaritea mō ngā pūkete kua whakakorea.

## Te Whakahohe i te Ohaurunga Takirua {#enabling-double-subscription}

I taua whārangi tautuhinga anō, ka taea e koe te whakahohe i te **Ohaurunga Takirua**. Mā tēnei kōwhiringa e tāpiri te kaiwhakamahi ki te rārangi taunoa kua tīpakohia, Ā, ki te rārangi kua tīpakohia mō te mahere a taua kaiwhakamahi. Mēnā kāore tēnei kōwhiringa i te whakahohea, ka tāpirihia te kaiwhakamahi ki te rārangi kua tīpakohia mō te mahere a te kaiwhakamahi anake. Mēnā kāore tētahi i whakaritea mō taua mahere motuhake, kātahi ka tāpirihia te kaiwhakamahi ki te rārangi taunoa kua tīpakohia.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: MailChimp double subscription toggle setting -->

_Ka taea e koe ō kaiwhakamahi te ohauru ki te rārangi taunoa me te rārangi a te mahere i te rēhitatanga_

## Te Tīpako Rārangi me ngā Rōpū mō ia Mahere {#selecting-lists-and-groups-for-each-of-the-plans}

Haere ki ō **Mahere** ka tīpako i te mahere e hiahia ana koe ki te whakatika. Kia mōhio, i te pouaka-meta **Kōwhiringa Arā Atu Anō**, ka wātea mai tētahi ripa hōu e kīia ana ko MailChimp.

I taua ripa, ka taea e koe te tīpako i ngā rārangi maha, i tētahi rārangi kotahi rānei me ngā rōpū maha (i runga i te aratau tuitui) mō taua mahere.

<!-- Screenshot unavailable: MailChimp tab on plan edit page showing lists and groups selection -->

_Tīpakohia ngā Rārangi me ngā Rōpū e hiahia ana koe ki te whakamahi mō ia o ō mahere_

Ina rēhita mai tētahi kaiwhakamahi hōu mō taua mahere, ka tāpirihia tana wāhitau īmēra ki ngā rārangi me ngā rōpū kua tīpakohia!
