# Whatunga-Maha {#multi-network}

Ka whakawhānui te tāpiri Ultimate Multisite: Whatunga-maha i tō tūāpapa Ultimate Multisite ki te āheinga whatunga-maha kaha. Mā tēnei tāpiri e āhei ai ō kiritaki ki te hoko me te whakahaere i ngā whatunga WordPress multisite katoa, kaua ko ngā pae takitahi anake.

## Ngā Āhuatanga Matua {#key-features}

* **Whakaratonga Whatunga**: Waihanga aunoa, whirihora hoki i ngā whatunga WordPress multisite oti mō ō kiritaki
* **Ngā Tātauira Whatunga**: Tukuna ngā kiritaki kia tirotiro, kia arokite, kia kōwhiri hoki i tētahi tātauira whatunga kua hangaia kē i mua i te rēhitatanga
* **Whakahaere Whatunga**: Ngā āheinga whakahaere katoa mō ngā taiao whatunga-maha
* **Ngā Whatunga Wehe**: Ka whakahaere motuhake ia whatunga kiritaki me āna ake ripanga pātengi raraunga me tana whirihoranga
* **SSO Whakawhiti-Whatunga**: Hoatu ki ngā kiritaki he urunga maheni ki ā rātou whatunga-iti mā te whakamotuhēhēnga hononga-mākutu
* **Hoahoanga Taea te Whakawhānui**: Whakahaere tōtika i ngā whatunga maha mā te tohatoha rauemi kua arotauhia
* **Tuitui**: Ka tuitui maheni ki ngā pūnaha ohaurunga me te nama a Ultimate Multisite

## Ngā Whakamahinga {#use-cases}

* **Manaaki Tapanga Mā**: Whakarato ki ngā kiritaki ā rātou ake whatunga multisite whai waitohu
* **Rongoā Umanga**: Whakaahei i ngā umanga ki te whakahaere i ngā whatunga kiritaki maha mai i tētahi tūāpapa kotahi
* **Whakatinanatanga Hinonga**: Tautoko i ngā whakahaere nui me ngā whakaritenga whatunga-maha matatini
* **Tūāpapa Mātauranga**: Waihanga whatunga motuhake mō ngā kura, tari rānei rerekē

## Ngā Whakaritenga {#requirements}

* Ultimate Multisite 2.0.0, teitei ake rānei
* Tāutanga WordPress Multisite
* PHP 7.4, teitei ake rānei
* MySQL 5.6, teitei ake rānei

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Tukuatu ngā kōnae mono ki te whaiaronga `/wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite-multinetwork`
2. Whakahohe i te mono mā te mata 'Plugins' i WordPress
3. Whirihora i ngā tautuhinga whatunga-maha mā Ultimate Multisite → Settings

## Ngā Tātauira Whatunga {#network-templates}

Ka tāpiri te putanga 1.3.0 i tētahi rerenga kimi me te kōwhiri tātauira mō ngā kiritaki e hoko ana i ngā whatunga hou. Whakamahia ngā tātauira whatunga ina hiahia koe kia tīmata ia whatunga kiritaki hou mai i tētahi hanganga kua whakaritea, hoahoa, whare pukapuka ihirangi, tautuhinga rānei mō tētahi rāngai motuhake.

### Kaitiro arokite tātauira {#template-previewer}

Mā te kaitiro arokite tātauira whatunga e āhei ai ngā kiritaki ki te tirotiro i ngā tātauira e wātea ana i mua i te whakaoti hoko. Ka taea ia tātauira te whakatuwhera ki tētahi paewhiri arokite ora, kia taea ai e ngā kiritaki te whakataurite i ngā tahora me ngā ihirangi i mua i tā rātou hoko whatunga.

Whakamahia te kaitiro arokite hei āwhina i ngā kiritaki ki te:

* Arotake i te āhua me te hanganga o ia tātauira whatunga e wātea ana
* Whakataurite i ngā tātauira maha me te kore e wehe i te rerenga hoko
* Kōwhiri i te tātauira e tino hāngai ana ki tā rātou whakamahinga i mua i te rēhitatanga

Kia pai ki te kiritaki ngā ingoa me ngā whakaahuatanga tātauira. Ka tino whaihua te kaitiro arokite ina mārama ngā kiritaki ki te whakamahinga kua whakaarotia, pēnei i ngā whatunga umanga, ngā whatunga kura, ngā whatunga pānati, ngā kohinga manaaki tapanga-mā rānei.

### Kaitīpako tātauira i te whakaoti hoko {#template-picker-at-checkout}

Ka taea te tāpiri i te kaitīpako tātauira ki te rerenga whakaoti hoko rēhita hei āpure. Ina whirihorahia, ka tīpako ngā kiritaki i te tātauira whatunga e hiahia ana rātou i te wā o te whakaoti hoko, ā, ka whakamahia te tātauira kua tīpakohia ina whakaratohia tō rātou whatunga hou.

Hei whakamahi i te kaitīpako i tētahi rerenga whakaoti hoko:

1. Whakaritea ngā whatunga e hiahia ana koe ki te tuku hei tātauira.
2. Whakahohea te āpure tīpako tātauira Whatunga-maha ki te puka rēhita, puka whakaoti hoko rānei e hāngai ana.
3. Kōwhiria ko ēhea tātauira ka wātea mō taua rerenga whakaoti hoko.
4. Whakaputaina te rerenga whakaoti hoko, ā, whakamātauria tētahi rēhitatanga hei whakaū kua hoatu te tātauira kua tīpakohia ki te whatunga hou.

Mēnā kotahi anake te hua whatunga e tukuna ana e tētahi rerenga whakaoti hoko, ka whaihua tonu te kaitīpako hei hīkoi whakatau arataki. Mō ngā kōrere mahere-maha, whakaaturia ko ngā tātauira anake e hototahi ana ki te hua, mahere rānei kua tīpakohia.

## SSO Hononga Mākutu Whakawhiti-Whatunga {#cross-network-magic-link-sso}

Mā te SSO hononga mākutu whakawhiti-whatunga e āhei ai ngā kiritaki ki te neke i waenga i tō rātou wāhi pūkete matua me ngā whatunga-iti me te kore e takiuru ā-ringa anō. Ina whai tētahi kiritaki i tētahi hononga urunga whakawhiti-whatunga, ka whakaputa a Ultimate Multisite i tētahi hononga mākutu wā-whāiti, ā, ka whakamotuhēhē i te kiritaki ki roto i te whatunga ūnga.

Ka whakapai tēnei i te wheako mō ngā kiritaki e whakahaere ana i neke atu i te kotahi whatunga, mō te hunga rānei me uru wawe ki ngā whatunga tātauira kua tāruatia i muri tonu i te hoko.

Rārangi taki kaiwhakahaere:

* Whakaū kei te hohe te tāpiri Whatunga-maha puta noa i te tāutanga WordPress multisite.
* Me whakarite kei ngā kiritaki te urunga whatunga tika i mua i te tuku hononga whakawhiti-whatunga ki a rātou.
* Kia tika te whirihora i ngā rohe whatunga me ngā rohe kua maherehia kia whakatauria ngā hononga mākutu ki te whatunga ūnga i whakaarotia.
* Whakamātauria te paewhiri kiritaki me ngā pātene urunga whai-muri i te whakaoti hoko i muri i te huri i ngā tautuhinga whatunga, rohe, whakaoti hoko rānei.

Me whakaarohia ngā hononga mākutu hei hononga takiuru. Kua hoahoatia mō te urunga kiritaki maheni, ā, kaua e tuku whakamua, e whakamahi anō rānei i waho i te wāhanga i whakaarotia.

## Ngā Pātai Auau {#frequently-asked-questions}

### He aha te whatunga-maha? {#what-is-multinetwork}

Ko te whatunga-maha tētahi āhuatanga WordPress e āhei ai koe ki te whakahaere i ngā whatunga multisite motuhake maha i runga i tētahi tāutanga WordPress kotahi.

### He pēhea te rerekē o tēnei i te multisite noa? {#how-is-this-different-from-regular-multisite}

Ka āhei te multisite noa ki ngā pae maha i roto i te whatunga kotahi. Ka āhei te whatunga-maha ki ngā whatunga maha, kei ia mea āna ake pae, kei roto katoa i tētahi tāutanga WordPress kotahi.

### Me whai whirihoranga tūmau motuhake tēnei? {#does-this-require-special-server-configuration}

Āe, me whai tatūnga me te whirihoranga tūmau motuhake te whatunga-maha. Tēnā tirohia te tuhinga mō ngā tohutohu tatūnga taipitopito.
