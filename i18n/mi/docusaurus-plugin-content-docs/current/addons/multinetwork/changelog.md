# Rangitaki Huringa Multi-Network

### 1.3.0 {#130}
* Hou: Kaitirotiro tauira whatunga — tirotirohia, arokitehia hoki ngā tauira whatunga mā tētahi paewhiri ora i mua i te hoko.
* Hou: Kaitīpako tauira whatunga i te checkout — ka taea e ngā kiritaki te tīpako i tētahi tauira whatunga i te wā o te tukanga signup checkout.
* Hou: SSO hononga mākutu whakawhiti-whatunga — ka taea e ngā kiritaki te uru māeneene ki ngā whatunga-iti mā te motuhēhēnga magic-link.
* Hou: Tāruatanga pāpāho whatunga — ka tāruatia tika ngā pāpāho pae ina tārua ana i ngā whatunga mai i tētahi tauira.
* Whakatika: Kei te mahi ināianei te tāruatanga whatunga me te kore te addon rēti-maha; ka whakahokia anō ngā mana super admin i muri i te tāruatanga.
* Whakatika: Kua hototahi ināianei te waihanga whatunga ki WooCommerce me ngā payment gateways katoa.
* Whakatika: Kua whakaurua ināianei te tau tauranga ki te whakaputanga rohe fallback.
* Whakatika: Kua nekehia te CSS kaitīpako tauira ki tētahi stylesheet kua enqueued mō te tāhuahua tika ki ngā theme katoa.
* Whakatika: Tiakina kia kore e tautuhia te kī `network_id` i te whānuitanga uiui pae.
* Whakatika: Ko te arowhai whakaaetanga whatunga i mua i te whakawhitinga horopaki ka ārai i ngā hapa āhuatanga ripanga `wpdb`.
