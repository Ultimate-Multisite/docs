# Kōwhiri Reo

Ultimate Multisite: Kōwhiri Reo i te Rēhitatanga

Tāpiritia he āpure kōwhiri reo ki te rerenga rēhitatanga.

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Tukuake 'ultimate-multisite-language-selector' ki te whaiaronga '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Whakahohea te plugin mā te tahua 'Plugins' i tō WordPress Kaiwhakahaere Whatunga

## Aratohu Whakamahi {#usage-guide}

Mā tēnei add-on koreutu ka taea e koe te tuku i ō kaiwhakamahi kia kōwhiri ko tēhea reo e hiahia ana rātou ki te kite i te rerenga rēhitatanga me te whārangi takiuru.

Whai muri i te [whakahohe i tō raihana](../../user-guide/getting-started/installing-ultimate-multisite.md) ka taea e koe te tāuta i ngā add-ons Ultimate Multisite e whakaaetia ana e tō raihana kia uru atu koe.

Haere ki tō whārangi tautuhinga Ultimate Multisite, ā, i te tahua kei te taha mauī, pāwhiritia te tapanga _Add-ons_.

![Whārangi Add-ons](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

_Haere ki te whārangi Add-ons kia tīmata ai te tāuta i te Kōwhiri Reo_

I reira, ka kitea e koe ngā add-ons katoa e tukuna ana e mātou.

E wātea ana ngā add-ons koreutu ki ā mātou kiritaki katoa, ahakoa te mahere ohaurunga i rēhita ai rātou.

Pāwhiritia te pātene _Tāutatia Ināianei!_ kei raro iho i te karakōnui o te add-on. Kātahi ka whakahohe ki te whārangi Plugins.

![Whārangi Add-ons me te pātene tāuta](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

_Pāwhiritia te pātene hei tāuta i te add-on_

_Whakahohea ki tō whārangi Plugins_

Ināianei, haere ki te tahua Tautuhinga a Ultimate Multisite, ā, tomo ki te ripa _Tautuhinga Whatunga_. I reira, kei raro o te whārangi, ka kitea e koe tētahi wāhanga hou e kīia ana ko _Ngā Reo_.

![Whārangi tautuhinga](/img/config/settings-general.png)

_Ka puta mai he wāhanga hou rawa!_

Ka taea e koe te waiho kia pātea hei whakaatu i ngā reo katoa hei kōwhiringa, ka taea rānei te tīpako ko ēhea e hiahia ana koe kia wātea. Ko ngā reo ko ērā kua tāutatia e koe ki tō tautuhinga WordPress.

_Rārangi o ngā reo e wātea ana i tēnei tāutanga WP_

Whai muri i te tiaki i ngā huringa i mahia e koe, ka whakaaturia he tahua kōwhiri me ngā reo i kōwhiria e koe ki te whārangi Takiuru, me te Rerenga Rēhitatanga hoki.

## Te Tautuhi i te Reo mā te URL {#setting-the-language-via-the-url}

Ka taea hoki e koe te huri i te reo o tētahi whārangi mā te tuku hāngai i te tawhā locale ki te URL. Mēnā e hiahia ana koe ki te anga atu ki tētahi whārangi rēhita i te reo Itāriana, hei tauira, tāpiritia ?locale=it_IT ki te URL.

[https://yournetwork.com/wp-signup.php?locale=it_IT](https://yournetwork.com/wp-signup.php?locale=it%5FIT)

Me tāuta te reo ki tō whatunga WordPress kia mahi ai tēnei.
