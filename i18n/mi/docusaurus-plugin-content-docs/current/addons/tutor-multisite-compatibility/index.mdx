# Tutor Multisite Compatibility

He WordPress plugin e tāpiri ana i ngā whakatikanga hototahitanga mō Tutor LMS i ngā taiao multisite.

## Whakaahuatanga {#description}

Ka whakarato tēnei plugin i ngā whakatikanga hototahitanga matua mō te whakahaere i Tutor LMS i runga i ngā whatunga WordPress multisite. Ka whakahaere i te whakauru kaiwhakamahi, te whakahaere kaiwhakaako, ngā here akoranga, me ētahi atu āheinga e hāngai ana ki te multisite.

## Āhuatanga {#features}

- **Whakahaere Ripanga Pātengi Raraunga**: Ka kite aunoa, ka waihanga hoki i ngā ripanga Tutor LMS e ngaro ana nā te rahunga i ngā here kī rāwaho multisite
- **Whakauru Kaiwhakamahi Multisite**: Ngā pūwhakahaere AJAX ritenga mō te whakahaere i te whakauru kaiwhakamahi puta noa i ngā pae o te whatunga
- **Whakahaere Tūranga Kaiwhakaako**: Te tautapa me te whakahaere aunoa i te tūranga kaiwhakaako i ngā taiao multisite
- **Ngā Here Akoranga**: Te kōmitimiti ki Ultimate Multisite mō te whakahaere i ngā here waihanga akoranga me te funnel e ai ki ngā mahere pae
- **Ngā Tirohanga Admin Ritenga**: He rārangi kaiwhakaako me tētahi atanga whakahaere e mōhio ana ki te multisite
- **Ngā Utauta Admin**: He atanga whakahaere pātengi raraunga mō ngā kaiwhakahaere whatunga me ngā kaiwhakahaere pae

## Ngā Whakaritenga {#requirements}

- WordPress 5.0+
- PHP 7.4+
- Tutor LMS plugin (ka whakamanahia aunoa mā te pane `Requires Plugins`)
- Tāutanga WordPress Multisite
- Ultimate Multisite plugin (mō te āheinga here akoranga)

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Tukuake ngā kōnae plugin ki `/wp-content/plugins/tutor-multisite-compatibility/`
2. Me whakarite kua tāutatia, kua whakahohe hoki a Tutor LMS
3. Whakahohe i te plugin mā te tahua 'Plugins' i WordPress
4. Ka kōmitimiti aunoa te plugin ki Tutor LMS

## Whakahaere Pātengi Raraunga {#database-management}

### Te Raru {#the-problem}

I ngā taiao WordPress multisite, ka rahua te waihanga ripanga Tutor LMS mō ngā ripanga whai here kī rāwaho ki te ripanga kaiwhakamahi. Ka tupu tēnei nā te mea:

- Ka whakamahi ngā ripanga motuhake-ki-te-pae i ngā kuhimua pēnei i `wp_2_tutor_carts`
- Ka tohutoro ngā kī rāwaho ki `wp_2_users` (kāore e tīariari)
- Ko te ripanga kaiwhakamahi tūturu ko `wp_users` (ka tiritiria puta noa i te whatunga)

### Te Rongoā {#the-solution}

Ka mahi aunoa tēnei plugin i ēnei:

1. **Ka Kite i ngā Ripanga e Ngaro Ana**: Ka tirotiro i ngā ripanga i rahua te waihanga nā ngā here kī rāwaho
2. **Ka Waihanga Ripanga me ngā Tohutoro Tika**: Ka whakamahi i te ingoa ripanga kaiwhakamahi tika (`wp_users` i te multisite)
3. **Ka Whakahaere i ngā Āhuatanga Maha**:
   - Te whakahohe/whakahohe anō i te plugin
   - Te waihanga pae hou i te whatunga
   - Te tirotiro ā-ringa i ngā ripanga mā te atanga admin

### Ngā Ripanga e Pāngia Ana {#affected-tables}

- `tutor_coupon_usages` - Kei roto he kī rāwaho ki te ripanga kaiwhakamahi
- `tutor_carts` - Kei roto he kī rāwaho ki te ripanga kaiwhakamahi
- `tutor_notification_preferences` - He ripanga TutorPro whai kī rāwaho ki te ripanga kaiwhakamahi

### Atanga Admin {#admin-interface}

Uru ki ngā utauta whakahaere pātengi raraunga i:

- **Network Admin**: Settings → Tutor Multisite DB
- **Site Admin**: Tools → Tutor Multisite DB

Mā te atanga ka āhei koe ki te:
- Tiro i ngā ripanga e ngaro ana puta noa i ngā pae katoa
- Whakakā ā-ringa i te waihanga ripanga
- Tirotiro i te whirihoranga pātengi raraunga
- Tiro i ngā mōhiohio tūnga taipitopito
