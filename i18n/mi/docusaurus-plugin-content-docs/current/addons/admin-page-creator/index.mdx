# Admin Page Creator {#admin-page-creator}

## Ihirangi {#content}
Mā Admin Page Creator koe e āhei ai ki te tāpiri māmā i ngā whārangi whakahaere hou me ngā whārangi-iti ki roto i te papatohu whakahaere WordPress. Ka taea te whakamahi hei waihanga i tētahi whārangi pōwhiri, i tētahi whārangi akoranga rānei, ka taea hoki te tū hei whārangi taunga mō ngā kaiwhakamahi hou. Ka taea e koe te whakamahi i tētahi kaihanga whārangi pēnei i Beaver Builder, Elementor, Brizy, me Oxygen hei hoahoa i ngā whārangi me te kore raruraru.

Ka tuku hoki i te tautoko HTML, CSS me JavaScript ritenga, me te whakahaere urunga e hāngai ana ki te mahere me te tūranga kaiwhakamahi!

## Āhuatanga {#features}
* Tautoko i te HTML me te CSS Ritenga
* Tautoko JavaScript
* Aratau Ihirangi PHP
* Whakaaetanga e hāngai ana ki te tūranga Kaiwhakamahi
* Tautoko ki ngā kaihanga whārangi

## Tuhinga āwhina {#documentation}
[Te waihanga i ngā Whārangi Whakahaere Ritenga me ngā Whārangi-iti mā te whakamahi i Multisite Ultimate: Admin Page Creator](https://help.multisiteultimate.com/article/305-creating-custom-admin-pages-and-sub-pages-using-ultimate-multisite-admin-page-creator)

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Tukuatu **ultimate-multisite-admin-page-creator** ki te whaiaronga **/wp-content/plugins/**
2. Whakahohea te monomai mā te tahua **Ngā Monomai** i tō WordPress Network Admin

## Aratohu Whakamahi {#usage-guide}

Ahakoa ka tino māmā a WordPress ki te tāpiri ihirangi ki te pito mua o ngā pae, ko te waihanga whārangi me ngā whārangi-iti ki te taha whakahaere me whai pūkenga waehere taketake. Ko te whāinga o Ultimate Multisite: Admin Page Creator he kawe mai i taua māmā o te whakamahi o te pito mua ki te pito muri o ō pae whatunga.

## He aha te take me whakamahi i Admin Page Creator? {#why-use-admin-page-creator}

Tērā pea e hiahia ana koe ki te tuku i tētahi whārangi ritenga ki te paewhiri whakahaere a ō kiritaki me ngā akoranga, tērā pea e hiahia ana koe ki te tāpiri i tētahi whārangi ritenga me ngā pātai auau me tētahi widget whakapā. He mutunga kore ngā āheinga me Admin Page Creator, ā, koirā tā tātou ka tūhura i te akoranga o tēnei rā.

## Kōwhiringa Tahua {#menu-options}

Ki te taha, i te pouaka-meta Kōwhiringa Whānui, ka taea e koe te whirihora i te momo tahua, te tapanga, me te maha atu.

### Ngā Momo Tahua {#menu-types}

Ka taea e tō whārangi whakahaere ritenga hou te whai momo tahua rerekē e rua: tahua taumata-runga me te tahua-iti.

Ko tētahi whārangi tahua taumata-runga, e ai ki te ingoa, ka tāpiri i tō whārangi hou kua waihangatia ki te tahua whakahaere i te taha o ngā whārangi WordPress taunoa.

Mēnā ka tīpakohia e koe te kōwhiringa taumata-runga, ka taea hoki e koe te tīpako i te raupapa tahua me tētahi ata tahua.

Mēnā e hiahia ana koe kia noho tēnei whārangi hei whārangi tahua-iti, ka taea e koe te tīpako i tētahi whārangi matua hei whakapiri atu i tēnei whārangi. Ka taea e koe te tāpiri i tō whārangi ritenga ki ngā whārangi WordPress taunoa, ki ō whārangi whakahaere ritenga taumata-runga rānei.

<!-- Kāore te hopu mata i te wātea: Ngā kōwhiringa momo tahua Admin Page Creator me ngā tautuhinga tapanga, raupapa, me te ata -->

_Whakaritea katoatia te tūemi tahua mō tēnei Whārangi Ritenga_

<!-- Kāore te hopu mata i te wātea: Tīpakonga whārangi matua tahua-iti Admin Page Creator -->

_Ka taea hoki e koe te tāpiri i tēnei whārangi hei tahua-iti o ētahi atu whārangi tahua, tae atu ki ō ake mea taumata-runga ritenga._

## Kōwhiringa Arā Atu Anō {#advanced-options}

Me āhei koe ki te tāpiri i ngā momo ihirangi katoa ki ō whārangi whakahaere ritenga. Kei roto ko ngā ture me ngā kōnae CSS, JavaScript, me ngā whare pukapuka o waho hoki. Ka taea e koe tērā mā te whakamahi i te Ripa Kōwhiringa Arā Atu Anō.

### CSS Ritenga {#custom-css}

I te ripa CSS, ka taea e koe te tāpiri i tō ake waehere CSS ritenga, me te kawemai hoki i ngā kōnae CSS mai i ngā pae mamao (pēnei i ngā CDN, Google Fonts, aha atu).

<!-- Kāore te hopu mata i te wātea: Ētita CSS ritenga Admin Page Creator me te kawemai ripanga kāhua o waho -->

_E tautokona ana hoki te CSS Ritenga. Ka taea hoki e koe te whakauru i ngā kōnae kāhua o waho!_

### JavaScript Ritenga {#custom-javascript}

He ōrite anō mō JavaScript. Ka taea e koe te tāpiri i ngā whare pukapuka o waho hei whakamahi i tō waehere JavaScript ritenga.

<!-- Kāore te hopu mata i te wātea: Ētita JavaScript ritenga Admin Page Creator me te kawemai whare pukapuka o waho -->

_Tāpirihia tō ake waehere JavaScript!_
