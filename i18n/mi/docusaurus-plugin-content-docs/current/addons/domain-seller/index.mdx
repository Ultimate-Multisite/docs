# Domain Seller

**Multisite Ultimate: Domain Seller** ka huri i tō whatunga WordPress Multisite hei tūāpapa hoko rohe kaha. Tukuna ngā kiritaki kia rēhita rohe ritenga tika tonu i te wā waitohu mai, mai rānei i tō rātou Dashboard, me te tirotiro wātea ā-wā-tūturu me te utu mārama.

### He aha a Domain Seller? {#why-domain-seller}

Ko te rēhitatanga rohe tētahi o ngā hokonga-ake e tino tonoa ana mō tētahi tūāpapa WaaS (Website as a Service). Engari i te tuku i ō kiritaki ki tētahi kaiwhakarēhita rōpū-tuatoru, ka taea e koe te tuku rohe tika tonu — kia nui ake te moni whiwhi e noho ki tō whatunga, ā, ka hoatu hoki ki ngā kiritaki he wheako maeneene.

### Ngā Āhuatanga Matua {#key-features}

#### Tautoko mō Ngā Kaiwhakarēhita Rohe Maha {#multiple-domain-registrar-support}
* **OpenSRS** — Te rēhitatanga, te whakahou, me te whakahaere katoa
* **Namecheap** — Te tuitui katoa me WhoisGuard me te whakamātautau sandbox
* **HostAfrica** — Te rapu rohe, te utu, te rēhitatanga, te whakawhiti, te DNS, me te whakamarumaru ID mā te Domains Reseller API
* **Openprovider** — Utu kaihoko-anō, rēhitatanga, whakahou, whakawhitinga, DNS, tūmataitinga WHOIS, me te tukutahi TLD
* **Hostinger** — Te rapu rohe me te rēhitatanga mā te token tuitui Hostinger tiritiri
* **Hoahoanga Whakawhānui** — I hangaia ki runga i te tauira Integration Registry, kia māmā ai te tāpiri kaiwhakarato anō
* **Whakamātautau Hononga ā-Roto** — Whakamana i ngā taipitopito takiuru kaiwhakarato me te tūhononga i mua i te haere ora

#### Ruānuku Tatūnga Ārahi {#guided-setup-wizard}
* **Whakaurunga pāwhiri-kotahi** — Ka ārahi i a koe mā te whirihoranga kaiwhakarato, te kawemai TLD, me te waihanga hua rohe
* **Tukutahi TLD aunoa** — Ka kawemai i ngā TLD katoa e wātea ana me ngā utu rarawe mai i ō kaiwhakarato kua tūhonoa
* **Waihanga hua taunoa** — Ka waihanga i tētahi hua rohe kua rite mō te whakamahi, me ngā taunoa tāpiritanga utu whai tikanga

#### Utu Rohe Hangore {#flexible-domain-pricing}
* **E toru ngā aratau tāpiritanga utu** — Tāpiritanga ōrau, tāpiritanga pūmau, utu pūmau rānei mō ia TLD
* **Ngā hua motuhake-ki-te-TLD** — Waihangatia he hua rohe motuhake mō ngā rōpū TLD rerekē (.com/.net, .io, ngā waehere whenua, aha atu)
* **Utu whakataki** — Tukuna he utu tau-tuatahi kua whakahekea hei kukume i ngā rēhitatanga hou
* **Utu wā-tūturu** — Utu rarawe ora mai i ngā kaiwhakarato me te keteroki atamai
* **Tautoko tīkitipati** — Hototahitanga katoa ki ngā waehere hekenga utu a Multisite Ultimate

#### Whakamarumaru Tūmataitinga WHOIS {#whois-privacy-protection}
* **Whirihoranga mō ia hua** — Tautuhia te tūmataitinga WHOIS ki te Monokia, Whakaurua I Ngā Wā Katoa, Kōwhiringa Kiritaki rānei
* **Whakaae-mai a te kiritaki** — Ina tautuhia ki te Kōwhiringa Kiritaki, mā tētahi pouaka taki i te tukanga utu e tuku ki ngā kiritaki kia tāpiri whakamarumaru tūmataitinga
* **Utu ka taea te whirihora** — Tautuhia he utu ā-tau mō te tāpiritanga tūmataitinga, tukua kore utu rānei
* **Tuitui kaiwhakarēhita** — Ka whakahohe aunoa i te WhoisGuard (Namecheap) te tūmataitinga WHOIS (OpenSRS) rānei i te wā rēhitatanga

#### Wheako Utu Kotahi {#unified-checkout-experience}
* **E toru ngā aratau rohe** — Ka kōwhiri ngā kiritaki i waenga i tētahi rohe-iti (kore utu), te rēhita i tētahi rohe hou, te mahere rānei i tētahi rohe onāianei kua nō rātou kē
* **Rapu rohe wā-tūturu** — Tirotiro wātea e whakakahaia ana e AJAX, kāore he uta anō i te whārangi
* **Tūtohunga atamai** — Ngā tūtohunga TLD kē ina kua tangohia kē te rohe e hiahiatia ana
* **Utu mārama** — Whakaaturanga mārama o te utu rēhitatanga, te utu whakahou, me te utu tūmataitinga WHOIS kōwhiringa
* **Ngā āpure whakapā kaiwhakarēhita** — Ka kohikohi i ngā mōhiohio kaiwhakarēhita e hiahiatia ana (ingoa, wāhitau, waea) ki roto tonu i te tukanga utu
* **URL pae hanga-aunoa** — Ka ahu aunoa te wāhitau pae i te rohe kua kōwhiria

#### Pūnaha Whakahou Aunoa {#auto-renewal-system}
* **Whakahou e herea ana ki te mematanga** — Ka whakahou aunoa ngā rohe i te wā e hohe ana te mematanga a te kiritaki
* **Whakariterite atamai** — Ka rārangihia ngā mahi whakahou e ai ki ngā rā paunga o te rohe
* **Arorau whakamātau anō** — Ngā whakamātau anō atamai mō ngā nganatanga whakahou i rahua
* **Whakamōhiotanga īmēra** — Ngā whakatūpato ka taea te whirihora mō ngā whakahou e tata mai ana, ngā whakahou angitū, ngā rahunga, me ngā paunga

#### Whakahaere DNS a te Kiritaki {#customer-dns-management}
* **DNS mahi-whaiaro** — Ka taea e ngā kiritaki te tāpiri, te whakatika, me te muku pūkete DNS (A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, TXT) mai i tō rātou Dashboard
* **Tūnga rohe** — Tūnga rēhitatanga wā-tūturu, rā paunga, me ngā mōhiohio nameserver
* **Mahere rohe aunoa** — Ka mahere aunoatia ngā rohe kua rēhitatia, ngā rohe onāianei rānei, ki te pae a te kiritaki

#### Utauta Kaiwhakahaere {#admin-tools}
* **Rēhitatanga rohe ā-ringa** — Rēhitatia ngā rohe mō ngā kiritaki mai i te paewhiri kaiwhakahaere
* **Aroturuki rohe** — Tirohia ngā rohe kiritaki katoa, ngā tūnga, me ngā rā paunga mai i te whārangi kotahi
* **Rangitaki motuhake-ki-te-kaiwhakarato** — Ngā hongere rangitaki motuhake mō ia kaiwhakarēhita (hei tauira, domain-seller-namecheap.log)
* **Taipitopito rēhitatanga** — Tirohia ngā mōhiohio kaiwhakarēhita me ngā raraunga rēhitatanga i te whārangi matua whakatika rohe

### Ngā Kaiwhakarato e Tautokona Ana {#supported-providers}

* **OpenSRS** — Tuitui katoa ki te rēhitatanga, whakahou, whakahaere DNS, me te tūmataitinga WHOIS
* **Namecheap** — Tuitui katoa ki te rēhitatanga, whakahou, tirotiro wātea ā-pūtea, me te WhoisGuard kore utu
* **HostAfrica** — Rēhitatanga, whakahou, whakawhitinga, whakahaere DNS, tiki waehere EPP, raka kaiwhakarēhita, me te whakamarumaru ID
* **Openprovider** — Rēhitatanga, whakahou, whakawhitinga, whakahaere DNS, tiki waehere EPP, raka kaiwhakarēhita, tūmataitinga WHOIS, me te tukutahi TLD kaihoko-anō
* **Hostinger** — Rēhitatanga, rapu rohe, whakahōu nameserver, raka kaiwhakarēhita, me te tūmataitinga WHOIS; ka whakahaeretia ngā whakahou e te whakahou-aunoa a Hostinger
* **GoDaddy** — Rēhitatanga me te whakahou
* **ResellerClub** — Rēhitatanga, whakahou, me te whakahaere DNS
* **NameSilo** — Rēhitatanga me te whakahou
* **Enom** — Rēhitatanga, whakahou, me te whakahaere DNS

### Taipitopito Hangarau {#technical-details}

* **E hiahiatia ana:** PHP 7.4+, Multisite Ultimate 2.4.12+
* **Hoahoanga:** Tauira Integration Registry me te atanga Domain_Selling_Capability
* **Autoloading:** Jetpack autoloader me te mahere akomanga PSR-4
* **Paerewa:** Ngā paerewa waehere WordPress, pato mārō, tauākī kua whakaritea

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Me whakarite kua tāutatia, kua whakahohe-whatungatia hoki **Multisite Ultimate v2.4.12+**
2. Tukuatu te monomai ki `/wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite-domain-seller/`
3. Whakahohe-whatungatia te monomai mai i **Ngā Monomai** i te Kaiwhakahaere Whatunga WordPress
4. Ka whakarewa aunoa te **Ruānuku Tatūnga** i te whakahohe tuatahi — whai i ngā hipanga kia:
   * Tūhono i tō rēhita rohe
   * Whakamātau i te tūhononga
   * Kawemai i ngā TLD e wātea ana me te utu rarawe
   * Waihanga i tō hua rohe tuatahi me te tāpiritanga utu
5. Tāpiri i te āpure **Tīpakonga Rohe** ki tō puka checkout i **Ultimate Multisite > Ngā Puka Checkout**

## Ngā Pātai Auau {#frequently-asked-questions}

### He aha te putanga o Multisite Ultimate e hiahiatia ana? {#what-version-of-multisite-ultimate-is-required}

Putanga 2.4.12, teitei ake rānei. Kāore te tāpiritanga e hototahi ki WP Ultimo v1.x, ki ngā putanga Ultimate Multisite kei raro iho i te 2.4.12 rānei.

### Ko ēhea kaiwhakarato rohe e tautokona ana? {#which-domain-providers-are-supported}

E iwa ngā rēhita e tautokona ana: **OpenSRS**, **Namecheap**, **HostAfrica**, **Openprovider**, **Hostinger**, **GoDaddy**, **ResellerClub**, **NameSilo**, me **Enom**. He rerekē ngā āheinga i ia kaiwhakarato nā te mea ka whakakite ngā API rēhita i ngā mahi rerekē; tirohia te aratohu tatūnga mō tētahi tirohanga whānui ā-kaiwhakarato. Mā te hanganga Integration Registry o te tāpiritanga ka māmā te tāpiri kaiwhakarato ritenga — tirohia te [aratohu kaiwhakawhanake](/developer/integration-guide/custom-registrar).

### Ka taea e au te tuku parenga tūmataiti WHOIS? {#can-i-offer-whois-privacy-protection}

Āe. Kei ia hua rohe tētahi tautuhinga Tūmataiti WHOIS me ngā aratau e toru: Kua Monokia, Ka Whakaurua Tonutia, Kōwhiringa Kiritaki rānei. Ina tautuhia ki Kōwhiringa Kiritaki, ka kite ngā kiritaki i tētahi pouakataki i te wā checkout me te utu (ko "Koreutu" rānei mō WhoisGuard a Namecheap). Ka tukuna aunoatia te kara tūmataiti ki te rēhita i te wā rēhitatanga.

### Ka taea e au te whakarite utu rerekē mō ngā TLD rerekē? {#can-i-set-different-pricing-for-different-tlds}

Āe. Waihangatia he hua rohe motuhake mō ngā rōpū TLD rerekē. Ka taea e ia hua tana ake momo tāpiritanga (ōrau, tāpiritanga pūmau, utu pūmau rānei), ngā TLD e tautokona ana, me te utu whakataki.

### He aha ngā kōwhiringa checkout ka kitea e ngā kiritaki? {#what-checkout-options-do-customers-see}

Ka hoatu e te āpure Tīpakonga Rohe ki ngā kiritaki he ripa e toru: **Rohe-iti** (whakamahia tētahi rohe-iti koreutu i tō whatunga), **Rēhita Rohe Hōu** (rapua, rēhitatia hoki tētahi rohe hōu), me **Rohe Tīariari** (maherea tētahi rohe nō rātou kē). Ko ngā aratau anake ka whakahohea e koe ka puta.

### E tautoko ana i ngā whakahoutanga aunoa? {#does-it-support-automatic-renewals}

Āe. Ka taea te whakarite i ngā rohe kia whakahou-aunoa, ā, ka herea ki te tūnga mematanga o te kiritaki. Ka tukatukahia ngā utu whakahou mā te tomokanga utu tīariari a te kiritaki.

### Ka taea e ngā kiritaki te whakahaere i ā rātou pūkete DNS? {#can-customers-manage-their-dns-records}

Āe. Ka whiwhi ngā kiritaki i tētahi atanga whakahaere DNS i roto i tō rātou Dashboard hei tāpiri, whakatika, muku hoki i ngā pūkete A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, me TXT. E wātea ana te whakahaere DNS me OpenSRS, ResellerClub, Enom, HostAfrica, me Openprovider. Ka whakahaerehia te DNS a Hostinger mō ngā rohe kua hautūngia e te whakaurunga matua mahere-rohe a Hostinger.

### He ruānuku tatūnga tōna? {#is-there-a-setup-wizard}

Āe. Ka rere aunoa te ruānuku tatūnga i te whakahohe tuatahi, ā, ka ārahi i a koe ki te tūhono i tētahi rēhita, ki te kawemai TLD, me te waihanga i tō hua rohe tuatahi. Ka taea hoki e koe te whakahaere anō i te ruānuku mai i ngā tautuhinga tāpiritanga.
