# Tātari: wu_domain_seller_search_delay {#filter-wudomainsellersearchdelay}

Ka tātari i te roanga whakaroa debounce mō te rapunga rohe, i roto i ngā mirimēkona.

Whakanuia tēnei uara hei whakaiti i ngā karanga API i runga i ngā hononga pōturi.

## Tawhā {#parameters}

| Ingoa | Momo | Whakaahuatanga |
|------|------|-------------|
| $delay | `int` | Te roanga whakaroa debounce i roto i ngā mirimēkona. Taunoa 500. |
