# Gratis AI Agent

Ko Gratis AI Agent he kaiāwhina AI motuhake mō WordPress ka taea te whakamahere, te hanga, me te whakahaere i ngā pae WordPress katoa mā ngā tohutohu reo māori. Ka whakaatu i tētahi huinga **āheinga** kua hanganga — he mahi motuhake, ka taea te whakamātau, ka taea e te kaiāwhina te karanga — e kapi ana i ngā mea katoa mai i te whakahaere ihirangi ki te whakarite pūnaha hoahoa. Ka tāpiri te putanga 1.9.0 i ngā āheinga hanga ihirangi, te arotake whārangi ā-ataata mā ngā screenshots taha-kiritaki, e rima ngā kōtaha kaiāwhina ā-roto, me ngā haki āhuatanga mō te whakahaere uru me te waitohu.

## Ngā Āhuatanga Matua

- **Pūnaha āheinga** — he rārangi mahi ā-waeine, ka taea te whakawhānui, ka taea e te kaiāwhina te karanga i runga i tō tāutanga WordPress
- **Whakahaere Ihirangi** — hanga whakairinga me ngā whārangi (tae atu ki te tautapa tauira whārangi), hanga-ā-puranga i ngā whakairinga maha i te karanga kotahi, tautuhi whakaahua ārahi, me te hanga puka whakapā
- **Arotake Ataata** — hopu screenshots o ngā whārangi ora, whakataurite i ngā āhua i mua/i muri, ā, whiwhi arotake hoahoa i hangaia e AI e kapi ana i te tahora, te momotuhi, te tae, me te āheinga uru
- **E rima ngā kaiāwhina ā-roto** — Kaituhi Ihirangi, Kaihanga Pae, Taiwhanga Hoahoa, Kaiwhakahaere Mono-mai, me te Kaiāwhina Tautoko; ka taea te whakawhiti mā te Agent Picker i te pane kōrerorero
- **Ngā haki āhuatanga** — ngā whakakā whakahaere-uru me te waitohu i Settings → Feature Flags mō ngā here tūranga, te whakaingoa white-label, me te whakarite widget
- **Whakahaere Momo Whakairinga Ritenga** — rēhita, whakarārangi, me te muku i ngā momo whakairinga ritenga me te toitūtanga puta noa i ngā tīmatanga anō
- **Whakahaere Tāke Ritenga** — rēhita, whakarārangi, me te muku i ngā tāke ritenga me te toitūtanga
- **Ngā āheinga Pūnaha Hoahoa** — kōkuhu CSS ritenga, whakahaere tauira paraka kua kōwhiria, tautuhi i te waitohu pae, me te whakamahi i ngā tatūkē theme.json
- **Whakahaere Kāhua Ao** — pānui me te tuhi uara theme.json mō ngā papatae tae, momotuhi, mokowā, me ētahi atu
- **Whakahaere Tahua Whakatere** — hanga me te whakahou i ngā tahua whakatere me ā rātou tūemi mā te hōtaka
- **Whakahaere Kōwhiringa** — pānui me te tuhi i ngā kōwhiringa WordPress me tētahi rārangi-aukati haumaru ā-roto hei tiaki i ngā tautuhinga hira
- **Rēhita Āheinga Ka Tāutatia** — kimi me te whakahohe i ētahi atu mōkihi āheinga me te kore e whakarerekē i ngā kōnae mono-mai matua
- **Whakaraupapa Kaihanga Pae v2** — ngā mahere hanga pae hipanga-maha me te kimi mono-mai, te aroturuki ahunga whakamua, me te whakaora hapa
- **Tono paearu WP-CLI** — whakahaere i te huinga paearu āheinga kaiāwhina mai i te rārangi tono mō te CI me te arotake tauira

## Ngā Whakaritenga

- WordPress 6.0, teitei ake rānei
- PHP 8.1, teitei ake rānei
- WordPress Multisite (kua whakahohe-whatunga) tētahi tāutanga pae-kotahi rānei
- Tētahi mono-mai tūhono kaiwhakarato AI hohe (Anthropic, OpenAI, he hototahi rānei)

## Tāutanga

1. Tukuatu i ngā kōnae mono-mai ki `/wp-content/plugins/gratis-ai-agent`
2. Whakahohe i te mono-mai mā te mata **Plugins** i WordPress
3. Whakatere ki **Gratis AI Agent → Settings** ka tūhono i tō kaiwhakarato AI
4. (Kōwhiringa) Tirotiro i te **Rēhita Āheinga** hei tāuta i ētahi atu mōkihi āheinga

## Tīmata Tere

Ina whakahohea, kua tūhono hoki ki tētahi kaiwhakarato AI, whakatuwheratia te paewhiri **Gratis AI Agent** i te paetaha whakahaere WordPress. Patohia tētahi tohutohu reo māori, ā, ka whakamahere, ka whakatutuki hoki te kaiāwhina i ngā hipanga e hiahiatia ana mā āna āheinga e wātea ana.

**Ngā tauira tohutohu:**

```
Hangaia tētahi momo whakairinga ritenga "Portfolio" me tētahi tāke "Project Category".
```

```
Kōkuhua tētahi papatae tae aratau-pōuri ki roto i te kaupapa, ā, tautuhia te tae matua ki #1a1a2e.
```

```
Hangaia tētahi paetukutuku wharekai me tētahi tahua tuihono, puka tāpui, me tētahi whārangi whakapā.
```

## Tirohanga Whānui ki ngā Āheinga

Ko ngā āheinga ngā mahi ngota ka taea e te kaiāwhina te mahi. Ko ia āheinga he akomanga PHP kua rēhitatia e whakaatu ana i tētahi aronuinga (ngā tawhā me ngā momo whakahoki) ka pānuitia e te kaiāwhina i te wā whakahaere. Ka whakarōpūngia ngā āheinga ki ngā wāhanga mahi:

| Rohe | Āheinga |
|---|---|
| **Whakahaere Ihirangi** | `create_post`, `update_post`, `batch_create_posts`, `set_featured_image`, `create_contact_form` |
| **Arotake Ataata** | `capture_screenshot`, `compare_screenshots`, `review_page_design` |
| **Ngā Momo Whakairinga Ritenga** | `register_post_type`, `list_post_types`, `delete_post_type` |
| **Ngā Tāke Ritenga** | `register_taxonomy`, `list_taxonomies`, `delete_taxonomy` |
| **Pūnaha Hoahoa** | `inject_custom_css`, `add_block_pattern`, `list_block_patterns`, `set_site_logo`, `apply_theme_json_preset` |
| **Kāhua Ao** | `get_global_styles`, `set_global_styles`, `reset_global_styles` |
| **Ngā Tahua Whakatere** | `create_menu`, `update_menu`, `add_menu_item`, `remove_menu_item`, `list_menus` |
| **Whakahaere Kōwhiringa** | `get_option`, `set_option`, `delete_option`, `list_options` |
| **Ngā Āheinga Ka Tāutatia** | `list_available_abilities`, `install_ability`, `recommend_plugin` |
| **Kaihanga Pae** | `create_site_plan`, `execute_site_plan`, `get_plan_progress`, `handle_plan_error` |

Tirohia te [Tohutoro Āheinga](./abilities) mō ngā tuhinga tawhā katoa me ngā tauira whakamahinga.

## WP-CLI

Ka kawea mai e Gratis AI Agent tētahi whānau tono `wp gratis-ai-agent` mō te whakahaere paearu me te whakahaere āheinga mai i te rārangi tono. Tirohia te [Tohutoro WP-CLI](./wp-cli) mō te rārangi tono katoa.

```bash
# Run the full benchmark suite
wp gratis-ai-agent benchmark

# List installed abilities
wp gratis-ai-agent abilities list

# Install an ability pack
wp gratis-ai-agent abilities install <slug>
```

## Whirihoranga

Kei raro ngā tautuhinga katoa i **Gratis AI Agent → Settings** i te kaiwhakahaere WordPress:

| Tautuhinga | Whakaahuatanga |
|---|---|
| **Kaiwhakarato AI** | Tīpakohia, whirihorahia hoki te tūhono kaiwhakarato AI hohe |
| **Tauira** | Kōwhiria te tauira reo hei whakamahi mō ngā wātū kaiāwhina |
| **Max Tokens** | Pūtea token mō ia tono (taunoa 4096) |
| **Wāmutunga Āheinga** | Te nui rawa o ngā hēkona ka taea e tētahi āheinga takitahi te whakahaere (taunoa 30) |
| **Rangitaki Patuiro** | Takiuru i ngā karanga āheinga me te whakaarotanga kaiāwhina ki te rangitaki patuiro WordPress |

## Rārangi-aukati Haumaru Whakahaere Kōwhiringa

Kei roto i te āheitanga Whakahaere Kōwhiringa tētahi rārangi-aukati kua hangaia ki roto, e ārai ana i te kaiāwhina kia kaua e pānui, e tuhi rānei i ngā kōwhiringa tairongo o WordPress. Ka kapi te rārangi-aukati taunoa i:

- `admin_email`, `blogadmin_email`
- `siteurl`, `home` (ngā kōwhiringa URL — whakamahia tētahi taputapu hekenga motuhake mō ēnei)
- `auth_key`, `secure_auth_key`, `logged_in_key`, `nonce_key` (ngā kī muna)
- `active_plugins`, `template` (whakahohe mono/kaupapa — whakamahia ngā āheitanga motuhake)
- Ngā taipitopito tuakiri kūwaha utu me ngā kī API kua rēhitatia e ngā mono kua tāutatia

Ka taea te tāpiri urunga anō mā te filter `gratis_ai_agent_options_blocklist`.

## Ngā Hooks me ngā Filters

### Ngā Actions

- `gratis_ai_agent_ability_registered` — ka whakakāngia i muri i te rēhitatanga o tētahi āheitanga, ka whiwhi i te ingoa karaehe āheitanga
- `gratis_ai_agent_plan_started` — ka whakakāngia ina tīmata te mahere kaihanga pae ki te whakahaere
- `gratis_ai_agent_plan_completed` — ka whakakāngia ina oti pai tētahi mahere
- `gratis_ai_agent_plan_failed` — ka whakakāngia ina tūtaki tētahi mahere ki tētahi hapa kāore e taea te whakaora

### Ngā Filters

- `gratis_ai_agent_abilities` — whakarerekē i te rārangi āheitanga kua rēhitatia i mua i te utaina e te kaiāwhina
- `gratis_ai_agent_options_blocklist` — whakawhānui, whakaiti rānei i te rārangi-aukati haumaru kōwhiringa
- `gratis_ai_agent_benchmark_questions` — tāpiri pātai paearu ritenga ki te huinga aromātai
- `gratis_ai_agent_plan_plugins` — whakarerekē i te rārangi kitenga mono i te wā e waihangatia ana te mahere pae
