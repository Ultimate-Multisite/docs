# Kairīhi-maha

Tohaina ngā pae-iti puta noa i ngā tūmau me ngā pātengi raraunga mō te mahinga me te haumarutanga

## Ngā miramira o te tukunga putanga 1.2.0

Ka tāpiri a Kairīhi-maha 1.2.0 i te tūāpapa kairīhi rangatiratanga mō ngā rerengamahi wehehanga whakaputanga:

- Takiuru-aunoa kairīhi kore-tūāhua me ngā toronga SSO poto-te-ora, kua parengia i te tukurua.
- Wehehanga pātengi raraunga me te pūnaha kōnae mō ia pae-iti mō ngā kairīhi rangatiratanga.
- Āheinga kairīhi-maha Hostinger mō te whakarato kairīhi e mōhio ana ki te hautū.
- Ngā kēti manatoko hekenga rangatiratanga mō ngā arowhai raraunga tawhito me te tukatuka pana async.
- Here hautū pātengi raraunga mīhini-kotahi ka taea te whirihora mō ngā tāutanga Bedrock, FrankenPHP, me localhost.
- Whakapai ake i te whakaoho kairīhi, te kaituhi pātengi raraunga, te whakaoho schema, me te whakarato kaiwhakamahi.
- Wetewete rangatiratanga haumaru ake e horoi ana i ngā taipitopito takiuru kairīhi.

Tirohia ngā aratohu kaiwhakamahi mō te [rerengamahi hekenga rangatiratanga](/user-guide/getting-started/sovereign-migration-workflow), [wehehanga kairīhi-maha](/user-guide/configuration/multi-tenancy-isolation), me ngā [mahi kairīhi rangatiratanga](/user-guide/administration/sovereign-tenant-operations).

## Ngā whakaritenga

- WordPress 5.3, teitei ake rānei
- PHP 7.4, teitei ake rānei
- plugin Multisite Ultimate (e hohe ana)

## Tāutanga

1. Tukuatu ngā kōnae tāpiri ki tō whaiaronga `/wp-content/plugins/`
2. Whakahohea te plugin mā te tahua 'Plugins' i WordPress
3. Whirihorahia ngā tautuhinga tāpiri i te kaiwhakahaere whatunga

## Whakawhanaketanga

### Tatūnga

```bash
# Install dependencies
composer install
npm install

# Run tests
npm run test

# Run code standards checks
vendor/bin/phpcs
vendor/bin/phpstan

# Fix code style issues
vendor/bin/phpcbf

# Build for production
npm run build
```

### Whakamātautau

Kei roto i te tāpiri tētahi anga whakamātautau whānui:

- **Ngā Whakamātautau Waeine**: Ngā whakamātautau e hāngai ana ki PHPUnit me te huinga whakamātautau WordPress
- **Karaehe Whakamātautau Taketake**: Ka whakarato tikanga āwhina mō ngā āhuatanga whakamātautau noa
- **Kapi Whakamātautau**: Kua whirihorahia kia aroturuki i te kapi waehere
- **Whakamātautau Pae-maha**: Ka whakahaerehia ngā whakamātautau i te taiao pae-maha

### Ngā Paerewa Waehere

- **Ngā Paerewa Waehere WordPress**: Ka whakamanahia mā PHPCS
- **Hototahitanga PHP 7.4+**: Ka tautokona ngā āhuatanga PHP hou
- **Tātari Pūmau**: Whakaurunga PHPStan mō te arowhai momo
- **Whakahou Waehere**: Ngā ture Rector mō ngā whakahōu PHP

### Pūnaha Hanga

- **Tukatuka Rawa**: Whakaiti mō ngā kōnae CSS/JS
- **Whakamāoritanga**: Hanga kōnae POT
- **Pōkai**: Waihanga pūranga mō te tohatoha
- **Aratau Whakawhanake**: Ngā rawa kāore i whakaititia mō te patuiro

## Whirihoranga

Ka tautoko te tāpiri i ngā kōwhiringa whirihoranga huhua e wātea ana mā te atanga kaiwhakahaere whatunga.

### Whirihoranga kairīhi rangatiratanga

Mō ngā kairīhi rangatiratanga, whirihorahia te pātengi raraunga kairīhi, te pūtake pūnaha kōnae, te here hautū pātengi raraunga, me te āheinga hautū i mua i te ararere i te waka ki te kairīhi. Whakahaerehia te manatoko hekenga i muri i ia huringa e pā ana ki ngā taipitopito takiuru pātengi raraunga, te here hautū, te ararere, te whakarato kaiwhakamahi rānei.

Whakamahia `localhost` anake ina tūmanako te wāwhakahaere kairīhi me ngā whakaaetanga pātengi raraunga ki te turanga ā-rohe, ki te uru mīhini-kotahi rānei. Whakamahia `127.0.0.1`, tētahi ingoa hautū ratonga rānei ina koirā te hautū whakaaetanga kua whirihorahia e tō kaiwhakarato.

### Āheinga Hostinger

Ka mahi te āheinga kairīhi-maha Hostinger me te tohu whakaurunga Hostinger tiritahi. Whakaūngia kei roto te rohe i hPanel, e tohu ana a DNS ki te Account Hostinger, ā, e ōrite ana ngā tautuhinga pātengi raraunga kairīhi ki hPanel i mua i te manatoko.

## Ngā Hook me ngā Tātari

### Ngā Hohenga

- `ultimate-multisite-addon-template_init` - Ka whakakāngia i muri i te arawhitinga tāpiri
- `ultimate-multisite-addon-template_loaded` - Ka whakakāngia i muri i te utanga o ngā kōnae tāpiri katoa

### Ngā Tātari

- `ultimate-multisite-addon-template_settings` - Whakakēhia ngā tautuhinga tāpiri
- `ultimate-multisite-addon-template_enabled` - Takahia te tūnga whakahohe/mono o te tāpiri
