# Rangitaki panonitanga {#changelog}

## 1.18.0 — I tukuna i 2026-06-29 {#1180--released-on-2026-06-29}

### Hou {#new}

- **Ngā taputapu Google Calendar** — pānui i ngā maramataka me ngā takunetanga kua whirihoratia mō ngā aunoatanga mōhio-hōtaka
- **Mahere whakapā me ngā kaitautoko kaitāpae** — whakaōrite i ngā kaiuru maramataka ki ngā kaiwhakamahi pae me ngā whakapā
- **Ngā kēti whakaaetanga tangata me ngā pūkete whakamaumahara** — whakatārewatia ngā aunoatanga hei arotake, ā, karohia ngā whakamōhiotanga tārite
- **Kaiwhakarato SMS TextBee** — tuku whakamōhiotanga karere-kuputuhi kua whirihoratia mā TextBee
- **Mōkihi hoa arā atu anō** — tāpiri i ngā taputapu pūnaha-kōnae kaiwhakawhanake-whirinaki, pātengi raraunga, WP-CLI, kaituku REST, kaihanga plugin, whakaahua git, whakahaere-kaiwhakamahi, me ngā taputapu paetohu e tohaina motuhake ana i te hanga WordPress.org

### Kua whakapaingia {#improved}

- **Tatūnga ratonga Superdav kua whakahaerehia** — tāpiri i ngā endpoint ratonga kua hautūngia me te whakarato hononga aunoa mō ngā pae tautokona
- **Whakapōkai tukunga** — hangaia ngā ZIP matua me Advanced motuhake, whakaputaina ngā mea e rua ki GitHub, ā, tukuna ko te mōkihi matua anake ki WordPress.org

### Kua whakatikaina {#fixed}

- **Pono tono AI** — whakapai ake i te kōwhiringa tauira, ngā paunga-wā tono, te tauira tatūnga taunoa, te whakahaere kuputuhi-whakaaro, me te aratohu whakamātau-anō mō ngā karanga taputapu muhu
- **Whakakaha maramataka me te whakamaumahara** — whakakaha i ngā token Google Calendar me te whakakore-tārite whakamaumahara
- **Whakataki me ngā tīmata-anō whakaaetanga** — whakatika i ngā whakarewanga whakataki frontend me ngā tīmata-anō āheinga kua whakaūngia
- **Ngā take arotake whakapōkai WordPress.org** — whakatutuki i ngā urupare arotake whakapōkai mō te tukunga matua

## 1.16.0 — I tukuna i 2026-05-20 {#1160--released-on-2026-05-20}

### Hou {#new-1}

- **Āheinga Waihanga Logo SVG** — ka taea ināianei e Theme Builder te waihanga me te tāmau i ngā SVG waitohu ritenga me te whakawātea haumaru-mokowāingoa
- **Tukuake whakaahua i te uiui tūhura** — kei roto ināianei i te uiui tūhura Theme Builder tētahi hipanga tukuake whakaahua mō te horopaki hoahoa hōhonu ake
- **Āheinga Whakamana Rerekētanga Papatae** — tirohia ngā takirua tae mō te ū ki WCAG i mua i te hoatu ki tētahi theme
- **Ngā tahua manaaki** — ka taea ināianei e Theme Builder te waihanga whārangi tahua kai me te inu kua hanganga mō ngā pakihi manaaki
- **Whakaaturanga arokite papamahi me te pūkoro** — arokite i tō hoahoa ki ngā pūrere papamahi me te pūkoro i te wā o te kōwhiringa ahunga-hoahoa
- **Tawhā tapanga whakatere** — e tautoko ana ināianei te āheinga Waihanga Tahua i tētahi `navigation_label` motuhake i tua atu i te taitara whārangi
- **Wāteatanga taputapu Tier 1** — he taputapu Tier 1 ināianei a sd-ai-agent/site-scrape e wātea taunoa ana i Theme Builder

### Kua whakatikaina {#fixed-1}

- **Keteroki AI Client** — kua tautokona ināianei e ngā transients mō te toitūtanga whakawhiti-tono, hei ārai i te ngaronga raraunga i ngā mahi agent roa
- **Ngā hononga mahi haupae plugin** — kua whakatikaina, kua whakaingoatia anō kia mārama ake

## 1.10.0 — I tukuna i 2026-05-05 {#1100--released-on-2026-05-05}

### Hou {#new-2}

- **Rapu ipurangi Tavily** — tāpiri i a Tavily hei kaiwhakarato rapu mō ngā hua rapu ipurangi whai kiko ake i te taha o Brave Search
- **Ngā pūkenga ā-roto mōhio-theme** — kua whakaurua ināianei ngā aratohu pūkenga Block Themes, Classic Themes, Kadence Blocks, me Kadence Theme ki te plugin
- **Āheinga puka whakapā kaihanga pae** — tāpirihia he puka whakapā ki tētahi whārangi ahakoa, tika tonu mai i te atanga kōrerorero

### Kua whakapaingia {#improved-1}

- **Kua whakahanganuitia te whakaurunga WooCommerce** — ka whakamahi ināianei i ngā API taketake WooCommerce mō te pono me te hototahitanga pai ake
- **Ka whakahou aunoa te rārangi kaiwhakarato** — ina whakahohehia, ina whakakorehia rānei tētahi plugin

### Kua whakatikaina {#fixed-2}

- **Āheinga navigate-to** — kua whakatikaina te koromeke uta-anō mutunga-kore ki ētahi whārangi admin
- **Āheinga list-posts** — ka whakaoti tika ināianei i ngā ingoa kāwai me ngā ingoa tūtohu hei slug
- **Ngā tono WP-CLI** — kua whakahokia mai ngā ingoa-kē mokowāingoa i ngaro i muri i tētahi whakahanganui o mua
- **Aunoatanga takunetanga** — ka whakahaere pai i ngā pae kāore anō kia waihangatia ngā tēpu aunoatanga
- **Āheinga memory-save** — ka whakamahi ināianei i te kūmua mokowāingoa tika i te kaihanga tohutohu pūnaha
- **Ngā hua taputapu Scalar** — kua tākai tika ināianei i mua i te whakahokinga ki te AI
- **Tatauranga whakamahinga** — ka whakahaere tika ināianei i te hōputu kī āheinga tuku iho i te whakahōutanga mai i ngā putanga tawhito
