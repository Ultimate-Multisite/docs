# Superdav AI Agent

Ko Superdav AI Agent he kaiāwhina AI WordPress matatau e whakawhānui ana i Gratis AI Agent mā ngā pūkenga mōhio-ki-te-kaupapa, ngā āheinga rapu ipurangi kua whakarei ake, ngā āhuatanga kaihanga pae, me ngā whakaurunga aunoa kōwhiringa. Ka tāpiri te putanga 1.18.0 i ngā taputapu pānui Google Calendar, te mahere hoapā me te hunga tae atu, ngā kūwaha whakaae tangata, te whakakore-tārite whakamaumahara, ngā whakamōhiotanga SMS TextBee, te whakarato ratonga Superdav whakahaere, me tētahi mōkihi hoa Advanced kua tohaina wehe mō ngā rerengamahi kaiwhakawhanake pono.

## Ngā Āhuatanga Matua {#key-features}

- **Ngā pūkenga mōhio-ki-te-kaupapa** — ka urutau aunoa i te aratohu me ngā āheinga i runga i tō kaupapa WordPress hohe me ngā monomai kua tāutatia
- **Ngā kaiwhakarato rapu ipurangi maha** — kōwhiria i waenga i Brave Search me Tavily mō ngā hua rapu whānui ake me ngā āheinga rangahau matatau
- **Ngā puka whakapā kaihanga pae** — waihangatia he puka whakapā tino mahi tika mai i te atanga kōrerorero mā tō monomai puka kua tāutatia
- **Kaihanga pae kua whakarei ake** — he mahere hanga pae wāhanga-maha kua whakapai ake me te kitenga monomai pai ake me te whakaora hapa
- **Ngā pūkenga kaupapa ā-roto** — he aratohu motuhake mō Block Themes, Classic Themes, Kadence Blocks, me Kadence Theme
- **Whakaurunga WooCommerce kua whakapai ake** — kua whakahāngai anō kia whakamahi i ngā API WooCommerce taketake mō te pono me te hototahi pai ake
- **Kitenga kaiwhakarato aunoa** — ka kitea, ka whakahou aunoa i ngā monomai me ngā kaupapa e wātea ana ina whakahohea, ina whakawetohia rānei
- **Waihanga waitohu ritenga** — waihanga, tāmau hoki i ngā waitohu SVG ritenga me te whakapai aunoa haumaru-namespace
- **Whakamana rerekētanga papatae** — tirohia ngā takirua tae mō te ū ki te āheitanga WCAG i mua i te hoatu ki tō kaupapa
- **Kitenga kua whakarei ki te whakaahua** — tukuatu whakaahua i te uiuitanga kitenga Theme Builder hei whakaawe i te ahunga hoahoa
- **Ngā tahua manaaki** — waihangatia ngā whārangi tahua kai me te inu kua hanganga mō ngā wharekai, ngā kawhe, me ngā pakihi manaaki
- **Ngā arokite papamahi me te pūkoro** — arokitea tō hoahoa ki ngā pūrere papamahi me te pūkoro i te kōwhiringa ahunga-hoahoa
- **Waruwaru pae Taumata 1** — kei te wātea taunoa ināianei te sd-ai-agent/site-scrape mō te tātari i ngā paetukutuku kaiwhakataetae me ngā paetukutuku whakahihiko
- **Ngā taputapu pānui Google Calendar** — pānuitia ngā maramataka me ngā takahanga kua whirihoratia kia taea e ngā aunoatanga te whakaaro mō te horopaki hōtaka e heke mai ana
- **Mahere hoapā me te hunga tae atu** — tauritea ngā kaiuru maramataka ki ngā kaiwhakamahi pae me ngā hoapā e mōhiotia ana i mua i te waihanga mahi whai-ake
- **Ngā kūwaha whakaae tangata** — whakatārewatia ngā aunoatanga tairongo kia arotakengia i mua i te tuku karere, te whakaara anō i te mahi, te mahi panonitanga rānei
- **Ngā pūkete whakamaumahara me te whakakore-tārite** — aroturukitia ngā whakamaumahara kua tukuna kē kia kaua ngā whakahaere aunoatanga tukurua e whakamōhio i taua kaiwhiwhi anō kia rua
- **Ngā whakamōhiotanga SMS TextBee** — tukuna ngā whakamōhiotanga karere-kuputuhi kua whirihoratia mā TextBee ina whakahohea tētahi kaiwhakarato SMS
- **Whakarato ratonga Superdav whakahaere** — hono atu ngā pae tautokona ki ngā endpoint Superdav manaaki me te tatūnga aunoa, kaua ki te whirihoranga endpoint ā-ringa
- **Mōkihi hoa Advanced** — he taputapu kaiwhakawhanake-pono kua tohaina wehe mō te pūnaha kōnae, te pātengi raraunga, WP-CLI, te kaiwhakahaere REST, te kaihanga monomai, te hopuāhua git, te whakahaere-kaiwhakamahi, me ngā rerengamahi paearu

## Ngā Whakaritenga {#requirements}

- WordPress 6.0 neke atu rānei
- PHP 8.1 neke atu rānei
- WordPress Multisite (kua whakahohe-whatunga) tētahi tāutatanga pae-kotahi rānei
- He monomai tūhono kaiwhakarato AI hohe (Anthropic, OpenAI, he hototahi rānei)
- Gratis AI Agent v1.9.0 neke atu rānei (ka whakawhānui a Superdav AI Agent i Gratis AI Agent)

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Tukuatu ngā kōnae monomai ki `/wp-content/plugins/superdav-ai-agent`
2. Whakahohea te monomai mā te mata **Ngā Monomai** i WordPress
3. Me whakarite kua whakahohea hoki a Gratis AI Agent
4. Whakatere ki **Gratis AI Agent → Tautuhinga** ka whirihora i tō kaiwhakarato rapu ipurangi, hononga ratonga Superdav whakahaere, Google Calendar, ngā taipitopito tuakiri TextBee rānei ina hiahiatia

Kei roto i te mōkihi WordPress.org ngā āhuatanga matua o Superdav AI Agent. Ka hangaia, ka tohaina wehe te mōkihi hoa Advanced mō ngā taiao e tāutatia, e whakamana mārika ana ngā taputapu kaiwhakawhanake pono.

## Ngā Kaiwhakarato Rapu Ipurangi {#internet-search-providers}

Ka tāpiri a Superdav AI Agent v1.10.0 i **Tavily** hei kē atu mō Brave Search mō ngā uiui rapu ipurangi. Ka tuku ngā kaiwhakarato e rua i ngā hua rapu wā-tūturu, ā, ka taea te whirihora ki **Gratis AI Agent → Tautuhinga → Matatau**.

| Kaituku | Āhuatanga | Tatūnga |
|---|---|---|
| **Brave Search** | Ngā hua rapu tere, e aro ana ki te tūmataitinga | [Brave Search API](https://brave.com/search/api/) |
| **Tavily** | Ngā hua rapu whai kiko ake, ngā āheinga rangahau arā atu anō | [Tavily API](https://tavily.com/) |

Tirohia [Rapu Ipurangi](../../user-guide/configuration/internet-search) mō ngā tohutohu whirihoranga.

## Ngā Rerengamahi Maramataka, Whakaaetanga, me te Whakamōhiotanga {#calendar-approval-and-notification-workflows}

Ka whakawhānui a Superdav AI Agent v1.18.0 i ngā rerengamahi aunoatanga ki tua atu i ngā mahi ihirangi me te hanga pae:

- Mā **ngā taputapu Google Calendar** e āhei ai te agent ki te pānui i ngā maramataka kua whirihorahia me ngā taipitopito takahanga ina wātea ngā taunaki maramataka.
- Mā **ngā kaitautoko whakapā me te hunga tae atu** e mahere ngā kaiuru takahanga ki ngā kaiwhakamahi WordPress, ki ngā whakapā mōhiotia rānei, i mua i te whakarite whakamaumahara, mahi whaiwhai rānei.
- Mā **ngā kēti whakaaetanga tangata** e āhei ai tētahi aunoatanga ki te whakatā kia arotakehia rā anō e tētahi kaiwhakamahi whai mana te hohenga kua whakaarohia.
- Mā **ngā pūkete whakamaumahara** e ārai ngā whakamaumahara maramataka, whakamōhiotanga rānei tārite ina whakamātauria anō, ina whakaritea auautia rānei tētahi aunoatanga.
- Mā **te tautoko TextBee SMS** e whakaahei ngā whakamōhiotanga karere-tuhi ina kua whirihorahia te kaituku TextBee.

Whakamahia ēnei taputapu mō te āwhina e mōhio ana ki te hōtaka, te whaiwhai i te hunga tae atu, me ngā rerengamahi whakamaumahara e hiahia tonu ana ki te arotake tangata i mua i te tukunga o ngā karere tairongo.

## Whirihoranga {#configuration}

Kei raro ngā tautuhinga katoa i **Gratis AI Agent → Settings** i te kaiwhakahaere WordPress. Ka tāpiri a Superdav AI Agent i ēnei kōwhiringa whirihoranga:

| Tautuhinga | Whakaahuatanga |
|---|---|
| **Internet Search Provider** | Kōwhiria i waenga i Brave Search me Tavily (kōwhiringa) |
| **Tavily API Key** | API key mō te rapu ipurangi Tavily (mēnā kua kōwhiria a Tavily) |
| **Managed Superdav Service** | Honoa ngā pae tautokona ki te ratonga Superdav kua hautūngia me te whakarato endpoint aunoa |
| **Google Calendar Credentials** | Whirihorahia te urunga maramataka kia taea ai e te agent te pānui maramataka me ngā takahanga |
| **TextBee SMS Provider** | Whirihorahia ngā taunaki TextBee mō ngā rerengamahi whakamōhiotanga SMS kua whakaaetia |

## Mōkihi Hoa Advanced {#advanced-companion-package}

Ka tāpiri a Superdav AI Agent v1.18.0 i tētahi mōkihi hoa Advanced ka tohaina motuhaketia mō ngā rerengamahi kaiwhakawhanake pono. Kāore ēnei āheinga i te paiherea ki roto i te mōkihi matua WordPress.org. Tāutatia te mōkihi hoa ki ngā pae anake e tino whakawhirinaki ai ngā kaiwhakahaere ki te agent kia uru ki ngā taputapu kaiwhakawhanake.

Kei roto i ngā taputapu Advanced ngā whakahaerenga pūnaha-kōnae, urunga pātengi raraunga, WP-CLI, rerengamahi kaituku REST, taputapu kaihanga plugin, hopuāhua git, kaitautoko whakahaere-kaiwhakamahi, me ngā taputapu ine paearu. Kia herea ēnei taputapu ki ngā kaiwhakahaere pono, ā, arotakengia te whirihoranga kēti-whakaaetanga i mua i te whakahohe i runga i ngā pae whakaputa.

- Kua whakatikahia te āheinga `navigate-to` i whakatupu koromeke uta-anō mutunga-kore ki ētahi whārangi kaiwhakahaere
- Kua whakatikahia te āheinga `list-posts` kāore i whakatau tika i ngā ingoa kāwai me ngā ingoa tūtohu hei slugs
- Kua whakatikahia ngā whakahau WP-CLI i ngaro ai ngā ingoa kē namespace i muri i tētahi refactor o mua

- Kua whakatikahia te āheinga `memory-save` kia whakamahi i te kuhimua namespace tika i roto i te kaihanga tohutohu pūnaha
- Kua whakatikahia ngā hua taputapu scalar kia tika te takai i mua i te whakahokinga atu ki te AI
- Kua whakatikahia ngā tatauranga whakamahinga kia tika te whakahaere i te hōputu tawhito o te kī āheinga i te whakahoutanga mai i ngā putanga tawhito ake
