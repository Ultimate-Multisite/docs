# Rārangi Huringa VAT {#vat-changelog}

Putanga 1.0.7 - I tukuna i 2026-02-03

* Whakatika: I rahua te whakamana i ngā tau VAT Tiamana (DE) nā ngā hapa MS_MAX_CONCURRENT_REQ o te ratonga VIES SOAP. I whakawhitia mai i te SOAP API tawhito ki te EU VIES REST API mana mō te whakamana pono ake.
* Whakatika: Ka whakaaetia ināianei ngā tau VAT ina wātea-kore rangitahi te ratonga VIES, kaua e whakakāhoretia hētia. Ka waihangatia he tāurunga rangitaki hei arotake ā muri ake.
* Whakatika: Ka kitea ināianei te āpure VAT/Tax ID mō ngā whenua katoa, kaua ko ngā mema EU anake. Ka rokirokitia ngā ID tāke ehara i te EU (hei tauira, ngā tau CHE o Huiterangi) hei whakaatu ki te nama utu, kāore he whakamana VIES.
* Whakatika: I pakaru te rapunga kuhimua VAT mō Kirihi (GR), Monaco (MC), me te Isle of Man (IM) nā te arorau rapu huānga hē.
* Whakapai ake: I tangohia te whakawhirinakitanga whanaketanga wp-cli-bundle kāore i hiahiatia, tērā pea ka puta he hapa whakamate ina whakamahia ngātahitia me te tāpiritanga Site Exporter.
* Whakapai ake: I tāpirihia he huinga whakamātautau wae whānui (53 ngā whakamātautau).

Putanga 1.0.6 - I tukuna i 2026-01-25

* Whakatika: Ka utaina tika ināianei te VAT ki ngā tauwhitinga B2B ā-rohe. E ai ki ngā ture VAT a EU, ka hāngai te utu whakamuri ki ngā tauwhitinga B2B whakawhiti-rohe anake, kāore ina ōrite te whenua o te kiritaki ki te whenua o te kamupene.

Putanga 1.0.5 - I tukuna i 2026-01-22

* Whakatika: Kāore te tāpiritanga i te uta, i te mahi tika rānei.
* Huringa: I whakawhitia te pūtake raraunga reiti VAT mai i euvatrates.com ki te pātaka ibericode/vat-rates mō ngā raraunga pono ake, e tiakina hohe ana hoki.
* Whakatika: I whakatikaina te hē pato o te uara kōwhiringa super_reduced_rates.
* Whakapai ake: I tāpirihia ngā arowhai null mō te whakahaere waehere whenua hei ārai hapa.
* Huringa: I tangohia ngā kōnae whakamāori kua paiherea, kia whakamahi kē i ngā whakahōu aunoa mā Traduttore.
