# Kaiwhakahaere Monomai me te Kaupapa

Ultimate Multisite: Kaiwhakahaere Monomai me te Kaupapa

Whakatikaina, huna rānei ngā mōhiohio meta (taitara, kaituhi, karakōnui me te whakaahuatanga) o ngā monomai me ngā kaupapa kua tāutatia. Waihangatia he kāwai mō ngā monomai me ngā kaupapa kia āhei ai ō kaiwhakamahi ki te tātari i aua mea, ā, tāpirihia he whārangi Monomai ritenga ātaahua ki te paewhiri a ō kaiwhakamahi.

Hei whakamahi i te monomai, me whakatere noa ki ō whārangi Monomai, Kaupapa rānei i tō kaiwhakahaere whatunga, ā, pāwhiri i te "Whakatika mōhiohio Monomai/Kaupapa".

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Tukuna ake 'ultimate-multisite-plugin-and-theme-manager' ki te whaiaronga '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Whakahohea te monomai mā te tahua 'Monomai' i tō WordPress Network Admin

## Aratohu Whakamahi {#usage-guide}

Ultimate Multisite: He tāpiri **utu** kaha te Kaiwhakahaere Monomai me te Kaupapa mō Ultimate Multisite e hoatu ana ki a koe te mana katoa ki te āhua e kite ai, e pāhekoheko ai hoki ō kaiwhakamahi whatunga ki ngā kōwhiringa monomai me ngā kōwhiringa kaupapa i tō whatunga.

E toru ngā kaupapa o tēnei tāpiri:

  1. Ka tuku i a koe kia tino panoni i ngā raraunga meta o ngā monomai me ngā kaupapa. Kei roto ko te ingoa, te whakaahuatanga, te ingoa kaituhi, tae atu hoki ki ngā karakōnui!

  2. Ka tuku i a koe kia whakarōpū i ngā monomai me ngā kaupapa, kia āhei ai ō kaiwhakamahi ki te kōmaka ngāwari i aua mea.

  3. Ka hoatu ki a koe te kōwhiringa ki te whakakapi i te whārangi monomai tawhito, hōhā hoki ki tētahi whārangi hou he rite ki ngā kaupapa, me te kōmaka mā ngā kāwai.

Whai muri i te tikiake me te tāuta, ka puta mai he ripa hou - _Tautuhinga Tāpiri_ \- ki te whārangi Tautuhinga Ultimate Multisite.
