# AffiliateWP {#affiliatewp}

Ultimate Multisite: Whakaurunga AffiliateWP

Whakamahia te AffiliateWP kaha hei whakatipu i te pūtake kiritaki o tō Ultimo Network!

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Tukuake 'ultimate-multisite-git ' ki te whaiaronga '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Whakahohe i te mono mā te tahua 'Ngā Mono' i WordPress
3. Whāia te Wizard ā-taahiraa ki te whakarite i te mono

## Aratohu Whakamahi {#usage-guide}

Kua noho a [AffiliateWP](https://affiliatewp.com/) hei otinga matua ina hangaia he hōtaka hoa hokohoko mō tō pakihi tuihono. Ultimate Multisite: AffiliateWP te piriti e āhei ai koe ki te hanga i taua mea anō mō tō whatunga moni.

## Tīmata {#getting-started}

Tuatahi rawa, **me whai** koe i a [**AffiliateWP**](https://affiliatewp.com/) **kua hohe**! Kāore koe e āhei ki te whakamahi i tā mātou tāpiri mēnā kāore i a koe **te mono e whakauru ana ki a ia**. Ki te kore koe e whakaaro kia whakamahia e ō kiritaki ki ō rātou pae hoki, me hohe a AffiliateWP ki tō pae matua anake.

Waihoki, me **whakahohe** **puta noa i te whatunga** a **Ultimate Multisite** me **Ultimate Multisite: AffiliateWP**.

## Te tāpiri i a Ultimate Multisite hei Whakaurunga AffiliateWP {#adding-ultimate-multisite-as-an-affiliatewp-integration}

I tō pae matua, haere ki Ngā Tautuhinga Affiliates, ā, i te ripa Integrations, tohua a Ultimate Multisite. Mā tēnei e kī atu ki [AffiliateWP](https://affiliatewp.com/) kia tīmata te aroturuki i ngā toronga me ngā whakawhitinga mai i te rere rēhitatanga Ultimate Multisite.

<!-- Kāore te whakaahua mata i te wātea: ripa tautuhinga AffiliateWP Integrations me te pouakataki Ultimate Multisite -->

_Tohua te pouaka Ultimate Multisite kei raro, ā, tiakina_

Koia noa iho te nuinga e hiahiatia ana. Ka aroturukihia ngā toronga e ahu mai ana i ngā hononga tuku, ā, ina utu tuatahi taua kaiwhakamahi ā muri ake, ka waihangatia te whakawhitinga, ā, ka whakatahatia he kōmihana mā te hoa hokohoko nāna te whakawhitinga.

Heoi anō, ko te utu tuatahi anake ka riro mai i te kaiwhakamahi hou kua waihangatia ka aroturukihia. Hei whakahohe i ngā utu tuku auau, me whai koe i te [AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals Add-on](https://affiliatewp.com/add-ons/pro/recurring-referrals/)

## Te Whakahohe i ngā Tuku Auau {#enabling-recurring-referrals}

Whai muri i te tāuta i te [AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals Add-on](https://affiliatewp.com/add-ons/pro/recurring-referrals/), haere ki te ripa Affiliates Settings Recurring Referrals, ā, whakahohea te whakamahi i ngā tuku auau.

<!-- Kāore te whakaahua mata i te wātea: tautuhinga AffiliateWP Recurring Referrals me te whakakā Enable Recurring Referrals -->

_He ngāwari rawa: whakahohea te Enable Recurring Referrals, ā, kua rite koe!_

Anō, koia noa iho te mea e hiahiatia ana!

Ināianei, ia wā ka riro mai he utu hou ki tētahi Account kua tukuna mai, ka whiwhi aunoa te hoa hokohoko nāna i tuku i tana wāhanga ake!
