# Mailster

Hurihia tō whatunga pae-maha hei mīhini kaha mō te hanga rārangi īmēra mā te tāpiri Tuituinga Ultimate Multisite Mailster. Tāpirihia aunoatia ngā kiritaki hou ki ō rārangi īmēra Mailster ina rēhita rātou, ina hoko rānei, e waihanga ana i tētahi hononga māeneene i waenga i tō tūāpapa mematanga me tō rautaki hokohoko īmēra.

## Ngā Āhuatanga Matua {#key-features}

**Tukutahi Kaiohauru Aunoa**
* Tāpirihia aunoatia ngā kiritaki ki ngā rārangi Mailster ina rēhita rātou
* Kōwhiria kia tukutahi tonutia i te rēhitatanga, kia tatari rānei mō te whakaūnga utu
* Puritia ō rārangi īmēra kia hou, kia tika hoki me te kore wawaotanga ā-ringa

**Whakahaere Rārangi Ngāwari**
* Tautuhia ngā rārangi taunoa ā-ao mō ngā kiritaki hou katoa
* Whakakapia ngā taunoa ki ngā rārangi motuhake-ki-te-hua mō ngā kaupapa whakatairanga whāinga
* Whakahaeretia ngā tautapatanga rārangi mā te māmā, mā te atanga taunga o Mailster

**Ngā Kōwhiringa Whakaae E Hāngai ana ki GDPR**
* Kōwhiria i waenga i te ohaurunga aunoa me te whakaaetanga ā-pouakataki
* Whakahohea te whakaae-rua mō ngā ohaurunga kua whakaūngia
* Whakaatu/huna i te pouakataki whakaae i runga i ō whakaritenga tautukunga

**Mahere Raraunga Atamai**
* Tukutahia aunoatia ngā ingoa kiritaki me ngā mōhiohio whakapā
* Maheretia ngā wāhitau nama ki ngā āpure kaiohauru
* Aroturukihia ngā rā rēhita mō ngā kaupapa whakatairanga whāinga

**Mana Taumata-Hua**
* Tautapatia ngā rārangi rerekē ki ngā hua rerekē
* Waihangatia ngā kaupapa whakatairanga whāinga i runga i ngā mea e hokona ana e ngā kiritaki
* Monokia a Mailster mō ngā hua motuhake ina hiahiatia

**Wheako Ngaio**
* Tuituinga UI taketake o Mailster mō te kōwhiri rārangi
* Rangitaki taipitopito mō te rapurongoā me te manatoko
* Ngā whakahōunga māeneene o ngā kaiohauru o nāianei

## He Pai Mō {#perfect-for}

* Ngā rangatira pae mematanga e hanga ana i ngā rārangi īmēra
* Ngā tūāpapa SaaS e hiahia ana ki te poipoi i ngā kiritaki hou
* Ngā kaihanga akoranga e wehewehe ana i ngā ākonga mā te whakaurunga
* Te tangata e whakahaere ana i Ultimate Multisite me Mailster

## Te Āhua o Tana Mahi {#how-it-works}

1. Tāutahia, whakahohea hoki te tāpiri ki tō whatunga Ultimate Multisite
2. Whirihorahia ngā rārangi taunoa ā-ao ki ngā tautuhinga Ultimate Multisite
3. Ki te hiahia, whakakapia ki ngā rārangi motuhake-ki-te-hua
4. Kōwhiria tō aratau whakaae me te wā ohaurunga
5. Mātakina ō rārangi Mailster e tipu aunoa ana!

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Tukuatu ngā kōnae plugin ki tō whaiaronga tāpiri Ultimate Multisite
2. Me whakarite kua tāutahia, kua hohe hoki a Mailster ki tō pae matua
3. Whakahohea te tāpiri mā te whārangi tāpiri Ultimate Multisite
4. Haere ki Tautuhinga > Tuituinga Mailster hei whirihora
5. Tīpakohia ō rārangi taunoa, ā, whirihorahia ngā manakohanga whakaae

## Ngā Pātai Auau {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Ka mahi tēnei tāpiri me te kore Ultimate Multisite? {#does-this-addon-work-without-ultimate-multisite}

Kāo, me whai Ultimate Multisite tēnei tāpiri kia mahi ai. Kua hoahoatia motuhaketia hei tuitui i a Mailster ki te pūnaha kiritaki me te hua o Ultimate Multisite.

## Me tāuta a Mailster ki ngā pae katoa? {#do-i-need-mailster-installed-on-all-sites}

Kāo, me tāuta, me whakahohe anake a Mailster ki tō pae whatunga matua. Ka hono aunoa te tāpiri ki reira mai i ngā pae-iti katoa.

## Ka taea e au te tautapa rārangi rerekē ki ngā hua rerekē? {#can-i-assign-different-lists-to-different-products}

Āe! Ka taea e koe te tautuhi rārangi taunoa ā-ao, kātahi ka whakakapi i aua mea i runga i ia hua. Mā tēnei ka taea e koe te waihanga kaupapa whakatairanga īmēra tino whāinga i runga i ngā mea e hokona ana e ngā kiritaki.

## Ka ahatia mēnā kua noho kē tētahi kiritaki ki Mailster? {#what-happens-if-a-customer-already-exists-in-mailster}

Mā te taunoa, ka whakahōu te tāpiri i ngā kaiohauru o nāianei, ā, ka tāpiri i a rātou ki ngā rārangi hou. Ka taea e koe tēnei te mono ki ngā tautuhinga mēnā e pai ake ana ki a koe te peke i ngā kaiohauru o nāianei.

## E tautuku ana tēnei ki GDPR? {#is-this-gdpr-compliant}

Āe! Kei roto i te tāpiri tētahi aratau whakaae ā-pouakataki, me whakaae mārama ngā kiritaki kia whiwhi īmēra. Ka tutuki tēnei i ngā whakaritenga GDPR ina whakakotahitia ki ngā kaupapahere tūmataiti tika.

## Ka taea e au te whakamahi i te whakaae-rua? {#can-i-use-double-opt-in}

Āe rawa! Whakahohea te tautuhinga whakaae-rua, ā, ka tuku a Mailster i ngā īmēra whakaū i mua i te whakahohe i ngā ohaurunga.
