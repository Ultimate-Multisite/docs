# Ultimate AI Connector for Compatible Endpoints

Ka kōtuitui te Ultimate AI Connector for Compatible Endpoints i ngā API kaiwhakarato AI e hototahi ana ki OpenAI ki tō whatunga pae maha WordPress. Whirihorahia ngā kaiwhakarato AI maha — kei ia mea tōna ake URL pito-mutunga, kī API, me te tauira taunoa — ā, arahia ngā tono mā te hoki-aunoa ina kore tētahi kaiwhakarato e wātea.

## Ngā Āhuatanga Matua

- **Tautoko kaiwhakarato-maha**: Tāpirihia ngā kāri kaiwhakarato AI maha, kei ia mea tōna ake URL pito-mutunga, kī API, me te tauira taunoa
- **Tō-ki-te-whakaraupapa-anō**: Whakaraupapatia ngā kaiwhakarato mā te matua mā te whakamahi i te kakau pupuri i ia kāri; ka whakamahi ngā tono i ngā kaiwhakarato i runga i te raupapa
- **Ararere hoki-aunoa**: Ki te kore te kaiwhakarato matua e wātea, ka hoki aunoa ngā tono ki te kaiwhakarato kua whirihorahia e whai ake nei
- **Whirihoranga ā-kaiwhakarato**: Ka whirihorahia motuhaketia ia kaiwhakarato me ōna ake taipitopito uru me te kōwhiringa tauira
- **UI tautuhinga kāri**: He mea hanga ki runga i ngā wae Card pūmau o WordPress 6.9+ (CardBody, CardHeader, CardDivider)
- **Whakaroha-aunoa ina tāpiri**: Ka whakaroha aunoa ngā kāri kaiwhakarato hou kia taea ai e koe te whakakī wawe i ngā taipitopito
- **Whakakore keteroki hōtuhi**: Ka whakakorea te keteroki o ngā hōtuhi kaiwhakarato i te whakahōutanga monomai kia utaina ai te putanga hou rawa

## Ngā Whakaritenga

- WordPress 5.3, teitei ake rānei
- PHP 7.4, teitei ake rānei
- Ultimate Multisite plugin (e hohe ana)
- He pito-mutunga API e hototahi ana ki OpenAI (he mea whakahaere-ā-kiri, he rōpū-tuatoru rānei)

## Tāutanga

1. Tukuatu ngā kōnae tāpiri ki tō whaiaronga `/wp-content/plugins/`
2. Whakahohea te plugin mā te tahua 'Plugins' i WordPress
3. Haere ki **Ultimate Multisite → AI Connector** hei whirihora i te tāpiri

## Whirihoranga

### Tatūnga Kaiwhakarato-Maha

Ka whakamahi te mata whirihoranga i tētahi tahora kāri, ā, ko ia kāri he māngai mō tētahi kaiwhakarato AI kotahi. Ka taea e koe te tāpiri i te maha o ngā kaiwhakarato e hiahiatia ana.

#### Te Tāpiri Kaiwhakarato

1. Haere ki **Ultimate Multisite → AI Connector** i te kaiwhakahaere whatunga
2. Pāwhiritia **Tāpiri Kaiwhakarato**
3. Ka puta tētahi kāri kaiwhakarato hou, ā, ka whakaroha aunoa
4. Whakakīa ngā taipitopito kaiwhakarato:
   - **Ingoa Kaiwhakarato** — he tapanga hei tautuhi i tēnei kaiwhakarato (hei tauira, "OpenAI", "Mistral", "Local Ollama")
   - **URL Pito-mutunga** — te URL taketake o te API e hototahi ana ki OpenAI (hei tauira, `https://api.openai.com/v1`)
   - **Kī API** — tō kī API mō tēnei kaiwhakarato
   - **Tauira Taunoa** — te tautuhinga tauira hei whakamahi taunoa mō tēnei kaiwhakarato (hei tauira, `gpt-4o`, `mistral-small`)
5. Pāwhiritia **Tiaki Tautuhinga**

#### Te Tango Kaiwhakarato

Pāwhiritia te pātene **Tango** i runga i tētahi kāri kaiwhakarato hei muku. Tiakina ngā tautuhinga hei whakamahi i te panonitanga.

#### Te Whakaraupapa Anō i Ngā Kaiwhakarato

Tōia te ata pupuri (⣿) i te taha mauī o tētahi kāri kaiwhakarato hei whakaraupapa anō. Mā te raupapa e whakatau te matua hoki: ka tukuna ngā tono ki te kaiwhakarato tuatahi, ā, ka hoki ki ngā kaiwhakarato o muri mai mēnā ka rahua tētahi tono.

### Ararere Hoki

Ina rahua tētahi tono ki te kaiwhakarato matua — nā te paunga wā, te tepenga auau, te aukatinga ratonga rānei — ka ngana anō aunoa te tūhono ki te kaiwhakarato e whai ake nei i te rārangi. Ka haere tonu tēnei ki raro i te rārangi kia angitū rā anō tētahi kaiwhakarato, kia whakamātauria rā anō rānei ngā kaiwhakarato katoa.

| Āhuatanga | Whanonga |
|----------|-----------|
| Ka whakautu te kaiwhakarato matua | Ka tukuna te tono i runga i te tikanga noa |
| Ka rahua te kaiwhakarato matua | Ka whakamātauria anō te tono ki te kaiwhakarato e whai ake nei |
| Ka rahua ngā kaiwhakarato katoa | Ka whakahokia he hapa ki te kaitono |

He pūataata te hoki ki te plugin, theme rānei e karanga ana — ka whiwhi rātou i tētahi whakautu angitū, i tētahi hapa whakamutunga rānei, ahakoa te maha o ngā kaiwhakarato i whakamātauria.

### Tohutoro Whirihoranga Kaiwhakarato

| Āpure | Me Whai | Whakaahuatanga |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Ingoa Kaiwhakarato | Āe | Tapanga whakaatu mō tēnei kaiwhakarato |
| URL Pito-mutunga | Āe | URL taketake o te API e hototahi ana ki OpenAI |
| Kī API | Āe | Kī motuhēhēnga mō tēnei kaiwhakarato |
| Tauira Taunoa | Āe | Tautuhinga tauira e whakamahia ana ina kāore he tauira i tohua i roto i te tono |

## Raparongoā

### Kāore he kaiwhakarato i te rārangitia

Mēnā he putu te rārangi kaiwhakarato i muri i te tiaki, whakaūngia ēnei:

- I pāwhiri koe i **Tiaki Tautuhinga** i muri i te tāpiri kaiwhakarato
- Kāore tō pūtirotiro i ārai i te whārangi tautuhinga kia tiakina (tirohia te papatohu pūtirotiro mō ngā hapa JavaScript)
- Kei te hohe te plugin, ā, kei te mata kaiwhakahaere whatunga koe

### Kei te rahua ngā tono mō ngā kaiwhakarato katoa

- Manatokohia ka taea te toro atu ki te URL pito-mutunga o ia kaiwhakarato mai i tō tūmau (ehara i tō pūtirotiro anake)
- Tirohia kei te tika ia kī API, ā, kāore anō kia mōnehu
- Arotakengia te tūnga tepenga-auau me te kōta a te kaiwhakarato i tō rātou Dashboard
- Whakahohea te rangitaki patuiro WordPress (`WP_DEBUG_LOG`) hei hopu i ngā karere hapa taipitopito mai i te tūhono

### Kāore tētahi kaiwhakarato e whakamahia ana mō te hoki

Ka panoni te tō-ki-te-whakaraupapa-anō i te raupapa hoki. Whakaūngia kei te tūnga tika i te rārangi te kaiwhakarato e tūmanakohia ana e koe kia whiwhi i ngā tono hoki, ā, kei te tika tana URL pito-mutunga me tana kī API.

### Kāore te kāri kaiwhakarato hou e whakaroha aunoa

Me whakahohe te JavaScript i tō pūtirotiro kia mahi ai te whakaroha-aunoa. Ki te kore te kāri e whakaroha ina pāwhiria, tirohia te papatohu pūtirotiro mō ngā hapa. Ka taea tonu ngā ihirangi kāri te toro mā te pāwhiri i te pane kāri ahakoa kāore te whakaroha-aunoa e tīmata.
