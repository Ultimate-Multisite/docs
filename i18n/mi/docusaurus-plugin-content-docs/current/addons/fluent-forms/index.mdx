# Fluent Forms

Mā tēnei tāpiritanga ka taea te tāpiri tepe ki Fluent Forms i runga i Multisite Ultimate i ngā hua, ngā mematanga, ngā pae rānei.
Ka whakamahi a Fluent Forms i ngā ripanga ritenga hei utu mō ngā momo whakairinga ritenga, nō reira kāore ngā kōwhiringa tepenga noa e mahi ki Fluent Forms.

## Āhuatanga

- Tāpiri tepe mō Fluent Forms, mō Conversational Fluent Forms rānei
- Ka whiwhi ngā kiritaki i tētahi karere hoa e akiaki ana kia whakahou ake i tā rātou mahere ina tae rātou ki te tepe
