# Ngā Moni Maha {#multi-currency}

Ultimate Multisite: Ngā Moni Maha ka āhei tō whatunga ki te whakaae utu i roto i ngā moni maha. Ka whakawhiti aunoatia ngā utu mā te whakamahi i ngā reiti whakawhiti ora, ā-ringa rānei, ka tuku kia whakaritea e koe ngā utu pū mō ia hua mō tētahi moni, ā, ka kite hoki i te moni manakohia e ia manuhiri mā te tauwāhi ā-whenua — ka whakahaerehia katoatia mai i tētahi Dashboard kaiwhakahaere kotahi.

## Ngā Āhuatanga Matua {#key-features}

- **Whakawhitinga reiti whakawhiti aunoa** — ka whakawhitihia ngā utu mai i tō moni taketake mā ngā reiti i tikina mai i tō kaiwhakarato i kōwhiria
- **Ngā whakakapi utu mō ia hua** — whakaritea ngā utu pū motuhake-ki-te-moni ki runga i tētahi hua kia hipa i te whakawhitinga aunoa
- **Āpure rēhita kaiwhiri moni** — ka tuku i ngā kiritaki kia kōwhiri i tō rātou moni manakohia i te wā utu/rēhita
- **Ngā kaiwhakarato reiti whakawhiti maha** — ECB, Frankfurter, Stripe, me ngā reiti ā-ringa katoa rānei
- **Kitea moni i runga i te tauwāhi ā-whenua** — ka kōwhiri-mua aunoatia i te moni e ōrite ana ki te whenua o te manuhiri
- **Dashboard kaiwhakahaere** — whakahaerehia ngā moni hohe, ngā reiti whakawhiti, me ngā tautuhinga kaiwhakarato mai i te wāhi kotahi

## Ngā Whakaritenga {#requirements}

- Ultimate Multisite 2.0.0, teitei ake rānei
- Tāutanga WordPress Multisite
- PHP 7.4, teitei ake rānei

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Tukuna ake ngā kōnae mono ki `/wp-content/plugins/ultimate-multisite-multi-currency`
2. Whakahohea te mono mā te mata **Ngā Mono** i WordPress (Whakahohe Whatunga)
3. Whakatere ki **Ultimate Multisite → Ngā Moni Maha** hei whakatuwhera i te Dashboard kaiwhakahaere
4. Tāpirihia ngā moni e hiahia ana koe ki te whakaae, ā, whirihorahia tō kaiwhakarato reiti whakawhiti

## Whirihoranga {#configuration}

### Dashboard Kaiwhakahaere {#admin-dashboard}

Ko te Dashboard kaiwhakahaere Ngā Moni Maha (**Ultimate Multisite → Ngā Moni Maha**) te wāhi matua hei:

- Tāpirihia, whakahohea, mono rānei i ngā moni
- Kōwhiria tētahi kaiwhakarato reiti whakawhiti, ā, whakahoungia ngā reiti ina tonoa
- Arotakehia ngā reiti o nāianei mō ngā moni hohe katoa
- Whakaritea ngā reiti ā-ringa ina kāore ngā kaiwhakarato aunoa e hāngai ana

### Ngā Kaiwhakarato Reiti Whakawhiti {#exchange-rate-providers}

| Kaiwhakarato | Whakaahuatanga |
|---|---|
| **ECB** | Pēke Pokapū o Ūropi — he reiti koreutu ia rā mō ngā whatunga e hāngai ana ki te EUR |
| **Frankfurter** | API whakaata ECB puna-tuwhera — koreutu, kāore he kī API e hiahiatia |
| **Stripe** | Ka whakamahi i ngā reiti FX ora a Stripe — me whai tuituitanga Stripe hohe |
| **Ā-ringa** | Ka tāuru tika koe i ngā reiti; kāore he karanga API ā-waho |

Tīpakohia tō kaiwhakarato i raro i **Ultimate Multisite → Ngā Moni Maha → Tautuhinga → Kaiwhakarato Reiti Whakawhiti**. Ka taea te whakahou ā-ringa i ngā reiti i ngā wā katoa, te whakarite rānei kia whakahou aunoatia.

### Ngā Whakakapi Utu mō ia Hua {#per-product-pricing-overrides}

Ina kāore te whakawhitinga aunoa i te tino tika, ka taea e koe te whakarite utu pū mō tētahi moni motuhake ki runga i tētahi hua:

1. Whakatuwheratia te ētita hua (**Ultimate Multisite → Ngā Hua → Whakatika**)
2. Panuku ki te wāhanga **Utu Moni**
3. Tāurua te utu pū mō ia moni e hiahia ana koe ki te whakakapi
4. Tiakina te hua

Ko ngā hua kāore he whakakapi ka whakamahi i te utu kua whakawhitihia mai i tō moni taketake.

### Āpure Rēhita Kaiwhiri Moni {#currency-selector-signup-field}

Ka taea te tāpiri te āpure kaiwhiri moni ki tō rerenga rēhita/utu kia kōwhiri ai ngā kiritaki i tō rātou moni manakohia i te rēhitatanga:

1. Haere ki **Ultimate Multisite → Ngā Moni Maha → Tautuhinga**
2. Whakahohea **Whakaaturia te kaiwhiri moni i te rēhitatanga**
3. Ki te hiahia, whakaritea tētahi moni taunoa, waiho rānei mā te tauwāhi ā-whenua e whakatau te uara kua kōwhiria-mua

Ka rokirokitia te moni kua tīpakohia me te ohaurunga, ā, ka whakamahia mō ngā utu nama katoa o muri mai.

### Kitea Moni i Runga i te Tauwāhi ā-Whenua {#geolocation-based-currency-detection}

Ina whakahohea te kitenga tauwāhi ā-whenua, ka mahere te tāpiritanga i te whenua o te manuhiri ki tētahi moni, ā, ka kōwhiri-mua i taua moni ki te āpure kaiwhiri moni. Hei whakahohe:

1. Haere ki **Ultimate Multisite → Ngā Moni Maha → Tautuhinga**
2. Whakahohea **Kitea te moni mai i te tauwāhi o te manuhiri**
3. Maheretia ngā whenua ki ngā moni i te ripanga **Mahere Tauwāhi ā-Whenua**

Ki te kore he mahere mō te whenua o tētahi manuhiri, ka whakamahia te moni taketake o te whatunga hei kōwhiringa hoki.

## Ngā Pātai Auau {#frequently-asked-questions}

### Ka aha mēnā kāore tētahi kaiwhakarato reiti whakawhiti e wātea ana? {#what-happens-if-an-exchange-rate-provider-is-unavailable}

Ka hoki te tāpiritanga ki ngā reiti whakamutunga i tikina angitu. Ki te kore anō kia tikina he reiti, ka hoki ki ngā reiti ā-ringa kua whirihorahia e koe. E taunakitia ana kia whirihorahia ngā reiti ā-ringa hei kupenga haumaru.

### Ka taea e au te whakamahi kaiwhakarato rerekē mō ngā moni rerekē? {#can-i-use-different-providers-for-different-currencies}

Kāo — kotahi te kaiwhakarato e hohe ana i te wā kotahi mō ngā moni katoa. Whakamahia **Ā-ringa** mēnā me whai mana katoa koe ki ngā reiti takitahi.

### Me whai mono motuhake te tauwāhi ā-whenua? {#does-geolocation-require-a-separate-plugin}

Kāore he mono tauwāhi ā-whenua ā-waho e hiahiatia. Ka whakamahi te tāpiritanga i te kitenga IP-ki-te-whenua kua hangaia ki roto i WordPress. Mō te tika ake, ka taea te tuitui tētahi ratonga tauwāhi ā-whenua motuhake mā ngā matau tātari kua whakaratohia.

### Ka whakamahia ngā whakakapi mō ia hua i mua, i muri rānei i te tāke? {#are-per-product-overrides-applied-before-or-after-tax}

Ka whakarite ngā whakakapi mō ia hua i te utu i mua i te tāke i roto i te moni ūnga. Ka tātaihia te tāke ki runga ake i te utu whakakapi mā te whakamahi i tō whirihoranga tāke o nāianei.
