# Loco Translate {#loco-translate}

Ka kawea mai e te tāpiri Ultimate Multisite: Loco Translate Integration ngā āheinga whakamāoritanga motuhake-ki-te-pae ki tō whatunga multisite WordPress. Mēnā kua hiahia koe kia rerekē ngā whakamāoritanga o te monomai, o te kaupapa rānei kotahi, i ngā pae rerekē o tō whatunga, ko tēnei tāpiri te otinga tino pai.

**He Aha Tā Tēnei Tāpiri Mahi**

Ka tuitui mārire tēnei tāpiri i Ultimate Multisite ki te monomai rongonui Loco Translate, e āhei ai ia pae-iti i tō whatunga ki te pupuri i āna ake whakamāoritanga ritenga. Ahakoa e whakahaere ana koe i tētahi tūāpapa rohe-maha, e tautoko ana i ngā hapori reo rerekē, e hiahia noa ana rānei ki te ngāwari o te whakamāori puta noa i tō whatunga, mā tēnei tāpiri ka māmā noa iho.

**Tino Pai Mō:**

* Ngā pakihi rohe-maha me ngā hiahia whakakāinga rerekē
* Ngā whare mātauranga e tautoko ana i ngā hapori kanorau
* Ngā tūāpapa SaaS e tuku ana i te tautoko reo-maha
* Ngā tari tukutuku e whakahaere ana i ngā pae kiritaki maha
* Tētahi whatunga multisite e hiahia ana ki te ngāwari o te whakamāori

## Tāutanga {#installation}

1. Me whakarite kua tāutatia a Ultimate Multisite, ā, kua whakahohea ki te whatunga
2. Tāutatia, whakahohea hoki te monomai Loco Translate
3. Tukuna ake tēnei tāpiri ki tō whaiaronga monomai
4. Whakahohea ki te whatunga te tāpiri Loco Translate Integration
5. Tīmatahia te waihanga whakamāoritanga motuhake-ki-te-pae mā Loco Translate i runga i tētahi pae-iti

Kua oti! Ka mahi aunoa te tuituinga me te kore whirihoranga tāpiri.

## Ngā Pātai Auau {#frequently-asked-questions}

## Me whirihora rānei au i tētahi mea i muri i te tāutanga? {#do-i-need-to-configure-anything-after-installation}

Kāo! He aunoa te tuituinga. Ina hohe te tāpiri me Loco Translate, ka taea tonu e ngā pae-iti te tīmata ki te waihanga i ā rātou ake whakamāoritanga ritenga.

## Ka ahatia mēnā kāore tētahi pae-iti e waihanga whakamāoritanga ritenga? {#what-happens-if-a-subsite-doesnt-create-custom-translations}

Ka whakamahi aunoa te pae i ngā whakamāoritanga puta noa i te whatunga hei kōwhiringa hoki. Kāore he mea e pakaru, ā, ka mahi mārire ngā mea katoa.

## Ka taea e ngā pae-iti te uru ki ngā whakamāoritanga puta noa i te whatunga? {#can-subsites-access-network-wide-translations}

Ka kite, ka whakatika hoki ngā kaiwhakahaere pae noa i ngā whakamāoritanga o tō rātou ake pae anake. Ka taea e ngā kaiwhakahaere matua te uru ki ngā whakamāoritanga puta noa i te whatunga me ngā whakamāoritanga motuhake-ki-te-pae, e ai ki te pae e tirohia ana e rātou.

## Ka mahi tēnei ki ngā monomai me ngā kaupapa? {#does-this-work-with-both-plugins-and-themes}

Āe! Ka mahi ngā whakamāoritanga motuhake-ki-te-pae mō ngā monomai me ngā kaupapa WordPress.

## Ka whakatōmuri tēnei i taku whatunga multisite? {#will-this-slow-down-my-multisite-network}

Kāo. Ka whakamahi te tāpiri i te uta kōnae tōtika, ā, ka tirotiro noa mō ngā whakamāoritanga motuhake-ki-te-pae ina hiahiatia. Kāore he pānga mahi ka kitea.

## Ka taea e au te tārua i ngā whakamāoritanga whatunga ki tētahi pae motuhake hei tīmatanga? {#can-i-copy-network-translations-to-a-specific-site-as-a-starting-point}

Āe! I a koe e whakatika ana i ngā whakamāoritanga i Loco Translate, ka taea e koe te tīmata ngāwari ki ngā whakamāoritanga whatunga, ā, te whakarite mō ia pae.

## Ka mahi tēnei ki WooCommerce me ētahi atu monomai? {#does-this-work-with-woocommerce-and-other-plugins}

Āe, tino! Ka mahi tētahi monomai, kaupapa rānei e whakamahi ana i ngā taumahi whakamāori paerewa WordPress ki ngā whakamāoritanga motuhake-ki-te-pae.
