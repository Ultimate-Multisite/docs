# PayFast

Ultimate Multisite: Kuaha Payfast

Ka tāpiri i a Payfast - he kuaha utu tuihono tere, haumaru hoki i Āwherika ki te Tonga - hei kōwhiringa utu mō Ultimate Multisite.

## Tāutanga

1. Tukuatu 'ultimate-multisite-payfast' ki te whaiaronga '/wp-content/plugins/'
2. Whakahohe i te mono-mai mā te tahua 'Plugins' i WordPress
3. Whāia te Wizard ā-taahiraa hei whakarite i te mono-mai

## Aratohu Whakamahi

He pūtukatuka utu tuihono matua a PayFast mō ngā pakihi i Āwherika ki te Tonga. E ū ana ki te āwhina i ngā kaihoko tuihono kia tipu mā te hanga i ngā tauwhitinga kia māeneene, kia haumaru hoki. E ai ki te tono a te kaiwhakamahi Ultimate Multisite, i whakatau mātou ki te waihanga i tētahi tāpiritanga e taea ai te whakaurunga ki PayFast. Mā tēnei tuhinga e whakaatu me pēhea te whakarite i a PayFast hei kuaha utu hei whiwhi utu i tō whatunga Ultimate Multisite.

Ko te taahiraa tuatahi ko te tāuta i te tāpiritanga.

# Me pēhea te tāuta i te tāpiritanga Payfast:

Haere ki te whārangi tāpiritanga, ka tīpako i te tāpiritanga Ultimate Multisite: Payfast Gateway.

![Whārangi tāpiritanga](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

Ka puta ake te matapihi tāpiritanga. Pāwhiritia te Tāuta Ināianei.

![Whārangi tāpiritanga me te kōwhiringa tāuta](/img/admin/addons-page.png)

Kia oti te tāutanga, ka tukuna koe ki te whārangi mono-mai. I konei, pāwhiri noa i te Whakahohe Whatunga, ā, ka whakahohea te tāpiritanga _Payfast_ ki tō whatunga.

# Te Whakarite i a PayFast

Whai muri i te tāuta i te tāpiritanga, ka taea ināianei te haere ki ngā tautuhinga Ultimate Multisite, ka tīmata ki te whirihora i a PayFast.

I raro i te whārangi **tautuhinga Ultimate Multisite**, ka kitea e koe te ripa "Utu". I raro i tēnei ripa, me kite koe i te "**Payfast** " me tētahi pātene takahuri hei whakahohe, hei mono rānei i tēnei kuaha utu.

![Tautuhinga kuaha utu](/img/config/settings-payment-gateways.png)Haere tonu, pāwhiritia te pātene takahuri kia whakahohe. Kātahi ka whakawhānui i te pouaka hei whakaatu i ngā āpure rerekē me whakakī e koe, pērā i te **ID Kaihokohoko, Kī Kaihokohoko me te Kupuhipa-roa.** Ka taea e koe te tiki i ēnei mōhiohio mai i tō **Payfast** Account

<!-- Kāore te whakaahua mata i te wātea: tautuhinga PayFast kua whakawhānuitia e whakaatu ana i ngā āpure ID Kaihokohoko, Kī Kaihokohoko, me te Kupuhipa-roa -->

Ko te taahiraa e whai ake nei ko te takiuru ki tō PayFast Account kia tiki ai i ngā mōhiohio e hiahiatia ana e koe.

Ina takiuru, ka taea tonutia te kite i ngā taunakitanga e hiahiatia ana mai i tō **Payfast** Account Dashboard.

<!-- Kāore te whakaahua mata i te wātea: PayFast Account Dashboard e whakaatu ana i ngā taunakitanga ID Kaihokohoko me te Kī Kaihokohoko -->Ka taea noa e koe te tārua me te whakapiri i te taunakitanga, ā, te whakakī i ngā taipitopito ki ō tautuhinga Ultimate Multisite. Kātahi ka pāwhiri i te pātene **"Tiaki tautuhinga** " hei tiaki i ngā panonitanga.

<!-- Kāore te whakaahua mata i te wātea: tautuhinga Ultimate Multisite PayFast kua whakakīngia ki ngā taunakitanga me te pātene Tiaki -->

Koirā, kua oti tō whakaurunga utu PayFast!
