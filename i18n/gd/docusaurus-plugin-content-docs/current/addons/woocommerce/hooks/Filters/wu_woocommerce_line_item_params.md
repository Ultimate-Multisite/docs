# Filter: wu_woocommerce_line_item_params

Criathraich na paramadairean a chleachdar gus na nithean loidhne a chruthachadh air Cart WooCommerce, mus tèid an custamaiche ath-stiùireadh gu checkout WooCommerce.

## Paramadairean

| Ainm | Seòrsa | Tuairisgeul |
|------|------|-------------|
| $line_item_params | `array` | Paramadairean nì loidhne na cairt. |
| $wu_line_item | `\WP_Ultimo\Checkout\Line_Item` | Eisimpleir nì loidhne Ultimate Multisite. |
| $wc_product | `\WC_Product` | Am bathar WooCommerce. |
