# Criathrag: secure_logged_in_cookie {#filter-secureloggedincookie}

Criathraich am bu chòir don cookie airson neach a tha air logadh a-steach a bhith air a chur a-mhàin thairis air HTTPS.

## Paramadairean {#parameters}

| Ainm | Seòrsa | Tuairisgeul |
|------|------|-------------|
| $secure_logged_in_cookie | `bool` | Am bu chòir don cookie airson neach a tha air logadh a-steach a bhith air a chur a-mhàin thairis air HTTPS. |
| $user_id | `int` | ID a’ chleachdaiche. |
| $secure | `bool` | Am bu chòir don cookie dearbhaidh a bhith air a chur a-mhàin thairis air HTTPS. |
