# Criathrag: wu_sso_enabled

Cuir an comas/cuir à comas comas single-sign-on thar àrainnean.

Criathraich an luach seo gus single-sign-on a chur dheth gu tur, no gus a chur an comas gu cumhach na àite.

## Paramadairean {#parameters}

| Ainm | Seòrsa | Tuairisgeul |
|------|------|-------------|
| $enabled | `bool` | Am bu chòir SSO a bhith an comas? True airson air, false-ish airson dheth. |

### Bho {#since}

- 2.0.11
### Tùs {#source}

Air a mhìneachadh ann an [`inc/sso/class-sso.php`](https://github.com/Ultimate-Multisite/ultimate-multisite/blob/main/inc/sso/class-sso.php#L110) aig loidhne 110


## Tilleadh {#returns}
Co-dhiù a bheil SSO an comas no nach eil.
